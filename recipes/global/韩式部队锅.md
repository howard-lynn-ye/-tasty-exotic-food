> 韩国最接地气的一锅出——泡面、午餐肉、芝士、泡菜统统丢进去煮。诞生于战后物资匮乏年代，用美军基地剩余食材混搭韩式调味，反而创造出了让人上瘾的味道。在美国做这道菜再合适不过，因为原材料本来就是美国食材！
>
> *Korea's most down-to-earth one-pot meal — instant ramen, Spam, cheese, and kimchi all thrown into one bubbling stew. Born in the post-war era from surplus American army base ingredients mixed with Korean flavors, it became an addictive classic. Making this in America is almost poetic — the ingredients were American to begin with!*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 泡面 | Instant ramen noodles (discard seasoning packet) | 1包 / 1 pack |
| 午餐肉 (Spam) | Spam | 半罐切片 / half can, sliced |
| 泡菜 | Kimchi | 1杯 / 1 cup (~150g) |
| 韩式辣酱 | Gochujang (Korean chili paste) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 韩式辣椒粉 | Gochugaru (Korean chili flakes) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 芝士片 | American cheese slices | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 豆腐 | Firm tofu | 半块 / half block |
| 热狗或香肠 | Hot dogs or sausage | 2根 / 2 |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 水 | Water | 600ml |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
Spam 切片，热狗斜切，豆腐切块，蒜切末，葱切段。

Slice Spam, cut hot dogs on a diagonal, cube the tofu, mince garlic, cut scallions into segments.

### 2. 摆锅 | Arrange in Pot
在一个宽口锅中，将 Spam、热狗、泡菜、豆腐分区摆放（好看又好吃）。

In a wide pot, arrange Spam, hot dogs, kimchi, and tofu in separate sections (looks great and cooks evenly).

### 3. 调味 | Season
在食材上撒蒜末、gochujang 和 gochugaru。倒入600ml水。

Sprinkle garlic, gochujang, and gochugaru over the ingredients. Pour in 600 ml water.

### 4. 煮沸 | Boil
大火烧开，放入泡面，煮3-4分钟至面熟。

Bring to a boil. Add ramen noodles and cook 3–4 minutes until noodles are done.

### 5. 加芝士上桌 | Add Cheese & Serve
铺上芝士片，盖上盖子焖30秒至芝士融化。撒葱花，直接端锅上桌。

Lay cheese slices on top. Cover for 30 seconds until the cheese melts. Sprinkle scallions. Serve the pot directly at the table.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 泡面的调料包不要用，味道太咸 | Discard the ramen seasoning packet — the stew has enough flavor |
| 芝士融化那一刻是灵魂 | The moment the cheese melts is the soul of this dish |
| 可以加任何你喜欢的食材 | Add anything you like — baked beans, rice cakes, mushrooms |
| 剩汤可以加米饭做泡饭 | Leftover broth + rice = amazing next-day porridge |
