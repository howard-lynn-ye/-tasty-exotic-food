# 西班牙蒜虾 | Spanish Gambas al Ajillo

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 8分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 西班牙、快手、全超市可买、派对菜

> Gambas al Ajillo（蒜香虾）是西班牙tapas中最经典的一道，做法极其简单——大量蒜片和干辣椒在橄榄油中慢煎至金黄，再放入虾快速翻炒，最后撒上欧芹和一挤柠檬。配面包蘸着滚烫的蒜油吃，这就是西班牙式的幸福。
>
> *Gambas al Ajillo (garlic shrimp) is the crown jewel of Spanish tapas, and the recipe is almost embarrassingly simple — sliced garlic and dried chili sizzle in olive oil until golden, shrimp are tossed in briefly, then finished with parsley and lemon. Dip crusty bread into the sizzling garlic oil and you'll understand why this dish has conquered the world.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 虾 | Shrimp (peeled, deveined) | 400g (about 1 lb) |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 8-10瓣 / 8-10 cloves |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chili (or red pepper flakes) | 2根 / 2 (or 1/2 tsp flakes) |
| 特级初榨橄榄油 | Extra virgin olive oil | 1/3杯 / 1/3 cup (~80ml) |
| 甜椒粉 (可选) | Smoked paprika (optional) | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 白葡萄酒或雪利酒 | Dry white wine or sherry | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 欧芹 | Fresh parsley (chopped) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | 1个 / 1 |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 面包 | Crusty bread (for serving) | 适量 / for dipping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
蒜切薄片（不要切末，要片）。虾擦干，撒少许盐。干辣椒切段去籽（怕辣可少放）。

Slice garlic thinly (slices, not minced). Pat shrimp dry and season lightly with salt. Cut dried chilies into segments and remove seeds (use fewer if heat-sensitive).

### 2. 煎蒜 | Sizzle Garlic
小锅或小平底锅中倒入橄榄油，小火加热。放入蒜片和干辣椒，慢慢煎至蒜片边缘微微金黄（约2-3分钟）。注意不要煎焦。

Pour olive oil into a small pan or skillet. Heat over low heat. Add garlic slices and dried chilies. Cook slowly until garlic edges turn just golden (~2-3 min). Be careful not to burn them.

### 3. 下虾 | Cook Shrimp
转中大火，下虾翻炒约1分钟至变色。倒入白葡萄酒，再翻炒1分钟至虾完全熟透、酒精蒸发。加入甜椒粉（可选），撒盐调味。

Raise heat to medium-high. Add shrimp and toss for ~1 minute until they turn pink. Pour in white wine and cook another minute until shrimp are just cooked through and alcohol has evaporated. Add smoked paprika if using. Season with salt.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒欧芹，挤柠檬汁，连锅端上桌（或倒入小碗）。配烤面包蘸蒜油吃。

Sprinkle parsley, squeeze lemon juice, and serve straight from the pan (or transfer to a small dish). Serve with crusty bread for dipping in the garlic oil.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 蒜一定要切片不要切末，慢煎才香 | Slice garlic, don't mince — slow frying slices gives the best flavor |
| 小火煎蒜，焦了就苦了，要金黄不要焦黄 | Low heat for garlic — burnt garlic is bitter; aim for light gold |
| 虾不要煮过头，弹牙的口感才对 | Don't overcook shrimp — they should be bouncy and juicy |
| 橄榄油不要省，蒜油蘸面包才是灵魂 | Don't skimp on olive oil — the garlic-infused oil with bread is the soul |
