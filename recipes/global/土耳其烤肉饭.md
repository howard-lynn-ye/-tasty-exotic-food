# 土耳其烤肉饭 | Turkish Doner Kebab Rice Bowl
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/global/土耳其烤肉饭.svg" width="500" alt="土耳其烤肉饭"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 腌制 30分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 中东、全超市可买、高蛋白

> 土耳其烤肉（Doner Kebab）是全球最受欢迎的街头美食之一，但家里没有旋转烤肉架也能做出神还原的版本。将调味好的薄肉片在平底锅里煎至焦香，配上米饭、蔬菜沙拉和自制酸奶酱，一碗就是满满的中东风情。
>
> *Doner Kebab is one of the world's most beloved street foods. You don't need a vertical rotisserie at home — thinly sliced, well-seasoned meat pan-seared until crispy delivers an amazingly close result. Served over rice with fresh salad and homemade yogurt sauce, it's a complete Mediterranean bowl.*

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌肉 | Marinate
肉切薄片（约3mm），加洋葱碎、蒜末、孜然、辣椒粉、oregano、橄榄油、盐和黑胡椒拌匀。腌制30分钟（或冰箱过夜更好）。

Slice meat thin (~3mm). Toss with grated onion, garlic, cumin, paprika, oregano, olive oil, salt, and pepper. Marinate 30 minutes (or overnight in the fridge for better flavor).

### 2. 做酸奶酱 | Make Yogurt Sauce
酸奶加蒜末、柠檬汁和盐搅匀。冰箱冷藏备用。

Mix yogurt with garlic, lemon juice, and salt. Refrigerate until ready to serve.

### 3. 煎肉 | Sear Meat
平底锅大火烧热，不加油（腌料自带油），将肉片摊开煎至边缘焦脆，不要频繁翻动。分批煎，不要堆叠。

Heat a skillet over high heat. No extra oil needed (the marinade has enough). Spread meat slices flat and sear until the edges are crispy. Don't move them too often. Cook in batches — don't crowd the pan.

### 4. 组装 | Assemble Bowl
碗中盛米饭，铺上生菜和番茄，摆上煎好的烤肉，淋上酸奶酱和辣酱，放上红洋葱圈。

Place rice in a bowl. Layer lettuce and diced tomato. Top with seared meat. Drizzle yogurt sauce and hot sauce. Add red onion rings.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 肉要切薄，焦边才是灵魂 | Slice meat thin — the crispy charred edges are the soul of doner |
| 大火煎，不要翻太勤，让美拉德反应充分发生 | High heat, don't flip too often — let the Maillard reaction do its work |
| 酸奶酱的蒜味要明显，不要太少 | The garlic in the yogurt sauce should be assertive — don't skimp |
| 用鸡腿肉比鸡胸肉好吃很多 | Chicken thigh is far superior to chicken breast for this |
