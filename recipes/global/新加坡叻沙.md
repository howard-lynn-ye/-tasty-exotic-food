# 新加坡叻沙 | Singapore Laksa

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 东南亚、汤面、需要亚洲超市

> 叻沙是新加坡的国民美食，一碗浓郁的椰奶咖喱汤底，配上米粉、虾、豆腐泡和鱼饼，鲜辣香甜交织。自己做比外面便宜一半，而且用叻沙酱（laksa paste）大大简化了工序。
>
> *Laksa is Singapore's national comfort food — a bowl of rich coconut curry broth loaded with rice noodles, shrimp, tofu puffs, and fish cake. Spicy, creamy, sweet, and aromatic all at once. Making it at home costs half the restaurant price, and laksa paste makes the process surprisingly simple.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 叻沙酱 | Laksa paste | 3-4汤匙 / 3-4 tbsp |
| 椰奶 | Coconut milk (full fat) | 1罐 (400ml) |
| 鸡汤 | Chicken broth | 500ml (2 cups) |
| 米粉 (或濑粉) | Rice vermicelli (or laksa noodles) | 200g |
| 虾 | Shrimp (shell-on or peeled) | 200g |
| 豆腐泡 | Fried tofu puffs | 6-8个 / 6-8 pieces |
| 鱼饼 (可选) | Fish cake (optional) | 4-6片 / 4-6 slices |
| 豆芽 | Bean sprouts | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Hard-boiled egg | 2个 / 2 |
| 辣椒酱 | Sambal oelek (chili paste) | 适量 / to taste |
| 青柠 | Lime | 1个 / 1 |
| 香菜或叻沙叶 | Cilantro or laksa leaf | 少许 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒叻沙酱 | Fry Laksa Paste
锅中放1汤匙油，中火炒叻沙酱2-3分钟至出香味、油分离出来。

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a pot. Fry laksa paste over medium heat for 2-3 minutes until fragrant and the oil separates.

### 2. 煮汤底 | Make the Broth
倒入鸡汤和椰奶，搅匀烧开，转小火煮10分钟让味道融合。尝一下，加盐或糖调味。

Pour in chicken broth and coconut milk. Stir, bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and simmer 10 minutes. Taste and adjust with salt or sugar.

### 3. 煮配料 | Cook Toppings
虾放入汤中煮至变色（约2分钟）。豆腐泡对半切开放入。米粉另起锅用开水泡软（按包装说明）。

Add shrimp to the broth and cook until pink (~2 min). Halve tofu puffs and add to the broth. Soak rice vermicelli in boiling water separately (follow package instructions).

### 4. 组装上桌 | Assemble
碗底放米粉和豆芽，浇上叻沙汤，摆上虾、豆腐泡、鱼饼和半个水煮蛋。挤青柠汁，加辣椒酱，撒香菜。

Place noodles and bean sprouts in a bowl. Ladle the laksa broth over them. Arrange shrimp, tofu puffs, fish cake, and a halved boiled egg on top. Squeeze lime, add chili paste, garnish with cilantro.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 叻沙酱一定要先炒，炒出香味才好喝 | Fry the laksa paste first — this blooms the spices and deepens the flavor |
| 椰奶用全脂的，低脂汤底寡淡 | Use full-fat coconut milk — low-fat makes a thin, sad broth |
| 虾不要煮太久，2分钟足够 | Don't overcook shrimp — 2 minutes is plenty |
| 青柠汁是点睛之笔，不可省略 | Lime juice is essential — it brightens the whole bowl |
