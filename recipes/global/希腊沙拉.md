# 希腊沙拉 | Greek Salad

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 0分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 免烹饪、清爽、地中海风味、健康

> 希腊沙拉（Horiatiki）是地中海饮食的代表之作，完全不需要开火。脆嫩的蔬菜、咸香的菲达奶酪、酸甜的油醋汁，简单到不像话，却好吃到停不下来。夏天的救命菜。
>
> *The classic Greek salad (Horiatiki) is the jewel of Mediterranean cooking — zero cooking required. Crisp vegetables, briny feta cheese, and a bright olive-oil dressing come together in minutes. Your summer survival dish.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 英式黄瓜 | English cucumber | 1根 / 1 |
| 番茄 | Tomatoes | 2个大 / 2 large |
| 红洋葱 | Red onion | 1/2个 / 1/2 |
| 青椒 | Green bell pepper | 1个 / 1 |
| 卡拉马塔橄榄 | Kalamata olives | 1/3杯 / 1/3 cup |
| 菲达奶酪 | Feta cheese | 100g / 3.5 oz (一块) |
| 特级初榨橄榄油 | Extra virgin olive oil | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 红酒醋 | Red wine vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 干牛至（Oregano） | Dried oregano | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐和黑胡椒 | Salt & black pepper | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切蔬菜 | Chop the Vegetables
黄瓜切半月形厚片，番茄切大块，红洋葱切薄环，青椒切圈或块。

Cut the cucumber into thick half-moons, tomatoes into chunky wedges, red onion into thin rings, and bell pepper into rings or chunks.

### 2. 组装 | Assemble
将所有蔬菜和橄榄放入大碗或盘中，轻轻拌匀。将整块菲达奶酪放在最上面（正宗做法是整块，不是碎的）。

Place all vegetables and olives in a large bowl or platter, toss gently. Set the feta block on top (authentic style is one whole slab, not crumbled).

### 3. 调味上桌 | Dress & Serve
淋上橄榄油和红酒醋，撒上干牛至、盐和黑胡椒。立即上桌，配面包蘸汁更棒。

Drizzle with olive oil and red wine vinegar. Sprinkle with oregano, salt, and pepper. Serve immediately — crusty bread for dipping in the juices is a bonus.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 蔬菜切大块才正宗，不是切丝 | Cut vegetables chunky — this isn't a chopped salad |
| 菲达整块放，吃时再掰 | Place feta as a whole block; break off pieces while eating |
| 正宗希腊沙拉不放生菜 | Authentic Greek salad has NO lettuce |
| 用好的橄榄油，味道差别很大 | Use quality olive oil — it makes a huge difference |
| 番茄要当季的最好吃 | In-season tomatoes are infinitely better |
