# 日式亲子丼 | Japanese Oyakodon
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/global/日式亲子丼.svg" width="500" alt="日式亲子丼"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 日式、盖饭、快手、暖心

> 亲子丼是日本国民级盖饭，"亲子"指的是鸡肉（亲）和鸡蛋（子）的组合。鸡肉和洋葱在甜咸的出汁中炖煮，最后淋上半熟的蛋液，盖在热米饭上——简单、温暖、让人想家。
>
> *Oyakodon is Japan's quintessential comfort bowl. "Oyako" means parent-and-child — chicken (parent) and egg (child) united in a sweet-savory dashi broth, draped over steaming hot rice. Simple, warm, and deeply satisfying.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿肉（去骨） | Boneless chicken thighs | 200g / 7 oz |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 3个 / 3 |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1/2个 / 1/2 medium |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 味醂 | Mirin | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 出汁（或鸡汤） | Dashi stock (or chicken broth) | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 米饭 | Steamed rice | 2碗 / 2 bowls |
| 葱花 | Scallion, sliced | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
鸡腿肉切小块（约2cm），洋葱切薄丝，鸡蛋打散（不要打太匀，保留蛋白蛋黄的纹路）。

Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces (~2cm). Slice onion thinly. Beat eggs loosely — don't over-mix; you want streaks of white and yolk.

### 2. 煮鸡肉和洋葱 | Simmer Chicken & Onion
小锅或平底锅中混合出汁、酱油、味醂和糖，烧开后放入洋葱丝和鸡肉块，中火煮5-6分钟直到鸡肉熟透。

In a small pan, combine dashi, soy sauce, mirin, and sugar. Bring to a simmer, add onion and chicken. Cook on medium heat for 5-6 minutes until chicken is cooked through.

### 3. 淋蛋液 | Add the Egg
将2/3的蛋液均匀淋在鸡肉上，盖盖焖1分钟。打开盖子淋上剩余1/3蛋液，再焖30秒即关火（蛋要半熟滑嫩）。

Pour 2/3 of the beaten egg evenly over the chicken. Cover and cook 1 minute. Uncover, drizzle remaining 1/3 of egg on top, cover again for 30 seconds, then turn off heat. The egg should be soft and just barely set.

### 4. 盛饭上桌 | Serve over Rice
将鸡蛋鸡肉连汁滑到热米饭上，撒上葱花即可。

Slide the chicken-egg mixture with all its sauce over a bowl of hot rice. Top with sliced scallions.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 蛋分两次加是关键，口感更嫩 | Adding egg in two stages gives the best silky texture |
| 蛋不要全熟，半熟最好吃 | Don't overcook the egg — soft and custardy is the goal |
| 用鸡腿肉不用鸡胸肉 | Thighs stay juicy; breast gets dry |
| 没有味醂可以用料酒+糖代替 | No mirin? Use rice wine + a pinch of sugar |
| 配日式腌菜或味噌汤更完整 | Pickles or miso soup on the side complete the meal |
