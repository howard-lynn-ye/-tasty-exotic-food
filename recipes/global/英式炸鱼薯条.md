# 英式炸鱼薯条 | British Fish & Chips

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 英国、全超市可买、周末大餐

> 炸鱼薯条是英国的国民美食，外酥里嫩的啤酒面糊炸鱼配上粗切薯条，再挤上柠檬汁、蘸塔塔酱，这就是英国人的comfort food。秘诀在于面糊里加啤酒——产生的气泡让外皮酥脆无比。
>
> *Fish and Chips is Britain's national dish — flaky white fish in a crispy beer batter served with thick-cut fries, a squeeze of lemon, and tartar sauce. The secret to the impossibly crispy batter? Beer — the carbonation creates air pockets that shatter on first bite.*

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备薯条 | Prep Fries
土豆去皮切粗条（约1cm宽），浸泡冷水20分钟去除淀粉。捞出彻底擦干。

Peel potatoes and cut into thick sticks (~1cm wide). Soak in cold water for 20 minutes to remove starch. Drain and pat completely dry.

### 2. 炸薯条 (两次炸) | Fry Chips (Twice)
油温160°C (320°F) 先炸5分钟至薯条软熟但不上色，捞出。将油温升至190°C (375°F)，再炸3-4分钟至金黄酥脆。撒盐。

Fry at 160°C (320°F) for 5 minutes until soft but not browned. Remove. Raise oil to 190°C (375°F) and fry again for 3-4 minutes until golden and crispy. Season with salt.

### 3. 调面糊 | Make Batter
面粉、泡打粉和盐混合。倒入冰啤酒搅拌至顺滑（不要过度搅拌，有小颗粒没关系）。

Mix flour, baking powder, and salt. Pour in cold beer and whisk until smooth (don't over-mix — a few small lumps are fine).

### 4. 炸鱼 | Fry Fish
鱼片擦干，先薄薄裹一层干面粉，再裹上面糊。油温180°C (360°F)，将鱼轻轻放入，炸5-6分钟至金黄酥脆，翻面一次。捞出沥油。

Pat fish dry. Dust with a thin layer of flour, then dip into batter. Fry at 180°C (360°F) for 5-6 minutes until golden and crispy, turning once. Drain on a wire rack or paper towels.

### 5. 上桌 | Serve
薯条和炸鱼装盘，配柠檬角、塔塔酱和麦芽醋。

Plate the chips and fish together. Serve with lemon wedges, tartar sauce, and malt vinegar.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 啤酒一定要冰的，温度差让面糊更酥脆 | Beer MUST be ice cold — the temperature contrast makes crispier batter |
| 鱼先裹干面粉再裹面糊，面糊才能挂住 | Dust fish with dry flour first — the batter won't stick otherwise |
| 薯条两次炸是关键，一次炸不够脆 | Double-frying is essential — single frying won't get them crispy enough |
| 炸好立刻吃，放久了就不酥了 | Eat immediately — crispiness fades fast |
