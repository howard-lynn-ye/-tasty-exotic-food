# 印度黄油鸡 | Indian Butter Chicken
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/global/印度黄油鸡.svg" width="500" alt="印度黄油鸡"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 咖喱、印度经典、配米饭或馕

> 黄油鸡（Murgh Makhani）是印度菜在全世界最受欢迎的一道。嫩滑的鸡肉裹在浓郁的番茄奶油咖喱酱中，微甜微辣，配上米饭或烤馕，让人一口接一口停不下来。
>
> *Butter Chicken (Murgh Makhani) is India's most beloved export. Tender chicken pieces swim in a velvety tomato-cream curry sauce — gently sweet, gently spiced — begging for fluffy rice or warm naan to soak up every last drop.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿肉（去骨） | Boneless chicken thighs | 500g / 1 lb |
| 酸奶（原味） | Plain yogurt | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 姜蒜末 | Minced ginger & garlic | 各1汤匙 / 1 tbsp each |
| 番茄罐头（碎番茄） | Canned crushed tomatoes | 1罐400g / 1 can (14 oz) |
| 黄油 | Butter | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 重奶油（Heavy cream） | Heavy cream | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium |
| 咖喱粉 | Curry powder | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 孜然粉 | Ground cumin | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 辣椒粉（Paprika） | Paprika | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 香菜 | Fresh cilantro | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌鸡肉 | Marinate the Chicken
鸡腿肉切块，用酸奶、一半的姜蒜末、咖喱粉和盐拌匀，腌10分钟（或提前冰箱过夜更好）。

Cut chicken into bite-sized pieces. Mix with yogurt, half the ginger-garlic, curry powder, and salt. Marinate 10 minutes (or overnight in the fridge for best results).

### 2. 煎鸡肉 | Sear the Chicken
锅中放1汤匙黄油，中大火将鸡肉块煎至两面金黄，盛出备用。不需要完全熟透。

Melt 1 tbsp butter in a pan over medium-high heat. Sear chicken pieces until golden on both sides. Set aside — they don't need to be cooked through yet.

### 3. 做酱汁 | Build the Sauce
同一锅中加剩余黄油，炒洋葱碎至透明，加入剩余姜蒜末炒香，倒入碎番茄、孜然粉、辣椒粉和糖，中火煮10分钟至酱汁浓稠。用搅拌机或手持料理棒打成顺滑酱汁。

In the same pan, add remaining butter. Saut onion until translucent, add remaining ginger-garlic and cook until fragrant. Pour in crushed tomatoes, cumin, paprika, and sugar. Simmer 10 minutes until thickened. Blend until smooth with an immersion blender (or transfer to a blender).

### 4. 合并炖煮 | Simmer Together
将鸡肉放回酱汁中，倒入重奶油，小火炖10分钟至鸡肉完全熟透、酱汁浓郁。尝味调盐，撒香菜出锅。

Return chicken to the sauce, stir in the heavy cream, and simmer on low for 10 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce is rich. Adjust salt, garnish with cilantro, and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用鸡腿肉而不是鸡胸，更嫩更香 | Use thighs, not breasts — far more tender and flavorful |
| 酱汁一定要打成顺滑 | Blend the sauce smooth for authentic texture |
| 奶油最后加，不要煮沸 | Add cream at the end and don't boil it |
| 配印度香米（Basmati）最正宗 | Serve with basmati rice for the most authentic pairing |
| 可以加一点蜂蜜增加浓郁度 | A touch of honey adds depth |
