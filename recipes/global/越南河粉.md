# 越南河粉 | Vietnamese Pho

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 汤面、越南经典、暖胃

> 越南河粉是世界上最治愈的汤面之一。清澈却浓郁的牛骨汤底，配上滑嫩的米粉和薄切牛肉，再堆上满满的新鲜香草和豆芽，挤一点青柠汁——一碗下去，什么烦恼都没了。
>
> *Pho is one of the world's most comforting noodle soups. A clear yet deeply savory broth ladled over silky rice noodles and paper-thin beef, topped with a mountain of fresh herbs, bean sprouts, and a squeeze of lime — pure healing in a bowl.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛骨汤 | Beef broth | 6杯 / 6 cups |
| 干河粉（宽米粉） | Dried rice noodles (pad thai style) | 200g / 7 oz |
| 牛肉片（肥牛或眼肉） | Beef sirloin, sliced paper-thin | 150g / 5 oz |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium |
| 生姜 | Ginger | 1块（5cm） / 2-inch piece |
| 八角 | Star anise | 2个 / 2 whole |
| 肉桂棒 | Cinnamon stick | 1根 / 1 stick |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 冰糖或白糖 | Rock sugar or white sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 豆芽 | Bean sprouts | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 新鲜罗勒（泰式最佳） | Fresh basil (Thai basil preferred) | 一把 / 1 handful |
| 青柠 | Lime | 1个 / 1 |
| 是拉差辣酱 | Sriracha | 适量 / to taste |
| 海鲜酱 | Hoisin sauce | 适量 / to taste |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 熬汤底 | Build the Broth
洋葱和生姜切半，放在干锅中或烤箱里烤至微焦。然后加入牛骨汤、八角、肉桂棒，中小火煮30分钟。加入鱼露和糖调味，捞出香料。

Halve the onion and ginger and char them in a dry skillet or under the broiler until lightly blackened. Add to the beef broth with star anise and cinnamon stick. Simmer on medium-low for 30 minutes. Season with fish sauce and sugar, then strain out the spices.

### 2. 煮河粉 | Cook the Noodles
按包装说明将干河粉用热水泡软或煮熟，沥干备用。

Soak or boil the rice noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.

### 3. 切牛肉 | Prep the Beef
牛肉放冰箱冷冻15分钟后更容易切薄片。切成2毫米薄片。

For easier slicing, freeze the beef for 15 minutes. Slice across the grain into 2mm-thin pieces.

### 4. 组装上桌 | Assemble & Serve
碗底放河粉，铺上生牛肉片，将滚烫的汤底浇上去（热汤会将牛肉烫熟）。旁边摆上豆芽、罗勒、青柠角和辣酱，让每个人自己加。

Place noodles in a bowl, arrange raw beef slices on top, and pour the boiling-hot broth over everything (the hot broth cooks the beef). Serve with bean sprouts, basil, lime wedges, sriracha, and hoisin on the side.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 汤一定要滚烫才能烫熟牛肉 | The broth must be boiling hot to cook the raw beef |
| 烤洋葱姜是风味关键 | Charring onion and ginger is key to authentic flavor |
| 牛肉切得越薄越好 | The thinner the beef, the better |
| 配菜要新鲜，不要提前切 | Keep garnishes fresh — don't pre-cut herbs |
| 鱼露分次加，边尝边调 | Add fish sauce gradually and taste as you go |
