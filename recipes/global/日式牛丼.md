# 日式牛丼 | Japanese Beef Rice Bowl (Gyudon)

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/global/gyudon.jpg" width="500" alt="日式牛丼">
</p>

> 日本吉野家的招牌菜，留学生在美国也能轻松复刻。薄切牛肉片配上甜咸酱汁，浇在热米饭上，15分钟就是一碗暖心饭。Costco 的牛肉薄片是最佳选择。
>
> *The signature dish of Yoshinoya, Japan's most famous fast-food chain — and incredibly easy to recreate at home. Thin-sliced beef simmered in a sweet-savory sauce, ladled over hot rice. Fifteen minutes from start to finish. Costco's thinly sliced beef is perfect for this.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛肉薄片 | Thinly sliced beef (ribeye or chuck) | 300g |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 large |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 味醂 | Mirin | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 150ml |
| 姜 | Ginger | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 米饭 | Cooked rice | 2碗 / 2 bowls |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg (optional, for topping) | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切料 | Prep
洋葱切薄片。牛肉如果太大片就切成适口的大小。

Slice the onion thinly. Cut beef into bite-sized pieces if the slices are too large.

### 2. 煮汁 | Make the Sauce
锅中加水、酱油、味醂、白糖和姜片，烧开。

Combine water, soy sauce, mirin, sugar, and ginger in a pan. Bring to a boil.

### 3. 煮洋葱和牛肉 | Simmer
先放入洋葱煮3分钟至软，再放入牛肉片，煮2-3分钟至熟。不要煮太久，牛肉会老。

Add onion slices and simmer 3 minutes until soft. Add beef slices and cook 2–3 minutes until just done. Don't overcook — the beef should stay tender.

### 4. 盖饭 | Serve
将牛肉和洋葱连汤汁浇在热米饭上。可以加一个温泉蛋或生蛋黄。

Ladle the beef, onion, and sauce over hot steamed rice. Top with a soft-boiled egg or raw egg yolk if desired.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 牛肉要切薄，太厚煮不入味 | Slice beef thin — thick pieces won't absorb the sauce |
| 味醂是关键，带出日式甜味 | Mirin is essential — it gives the signature Japanese sweetness |
| 不要煮太久，2-3分钟足够 | Don't overcook — 2-3 minutes is plenty |
| 加一个温泉蛋，幸福感翻倍 | A soft-boiled egg on top doubles the happiness |
