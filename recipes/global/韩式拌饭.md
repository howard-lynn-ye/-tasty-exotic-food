# 韩式拌饭 | Bibimbap

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 韩国、健康、五彩、Meal Prep

> 韩国的"什么都有饭"——米饭上摆满五颜六色的蔬菜、牛肉和煎蛋，拌上辣酱石锅吃。冰箱里有什么就放什么，是清冰箱的最佳方式。在美国做 bibimbap 特别方便——Trader Joe's 和 Costco 有你需要的一切。
>
> *Korea's "everything bowl" — rice topped with colorful vegetables, beef, and a fried egg, mixed with gochujang. Use whatever's in your fridge — the ultimate fridge-cleaner. Making bibimbap in America is especially easy — Trader Joe's and Costco have everything you need.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 米饭 | Cooked rice | 2碗 / 2 bowls |
| 牛肉末或牛肉片 | Ground beef or sliced beef | 150g |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot (julienned) | 1根 / 1 |
| 菠菜 | Spinach | 1把 / 1 bunch |
| 黄瓜 | Cucumber (julienned) | 半根 / half |
| 豆芽 | Bean sprouts | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 韩式辣酱 | Gochujang | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣 / 2 cloves |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备菜 | Prep Vegetables
胡萝卜切丝炒1分钟。菠菜焯水拧干拌芝麻油。豆芽焯水。黄瓜切丝。每种蔬菜分开备好。

Julienne carrot, sauté 1 minute. Blanch spinach, squeeze dry, toss with sesame oil. Blanch bean sprouts. Julienne cucumber. Keep each vegetable separate.

### 2. 炒肉 | Cook Meat
牛肉加酱油、蒜泥、芝麻油拌匀，炒至变色。

Season beef with soy sauce, garlic, sesame oil. Stir-fry until cooked.

### 3. 煎蛋 | Fry Eggs
煎2个太阳蛋（溏心）。

Fry 2 sunny-side-up eggs (runny yolk).

### 4. 组装 | Assemble
碗底铺热米饭，四周摆上各色蔬菜和牛肉，中间放煎蛋。加一大勺 gochujang。

Hot rice at the bottom. Arrange vegetables and beef around the bowl in sections. Place egg in the center. Add a generous spoonful of gochujang.

### 5. 拌！| MIX!
大力搅拌至所有食材混合均匀。这一步是灵魂。

Stir vigorously until everything is thoroughly mixed. THIS is the soul of bibimbap.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 蔬菜种类越多越好看越好吃 | More vegetable varieties = prettier AND tastier |
| 拌的时候要大力，不要害羞 | Mix aggressively — don't be shy |
| Gochujang 按口味加，辣度自控 | Add gochujang to taste — you control the heat |
| 冰箱里有什么就放什么 | Use whatever's in your fridge |
