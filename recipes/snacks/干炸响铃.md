# 干炸响铃 | Crispy Tofu Skin Rolls

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/snacks/ganzha-xiangling.jpg" width="500" alt="干炸响铃">
</p>

> "响铃"之名源于炸好的腐皮卷形似铃铛，咬下去酥脆作响。这道杭州传统前菜以薄腐皮裹入肉馅，高油温炸至金黄酥脆，蘸甜面酱食用，外酥里嫩，香气诱人。
>
> *The name "Ringing Bells" comes from the bell-like shape of the fried rolls and the crispy crunch when you bite into one. Paper-thin tofu skin is wrapped around a savory pork filling, deep-fried until shatteringly crisp, and served with sweet bean sauce — a beloved Hangzhou appetizer.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 干豆腐皮 | Dried tofu skin (yuba sheets) | 4张 / 4 sheets |
| 猪肉馅 | Ground pork | 200g |
| 姜末 | Minced ginger | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 / a pinch |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch (for sealing) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil (for frying) | 适量 / as needed |
| 甜面酱 | Sweet bean sauce (for dipping) | 适量 / for serving |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调馅 | Make the Filling
猪肉馅加入姜末、葱花、酱油、料酒、盐和白胡椒粉，搅拌均匀上劲。

Combine the ground pork with minced ginger, scallion, soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, salt, and white pepper. Mix until the filling is cohesive and slightly sticky.

### 2. 处理腐皮 | Prep the Tofu Skin
干腐皮用湿布盖住回软（约10分钟），然后切成约10cm×15cm的长方形。

Cover the dried tofu skin sheets with a damp cloth to soften (~10 minutes). Cut into rectangles, roughly 10 cm × 15 cm.

### 3. 包卷 | Roll
在每张腐皮上薄薄涂一层肉馅，卷成紧实的小卷，用淀粉糊封口。

Spread a thin layer of filling on each tofu skin rectangle. Roll up tightly and seal the edge with a cornstarch paste.

### 4. 炸制 | Deep-fry
油温升至180°C (350°F)，放入腐皮卷炸至金黄酥脆，约2-3分钟。捞出沥油。

Heat oil to 180°C (350°F). Fry the rolls until golden and crispy, about 2–3 minutes. Drain on paper towels.

### 5. 装盘 | Serve
斜刀切段，搭配甜面酱蘸食。

Cut each roll diagonally into segments. Serve with sweet bean sauce for dipping.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 腐皮要用干的，湿腐皮炸不脆 | Use dried tofu skin — fresh/wet sheets won't crisp up |
| 肉馅要薄涂，太厚则外焦里生 | Spread the filling thin — too thick and the inside won't cook through |
| 油温要够高，入锅即起泡 | Oil must be hot enough — rolls should bubble immediately on contact |
| 炸好趁热吃，凉了口感会变 | Eat immediately — crispness fades as they cool |
