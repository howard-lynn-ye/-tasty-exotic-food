# 葱油饼 | Scallion Pancakes

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 醒面 30分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 面食、全超市可买、素食

> 葱油饼是中国街头最常见的面食之一，外酥内软、层层分明、葱香扑鼻。不需要发面，不需要烤箱，一个平底锅就能搞定。揉面、擀开、撒葱花、卷起再压扁，简单的手法却做出令人惊叹的层次感。
>
> *Scallion pancakes are one of China's most iconic street foods — crispy outside, flaky and soft inside, with layers upon layers of fragrant scallion. No yeast, no oven — just a skillet. Roll, scatter scallions, coil, flatten, and fry. Simple technique, extraordinary texture.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2杯 / 2 cups (250g) |
| 开水 | Boiling water | 3/4杯 / 3/4 cup (~180ml) |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱 | Scallions (chopped) | 6根 / 6 stalks |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 适量 / as needed |
| 香油 | Sesame oil (optional) | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 和面 | Make Dough
面粉加盐混合，慢慢倒入开水，边倒边用筷子搅拌。稍凉后揉成光滑面团。盖上保鲜膜醒面30分钟。

Mix flour and salt. Slowly pour in boiling water while stirring with chopsticks. Once cool enough to handle, knead into a smooth dough. Cover with plastic wrap and rest for 30 minutes.

### 2. 擀开撒葱 | Roll & Fill
面团分成4份。取一份擀成薄长方形，刷一层薄油和香油，撒上葱花和少许盐。

Divide dough into 4 portions. Roll one portion into a thin rectangle. Brush with a thin layer of oil and sesame oil. Scatter chopped scallions and a pinch of salt.

### 3. 卷起成层 | Create Layers
从长边卷成长条，再盘成螺旋状（像蜗牛壳），按扁后擀成圆饼（约5mm厚）。

Roll up tightly from the long side into a log. Coil the log into a spiral (like a snail shell). Press flat, then roll into a round pancake (~5mm thick).

### 4. 煎饼 | Pan-fry
平底锅中火加1-2汤匙油，放入饼煎3-4分钟至底部金黄，翻面再煎3-4分钟。

Heat 1-2 tbsp oil in a skillet over medium heat. Fry the pancake for 3-4 minutes until the bottom is golden. Flip and fry another 3-4 minutes.

### 5. 切块上桌 | Serve
切成三角块，趁热吃。可蘸醋或辣酱。

Cut into wedges and serve hot. Dip in vinegar or chili sauce if desired.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用开水和面（烫面），做出来才外酥内软 | Use boiling water (hot-water dough) — this is what makes it tender inside |
| 醒面不能跳过，面团松弛后更好操作 | Don't skip resting — relaxed gluten is much easier to roll |
| 卷得越紧、层次越多 | The tighter you roll, the more layers you get |
| 煎的时候不要压饼，保持蓬松 | Don't press the pancake while frying — keep it fluffy |
