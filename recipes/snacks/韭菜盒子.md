# 韭菜盒子 | Chive Pockets
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/snacks/韭菜盒子.svg" width="500" alt="韭菜盒子"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 醒面 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 面食、全超市可买、早餐

> 韭菜盒子是中国北方最经典的家常面食，薄脆的外皮包裹着韭菜鸡蛋粉丝馅，咬一口就能闻到韭菜特有的浓郁香气。早餐、午餐、晚餐都能吃，配一碗粥就是完美的一餐。
>
> *Chive Pockets are a beloved staple of northern Chinese home cooking — thin, crispy dough enveloping a filling of garlic chives, scrambled egg, and glass noodles. The unmistakable aroma of chives hits you with the first bite. Perfect for any meal of the day, paired with a bowl of congee.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

### 面皮 | Dough
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2杯 / 2 cups (250g) |
| 开水 | Boiling water | 3/4杯 / 3/4 cup (~180ml) |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |

### 馅料 | Filling
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 韭菜 | Chinese garlic chives | 200g (1大把 / 1 large bunch) |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs (scrambled) | 3个 / 3 |
| 粉丝 | Glass noodles (mung bean) | 1小把 / 1 small bundle (~30g) |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 适量 / for frying |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 和面 | Make Dough
面粉加盐，倒入开水搅拌，稍凉后揉成光滑面团，盖上醒面20分钟。

Mix flour and salt. Pour in boiling water and stir. Once cool enough, knead into a smooth dough. Cover and rest 20 minutes.

### 2. 准备馅料 | Make Filling
粉丝用温水泡软，切短段。鸡蛋炒碎放凉。韭菜洗净沥干，切碎（切之前先拌油锁水）。将韭菜、鸡蛋碎、粉丝段加盐和香油拌匀。

Soak glass noodles in warm water until soft, then cut into short pieces. Scramble eggs and let cool. Wash and dry chives, chop them (toss with oil BEFORE adding salt to lock in moisture). Combine chives, egg, noodles, salt, and sesame oil.

### 3. 包盒子 | Assemble
面团分成8-10个小剂子。每个擀成圆形薄皮，放入馅料，对折成半圆形，边缘捏紧压花。

Divide dough into 8-10 pieces. Roll each into a thin circle. Add filling to one half, fold into a half-moon shape, and crimp the edges tightly.

### 4. 煎盒子 | Pan-fry
平底锅放少许油，中小火放入盒子，煎3-4分钟至底部金黄，翻面再煎3-4分钟至两面酥脆。

Add a little oil to a skillet over medium-low heat. Fry pockets 3-4 minutes until the bottom is golden. Flip and fry another 3-4 minutes until both sides are crispy.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 韭菜切碎后先拌油再加盐，防止出水 | Toss chopped chives with oil BEFORE adding salt — this prevents water leakage |
| 用开水和面（烫面），皮才薄而不破 | Boiling water dough makes the skin thin yet strong |
| 中小火慢煎，大火容易外焦内生 | Use medium-low heat — high heat burns the outside before the inside is done |
| 捏边缘时要紧实，防止煎的时候漏馅 | Seal the edges tightly to prevent filling from leaking |
