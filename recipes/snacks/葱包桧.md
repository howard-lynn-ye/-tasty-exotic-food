# 葱包桧 | Scallion Pancake Rolls

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 小吃、街头美食、需要亚洲超市

> 南宋时期，杭州百姓痛恨陷害岳飞的奸臣秦桧，有人将面团捏成秦桧形状下锅油炸以泄恨。后演变为将油条和葱裹入薄饼，在铁板上压烤，刷上甜面酱或辣酱，成为杭州最有故事的街头小吃。
>
> *During the Southern Song Dynasty, the people of Hangzhou so despised the traitorous minister Qin Hui — who engineered the execution of the hero General Yue Fei — that they fried dough effigies of him in oil. Over the centuries, this evolved into a beloved street snack: a crispy you tiao (fried dough stick) and scallions wrapped in a thin pancake, pressed on a griddle, and brushed with sweet bean or chili sauce.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 春饼皮（薄面饼） | Thin flour pancakes (spring pancake wrappers) | 4张 / 4 pieces |
| 油条 | You tiao (fried dough sticks) | 2根 / 2 pieces |
| 小葱 | Scallions | 8根 / 8 stalks |
| 甜面酱 | Sweet bean sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 辣椒酱（可选） | Chili sauce (optional) | 适量 / to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 少许 / a little |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备油条 | Prep the You Tiao
将油条切成与饼皮等长的段（约15cm）。如果用冷油条，可先在锅中小火煎至回脆。

Cut each you tiao into segments about the same length as the pancake wrappers (~15 cm). If using day-old you tiao, re-crisp them in a dry pan over low heat.

### 2. 备葱 | Prep Scallions
小葱洗净，取葱白和浅绿部分，长度与油条一致。

Wash the scallions. Trim to the same length as the you tiao, using the white and light-green parts.

### 3. 包卷 | Wrap
在饼皮上刷一层薄薄的甜面酱，放上半根油条和两根小葱，卷起来。

Brush a thin layer of sweet bean sauce on the pancake. Place half a you tiao and 2 scallion stalks on top. Roll up snugly.

### 4. 煎烤 | Griddle-Press
平底锅刷少许油，中火加热。将卷好的葱包桧放入，用铲子压扁，煎至两面金黄酥脆。

Heat a flat pan with a thin film of oil over medium heat. Place the rolls seam-side down and press flat with a spatula. Griddle until both sides are golden and crispy.

### 5. 上桌 | Serve
趁热食用，可再刷甜面酱或辣酱。

Serve piping hot. Brush on extra sweet bean sauce or chili sauce to taste.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 油条要脆，隔夜的需重新煎脆 | The you tiao must be crispy — re-fry if stale |
| 用铲子压扁是关键，使饼皮贴合油条 | Pressing flat with a spatula is key — it fuses the wrapper to the you tiao |
| 甜面酱不要涂太多，防止饼皮发软 | Don't over-sauce — too much will make the pancake soggy |
| 火候中火为宜，大火会焦而内不热 | Medium heat is ideal — high heat burns the outside before the inside heats |
