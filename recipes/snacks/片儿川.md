> 片儿川是杭州最具代表性的面食，起源于南宋，至今已有近千年历史。"片儿"指的是笋片和肉片，"川"即汆烫之意。以雪菜、笋片、肉片为浇头，汤清味鲜，是杭州人百吃不厌的市井美味。
>
> *Pian'er Chuan is Hangzhou's most iconic noodle dish, with roots stretching back nearly a thousand years to the Southern Song Dynasty. "Pian'er" refers to the thin slices of bamboo shoot and pork; "Chuan" means to blanch. Topped with pickled mustard greens, sliced bamboo shoots, and pork in a clear, savory broth — it is the everyday comfort food that Hangzhou locals never tire of.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 碱面/切面 | Alkaline noodles (or fresh wheat noodles) | 200g |
| 猪里脊肉 | Pork tenderloin | 100g |
| 冬笋（或春笋） | Bamboo shoots (winter or spring) | 80g |
| 雪菜 | Pickled mustard greens (xuecai) | 50g |
| 姜 | Ginger | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 猪油或植物油 | Lard or vegetable oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 高汤 | Stock (pork or chicken) | 500ml |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
猪肉切薄片，加料酒、酱油和淀粉抓匀腌制10分钟。笋切薄片。雪菜洗净切碎（如太咸需先浸泡）。

Slice the pork thinly. Marinate with wine, soy sauce, and cornstarch for 10 minutes. Slice the bamboo shoots thin. Rinse and chop the pickled mustard greens (soak first if too salty).

### 2. 炒浇头 | Cook the Topping
锅中热油，先炒肉片至变色，加入笋片翻炒，再加雪菜炒匀，加少许高汤，调味后盛出备用。

Heat oil in a wok. Stir-fry the pork slices until they change color. Add bamboo shoots, then pickled greens. Toss together, add a splash of stock, season, and set aside.

### 3. 煮面 | Cook the Noodles
另起锅烧水，下面条煮至八成熟（略带硬芯），捞出放入碗中。

Bring a pot of water to a boil. Cook the noodles until about 80% done (still slightly firm). Drain and place in a serving bowl.

### 4. 浇汤装碗 | Assemble
高汤烧开，调入盐，浇入面碗中。将炒好的浇头铺在面上，即可食用。

Bring the stock to a boil, season with salt, and ladle over the noodles. Top with the stir-fried topping and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 面条要选有碱水的切面，口感筋道 | Use alkaline noodles for the right chewy texture |
| 雪菜是灵魂，不可省略 | Pickled mustard greens are the soul — do not skip them |
| 笋要切薄片，与肉片厚度一致 | Slice bamboo shoots thin, matching the pork slices |
| 面条不要煮过头，保留嚼劲 | Do not overcook the noodles — keep them al dente |
| 传统用猪油，香气更浓 | Traditional recipes use lard for a richer aroma |
