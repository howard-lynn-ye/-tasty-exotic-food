# 锅贴 | Pan-Fried Dumplings (Potstickers)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/snacks/锅贴.svg" width="500" alt="锅贴"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 小吃、全超市可买、可批量冷冻

> 锅贴是中国最受欢迎的街头小吃之一。和水饺不同，锅贴不封口，两端开放，底部煎得金黄酥脆，上面却是柔软的面皮，一口咬下去"咔嚓"一声，肉汁四溢。自己包可以冷冻保存，随吃随煎。
>
> *Potstickers are one of China's most beloved street snacks. Unlike boiled dumplings, potstickers are left open at both ends, pan-fried until the bottoms turn golden and crispy while the tops stay soft. One bite delivers a satisfying crunch followed by a burst of juicy filling. Make a big batch and freeze for anytime cooking.*

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌馅 | Make Filling
白菜切碎，撒盐腌5分钟，挤干水分。将猪肉末、白菜碎、姜末、葱花、酱油、料酒、香油和白胡椒粉混合，朝一个方向搅拌至起黏性。

Finely chop napa cabbage, salt it, let sit 5 minutes, then squeeze out water. Mix ground pork, cabbage, minced ginger, chopped scallion, soy sauce, wine, sesame oil, and white pepper. Stir vigorously in one direction until the mixture becomes sticky.

### 2. 包锅贴 | Wrap Potstickers
取一张饺子皮，放入约1汤匙馅料。对折捏紧中间，两端留开口不封。

Place ~1 tbsp filling on a wrapper. Fold in half and pinch the center shut, leaving both ends open.

### 3. 煎锅贴 | Pan-fry
平底锅放1汤匙油，中火烧热。将锅贴整齐排列在锅中，煎2分钟至底部金黄。

Heat 1 tbsp oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat. Arrange potstickers in neat rows and fry for 2 minutes until the bottoms are golden.

### 4. 加水焖熟 | Steam-fry
沿锅边加入约80ml水（或面粉水做冰花），立刻盖盖。中火焖至水分完全蒸发（约5-6分钟），底部会变得更酥脆。

Pour ~80ml water along the edge of the pan (or flour-water for a lace skirt). Cover immediately. Cook over medium heat until all water evaporates (~5-6 min). The bottoms will crisp up.

### 5. 出锅 | Serve
用盘子倒扣在锅上，翻转出锅。配醋+辣椒油蘸食。

Place a plate upside-down over the pan. Flip to unmold. Serve with a dipping sauce of vinegar + chili oil.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 白菜一定要挤干水，否则馅料太湿 | Squeeze cabbage VERY dry — wet filling makes soggy potstickers |
| 用不粘锅最保险，避免粘锅悲剧 | Use a non-stick pan — it's the safest bet against sticking |
| 加面粉水（10g面粉+80ml水）可以做出冰花锅贴 | Flour-water (10g flour + 80ml water) creates beautiful lace wings |
| 多包一些冷冻保存，不用解冻直接煎 | Make extra and freeze — cook straight from frozen (add 2 min) |
