# 糖醋排骨 | Sweet & Sour Spare Ribs

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/home-style/tangcu-paigu.jpg" width="500" alt="糖醋排骨">
</p>

> 糖醋排骨是杭州家庭餐桌上的经典之作。不同于其他地方的浓油赤酱，杭式糖醋排骨讲究酸甜适中、色泽红亮、外焦里嫩，用镇江香醋调味更是点睛之笔，酸中带甜、甜而不腻。
>
> *Sweet and sour spare ribs are a staple of the Hangzhou home kitchen. Unlike heavier regional versions, the Hangzhou style strikes a refined balance — a glistening crimson glaze that is tangy yet sweet, crisp on the outside yet tender within. The secret is Chinkiang vinegar, which adds a mellow acidity that elevates the dish above its imitators.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 小排 | Pork spare ribs (cut into segments) | 500g |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 4汤匙 / 4 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 适量 / as needed |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds (garnish) | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 焯水 | Blanch
排骨冷水下锅，焯水5分钟去血沫，捞出洗净沥干。

Place ribs in cold water. Bring to a boil and blanch for 5 minutes to remove impurities. Rinse and drain.

### 2. 煎排骨 | Pan-fry
锅中放油，中火将排骨煎至表面微焦，两面金黄。

Add oil to a pan over medium heat. Pan-fry the ribs until golden and lightly charred on both sides.

### 3. 调味炖煮 | Braise with Sauce
加入姜片、葱段，倒入料酒、酱油，加适量水没过排骨。大火烧开后转小火，加盖炖煮30分钟。

Add ginger and scallion. Pour in Shaoxing wine, soy sauce, and enough water to cover the ribs. Bring to a boil, reduce to low heat, cover, and braise for 30 minutes.

### 4. 加糖醋收汁 | Add Sugar & Vinegar, Reduce
加入白糖搅匀，转中火收汁。待汤汁将干时加入香醋，大火翻炒至酱汁浓稠，均匀裹在排骨上。

Stir in the sugar. Raise to medium heat and reduce. When the liquid is nearly gone, add the Chinkiang vinegar. Toss over high heat until the sauce is thick and glossy, coating every rib.

### 5. 装盘 | Plate
盛出撒上白芝麻即可。

Transfer to a plate and garnish with white sesame seeds.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 排骨要选肉多的小排，让卖肉的帮你剁成小段 | Choose meaty ribs — ask the butcher to cut them into 2-inch segments |
| 醋要最后放，早放酸味会挥发 | Add vinegar at the very end — adding it early lets the acidity evaporate |
| 糖醋比例约4:3，可根据口味调整 | Sugar-to-vinegar ratio is roughly 4:3 — adjust to your palate |
| 收汁时要不停翻炒，防止粘锅焦糊 | Keep tossing while reducing — don't let the glaze burn |
