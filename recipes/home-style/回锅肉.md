# 回锅肉 | Twice-Cooked Pork (Hui Guo Rou)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/home-style/回锅肉.svg" width="500" alt="回锅肉"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 川菜、家常、下饭

> 回锅肉是四川人心中的第一家常菜，"回锅"意思是把煮熟的肉再次下锅爆炒。五花肉先煮后切薄片，再与豆瓣酱和蒜苗一起爆炒，肉片微微卷曲呈"灯盏窝"状，肥而不腻、香辣过瘾。
>
> *Twice-Cooked Pork is the number-one home-style dish in Sichuan. "Hui Guo" means returning to the wok — pork belly is first boiled, then sliced thin and stir-fried with doubanjiang and garlic shoots. The slices curl into little cups ("lamp bowls"), rendering out excess fat and soaking up the spicy-savory sauce. Rich but never greasy.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 五花肉 | Pork belly (skin-on) | 400g |
| 蒜苗 (或青蒜) | Garlic shoots (or leeks) | 3-4根 / 3-4 stalks |
| 青椒 | Green bell pepper | 1个 / 1 |
| 郫县豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang (Pixian chili bean paste) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 甜面酱 | Sweet bean paste (tianmianjiang) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 少许 / a pinch |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮肉 | Boil Pork
五花肉整块冷水下锅，加姜片、葱段、料酒，大火烧开后转中火煮15-20分钟至筷子能插入。捞出放凉。

Place whole pork belly in cold water with ginger, scallion, and wine. Bring to a boil, reduce to medium, and cook 15-20 minutes until a chopstick pierces through. Remove and let cool.

### 2. 切片备料 | Slice & Prep
肉切大薄片（约3mm厚）。蒜苗斜切段，青椒切片。豆瓣酱剁细。

Slice pork into large, thin pieces (~3mm thick). Cut garlic shoots diagonally. Slice green pepper. Mince the doubanjiang.

### 3. 煸肉 | Render Pork
锅中放少许油，中火下肉片煸炒至边缘微卷、出油、呈半透明状（"灯盏窝"）。

Add a little oil to the wok. Stir-fry pork slices over medium heat until the edges curl up, fat renders out, and the slices become slightly translucent ("lamp bowl" shape).

### 4. 下酱炒香 | Add Paste
将肉推到一边，下豆瓣酱炒出红油，再加甜面酱和少许糖，炒匀。

Push pork to one side. Add doubanjiang and stir-fry until the oil turns red. Add sweet bean paste and sugar, mix well.

### 5. 加蔬菜出锅 | Add Vegetables & Serve
下蒜苗和青椒，大火快炒1分钟至断生，翻匀即可。

Add garlic shoots and green pepper. Toss over high heat for 1 minute until just cooked. Mix everything together and serve immediately.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 五花肉煮好后放冰箱冷藏30分钟，更容易切薄片 | Chill boiled pork 30 min in the fridge for easier slicing |
| 煸肉时不要急，等肉片卷起来才是到位 | Be patient when rendering — wait for the slices to curl |
| 豆瓣酱已经很咸，不需要额外加盐 | Doubanjiang is very salty — no extra salt needed |
| 蒜苗是灵魂配菜，不要省略 | Garlic shoots are essential — don't skip them |
