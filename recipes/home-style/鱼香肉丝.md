# 鱼香肉丝 | Yu Xiang Shredded Pork
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/home-style/鱼香肉丝.svg" width="500" alt="鱼香肉丝"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 川菜、家常、下饭神器

> 鱼香肉丝是川菜中的经典名菜，名字里有"鱼"，实际跟鱼没有任何关系——"鱼香"是一种以泡椒、姜蒜、糖醋调配出的复合味型。酸甜咸辣鲜五味俱全，配白米饭能吃三碗。
>
> *Yu Xiang Shredded Pork is a Sichuan classic. Despite "fish" in the name, there's no fish at all — "Yu Xiang" refers to a complex flavor profile built from pickled chilies, ginger, garlic, sugar, and vinegar. It hits every taste bud: sour, sweet, salty, spicy, and savory. Served over rice, it's dangerously addictive.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪里脊 | Pork tenderloin | 250g |
| 木耳 | Wood ear mushrooms (dried) | 一小把 / a small handful |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot | 1根 / 1 |
| 青椒 | Green bell pepper | 1个 / 1 |
| 泡椒 (或豆瓣酱) | Pickled chilies (or doubanjiang) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 1小块 / 1 small piece |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
猪肉切细丝，加料酒、酱油和1茶匙淀粉抓匀腌10分钟。木耳温水泡发切丝。胡萝卜、青椒切丝。姜蒜切末，葱切葱花。

Shred pork thinly. Marinate with wine, soy sauce, and 1 tsp cornstarch for 10 minutes. Soak wood ear mushrooms in warm water, then shred. Julienne carrot and green pepper. Mince ginger and garlic, chop scallions.

### 2. 调鱼香汁 | Mix the Sauce
碗中混合：醋、糖、酱油（1茶匙）、1茶匙淀粉和2汤匙水，搅匀备用。

In a bowl, combine: vinegar, sugar, soy sauce (1 tsp), 1 tsp cornstarch, and 2 tbsp water. Stir well and set aside.

### 3. 炒肉丝 | Stir-fry Pork
大火热油，下肉丝快炒至变色，盛出。

Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Stir-fry pork shreds until just cooked through. Remove and set aside.

### 4. 炒蔬菜和酱 | Cook Vegetables & Sauce
锅中留油，放入泡椒（或豆瓣酱）、姜蒜末炒出红油和香味。下木耳、胡萝卜丝、青椒丝翻炒1分钟。

In the remaining oil, add pickled chilies (or doubanjiang) with minced ginger and garlic. Stir-fry until the oil turns red and fragrant. Add wood ear, carrot, and green pepper. Toss for 1 minute.

### 5. 合炒收汁 | Combine & Finish
倒回肉丝，倒入鱼香汁，大火翻炒至酱汁浓稠裹住所有食材。撒葱花出锅。

Return the pork. Pour in the sauce mixture. Toss over high heat until the sauce thickens and coats everything. Garnish with scallions and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 肉丝要切均匀，粗细一致才好看好吃 | Cut pork shreds evenly — consistent size means even cooking |
| 鱼香汁的关键是糖醋平衡，可以尝一下再调 | The sauce's magic is sugar-vinegar balance — taste and adjust |
| 大火快炒，蔬菜要保持脆感 | High heat, fast tossing — vegetables should stay crisp |
| 没有泡椒可以用豆瓣酱+一点辣椒粉代替 | No pickled chilies? Use doubanjiang + a pinch of chili flakes |
