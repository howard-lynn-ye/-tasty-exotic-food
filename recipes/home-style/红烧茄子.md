# 红烧茄子 | Braised Eggplant in Soy Sauce

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 家常、素菜、全超市可买、新手友好

> 红烧茄子是中国家庭最常见的下饭菜之一，茄子吸满了酱汁，咸甜交织，软糯入味。比餐厅版本少用很多油，但一样好吃。
>
> *Braised eggplant is one of China's most beloved home-cooked dishes. The eggplant soaks up every drop of the savory-sweet sauce, turning silky and deeply flavorful. This home version uses far less oil than restaurant renditions but tastes just as good.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 茄子 | Chinese eggplant (or Japanese eggplant) | 2根 / 2 |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp (mixed with 2 tbsp water) |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切茄子 | Cut Eggplant
茄子切滚刀块（约一口大小），撒少许盐拌匀，静置5分钟后挤去水分。

Cut eggplant into rolling-knife chunks (bite-sized). Sprinkle with a little salt, toss, and let sit 5 minutes. Squeeze out excess moisture.

### 2. 煎茄子 | Pan-fry Eggplant
锅中放油，中大火将茄子煎至两面金黄微软，盛出备用。

Heat oil in a wok or skillet over medium-high heat. Pan-fry eggplant until golden and slightly softened on both sides. Remove and set aside.

### 3. 炒香料 | Aromatics
锅中留底油，放入蒜末、姜片和葱白段，炒出香味。

In the remaining oil, add minced garlic, ginger slices, and scallion whites. Stir-fry until fragrant.

### 4. 红烧 | Braise
放入茄子，加酱油、老抽、白糖和少许水（约100ml），翻匀后盖盖中小火焖煮5分钟。

Return the eggplant. Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and a splash of water (~100ml). Toss to coat, cover, and braise on medium-low heat for 5 minutes.

### 5. 收汁装盘 | Reduce & Plate
淋入醋，倒入水淀粉勾芡，大火翻炒至酱汁浓稠裹住茄子。撒葱花装盘。

Drizzle in vinegar. Pour in the cornstarch slurry and toss over high heat until the sauce is thick and glossy. Garnish with scallion greens and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 茄子切好后撒盐腌一下，可以减少吸油 | Salting eggplant draws out moisture and reduces oil absorption |
| 用中国茄子或日本茄子，皮薄肉嫩 | Use Chinese or Japanese eggplant — thinner skin, more tender flesh |
| 不要煮太久，茄子容易烂成泥 | Don't overcook — eggplant can turn to mush quickly |
| 老抽只需一点点，用来上色 | Dark soy sauce is just for color — a tiny amount goes a long way |

| 原料 | Ingredient | 替代 / Substitute | 备注 / Notes |
