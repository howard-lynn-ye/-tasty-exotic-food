# 油焖春笋 | Braised Spring Bamboo Shoots

> 杭州人说"不时不食"，春笋是春天最让人期盼的时令食材。油焖春笋做法简单却鲜美至极，以重油、重糖的方式将春笋的清甜激发到极致，是杭州家家户户春天必做的一道菜。
>
> *Hangzhou locals live by the principle "eat only what is in season." Spring bamboo shoots are the most anticipated ingredient of the year. This deceptively simple dish uses generous oil and sugar to coax out the shoots' natural sweetness — a home-cooking staple that every Hangzhou family makes when spring arrives.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 春笋 | Spring bamboo shoots | 500g |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 少许 / a drizzle |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 处理春笋 | Prep the Shoots
春笋剥壳，切去老根，纵向切成两半，再切成滚刀块或条状。焯水3分钟去涩，捞出沥干。

Peel the bamboo shoots and trim the tough base. Halve lengthwise, then cut into chunks or wedges. Blanch in boiling water for 3 minutes to remove bitterness. Drain well.

### 2. 煸炒 | Sear
锅中倒入较多植物油，大火烧热，放入笋块翻炒2-3分钟，至边缘微焦。

Heat a generous amount of oil in a wok over high heat. Add the bamboo shoots and stir-fry for 2–3 minutes until the edges begin to char slightly.

### 3. 调味焖煮 | Braise
加入酱油、老抽、白糖和料酒，翻炒均匀。加少量水（约100ml），转中小火，加盖焖煮10-15分钟。

Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, sugar, and Shaoxing wine. Toss to coat. Add a small amount of water (~100 ml), reduce to medium-low heat, and cover. Braise for 10–15 minutes.

### 4. 收汁 | Reduce
揭盖转大火收汁，使酱汁浓稠裹在笋上。淋香油，翻匀出锅。

Uncover, raise the heat, and reduce the sauce until it clings to the shoots. Drizzle with sesame oil, toss, and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 春笋最佳赏味期为3-4月 | Peak season for spring bamboo shoots is March–April |
| 焯水是关键，去除草酸涩味 | Blanching is essential to remove oxalic acid and bitterness |
| 油要多，"油焖"重在油 | Be generous with oil — the "oil-braised" name says it all |
| 糖不可少，提鲜增色 | Sugar is essential — it enhances both flavor and color |
