# 水煮肉片 | Sichuan Boiled Pork in Chili Oil
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/home-style/水煮肉片.svg" width="500" alt="水煮肉片"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 川菜、重口味、下饭

> 水煮肉片是川菜的灵魂之作，别被"水煮"这个温柔的名字骗了——它其实是一道麻辣滚烫的硬菜。嫩滑的肉片铺在蔬菜上，浇上红亮的辣椒油，再用热油"泼"出香味，端上桌就是无敌的存在。
>
> *Don't let the gentle name "water-boiled" fool you — this is one of Sichuan's most fiery dishes. Velveted pork slices rest on a bed of greens, drenched in blazing chili oil, then finished with a sizzling pour of hot oil over dried chilies and Sichuan peppercorns. The aroma alone is intoxicating.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪里脊 (或牛肉) | Pork loin or beef sirloin | 300g |
| 生菜或娃娃菜 | Lettuce or baby napa cabbage | 200g |
| 豆芽 | Bean sprouts | 100g |
| 郫县豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chilies | 10-15个 / 10-15 |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 4片 / 4 slices |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 蛋清 | Egg white | 1个 / 1 |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 适量 / as needed (~5 tbsp total) |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 葱花 | Scallion (garnish) | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌肉 | Marinate
肉切大薄片（约2mm），加料酒、酱油、蛋清和淀粉抓匀，腌制10分钟。

Slice meat into large, thin pieces (~2mm). Marinate with wine, soy sauce, egg white, and cornstarch for 10 minutes.

### 2. 焯蔬菜 | Blanch Vegetables
锅中烧水，将生菜和豆芽焯水30秒，捞出铺在大碗底部。

Bring water to a boil. Blanch lettuce and bean sprouts for 30 seconds. Drain and spread in the bottom of a large serving bowl.

### 3. 炒底料 | Cook the Base
锅中热2汤匙油，小火炒豆瓣酱至出红油，加姜蒜末炒香。加入约500ml水烧开，调入少许盐。

Heat 2 tbsp oil. Stir-fry doubanjiang on low heat until the oil turns red. Add minced ginger and garlic, cook until fragrant. Add ~500ml water, bring to a boil, season with salt.

### 4. 煮肉片 | Poach Meat
将腌好的肉片一片片滑入锅中，轻轻拨散，煮约1-2分钟至刚熟。将肉片和汤汁一起倒在蔬菜上。

Slide the marinated meat slices into the broth one by one, gently separating them. Cook 1-2 minutes until just done. Pour the meat and broth over the vegetables.

### 5. 泼油 | Sizzling Oil Finish
在肉片表面铺上干辣椒段、花椒和蒜末。另起锅烧3汤匙油至冒烟，直接浇在辣椒和花椒上——"滋啦"一声，香气四溢。撒葱花即可。

Top the meat with dried chili segments, Sichuan peppercorns, and minced garlic. Heat 3 tbsp oil in a small pan until smoking hot. Pour directly over the chilies and peppercorns — it should sizzle dramatically. Garnish with scallions.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 肉片要切薄，腌制上浆后口感才滑嫩 | Slice thin and velvet properly — this is what makes the meat silky |
| 肉片不要煮过头，变色即熟 | Don't overcook the meat — it's done as soon as it changes color |
| 最后泼油是灵魂步骤，油一定要烧到冒烟 | The final oil pour is crucial — oil MUST be smoking hot |
| 怕辣可以减少干辣椒数量，但不能全去掉 | If heat-sensitive, reduce dried chilies, but don't skip them entirely |
