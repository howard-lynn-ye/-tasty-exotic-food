# 杭州小炒 | Hangzhou Stir-fry

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/home-style/hangzhou-xiaochao.jpg" width="500" alt="杭州小炒">
</p>

> 杭州小炒是杭帮菜中最日常的家常菜之一，看似简单，却最考验镬气和火候。以猪里脊、青椒、冬笋为主料，大火快炒，调味清淡爽利，是杭州本地饭馆的必点菜，也是家庭厨房的快手好菜。
>
> *Hangzhou Stir-fry is the quintessential everyday dish of Hangzhou home cooking. Simple in appearance, it demands precise wok skill and fierce heat. Pork tenderloin, green peppers, and bamboo shoots are flash-fried to preserve their crunch and natural sweetness — a staple on every local restaurant menu and a quick weeknight go-to in Hangzhou kitchens.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪里脊肉 | Pork tenderloin | 200g |
| 青椒 | Green bell pepper | 2个 / 2 pieces |
| 冬笋 | Winter bamboo shoots | 100g |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot | 半根 / half |
| 姜 | Ginger | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣 / 2 cloves |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 / a pinch |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切配 | Prep
猪肉切薄片，加料酒、少许酱油和淀粉腌10分钟。青椒去籽切片，笋切片，胡萝卜切片。

Slice the pork thinly. Marinate with wine, a splash of soy sauce, and cornstarch for 10 minutes. Seed and slice the green peppers. Slice the bamboo shoots and carrot.

### 2. 滑肉 | Flash-fry the Pork
锅中油烧至冒烟，将腌好的肉片快速滑散至变色，盛出备用。

Heat oil in a wok until smoking. Flash-fry the pork slices, stirring quickly until just cooked. Remove and set aside.

### 3. 炒蔬菜 | Stir-fry Vegetables
锅中留底油，爆香姜蒜，先下笋片和胡萝卜翻炒1分钟，再下青椒大火翻炒30秒。

In the remaining oil, sear ginger and garlic until fragrant. Add bamboo shoots and carrot, stir-fry 1 minute. Add green peppers and toss 30 seconds over high heat.

### 4. 合炒 | Combine
倒回肉片，加酱油、盐和白胡椒粉，大火翻匀即可出锅。

Return the pork to the wok. Season with soy sauce, salt, and white pepper. Toss everything together over high heat and serve immediately.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 大火快炒是关键，全程不超过5分钟 | High heat, fast hands — the entire stir-fry takes under 5 minutes |
| 肉片要切薄，上浆保嫩 | Slice the pork thin and velvet it to keep it tender |
| 青椒要最后放，保留脆感 | Add green peppers last to preserve their crunch |
| 不要加水，保持镬气 | Do not add water — keep the wok dry for maximum "wok hei" |
