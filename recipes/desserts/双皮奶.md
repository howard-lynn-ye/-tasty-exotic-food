# 双皮奶 | Double-Skin Milk Pudding (Cantonese)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 + 冷藏 2小时 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 粤式甜品、全超市可买

> 双皮奶是广东顺德的经典甜品，因表面有两层奶皮而得名。口感滑嫩如丝绸，奶香浓郁，冷热皆宜。制作过程看似神奇——把牛奶煮沸放凉形成奶皮，再冲入蛋白液蒸制——但其实只需要三种食材。
>
> *Double-Skin Milk Pudding is a legendary Cantonese dessert from Shunde, named for the two delicate "skins" of cream that form on top. Its texture is silky-smooth, rich with pure milk flavor, wonderful hot or cold. The process seems magical — boil milk, cool to form a skin, pour in egg whites, and steam — but it only requires three ingredients.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 全脂牛奶 | Whole milk | 500ml (2 cups) |
| 蛋清 | Egg whites | 3个 / 3 |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮奶形成第一层奶皮 | Boil Milk to Form First Skin
牛奶倒入锅中，中火加热至快要沸腾（边缘冒小泡），倒入碗中，静置不动，放凉至室温。表面会形成一层奶皮。

Pour milk into a saucepan. Heat over medium heat until nearly boiling (small bubbles at the edge). Pour into a bowl and let it sit undisturbed until it cools to room temperature. A cream skin will form on top.

### 2. 混合蛋白 | Mix Egg Whites
蛋清加糖搅拌均匀（不要打发，只需混合均匀）。

Beat egg whites with sugar until well combined (do NOT whip to a foam — just stir evenly).

### 3. 奶皮下的魔法 | The Magic Under the Skin
用小刀在奶皮边缘划一个小口，缓缓将碗中的牛奶倒出（保留奶皮留在碗底）。将牛奶与蛋白糖液混合，过滤，再从小口缓缓倒回碗中，让奶皮浮在最上面。

Use a knife to cut a small opening at the edge of the milk skin. Slowly pour the milk out from underneath (keeping the skin in the bowl). Mix the milk with the egg white mixture, strain, then gently pour it back into the bowl through the opening, letting the skin float back to the top.

### 4. 蒸制 | Steam
碗盖上保鲜膜（防止水蒸气滴入），放入蒸锅中火蒸15分钟。蒸好后自然放凉。

Cover the bowl with plastic wrap (to prevent condensation from dripping in). Steam over medium heat for 15 minutes. Let cool naturally.

### 5. 冷藏食用 | Chill & Serve
放入冰箱冷藏至少2小时。冷食口感最佳，表面有两层奶皮，内部嫩滑如豆腐。

Refrigerate for at least 2 hours. Best served cold — two cream skins on top, silky pudding beneath, smooth as tofu.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 必须用全脂牛奶，脂肪含量越高越容易结奶皮 | Must use whole milk — higher fat = better skin formation |
| 蛋清不要打发，搅匀即可，否则蒸出来有气泡 | Don't whip egg whites — just stir; whipping creates air bubbles |
| 一定要过滤蛋奶液，口感才丝滑 | Always strain the mixture — this is what makes it silky |
| 蒸的时候不要大火，中火慢蒸，表面才光滑 | Use medium heat while steaming — high heat makes a rough surface |
