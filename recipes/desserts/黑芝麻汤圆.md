# 黑芝麻汤圆 | Black Sesame Tangyuan (Rice Balls)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 甜品、节日、需要亚洲超市

> 汤圆是中国元宵节和冬至的传统食物，象征团圆美满。黑芝麻馅是最经典的口味——咬开糯米外皮，流沙般的黑芝麻馅涌出，浓郁的芝麻香和甜蜜交织在一起，每一口都是幸福。
>
> *Tangyuan are traditional Chinese rice balls eaten during the Lantern Festival and Winter Solstice, symbolizing family reunion. Black sesame is the most classic filling — bite through the chewy glutinous rice skin and a lava-like stream of rich, nutty sesame paste flows out. Pure comfort in every bite.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

### 馅料 | Filling
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 黑芝麻 | Black sesame seeds | 100g |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 40g |
| 猪油或黄油 | Lard or butter | 40g |

### 外皮 | Dough
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 糯米粉 | Glutinous rice flour | 200g |
| 温水 | Warm water | 约160ml / ~160ml |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做馅 | Make Filling
黑芝麻小火干炒至出香（约5分钟），放凉后用料理机打成粉。加入糖和软化的猪油（或黄油），混合均匀，冰箱冷藏至硬，搓成小球（约10g/个）。

Dry-toast sesame seeds over low heat until fragrant (~5 min). Cool, then grind to a powder in a food processor. Mix with sugar and softened lard (or butter). Refrigerate until firm, then roll into small balls (~10g each).

### 2. 和面 | Make Dough
糯米粉中慢慢加入温水，揉成光滑不粘手的面团（像耳垂一样软）。

Gradually add warm water to glutinous rice flour. Knead into a smooth, non-sticky dough (soft as an earlobe).

### 3. 包汤圆 | Wrap
面团分成约20g的小剂子，按扁成圆片，包入一个芝麻馅球，收口搓圆。

Divide dough into ~20g portions. Flatten each into a disc, place a sesame ball in the center, seal, and roll smooth.

### 4. 煮汤圆 | Cook
大锅烧开水，轻轻放入汤圆。煮至全部浮起后再煮2分钟即可。可配原汤或桂花糖水食用。

Bring a large pot of water to a boil. Gently add tangyuan. Cook until they all float, then continue for 2 more minutes. Serve in the cooking water or with osmanthus sugar syrup.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 馅料一定要冷藏变硬，否则包的时候会漏 | Filling MUST be chilled firm — otherwise it leaks while wrapping |
| 面团不能太干也不能太湿，慢慢加水 | Dough can't be too dry or too wet — add water gradually |
| 煮的时候水要宽，火不要太大，防止煮破 | Use plenty of water and gentle heat to prevent bursting |
| 用猪油做馅更传统，黄油味道也很好 | Lard is traditional; butter tastes great too |
