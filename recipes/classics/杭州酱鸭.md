# 杭州酱鸭 | Hangzhou Soy-Braised Duck

> 杭州酱鸭是浙江传统名菜，历史可追溯至南宋。选用当年仔鸭，以酱油、黄酒、冰糖等慢火酱制，晾凉后切块食用，色泽红亮，肉质紧实，咸鲜回甘，是杭州人逢年过节的必备冷菜。
>
> *Hangzhou soy-braised duck is a time-honored Zhejiang classic dating to the Southern Song Dynasty. A young duck is slow-simmered in a rich bath of soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and rock sugar, then cooled and sliced. Glossy, mahogany-red, with firm yet tender meat and a lingering savory-sweet finish — it is the quintessential cold dish on every Hangzhou holiday table.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 仔鸭 | Young duck (whole) | 1只 (~1.5kg) |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 100ml |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 绍兴黄酒 | Shaoxing yellow wine | 150ml |
| 冰糖 | Rock sugar | 40g |
| 姜 | Ginger | 5片 / 5 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 3根 / 3 stalks |
| 八角 | Star anise | 2颗 / 2 pieces |
| 桂皮 | Cinnamon stick | 1小段 / 1 small piece |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 处理鸭子 | Prep the Duck
鸭子洗净，去除内脏和多余脂肪，用厨房纸擦干。内外抹上盐，腌制1小时。

Clean the duck, remove giblets and excess fat, and pat dry. Rub salt inside and out, then let it cure for 1 hour.

### 2. 焯水 | Blanch
整鸭放入冷水锅中，大火烧开后焯水5分钟，捞出冲洗干净浮沫。

Place the duck in a pot of cold water. Bring to a boil and blanch for 5 minutes. Remove and rinse off any scum.

### 3. 酱制 | Braise
锅中放入姜片、葱段、八角、桂皮，将鸭子放入。倒入酱油、老抽、黄酒，加冰糖和适量水（液面没过鸭身的三分之二）。大火烧开后转小火，加盖慢炖1.5小时，期间翻面两次。

Place ginger, scallion, star anise, and cinnamon in the pot. Add the duck. Pour in soy sauce, dark soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, rock sugar, and enough water to cover two-thirds of the duck. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat. Cover and braise for 1.5 hours, flipping twice.

### 4. 收汁 | Reduce the Sauce
转中火，揭盖收汁，不断用汤汁浇淋鸭身，至汤汁浓稠挂在鸭皮上。

Raise the heat to medium and uncover. Baste the duck continuously with the braising liquid until the sauce thickens and coats the skin.

### 5. 晾凉切块 | Cool & Slice
取出鸭子，自然晾凉至室温，斩块装盘。可将剩余酱汁浇在上面。

Remove the duck and let it cool to room temperature. Chop into pieces and arrange on a platter. Drizzle with remaining sauce.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 酱鸭是冷菜，晾凉后食用风味最佳 | This is a cold dish — flavor is best at room temperature |
| 翻面确保上色均匀 | Flip the duck to ensure even color on all sides |
| 收汁时要耐心，酱色浓郁是关键 | Be patient when reducing — a rich, glossy glaze is the hallmark |
| 冷藏可保存3-4天 | Keeps 3–4 days refrigerated |
| 传统做法会悬挂风干半日 | Traditionally the duck is hung to air-dry for half a day before slicing |
