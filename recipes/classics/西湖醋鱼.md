# 西湖醋鱼 | West Lake Fish in Vinegar Gravy

  <img src="../../images/classics/xihu-cuyu.jpg" width="500" alt="西湖醋鱼">

> 杭州十大名菜之首，相传始于南宋，以西湖草鱼为主料，活杀现烹，浇以糖醋芡汁，色泽红亮，酸甜适口。
>
> *The crown jewel of Hangzhou's top ten dishes. Originating in the Southern Song Dynasty, this dish features fresh grass carp from West Lake, poached and draped in a glistening sweet-and-sour glaze — vibrant in color, perfectly balanced in flavor.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 草鱼 | Grass carp | 1条 / 1 whole (~750g) |
| 姜 | Ginger | 30g |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 绍兴料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 4汤匙 / 4 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 处理鱼 | Prepare the Fish
草鱼去鳞去内脏，从背部剖开成两片，在鱼身上斜切数刀（不切断）。

Scale and gut the carp. Split it open from the back into two halves. Score diagonal cuts on each side (do not cut through).

### 2. 煮鱼 | Poach the Fish
锅中加水烧开，加入姜片、葱段、料酒，将鱼片放入，大火煮3分钟后转小火，煮至鱼眼凸出、鱼肉熟透，捞出装盘。

Bring a pot of water to a boil. Add ginger slices, scallion segments, and Shaoxing wine. Slide the fish in, boil for 3 minutes, then reduce to low heat. Cook until the eyes bulge and flesh is opaque. Transfer to a serving plate.

### 3. 调芡汁 | Make the Glaze
取少许鱼汤，加入白糖、香醋、酱油调匀，烧开后用水淀粉勾芡。

Ladle some poaching liquid into a saucepan. Stir in sugar, vinegar, and soy sauce. Bring to a boil, then thicken with a cornstarch slurry.

### 4. 浇汁装盘 | Plate & Serve
将糖醋芡汁均匀浇在鱼身上，撒上姜末即可。

Pour the sweet-and-sour glaze evenly over the fish. Garnish with minced ginger and serve immediately.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鱼必须鲜活，现杀现烹 | The fish must be live — kill and cook on the spot |
| 煮鱼火候很关键，过老则肉柴，过嫩则不熟 | Timing is critical: overcook and it turns tough; undercook and it's raw |
| 芡汁要亮而不厚，酸甜比例约 4:3 | The glaze should be glossy but thin; vinegar-to-sugar ratio ≈ 4:3 |
