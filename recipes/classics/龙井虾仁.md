# 龙井虾仁 | Stir-fried Shrimp with Longjing Tea

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 5分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 经典、快炒、需要亚洲超市

> 杭州名菜，将西湖龙井茶与鲜河虾仁同烹，茶香清雅，虾仁玉白鲜嫩，色泽素雅，口感清爽。
>
> *A signature Hangzhou creation that pairs premium Longjing (Dragon Well) tea leaves with delicate river shrimp. The tea lends a subtle, elegant fragrance; the shrimp remain jade-white and silky. A study in understated refinement.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鲜河虾仁 | Fresh river shrimp (peeled) | 250g |
| 龙井茶叶 | Longjing tea leaves | 5g |
| 鸡蛋清 | Egg white | 1个 / 1 |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 适量 / as needed |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌虾仁 | Marinate the Shrimp
虾仁洗净沥干，加盐、蛋清、淀粉抓匀，腌制15分钟。

Rinse and drain the shrimp. Toss with salt, egg white, and cornstarch. Marinate for 15 minutes.

### 2. 泡茶 | Brew the Tea
龙井茶叶用80°C热水泡开，茶叶和茶汤分别留用。

Steep the Longjing leaves in 80 °C water. Reserve the brewed leaves and tea liquor separately.

### 3. 滑油 | Oil-Blanch
锅中倒油，烧至四成热（约120°C），放入虾仁滑散至变色，迅速捞出沥油。

Heat oil to ~120 °C (medium-low). Slide in the shrimp and stir gently until they just turn opaque. Remove and drain immediately.

### 4. 炒制 | Stir-fry
锅留少许底油，倒入虾仁，加料酒、少许茶汤翻炒。放入泡开的茶叶，快速翻炒均匀，出锅装盘。

Leave a thin film of oil in the wok. Return the shrimp, splash in the wine and a little tea liquor, and toss. Add the steeped tea leaves, stir-fry briefly until fragrant, and plate.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 必须用明前龙井，茶香最佳 | Use pre-Qingming Longjing for the finest aroma |
| 虾仁要鲜活河虾手剥，口感完全不同 | Hand-peel live river shrimp — the texture is incomparable |
| 油温不可过高，虾仁要滑嫩 | Keep oil temperature low; the shrimp must stay silky |
| 全程快炒，动作要快，保持虾仁脆嫩 | Work fast — speed preserves the shrimp's tender bite |
