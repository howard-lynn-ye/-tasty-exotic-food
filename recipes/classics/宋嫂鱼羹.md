# 宋嫂鱼羹 | Sister Song's Fish Soup

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/classics/songsao-yugeng.png" width="500" alt="宋嫂鱼羹">
</p>

> 源于南宋时期西湖边宋五嫂所制鱼羹，宋高宗赵构品尝后赞不绝口，赐金褒奖。此羹以鳜鱼（或鲈鱼）为主料，配以火腿丝、笋丝、香菇丝，勾薄芡，打蛋花，酸辣适口，鲜美无比。
>
> *This storied soup dates to the Southern Song Dynasty, when a fisherwoman known as Sister Song served her fish chowder to Emperor Gaozong by the shores of West Lake. Enchanted, he rewarded her with gold. The silky broth is studded with shredded fish, Jinhua ham, bamboo shoots, and mushrooms — finished with wisps of egg and a splash of vinegar.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鳜鱼（或鲈鱼） | Mandarin fish (or sea bass) | 1条 (~500g) |
| 金华火腿 | Jinhua ham | 30g |
| 冬笋 | Winter bamboo shoots | 50g |
| 香菇 | Shiitake mushrooms | 3朵 / 3 pieces |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 / 1 |
| 姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 绍兴黄酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 适量 / to taste |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 少许 / a drizzle |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮鱼拆骨 | Poach & Flake the Fish
鱼洗净，加姜片和黄酒，入开水中煮至刚熟（约8分钟）。捞出放凉，拆去鱼骨，将鱼肉撕成细丝。

Clean the fish, add ginger slices and Shaoxing wine, and poach in boiling water until just cooked (~8 minutes). Let cool, debone, and shred the flesh into fine strips.

### 2. 切配料 | Prep the Garnishes
火腿、冬笋、香菇分别切细丝。

Julienne the ham, bamboo shoots, and mushrooms into fine shreds.

### 3. 煮羹 | Build the Soup
锅中加鸡汤或煮鱼的原汤，烧开后放入火腿丝、笋丝、香菇丝，煮3分钟。加入鱼丝，调入酱油、盐、白胡椒粉。

Bring chicken stock (or the fish poaching liquid) to a boil. Add the ham, bamboo shoot, and mushroom shreds. Simmer 3 minutes. Add the fish shreds, soy sauce, salt, and white pepper.

### 4. 勾芡打蛋花 | Thicken & Finish
淀粉加水调匀，缓缓倒入锅中搅匀至羹汁浓稠。将打散的鸡蛋沿锅边淋入，轻轻搅动形成蛋花。出锅前淋入香醋和香油。

Mix cornstarch with water and stir into the soup until it reaches a silky consistency. Drizzle the beaten egg along the edge of the pot, stirring gently to form ribbons. Finish with Chinkiang vinegar and a drizzle of sesame oil.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鱼肉要细心拆骨，不能有碎骨残留 | Debone the fish meticulously — no bone fragments should remain |
| 勾芡要薄，羹汁似流非流 | The starch thickening should be light — the soup should flow like silk, not paste |
| 蛋花要沿锅边淋入，不可搅散 | Pour the egg along the pot's edge for beautiful ribbons — do not stir vigorously |
| 醋是点睛之笔，出锅前加 | The vinegar is the finishing touch — add it right before serving |
| 白胡椒不可少，提鲜去腥 | White pepper is essential — it brightens the broth and tames any fishiness |

## 替代食材 | American Substitutions

| 原料 | Ingredient | 替代 / Substitute | 备注 / Notes |
|------|-----------|-------------------|--------------|
| 鳜鱼/鲈鱼 | Mandarin fish / Sea bass | Striped bass, halibut, or cod fillet | 选肉质紧实的白鱼 / Any firm white fish works |
| 金华火腿 | Jinhua ham | Prosciutto 或 Smithfield country ham | 切极细丝 / Slice paper-thin, then julienne |
| 冬笋 | Winter bamboo shoots | 罐装竹笋 (canned bamboo shoots) | 任何超市有售 / Available at any supermarket |
| 香菇 | Shiitake mushrooms | 新鲜或干香菇均可 / Fresh or dried shiitake | Whole Foods, Trader Joe's 常见 |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 亚洲超市有售；替代：aged balsamic + rice vinegar | 建议买正宗镇江醋 / Worth buying the real thing |
