# 西湖莼菜汤 | West Lake Water Shield Soup

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/classics/xihu-cuncaitang.jpg" width="500" alt="西湖莼菜汤">
</p>

> 莼菜产于西湖，自古被视为珍味。晋代张翰因思念家乡莼菜鲈鱼，毅然辞官归乡，留下"莼鲈之思"的典故。此汤以莼菜配鸡丝、火腿丝，清淡鲜美，滑嫩爽口，是杭州最雅致的一道汤。
>
> *Water shield (Brasenia) has been harvested from West Lake since antiquity. A Jin Dynasty official famously resigned his post because he missed his hometown's water shield and perch — the idiom "longing for water shield and perch" (莼鲈之思) still means homesickness today. This delicate soup pairs the slippery, jewel-like leaves with shredded chicken and Jinhua ham — pure, elegant, and unmistakably Hangzhou.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 莼菜 | Water shield (Brasenia) | 200g（鲜品或罐装） |
| 鸡胸肉 | Chicken breast | 100g |
| 金华火腿 | Jinhua ham | 20g |
| 鸡汤 | Chicken stock | 800ml |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 / a pinch |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 少许 / a drizzle |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
鸡胸肉切极细丝，加少许盐、料酒和淀粉抓匀上浆。火腿切细丝。莼菜洗净沥干。

Shred the chicken breast very finely. Toss with a pinch of salt, wine, and cornstarch to velvet. Julienne the ham. Rinse and drain the water shield.

### 2. 煮汤 | Simmer the Broth
锅中倒入鸡汤烧开，放入火腿丝，小火煮2分钟。

Bring the chicken stock to a boil. Add the ham shreds and simmer gently for 2 minutes.

### 3. 下鸡丝 | Add the Chicken
将上浆的鸡丝散开放入汤中，轻轻搅散，煮至鸡丝变白。

Scatter the velveted chicken shreds into the broth. Stir gently until the chicken turns opaque.

### 4. 入莼菜调味 | Add Water Shield & Season
放入莼菜，煮约1分钟即可。调入盐和白胡椒粉，淋香油，即可盛出。

Add the water shield and cook for about 1 minute — just enough to heat through. Season with salt and white pepper, drizzle with sesame oil, and serve immediately.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 莼菜不可久煮，否则失去滑嫩口感 | Do not overcook the water shield — it will lose its silky texture |
| 此汤讲究清淡，调味宜少不宜多 | This soup celebrates subtlety — season sparingly |
| 鸡丝要切极细，与莼菜匹配 | Shred the chicken as finely as possible to match the delicate leaves |
| 火腿提鲜即可，不宜过多 | The ham is for umami accent — too much will overpower the soup |
