# 酸辣土豆丝 | Shredded Potato Stir-fry (Sichuan)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/quick/酸辣土豆丝.svg" width="500" alt="酸辣土豆丝"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 5分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 快手、下饭、川味、素菜

> 酸辣土豆丝是中国大街小巷最常见的家常菜之一。脆爽的土豆丝配上醋的酸和干辣椒的香辣，简单便宜到不可思议，却是无数人心中的"白月光"下饭菜。
>
> *Hot-and-sour shredded potato is one of China's most ubiquitous home-cooked dishes. Crispy, crunchy potato slivers tossed with vinegar tang and dried chili heat — impossibly cheap, impossibly addictive, and the ultimate rice companion.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 土豆 | Potatoes (waxy preferred) | 2个大 / 2 large |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chilies | 5-6个 / 5-6 |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorns | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp (可选/optional) |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 白醋 | White vinegar | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 1根 / 1 stalk |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切丝泡水 | Shred & Soak
土豆去皮切成细丝（越细越好，约2mm）。切好后泡在冷水中5分钟，洗去淀粉，沥干。这是脆爽的关键。

Peel and cut potatoes into thin matchsticks (~2mm). Soak in cold water for 5 minutes to remove starch, then drain thoroughly. This is the key to crispness.

### 2. 炝锅 | Flavor the Oil
锅烧热加油，放入干辣椒和花椒小火炸出香味（不要炸黑），加入蒜末爆香。

Heat oil in a wok. Add dried chilies and Sichuan peppercorns over low heat until fragrant (don't burn them). Add garlic and stir briefly.

### 3. 大火快炒 | Stir-fry on High
转大火，倒入土豆丝快速翻炒2-3分钟。沿锅边淋入白醋（醋遇到热锅会瞬间激发香气），加盐调味，翻炒均匀。土豆丝要保持脆爽，不要炒软。撒葱花出锅。

Turn to high heat. Add potato shreds and stir-fry vigorously for 2-3 minutes. Drizzle vinegar along the edge of the wok (the heat instantly blooms the aroma). Season with salt and toss well. The potatoes should stay crispy, not mushy. Garnish with scallion and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 土豆丝一定要泡水去淀粉 | Soaking in water to remove starch is non-negotiable |
| 切得越细越好，练刀工的好菜 | The thinner the shreds, the better — great knife practice |
| 大火快炒，不能炒过头 | High heat, fast stir-fry — don't overcook |
| 醋沿锅边淋，不要直接倒菜上 | Pour vinegar along the wok edge, not directly on the food |
| 嫌切丝麻烦可以用刨丝器 | A mandoline or julienne peeler saves time |
