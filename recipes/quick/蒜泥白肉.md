> 四川经典凉菜——五花肉煮熟切薄片，浇上蒜泥红油酱汁，鲜辣蒜香。夏天不想吃热菜的时候，这道菜是救星。可以提前做好放冰箱，随时取用。
>
> *A Sichuan cold dish classic — boiled pork belly sliced paper-thin, drenched in a garlic-chili oil sauce. When summer heat kills your appetite for hot food, this dish saves the day. Make it ahead, keep it in the fridge, grab it anytime.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 五花肉 | Pork belly (skin-on) | 500g |
| 姜 | Ginger | 5片 / 5 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| **蒜泥酱 / Garlic Sauce:** | | |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 8瓣 / 8 cloves (mashed) |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 辣椒油 | Chili oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮肉 | Boil Pork
五花肉冷水下锅，加姜葱料酒，大火烧开转小火煮30分钟至筷子能插透。

Place pork in cold water with ginger, scallion, wine. Boil, reduce to low, cook 30 min until chopsticks go through.

### 2. 冰水浸泡 | Ice Bath
捞出放入冰水中浸泡15分钟（使肉质紧实，切的时候不会散）。

Transfer to ice water for 15 minutes (firms up the meat for clean slicing).

### 3. 切片 | Slice
肉冷透后切成大薄片（越薄越好）。摆盘。

When fully cold, slice into large, thin pieces (thinner = better). Arrange on a plate.

### 4. 浇酱 | Sauce
蒜捣成泥，加酱油、辣椒油、醋、糖、香油调匀。浇在肉片上。

Mash garlic to a paste. Mix with soy sauce, chili oil, vinegar, sugar, sesame oil. Pour over the pork.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 冰水浴是关键，肉紧实才好切 | Ice bath is essential — firm meat slices cleanly |
| 蒜泥一定要捣不要切，蒜汁更多 | MASH garlic, don't mince — you need the juice |
| 越薄越好吃 | Thinner slices = better flavor absorption |
| 可以提前一天做好冷藏 | Can be made a day ahead and refrigerated |
