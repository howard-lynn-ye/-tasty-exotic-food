# 可乐鸡翅 | Cola Chicken Wings

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/quick/kele-jichi.jpg" width="500" alt="可乐鸡翅">
</p>

> 可乐鸡翅是中国年轻人最爱的"懒人菜"——可口可乐加鸡翅，一锅煮，零失败。可乐的焦糖和碳酸让鸡翅色泽红亮、肉质嫩滑，甜咸适口。请客、聚会、自己解馋，都是满分选择。
>
> *Cola Chicken Wings are the ultimate "lazy genius" dish in Chinese cooking — Coca-Cola plus chicken wings, one pot, zero chance of failure. The cola's caramel and carbonation give the wings a gorgeous glaze and impossibly tender meat. Perfect for parties, potlucks, or a solo treat.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡翅中 | Chicken wing mid-joints (or whole wings) | 500g (~10个 / ~10 pieces) |
| 可口可乐 | Coca-Cola (regular, NOT diet) | 1罐 / 1 can (355ml) |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 少许 / a little |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 处理鸡翅 | Prep the Wings
鸡翅正反面各划两刀（方便入味）。冷水下锅焯水2分钟，捞出。

Score both sides of each wing with 2 cuts (helps flavor penetrate). Blanch in cold water brought to a boil for 2 minutes. Drain.

### 2. 煎鸡翅 | Pan-fry
锅中少许油，放入鸡翅煎至两面金黄。

Add a thin film of oil to a pan. Pan-fry the wings until golden on both sides.

### 3. 加可乐炖煮 | Braise with Cola
加入姜片、葱段、料酒、酱油、老抽，倒入一整罐可乐。大火烧开后转中小火，加盖煮15分钟。

Add ginger, scallion, wine, soy sauce, and dark soy sauce. Pour in the entire can of Coca-Cola. Bring to a boil, reduce to medium-low heat, cover, and simmer 15 minutes.

### 4. 收汁 | Reduce the Glaze
揭盖转大火收汁，不断翻动鸡翅，至汤汁浓稠挂在鸡翅上。

Uncover and raise to high heat. Keep turning the wings as the sauce reduces until it's thick and glossy, coating every wing.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 必须用含糖的可乐，不能用 Diet/Zero | Must use REGULAR Coca-Cola — Diet/Zero won't caramelize |
| 百事也行，但可口可乐效果最好 | Pepsi works, but Coca-Cola gives the best results |
| 收汁是关键，酱汁要浓到发亮 | Reducing is key — the glaze should be shiny and thick |
| 划刀不要太深，鸡翅不能碎 | Score the wings, but don't cut too deep |
| 可以用 air fryer 最后烤2分钟，更脆 | Pop them in an air fryer for 2 min at the end for extra crispiness |
