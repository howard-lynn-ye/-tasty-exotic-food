# 韭菜炒鸡蛋 | Chive & Egg Stir-fry
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/quick/韭菜炒鸡蛋.svg" width="500" alt="韭菜炒鸡蛋"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 3分钟 | 💰 ~$1.50/份 | 🏷️ 快手、家常、两种食材、下饭

> 韭菜炒鸡蛋是中国家庭里最简单的"快手菜"之一，两种食材三分钟搞定。韭菜的辛香配上嫩滑的鸡蛋，是最朴素却最让人惦记的味道。春天的韭菜最鲜嫩，有句老话叫"春韭赛黄金"。
>
> *Chive and egg stir-fry is Chinese home cooking at its simplest — two ingredients, three minutes. The punchy fragrance of Chinese chives paired with soft scrambled eggs is the kind of humble dish you never stop craving. Spring chives are the tenderest, and an old saying goes: "Spring chives are worth their weight in gold."*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 韭菜 | Chinese chives (garlic chives) | 1把约200g / 1 bunch (~7 oz) |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 4个 / 4 |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
韭菜洗净切3厘米长段（头尾分开，头部更老需要先下锅）。鸡蛋打散加一小撮盐。

Wash chives and cut into 3cm / 1-inch pieces (separate the thicker root ends from the tender tops — roots go in first). Beat eggs with a tiny pinch of salt.

### 2. 炒蛋 | Scramble the Eggs
锅烧热多放油，倒入蛋液，稍微凝固后拨成大块，盛出。

Heat oil in a wok until shimmering. Pour in beaten eggs. Once the bottom sets, fold into large curds. Remove and set aside.

### 3. 炒韭菜合并 | Stir-fry Chives & Combine
锅中留底油，大火先放韭菜根部翻炒30秒，再加入叶子部分炒30秒。将鸡蛋倒回，加盐快速翻炒均匀，立即出锅。韭菜不能炒过头，变黄就老了。

In the remaining oil, stir-fry the root ends on high heat for 30 seconds, then add the leafy tops for another 30 seconds. Return the eggs, add salt, toss quickly, and plate immediately. Don't overcook the chives — they should be bright green, not yellowed.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 大火快炒是关键，不超过3分钟 | High heat and speed — total cook time under 3 minutes |
| 韭菜不能炒太久，会出水变烂 | Overcooking chives makes them watery and mushy |
| 蛋要嫩，先炒出来再合并 | Scramble eggs separately for the best texture |
| 不需要加酱油，盐就够了 | No soy sauce needed — salt alone is best here |
| 韭菜头部比叶子老，要先下锅 | Root ends are tougher — add them to the wok first |
