# 宫保鸡丁 | Kung Pao Chicken

> 宫保鸡丁可能是全世界最出名的中国菜。在美国的中餐馆，它永远是菜单第一页。但外卖的版本往往甜腻油重——自己做才知道正宗的宫保鸡丁有多好吃：鸡丁滑嫩，花生酥脆，咸甜微辣，下饭一绝。
>
> *Kung Pao Chicken may be the most famous Chinese dish in the world — it's on the first page of every Chinese restaurant menu in America. But takeout versions are often too sweet and oily. Make it yourself and discover the real deal: silky chicken, crunchy peanuts, a savory-sweet-spicy sauce, and an irresistible urge to eat three bowls of rice.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿肉 | Chicken thighs (boneless, skinless) | 300g |
| 花生米 | Roasted peanuts | 50g |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chilies | 8-10个 / 8-10 |
| 葱 | Scallion (white part) | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorns (optional) | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌鸡丁 | Marinate
鸡腿肉切1.5cm丁，加1汤匙酱油、料酒和1茶匙淀粉抓匀，腌10分钟。

Cut chicken into 1.5 cm dice. Toss with 1 tbsp soy sauce, wine, and 1 tsp cornstarch. Marinate 10 minutes.

### 2. 调酱汁 | Mix the Sauce
碗中混合：1汤匙酱油、香醋、白糖、1茶匙淀粉和2汤匙水，搅匀备用。

In a small bowl, combine: 1 tbsp soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, 1 tsp cornstarch, and 2 tbsp water. Stir well and set aside.

### 3. 炒鸡丁 | Stir-fry the Chicken
锅中热油至冒烟，放入鸡丁大火快炒至变色微焦，盛出。

Heat oil in a wok until smoking. Add chicken and stir-fry over high heat until golden. Remove and set aside.

### 4. 炒辣椒 | Fry the Aromatics
锅中留底油，小火放入干辣椒和花椒炒香（不要炒黑）。加入葱白、蒜片、姜片爆香。

In remaining oil, add dried chilies and Sichuan peppercorns on low heat until fragrant (don't burn them). Add scallion whites, garlic, and ginger. Stir until aromatic.

### 5. 合炒 | Combine
倒回鸡丁，倒入调好的酱汁，大火快速翻炒至酱汁浓稠裹住鸡丁。最后加入花生米翻匀，出锅。

Return the chicken. Pour in the sauce mixture. Toss over high heat until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken. Add peanuts at the very end, toss once, and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鸡腿肉比鸡胸肉嫩得多 | Thigh meat is far more tender than breast |
| 花生最后放，保持酥脆 | Add peanuts at the very end to keep them crunchy |
| 干辣椒不要炒黑，否则发苦 | Don't blacken the chilies — they'll taste bitter |
| 酱汁要提前调好，下锅才来得及 | Pre-mix the sauce — things move fast once cooking starts |
| 大火快炒是关键 | High heat, fast hands — that's the secret |
