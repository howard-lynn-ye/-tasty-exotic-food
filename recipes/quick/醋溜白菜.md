# 醋溜白菜 | Vinegar Cabbage Stir-fry
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/quick/醋溜白菜.svg" width="500" alt="醋溜白菜"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 5分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 快手、素菜、酸甜、北方家常

> 醋溜白菜是北方家庭的"万能菜"，食材便宜到极致，味道却酸甜开胃。大白菜在猛火和香醋的加持下变得脆嫩多汁，是学做中国菜的最佳入门之一。
>
> *Vinegar cabbage is the ultimate Northern Chinese weeknight staple — dirt-cheap ingredients, but the sweet-sour tang is absolutely addictive. Napa cabbage flash-fried with vinegar hits different. One of the best beginner Chinese dishes.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大白菜 | Napa cabbage | 半棵约400g / 1/2 head (~14 oz) |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chilies | 4-5个 / 4-5 |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 香醋（或陈醋） | Chinkiang vinegar (black vinegar) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 淀粉水 | Cornstarch slurry | 1茶匙淀粉+2汤匙水 / 1 tsp starch + 2 tbsp water |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
白菜叶和帮分开，帮切菱形片，叶子撕大块。蒜切末，干辣椒剪段去籽。提前调好碗汁：醋、酱油、糖、盐混合。

Separate the napa cabbage stems from the leafy parts. Cut stems into diamond shapes, tear leaves into large pieces. Mince garlic, snip chilies and remove seeds. Mix the sauce in advance: vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, and salt.

### 2. 炝锅爆炒 | Stir-fry
锅烧热加油，放干辣椒和蒜末爆香。先放白菜帮大火翻炒1分钟，再放叶子炒30秒。

Heat oil in a wok until smoking. Add dried chilies and garlic, stir until fragrant. Add cabbage stems first and stir-fry on high heat for 1 minute. Then add the leaves and stir-fry 30 seconds.

### 3. 调味出锅 | Sauce & Serve
倒入调好的碗汁，翻炒均匀。淋入淀粉水勾薄芡，让酱汁挂在菜上，立即出锅。

Pour in the pre-mixed sauce and toss well. Drizzle in the cornstarch slurry to lightly thicken — just enough to coat the cabbage. Plate immediately.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 帮和叶分开炒，帮先叶后 | Stems and leaves cook at different speeds — stems first |
| 大火快炒，白菜要脆不要软 | High heat keeps cabbage crisp, not soggy |
| 醋的香气靠热锅激发 | Hot wok brings out the vinegar's aroma |
| 勾芡不要太厚，薄薄一层即可 | Keep the starch slurry thin — just a light glaze |
| 碗汁提前调好，下锅不手忙脚乱 | Pre-mix the sauce so you're not scrambling at the wok |
