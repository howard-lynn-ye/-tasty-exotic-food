# 葱油拌面 | Scallion Oil Noodles

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$1.50/份 | 🏷️ 快手、上海、极简、全超市可买

> 上海人的灵魂面——只用葱、油和酱油，就能做出一碗让人惊叹的面。秘密在于"熬葱油"：把葱慢慢炸至焦脆，葱的甜味和焦香全部释放到油里。这碗面告诉你：最好的味道往往来自最简单的食材。
>
> *The soul of Shanghai noodles — just scallions, oil, and soy sauce create a bowl that stuns. The secret is "slow-fried scallion oil": scallions are slowly crisped until their sweetness and char infuse the oil. This bowl teaches you: the best flavors often come from the simplest ingredients.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 面条 | Noodles (any kind) | 200g |
| 葱 | Scallions | 6根 / 6 stalks |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 4汤匙 / 4 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 熬葱油 | Make Scallion Oil
葱切段。锅中放油，**小火**放入葱段，慢慢炸8-10分钟至葱变深棕色焦脆（千万不要大火！）。

Cut scallions into segments. Heat oil, add scallions on **LOW heat**. Slowly fry 8–10 minutes until dark brown and crispy (never use high heat!).

### 2. 调味 | Season
关火，加入酱油、老抽和糖，搅匀。这就是葱油酱汁。

Turn off heat. Add soy sauce, dark soy sauce, and sugar. Stir. This is your scallion oil sauce.

### 3. 煮面拌匀 | Cook & Toss
煮面至你喜欢的软硬度。沥干，倒入葱油酱汁拌匀。

Cook noodles to your preferred doneness. Drain, toss with the scallion oil sauce.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 小火慢炸是关键！大火葱会焦苦 | LOW heat is everything — high heat makes scallions bitter |
| 用细面或挂面最好 | Thin noodles or dried Chinese noodles work best |
| 可以加一个煎蛋或卤蛋 | Top with a fried egg or braised egg for protein |
