# 西红柿牛腩汤 | Tomato Beef Stew
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/quick/西红柿牛腩汤.svg" width="500" alt="西红柿牛腩汤"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 60分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 炖菜、番茄、牛肉、暖身

> 西红柿炖牛腩是中国家庭冬天最常做的硬菜之一。大块牛腩在番茄的酸甜中慢慢炖到软烂，汤汁浓郁鲜美，泡饭、拌面都是一绝。虽然要花点时间，但几乎零难度。
>
> *Tomato beef stew is a Chinese winter staple. Chunky beef brisket slow-braised in a tangy, sweet tomato broth until fork-tender — the sauce is incredible over rice or tossed with noodles. It takes time, but almost zero skill.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛腩 | Beef chuck or brisket | 500g / 1 lb |
| 番茄 | Tomatoes | 3个大 / 3 large |
| 番茄酱 | Tomato paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium |
| 生姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 料酒 | Cooking wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 冰糖或白糖 | Rock sugar or white sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 八角 | Star anise | 1个 / 1 whole |
| 香叶 | Bay leaf | 1片 / 1 |
| 葱花或香菜 | Scallion or cilantro | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 牛腩焯水 | Blanch the Beef
牛腩切大块（约3cm），冷水下锅加姜片和料酒，煮开后撇去浮沫，捞出冲洗干净。

Cut beef into large chunks (~3cm). Place in a pot with cold water, ginger, and cooking wine. Bring to a boil, skim off the foam, then drain and rinse the beef.

### 2. 炒番茄底 | Build the Tomato Base
锅中少许油，炒洋葱碎至微黄，加入番茄块炒至出汁软烂，加番茄酱炒匀。

Heat a little oil in a pot. Saut diced onion until lightly golden. Add tomato chunks and cook until they break down and release juice. Stir in tomato paste.

### 3. 炖煮 | Braise
放入焯好的牛腩、八角、香叶，加入足够的开水没过食材。加酱油和糖，大火烧开后转小火炖50-60分钟，直到牛肉用筷子能轻松戳透。

Add the blanched beef, star anise, and bay leaf. Pour in enough boiling water to cover everything. Add soy sauce and sugar. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and simmer for 50-60 minutes until the beef is fork-tender.

### 4. 调味出锅 | Season & Serve
尝味加盐调整，撒葱花或香菜，配米饭或面条。

Taste and adjust with salt. Garnish with scallion or cilantro. Serve with rice or noodles.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 牛腩一定要焯水去腥 | Always blanch beef first to remove impurities |
| 番茄要炒出汁再加肉 | Cook tomatoes until juicy before adding beef |
| 加开水而不是冷水，肉更嫩 | Add boiling water (not cold) for more tender meat |
| 炖的时间越长越软烂 | Longer simmer = more tender beef |
| 番茄酱增色增鲜 | Tomato paste boosts both color and flavor |
