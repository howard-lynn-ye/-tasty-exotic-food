# 部队火锅 | Chinese Hot Pot (Simplified)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/one-pot/部队火锅.svg" width="500" alt="部队火锅"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 一锅搞定、聚餐、辣、丰盛

> 简易火锅是中国最欢乐的吃法——一锅滚烫的汤底，把喜欢的菜往里涮就行。这个简化版不需要专业器具，一口大锅加超市食材就能在家复刻火锅夜的快乐。朋友聚会必备。
>
> *Hot pot is China's most joyful way to eat — a bubbling pot of broth, and you throw in whatever you love. This simplified version needs no special equipment: one big pot, supermarket ingredients, and you've got a hot pot party at home. Perfect for gatherings.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 火锅底料（麻辣或番茄） | Hot pot base (spicy or tomato) | 半包约100g / 1/2 pack (~3.5 oz) |
| 肥牛卷 | Thinly sliced beef | 200g / 7 oz |
| 豆腐（老豆腐或冻豆腐） | Firm tofu | 1块 / 1 block |
| 大白菜 | Napa cabbage | 1/4棵 / 1/4 head |
| 金针菇 | Enoki mushrooms | 1包 / 1 pack |
| 土豆 | Potato | 1个 / 1, sliced |
| 宽粉或方便面 | Wide glass noodles or instant noodles | 1把 / 1 bundle |
| 午餐肉（Spam） | Spam or luncheon meat | 半罐 / 1/2 can |
| 鱼丸或肉丸 | Fish balls or meatballs | 6-8个 / 6-8 |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香菜和葱花 | Cilantro & scallion | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备菜 | Prep Everything
所有食材洗净切好摆盘：白菜撕块，豆腐切块，午餐肉切片，土豆切薄片，金针菇撕散。调一碗蘸料：蒜泥+香油+酱油+醋+葱花。

Wash and prep all ingredients on plates: tear cabbage into pieces, cube the tofu, slice the Spam, thin-slice the potato, separate enoki mushrooms. Mix a dipping sauce: minced garlic + sesame oil + soy sauce + vinegar + scallion.

### 2. 煮汤底 | Heat the Broth
大锅中加约6杯水，放入火锅底料搅匀，大火烧开。

Add about 6 cups of water to a large pot. Stir in the hot pot base until dissolved. Bring to a rolling boil.

### 3. 分批涮煮 | Cook in Batches
先放耐煮的食材：土豆、豆腐、午餐肉、丸子，煮5分钟。再放白菜和金针菇煮2分钟。最后放肥牛卷（涮10秒变色即熟）和粉条（煮3分钟）。

Add slow-cooking items first: potato, tofu, Spam, meatballs — cook 5 minutes. Then add cabbage and enoki — cook 2 minutes. Lastly, swish the beef slices (10 seconds until just pink) and add noodles (cook 3 minutes).

### 4. 蘸料吃 | Dip & Enjoy
捞出食材蘸蘸料吃，边煮边吃，不断加菜。最后的汤底下粉条或面条收尾。

Fish out cooked items and dip in your sauce. Keep adding ingredients as you eat. Finish by cooking noodles in the remaining flavorful broth.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 买现成火锅底料最省事 | Pre-made hot pot base saves you 20+ spices |
| 耐煮的先放，叶菜和肉最后 | Hardy items first, greens and meat last |
| 肥牛涮几秒就好，老了不好吃 | Beef needs only seconds — overcooking makes it tough |
| 蘸料可以随自己喜好调 | Customize dipping sauce to your taste |
| 最后煮面条是灵魂操作 | Noodles in the leftover broth is the best part |
