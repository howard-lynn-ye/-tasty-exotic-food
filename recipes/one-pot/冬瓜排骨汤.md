# 冬瓜排骨汤 | Winter Melon & Pork Rib Soup

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 一锅搞定、清淡、养生、中式汤

> 冬瓜排骨汤是中国家庭最经典的养生汤之一。排骨的鲜香和冬瓜的清甜在慢炖中完美融合，汤色清亮，味道温润。夏天喝清热，冬天喝暖身，一年四季都合适。
>
> *Winter melon pork rib soup is one of China's most classic nourishing soups. The savory depth of pork ribs and the mild sweetness of winter melon meld through slow simmering into a clear, gentle broth. Cooling in summer, warming in winter — perfect year-round.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 排骨（小排） | Pork spare ribs | 400g / 14 oz |
| 冬瓜 | Winter melon | 300g / 10 oz |
| 生姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 料酒 | Cooking wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 枸杞 | Goji berries (optional) | 1小把 / 1 small handful |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 排骨焯水 | Blanch the Ribs
排骨冷水下锅，加几片姜和料酒，大火煮开后撇去浮沫，捞出冲洗干净。

Place ribs in a pot with cold water, ginger slices, and cooking wine. Bring to a boil, skim off all foam and impurities, then drain and rinse the ribs clean.

### 2. 炖排骨 | Simmer the Ribs
将焯好的排骨放入干净的锅中，加足够的清水（约6杯），放入姜片，大火烧开后转小火慢炖30分钟。

Place blanched ribs in a clean pot with enough water (~6 cups) and fresh ginger slices. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and simmer gently for 30 minutes.

### 3. 加冬瓜 | Add Winter Melon
冬瓜去皮去籽，切大块，放入汤中继续炖15分钟，直到冬瓜变半透明软烂。

Peel and seed the winter melon, cut into large chunks. Add to the soup and simmer another 15 minutes until the melon turns translucent and tender.

### 4. 调味出锅 | Season & Serve
加盐和白胡椒粉调味，撒入枸杞（如果用的话），最后撒葱花。

Season with salt and white pepper. Drop in goji berries if using. Ladle into bowls and garnish with scallion.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 排骨焯水是必须步骤，去血水去腥 | Blanching ribs is essential — removes blood and gaminess |
| 冬瓜后放，不要和排骨一起煮 | Add winter melon later — it cooks much faster than ribs |
| 小火慢炖汤才清亮 | Low and slow keeps the broth clear, not cloudy |
| 不需要太多调料，盐和胡椒就够 | Keep it simple — salt and white pepper are all you need |
| 冬瓜煮到半透明就好，别煮烂 | Cook melon until translucent but not mushy |
