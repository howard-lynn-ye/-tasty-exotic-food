# 酸菜鱼 | Sichuan Pickled Vegetable Fish Stew

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 一锅出、川菜、酸辣、Costco鱼

> 留学生最想念的中国菜之一！酸菜的酸爽、花椒的麻、辣椒的辣，加上滑嫩的鱼片——一口汤下去浑身舒畅。现在在美国也能做，Costco 的三文鱼或鲈鱼切片就行。
>
> *One of the most-missed Chinese dishes abroad! The tang of pickled mustard greens, the numbing of Sichuan pepper, the heat of chili — and silky fish slices swimming in a fiery broth. One sip and your whole body relaxes. Costco salmon or bass fillets work perfectly.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鱼片 | Fish fillets (bass, salmon, or tilapia) | 400g |
| 酸菜 | Chinese pickled mustard greens (suan cai) | 200g |
| 鸡蛋清 | Egg white | 1个 / 1 |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chilies | 10个 / 10 |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorns | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 5瓣 / 5 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 5片 / 5 slices |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 适量 / to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 水或鸡汤 | Water or chicken broth | 800ml |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌鱼 | Velvet Fish
鱼片加盐、料酒、蛋清和淀粉抓匀，腌15分钟。

Toss fish slices with salt, wine, egg white, and cornstarch. Marinate 15 minutes.

### 2. 炒酸菜底 | Cook the Base
锅中热油，爆香姜蒜，放入酸菜翻炒2分钟出香。加水/鸡汤烧开，煮5分钟。

Heat oil, sizzle ginger and garlic. Add pickled greens, stir-fry 2 minutes. Add water/broth, boil 5 minutes.

### 3. 下鱼片 | Add Fish
将鱼片一片一片放入汤中（不要搅动），小火煮2分钟至鱼片变白。

Slide fish slices in one by one (don't stir). Simmer 2 minutes until opaque.

### 4. 浇热油 | Hot Oil Finish
盛入大碗，顶上铺花椒和干辣椒。烧热3汤匙油至冒烟，浇在花椒辣椒上——滋啦一声！

Transfer to a large bowl. Top with Sichuan peppercorns and dried chilies. Heat 3 tbsp oil until smoking, pour over the spices — SIZZLE!

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鱼片不要搅动，轻轻放入 | Don't stir the fish — slide it in gently |
| 最后浇热油是灵魂！激发花椒和辣椒的香气 | The hot oil pour is the SOUL — it activates all the aromatics |
| 买不到酸菜可用 sauerkraut 替代 | No suan cai? Sauerkraut works as a substitute |
