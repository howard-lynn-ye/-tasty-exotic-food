# 番茄鸡蛋面 | Tomato Egg Noodle Soup

<p align="center">
  <img src="../../images/one-pot/fanqie-jidan-mian.jpg" width="500" alt="番茄鸡蛋面">
</p>

> 留学生的第一道菜，没有之一。一口锅、三种食材、十五分钟，就能煮出一碗热气腾腾、酸甜鲜美的面。下课回家，打开冰箱，番茄和鸡蛋永远不会让你失望。
>
> *The first dish every Chinese student abroad learns to make — and for good reason. One pot, three ingredients, fifteen minutes. A steaming bowl of tangy, savory noodle soup that never fails to comfort. After a long day of classes, tomatoes and eggs will always have your back.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 番茄 | Tomatoes | 2个 / 2 medium |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 面条 | Noodles (any kind) | 1人份 / 1 serving (~150g) |
| 葱 | Scallion | 1根 / 1 stalk |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
番茄切小块，鸡蛋打散，葱切葱花。

Dice the tomatoes. Beat the eggs. Slice the scallion.

### 2. 炒蛋 | Scramble the Eggs
锅中热油，倒入蛋液，炒成大块后盛出备用。

Heat oil in a pot. Pour in the eggs, scramble into large curds, and set aside.

### 3. 炒番茄 | Cook the Tomatoes
同一口锅，放入番茄块翻炒，加少许盐和糖，炒至番茄出汁软烂。

In the same pot, add the tomatoes. Sprinkle with salt and sugar. Stir-fry until the tomatoes break down and release their juices.

### 4. 加水煮面 | Add Water & Cook Noodles
加入约500ml水，大火烧开。下面条煮至你喜欢的软硬程度。

Add ~500 ml water and bring to a boil. Add noodles and cook to your preferred doneness.

### 5. 合并出锅 | Combine & Serve
将炒好的鸡蛋倒回锅中，加酱油调味，撒葱花，连汤带面盛入碗中。

Return the scrambled eggs to the pot. Season with soy sauce. Sprinkle with scallions. Ladle noodles and soup into a bowl.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 番茄要炒到出汁，汤底才鲜 | Cook the tomatoes until they release juice — that's your broth |
| 加一点糖能提鲜去酸 | A pinch of sugar balances the acidity and boosts flavor |
| 面条什么都行：挂面、意面、ramen 都可以 | Any noodle works: dried Chinese noodles, spaghetti, or ramen |
| 想更丰富可以加青菜、火腿肠 | Level up with greens or sliced hot dogs |
