# 花生酱香蕉吐司 | PB Banana Toast

> ⏱ 准备 3分钟 + 烹饪 2分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 早餐、免烹饪、5分钟、高能量

> 这大概是世界上最简单的早餐之一了——烤吐司、抹花生酱、放香蕉片。但简单不代表不好吃，花生的浓香、香蕉的清甜，再来一点蜂蜜和肉桂粉的点缀，五分钟的早餐也可以很幸福。留学生和上班族的续命早餐。

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 吐司面包 | Bread (any sliced bread) | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 花生酱 | Peanut butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 香蕉 | Banana | 1根 / 1 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 适量 / to taste (optional) |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 少许 / a pinch (optional) |
| 奇亚籽 | Chia seeds | 少许 / a sprinkle (optional) |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤吐司 | Toast the Bread
吐司放入烤面包机或平底锅中烤至金黄酥脆。

Toast the bread in a toaster or in a dry skillet until golden and crisp.

### 2. 抹花生酱 | Spread the PB
趁热在吐司上厚厚抹一层花生酱（热的吐司会让花生酱微微融化，更好涂抹）。

While the toast is hot, spread a generous layer of peanut butter on each slice. The warmth slightly melts the PB, making it easier to spread.

### 3. 摆香蕉装饰 | Top & Finish
香蕉切薄片均匀铺在花生酱上。淋蜂蜜，撒肉桂粉和奇亚籽（如果用的话）。直接开吃。

Slice the banana and arrange on top of the peanut butter. Drizzle with honey, sprinkle with cinnamon and chia seeds if using. Eat immediately.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花生酱选颗粒型（crunchy）口感更好 | Crunchy PB adds better texture than smooth |
| 吐司要趁热抹，花生酱更好涂 | Spread PB on hot toast — it melts slightly and spreads easier |
| 香蕉选微微带斑点的，最甜 | Bananas with a few brown spots are the sweetest |
| 可以加草莓片或蓝莓 | Add sliced strawberries or blueberries for extra fruit |
| 想更饱可以加一个水煮蛋 | Add a boiled egg on the side for extra protein |
