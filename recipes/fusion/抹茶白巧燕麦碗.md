# 抹茶白巧克力燕麦碗 | Matcha White Chocolate Oat Bowl

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 我分析了为什么抹茶拿铁在美国这么火：抹茶的苦涩+牛奶的甜柔=味觉对比的黄金比例。我把这个原理应用到早餐燕麦上：抹茶粉提供 L-茶氨酸（让你专注但不焦虑的天然成分），白巧克力提供可可脂的丝滑甜味，燕麦提供缓释碳水。这碗早餐让你精力充沛地上完4节课。

> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *I analyzed why matcha lattes are so popular in America: matcha's bitterness + milk's sweetness = a golden ratio of taste contrast. I applied this to breakfast oats: matcha delivers L-theanine (a natural compound for calm focus), white chocolate adds silky sweetness from cocoa butter, and oats provide slow-release carbs. This bowl powers you through 4 classes.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 燕麦片 | Rolled oats | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 牛奶 | Milk (any kind) | 3/4杯 / 3/4 cup |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 白巧克力 | White chocolate chips | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| **配料 / Toppings:** | | |
| 香蕉切片 | Sliced banana | 半根 / half |
| 坚果 | Nuts (almonds, walnuts) | 一小把 / a handful |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 混合 | Mix
碗中放燕麦、牛奶和抹茶粉，搅匀。

Combine oats, milk, and matcha in a microwave-safe bowl. Stir.

### 2. 微波 | Microwave
微波高火2分钟。取出搅拌，再微波1分钟。

Microwave on high 2 minutes. Stir. Microwave 1 more minute.

### 3. 加料 | Top
加入白巧克力碎（让它自己融化），淋蜂蜜，加香蕉和坚果。

Stir in white chocolate chips (they'll melt on their own). Drizzle honey. Add banana and nuts.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 抹茶要先和少量牛奶搅开，防止结块 | Whisk matcha with a splash of milk first to prevent lumps |
| 白巧克力不要加太多，甜度刚好就行 | Don't overdo white chocolate — just enough sweetness |
| 宿舍微波炉就能做，零厨房技术 | Dorm microwave works perfectly — zero cooking skill needed |
