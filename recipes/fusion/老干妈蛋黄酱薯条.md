|------|-----------|---------------|

| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤薯条 | Cook Fries
按包装说明烤薯条（烤箱或空气炸锅都行）。烤到额外脆——多烤2-3分钟超过建议时间。

Bake fries per package directions (oven or air fryer). Go EXTRA crispy — add 2-3 minutes beyond recommended time.

### 2. 调酱 | Make the Sauce
蛋黄酱+老干妈+蒜泥+米醋搅拌均匀。老干妈要连油带渣一起加。

Mix mayo + Lao Gan Ma + garlic + rice vinegar. Include BOTH the oil and the crunchy bits from LGM.

### 3. 摆盘上桌 | Plate & Serve
薯条堆盘，淋一部分酱在上面，剩余酱放小碗蘸着吃。撒葱花和芝麻。

Pile fries on a plate, drizzle some sauce on top, put remaining sauce in a small bowl for dipping. Garnish with scallion and sesame.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么这三者混在一起如此上瘾 / Why this works:**
> 蛋黄酱是一种油-水乳化液，其卵磷脂微球完美地分散了老干妈的辣椒素（capsaicin），把"点状刺痛"变成"全面温暖"。同时蛋黄酱的蛋黄含有丰富的卵磷脂和胆碱，这些分子与老干妈豆豉中的谷氨酸发生鲜味协同。薯条的美拉德反应外壳（炸制产生的丙烯酰胺和呋喃类化合物）提供了一个酥脆的"传送平台"，让酱料的风味在咀嚼中持续释放。
>
> *Mayo is an oil-water emulsion whose lecithin microspheres perfectly disperse LGM's capsaicin, converting "point-like sting" into "all-over warmth." Mayo's egg yolk also contains lecithin and choline that synergize with the glutamate in LGM's fermented black beans. Fries' Maillard-reaction crust (acrylamide and furan compounds from frying) acts as a crunchy "delivery platform," sustaining sauce flavor release during chewing.*
