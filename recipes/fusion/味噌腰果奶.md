# 味噌腰果奶 | Miso Cashew Milk

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 腰果富含油脂，浸泡后搅打会形成天然乳液（水包油型乳化液），质地比杏仁奶更浓厚丝滑。加入白味噌后，味噌中的谷氨酸钠充当天然增味剂，让坚果奶从平淡变得有"奶味"——这就是为什么日本味噌汤喝起来有奶感的秘密。一小匙味噌就能让自制坚果奶的风味超越商店产品。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Cashews are rich in oils; when soaked and blended, they form a natural oil-in-water emulsion thicker and silkier than almond milk. White miso's sodium glutamate acts as a natural flavor enhancer, transforming plain nut milk into something genuinely "milky" — this is why Japanese miso soup has a creamy mouthfeel. One teaspoon of miso makes homemade nut milk surpass store-bought.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 生腰果 | Raw cashews | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 水 | Water | 3杯 / 3 cups |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 浸泡 | Soak
腰果冷水浸泡至少4小时（或沸水浸泡30分钟快速法）。沥干。

Soak cashews in cold water at least 4 hours (or 30 min in boiling water for quick method). Drain.

### 2. 搅打 | Blend
腰果+3杯新鲜水+味噌+枫糖+香草精+盐，高速搅打2分钟至完全顺滑。

Blend soaked cashews + 3 cups fresh water + miso + maple syrup + vanilla + salt on high for 2 minutes until perfectly smooth.

### 3. 过滤储存 | Strain & Store
可以直接喝，也可以用纱布过滤更细腻。冰箱保存3-4天，喝前摇匀。

Drink as-is, or strain through cheesecloth for extra smoothness. Keeps 3-4 days refrigerated — shake before use.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 腰果不需要过滤就很细腻，不像杏仁有渣 | Cashews blend smooth enough to skip straining, unlike almonds |
| 味噌不能多加，1茶匙刚好增鲜，多了就咸 | 1 tsp miso is the sweet spot — more makes it too salty |
| 可以用来做咖啡拿铁，比商店燕麦奶还好喝 | Makes incredible coffee lattes — better than store-bought oat milk |
