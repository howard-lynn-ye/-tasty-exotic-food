> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 酱油在高温糖浆中发生焦糖化时，其中的氨基酸与糖的美拉德反应产生数百种风味化合物，比纯糖焦糖化丰富得多。酱油的咸鲜味与焦糖的甜苦味形成完美的甜咸平衡——这就是为什么salted caramel能风靡全球的原理，而酱油版本多了一层发酵的深度。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *When soy sauce meets high-heat sugar syrup, its amino acids undergo Maillard reactions with sugar, producing hundreds more flavor compounds than plain caramelization. Soy sauce's salty umami balances caramel's sweet bitterness — the same principle behind salted caramel's global success, but soy adds an extra layer of fermented depth.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 砂糖 | Granulated sugar | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 黄油 | Butter | 4汤匙 / 4 tbsp (1/2 stick) |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 海盐片 | Sea salt flakes | 少许 / for topping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 熬糖 | Cook Sugar
中火将糖在厚底锅中干熬，不要搅拌，只轻轻晃锅。熬至深琥珀色（约8分钟）。

Heat sugar in a heavy saucepan over medium heat. Don't stir — just swirl the pan. Cook to deep amber (~8 min).

### 2. 加料 | Add Butter & Cream
离火加入黄油搅匀（会剧烈冒泡，小心），再加淡奶油和酱油，搅至顺滑。重新上火煮2分钟至浓稠。

Off heat, stir in butter (it will bubble violently — careful). Add cream and soy sauce, stir smooth. Return to heat 2 min until thickened.

### 3. 冷却切块 | Cool & Cut
倒入铺了油纸的方形模具，撒海盐片。室温冷却1小时后冰箱30分钟，切成小块。

Pour into a parchment-lined square pan, sprinkle sea salt. Cool 1 hour at room temp, then 30 min in fridge. Cut into pieces.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 熬糖时温度要用糖果温度计，目标300°F | Use a candy thermometer — target is 300°F (hard crack stage) |
| 酱油在最后加，避免过早焦化 | Add soy sauce last to avoid premature burning |
| 用油纸包起来送人特别体面 | Wrap in wax paper for elegant gifts |
