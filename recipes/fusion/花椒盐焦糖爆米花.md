## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 爆米花粒 | Popcorn kernels | 1/3杯 / 1/3 cup |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | 100g / 1/2 cup |
| 黄油 | Butter | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 爆玉米花 | Pop the Corn
大锅加油和3粒玉米粒，盖盖中火加热。听到3粒全部爆开后倒入剩余玉米粒，盖盖摇锅爆至声音稀疏。

Heat oil with 3 test kernels in a large pot, lid on. When all 3 pop, add remaining kernels. Shake pot until popping slows to 2-second gaps.

### 2. 做焦糖 | Make Caramel
另起锅，中火融化黄油，加入白糖不断搅拌直到变成琥珀色焦糖（约4分钟）。立刻撒入花椒粉和海盐，搅匀。

In a separate pan, melt butter over medium heat. Add sugar, stir constantly until it turns amber caramel (~4 min). Immediately add Sichuan pepper and salt, stir.

### 3. 裹酱 | Coat
将爆米花倒入焦糖锅，快速翻拌让每一颗都裹上花椒焦糖。

Pour popcorn into the caramel pan. Toss rapidly to coat every piece.

### 4. 冷却 | Cool
倒在铺了烘焙纸的烤盘上，摊开冷却5分钟至酥脆。

Spread on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Cool 5 minutes until crunchy.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么花椒让焦糖更甜 / Why Sichuan pepper makes caramel sweeter:**
> - 花椒素（hydroxy-alpha-sanshool）以50Hz频率刺激触觉神经末梢
> - 这种"振动"状态下的味蕾对甜味受体T1R2/T1R3的响应增强40%
> - 焦糖在170-180°C产生的呋喃醛（furfural）散发太妃糖香气
> - 海盐抑制苦味受体，间接增强甜味感知——"减苦=增甜"
>
> *Hydroxy-alpha-sanshool stimulates tactile nerve endings at ~50Hz. Taste buds in this "vibrating" state show ~40% enhanced response to sweet receptors T1R2/T1R3. Caramel produces furfural at 170–180°C, giving off toffee aromas. Sea salt suppresses bitter receptors, indirectly boosting sweetness — "less bitter = more sweet."*
