> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 生萝卜辛辣刺鼻，但烤过之后辛辣成分挥发殆尽，只剩下温和的甜和微微的泥土气息。味噌的发酵咸鲜给这个朴素的根茎蔬菜穿上"鲜味外套"，蜂蜜在高温下焦糖化成琥珀色的薄壳。烤萝卜是那种让人重新认识蔬菜的食物——"这真的是萝卜？"
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Raw radishes are pungent and sharp, but roasting evaporates the volatile compounds, leaving only gentle sweetness and earthy undertones. Miso's fermented saltiness dresses this humble root vegetable in an "umami coat," while honey caramelizes into an amber shell at high heat. Roasted radish is the kind of food that makes people rediscover vegetables — "This is really a radish?"*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 樱桃萝卜 | Radishes | 2把（约20个，切半）/ 2 bunches (~20, halved) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Make Glaze
碗中混合味噌、蜂蜜、橄榄油、米醋和芝麻油，搅至味噌完全溶解。

Whisk miso, honey, olive oil, rice vinegar, and sesame oil until miso is fully dissolved.

### 2. 拌萝卜 | Toss Radishes
萝卜切半，倒入酱汁翻拌至均匀裹上。

Halve radishes, toss with glaze until evenly coated.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱预热 220°C (425°F)。萝卜切面朝下铺在烤盘上。烤 20-25分钟至切面焦糖化金黄、萝卜变软。

Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Place radishes cut-side down on a baking sheet. Roast 20–25 minutes until cut sides are caramelized golden and radishes are tender.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻和葱花，趁热当配菜吃。

Sprinkle sesame seeds and scallions. Serve hot as a side dish.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 切面朝下！最大化焦糖化面积 | Cut-side down! Maximizes caramelization surface area |
| 烤到萝卜可以用叉子轻松戳穿的程度 | Roast until a fork slides through easily |
| 萝卜叶子如果新鲜，切碎撒上去当装饰 | If radish greens are fresh, chop and use as garnish |
| 大萝卜切四瓣，小萝卜切半就好 | Quarter large radishes, halve small ones |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么烤萝卜和生萝卜是两种食物 / Why roasted radish ≠ raw radish:**
> - 萝卜辛辣来自异硫氰酸盐（glucosinolate breakdown），加热后挥发
> - 热处理将萝卜淀粉转化为糖，释放内在甜味
> - 味噌的谷氨酸弥补了萝卜本身鲜味的不足
> - 蜂蜜焦糖化+萝卜自身糖分焦糖化=双重焦香
>
> *Radish pungency comes from isothiocyanates (glucosinolate breakdown), which volatilize when heated. Heat converts starch to sugar, releasing inherent sweetness. Miso's glutamate compensates for radish's low natural umami. Honey caramelization + radish's own sugar caramelization = double char-sweetness.*
