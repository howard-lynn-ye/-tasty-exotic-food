## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪排 | Pork chops (bone-in, thick) | 2块 / 2 pieces (~2.5cm厚) |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 生姜末 | Minced ginger | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 米酒/料酒 | Rice wine or Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
混合五香粉、酱油、1汤匙蜂蜜、蒜泥、姜末、米酒和黑胡椒。猪排两面均匀裹满腌料，腌30分钟至2小时。

Mix five-spice, soy, 1 tbsp honey, garlic, ginger, rice wine, and pepper. Coat chops thoroughly, marinate 30 min to 2 hours.

### 2. 煎封 | Sear
铸铁锅大火烧到冒烟，猪排擦干表面下锅，每面煎2分钟至深棕色焦壳（不要动它！）。

Heat cast iron until smoking. Pat chops dry, sear 2 min per side until deep brown crust forms (don't touch!).

### 3. 蜜汁烤制 | Honey Roast
猪排连锅放入烤箱200°C (400°F)烤10分钟。取出刷剩余2汤匙蜂蜜，Broil 3分钟至釉面冒泡。

Transfer skillet to oven at 200°C (400°F), roast 10 min. Remove, brush with remaining 2 tbsp honey. Broil 3 min until glaze bubbles.

### 4. 静置上桌 | Rest & Serve
出炉静置5分钟（这是保汁的关键！）。撒芝麻，搭配蔬菜或米饭。

Rest 5 min out of oven (crucial for juice retention!). Sprinkle sesame, serve with veggies or rice.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **五香粉在猪肉脂肪中的溶解动力学 / Five-spice dissolution kinetics in pork fat:**
> - 五香粉中的肉桂醛、八角茴香脑和丁香酚都是脂溶性芳香分子
> - 猪排的肌间脂肪（IMF）在腌制过程中充当"香料溶剂"，让风味从表面渗透到中心
> - 煎封时表面蛋白质发生美拉德反应（160°C+），生成吡嗪类（烤肉香）和噻唑类（肉香）
> - 蜂蜜的果糖在180°C焦糖化，与五香的芳香分子在鼻后嗅觉中形成"蜜烤叉烧"的复合香
> - 静置5分钟让肌纤维松弛，肌红蛋白重新吸收游离水分——切开不流血=成功
>
> *Five-spice's cinnamaldehyde, anethole, and eugenol are all fat-soluble aromatics. Pork's intramuscular fat (IMF) acts as a "spice solvent" during marination, carrying flavors from surface to center. Searing triggers Maillard at 160°C+, producing pyrazines (roast aroma) and thiazoles (meat aroma). Honey fructose caramelizes at 180°C, combining with five-spice aromatics for a "honey-roast char siu" retro-nasal effect. Resting 5 min lets muscle fibers relax, myoglobin reabsorbs free water — no juice loss on cutting = success.*
