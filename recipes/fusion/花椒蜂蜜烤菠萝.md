This dessert is the perfect BBQ finale — straight from grill to table, one scoop of ice cream, and you've won everyone over.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 菠萝 | Pineapple | ½个切环或切条 / ½, sliced into rings or spears |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 酸橙汁 | Lime juice | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a tiny pinch |
| 椰子油 | Coconut oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp (for grilling) |
| 冰淇淋（可选） | Ice cream (optional) | 1球 / 1 scoop |
| 薄荷叶（可选） | Mint leaves (optional) | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Make Glaze
混合蜂蜜、花椒粉、酸橙汁和盐。

Mix honey, Sichuan pepper, lime juice, and salt.

### 2. 烤菠萝 | Grill Pineapple
烤架或平底锅涂椰子油，大火预热。菠萝片放上去烤3-4分钟至出现焦痕，翻面。

Oil grill or skillet with coconut oil, preheat on high. Place pineapple slices and grill 3–4 minutes until grill marks appear, then flip.

### 3. 刷酱 | Glaze
翻面后刷上花椒蜂蜜酱，再烤2-3分钟至酱汁冒泡焦糖化。

After flipping, brush with the Sichuan honey glaze. Grill 2–3 more minutes until glaze bubbles and caramelizes.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
盘中放烤菠萝，旁边一球香草冰淇淋，撒薄荷叶。热冰搭配，神仙组合。

Plate grilled pineapple, add a scoop of vanilla ice cream beside it, garnish with mint. Hot meets cold — heavenly.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 大火快烤，不要慢炖——要焦痕不要果酱 | High heat, quick cook — you want char marks, not jam |
| 花椒粉只要½茶匙，菠萝已经有很强的味道了 | Only ½ tsp Sichuan pepper — pineapple already has strong flavor |
| 新鲜菠萝比罐头菠萝好一万倍 | Fresh pineapple is 10,000x better than canned |
| 没有烤架用铸铁锅也能出焦痕 | No grill? A cast-iron skillet makes great char marks too |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **烤菠萝为什么比生菠萝好吃 / Why grilled pineapple beats raw:**
> - 高温使菠萝蛋白酶（bromelain）失活——不再"咬嘴"
> - 菠萝含16%糖，在高温下迅速焦糖化
> - 焦糖化产生的呋喃类化合物带来"焦糖+太妃糖"香气
> - 花椒素的"触觉刺激"在甜味背景下格外突出
>
> *High heat deactivates bromelain — no more "tongue bite." Pineapple contains 16% sugar, which caramelizes rapidly at high heat. Caramelization produces furan compounds with "caramel + toffee" aromas. Sanshool's tactile stimulation stands out dramatically against the sweet background.*
