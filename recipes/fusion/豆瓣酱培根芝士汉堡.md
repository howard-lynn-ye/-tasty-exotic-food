# 豆瓣酱培根芝士汉堡 | Doubanjiang Bacon Cheeseburger

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、平底锅、快手、中美混血

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 用四川郫县豆瓣酱替代番茄酱作为汉堡酱料，发酵蚕豆的浓厚鲜味和辣椒的热情让美式汉堡脱胎换骨。培根的烟熏和芝士的奶油包裹住豆瓣酱的霸道，每一口都是成都和纽约的味觉碰撞。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Replace ketchup with Sichuan doubanjiang as the burger condiment — fermented broad bean's intense umami and chili's heat give the American burger a total rebirth. Bacon's smoke and cheese's cream envelop doubanjiang's boldness, making every bite a Chengdu-meets-NYC flavor collision.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛绞肉 | Ground beef (80/20) | 250g / 9 oz |
| 豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang (Pixian preferred) | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 培根 | Bacon strips | 2-3片 / 2-3 strips |
| 切达芝士 | Cheddar cheese slice | 1片 / 1 slice |
| 汉堡面包 | Burger bun | 1个 / 1 |
| 蛋黄酱 | Mayonnaise | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 生菜 | Lettuce | 1-2片 / 1-2 leaves |
| 腌黄瓜片 | Pickle slices | 3-4片 / 3-4 slices |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做豆瓣酱蛋黄酱 | Make Doubanjiang Mayo
蛋黄酱+1汤匙豆瓣酱搅拌均匀（豆瓣酱要先剁细）。这就是你的汉堡酱。

Mix mayo + 1 tbsp doubanjiang (mince the paste first). This is your burger sauce.

### 2. 煎培根和肉饼 | Cook Bacon & Patty
培根煎至酥脆取出。牛肉捏成饼状，表面抹剩余半汤匙豆瓣酱。大火煎3分钟翻面，放芝士片，盖锅盖焖1分钟化芝士。

Fry bacon until crispy, set aside. Shape beef into a patty, rub remaining 1/2 tbsp doubanjiang on top. Sear 3 min over high heat, flip, add cheese, cover for 1 min to melt.

### 3. 烤面包 | Toast the Bun
面包切面朝下放平底锅烤30秒至金黄。

Toast bun cut-side down in the pan for 30 seconds until golden.

### 4. 组装 | Build & Bite
底层面包涂豆瓣蛋黄酱→生菜→肉饼→培根→腌黄瓜→上层面包。用力握紧，大口咬下。

Bottom bun + doubanjiang mayo + lettuce + patty + bacon + pickles + top bun. Grip tight. Take a massive bite.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么豆瓣酱是番茄酱的终极升级 / Why this works:**
> 番茄酱的核心是番茄红素+醋酸+糖——提供酸甜。豆瓣酱的核心是发酵蚕豆的游离氨基酸+辣椒的辣椒素+自然发酵产生的有机酸——提供鲜+辣+酸三维度。豆瓣酱经过6个月以上的日晒发酵（翻、晒、露），产生的风味化合物超过200种，远超番茄酱的加工简单性。培根的烟熏酚类化合物与豆瓣酱的发酵酚类属于同一化学家族，形成"风味和声"。切达芝士的酪蛋白中和辣椒素的刺激，让辣味变得温柔可控。
>
> *Ketchup's core: lycopene + acetic acid + sugar (sweet-sour). Doubanjiang's core: fermented broad bean amino acids + capsaicin + organic acids from natural fermentation (umami + heat + tang — three dimensions). After 6+ months of sun-fermentation, doubanjiang develops 200+ flavor compounds, far exceeding ketchup's processing simplicity. Bacon's smoke phenolics and doubanjiang's fermentation phenolics belong to the same chemical family, creating "flavor harmony." Cheddar's casein neutralizes capsaicin's sting, making the heat gentle and manageable.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 豆瓣酱要先剁碎，大块蚕豆会影响口感 | Mince the doubanjiang first — large bean chunks ruin the texture |
| 用郫县豆瓣酱（Pixian），其他品牌味道差很多 | Use Pixian doubanjiang — other brands lack the depth |
| 肉饼不要压太薄，2cm厚度最佳 | Don't flatten the patty too thin — 2cm thick is ideal |
| 培根要煎到酥脆，和肉饼的嫩形成对比 | Bacon must be crispy — contrasts with the juicy patty |
