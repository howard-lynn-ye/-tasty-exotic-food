> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 奶油芝士的pH约4.5，泡菜的pH约3.5-4.0，两种酸性发酵食品的酸度互补但不冲突。奶油芝士的脂肪包裹泡菜的辣椒素让辣味变得柔和，而泡菜的乳酸菌发酵产物（organic acids）切开奶油芝士的油腻感。贝果的嚼劲（高筋面粉的面筋网络）提供完美的质感载体。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Cream cheese sits at ~pH 4.5, kimchi at ~pH 3.5-4.0 — two fermented foods with complementary acidity. Cream cheese fat wraps kimchi's capsaicin for mellowed heat, while kimchi's organic acids from lactic fermentation cut through the richness. A bagel's chew (from high-gluten flour's protein network) provides the perfect textural vehicle.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 贝果 | Bagel (plain or everything) | 1个 / 1 |
| 奶油芝士 | Cream cheese | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 韩式泡菜 | Kimchi (chopped, drained) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 (可选) | Honey drizzle (optional) | 少许 / a drizzle |
| 黄瓜片 (可选) | Cucumber slices (optional) | 3-4片 / 3-4 slices |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤贝果 | Toast Bagel
贝果切半，烤至表面金黄酥脆。

Slice bagel in half, toast until golden and crispy.

### 2. 涂抹 | Spread
厚厚地涂上奶油芝士，铺上沥干的泡菜碎。

Spread a generous layer of cream cheese, top with drained chopped kimchi.

### 3. 点缀上桌 | Garnish & Serve
撒芝麻，可以淋一点蜂蜜增加甜度对比。加几片黄瓜增加清爽感。

Sprinkle sesame seeds, optionally drizzle honey for sweet contrast. Add cucumber slices for freshness.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 泡菜一定要沥干，否则贝果底部泡软 | Drain kimchi well or the bagel bottom gets soggy |
| Everything bagel + 泡菜是最佳组合 | Everything bagel + kimchi is the ultimate combo |
| 可以提前把泡菜拌入奶油芝士冷藏，早上直接涂 | Mix kimchi into cream cheese the night before for instant mornings |
