# 花椒蜂蜜烤鹌鹑 | Sichuan Honey Quail
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/花椒蜂蜜烤鹌鹑.svg" width="500" alt="花椒蜂蜜烤鹌鹑"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 腌制 2小时 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$10/份(2只) | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、宴客菜

> 花椒的麻与蜂蜜的甜在小巧的鹌鹑身上施展魔法。高温烤制让蜂蜜焦糖化成琥珀色外壳，花椒的麻香渗透到每一寸嫩肉中。这道菜端上桌绝对是满座惊艳的showstopper。
>
> *Sichuan peppercorn's electric tingle and honey's golden sweetness cast their spell on petite quail. High heat caramelizes the honey into an amber shell while the peppercorn's numbing fragrance penetrates every tender morsel. This dish is a guaranteed showstopper at any dinner table.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鹌鹑 (去内脏) | Quail (cleaned) | 4只 / 4 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 花椒 (整粒) | Sichuan peppercorn (whole) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣，拍碎 / 4 cloves, smashed |
| 姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 磨花椒 | Toast & Grind Peppercorn
小火干锅炒花椒2分钟至出香，放凉后用研钵碾碎（不用太细，保留颗粒感）。

Dry-toast peppercorns in a pan over low heat for 2 min until fragrant. Cool, then crush in a mortar (keep it coarse).

### 2. 腌制 | Marinate
碾碎的花椒与酱油、蜂蜜、料酒、蒜、姜、五香粉和芝麻油混合。鹌鹑从背部剪开压平，抹满腌料，冷藏腌2小时。

Mix crushed peppercorns with soy, honey, wine, garlic, ginger, five-spice, and sesame oil. Spatchcock quail by cutting along the backbone, flatten, and coat with marinade. Refrigerate 2 hours.

### 3. 高温烤制 | Roast
预热烤箱220°C。鹌鹑皮面朝上放在烤架上（下面接烤盘），烤15-18分钟。最后3分钟刷一层蜂蜜。

Preheat oven to 220°C/425°F. Place quail skin-up on a rack over a tray. Roast 15-18 min. Brush with extra honey in the last 3 minutes.

### 4. 出盘 | Serve
撒一点现磨花椒盐，配烤蔬菜或沙拉上桌。手抓着吃最过瘾。

Sprinkle fresh Sichuan pepper-salt. Serve with roasted vegetables or salad. Best eaten with your hands.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花椒必须干锅炒过再碾，生花椒味道不够 | Always toast peppercorns before grinding — raw ones lack depth |
| 鹌鹑剪开压平烤，受热均匀又好看 | Spatchcocking ensures even cooking and a beautiful presentation |
| 买不到鹌鹑可以用鸡翅代替，时间加5分钟 | Can't find quail? Use chicken wings, add 5 min cooking time |
| 蜂蜜最后刷，太早会焦黑 | Brush honey at the end — too early and it burns |
