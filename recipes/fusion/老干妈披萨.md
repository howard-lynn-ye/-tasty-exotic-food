## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备底酱 | Prep the Base
烤箱预热230°C (450°F)。在 naan 或面饼上均匀涂抹老干妈，像涂番茄酱一样。

Preheat oven to 230°C (450°F). Spread Lao Gan Ma evenly over naan or dough — treat it like tomato sauce.

### 2. 加料 | Top It
铺芝士，摆上切碎的培根或午餐肉，撒葱花。中间留一个凹槽，打入一个鸡蛋。

Layer cheese, scatter chopped bacon or Spam, sprinkle scallions. Leave a well in the center and crack an egg into it.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
放入烤箱烤10-12分钟，直到芝士起泡、蛋白凝固但蛋黄溏心。

Bake 10–12 minutes until cheese bubbles, egg white sets but yolk stays runny.

### 4. 出炉 | Finish
撒芝麻，切开，让蛋黄流下来——这就是你的酱汁。

Sprinkle sesame seeds, slice, and let the yolk flow — that IS your sauce.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么老干妈能当披萨酱 / Why this works:**
> 传统披萨酱的核心是番茄的谷氨酸（鲜味）+ 橄榄油（脂肪载体）。老干妈完美替代了这两个角色：豆豉提供的谷氨酸含量甚至高于番茄，辣椒油是天然的脂肪载体。加上高温下美拉德反应让豆豉的风味更加复杂，芝士的酪蛋白与辣椒素结合降低辣度——科学上，这比番茄酱披萨的风味层次更多。
>
> *Traditional pizza sauce works via tomato's glutamate (umami) + olive oil (fat carrier). Lao Gan Ma replaces both: fermented black beans have MORE glutamate than tomatoes, and chili oil is a natural fat carrier. High-heat Maillard reactions deepen the bean complexity, while cheese casein binds capsaicin to tame the heat — scientifically, this has MORE flavor layers than a tomato-sauce pizza.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| Naan bread 做底最快，Trader Joe's 有现成的 | Naan bread is the fastest base — TJ's sells ready-made |
