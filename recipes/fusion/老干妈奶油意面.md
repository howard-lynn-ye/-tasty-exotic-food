# 老干妈奶油意面 | Lao Gan Ma Cream Pasta

> 在经典Alfredo白酱意面的基础上加入老干妈——奶油的丝滑与辣椒脆的香辣形成惊艳对比。这不是简单的辣酱拌面，而是一道有层次感的中西融合奶油意面。

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 意面 | Fettuccine or spaghetti | 100g / 1 serving |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 80ml / 1/3 cup |
| 老干妈风味豆豉 | Lao Gan Ma Spicy Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 帕马森芝士 | Parmesan cheese (grated) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣 / 2 cloves |
| 黑胡椒+葱花 | Black pepper + scallion | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮面备料 | Cook Pasta
盐水煮面至 al dente，留半杯面汤。同时切蒜末。

Cook pasta in salted water to al dente. Reserve 1/2 cup pasta water. Mince garlic.

### 2. 炒奶油酱 | Build Cream Sauce
锅中用老干妈自带的油炒蒜末10秒，倒入淡奶油小火煮至微稠，加入老干妈和帕马森芝士搅匀。

Sauté garlic in Lao Gan Ma's oil for 10 sec. Pour in cream, simmer until slightly thickened. Stir in Lao Gan Ma and Parmesan.

### 3. 拌面收汁 | Toss & Finish
加入面条和2-3汤匙面汤，大火快速翻拌至酱汁裹面。盛盘后撒黑胡椒和葱花。

Add pasta and 2-3 tbsp pasta water, toss over high heat until sauce clings. Plate, finish with black pepper and scallions.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

淡奶油中的乳脂肪分子包裹住老干妈中的辣椒素，降低直接辣感的同时延长香辣余韵——这就是为什么辣+奶油永远是完美搭配。帕马森芝士的酪蛋白分解产物提供额外的umami层次，与老干妈的发酵豆豉形成鲜味叠加。

*Cream's milkfat encapsulates capsaicin from Lao Gan Ma, softening the burn while prolonging spicy afterglow — the reason chili + cream is a timeless pairing. Parmesan's casein-derived peptides add another umami layer that stacks with fermented black bean depth.*
