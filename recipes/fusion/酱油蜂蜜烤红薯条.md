## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 红薯 | Sweet potatoes | 2个大 / 2 large (~500g) |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 玉米淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切条裹粉 | Cut & Coat
红薯去皮切成手指粗细条状，用厨房纸擦干。撒入玉米淀粉拌匀（这是酥脆的秘密）。

Peel and cut sweet potatoes into finger-thick sticks. Pat dry. Toss with cornstarch (the secret to crispiness).

### 2. 调味 | Season
加入橄榄油和蒜粉拌匀。单层铺在烤盘上，不要叠放（叠放=蒸而不是烤）。

Toss with olive oil and garlic powder. Spread in a single layer on a sheet pan — no overlapping (overlap = steam, not roast).

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
220°C (425°F) 烤15分钟，翻面，再烤8分钟至外酥内软。

Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 15 min, flip, roast 8 more min until crispy outside, soft inside.

### 4. 酱油蜂蜜釉 | Soy Honey Glaze
小碗中混合酱油和蜂蜜。薯条出炉后立即淋上，快速翻拌。撒芝麻和葱花。

Mix soy and honey in a small bowl. Drizzle over fries immediately after roasting, toss quickly. Top with sesame and scallion.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **玉米淀粉是烤薯条的终极武器 / Cornstarch: the ultimate weapon for baked fries:**
> - 玉米淀粉在红薯表面形成薄薄的"淀粉铠甲"，烤制时脱水形成酥脆外壳
> - 红薯的β-淀粉酶在60-70°C活跃，将淀粉转化为麦芽糖——这就是红薯"越烤越甜"的原因
> - 酱油的氨基酸在200°C以上与麦芽糖发生美拉德反应，产生吡嗪类（坚果香）和呋喃类（焦糖香）
> - 蜂蜜的高粘度确保酱油风味均匀"粘附"在每根薯条表面，不会滑落
>
> *Cornstarch forms a thin "starch armor" on sweet potato surfaces, dehydrating into a crispy shell during roasting. Sweet potato's beta-amylase is active at 60–70°C, converting starch to maltose — why sweet potatoes get "sweeter as they roast." Soy amino acids undergo Maillard with maltose above 200°C, producing pyrazines (nutty) and furans (caramel). Honey's high viscosity ensures soy flavors evenly "adhere" to every fry surface without sliding off.*
