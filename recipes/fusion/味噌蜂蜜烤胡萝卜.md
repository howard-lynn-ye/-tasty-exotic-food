# 味噌蜂蜜烤胡萝卜 | Miso Honey Roasted Carrots

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、烤箱、配菜

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 我用味噌的"发酵鲜"唤醒了胡萝卜沉睡的甜度。胡萝卜含6%天然糖分，在高温烤制时焦糖化——味噌的谷氨酸与这些焦糖化产物共同作用，产生"比胡萝卜本身更像胡萝卜"的浓缩风味。蜂蜜提供额外甜度和釉面光泽。这道菜能让讨厌胡萝卜的人改变信仰。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *I used miso's "fermented umami" to awaken carrots' dormant sweetness. Carrots contain 6% natural sugars that caramelize during roasting — miso's glutamate amplifies these caramelized products, producing a flavor "more carrot than carrot itself." Honey adds extra sweetness and a glossy glaze. This dish converts carrot-haters into believers.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 胡萝卜 | Carrots (whole, medium) | 6根 / 6 (~400g) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 香菜（可选） | Cilantro (optional) | 几枝 / a few sprigs |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调釉酱 | Make Glaze
碗中混合味噌、蜂蜜、橄榄油和米醋，搅拌至顺滑。

Whisk miso, honey, olive oil, and vinegar in a bowl until smooth.

### 2. 裹酱 | Coat Carrots
胡萝卜纵向切半（小的可以整根），放入釉酱中拌匀。

Halve carrots lengthwise (leave small ones whole). Toss in the glaze until coated.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
切面朝下放在烤盘上。200°C (400°F) 烤20分钟。翻面，刷剩余釉酱，再烤5分钟至焦糖色。

Place cut-side down on a sheet pan. Roast at 200°C (400°F) for 20 min. Flip, brush with remaining glaze, roast 5 more min until caramelized.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻和香菜叶。可以配谷物碗、烤肉，或者直接用手拿着吃。

Top with sesame and cilantro. Serve with grain bowls, grilled meat, or eat with your hands.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **味噌如何放大胡萝卜的甜度 / How miso amplifies carrot sweetness:**
> - 胡萝卜含蔗糖(~3.6%)和葡萄糖(~1%)，在180°C以上开始焦糖化
> - 味噌的谷氨酸(~200mg/100g)激活鲜味受体，大脑将"鲜"信号解读为"更浓郁的甜"——这叫"跨模态增强"
> - 胡萝卜的β-胡萝卜素是脂溶性的，橄榄油确保烤制中的吸收率提升6倍
> - 米醋的微量乙酸提供酸度对比，让甜味更加突出（"酸衬甜"原理）
>
> *Carrots contain sucrose (~3.6%) and glucose (~1%), caramelizing above 180°C. Miso's glutamate (~200mg/100g) activates umami receptors — the brain interprets "umami" as "richer sweetness," called "cross-modal enhancement." Carrot beta-carotene is fat-soluble; olive oil boosts absorption 6x during roasting. Rice vinegar's trace acetic acid provides contrast, making sweetness pop ("sour frames sweet" principle).*
