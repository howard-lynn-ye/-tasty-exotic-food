> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 在焦糖烤桃这个经典甜品里加入一点酱油，就像给桃子穿上了"鲜味大衣"。酱油的谷氨酸和桃子自身的有机酸产生"鲜酸互动"，让甜度感知增加30%以上。焦糖化的红糖在桃子凹陷处形成琥珀色的"糖湖"，一勺下去带着温热的桃汁和丝滑的冰淇淋——这是夏天最好的结束方式。

> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Adding a touch of soy sauce to caramel roasted peach dresses the fruit in an "umami coat." Soy sauce's glutamate and peach's organic acids create a "savory-acid interaction" that increases perceived sweetness by over 30%. Caramelized brown sugar forms an amber "sugar lake" in the peach cavity — one spoonful with warm peach juice and silky ice cream is the best way to end a summer evening.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 桃子（成熟） | Ripe peaches | 3个（对半切去核）/ 3 (halved, pitted) |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Cinnamon | ¼茶匙 / ¼ tsp |
| 香草冰淇淋 | Vanilla ice cream | 适量 / for serving |
| 薄荷叶（可选） | Mint (optional) | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调焦糖酱 | Make Caramel Sauce
小锅融化黄油，加入红糖搅至溶化冒泡。加入酱油和肉桂粉搅匀，离火。

Melt butter in a small pan, add brown sugar and stir until dissolved and bubbling. Add soy sauce and cinnamon, stir, remove from heat.

### 2. 涂酱 | Coat Peaches
桃子切面朝上放烤盘。用刷子或勺子将焦糖酱涂在桃子切面上，特别是凹陷处。

Place peach halves cut-side up on a baking sheet. Brush or spoon caramel sauce over cut surfaces, especially in the cavities.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱 200°C (400°F) 烤 12-15分钟至桃子软化、边缘微焦。

Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 12–15 minutes until peaches soften and edges caramelize slightly.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
每半个桃子配一球冰淇淋，淋上烤盘中的焦糖汁，放薄荷叶。

Pair each peach half with a scoop of ice cream, drizzle with pan juices, garnish with mint.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 酱油只要1茶匙！它是"隐形增味"不是主角 | Only 1 tsp soy sauce — it's an "invisible enhancer," not the star |
| 桃子要熟但不能烂，捏起来微软最佳 | Peaches should be ripe but firm — slightly soft to the touch |
| 烤盘里的焦糖汁别浪费，全部淋到盘子里 | Don't waste pan juices — drizzle every drop onto the plate |
| 夏天用烤架也很好，加烟熏味 | In summer, use a grill for smoky notes |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **酱油+甜品的"隐身增味"原理 / Soy sauce's "stealth enhancement" in desserts:**
> - 极少量酱油（<0.5%总重量）不产生酱油风味
> - 但谷氨酸钠作为味觉增强剂，放大甜味和果香
> - 红糖焦糖化产生的太妃糖味和酱油的酱香有共同的呋喃类化合物
> - 桃子含有丰富的有机酸，酸+鲜的组合让甜味感知飙升
>
> *Tiny amounts of soy sauce (<0.5% of total weight) produce no soy flavor, but sodium glutamate acts as a taste enhancer, amplifying sweetness and fruitiness. Brown sugar caramelization and soy sauce share furan compounds (toffee notes). Peaches are rich in organic acids — the acid + umami combination causes sweetness perception to skyrocket.*
