# 枫糖酱油鸡翅 | Maple Soy Wings
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/枫糖酱油鸡翅.svg" width="500" alt="枫糖酱油鸡翅"></p>

> ⏱ 40分钟 | 💰~$8/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、烤箱、派对小食

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 加拿大枫糖浆遇上中式酱油，甜咸交织的焦香鸡翅让东西方味觉在舌尖握手。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Canadian maple syrup meets Chinese soy sauce — sweet-savory caramelized wings where East and West shake hands on your palate.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡翅中 | Chicken wing flats & drumettes | 1kg / 2.2 lb |
| 枫糖浆 | Pure maple syrup | 60ml / ¼ cup |
| 生抽 | Light soy sauce | 45ml / 3 tbsp |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 15g / 3 cloves |
| 苹果醋 | Apple cider vinegar | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds | 10g / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
将鸡翅与枫糖浆、生抽、蒜末、苹果醋混合，冷藏腌制至少30分钟。
Mix wings with maple syrup, soy sauce, garlic, and apple cider vinegar; refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 烘烤 | Roast
烤箱预热200°C/400°F，鸡翅铺在烤架上，烤25-30分钟，中途翻面并刷余下酱汁。
Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F. Arrange wings on a rack, roast 25-30 min, flipping halfway and basting with remaining marinade.

### 3. 收汁装盘 | Glaze & Serve
出炉后撒白芝麻和葱花，趁热上桌。
Remove from oven, sprinkle with sesame seeds and scallions, serve hot.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 枫糖的蔗糖与氨基酸在高温下发生美拉德反应，与酱油中的谷氨酸钠协同增鲜，形成深层焦香。 *Maple's sucrose undergoes Maillard reactions with amino acids at high heat, synergizing with soy's glutamates for deep caramelized umami.*
