> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 味噌焦糖是精品咖啡界的隐藏宝藏。白味噌中的谷氨酸与焦糖的蔗糖热解产物（diacetyl，奶油味的来源）结合，创造出比普通焦糖深邃十倍的风味。味噌的咸鲜味抑制咖啡的苦涩，同时放大焦糖的甜感——这就是为什么日本的Blue Bottle Coffee会推出味噌拿铁的科学原因。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Miso caramel is specialty coffee's hidden gem. White miso's glutamate combines with caramel's sucrose pyrolysis products (diacetyl, the source of buttery flavor) to create something ten times deeper than plain caramel. Miso's salty umami suppresses coffee's bitterness while amplifying caramel's sweetness — the scientific reason Japanese Blue Bottle Coffee introduced a miso latte.*

|------|-----------|---------------|
| 浓缩咖啡 | Espresso (or strong coffee) | 1杯 / 1 shot |
| 牛奶 | Milk | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| **味噌焦糖酱:** | **Miso Caramel Sauce:** | |
| 砂糖 | Sugar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做味噌焦糖 | Make Miso Caramel
小锅中火熬糖至琥珀色。离火加黄油搅匀（会冒泡），加淡奶油搅顺滑，最后拌入味噌。可一次多做，冰箱存两周。

Heat sugar in a small pan to amber. Off heat, stir in butter (it will bubble), add cream and stir smooth, then whisk in miso. Make a batch — keeps 2 weeks refrigerated.

### 2. 打奶泡 | Froth Milk
牛奶加热并打出泡沫。

Heat milk and froth until foamy.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
杯底加2汤匙味噌焦糖酱，倒入浓缩咖啡搅匀，缓缓加入奶泡。最上面淋一点焦糖酱画花。

Add 2 tbsp miso caramel to the mug, pour in espresso and stir. Slowly add frothed milk. Drizzle extra caramel on top for art.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 焦糖不要熬太深，浅琥珀色最佳 | Don't over-caramelize — light amber is the sweet spot |
| 味噌在最后加，温度太高会破坏发酵风味 | Add miso last — high heat destroys fermentation flavors |
| 焦糖酱多做一些存冰箱，随时做拿铁 | Batch the caramel sauce and refrigerate for instant lattes anytime |
