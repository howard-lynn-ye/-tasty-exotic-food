# 酱油蜂蜜烤蘑菇串 | Soy Honey Mushroom Skewers

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪15分 | 💰~$7/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、素食、烧烤

> **💡 灵感** — 酱油的鲜和蜂蜜的甜在烤制中产生美拉德反应，让蘑菇拥有媲美肉类的焦香口感。简单三步，素食也能是烧烤主角。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Soy sauce umami and honey sweetness trigger a Maillard reaction during grilling, giving mushrooms a meaty, caramelized crust. Three simple steps — proof that vegetables can steal the BBQ spotlight.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大口蘑 | Cremini mushrooms | 300g / 10 oz |
| 杏鲍菇 | King oyster mushroom | 2根 / 2 large |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 25ml / 1½ tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves (minced) |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 / To taste |
| 葱花 | Scallion greens | 适量 / For garnish |
| 竹签 | Bamboo skewers | 8根 / 8 pcs |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调酱 | Mix Glaze
酱油、蜂蜜、蒜末和橄榄油搅匀成蜜汁酱。Whisk soy sauce, honey, minced garlic, and olive oil into a glaze.

### 2. 穿串 | Skewer
口蘑整颗穿串，杏鲍菇切厚片穿串，刷满蜜汁酱，腌5分钟。Thread whole cremini and thick-sliced king oyster onto skewers, brush generously with glaze, rest 5 minutes.

### 3. 烤制 | Grill
烤架或烤箱220°C烤12-15分钟，中途翻面并再刷一次酱，烤至菇面焦褐起泡。Grill or broil at 425°F for 12-15 minutes, flip and re-glaze halfway, until mushrooms are browned and bubbling.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒黑胡椒和葱花，趁热食用。可搭配米饭或作为烧烤配菜。Sprinkle with black pepper and scallion greens, serve hot. Great with rice or as a BBQ side.
