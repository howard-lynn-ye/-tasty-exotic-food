# 辣椒蜂蜜黄油烤饼 Chili Honey Butter Biscuit

> 美国南方 x 东亚辣味 | 早餐/小食
> American South x East Asian Heat | Breakfast / Snack

---

## 简介 Introduction

美国南方烤饼（Biscuit）是早餐文化的基石——外皮酥脆、内部松软如云朵、层层分明。
在它上面涂抹辣椒蜂蜜黄油，咸甜辣三味在温热的面饼上交融。蜂蜜黄油是南方
经典，加入韩式辣椒粉（gochugaru）或中式辣椒油后，温柔的甜蜜中多了一丝
挑逗的辛辣，让人一块接一块停不下来。

The Southern American biscuit is a breakfast cornerstone — crispy shell,
cloud-soft interior, visible flaky layers. Spread with chili honey butter,
salt-sweet-heat meld on the warm bread. Honey butter is a Southern classic;
adding Korean gochugaru or Chinese chili oil brings a teasing heat to the
gentle sweetness. You will not stop at one.

## 食材 Ingredients

### 烤饼 Biscuit Dough
| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 300g |
| 泡打粉 Baking powder | 15g |
| 盐 Salt | 5g |
| 白砂糖 Sugar | 10g |
| 冷黄油（切丁）Cold butter (cubed) | 100g |
| 冷牛奶 Cold buttermilk | 180ml |

### 辣椒蜂蜜黄油 Chili Honey Butter
| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 软化黄油 Softened butter | 100g |
| 蜂蜜 Honey | 40ml |
| 韩式辣椒粉 Gochugaru | 8g |
| 海盐 Sea salt | 2g |
| 辣椒油（可选）Chili oil | 5ml |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **混合干料 Mix dry**: 面粉、泡打粉、盐、糖过筛混合入大碗。
   Sift flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar into a large bowl.

2. **切黄油 Cut butter**: 冷黄油丁加入干料，用手或叉子搓成粗粒状（保留豌豆大小黄油块）。
   Add cold butter cubes, rub with fingers or fork into coarse meal (keep pea-sized pieces).

3. **加奶 Add milk**: 倒入冷牛奶，用刮刀快速翻拌至刚好成团（不要过度揉捏）。
   Pour cold buttermilk, fold with spatula until just combined (do NOT overwork).

4. **叠层 Laminate**: 面团压扁，折叠三次（像折信纸），每次轻轻擀平再折叠。
   Pat flat, fold in thirds (like a letter), gently roll out and fold 3 times total.

5. **切形 Cut**: 擀至2.5cm厚，用圆形切模直上直下切出饼坯（不要扭转）。
   Roll to 2.5cm thick, cut with round cutter pressing straight down (no twisting).

6. **烤制 Bake**: 烤箱220°C，饼坯紧挨排列在烤盘中，烤12-15分钟至金黄高耸。
   Bake at 220°C, place biscuits touching in pan, bake 12-15 min until tall and golden.

7. **做辣蜜油 Make chili butter**: 软黄油、蜂蜜、辣椒粉、盐和辣椒油搅打至蓬松。
   Whip soft butter, honey, gochugaru, salt, and chili oil until fluffy.

8. **涂抹 Serve**: 热饼横切开，涂上厚厚的辣椒蜂蜜黄油，立即享用。
   Split hot biscuit, spread generous chili honey butter inside, eat immediately.
