## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 白鱼排（鳕鱼/鲈鱼） | White fish fillet (cod/sea bass) | 2片 (~300g) |
| 黄油（软化） | Butter (softened) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 辣椒粉 | Chili flakes | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | ½个（汁+装饰）/ ½ (juice + garnish) |
| 盐 | Salt | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 / to taste |
| 欧芹（可选） | Parsley (optional) | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做辣椒蜂蜜黄油 | Make Chili Honey Butter
软化黄油中加入蜂蜜、辣椒粉、蒜泥和一半柠檬汁，搅拌均匀。

Mix softened butter with honey, chili flakes, garlic, and half the lemon juice until combined.

### 2. 准备鱼 | Prep Fish
鱼排擦干，两面撒盐和黑胡椒。放在铺了锡纸的烤盘上。

Pat fish dry, season both sides with salt and black pepper. Place on a foil-lined baking sheet.

### 3. 涂酱烤制 | Butter & Bake
鱼排上面均匀涂上辣椒蜂蜜黄油。烤箱 200°C (400°F) 烤 12-15分钟至鱼肉用叉子轻松拨开。

Spread chili honey butter generously over the fish. Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 12–15 minutes until fish flakes easily with a fork.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
挤剩余柠檬汁，撒欧芹。配白米饭或烤蔬菜。

Squeeze remaining lemon juice, sprinkle parsley. Serve with white rice or roasted vegetables.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鱼排一定擦干表面水分，否则黄油滑不住 | Pat fish bone-dry — otherwise butter slides off |
| 不要烤过头！鱼一变白就差不多了 | Don't overcook! Fish is done when it turns opaque |
| 辣度可以调节：怕辣就用½茶匙辣椒粉 | Adjust heat: use ½ tsp chili flakes if sensitive |
| 烤鱼出的汤汁可以浇在米饭上 | Pan juices from the fish are liquid gold — pour over rice |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **黄油为什么是鱼的最佳拍档 / Why butter is fish's best partner:**
> - 黄油中的丁酸（butyric acid）放大鱼肉的鲜甜风味
> - 黄油脂肪包裹辣椒素，使辣感柔和、持续、温暖
> - 蜂蜜的果糖在鱼肉表面美拉德反应产生焦香壳
> - 柠檬酸切割黄油的油腻感，让口感保持清爽
>
> *Butter's butyric acid amplifies fish's sweet-savory flavor. Butter fat wraps capsaicin, making heat gentle, sustained, and warm. Honey's fructose undergoes Maillard reaction on fish surface creating a fragrant crust. Citric acid from lemon cuts through butter's richness, keeping the palate refreshed.*
