> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 杏子的酸甜多汁遇上五香粉的温暖复合香气，在烤箱里变成一道令人意外的优雅甜品。五香粉中的八角和肉桂与杏子的果酸"共振"，产生类似香料苹果酒（mulled cider）的芳香；蜂蜜在杏子凹陷处形成小小的焦糖池塘。配一球冰淇淋或一块奶酪，这是水果甜品的天花板。

> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Apricot's tart-sweet juiciness meets five-spice's warm complex aroma, transforming in the oven into an unexpectedly elegant dessert. Star anise and cinnamon in five-spice "resonate" with apricot's fruit acids, producing aromatics similar to mulled cider; honey forms tiny caramel ponds in apricot cavities. With a scoop of ice cream or a piece of cheese, this is the ceiling of fruit desserts.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 新鲜杏子 | Fresh apricots | 6个（对半切去核）/ 6 (halved, pitted) |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | ¼茶匙 / ¼ tsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a tiny pinch |
| 希腊酸奶或冰淇淋 | Greek yogurt or ice cream | 适量 / for serving |
| 碎开心果（可选） | Crushed pistachios (optional) | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调蜜汁 | Make Glaze
碗中混合融化的黄油、蜂蜜、五香粉、柠檬汁和盐。

Mix melted butter, honey, five-spice, lemon juice, and salt.

### 2. 涂酱 | Coat Apricots
杏子切面朝上放在烤盘上。用刷子涂上蜜汁，特别注意凹陷处。

Place apricot halves cut-side up on a baking sheet. Brush with glaze, paying attention to the cavities.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱 200°C (400°F) 烤 12-15分钟至杏子软化、边缘焦糖化。

Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 12–15 minutes until apricots soften and edges caramelize.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
盘中放杏子，旁边一勺希腊酸奶或冰淇淋，撒碎开心果。淋烤盘里的汤汁。

Plate apricots, add a dollop of Greek yogurt or ice cream, sprinkle crushed pistachios. Drizzle pan juices.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 五香粉只要¼茶匙！杏子味道细腻，多了会被盖住 | Only ¼ tsp five-spice — apricot's delicate flavor gets buried easily |
| 杏子要成熟但不软烂，否则烤完变果酱 | Apricots should be ripe but firm — too soft = jam after roasting |
| 搭配咸味奶酪（如 ricotta + 盐）也很绝 | Pairing with salty cheese (like salted ricotta) is also amazing |
| 夏天杏子季很短，趁新鲜赶紧做 | Apricot season is short — make this while they're fresh |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **五香粉为什么和石头水果（stone fruit）搭 / Why five-spice pairs with stone fruits:**
> - 杏子含丰富的柠檬酸和苹果酸，和肉桂/八角的芳香化合物"酸甜共振"
> - 八角中的茴香脑和杏子中的苯甲醛（杏仁味）是近亲分子
> - 热烤使杏子释放更多挥发性酯类（水果香气的来源）
> - 蜂蜜焦糖化产生的呋喃类和五香粉的木质调和谐共存
>
> *Apricots are rich in citric and malic acids, creating "sweet-sour resonance" with cinnamon/star anise aromatics. Star anise's anethole and apricot's benzaldehyde (almond flavor) are molecular relatives. Roasting releases more volatile esters from apricots (source of fruity aroma). Honey caramelization's furans coexist harmoniously with five-spice's woody notes.*
