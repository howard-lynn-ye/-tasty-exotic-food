# 泡菜芝士烤年糕 | Kimchi Cheese Tteokbokki

> ⏱ 准备5分+烹饪20分 | 💰~$8/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、韩式、小食

> **💡 灵感** — 韩式辣炒年糕遇上烤芝士，泡菜的酸辣与芝士的浓郁拉丝让这道街头小吃升级为治愈系烤箱料理。用烤代替炒，更简单也更有焦香层次。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Korean tteokbokki meets baked cheese — kimchi's sour heat and stretchy cheese elevate this street snack into comforting oven fare. Baking instead of stir-frying is simpler and adds crispy layers.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 韩式年糕 | Tteok (rice cakes) | 300g / 10 oz |
| 韩国泡菜 | Kimchi | 150g / 5 oz |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella | 120g / 1 cup (shredded) |
| 韩式辣酱 | Gochujang | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 番茄酱 | Ketchup | 15g / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 10g / 2 tsp |
| 鱼饼 | Fish cake | 100g / 3.5 oz (optional) |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 水 | Water | 150ml / ⅔ cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮年糕 | Soften Rice Cakes
年糕用热水泡软（约5分钟），沥干。如用冷冻年糕，煮2分钟即可。Soak rice cakes in hot water until soft (~5 min), drain. If frozen, boil 2 minutes.

### 2. 调酱拌匀 | Mix Sauce
辣酱、番茄酱、糖和水搅匀，倒入烤盘，加入年糕、泡菜碎和鱼饼片，翻拌均匀。Whisk gochujang, ketchup, sugar, and water; pour into baking dish, add rice cakes, chopped kimchi, and sliced fish cake, toss to coat.

### 3. 铺芝士烤 | Top & Bake
表面铺满马苏里拉芝士，烤箱200°C烤15-18分钟至芝士金黄起泡，年糕酱汁浓稠。Cover with mozzarella, bake at 400°F for 15-18 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly, sauce is thick.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
出炉撒葱花，趁热用筷子拉丝享用。配米饭或直接当主食。Garnish with scallions, enjoy the cheese-pull while hot. Serve with rice or eat as a main.
