# 川味牛油果吐司 | Sichuan Avocado Toast (AI Original)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/川味牛油果吐司.svg" width="500" alt="川味牛油果吐司"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 3分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、早餐、全超市可买、素食

> 当硅谷的牛油果吐司遇上四川的辣椒脆——两个世界的碰撞，竟然天作之合。奶油般的牛油果搭配酥脆麻辣的辣椒油，再撒上花椒盐和香菜，这是一道让人重新定义brunch的创意料理。
>
> *When Silicon Valley's avocado toast meets Sichuan chili crisp — two worlds collide in perfect harmony. Creamy avocado paired with crunchy, numbing-spicy chili oil, finished with Sichuan pepper salt and cilantro. This is the dish that redefines brunch.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛油果 | Ripe avocado | 1个 / 1 |
| 吐司面包 | Sourdough bread (or any good bread) | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 辣椒脆 (老干妈或类似) | Chili crisp (Lao Gan Ma or similar) | 1-2汤匙 / 1-2 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 少许 / a small handful |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 少许 / a pinch |
| 青柠 (或柠檬) | Lime (or lemon) | 半个 / 1/2 |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt | 少许 / a pinch |
| 鸡蛋 (可选) | Egg (optional, fried or poached) | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤吐司 | Toast Bread
吐司烤至金黄酥脆。

Toast bread until golden and crispy.

### 2. 压牛油果 | Mash Avocado
牛油果切开去核，用叉子压碎（不用太细，保留一些颗粒感）。挤入青柠汁，加少许盐拌匀。

Halve and pit the avocado. Mash with a fork (keep it chunky). Squeeze in lime juice and a pinch of salt, mix.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
牛油果泥铺在吐司上。淋上辣椒脆，滴几滴酱油。撒花椒粉、芝麻和香菜。加一个煎蛋（可选）。撒上海盐片。

Spread avocado on toast. Drizzle chili crisp over it, then a few drops of soy sauce. Sprinkle Sichuan pepper, sesame seeds, and cilantro. Add a fried egg if desired. Finish with flaky salt.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 牛油果要选熟透的，按压微软即可 | Choose ripe avocados — they should yield slightly when pressed |
| 辣椒脆要舀底部的渣，不要只倒油 | Scoop the chunky bits from the bottom of the chili crisp jar |
| 花椒粉只要一点点，提味不麻嘴 | Just a tiny bit of Sichuan pepper — for flavor, not numbness |
| 酱油只需几滴，提鲜不抢味 | Just a few drops of soy sauce — for umami, not saltiness |
