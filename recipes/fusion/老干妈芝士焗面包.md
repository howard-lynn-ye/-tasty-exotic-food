# 老干妈芝士焗面包 Lao Gan Ma Cheese Bread

> 贵州 x 法国 | 早餐/小食
> Guizhou x France | Breakfast / Snack

---

## 简介 Introduction

老干妈辣椒酱是中国的"国民女神"调味料，而法式焗面包（Croque）则是巴黎咖啡馆
的经典。两者的结合听起来疯狂，味道却令人上瘾——老干妈的豆豉辣香与浓郁的
芝士在烤箱中融合，面包吸收了辣油后变得外酥里软。这是一道让中国留学生在
海外泪目、让外国朋友连呼"amazing"的终极融合小食。

Lao Gan Ma chili sauce is China's "national goddess" condiment, while the
French croque is a Parisian cafe staple. The combo sounds crazy but is
addictive — LGM's fermented black bean-chili punch merges with rich melted
cheese in the oven, and the bread absorbs chili oil to become crispy outside
and soft within. The ultimate fusion snack.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 厚切吐司 Thick-cut bread | 4片 / 4 slices |
| 老干妈风味豆豉 LGM fermented black bean chili | 40g |
| 马苏里拉芝士碎 Shredded mozzarella | 150g |
| 切达芝士片 Cheddar slices | 4片 / 4 |
| 黄油 Butter | 30g |
| 鸡蛋 Egg | 2个 / 2 |
| 葱花 Chopped scallion | 20g |
| 白芝麻 White sesame | 5g |
| 蒜末 Minced garlic | 5g |
| 黑胡椒 Black pepper | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **预热 Preheat**: 烤箱预热200°C。
   Preheat oven to 200°C.

2. **做辣油酱 Make LGM butter**: 软化黄油与老干妈、蒜末混合搅匀。
   Mix softened butter with LGM sauce and minced garlic.

3. **涂抹 Spread**: 吐司两面均匀涂上辣油黄油混合酱。
   Spread LGM butter evenly on both sides of each bread slice.

4. **铺芝士 Layer cheese**: 放上切达芝士片，再铺上厚厚一层马苏里拉碎。
   Place cheddar slices, then pile a generous layer of shredded mozzarella.

5. **加蛋 Add egg**: 在芝士中间按出凹槽，打入一个鸡蛋（每两片面包一个蛋）。
   Make a well in the cheese, crack an egg into it (one egg per two slices).

6. **烤制 Bake**: 200°C烤12-15分钟，至芝士起泡焦黄、蛋白凝固蛋黄微流。
   Bake 12-15 min at 200°C until cheese bubbles golden, whites set, yolks runny.

7. **装饰 Garnish**: 出炉撒葱花、白芝麻和黑胡椒，趁热享用。
   Top with scallion, sesame, and black pepper. Eat immediately while hot.
