## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 韩式年糕条 | Korean rice cake sticks (tteok) | 250g |
| 老干妈风味豆豉 | Lao Gan Ma Spicy Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 马苏里拉芝士碎 | Shredded mozzarella | 100g |
| 切达芝士碎 | Shredded cheddar | 50g |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣，切末 / 2 cloves, minced |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮年糕 | Cook Rice Cakes
年糕条放入沸水煮3-4分钟至变软但仍有嚼劲，沥干。

Boil rice cake sticks for 3-4 min until softened but still chewy. Drain.

### 2. 炒年糕 | Stir-Fry
锅中加少许油，爆香蒜末，加入老干妈、酱油和糖翻炒。放入年糕条大火翻拌均匀。

Heat a little oil, sizzle garlic, add Lao Gan Ma, soy sauce, and sugar. Toss in rice cakes and stir-fry over high heat until evenly coated.

### 3. 焗芝士 | Broil with Cheese
年糕转入耐热烤盘，铺上马苏里拉和切达芝士碎。200°C烤箱上火烤5-7分钟至芝士金黄冒泡。

Transfer to an oven-safe dish, top with mozzarella and cheddar. Broil at 200°C/400°F for 5-7 min until cheese is golden and bubbly.

### 4. 出盘 | Serve
出炉撒葱花和芝麻，趁热趁拉丝赶紧吃。

Sprinkle scallions and sesame right out of the oven. Eat while the cheese still pulls.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 年糕不要煮太软，后面还要烤，过软会糊 | Don't over-boil the rice cakes — they'll continue cooking in the oven |
| 两种芝士混合：马苏拉丝，切达增味 | Two cheeses: mozzarella for stretch, cheddar for flavor |
| 烤的时候盯着看，芝士从金黄到焦黑只要30秒 | Watch the broiler closely — cheese goes from golden to burnt in 30 seconds |
| 冷冻年糕直接用，不用解冻 | Frozen rice cakes can go straight into boiling water — no thawing needed |
