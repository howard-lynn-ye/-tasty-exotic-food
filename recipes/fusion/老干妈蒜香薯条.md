# 老干妈蒜香薯条 | LGM Garlic Fries

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、零食、派对

> 美式薯条遇上中国辣酱之王——老干妈。炸得金黄酥脆的薯条裹上蒜香老干妈油，辣椒碎和豆豉的颗粒感配上薯条的酥脆，这是炸薯条的终极升级方案。
>
> *American fries meet the queen of Chinese chili sauce — Lao Gan Ma. Golden crispy fries tossed in garlicky chili crisp oil, the crunch of chili flakes and fermented black beans on top of crunchy fries — this is the ultimate fry upgrade.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大土豆 | Large russet potato | 3个 / 3 |
| 老干妈风味豆豉 | Lao Gan Ma Spicy Chili Crisp | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 5瓣，切末 / 5 cloves, minced |
| 植物油 (炸用) | Vegetable oil (for frying) | 适量 / as needed |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 2根，切花 / 2 stalks, chopped |
| 香菜 (可选) | Cilantro (optional) | 一小把 / a small bunch |
| 帕玛森芝士粉 (可选) | Parmesan (optional) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切条泡水 | Cut & Soak
土豆去皮切成1cm粗的条，冷水浸泡20分钟去掉多余淀粉，沥干拍干。

Peel and cut potatoes into 1cm-thick sticks. Soak in cold water 20 min to remove excess starch. Drain and pat completely dry.

### 2. 炸薯条 | Fry
油温170°C先炸5分钟至微黄，捞出。升温至190°C复炸2-3分钟至金黄酥脆。

Fry at 170°C/340°F for 5 min until pale gold, remove. Raise to 190°C/375°F and fry again 2-3 min until golden and crispy.

### 3. 炒蒜酱 | Make Garlic-LGM Sauce
另起小锅，1汤匙油爆香蒜末至金黄，加入老干妈翻炒30秒，关火。

In a small pan, sizzle garlic in 1 tbsp oil until golden. Add Lao Gan Ma, stir 30 seconds, remove from heat.

### 4. 拌匀出盘 | Toss & Serve
炸好的薯条倒入大碗，浇上蒜香老干妈酱，撒盐、葱花、香菜和帕玛森，大力颠几下拌匀。

Dump hot fries into a large bowl. Pour over the garlic-LGM sauce, add salt, scallions, cilantro, and Parmesan. Toss vigorously.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 薯条必须完全拍干再炸，否则油会炸锅 | Fries must be bone-dry before frying or the oil will splatter violently |
| 两次油炸法是酥脆的关键 | Double-frying is the secret to crunchiness |
| 没有油炸锅可以用烤箱220°C烤25分钟代替 | No fryer? Oven-bake at 220°C/425°F for 25 min instead |
| 出锅立刻拌酱，趁热吃最好 | Toss sauce immediately while hot — eat right away |
