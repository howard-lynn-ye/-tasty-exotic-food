> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 希腊酸奶的乳酸菌发酵产生的酸味中和了老干妈的油腻感，同时酸奶的高蛋白质含量作为乳化剂，让辣椒油和水相完美融合成丝滑质地。这是用发酵对发酵（酸奶+豆豉）的策略——两种发酵食品的氨基酸叠加出超越单一食材的鲜味深度。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Greek yogurt's lactic acid cuts through Lao Gan Ma's oiliness, while yogurt's high protein acts as an emulsifier, blending chili oil and water phase into a silky texture. This is a ferment-meets-ferment strategy (yogurt + fermented black bean) — amino acids from two fermented foods create umami depth beyond either alone.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 希腊酸奶 | Greek yogurt (plain) | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 混合 | Combine
碗中放酸奶、老干妈、柠檬汁和蜂蜜，充分搅拌至均匀。

Stir yogurt, Lao Gan Ma, lemon juice, and honey in a bowl until well combined.

### 2. 调味 | Adjust
尝一下，按需加盐或多加点老干妈调辣度。

Taste and adjust — add salt or more Lao Gan Ma for extra heat.

### 3. 使用 | Use
拌沙拉、蘸蔬菜棒、涂三明治、浇烤鸡——万能百搭。冰箱保存一周。

Use on salads, as a veggie dip, sandwich spread, or drizzle over roast chicken — incredibly versatile. Keeps 1 week refrigerated.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用全脂希腊酸奶，质地最浓稠 | Full-fat Greek yogurt gives the thickest, creamiest texture |
| 想要更顺滑可以用搅拌机打 | Blend for an even smoother consistency |
| 放置30分钟后风味融合更好 | Flavor melds better after 30 min in the fridge |
