# 辣椒蜂蜜黄油烤饼干 | Chili Honey Butter Biscuit

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/批(约8个) | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、烘焙、南方风味

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **饼干** | **Biscuits** | |
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 250g |
| 泡打粉 | Baking powder | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 冰黄油 | Cold unsalted butter | 85g，切小丁 / 85g, cubed small |
| 酪乳 | Buttermilk | 160ml，冰的 / 160ml, cold |
| **辣椒蜂蜜黄油** | **Chili Honey Butter** | |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 融化黄油 | Melted butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 辣椒片 (红辣椒碎) | Red pepper flakes | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 混合干料 | Mix Dry
面粉、泡打粉、盐和糖拌匀。加入冰黄油丁，用手指或切刀搓成豌豆大小的颗粒（保留黄油块感）。

Mix flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar. Cut in cold butter with fingers or a pastry cutter until pea-sized (keep it chunky).

### 2. 加酪乳 | Add Buttermilk
倒入冰酪乳，用叉子拌至刚好成团。倒在撒了面粉的台面上，折叠3次（制造层次），压至2.5cm厚，切出圆形。

Pour in cold buttermilk, stir with a fork until just combined. Turn onto floured surface, fold 3 times (for layers), pat to 2.5cm thick, cut rounds.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
预热220°C。饼干紧挨着放在烤盘中（互相支撑会长得更高）。烤12-15分钟至顶部金黄。

Preheat 220°C/425°F. Place biscuits snugly together on a tray (they support each other and rise taller). Bake 12-15 min until tops are golden.

### 4. 刷辣蜜黄油 | Brush Hot Honey Butter
蜂蜜、融化黄油和辣椒片混合。饼干出炉立刻刷上，撒海盐。趁热吃。

Mix honey, melted butter, and pepper flakes. Brush on biscuits immediately out of the oven, sprinkle sea salt. Eat hot.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 所有材料必须冰冷，黄油一化饼干就不酥了 | Everything must be ice cold — warm butter = flat biscuits |
| 面团不要揉，只折叠，揉多了出筋会变硬 | Don't knead, just fold — overworking develops gluten and toughness |
| 没有酪乳可以用牛奶加1茶匙柠檬汁代替 | No buttermilk? Add 1 tsp lemon juice to regular milk |
| 辣度可以用红辣椒碎的量来调节 | Adjust heat by varying the amount of pepper flakes |
