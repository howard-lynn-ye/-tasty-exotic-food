# 花椒柠檬水 | Sichuan Pepper Lemonade

> ⏱ 10分钟 + 冷却 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、饮品、夏日、无酒精

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 花椒的麻+柠檬的酸+糖的甜，三种极端味觉在一杯水里同时爆发。花椒让你的嘴唇微微发麻，然后柠檬的酸味在这个"震动"的舌头上变得异常鲜明——这是一杯会让你嘴巴"触电"的柠檬水。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Sichuan pepper's numbing tingle + lemon's sour + sugar's sweet — three extreme tastes detonate simultaneously in one glass. Peppercorn makes your lips buzz, then lemon's acidity becomes extraordinarily vivid on the "vibrating" tongue — a lemonade that electrifies your mouth.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 花椒 | Whole Sichuan peppercorns | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemons | 2个 / 2 |
| 糖 | Sugar | 60g / 1/4 cup |
| 水 | Water | 3杯 / 3 cups (720ml) |
| 蜂蜜 (可选) | Honey (optional) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 冰块 | Ice cubes | 适量 / plenty |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮花椒糖浆 | Make Peppercorn Syrup
1杯水+糖+花椒中火煮至糖溶化后小火焖3分钟。关火，花椒浸泡10分钟后捞出。冷却。

Simmer 1 cup water + sugar + peppercorns over medium heat until sugar dissolves, then low heat 3 min. Remove from heat, steep peppercorns 10 min, then strain out. Cool completely.

### 2. 榨柠檬汁 | Juice the Lemons
2个柠檬榨汁，去籽。约得80ml柠檬汁。

Juice both lemons, remove seeds. Should yield about 80ml of juice.

### 3. 混合 | Combine
花椒糖浆+柠檬汁+剩余2杯冷水混合搅匀。尝味道——想更甜加蜂蜜，想更麻可以多浸花椒。

Combine peppercorn syrup + lemon juice + remaining 2 cups cold water. Taste — add honey for more sweetness, or steep peppercorns longer for more tingle.

### 4. 倒杯加冰 | Serve Over Ice
杯中装满冰块，倒入花椒柠檬水。可用柠檬片和几粒花椒装饰。

Fill glasses with ice, pour lemonade. Garnish with lemon slices and a few peppercorns.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么麻+酸+甜能让大脑短路 / Why this works:**
> 花椒素（sanshool）激活触觉神经产生50Hz振动感，这种"物理刺激"让味蕾进入"高敏模式"。在这个状态下，柠檬的柠檬酸对酸味受体的激活信号被放大2-3倍——你会觉得柠檬特别酸特别新鲜。糖的甜味则充当"安全网"，防止酸和麻的刺激过于激烈。三种感觉形成一个自我平衡的三角形，大脑在"麻-酸-甜"之间快速切换，产生持续的新鲜感和兴奋感。
>
> *Sanshool activates tactile nerves at ~50Hz vibration, putting taste buds into "heightened sensitivity mode." In this state, citric acid's activation of sour receptors is amplified 2-3x — lemon tastes extraordinarily sour and fresh. Sugar's sweetness acts as a "safety net" preventing the sour-numbing combo from overwhelming. Three sensations form a self-balancing triangle, and the brain rapidly switches between numbing-sour-sweet, producing sustained novelty and excitement.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花椒浸泡别超过10分钟，否则会苦 | Don't steep peppercorns more than 10 min — they turn bitter |
| 花椒糖浆可以一次多做，冷藏保存1周 | Make extra syrup — it keeps in the fridge for a week |
| 加一点点盐（1小撮）会让柠檬味更突出 | A tiny pinch of salt makes the lemon flavor pop |
| 想做成鸡尾酒？加伏特加或金酒完美 | Want a cocktail? Add vodka or gin — perfection |
