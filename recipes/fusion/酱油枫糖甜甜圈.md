> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 枫糖浆的主要甜味成分蔗糖与酱油中的氨基酸在加热时发生美拉德反应，产生独特的"焦糖咸鲜"风味。酱油中的天然谷氨酸像味觉放大器一样增强甜味的感知深度——这就是为什么高级巧克力师会在焦糖中加一点酱油。涂在甜甜圈上，每一口都是甜咸交织的层次。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Maple syrup's sucrose undergoes Maillard reactions with soy sauce's amino acids when heated, creating unique "caramel-umami" notes. Soy sauce's natural glutamate acts as a flavor amplifier, deepening the perception of sweetness — this is why artisan chocolatiers add a touch of soy to caramel. On a donut, every bite is a layered sweet-salty experience.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 甜甜圈 | Plain donuts (store-bought is fine) | 4个 / 4 |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup (pure) | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 糖粉 | Powdered sugar | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 黄油 | Butter (melted) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 海盐片 (可选) | Sea salt flakes (optional) | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调釉面 | Make Glaze
糖粉+枫糖浆+酱油+融化黄油搅拌至顺滑浓稠。太稠加几滴枫糖，太稀加糖粉。

Whisk powdered sugar + maple syrup + soy sauce + melted butter until smooth and thick. Too thick? Add a drop of syrup. Too thin? Add more sugar.

### 2. 蘸釉 | Dip
甜甜圈顶部朝下蘸入釉面，轻轻转一圈，让釉面均匀覆盖。

Dip donut tops into the glaze, twisting gently for even coverage.

### 3. 定型上桌 | Set & Serve
放在架子上让釉面自然凝固（约10分钟）。趁釉面还微粘时撒海盐片。

Place on a rack to let glaze set (~10 min). Sprinkle sea salt flakes while glaze is still tacky.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定用纯枫糖浆，不要用枫糖味糖浆（corn syrup） | Use pure maple syrup, NOT maple-flavored corn syrup |
| 酱油从少量开始加，太多就咸了 | Start with less soy sauce — you can always add more |
| 买现成甜甜圈完全没问题，重点是这个釉面 | Store-bought donuts are perfectly fine — the glaze is the star |
