## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大虾 | Large shrimp (peeled) | 300g / ~15-20只 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 辣椒粉 | Red chili flakes | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Make the Glaze
混合蜂蜜、辣椒粉、蒜泥、酱油、米醋和橄榄油。取一半拌入虾，腌5分钟。

Mix honey, chili flakes, garlic, soy sauce, vinegar, and oil. Toss half with shrimp, marinate 5 min.

### 2. 烤虾 | Roast
虾铺在烤盘上，220°C (425°F) 烤5分钟至虾变粉红色。

Spread shrimp on a sheet pan. Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 5 min until shrimp turn pink.

### 3. 刷釉 | Glaze
取出刷剩余的辣椒蜂蜜酱，Broil 2-3分钟至表面起泡焦化。

Remove, brush with remaining chili-honey glaze. Broil 2–3 min until surface bubbles and chars.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻和葱花。配米饭吃，或者单独作为开胃菜。

Sprinkle sesame and scallion. Serve over rice, or alone as an appetizer.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **虾+蜂蜜高温下的美拉德魔法 / Shrimp + honey Maillard magic at high heat:**
> - 虾肉富含半胱氨酸和核糖，在高温下反应生成2-乙酰基-2-噻唑啉——这就是"烤虾香"的分子本质
> - 蜂蜜在180°C以上焦糖化，产生的呋喃类化合物与虾的硫化物形成独特的"甜海鲜"风味
> - 辣椒素激活TRPV1受体后，大脑释放内啡肽——这就是"吃辣上瘾"的神经科学原理
> - 米醋的乙酸在高温下挥发，留下柔和的酸度平衡甜辣
>
> *Shrimp's cysteine and ribose react at high heat to produce 2-acetyl-2-thiazoline — the molecular essence of "roasted shrimp aroma." Honey caramelizes above 180°C, its furans combining with shrimp's sulfur compounds for a unique "sweet seafood" flavor. Capsaicin activates TRPV1, triggering endorphin release — the neuroscience of "chili addiction." Rice vinegar's acetic acid volatilizes at high heat, leaving gentle acidity to balance sweet-spicy.*
