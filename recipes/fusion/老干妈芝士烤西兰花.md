## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 西兰花 | Broccoli | 1大朵 / 1 large head (~400g) |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella (shredded) | 100g / 1 cup |
| 帕玛森芝士 | Parmesan (grated) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备西兰花 | Prep Broccoli
西兰花切成小朵，用橄榄油、蒜粉和盐拌匀。铺在烤盘上，不要叠放。

Cut broccoli into florets. Toss with olive oil, garlic powder, and salt. Spread on a baking sheet in a single layer.

### 2. 初烤 | First Roast
220°C (425°F) 烤12分钟至边缘开始焦化变脆。

Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 12 min until edges begin to char and crisp.

### 3. 加料二烤 | Add Toppings & Broil
取出，淋上老干妈，撒满马苏里拉和帕玛森芝士。Broil 3-5分钟至芝士冒泡金黄。

Remove, drizzle LGM over the top, pile on mozzarella and Parmesan. Broil 3–5 min until cheese is bubbly and golden.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
趁热吃，芝士拉丝+老干妈脆粒+焦化西兰花，三种口感一口满足。

Eat immediately — stretchy cheese + LGM crunch + charred broccoli, three textures in one bite.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么烤西兰花比蒸的好吃 / Why roasted broccoli beats steamed:**
> - 西兰花含硫代葡萄糖苷（glucosinolate），生吃/蒸煮时释放硫化物（=臭味）
> - 220°C高温烤制时，硫化物挥发殆尽，留下的是甜美的焦糖化产物和坚果香
> - 芝士中的酪蛋白在165°C发生美拉德反应，产生数百种鲜香化合物
> - 老干妈的发酵豆豉含谷氨酸500mg/100g以上——相当于"天然味精炸弹"
>
> *Broccoli contains glucosinolates; when raw/steamed, they release sulfur compounds (= stinky). At 220°C, sulfur volatilizes away, leaving sweet caramelized products and nutty aromas. Cheese casein undergoes Maillard reactions at 165°C, producing hundreds of savory compounds. LGM's fermented black beans contain 500mg+ glutamate/100g — a "natural MSG bomb."*
