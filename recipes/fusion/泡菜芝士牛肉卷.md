# 泡菜芝士牛肉卷 | Kimchi Cheese Beef Roll

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份(4卷) | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、主菜

> 薄切牛肉片卷上泡菜和芝士，煎至外焦内融——切开截面是漂亮的红白螺旋纹。泡菜的酸辣穿透牛肉的浓郁，芝士拉丝连接每一层，这是韩式烤肉与西式芝士卷的强强联合。
>
> *Thinly sliced beef wraps around kimchi and cheese, pan-seared until golden outside and molten inside — cut cross-sections reveal beautiful red-and-white spirals. Kimchi's tang cuts through beefy richness while stretchy cheese bridges every layer. Korean BBQ meets Western cheese roll in a power fusion.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛肉薄片 (肥牛/火锅片) | Thinly sliced beef (shabu-shabu grade) | 300g (约12片 / ~12 slices) |
| 韩国泡菜 | Korean kimchi | 1杯，挤干切碎 / 1 cup, squeezed dry & chopped |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella cheese | 100g，切条 / 100g, cut into sticks |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 韩式辣酱 | Gochujang | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 白芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 生菜叶 | Lettuce leaves | 配菜 / for wrapping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Make Sauce
酱油、辣酱、糖和芝麻油混合成酱汁备用。

Mix soy sauce, gochujang, sugar, and sesame oil into a sauce. Set aside.

### 2. 卷制 | Roll
2-3片牛肉微微重叠铺平，放一条芝士和一勺泡菜在一端，紧紧卷起，用牙签固定。重复做4卷。

Lay 2-3 beef slices slightly overlapping. Place a cheese stick and a spoonful of kimchi at one end, roll tightly, secure with a toothpick. Repeat for 4 rolls.

### 3. 煎制 | Pan-Sear
中大火热锅加少许油，牛肉卷接缝处朝下先煎，然后翻转煎至四面金黄（共约6-8分钟）。最后刷上酱汁，翻面让酱汁焦化。

Heat pan with a little oil over medium-high. Sear rolls seam-side down first, then rotate until golden on all sides (~6-8 min total). Brush with sauce, flip to caramelize.

### 4. 切块上桌 | Slice & Serve
取出牙签，对半斜切露出截面，撒芝麻。配生菜叶包着吃。

Remove toothpicks, cut on a diagonal to reveal cross-section. Sprinkle sesame. Wrap in lettuce to eat.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 泡菜必须挤干水分，否则卷不紧且煎时溅油 | Squeeze kimchi bone-dry or rolls won't seal and oil will splatter |
| 牛肉片越薄越好，火锅片最合适 | Thinner beef is better — shabu-shabu/hot-pot slices are ideal |
| 接缝朝下先煎可以自然封口，不用牙签也行 | Sear seam-side down first to self-seal — toothpicks become optional |
| 芝士放太多会爆出来，每卷一条刚好 | Too much cheese will burst out — one stick per roll is perfect |
