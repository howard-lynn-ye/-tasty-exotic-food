> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 蔬菜在高温烤制下自身糖分焦糖化，再叠加酱油的鲜和蜂蜜的甜，形成"鲜甜焦香"三重奏。酱油中的氨基酸和蔬菜表面的糖在200°C下疯狂进行美拉德反应，产生烤肉般的焦香——这就是为什么烤蔬菜可以让不爱吃菜的人改邪归正。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Vegetables caramelize their own sugars at high heat, then soy sauce's umami and honey's sweetness create a "savory-sweet-charred" trio. Amino acids in soy sauce react with surface sugars at 200°C in an aggressive Maillard reaction, producing BBQ-like aromas — this is why roasted veggies convert vegetable haters.*

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|

| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 / to taste |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱汁 | Make Glaze
碗中混合酱油、蜂蜜、橄榄油、蒜泥和黑胡椒，搅匀。

Whisk together soy sauce, honey, olive oil, garlic, and black pepper.

### 2. 拌蔬菜 | Toss Veggies
所有蔬菜放大碗中，倒入酱汁，翻拌至每块都裹上酱。

Place all vegetables in a large bowl, pour glaze over, toss until every piece is coated.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱预热 220°C (425°F)。蔬菜单层铺在烤盘上（不要堆叠！），烤 25分钟，中途翻一次面。

Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Spread vegetables in a single layer on a baking sheet (no stacking!). Roast 25 minutes, flipping once halfway.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻，趁热搭配米饭或单吃。

Sprinkle sesame seeds. Serve hot with rice or as a side.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **酱油让蔬菜更好吃的原理 / Why soy sauce makes veggies taste better:**
> - 酱油中的谷氨酸（鲜味）弥补了蔬菜缺少的"肉味满足感"
> - 高温烤制下酱油的氨基酸与蔬菜糖分发生美拉德反应
> - 蜂蜜的果糖比蔗糖更容易焦糖化，加速表面上色
> - 焦化的酱油产生类似烧烤的烟熏风味化合物
>
> *Soy sauce's glutamate (umami) fills the "meaty satisfaction" that vegetables lack. At high heat, soy amino acids react with vegetable sugars (Maillard reaction). Honey's fructose caramelizes faster than sucrose, accelerating browning. Charred soy sauce produces smoky BBQ-like flavor compounds.*
