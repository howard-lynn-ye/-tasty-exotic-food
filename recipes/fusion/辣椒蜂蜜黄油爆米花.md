# 辣椒蜂蜜黄油爆米花 | Chili Honey Butter Popcorn

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 爆米花（原味已爆） | Popped popcorn (plain) | 6杯 / 6 cups |
| 黄油 | Butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 辣椒粉（卡宴或韩式） | Chili flakes (cayenne or gochugaru) | 1/2-1茶匙 / 1/2-1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 烟熏辣椒粉（可选） | Smoked paprika (optional) | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 熬辣椒蜂蜜黄油 | Make Chili Honey Butter
小锅中融化黄油，加入蜂蜜、辣椒粉和盐，小火搅拌1分钟至冒小泡、完全融合。

Melt butter in a small pot. Add honey, chili flakes, and salt. Stir over low heat 1 min until bubbly and combined.

### 2. 淋在爆米花上 | Drizzle Over Popcorn
爆米花放在大碗中，将辣椒蜂蜜黄油分三次淋入，每次淋完翻拌均匀再淋下一次。

Place popcorn in a large bowl. Drizzle chili honey butter in three additions, tossing thoroughly between each.

### 3. 撒粉享用 | Dust & Devour
撒上烟熏辣椒粉（如用），再拌一次。趁温热酥脆时大把抓着吃。

Dust with smoked paprika if using, toss once more. Devour while warm and crispy by the handful.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

辣椒素在脂肪介质（黄油）中的溶解度远高于在水中，因此融化黄油能将辣椒的热感均匀分散到每一粒爆米花表面，避免"一口超辣一口不辣"的不均匀体验。蜂蜜中的果糖在受热时焦糖化速度比蔗糖快50%，在爆米花表面形成一层极薄的脆糖衣。

*Capsaicin's solubility in fat (butter) far exceeds its solubility in water, so melted butter distributes heat evenly across every kernel — no more "one bite fire, next bite nothing." Honey's fructose caramelizes 50% faster than sucrose when heated, forming an ultra-thin crispy glaze on each popcorn surface.*
