> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 味噌中的曲霉菌发酵产生的蛋白酶会分解鸡肉表面蛋白质，起到天然嫩肉的作用。同时味噌的谷氨酸+芝麻的坚果香+蜂蜜的焦糖化，三重风味层次让每一口都有不同感受。高温烤制让味噌表面形成一层酥脆的釉面。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Miso's koji enzymes break down surface proteins, naturally tenderizing the chicken. Miso's glutamate + sesame's nuttiness + honey's caramelization create triple-layered flavor in every bite. High-heat roasting forms a crispy miso glaze on the surface.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡翅 | Chicken wings | 1磅 / 1 lb (~8-10 pieces) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 2瓣 / 2 cloves |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱腌制 | Marinate
将味噌、芝麻油、蜂蜜、酱油、蒜泥混合均匀。涂抹在鸡翅上，腌制至少30分钟。

Mix miso, sesame oil, honey, soy sauce, and garlic. Coat wings and marinate at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 高温烤制 | Roast
烤箱预热425°F (220°C)。鸡翅放在铺了锡纸的烤盘上，烤20分钟翻面，再烤15分钟至金黄酥脆。

Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place wings on foil-lined baking sheet. Roast 20 min, flip, roast 15 more min until crispy.

### 3. 撒芝麻上桌 | Finish
出炉后撒白芝麻和葱花，趁热上桌。

Sprinkle sesame seeds and scallions, serve hot.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鸡翅要拍干水分，烤出来才脆 | Pat wings completely dry for maximum crispiness |
| 味噌容易焦，最后5分钟注意观察 | Miso burns easily — watch closely the last 5 minutes |
| 可用空气炸锅 390°F 20分钟替代 | Air fryer at 390°F for 20 min works great as a substitute |
