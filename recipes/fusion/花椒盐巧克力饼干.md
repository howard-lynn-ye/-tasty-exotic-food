# 花椒盐巧克力饼干 | Sichuan Salt Chocolate Cookie

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 12分钟 | 💰 ~$3/批(约20块) | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、烘焙

> 花椒那独特的麻与海盐的咸在浓郁黑巧克力饼干中制造出令人欲罢不能的味觉层次。咬下去先是巧克力的苦甜，接着花椒的酥麻在舌尖绽放，最后海盐收尾——一块饼干，三重惊喜。
>
> *Sichuan peppercorn's signature tingle meets flaky sea salt in a rich dark chocolate cookie, creating irresistible layers of flavor. First comes the bittersweet chocolate, then the numbing buzz blooms on your tongue, finished by a salty kiss — one cookie, three surprises.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 黑巧克力 (70%) | Dark chocolate (70%) | 200g |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 60g |
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 130g |
| 可可粉 | Cocoa powder | 30g |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 2个 / 2 |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 150g |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 泡打粉 | Baking powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt (Maldon) | 适量，撒面用 / for topping |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 融化巧克力 | Melt Chocolate
黑巧克力掰碎与黄油一起隔水融化，搅拌顺滑后放凉。

Chop dark chocolate and melt with butter over a double boiler. Stir until smooth, let cool slightly.

### 2. 混合湿料 | Whisk Wet
鸡蛋加红糖用电动打蛋器打发至蓬松发白（约3分钟），加入香草精和巧克力混合物拌匀。

Beat eggs and brown sugar until pale and fluffy (~3 min). Fold in vanilla and the chocolate mixture.

### 3. 混合干料 | Combine Dry
面粉、可可粉、花椒粉和泡打粉过筛加入湿料，用刮刀翻拌至刚好无干粉。冷藏30分钟。

Sift flour, cocoa, Sichuan peppercorn, and baking powder into the wet batter. Fold until just combined. Chill 30 min.

### 4. 烤制 | Bake
预热烤箱180°C。用冰淇淋勺挖球放在烤盘上（间距5cm），撒海盐片。烤10-12分钟，中心微微颤动即可。

Preheat oven to 180°C/350°F. Scoop dough balls onto baking sheet (5cm apart), sprinkle flaky salt. Bake 10-12 min until edges set but centers still wobble.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花椒粉要现磨的，超市瓶装的香气弱很多 | Freshly ground Sichuan peppercorn is far more aromatic than pre-ground |
| 别过度搅拌面糊，否则饼干会变硬 | Don't overmix or the cookies will turn tough |
| 微微underbake是关键，冷却后中心会凝固 | Slightly underbake — centers firm up as they cool |
| 海盐一定烤前撒，烤后撒会化掉 | Sprinkle salt before baking — it melts if added after |
