## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 全脂牛奶 | Whole milk | 1杯 / 1 cup (240ml) |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorns | 5-6粒 / 5-6 whole |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1-2茶匙 / 1-2 tsp |
| 肉桂棒（可选） | Cinnamon stick (optional) | 1小段 / 1 small piece |
| 姜片 | Fresh ginger slice | 1片 / 1 thin slice |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮香料牛奶 | Infuse Spiced Milk
小锅放入牛奶、花椒、姜片和肉桂棒（如用），小火慢煮3-4分钟至微微冒泡。

Place milk, peppercorns, ginger, and cinnamon (if using) in a small pot. Simmer gently 3-4 min until tiny bubbles appear.

### 2. 过滤调蜜 | Strain & Sweeten
用细筛过滤掉花椒和姜片，倒入杯中。加入蜂蜜搅匀（蜂蜜不要在高温时加，会破坏活性酶）。

Strain out peppercorns and ginger. Pour into a mug. Stir in honey (add after slight cooling to preserve enzymes).

### 3. 品饮入睡 | Sip & Sleep
小口慢饮，感受先甜后麻的层次变化。搭配一本好书或舒缓音乐。

Sip slowly, feeling the sweet-then-tingle layering. Pair with a good book or calming music.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

花椒中的山椒素激活TRPA1受体产生温热麻感，与牛奶中色氨酸转化为血清素的镇静效应形成有趣的"先兴奋后放松"体验曲线。蜂蜜中的果糖促进色氨酸穿越血脑屏障，实际上增强了牛奶的助眠效果。

*Sanshool activates TRPA1 receptors for a warm tingle, creating an intriguing "stimulate-then-relax" curve alongside milk tryptophan's serotonin-based calming effect. Honey's fructose helps tryptophan cross the blood-brain barrier, actually enhancing milk's sleep-promoting properties.*
