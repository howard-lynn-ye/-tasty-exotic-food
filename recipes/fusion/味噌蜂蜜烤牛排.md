# 味噌蜂蜜烤牛排 | Miso Honey Steak

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 腌制 30分钟 + 烹饪 12分钟 | 💰 ~$12/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、约会菜

> 白味噌的咸鲜与蜂蜜的甘甜在牛排表面形成一层焦香琥珀色的釉面，每一口都是日式发酵深度与西式炙烤快感的完美碰撞。味噌中的酶还能软化肉质，让牛排更加多汁。
>
> *White miso's umami depth meets honey's golden sweetness, forming a caramelized amber glaze on the steak. Every bite delivers Japanese fermentation complexity fused with Western searing satisfaction. The enzymes in miso also tenderize the meat, making each slice impossibly juicy.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛排 (西冷或肋眼) | Steak (sirloin or ribeye) | 2块，各约250g / 2 pieces, ~250g each |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣，磨泥 / 3 cloves, grated |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 / to taste |
| 牛油 | Butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调腌料 | Make Marinade
将味噌、蜂蜜、酱油、蒜泥和米醋搅拌均匀，涂满牛排两面，腌制至少30分钟。

Mix miso, honey, soy sauce, garlic, and rice vinegar. Coat steak on both sides, marinate at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 烤制 | Sear
大火热铸铁锅至冒烟。擦去牛排表面多余腌料（保留备用），下锅煎每面3-4分钟至深褐色焦壳。

Heat cast iron pan over high heat until smoking. Wipe off excess marinade (reserve it), sear each side 3-4 minutes until a deep brown crust forms.

### 3. 刷釉 | Glaze
最后一分钟加入牛油和保留的腌料，用勺子不断浇淋在牛排上，形成闪亮釉面。

In the last minute add butter and reserved marinade, baste the steak repeatedly to build a glossy glaze.

### 4. 休息出盘 | Rest & Serve
离火静置5分钟，逆纹切片，撒葱花，淋锅中剩余酱汁。

Rest 5 minutes off heat. Slice against the grain, sprinkle scallions, drizzle remaining pan sauce.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定要用白味噌，红味噌太咸会盖住蜂蜜的甜 | Use white (shiro) miso — red miso is too salty and overpowers the honey |
| 擦掉多余腌料再煎，否则味噌会焦糊 | Wipe off excess marinade before searing or the miso will burn |
| 内部温度52°C为三分熟，57°C为五分熟 | Internal temp 52°C for medium-rare, 57°C for medium |
| 搭配白米饭或烤蔬菜都很完美 | Pairs perfectly with steamed rice or roasted vegetables |
