# 抹茶提拉米苏 | Matcha Tiramisu

> ⏱ 准备 25分钟 + 冷藏 6小时 | 💰 ~$7/份(6人) | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、免烤箱、甜品、高级感

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 用抹茶液替代浓缩咖啡浸泡手指饼干，马斯卡彭奶油层染上一抹翡翠绿。日本茶道的禅意和意大利甜品的放纵在同一个容器里和解，出来的是一道让人安静下来细细品味的甜品。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Swap espresso for matcha-infused liquid to soak ladyfingers, and tint the mascarpone cream jade green. Japanese tea ceremony zen reconciles with Italian dessert indulgence in one dish — the result is a dessert that makes you pause and savor in silence.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 手指饼干 | Ladyfinger cookies | 200g / 7 oz |
| 马斯卡彭芝士 | Mascarpone cheese | 250g / 8 oz |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder (culinary grade) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蛋黄 | Egg yolks | 3个 / 3 |
| 糖 | Granulated sugar | 70g / 1/3 cup |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 1杯 / 1 cup (240ml) |
| 热水 | Hot water (for matcha) | 1杯 / 1 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调抹茶液 | Make Matcha Soak
1.5汤匙抹茶粉+热水+2汤匙糖搅匀至无颗粒。冷却至室温。

Whisk 1.5 tbsp matcha + hot water + 2 tbsp sugar until smooth and lump-free. Cool to room temperature.

### 2. 做奶油层 | Make Cream Layer
蛋黄+剩余糖隔水打发至浓稠。离火，拌入马斯卡彭和剩余1/2汤匙抹茶粉。另打发淡奶油至软性发泡，轻轻拌入。

Whisk yolks + remaining sugar over a double boiler until thick. Remove from heat, fold in mascarpone and remaining 1/2 tbsp matcha. Whip cream to soft peaks separately, then fold in gently.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
手指饼干快速蘸抹茶液（2秒），铺一层在容器底部。铺一层抹茶奶油。重复一层。

Quickly dip ladyfingers in matcha liquid (2 seconds). Layer in a dish. Spread matcha cream on top. Repeat once.

### 4. 冷藏上桌 | Chill & Serve
冷藏至少6小时（过夜更好）。上桌前筛抹茶粉装饰。

Refrigerate at least 6 hours (overnight is better). Dust with matcha powder before serving.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么抹茶能完美替代咖啡 / Why this works:**
> 传统提拉米苏的咖啡提供苦味+焦香。抹茶通过不同路径达到类似效果：L-茶氨酸提供"旨味"（一种甜味的鲜），茶多酚（EGCG）提供涩感代替苦味，叶绿素带来独特的"绿色风味"。马斯卡彭的乳脂溶解了抹茶的脂溶性风味分子，让抹茶的涩感变得丝滑绵长。抹茶的咖啡因含量是咖啡的1/3，加上茶氨酸的放松作用，产生"清醒但平静"的独特体验。
>
> *Classic tiramisu coffee provides bitterness + roast aroma. Matcha achieves similar depth differently: L-theanine delivers "umami sweetness," catechins (EGCG) provide astringency instead of bitterness, and chlorophyll brings a unique "green flavor." Mascarpone's milk fat dissolves matcha's fat-soluble flavor molecules, transforming astringency into silky persistence. Matcha has 1/3 the caffeine of coffee plus theanine's calming effect — creating a unique "alert but calm" experience.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用烘焙级抹茶就行，仪式级的太贵浪费了 | Culinary-grade matcha is fine — ceremonial grade is overkill and expensive |
| 抹茶要先用少量水调开再加剩余水，防结块 | Dissolve matcha in a little water first, then add the rest — prevents lumps |
| 冷藏6小时是底线，过夜风味最佳 | 6 hours minimum chill; overnight yields the best flavor |
| 上桌前再筛抹茶粉，久放会被湿气吸掉颜色 | Dust matcha right before serving — moisture dulls the color |
