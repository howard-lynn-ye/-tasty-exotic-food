# 芝麻花生酱拌面 | Sesame Peanut Butter Noodles
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/芝麻花生酱拌面.svg" width="500" alt="芝麻花生酱拌面"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 8分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、快手、宿舍可做、素食可选

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 中式芝麻酱的醇厚和美式花生酱的浓郁联手，再加上酱油和醋的调和，创造出一碗比任何单一酱料都更有深度的拌面。两种酱各补对方的短板——芝麻酱补香气，花生酱补稠度。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Chinese sesame paste's nuttiness and American peanut butter's creaminess join forces, balanced by soy sauce and vinegar, creating noodles with more depth than either sauce alone. Each fills the other's gap — sesame adds fragrance, peanut butter adds body.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 面条 | Noodles (spaghetti or ramen) | 200g / 7 oz |
| 花生酱 | Creamy peanut butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 芝麻酱 | Sesame paste or tahini | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 辣椒油 (可选) | Chili oil (optional) | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮面 | Cook Noodles
面条按包装煮熟，留半杯煮面水，沥干后过冷水（冷吃更好）。

Cook noodles per package, save 1/2 cup cooking water. Drain and rinse with cold water (cold noodles are better here).

### 2. 调酱 | Mix Sauce
碗中混合花生酱、芝麻酱、酱油、醋、蜂蜜和蒜泥。用2-3汤匙煮面水稀释搅匀至顺滑。

Combine peanut butter, sesame paste, soy sauce, vinegar, honey, and garlic. Thin with 2–3 tbsp noodle water until smooth.

### 3. 拌面上桌 | Toss & Serve
酱汁倒在面条上翻拌均匀。撒芝麻、葱花和黄瓜丝，淋辣椒油。

Pour sauce over noodles and toss thoroughly. Top with sesame seeds, scallion, cucumber shreds, and chili oil.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么芝麻酱+花生酱=1+1>2 / Why this works:**
> 芝麻酱含有芝麻酚（sesamol）和芝麻素（sesamin），这些是芝麻独特香气的来源，但蛋白质含量低导致口感偏薄。花生酱蛋白质含量是芝麻酱的2倍，提供粘稠的"挂面"能力和浓郁的坚果底味。两者混合后，芝麻酱负责"香气层"（挥发性芳香），花生酱负责"味觉层"（蛋白质稠度+甜味），形成完美的风味分层结构。醋的酸度激活了两种酱料中被脂肪掩盖的风味分子。
>
> *Sesame paste has sesamol and sesamin (unique fragrance sources) but low protein makes it thin. Peanut butter has 2x the protein, providing thick "noodle-coating" ability and rich nutty base. Combined: sesame handles the "aroma layer" (volatile fragrances), peanut butter handles the "taste layer" (protein body + sweetness) — perfect flavor stratification. Vinegar's acidity unlocks flavor molecules hidden beneath the fats in both pastes.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花生酱选 creamy 不选 chunky，拌面更顺滑 | Use CREAMY peanut butter — chunky won't blend smoothly |
| 煮面水是灵魂，淀粉让酱汁挂面 | Noodle water is key — starch helps sauce cling to noodles |
| 冷面比热面好吃，酱汁不会被热气蒸发 | Cold noodles taste better — heat doesn't evaporate the sauce |
| 加黄瓜丝提供清爽口感对比 | Cucumber shreds add refreshing crunch contrast |
