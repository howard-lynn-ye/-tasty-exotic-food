## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 燕麦片 | Rolled oats | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 混合坚果（碎） | Chopped mixed nuts | ½杯 / ½ cup |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | ⅓杯 / ⅓ cup |
| 花生酱 | Peanut butter | ¼杯 / ¼ cup |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | ¾茶匙 / ¾ tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | ¼茶匙 / ¼ tsp |
| 椰子油 | Coconut oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 巧克力碎（可选） | Chocolate chips (optional) | ¼杯 / ¼ cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌干料 | Mix Dry
碗中混合燕麦、坚果碎、花椒粉、盐和芝麻。

Combine oats, chopped nuts, Sichuan pepper, salt, and sesame seeds in a bowl.

### 2. 熬湿料 | Heat Wet
小锅中低火加热蜂蜜、花生酱和椰子油，搅至顺滑均匀。

Heat honey, peanut butter, and coconut oil in a small saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth.

### 3. 混合压模 | Combine & Press
湿料倒入干料，快速搅拌均匀。倒入铺了油纸的 8×8 方形烤盘，用勺背用力压紧压实。

Pour wet mixture over dry, stir quickly. Transfer to a parchment-lined 8×8 pan. Press firmly with the back of a spoon.

### 4. 烘烤 | Bake
烤箱预热 170°C (340°F)，烤 22-25分钟至边缘金黄。取出室温冷却30分钟再切条。

Preheat oven to 170°C (340°F). Bake 22–25 minutes until edges are golden. Cool completely (30 min) before slicing into bars.

### 5. 可选：淋巧克力 | Optional: Drizzle Chocolate
融化巧克力碎，用叉子在棒上画Z字线条，冷藏定型。

Melt chocolate chips, drizzle over bars in a zigzag, then refrigerate to set.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定要压实！否则切的时候散掉 | Press HARD — or the bars crumble when you cut |
| 花椒粉别超过1茶匙，零食不是药 | Keep Sichuan pepper under 1 tsp — it's a snack, not medicine |
| 冷却后才能切，热的时候是软的 | Slice only when fully cooled — they're soft when warm |
| 密封保存常温可放一周 | Sealed at room temperature, they keep for a week |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **花椒在甜食中的秘密 / Sichuan pepper's secret in sweets:**
> - 花椒素激活触觉受体而非味觉受体，产生"振动"而非"辣"
> - 微量花椒在甜味背景下产生"惊喜感"而非"攻击感"
> - 蜂蜜焦糖化后的太妃糖味和花椒的柑橘调非常互补
> - 坚果的油脂缓释花椒素，让麻感柔和持久
>
> *Sanshool activates tactile receptors (not taste), creating "vibration" rather than "heat." At low doses in a sweet context, it creates "surprise" instead of "assault." Honey's toffee notes after caramelization complement peppercorn's citrus tones. Nut oils slow-release sanshool, making the tingle gentle and lasting.*
