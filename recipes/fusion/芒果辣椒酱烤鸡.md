# 芒果辣椒酱烤鸡 | Mango Chili Chicken

> ⏱ 45分钟 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、烤箱、热带风味

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 热带芒果的蜜甜裹着亚洲辣椒酱的火焰，在烤鸡表皮化作一层金红色的甜辣铠甲。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Tropical mango sweetness wraps around Asian chili sauce heat, forming a golden-red sweet-spicy armor on roasted chicken skin.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿 | Chicken thighs (bone-in) | 4只 / 4 pieces |
| 芒果 | Ripe mango, diced | 1个 / 1 whole |
| 甜辣酱 | Sweet chili sauce | 60ml / ¼ cup |
| 青柠汁 | Lime juice | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 10g / 2 cloves |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 15ml / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调酱腌制 | Blend & Marinate
芒果取一半打成泥，与甜辣酱、青柠汁、蒜末、鱼露混合，涂抹鸡腿腌制20分钟。
Puree half the mango, mix with sweet chili sauce, lime juice, garlic, and fish sauce. Coat chicken thighs and marinate 20 min.

### 2. 烘烤 | Roast
200°C/400°F烤30分钟至鸡皮焦脆，中途刷一次酱汁。
Roast at 200°C/400°F for 30 minutes until skin is crispy, basting once halfway.

### 3. 装盘点缀 | Plate & Garnish
烤鸡配剩余芒果丁、新鲜薄荷叶和一瓣青柠上桌。
Serve roasted chicken with remaining mango cubes, fresh mint leaves, and a lime wedge.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 芒果中的β-紫罗兰酮与辣椒素的TRPV1受体激活形成感官对比，甜味降低辣度感知的同时鱼露的谷氨酸延长回味。 *Mango's beta-ionone contrasts with capsaicin's TRPV1 activation; sweetness lowers perceived heat while fish sauce glutamates extend the aftertaste.*
