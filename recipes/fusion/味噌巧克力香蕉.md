## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 香蕉 | Bananas (firm, not overripe) | 2根 / 2 |
| 黑巧克力 | Dark chocolate (70%+) | 100g / 3.5 oz |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt | 少许 / a pinch |
| 碎坚果（可选） | Crushed nuts (optional) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 冰棒棍（可选） | Popsicle sticks (optional) | 4根 / 4 |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备香蕉 | Prep Bananas
香蕉去皮切半，如果做冰棒版则插入冰棒棍，冷冻30分钟至表面变硬。常温版跳过冷冻。

Peel and halve bananas. For popsicle version, insert sticks and freeze 30 min. For room-temp version, skip freezing.

### 2. 融化巧克力 | Melt Chocolate
巧克力隔水融化（或微波炉30秒×3次搅拌）。离火后搅入白味噌至完全融合顺滑。

Melt chocolate over a double boiler (or microwave 30 sec × 3, stirring between). Remove from heat, whisk in miso until smooth.

### 3. 蘸巧克力 | Dip
香蕉蘸入味噌巧克力，让巧克力自然挂壁。立刻撒海盐和碎坚果。

Dip bananas in miso chocolate, letting it coat naturally. Immediately top with sea salt and crushed nuts.

### 4. 凝固享用 | Set & Eat
放在烘焙纸上凝固2分钟（冷冻版直接吃）。巧克力壳一咬即碎，内部香蕉软糯。

Place on parchment to set 2 min (frozen version eat immediately). The shell shatters on first bite, revealing soft banana inside.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **味噌+巧克力：意想不到的鲜苦共振 / Miso + chocolate: the unexpected umami-bitter resonance:**
> - 黑巧克力含可可碱（theobromine）和苯乙胺（phenylethylamine，"恋爱分子"）
> - 味噌的谷氨酸与可可碱在味觉皮层产生交叉激活——大脑将信号解读为"更浓更复杂的巧克力"
> - 白味噌的麦曲（koji）赋予微妙的"甜鲜"尾韵，软化巧克力的苦涩收尾
> - 海盐抑制苦味受体TAS2R，让甜味和鲜味在感知中"浮出水面"
>
> *Dark chocolate contains theobromine and phenylethylamine ("love molecule"). Miso's glutamate cross-activates with theobromine in the gustatory cortex — the brain reads the signal as "richer, more complex chocolate." White miso's koji imparts a subtle "sweet-umami" finish, softening chocolate's bitter aftertaste. Sea salt suppresses TAS2R bitter receptors, letting sweetness and umami "surface" in perception.*
