> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 老干妈的油脂在高温下会发生美拉德反应，与吐司表面的碳水化合物结合产生焦香。豆豉中的谷氨酸（天然味精）加上芝士的酪蛋白，形成双重鲜味叠加，让简单的吐司变成令人上瘾的umami炸弹。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Lao Gan Ma's oil undergoes Maillard reaction at high heat, bonding with the toast's carbohydrates to create deep caramelization. The glutamate in fermented black beans plus cheese's casein create a double umami stack, turning simple toast into an addictive savory bomb.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 吐司 | Bread slices | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella cheese | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup shredded |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 / 1 |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 涂酱 | Spread
吐司上均匀涂抹老干妈，确保边缘也覆盖到。

Spread Lao Gan Ma evenly on toast, making sure edges are covered.

### 2. 加料焗烤 | Top & Broil
撒上芝士丝，中间打一个鸡蛋。送入烤箱/烤面包机 400°F (200°C) 烤5-7分钟，至芝士起泡金黄。

Top with shredded cheese, crack an egg in the center. Broil at 400°F (200°C) for 5-7 minutes until cheese is bubbly and golden.

### 3. 装饰上桌 | Garnish & Serve
撒葱花，趁热吃。蛋黄最好还是流心的。

Sprinkle scallions, eat hot. Runny yolk is ideal.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 吐司选厚切的效果最好 | Thick-cut bread works best |
| 先烤2分钟让吐司底部定型，再打蛋上去 | Pre-toast 2 min to set the base before adding the egg |
| 没有烤箱可以用平底锅加盖焖 | No oven? Use a covered skillet instead |
