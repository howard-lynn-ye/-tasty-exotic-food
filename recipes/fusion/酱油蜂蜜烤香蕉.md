# 酱油蜂蜜烤香蕉 | Soy Honey Roasted Banana

> 🤖 AI Original | ⏱ 准备 3分钟 + 烹饪 8分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、甜品、空气炸锅友好

> 酱油的咸鲜与蜂蜜的甜蜜包裹在烤得焦糖化的香蕉表面——这是一道颠覆认知的甜咸融合甜品。烤箱高温让香蕉糖分焦化，酱油提供意想不到的深度。
>
> *Soy sauce's salinity and honey's sweetness coat caramelized roasted banana — a mind-bending sweet-savory fusion dessert. High heat caramelizes banana sugars while soy sauce adds unexpected depth.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 香蕉（偏熟） | Ripe bananas | 2根 / 2 |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp (melted) |
| 海盐 | Flaky sea salt | 少许 / a pinch |
| 香草冰淇淋（可选） | Vanilla ice cream (optional) | 1勺 / 1 scoop |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调蜜酱 | Make Glaze
混合融化黄油、蜂蜜和酱油，搅匀。香蕉纵切对半，放入烤盘，刷上蜜酱。

Mix melted butter, honey, and soy sauce. Halve bananas lengthwise, place on a baking sheet, brush with glaze.

### 2. 高温烤 | Roast Hot
烤箱 220°C / 425°F，烤 8分钟至表面金黄起泡，中途再刷一次蜜酱。

Roast at 220°C/425°F for 8 min until golden and bubbly. Brush with more glaze halfway through.

### 3. 撒盐上桌 | Salt & Serve
出炉后立刻撒海盐片，搭配一球香草冰淇淋，冷热交融。

Sprinkle flaky salt immediately. Serve with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for hot-cold contrast.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

酱油中的氨基酸在高温下与香蕉的天然糖分发生美拉德反应，产生正常烤香蕉无法达到的深层焦糖风味。少量盐分（酱油+海盐）触发味觉对比增强效应，让甜味感知提升约30%。

*Soy sauce amino acids undergo Maillard reactions with banana sugars at high heat, yielding caramel depth beyond normal roasting. The salt from soy sauce and sea salt triggers taste-contrast enhancement, boosting perceived sweetness by ~30%.*
