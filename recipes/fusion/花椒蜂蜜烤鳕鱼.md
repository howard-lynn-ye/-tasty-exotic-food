# 花椒蜂蜜烤鳕鱼 | Sichuan Honey Cod

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 腌制 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$10/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、海鲜、健康

> 鳕鱼的雪白细嫩遇上花椒蜂蜜的大胆风味——听起来反差极大，但效果惊人地和谐。花椒的麻提亮了鳕鱼温和的甜味，蜂蜜在鱼表面形成琥珀色保护层锁住水分。这道菜证明了温柔的食材也能承接强烈的调味。
>
> *Cod's snow-white delicacy meets Sichuan peppercorn-honey's bold flavors — sounds like a clash, but the harmony is stunning. Peppercorn's tingle brightens cod's gentle sweetness while honey forms an amber shield that locks in moisture. This dish proves that gentle ingredients can carry bold seasoning.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鳕鱼排 | Cod fillets | 2块，各约200g / 2 pieces, ~200g each |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 3/4茶匙 / 3/4 tsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣，磨泥 / 2 cloves, grated |
| 姜汁 | Ginger juice | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 葱丝 | Scallion shreds | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱腌制 | Marinate
蜂蜜、花椒粉、酱油、蒜泥、姜汁和米醋混合。鳕鱼拍干水分，抹上酱汁腌20分钟。

Mix honey, Sichuan peppercorn, soy, garlic, ginger juice, and vinegar. Pat cod dry, coat with the sauce, marinate 20 min.

### 2. 煎封 | Sear
中大火热锅加橄榄油，鳕鱼擦去多余腌料（保留），入锅每面煎2分钟至金黄。

Heat olive oil over medium-high. Wipe excess marinade off cod (reserve it), sear each side 2 min until golden.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
煎好的鳕鱼转到烤盘，刷上保留的腌料。200°C烤8-10分钟至鱼肉刚好变白、用叉子能轻松分层。

Transfer seared cod to a baking tray, brush with reserved marinade. Roast at 200°C/400°F for 8-10 min until flesh just turns opaque and flakes easily.

### 4. 出盘 | Serve
摆盘，铺上葱丝和香菜，淋烤盘中的酱汁。配米饭或烤蔬菜。

Plate, top with scallion shreds and cilantro, drizzle with pan juices. Serve with rice or roasted vegetables.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鳕鱼一定要拍干，否则煎不出金黄色 | Pat cod bone-dry or it won't sear golden |
| 花椒粉比整粒更适合鱼类——颗粒太粗会影响口感 | Ground peppercorn suits fish better — whole bits disrupt the texture |
| 别烤过头，鳕鱼一干就柴 | Don't over-roast — dry cod is chalky |
| 银鳕鱼（黑鳕）最佳，太平洋鳕鱼也可以 | Black cod is ideal; Pacific cod works too |
