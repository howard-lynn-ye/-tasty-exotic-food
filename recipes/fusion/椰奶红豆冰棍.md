# 椰奶红豆冰棍 | Coconut Red Bean Popsicle

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 冷冻 6小时 | 💰 ~$2/份(6根) | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、免烹饪、甜品、夏日

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 泰式椰奶的热带奶香和中式红豆沙的绵密甜蜜冻成冰棍，每一口都是东南亚夏天的味道。不需要冰淇淋机，不需要技术，倒进模具就完事——这是最简单的亚洲融合甜品。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Thai coconut milk's tropical creaminess and Chinese red bean paste's velvety sweetness frozen into popsicles — every bite tastes like a Southeast Asian summer. No ice cream machine, no technique, just pour and freeze — the simplest Asian fusion dessert you'll ever make.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 椰奶 | Coconut milk (full fat) | 1罐 / 1 can (400ml) |
| 红豆沙 | Sweetened red bean paste | 150g / 5 oz |
| 炼乳 | Sweetened condensed milk | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1小撮 / 1 pinch |
| 红豆粒 (可选) | Whole red beans (optional) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 混合 | Mix Base
碗中混合椰奶、红豆沙、炼乳、香草精和盐，搅拌至红豆沙完全融合（可以保留一些颗粒感）。

Combine coconut milk, red bean paste, condensed milk, vanilla, and salt. Stir until bean paste is mostly incorporated (some texture is good).

### 2. 加红豆粒 | Add Whole Beans
如果想要口感层次，在混合物中加入整颗红豆粒拌匀。

For textural interest, fold in whole red beans.

### 3. 冷冻 | Freeze
倒入冰棍模具，插入冰棍棒。冷冻至少6小时至完全凝固。

Pour into popsicle molds, insert sticks. Freeze at least 6 hours until solid.

### 4. 脱模 | Unmold & Enjoy
模具底部冲温水10秒帮助脱模。即可享用。

Run warm water over the bottom of the molds for 10 seconds to help release. Enjoy immediately.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么椰奶+红豆是天生一对 / Why this works:**
> 椰奶含约17%脂肪（月桂酸为主），这种中链脂肪酸的熔点恰好在口腔温度附近，产生"入口即化"的奶油感。红豆沙的主要甜味来自蔗糖和淀粉的配合，在冷冻状态下甜度降低约30%（甜味受体在低温下不那么敏感），所以炼乳的额外甜度弥补了这个"冰冻甜度损失"。椰奶的月桂酸还能包裹红豆中的皂苷类苦味化合物，让红豆只留甜不留苦。
>
> *Coconut milk's ~17% fat (mostly lauric acid) has a melting point near mouth temperature, creating a "melt-on-tongue" creaminess. Red bean paste's sweetness comes from sucrose + starch, but freezing reduces perceived sweetness by ~30% (sweet receptors are less sensitive at low temps). Condensed milk compensates for this "frozen sweetness loss." Lauric acid also encapsulates saponin-type bitter compounds in red beans, letting only sweetness through.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 椰奶一定要全脂（full fat），低脂的冻出来冰渣多 | Must use FULL-FAT coconut milk — low-fat gets icy |
| 冷冻前搅匀，红豆沙容易沉底 | Stir well before pouring — bean paste sinks to the bottom |
| 没有冰棍模具？用纸杯+筷子代替 | No popsicle molds? Use paper cups + chopsticks as sticks |
