# 味噌焦糖爆米花 | Miso Caramel Popcorn

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、零食、派对、无需烤箱

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 味噌的深层鲜味藏在焦糖的甜蜜外衣下，每一颗爆米花都是"先甜后鲜"的味觉过山车。咸、甜、鲜三重炸弹，让普通爆米花升级成让人停不下手的零食。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Miso's deep umami hides beneath a caramel coat, making each kernel a "sweet-then-savory" roller coaster. Salt, sweet, and umami triple-bomb — this turns ordinary popcorn into an unstoppable snack.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 爆米花 | Popped popcorn | 8杯 / 8 cups |
| 黄油 | Unsalted butter | 45g / 3 tbsp |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 60g / 1/4 cup |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 玉米糖浆/蜂蜜 | Corn syrup or honey | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 海盐 | Flaky sea salt | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 爆米花 | Pop the Corn
如果用微波爆米花，按包装操作；如果用玉米粒，锅中加油大火加盖爆好。倒入大碗备用。

Use microwave popcorn per package, or pop kernels in a covered pot with oil. Transfer to a large bowl.

### 2. 煮焦糖味噌酱 | Make Miso Caramel
中火融化黄油，加红糖和玉米糖浆搅拌至起泡（约2分钟）。离火后立刻搅入味噌，快速拌匀。

Melt butter over medium heat, add brown sugar and corn syrup, stir until bubbling (~2 min). Remove from heat and whisk in miso immediately.

### 3. 裹酱 | Coat & Toss
将味噌焦糖酱淋在爆米花上，用硅胶铲快速翻拌至每颗都裹上酱。撒海盐。

Pour miso caramel over popcorn and toss quickly with a silicone spatula until evenly coated. Sprinkle flaky salt.

### 4. 冷却 | Cool & Serve
铺在烘焙纸上冷却5分钟至酥脆，即可享用。

Spread on parchment paper, cool 5 minutes until crispy, then devour.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么味噌+焦糖是天才组合 / Why this works:**
> 味噌含有极高浓度的游离谷氨酸（鲜味的核心分子），而焦糖化的糖在加热过程中产生数百种挥发性芳香化合物。鲜味+甜味的组合会触发大脑"奖励回路"的双重激活——这就是为什么你吃了一颗就停不下来。味噌的微咸还会抑制焦糖的腻甜，形成完美平衡。
>
> *Miso is loaded with free glutamate (the core umami molecule), while caramelized sugar generates hundreds of volatile aromatics. Umami + sweet triggers a double activation of the brain's reward circuit — that's why you can't stop at one handful. Miso's subtle saltiness also cuts through caramel's cloying sweetness for perfect balance.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 味噌一定要离火后加，否则高温会破坏鲜味 | Add miso OFF heat — high temp destroys umami compounds |
| 用白味噌（mild），红味噌太咸 | Use white/yellow miso (mild); red miso is too salty for this |
| 焦糖酱要趁热裹，凉了就凝固了 | Coat while hot — the caramel sets as it cools |
