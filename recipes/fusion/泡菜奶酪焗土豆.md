# 泡菜奶酪焗土豆 | Kimchi Cheese Potato

> ⏱ 35分钟 | 💰~$6/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、烤箱、韩美混搭

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 韩式辣白菜的酸辣邂逅浓郁的切达奶酪，在烤土豆的金黄表皮下演绎一场首尔与威斯康辛的爱情故事。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Korean kimchi's tangy heat meets melty cheddar on a baked potato — a love story between Seoul and Wisconsin under a golden crust.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大土豆 | Large russet potatoes | 2个 / 2 |
| 韩式泡菜 | Korean kimchi, chopped | 100g / ½ cup |
| 切达奶酪丝 | Shredded cheddar cheese | 80g / ¾ cup |
| 酸奶油 | Sour cream | 60g / ¼ cup |
| 培根碎 | Bacon bits | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 葱花 | Scallions, sliced | 15g / 2 stalks |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 烤土豆 | Bake Potatoes
土豆刺孔，200°C/400°F烤45分钟至软透，对半切开挖出薯肉。
Pierce potatoes, bake at 200°C/400°F for 45 min until tender, halve and scoop out flesh.

### 2. 拌馅料 | Mix Filling
薯肉与泡菜、酸奶油、一半奶酪和培根碎拌匀，填回土豆壳，顶上剩余奶酪。
Mash potato flesh with kimchi, sour cream, half the cheese, and bacon bits. Fill shells, top with remaining cheese.

### 3. 焗烤 | Broil
放入烤箱上层220°C/425°F焗5-7分钟至奶酪起泡金黄，撒葱花上桌。
Broil on upper rack at 220°C/425°F for 5-7 min until cheese is bubbly and golden. Sprinkle scallions and serve.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 泡菜发酵产生的乳酸与奶酪的酪蛋白形成酸-脂平衡，辣椒素的热感被土豆淀粉的包裹效应温柔化解。 *Kimchi's lactic acid balances casein in cheese for an acid-fat harmony, while potato starch's coating effect tames capsaicin's burn.*
