## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 黑巧克力 | Dark chocolate (60%+) | 120g |
| 黄油 | Butter | 100g |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | ¾杯 / ¾ cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 面粉 | All-purpose flour | ½杯 / ½ cup |
| 可可粉 | Cocoa powder | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 焦糖酱 | Caramel sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 融化巧克力 | Melt Chocolate
黑巧克力和黄油隔水加热（或微波30秒×3次搅拌）至完全融化顺滑。

Melt dark chocolate and butter using a double boiler (or microwave in 30-second bursts, stirring between). Until smooth.

### 2. 拌面糊 | Make Batter
加入糖搅拌，再逐个加蛋搅匀，加香草精。筛入面粉和可可粉，翻拌至刚好无干粉（不要过度搅拌）。

Stir in sugar, then add eggs one at a time, stir in vanilla. Sift in flour and cocoa powder, fold until just combined (don't overmix).

### 3. 组装烤制 | Assemble & Bake
面糊倒入铺了油纸的 8×8 方形烤盘。表面随意滴入焦糖酱，用筷子画旋涡纹路。撒花椒粉和海盐片。烤箱 175°C (350°F) 烤 22-25分钟至表面结壳但中间微软。

Pour batter into a parchment-lined 8×8 pan. Randomly drizzle caramel sauce on top, swirl with a chopstick. Sprinkle Sichuan pepper and flaky salt. Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 22–25 minutes until top is set but center is slightly soft.

### 4. 冷却切块 | Cool & Cut
完全冷却后切成9块。中间微软是正常的——这就是 fudgy brownie。

Cool completely before cutting into 9 squares. Slightly soft center is normal — that's a fudgy brownie.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 不要过度搅拌面糊！面筋发展=蛋糕质地（不是布朗尼） | Don't overmix! Gluten development = cakey (not fudgy) |
| 花椒粉撒表面而非拌入面糊，效果更惊艳 | Sprinkle Sichuan pepper on top, not mixed in — more dramatic |
| 宁可少烤一分钟也不要多烤——凉了会继续凝固 | Under-bake by 1 minute rather than over — it sets while cooling |
| 用 Maldon 海盐片（大片状），不要用细盐 | Use Maldon flaky salt (big flakes), not fine salt |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **花椒+巧克力为什么是天才组合 / Why Sichuan pepper + chocolate is genius:**
> - 花椒素激活舌头的触觉受体，产生50Hz的"振动感"
> - 这种振动让味蕾对巧克力的苦和甜更加敏感（多模态增强）
> - 黑巧克力中的可可碱（theobromine）+ 花椒素 = 双重神经刺激
> - 海盐的咸提供对比，让焦糖更甜、巧克力更浓
>
> *Sanshool activates tongue's tactile receptors, producing a ~50Hz "vibration." This vibration increases sensitivity to chocolate's bitter and sweet (multimodal enhancement). Dark chocolate's theobromine + sanshool = double nervous system stimulation. Sea salt's savory provides contrast, making caramel sweeter and chocolate richer.*
