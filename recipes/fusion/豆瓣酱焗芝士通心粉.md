## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 通心粉 | Elbow macaroni | 250g |
| 切达芝士碎 | Shredded cheddar cheese | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 牛奶 | Whole milk | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 黄油 | Butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 郫县豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣 / 2 cloves |
| 面包屑 (可选) | Panko breadcrumbs (optional) | 1/3杯 / 1/3 cup |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮面 | Cook Pasta
通心粉按包装煮至 al dente，沥干。

Cook macaroni per package to al dente. Drain.

### 2. 做酱 | Make the Sauce
锅中融化黄油，加蒜末炒香。加面粉炒1分钟成 roux。慢慢加入牛奶，不断搅拌至浓稠。加入豆瓣酱搅匀（酱会变成漂亮的橙红色）。关火，加入3/4芝士搅至融化。

Melt butter, sizzle garlic. Add flour, cook 1 minute to make a roux. Slowly add milk, stirring constantly until thick. Stir in doubanjiang (the sauce turns a gorgeous orange-red). Remove from heat, stir in 3/4 of the cheese until melted.

### 3. 合并 | Combine
将面条倒入酱中拌匀。倒入烤盘，撒上剩余芝士和面包屑。

Fold pasta into the sauce. Transfer to a baking dish. Top with remaining cheese and panko.

### 4. 烤 | Bake
烤箱 200°C (400°F)，烤10-12分钟至表面金黄起泡。撒葱花。

Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 10–12 minutes until golden and bubbling. Garnish with scallions.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **中美融合的化学基础 / The chemistry of this fusion:**
> - 豆瓣酱含谷氨酸（鲜味）+ 辣椒素（辣味）+ 发酵产物（深度）
> - 芝士的酪蛋白在加热时形成弹性网络，包裹辣椒素分子
> - 脂肪是辣椒素的溶剂 → 辣味被"缓释"而非瞬间爆发
> - 面包屑的美拉德反应提供焦香对比层次
>
> *Doubanjiang = glutamate (umami) + capsaicin (heat) + fermentation products (depth). Cheese casein forms elastic networks that encapsulate capsaicin molecules. Fat dissolves capsaicin → heat is "slow-released" instead of explosive. Panko Maillard reaction adds a crispy contrast layer.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 豆瓣酱不要放太多，1.5汤匙刚好 | 1.5 tbsp doubanjiang is the sweet spot — more will overpower |
| 用 sharp cheddar 最好，味道更浓 | Sharp cheddar works best — bolder flavor |
| 没有烤箱可以直接锅里吃，也很好 | No oven? Eat it straight from the pot — still amazing |
| 加培根碎更过瘾 | Add crumbled bacon for extra indulgence |
