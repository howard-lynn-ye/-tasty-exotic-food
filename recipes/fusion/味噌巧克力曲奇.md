# 味噌巧克力曲奇 | Miso Chocolate Cookies

> 🤖 AI Original | ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烘烤 12分钟 | 💰 ~$5/12块 | 🏷️ 融合创意、烘焙、派对甜品

> 在经典巧克力曲奇面团中加入一勺白味噌——这个小改动让普通曲奇变成让人"猜不出秘密配料"的神秘甜品。味噌增加了焦糖般的咸鲜深度，让巧克力味更加浓郁立体。
>
> *Add a spoonful of white miso to classic chocolate cookie dough — this small tweak turns ordinary cookies into a "guess the secret ingredient" mystery dessert. Miso adds caramel-like savory depth that makes chocolate taste richer and more dimensional.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 1杯 / 1 cup (125g) |
| 黄油（室温） | Butter (room temp) | 85g / 6 tbsp |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 黑巧克力碎 | Dark chocolate chunks | 80g / ~1/2 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 打发黄油 | Cream Butter
黄油、红糖和味噌一起打发至蓬松发白（约2分钟）。加入鸡蛋拌匀。

Beat butter, brown sugar, and miso until fluffy and pale (~2 min). Add egg, mix until combined.

### 2. 拌面团 | Mix Dough
筛入面粉，用刮刀翻拌至刚好无干粉。折入巧克力碎。面团冷藏30分钟（可选但推荐）。

Fold in sifted flour until just combined. Fold in chocolate chunks. Chill dough 30 min (optional but recommended).

### 3. 烤曲奇 | Bake
挖成12个球，放在铺了烘焙纸的烤盘上（间隔5cm）。180°C/350°F 烤10-12分钟至边缘金黄、中心微软。出炉撒少许海盐片。

Scoop 12 balls onto parchment-lined tray (5cm apart). Bake 180°C/350°F for 10-12 min until edges golden, centers soft. Sprinkle flaky salt immediately.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

味噌中的美拉德反应产物（在发酵过程中已预先形成）与烘烤中新产生的美拉德反应叠加，创造出比普通曲奇更深层的焦糖-坚果-鲜味复合体。味噌的钠离子抑制苦味感知的同时增强甜味，让你可以减少30%的糖用量而不觉得不够甜。

*Miso's pre-formed Maillard products (created during fermentation) stack with newly generated Maillard compounds during baking, creating deeper caramel-nut-umami complexity than plain cookies. Miso's sodium suppresses bitterness while enhancing sweetness, letting you cut sugar by 30% without noticing.*
