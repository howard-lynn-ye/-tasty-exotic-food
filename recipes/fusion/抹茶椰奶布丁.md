# 抹茶椰奶布丁 | Matcha Coconut Pudding

> 日本抹茶的草木苦香融入东南亚椰奶的热带甜润——一款不用烤箱、不用鸡蛋的双重亚洲灵感布丁。翡翠绿的颜色和丝绒般的口感让它成为完美的dinner party甜品。
>
> *Japanese matcha's grassy bitterness melts into Southeast Asian coconut milk's tropical sweetness — a no-bake, no-egg double-Asian pudding. Its jade color and velvet texture make it a dinner-party showstopper.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 全脂椰奶 | Full-fat coconut milk | 1罐 / 1 can (400ml) |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 玉米淀粉 | Cornstarch | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 椰子片（装饰） | Toasted coconut flakes | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调糊 | Make Slurry
取3汤匙椰奶与玉米淀粉、抹茶粉混合搅匀成无颗粒糊状。

Whisk 3 tbsp coconut milk with cornstarch and matcha powder until smooth and lump-free.

### 2. 煮布丁液 | Cook the Pudding
剩余椰奶加糖和香草精，中火煮至冒小泡。倒入抹茶糊，持续搅拌2-3分钟至浓稠。

Heat remaining coconut milk with sugar and vanilla over medium until simmering. Pour in matcha slurry, stir constantly 2-3 min until thick.

### 3. 冷藏定型 | Chill & Set
倒入小杯/模具，贴面覆保鲜膜，冷藏至少2小时。脱模后撒椰子片。

Pour into cups or molds, press plastic wrap on surface. Chill at least 2 hours. Unmold and top with toasted coconut flakes.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

抹茶中的L-茶氨酸与椰奶中的月桂酸在味觉上互补——茶氨酸带来"鲜甜"回味，月桂酸提供脂肪润滑感，两者协同降低苦味感知。玉米淀粉糊化后的网状结构锁住抹茶微粒，防止沉淀，确保每一口都是均匀的翡翠绿。

*Matcha's L-theanine and coconut milk's lauric acid are taste complements — theanine adds savory-sweet aftertaste while lauric acid provides fat lubrication, both synergistically reducing bitterness perception. Gelatinized cornstarch traps matcha particles in its network, preventing settling for uniform jade color in every spoonful.*
