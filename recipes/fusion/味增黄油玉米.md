# 味增黄油玉米 | Miso Butter Corn (AI Original)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/味增黄油玉米.svg" width="500" alt="味增黄油玉米"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、小吃、全超市可买、素食

> 日本居酒屋的灵感碰上美国烤玉米——味增的咸鲜、黄油的浓郁和玉米的清甜融为一体，再撒上七味粉和葱花，这道菜简单到不像话，好吃到停不下来。
>
> *Japanese izakaya meets American corn on the cob — miso's savory depth, butter's richness, and corn's natural sweetness merge into something irresistible. Finished with shichimi togarashi and scallions, this dish is absurdly simple and impossibly delicious.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 玉米 | Corn (fresh, frozen, or canned) | 3根 / 3 ears (or 2 cups kernels) |
| 黄油 | Butter (unsalted) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 白味增 | White miso paste | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜或味醂 | Honey or mirin | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 七味粉 (可选) | Shichimi togarashi (optional) | 少许 / a pinch |
| 葱花 | Scallion (chopped) | 1根 / 1 stalk |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调味增黄油 | Make Miso Butter
黄油室温软化，加入味增和蜂蜜（或味醂），搅拌均匀。

Soften butter to room temperature. Mix in miso paste and honey (or mirin) until smooth.

### 2. 煮/烤玉米 | Cook Corn

**方法A — 平底锅（推荐）：** 玉米粒下锅，中大火干煎至微焦，加入味增黄油翻炒至裹匀。

**Method A — Skillet (recommended):** Add corn kernels to a dry skillet. Cook over medium-high heat until slightly charred. Add miso butter and toss until coated.

**方法B — 整根烤：** 玉米烤熟（烤箱400°F 20分钟，或水煮8分钟），趁热涂上味增黄油。

**Method B — Whole ear:** Roast corn (oven 400°F for 20 min, or boil 8 min). Brush miso butter on while hot.

### 3. 装盘 | Serve
撒上七味粉和葱花即可。

Top with shichimi togarashi and scallion. Serve immediately.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 白味增比红味增更温和，配玉米更和谐 | White miso is milder than red — it pairs better with sweet corn |
| 味增不要加太多，一点点就很鲜 | A little miso goes a long way — too much makes it salty |
| 煎玉米粒时不要一直翻，让它微微焦化更香 | Don't stir constantly — let the corn char a little for extra flavor |
| 夏天用新鲜玉米最好，冬天冷冻玉米也不错 | Fresh corn in summer is best; frozen works great in winter |
