# 酱油冰淇淋 | Soy Sauce Ice Cream

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 冷冻 4小时 | 💰 ~$4/份(4人) | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、甜品、免烤箱、无需冰淇淋机

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 在香草冰淇淋基底中加入酱油，听起来疯狂但尝过就回不去了。酱油的鲜味（谷氨酸）和焦糖化的糖分让冰淇淋产生一种类似咸焦糖却更深邃的神秘风味，像是东方的盐之花。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Adding soy sauce to vanilla ice cream sounds insane until you taste it. Soy sauce's umami (glutamate) and caramelized sugars create something like salted caramel but deeper and more mysterious — an Eastern fleur de sel that haunts your palate.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 浓奶油 | Heavy whipping cream | 2杯 / 2 cups (480ml) |
| 炼乳 | Sweetened condensed milk | 1罐 / 1 can (14 oz) |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce (naturally brewed) | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 海盐片 (可选) | Flaky sea salt (optional) | 少许 / for topping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 打奶油 | Whip Cream
浓奶油用电动打蛋器打至硬性发泡（提起打蛋器有尖角不倒）。

Whip heavy cream with an electric mixer until stiff peaks form (tips stand straight up when you lift the beaters).

### 2. 混合 | Fold Together
炼乳中加入酱油和香草精搅匀。将这个混合物轻轻拌入打发的奶油中，翻拌至均匀（不要搅拌，会消泡）。

Stir soy sauce and vanilla into condensed milk. Gently fold this mixture into the whipped cream (fold, don't stir — you'll deflate it).

### 3. 冷冻 | Freeze
倒入密封容器，表面铺保鲜膜贴面（防冰晶），冷冻至少4小时。

Pour into an airtight container, press plastic wrap directly on the surface (prevents ice crystals). Freeze at least 4 hours.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
提前5分钟从冷冻室取出回温。挖球，撒海盐片，淋几滴酱油做装饰。

Remove from freezer 5 min before serving. Scoop, top with flaky salt and a few drops of soy sauce for garnish.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么酱油+冰淇淋是隐藏神组合 / Why this works:**
> 天然酿造酱油在发酵过程中产生超过300种风味化合物，包括焦糖醛（caramelic aldehydes）和呋喃酮（furaneol）——这些和香草冰淇淋中的香草醛（vanillin）属于同一芳香化合物家族。酱油的谷氨酸激活舌头上的鲜味受体，与甜味受体形成"交叉激活"，让甜味被感知得更加丰满立体。简单说：酱油让冰淇淋的味道"3D化"了。
>
> *Naturally brewed soy sauce develops 300+ flavor compounds during fermentation, including caramelic aldehydes and furaneol — these belong to the same aromatic family as vanillin in vanilla ice cream. Soy's glutamate activates umami receptors that "cross-talk" with sweet receptors, making sweetness taste fuller and more three-dimensional. Simply put: soy sauce gives ice cream a flavor upgrade to 3D.*
