# 芝麻酱意面 | Tahini Sesame Pasta

> ⏱ 20分钟 | 💰~$6/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、面食、中东×中国

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 中东tahini与中式麻酱本是同根芝麻，在意大利面的舞台上终于合体，三个大洲的风味在一只锅里团聚。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Middle Eastern tahini and Chinese sesame paste are sesame siblings — reunited on Italian pasta, three continents in one pot.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 意大利面 | Spaghetti or linguine | 200g / 7 oz |
| 芝麻酱/tahini | Tahini or Chinese sesame paste | 45g / 3 tbsp |
| 生抽 | Light soy sauce | 20ml / 4 tsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 10g / 2 cloves |
| 辣椒油 | Chili oil | 10ml / 2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮面 | Cook Pasta
意面按包装说明煮至弹牙，留120ml面汤备用。
Cook pasta al dente per package directions, reserve 120ml / ½ cup pasta water.

### 2. 调酱 | Make Sauce
芝麻酱、生抽、米醋、蒜末、辣椒油与面汤搅拌成顺滑浓酱。
Whisk tahini, soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, chili oil, and pasta water into a smooth, thick sauce.

### 3. 拌面上桌 | Toss & Serve
面条沥水倒入酱中拌匀，盘上撒碎花生、黄瓜丝和芝麻。
Drain pasta, toss in sauce until coated. Top with crushed peanuts, cucumber shreds, and sesame seeds.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 芝麻酱的高亚油酸含量在舌面形成持久油膜，放大酱油谷氨酸的鲜味信号；米醋的低pH激活唾液分泌，增强整体风味感知。 *Tahini's high linoleic acid creates a lingering oil film on the palate, amplifying soy's glutamate signal; rice vinegar's low pH triggers saliva, boosting overall flavor perception.*
