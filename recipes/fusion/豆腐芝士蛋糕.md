# 豆腐芝士蛋糕 | Tofu Cheesecake

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 + 冷藏 4小时 | 💰 ~$5/份(6-8人) | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、烤箱、甜品、低卡

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 用嫩豆腐替代一半奶油芝士做芝士蛋糕，热量直降40%但口感依然绵密顺滑。豆腐的"无味"反而是它的超能力——它完美吸收芝士的浓郁，成为最安静的味觉放大器。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Replace half the cream cheese with silken tofu — calories drop 40% but the texture stays impossibly creamy. Tofu's "blandness" is actually its superpower: it absorbs cheese's richness perfectly, becoming the quietest flavor amplifier in your kitchen.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 奶油芝士 | Cream cheese | 225g / 8 oz (1 block) |
| 嫩豆腐 | Silken tofu | 300g / 10 oz |
| 糖 | Granulated sugar | 80g / 1/3 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 全麦饼干碎 | Graham cracker crumbs | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 黄油 | Melted butter (for crust) | 45g / 3 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做饼底 | Make Crust
饼干碎和融化黄油拌匀，压入9寸派盘底部压紧。180°C (350°F) 预烤8分钟。

Mix crumbs with melted butter, press into a 9-inch pan. Pre-bake at 180°C (350°F) for 8 minutes.

### 2. 做馅 | Make Filling
豆腐沥水后用料理机或搅拌器打至完全顺滑。加入软化的奶油芝士、糖、鸡蛋、柠檬汁和香草精，打至无颗粒。

Drain tofu, blend until perfectly smooth. Add softened cream cheese, sugar, eggs, lemon juice, and vanilla. Blend until no lumps remain.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
馅料倒在饼底上，160°C (325°F) 烤40-45分钟至边缘微固但中心仍轻微晃动。

Pour filling over crust. Bake at 160°C (325°F) for 40–45 min until edges set but center still jiggles slightly.

### 4. 冷却 | Cool & Chill
关火后开一条缝让蛋糕在烤箱里慢慢冷却1小时（防裂），然后冷藏至少4小时。

Turn off oven, crack the door, and let the cake cool slowly for 1 hour (prevents cracking). Refrigerate at least 4 hours.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么豆腐能替代芝士 / Why this works:**
> 嫩豆腐含约5%蛋白质和3%脂肪，口感极其细腻。它的大豆蛋白在加热时凝固的方式与蛋白质凝固类似，提供"蛋糕骨架"。奶油芝士负责味觉深度（乳脂+乳酸发酵风味），豆腐负责质地滑顺度——两者分工明确。柠檬汁的柠檬酸帮助豆腐蛋白质在正确的温度凝固，同时提供酸度对比，让蛋糕不腻。
>
> *Silken tofu (~5% protein, ~3% fat) has an ultra-fine texture. Its soy protein coagulates during baking similarly to egg protein, providing "cake structure." Cream cheese handles flavor depth (milk fat + lactic fermentation), tofu handles silky texture — clear division of labor. Lemon's citric acid helps tofu protein gel at the right temperature while providing acidity contrast so the cake never feels heavy.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 豆腐一定要用嫩豆腐（silken），老豆腐口感太粗 | Must use SILKEN tofu — firm tofu has the wrong texture |
| 豆腐要沥干水分再打，否则蛋糕会出水 | Drain tofu well before blending — excess water ruins the cake |
| 烤箱里慢冷是防裂的关键 | Slow cooling in the oven is the KEY to preventing cracks |
| 冷藏过夜口感最好 | Overnight chill yields the best creamy texture |
