## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 虾 | Large shrimp (shell-on or peeled) | 300g |
| 黄油 | Butter | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 老干妈 | Lao Gan Ma Spicy Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 6瓣 / 6 cloves (minced) |
| 白葡萄酒或料酒 | White wine or Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | 半个 / half |
| 欧芹或葱花 | Parsley or scallion | 适量 / for garnish |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 热锅 | Heat Pan
中大火融化黄油至起泡。

Melt butter over medium-high heat until foaming.

### 2. 煎虾 | Sear Shrimp
放入虾，煎1.5分钟至底面变粉红，翻面。加蒜末爆香30秒。

Add shrimp, sear 1.5 minutes until the bottom turns pink. Flip. Add garlic, sizzle 30 seconds.

### 3. 加酱 | Add Sauce
倒入酒（会滋一声），加老干妈，翻炒均匀让每只虾都裹上红油酱汁。

Pour in wine (it'll sizzle), add Lao Gan Ma, toss to coat every shrimp in the red-oil sauce.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
挤柠檬汁，撒欧芹/葱花。配法棍面包蘸汁吃。

Squeeze lemon, sprinkle parsley/scallion. Serve with crusty bread for dipping.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 虾不要煮过头，变色即熟 | Don't overcook shrimp — they're done the moment they turn pink |
| 黄油要用无盐的，老干妈自带咸味 | Use unsalted butter — Lao Gan Ma brings its own salt |
| 配法棍蘸盘底的酱汁，灵魂吃法 | Dipping crusty bread in the pan sauce is the soul of this dish |
| 请客时做这道，看起来很高级 | Make this when you have guests — it looks expensive |
