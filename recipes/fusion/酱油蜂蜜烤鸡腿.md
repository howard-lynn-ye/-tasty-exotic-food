> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 酱油和蜂蜜是亚洲烧烤的"黄金搭档"，在鸡腿这个最宽容的食材上表现得淋漓尽致。鸡腿肉的脂肪含量比鸡胸高，意味着更多的风味载体和更强的容错性——即使稍微烤过头也不会干柴。酱油的氨基酸和蜂蜜的糖在鸡皮上合作进行美拉德反应，产生一层深琥珀色的"照烧壳"。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Soy sauce and honey are Asian BBQ's "golden couple," and they perform best on chicken thigh — the most forgiving cut. Thigh meat's higher fat content means more flavor carriers and greater margin for error — even slightly overcooked, it stays juicy. Soy amino acids and honey sugars collaborate on the chicken skin's Maillard reaction, producing a deep amber "teriyaki shell."*

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
碗中混合酱油、蜂蜜、蒜泥、姜末、米醋、芝麻油和黑胡椒。鸡腿放入翻拌至完全裹上酱汁，腌至少30分钟。

Mix soy sauce, honey, garlic, ginger, rice vinegar, sesame oil, and black pepper. Toss chicken thighs in the marinade, coat completely, marinate at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱预热 200°C (400°F)。鸡腿皮面朝上放烤盘，淋一些腌酱。烤 30分钟。

Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Place thighs skin-side up on a baking sheet, spoon some marinade over. Bake 30 minutes.

### 3. 刷酱收汁 | Glaze
取出刷一层腌酱，开 Broil 档烤 3-5分钟至鸡皮酥脆深色。

Remove, brush with marinade. Switch to Broil for 3–5 minutes until skin is crispy and deeply colored.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻和葱花。配白米饭，把烤盘里的汤汁浇在饭上。

Sprinkle sesame seeds and scallions. Serve with white rice, pour pan juices over the rice.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定用带骨带皮鸡腿，去骨去皮的太干 | Must use bone-in, skin-on — boneless/skinless dries out |
| 皮面朝上烤，让鸡皮直接接受热辐射 | Skin-side up — let skin face the heat directly |
| Broil 时盯着看，酱油糖分很容易烧焦 | Watch during Broil — soy sauce sugars burn quickly |
| 内部温度达到 75°C (165°F) 即可食用 | Internal temp 75°C (165°F) = safe to eat |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **鸡腿为什么是最适合新手的食材 / Why chicken thigh is the best beginner-friendly cut:**
> - 鸡腿脂肪含量 ~13%（鸡胸只有 ~3%），脂肪=风味+保湿
> - 带骨烤制，骨髓中的胶原蛋白溶出增加汁感
> - 鸡皮在高温下油脂渲出，蛋白质变脆 = 天然酥壳
> - 酱油的氨基酸 + 蜂蜜的糖 = 完美美拉德反应原料配比
>
> *Thigh fat content ~13% (breast only ~3%) — fat = flavor + moisture retention. Bone-in cooking releases collagen from marrow, adding juiciness. Chicken skin renders fat at high heat, proteins crisp = natural crispy shell. Soy amino acids + honey sugars = perfect Maillard reaction ingredient ratio.*
