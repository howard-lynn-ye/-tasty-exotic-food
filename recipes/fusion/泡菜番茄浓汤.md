## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 韩国泡菜 | Korean kimchi (aged, sour) | 1杯 / 1 cup, chopped |
| 番茄罐头 | Canned crushed tomatoes | 1罐 / 1 can (400g) |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium, diced |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 鸡汤/蔬菜汤 | Chicken or veggie broth | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 韩式辣酱 | Gochujang | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 酸奶油（可选） | Sour cream (optional) | 适量 / for serving |
| 面包 | Crusty bread | 适量 / for dipping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒底料 | Build the Base
橄榄油炒洋葱3分钟至透明，加蒜末炒香。加入切碎的泡菜翻炒2分钟至边缘微焦。

Sauté onion in olive oil 3 min until translucent. Add garlic, then kimchi, stir-fry 2 min until edges char.

### 2. 加汤煮 | Simmer
倒入番茄罐头和高汤，加入韩式辣酱搅匀。大火煮开后转小火煮20分钟。

Add tomatoes and broth. Stir in gochujang. Bring to a boil, then simmer 20 min.

### 3. 搅拌（可选） | Blend (Optional)
如果喜欢顺滑质地，用手持搅拌棒打成泥。喜欢有块感的跳过此步。

For smooth texture, blend with an immersion blender. For chunky, skip this step.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
盛碗，淋一勺酸奶油画圈，配硬壳面包蘸着吃。

Ladle into bowls, swirl sour cream on top, serve with crusty bread for dipping.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **双发酵酸味的味觉层次 / Flavor layers of double-fermented acidity:**
> - 泡菜的乳酸（lactic acid）是"圆润的酸"，入口柔和，在口腔后部缓慢展开
> - 番茄的苹果酸（malic acid）+柠檬酸（citric acid）是"明亮的酸"，在舌尖迅速爆发
> - 两种酸味的"时间差"创造了"先锐后柔"的酸度曲线——比单一酸源丰富得多
> - 泡菜发酵产生的生物胺（biogenic amines）与番茄的谷氨酸协同，鲜味增强5-8倍
> - 番茄红素是脂溶性的，橄榄油炒制时吸收率提升4倍
>
> *Kimchi's lactic acid is a "round acid" — gentle entry, slowly building in the back palate. Tomato's malic + citric acids are "bright acids" — sharp burst on the tongue tip. This "time lag" creates a "sharp-then-soft" acidity curve far richer than any single acid source. Kimchi's biogenic amines synergize with tomato glutamate for 5–8x umami boost. Lycopene is fat-soluble; sautéing in olive oil boosts absorption 4x.*
