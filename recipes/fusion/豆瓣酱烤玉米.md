# 豆瓣酱烤玉米 | Doubanjiang Grilled Corn

> 🤖 AI Original | ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$1.5/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、烧烤季、墨西哥街头风

> 用四川郫县豆瓣酱替代墨西哥 elote 的辣椒粉——发酵辣豆瓣的鲜辣深度远超普通辣椒粉。刷上蛋黄酱和豆瓣酱的混合物，烤至焦香起泡，这是你从未吃过的烤玉米。
>
> *Swap Mexican elote's chili powder for Sichuan doubanjiang — fermented chili bean paste's savory depth outpaces plain chili. Brushed with a mayo-doubanjiang mix and grilled until blistered, this is corn like you've never had.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 玉米棒 | Corn on the cob | 2根 / 2 ears |
| 豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang (Pixian preferred) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蛋黄酱 | Mayonnaise | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酸橙 | Lime | 1个 / 1 |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Mix Sauce
将豆瓣酱剁细，与蛋黄酱、半个酸橙汁混合成均匀的橘红色酱。

Finely chop doubanjiang. Mix with mayo and juice of half a lime into a smooth orange-red sauce.

### 2. 刷酱烤制 | Grill with Glaze
玉米先在烤架/烤盘上烤至微焦（约5分钟翻面），然后刷上豆瓣酱蛋黄酱，再烤2-3分钟至起泡。

Grill corn until lightly charred (~5 min, turning). Brush doubanjiang-mayo all over, grill 2-3 more min until bubbly.

### 3. 装饰上桌 | Garnish & Serve
撒芝麻和香菜碎，挤上剩余酸橙汁，趁热食用。

Top with sesame seeds and cilantro, squeeze remaining lime. Serve hot.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

豆瓣酱经过长达数年的自然发酵，产生大量游离氨基酸和有机酸，其风味复杂度远超未发酵辣椒。蛋黄酱中的卵磷脂将豆瓣酱的水溶性鲜味分子和脂溶性辣椒素桥接在一起，形成均匀的风味涂层。

*Doubanjiang's years-long natural fermentation produces abundant free amino acids and organic acids, yielding far greater flavor complexity than raw chili. Mayo's lecithin bridges the paste's water-soluble umami compounds with fat-soluble capsaicin into a uniform flavor coating.*
