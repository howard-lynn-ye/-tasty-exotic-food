## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 椰奶（全脂罐装） | Full-fat coconut milk (canned) | 1罐 (400ml) |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 蜂蜜或糖 | Honey or sugar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a tiny pinch |
| 热水 | Hot water | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 化抹茶 | Dissolve Matcha
抹茶粉加热水，用茶筅或小叉子搅成无颗粒的浓抹茶液。

Add hot water to matcha powder. Whisk with a chasen or small fork until smooth with no lumps.

### 2. 混合 | Blend
碗中倒入椰奶、蜂蜜、香草精和盐，搅匀。倒入抹茶液再搅匀。（用搅拌器更均匀。）

Combine coconut milk, honey, vanilla, and salt in a bowl. Stir, then pour in the matcha mixture. Stir well. (A blender makes it smoother.)

### 3. 倒模冷冻 | Pour & Freeze
倒入冰棍模具，插入木棍。冷冻至少6小时或过夜。

Pour into popsicle molds, insert sticks. Freeze at least 6 hours or overnight.

### 4. 脱模 | Unmold
模具底部用温水冲5秒，轻拉即可脱模。

Run warm water over the mold bottom for 5 seconds, then gently pull to unmold.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 抹茶一定先用热水化开，直接加椰奶会结块 | Always dissolve matcha in hot water first — adding directly to coconut milk causes clumps |
| 用全脂椰奶（罐装），不要用饮料型椰奶 | Use full-fat canned coconut milk, NOT the drinking kind |
| 甜度可以根据口味调整，冷冻后甜感会减弱 | Adjust sweetness to taste — sweetness diminishes when frozen |
| 没有冰棍模具？用纸杯+保鲜膜+筷子 | No mold? Use paper cups + plastic wrap + chopstick |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **抹茶+椰奶的绿色化学 / The green chemistry of matcha + coconut:**
> - 抹茶的苦味（儿茶素）被椰奶的脂肪包裹后大幅减弱
> - 椰奶的月桂酸（中链脂肪酸）是天然乳化剂，让口感丝滑
> - 抹茶中的L-茶氨酸穿过血脑屏障，促进α脑波（放松+专注）
> - 冷冻状态下芳香物质释放更慢，所以味道比喝起来更"干净"
>
> *Matcha's bitterness (catechins) is significantly muted by coconut milk's fat. Coconut milk's lauric acid (MCT) is a natural emulsifier, creating silky texture. Matcha's L-theanine crosses the blood-brain barrier, promoting alpha waves (relaxation + focus). Frozen state slows aromatic release, so the flavor is "cleaner" than drinking it.*
