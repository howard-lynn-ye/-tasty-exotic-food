## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 熟透香蕉 | Overripe bananas | 3根 / 3 large |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 100g / 1/2 cup |
| 黄油（融化） | Melted butter | 80g / 1/3 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 200g / 1.5 cups |
| 泡打粉 | Baking powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 小苏打 | Baking soda | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt | 少许 / for topping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 焦糖味噌酱 | Miso Caramel
小锅中融化2汤匙黄油，加入3汤匙红糖，搅拌至冒泡。离火后搅入白味噌，放凉备用。

Melt 2 tbsp butter in a small pan, add 3 tbsp brown sugar, stir until bubbly. Remove from heat, whisk in miso. Set aside to cool.

### 2. 混合湿料 | Wet Ingredients
碗中压碎香蕉，加入融化黄油、鸡蛋、剩余红糖和香草精，搅拌均匀。

Mash bananas in a bowl. Stir in melted butter, eggs, remaining brown sugar, and vanilla.

### 3. 合并干湿 | Combine
面粉、泡打粉、小苏打过筛加入湿料，轻轻翻拌至刚好混合（有小面疙瘩没关系）。

Sift flour, baking powder, and baking soda into wet mixture. Fold gently until just combined (small lumps are fine).

### 4. 烤制 | Bake
倒入抹油的吐司模，表面淋上味噌焦糖酱并用筷子画"之"字。180°C (350°F) 烤50-55分钟。出炉撒海盐片。

Pour into a greased loaf pan. Drizzle miso caramel on top and swirl with a chopstick. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 50–55 min. Top with flaky sea salt.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么味噌+香蕉=深度甜感 / Why miso + banana = deep sweetness:**
> - 白味噌含丰富的游离谷氨酸（鲜味）和麦芽糖，本身就是"甜鲜双修"
> - 熟透香蕉的异戊酸酯（isoamyl acetate）产生浓郁果香，被味噌的鲜味放大
> - 焦糖化温度(170°C)下，味噌中的氨基酸参与美拉德反应，产生独特的"鲜焦糖"风味
> - 海盐的晶体结构让舌尖先感受到咸的冲击，随后甜味涌入——形成"时间差味觉波"
>
> *White miso is rich in free glutamate and maltose — a natural "sweet-umami dual cultivator." Overripe banana's isoamyl acetate creates intense fruitiness, amplified by miso's umami. At caramelization temp (170°C), miso's amino acids join the Maillard reaction, producing a unique "umami caramel" note. Flaky salt crystals hit the tongue with a salty shock first, followed by a sweet wave — a "time-lag taste wave."*
