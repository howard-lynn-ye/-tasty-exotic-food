# 花椒蜂蜜烤鸡胸 | Sichuan Honey Chicken Breast

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪22分 | 💰~$9/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、健身、川式

> **💡 灵感** — 鸡胸肉常被嫌弃干柴，但花椒蜂蜜酱汁是它的救赎：蜂蜜锁水保润，花椒赋予迷人麻香，高温烤制形成蜜汁焦壳。高蛋白低脂肪，健身党也能吃得有滋味。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Chicken breast is often dismissed as dry and bland, but Sichuan honey glaze is its redemption: honey seals in moisture, peppercorn adds captivating numbing fragrance, and high heat creates a caramelized crust. High protein, low fat — even gym-goers can eat with gusto.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡胸肉 | Chicken breast | 2块 / 2 pcs (~400g) |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan pepper | 3g / ½ tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 35ml / 2½ tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 20ml / 4 tsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 10g / 2 slices |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 5ml / 1 tsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame | 10g / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 拍松腌制 | Pound & Marinate
鸡胸用刀背拍至均匀厚度（约1.5cm），用花椒粉、酱油、蒜泥、姜泥和芝麻油腌10分钟。Pound chicken to even thickness (~1.5cm), marinate with Sichuan pepper, soy sauce, garlic paste, ginger paste, and sesame oil for 10 minutes.

### 2. 蜜汁烤 | Honey Roast
烤盘铺锡纸，鸡胸放上刷蜂蜜，烤箱200°C烤12分钟，翻面再刷蜂蜜，继续烤8-10分钟至内温达74°C。Line a pan with foil, place chicken and brush with honey, roast at 400°F for 12 minutes, flip, brush more honey, continue 8-10 minutes until internal temp reaches 165°F.

### 3. 静置切片 | Rest & Slice
出炉静置3分钟，斜刀切厚片，保留肉汁。Rest 3 minutes, slice on the bias to retain juices.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
摆盘撒白芝麻和花椒粉，淋盘底酱汁，搭配糙米饭和蔬菜。Plate with sesame and a dusting of Sichuan pepper, drizzle pan juices, serve with brown rice and vegetables.
