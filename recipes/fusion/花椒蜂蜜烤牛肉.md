# 花椒蜂蜜烤牛肉 | Sichuan Honey Roast Beef

> ⏱ 准备15分+烹饪25分 | 💰~$18/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、肉类、川式

> **💡 灵感** — 花椒的麻与蜂蜜的甜，在高温烤制下形成一层焦脆蜜釉，锁住牛肉汁水。这种"麻甜"组合打破了传统川菜的"麻辣"框架，是一种大胆又和谐的新味型。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Sichuan peppercorn's numbing tingle meets honey's sweetness, forming a crispy glaze under high heat that seals in beef juices. This "numbing-sweet" combo breaks from the traditional "numbing-spicy" Sichuan mold — bold yet harmonious.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛里脊 | Beef tenderloin | 500g / 1.1 lb |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan pepper | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 50ml / 3 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 25ml / 5 tsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 姜 | Ginger | 15g / 3 slices |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 10ml / 2 tsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame | 10g / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
牛肉整块抹上花椒粉、酱油、蒜泥、姜泥和芝麻油，腌制至少15分钟。Rub whole tenderloin with Sichuan pepper, soy sauce, garlic paste, ginger paste, and sesame oil, marinate at least 15 minutes.

### 2. 煎封 | Sear
平底锅大火烧热，牛肉表面煎至四面焦褐，约共4分钟。Heat a skillet on high, sear beef on all sides until deeply browned, about 4 minutes total.

### 3. 蜜汁烤制 | Honey Roast
牛肉移入烤盘，表面刷蜂蜜，烤箱200°C烤15-18分钟（五分熟）。中途再刷一次蜂蜜。Transfer to a baking sheet, brush with honey, roast at 400°F for 15-18 minutes (medium-rare). Brush honey again halfway through.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
静置5分钟后切厚片，撒白芝麻和少许花椒粉，搭配蔬菜沙拉。Rest 5 minutes, slice thick, sprinkle sesame and a dusting of Sichuan pepper, serve with green salad.
