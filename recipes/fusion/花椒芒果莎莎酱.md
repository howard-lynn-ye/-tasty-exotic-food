> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 花椒的麻感（山椒素激活TRPA1受体）让舌头进入"高敏感模式"，此时芒果的甜味和青柠的酸味会被放大感知。这和四川人吃麻辣火锅后喝冰粉的原理一样——麻感重置味觉后，后续的每一口都更鲜明。花椒+水果是尚未被主流发现的神级搭配。

Same principle as eating ice jelly after Sichuan hotpot — the numbing resets your palate, making every subsequent bite more vivid. Peppercorn + fruit is an elite pairing yet to go mainstream.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 熟芒果 | Ripe mango | 1个 / 1 large |
| 红洋葱 | Red onion (diced) | 1/4个 / 1/4 |
| 香菜 | Cilantro (chopped) | 一小把 / a handful |
| 青柠 | Lime juice | 1个 / juice of 1 |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 小米辣或墨西哥辣椒 | Thai chili or jalapeno (minced) | 1个 / 1 |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切丁 | Dice
芒果、红洋葱切小丁，香菜切碎，辣椒切末。

Dice mango and red onion small. Chop cilantro. Mince chili.

### 2. 拌匀 | Toss
所有食材放碗中，挤入青柠汁，撒花椒粉和盐，轻轻拌匀。

Combine everything in a bowl. Squeeze lime juice, add peppercorn powder and salt, toss gently.

### 3. 静置上桌 | Rest & Serve
冰箱放15分钟让风味融合。配玉米片、烤鱼或烤鸡都绝。

Refrigerate 15 min for flavors to meld. Serve with tortilla chips, grilled fish, or roast chicken.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花椒粉从少量开始，1/4茶匙已经很明显 | Start small — 1/4 tsp peppercorn is already quite noticeable |
| 芒果要选熟的但不软烂的 | Ripe but firm mango holds its shape best |
| 做好当天吃最佳，隔夜芒果会出水 | Best eaten same day — mango releases water overnight |
