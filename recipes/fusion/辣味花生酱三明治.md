# 辣味花生酱三明治 | Spicy PBJ

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 经典美式PBJ的果酱换成了辣椒酱，花生酱遇上是拉差，童年记忆瞬间长大成人。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *The classic PBJ swaps jelly for Sriracha — peanut butter meets chili sauce, and your childhood sandwich grows up overnight.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 吐司面包 | Sandwich bread | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 顺滑花生酱 | Creamy peanut butter | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 是拉差辣酱 | Sriracha sauce | 10ml / 2 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 10ml / 2 tsp |
| 黄瓜薄片 | Cucumber slices | 6片 / 6 slices |
| 香菜叶 | Cilantro leaves | 少许 / a few |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调辣花生酱 | Mix Spicy PB
花生酱与是拉差、蜂蜜混合均匀成辣甜酱。
Stir peanut butter with Sriracha and honey into a sweet-spicy spread.

### 2. 组装 | Assemble
一片面包涂辣花生酱，铺上黄瓜片和香菜叶，盖上另一片。
Spread spicy PB on one slice, layer cucumber and cilantro, close with the other slice.

### 3. 煎烤（可选）| Pan-Toast (Optional)
平底锅小火两面各煎1-2分钟至金黄酥脆，对角切开。
Optional: pan-toast over low heat 1-2 min per side until golden crisp. Cut diagonally.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 花生酱的脂肪与蜂蜜的果糖协同缓冲辣椒素的刺激，黄瓜的含水量提供清凉对比，形成热-凉-甜-辣的感官循环。 *PB's fat and honey's fructose buffer capsaicin's sting, while cucumber's water content provides cooling contrast — a hot-cool-sweet-spicy sensory loop.*
