## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 混合坚果 | Mixed nuts (cashew, almond, pecan) | 2杯 / 2 cups (250g) |
| 白砂糖 | Sugar | 1/3杯 / 1/3 cup |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan pepper | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 熬焦糖 | Make Caramel
小锅中放入糖和水，中火加热不搅拌，煮至金琥珀色（约5分钟）。立即加入黄油搅匀。

Combine sugar and water in a small pot. Heat over medium without stirring until golden amber (~5 min). Immediately add butter, stir smooth.

### 2. 裹坚果 | Coat the Nuts
倒入坚果快速翻拌，让焦糖均匀裹住每颗坚果。撒入花椒粉和海盐，再拌匀。

Pour in nuts, toss quickly to coat evenly. Sprinkle Sichuan pepper and sea salt, toss again.

### 3. 冷却定型 | Cool & Set
倒在铺了烘焙纸的烤盘上摊开，用叉子拨散粘连的坚果。完全冷却后变脆，密封保存。

Spread onto parchment-lined tray, separate any clumps with a fork. Once fully cooled and crispy, store in an airtight container.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

焦糖化过程中产生的呋喃酮（焦糖甜香核心分子）与花椒的芳樟醇（柑橘花香分子）在嗅觉上形成"甜蜜+清新"的对比和弦，比单纯的焦糖坚果多出整个柑橘花香维度。花椒的山椒素还能抑制脂肪口感的饱腻信号，让你不知不觉多吃好几把。

*Caramelization's furaneol (the core caramel-sweet molecule) and Sichuan pepper's linalool (citrus-floral) form a "sweet + fresh" olfactory contrast chord, adding an entire citrus-floral dimension beyond plain caramel nuts. Sanshool also suppresses fat-satiety signals, making you unknowingly reach for handful after handful.*
