> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 花椒的山椒素在低温烘烤中缓慢释放，与蜂蜜的焦糖化产物结合形成一种独特的"麻甜"风味——就像你吃了一口蜂蜜突然嘴唇微微发麻。格兰诺拉中燕麦的淀粉在烤制中糊化再脱水，形成酥脆的簇状结构（clusters），每一簇都是花椒蜂蜜味的小炸弹。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Sichuan peppercorn's sanshool slowly releases during low-temp baking, combining with honey's caramelization products to create a unique "tingly-sweet" sensation — like biting into honey that makes your lips tingle. Oat starch gelatinizes then dehydrates during baking, forming crunchy clusters — each one a little bomb of peppercorn-honey flavor.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 燕麦片 | Rolled oats | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 混合坚果 | Mixed nuts (chopped) | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup |
| 椰子油 | Coconut oil (melted) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 干果 (出炉后加) | Dried fruit (add after baking) | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌匀 | Mix
燕麦、坚果、蜂蜜、椰子油、花椒粉、肉桂、海盐全部拌匀。

Toss oats, nuts, honey, coconut oil, peppercorn, cinnamon, and salt together.

### 2. 烤制 | Bake
铺在烤盘上压平（压紧才能形成大块），300°F (150°C) 烤25分钟。中途不要翻动！出炉后让它在烤盘上完全冷却。

Spread on baking sheet and press down firmly (compacting = big clusters). Bake at 300°F (150°C) for 25 min. Don't stir during baking! Let cool completely on the pan.

### 3. 掰块加果干 | Break & Add Fruit
冷却后掰成大块，拌入干果（蔓越莓、枸杞都好）。密封保存两周。

Break into clusters when cool, toss in dried fruit (cranberries, goji berries). Sealed storage keeps 2 weeks.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 烤的时候不要搅拌，冷却后才掰，这样才有大块 | Don't stir while baking — break apart only after cooling for big clusters |
| 花椒粉要新鲜磨的，预磨的香味差很多 | Freshly ground peppercorn is far more aromatic than pre-ground |
| 配酸奶吃，酸+麻+甜的体验绝了 | Pair with yogurt — the sour + tingly + sweet combo is incredible |
