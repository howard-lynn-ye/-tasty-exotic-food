# 酱油焦糖榛子 | Soy Caramel Hazelnuts

> 酱油的鲜咸让焦糖的甜更有层次，裹在烤过的榛子上就是一道让人停不下嘴的零食。这颗小小的坚果融合了法式焦糖技法、日式调味哲学和地中海榛子传统——三大美食文化的结晶。
>
> *Soy sauce's savory salt gives caramel multi-dimensional depth, coating toasted hazelnuts in an irresistible snack. This tiny nut marries French caramel technique, Japanese seasoning philosophy, and Mediterranean hazelnut tradition — a crystallization of three great food cultures.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 榛子 (去皮) | Hazelnuts (blanched) | 200g |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | 100g |
| 水 | Water | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 15g |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt | 适量 / for finishing |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤榛子 | Toast Hazelnuts
160°C烤箱烤榛子8-10分钟至金黄飘香，备用。

Toast hazelnuts in 160°C/325°F oven for 8-10 min until golden and fragrant. Set aside.

### 2. 煮焦糖 | Cook Caramel
糖和水入锅中火加热，不要搅拌，轻轻摇晃直到变成深琥珀色（约5-7分钟）。

Heat sugar and water in a saucepan over medium heat. Do not stir — just swirl gently until deep amber (~5-7 min).

### 3. 加酱油拌匀 | Add Soy & Toss
关火，迅速加入酱油（小心溅射！）和黄油搅匀。倒入烤好的榛子快速翻拌，让每颗都裹上酱油焦糖。

Off heat, quickly add soy sauce (careful — it spatters!) and butter, stir smooth. Pour in toasted hazelnuts and toss rapidly to coat every nut.

### 4. 冷却 | Cool
倒在铺了硅胶垫或油纸的烤盘上，用叉子拨散，趁热撒海盐片。完全冷却后变成脆壳。

Pour onto a silicone mat or parchment-lined tray. Separate with a fork, sprinkle flaky salt while hot. Once fully cool, the coating becomes a crispy shell.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 酱油加入时会剧烈冒泡，一定要离火再加 | Soy sauce causes violent bubbling — always add off heat |
| 动作要快，焦糖冷了就裹不住了 | Work fast — caramel sets quickly and won't coat if cold |
| 密封保存可以放一周，但通常活不过一天 | Keeps a week sealed, but rarely survives a day |
| 可以用杏仁、腰果替代榛子，效果一样好 | Almonds or cashews work just as well as hazelnuts |
