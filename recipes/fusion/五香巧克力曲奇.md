# 五香巧克力曲奇 | Five-Spice Choc Cookies

> ⏱ 30分钟 | 💰~$5/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、烘焙、零食

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 中式五香粉潜伏在美式巧克力曲奇的每一口裂纹里，桂皮和丁香在可可的暗夜中闪烁。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Chinese five-spice hides in every crack of an American chocolate cookie — cinnamon and clove flickering in cocoa's dark night.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 250g / 2 cups |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 3g / ¾ tsp |
| 黑巧克力碎 | Dark chocolate chips | 150g / 1 cup |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter, softened | 115g / ½ cup |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 150g / ¾ cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调面团 | Mix Dough
黄油和红糖打发，加入鸡蛋，筛入面粉和五香粉拌匀，折入巧克力碎。
Cream butter and brown sugar, beat in egg, fold in sifted flour and five-spice, stir in chocolate chips.

### 2. 成型冷藏 | Shape & Chill
面团挖成核桃大小球状，间隔排列在烤盘上，冷藏15分钟定型。
Scoop walnut-sized dough balls onto lined baking sheets, spacing evenly. Chill 15 min to firm up.

### 3. 烘烤 | Bake
180°C/350°F烤10-12分钟至边缘金黄中间微软，出炉在烤盘上冷却5分钟。
Bake at 180°C/350°F for 10-12 min until edges are golden and centers are soft. Cool on sheet 5 min.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 五香粉中的丁香酚（eugenol）与可可中的苯乙醛共享温暖的芳香化学骨架，两者叠加产生比单独使用更深邃的香料感。 *Eugenol in five-spice shares a warm aromatic backbone with cocoa's phenylacetaldehyde; together they create a spice depth greater than either alone.*
