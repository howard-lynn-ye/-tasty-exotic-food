> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 白味噌的咸甜发酵底蕴，遇上蜂蜜的直球甜感，再加一点米醋的酸度来平衡，就形成了一层"鲜甜铠甲"。味噌中的谷氨酸（鲜味）和蜂蜜中的果糖在高温烤制下共同发生美拉德反应，翅根表面形成焦脆的琥珀色糖壳——一口咬下去，外壳脆裂、肉汁涌出。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *White miso's salty-sweet fermented depth meets honey's straightforward sweetness, balanced by a touch of rice vinegar acidity, forming a "umami-sweet armor." Glutamate from miso and fructose from honey undergo Maillard reactions together at high heat, creating a crispy amber glaze on the drumettes — one bite cracks the shell and releases a flood of juices.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 翅根 | Chicken drumettes | 500g (~12个 / ~12 pieces) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 姜末 | Grated ginger | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱腌制 | Mix & Marinate
碗中将味噌、蜂蜜、酱油、米醋、蒜泥、姜末和芝麻油搅拌均匀至味噌完全溶解。翅根擦干，放入酱中腌制至少30分钟。

Whisk miso, honey, soy sauce, rice vinegar, garlic, ginger, and sesame oil until miso is fully dissolved. Pat drumettes dry, then marinate at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 烤制 | Bake
烤箱预热200°C (400°F)。翅根铺在铺了锡纸的烤盘上，间隔留空。烤20分钟。

Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Arrange drumettes on a foil-lined baking sheet with space between them. Bake 20 minutes.

### 3. 刷酱收汁 | Glaze & Broil
取出刷一层腌制酱汁，翻面，再刷一层。开 Broil 档烤3-5分钟至表面焦糖化。

Remove, brush with marinade, flip, brush again. Switch to Broil for 3–5 minutes until the glaze caramelizes.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒白芝麻和葱花，搭配米饭或单独当零食吃。

Sprinkle sesame seeds and scallions. Serve with rice or as finger food.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用白味噌（sweet/white miso），不要用红味噌，太咸 | Use white/sweet miso, NOT red miso — too salty |
| 烤前一定擦干翅根表面水分，否则不脆 | Pat drumettes bone-dry before baking for crispiness |
| Broil 时紧盯着，蜂蜜糊了就苦了 | Watch closely during Broil — burnt honey turns bitter |
| 腌过夜效果最佳 | Overnight marination yields the best results |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么味噌+蜂蜜是天作之合 / Why miso + honey is a perfect match:**
> - 味噌的谷氨酸（鲜味）+ 蜂蜜的果糖（甜味）= 鲜甜叠加
> - 味噌中的发酵产物（氨基酸）在高温下加速美拉德反应
> - 蜂蜜 160°C 以上焦糖化产生太妃糖和坚果香气
> - 米醋的酸度切割油腻，让整体风味更加明亮
>
> *Miso's glutamate (umami) + honey's fructose (sweet) = layered savoriness. Fermented amino acids in miso accelerate Maillard reactions at high heat. Honey caramelizes above 160°C, producing toffee and nutty aromas. Rice vinegar's acidity cuts through richness, keeping the flavor profile bright.*
