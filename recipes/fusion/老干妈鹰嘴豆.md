## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鹰嘴豆（罐头） | Canned chickpeas | 1罐 / 1 can (400g), drained |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Spicy Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 花椒粉（可选） | Sichuan pepper powder (optional) | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 干燥鹰嘴豆 | Dry the Chickpeas
沥干鹰嘴豆，用厨房纸彻底擦干（关键步骤！越干越脆）。拌入橄榄油和盐。

Drain chickpeas, pat completely dry with paper towels (critical step — drier = crispier). Toss with olive oil and salt.

### 2. 烤至酥脆 | Roast Until Crispy
铺在烤盘上单层摊开，200°C/400°F 烤20-25分钟，中途翻动一次，烤至金黄酥脆。

Spread in a single layer on a baking sheet. Roast at 200°C/400°F for 20-25 min, shaking once, until golden and crispy.

### 3. 拌老干妈 | Toss with Lao Gan Ma
出炉趁热倒入碗中，加入老干妈和花椒粉（如用），翻拌均匀。撒芝麻，趁脆食用。

Transfer hot chickpeas to a bowl. Add Lao Gan Ma and Sichuan pepper (if using), toss to coat. Sprinkle sesame, eat while crispy.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

鹰嘴豆烘烤后外壳脱水形成玻璃态淀粉层，提供类似薯片的酥脆口感，而内部保持柔软的蛋白质核心。老干妈的辣椒油渗入微裂缝中，每一口都从外到内释放辣-鲜-脆的三重层次。

*Roasting dehydrates chickpea shells into a glassy starch layer that mimics chip-like crunch while the interior stays soft and protein-rich. Lao Gan Ma's chili oil seeps into micro-cracks, delivering spicy-umami-crunchy triple layers from outside in with each bite.*
