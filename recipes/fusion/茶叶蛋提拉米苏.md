# 茶叶蛋提拉米苏 | Tea Egg Tiramisu

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 冷藏 4小时 | 💰 ~$6/份(4-6人) | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、免烤箱、甜品、惊艳

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 用茶叶蛋的卤汁（红茶+八角+酱油）替代咖啡去浸泡手指饼干，这可能是世界上最大胆的提拉米苏变奏。茶叶蛋卤汁的温暖香料风味和马斯卡彭的奶油感碰撞，创造出一种前所未有的"东方提拉米苏"。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Replace espresso with tea egg braising liquid (black tea + star anise + soy sauce) to soak ladyfingers — possibly the world's most audacious tiramisu variation. The warm spice notes of tea egg broth collide with mascarpone's creaminess to create an unprecedented "Eastern tiramisu."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 手指饼干 | Ladyfinger cookies | 200g / 7 oz |
| 马斯卡彭芝士 | Mascarpone cheese | 250g / 8 oz |
| 红茶包 | Black tea bags | 3个 / 3 bags |
| 八角 | Star anise | 2个 / 2 pieces |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 60g / 1/4 cup |
| 鸡蛋黄 | Egg yolks | 3个 / 3 |
| 可可粉 | Cocoa powder | 适量 / for dusting |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮茶卤 | Brew Tea Egg Liquid
400ml水煮沸，加3个红茶包、八角、酱油和1汤匙红糖，小火煮5分钟。捞出茶包和八角，冷却备用。

Boil 400ml water, add tea bags, star anise, soy sauce, and 1 tbsp brown sugar. Simmer 5 min. Remove bags and anise, cool completely.

### 2. 做奶油层 | Make Cream
蛋黄和剩余红糖隔水加热搅打至浓稠发白。离火，拌入马斯卡彭至顺滑。

Whisk yolks and remaining brown sugar over a double boiler until thick and pale. Remove from heat, fold in mascarpone until smooth.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
手指饼干快速蘸茶卤（2秒即可，别泡烂），铺一层在容器底部。铺一层奶油。重复一次。

Briefly dip ladyfingers in tea liquid (2 seconds — don't soak). Layer in a dish. Spread cream on top. Repeat layers once.

### 4. 冷藏上桌 | Chill & Serve
盖保鲜膜冷藏至少4小时（过夜最佳）。上桌前筛可可粉，可用八角装饰。

Cover and refrigerate at least 4 hours (overnight is best). Dust cocoa powder before serving. Garnish with a star anise.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么茶叶蛋风味能做提拉米苏 / Why this works:**
> 传统提拉米苏用咖啡提供苦味深度。茶叶蛋卤汁用不同路径达到同样目的：红茶的茶多酚提供涩感和苦味（类比咖啡因），八角的茴香脑增添温暖甜感，酱油的谷氨酸添加隐藏的鲜味层——最终效果比咖啡更复杂。马斯卡彭的乳脂是完美的风味缓冲区，让所有香料风味变得丝滑圆润。
>
> *Classic tiramisu uses espresso for bitter depth. Tea egg broth achieves this differently: black tea's tannins deliver astringency and bitterness (paralleling caffeine), star anise's anethole adds warm sweetness, soy sauce's glutamate layers hidden umami — the result is MORE complex than coffee. Mascarpone's milk fat acts as a perfect flavor buffer, smoothing all spice notes into silk.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 茶卤一定要完全冷却再蘸饼干 | Cool the tea liquid COMPLETELY before dipping ladyfingers |
| 蘸饼干速度要快，2秒足够，泡久了会烂 | Quick dip only (2 sec) — longer and the cookies disintegrate |
| 过夜冷藏风味最好，让茶香渗透每一层 | Overnight chilling yields the best flavor — tea infuses every layer |
| 酱油别多加，微微一点提鲜就好 | Easy on the soy sauce — just a hint for umami, not saltiness |
