# 五香枫糖格兰诺拉 | Five-Spice Maple Granola

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$4/批(约6份) | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、早餐、健康

> 中国五香粉遇上加拿大枫糖浆，在烤箱里变成金黄酥脆的格兰诺拉麦片。八角和桂皮的暖香与枫糖的焦甜交织，配上酸奶或牛奶就是一顿让人期待起床的早餐。
>
> *Chinese five-spice meets Canadian maple syrup, baked into golden crunchy granola clusters. Star anise and cinnamon warmth weaves through maple's caramel sweetness — paired with yogurt or milk, it's a breakfast worth waking up for.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 燕麦片 (大片) | Rolled oats (old-fashioned) | 300g |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup (pure) | 80ml |
| 椰子油 | Coconut oil (melted) | 60ml |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1.5茶匙 / 1.5 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 混合坚果 (核桃、杏仁) | Mixed nuts (walnut, almond) | 100g，粗切 / 100g, roughly chopped |
| 南瓜籽 | Pumpkin seeds | 50g |
| 椰子片 | Coconut flakes | 30g |
| 枸杞或蔓越莓干 | Goji berries or dried cranberries | 50g |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 混合 | Combine
大碗中将燕麦、坚果、南瓜籽和五香粉盐拌匀。另将枫糖和融化的椰子油混合后浇入，翻拌至每片燕麦都裹上糖油。

In a large bowl toss oats, nuts, pumpkin seeds, five-spice, and salt. Mix maple syrup with melted coconut oil, pour over, and stir until every flake is coated.

### 2. 烤制 | Bake
铺在烤盘上压平压实（这样出来才有大块），160°C烤25分钟。中途不要搅拌！只轻轻转一次烤盘方向。

Spread on baking sheet, press down firmly (this creates clusters). Bake at 160°C/325°F for 25 min. Do NOT stir mid-bake — just rotate the tray once.

### 3. 加配料 | Add Mix-Ins
出炉后立刻撒上椰子片，静置完全冷却（至少30分钟）。冷却后掰成大块，拌入枸杞或蔓越莓干。

Sprinkle coconut flakes immediately. Cool completely on the tray (at least 30 min). Break into clusters, then fold in goji berries or cranberries.

### 4. 存储 | Store
装入密封罐，室温可保存2周。配酸奶、牛奶或直接抓着吃。

Store in airtight jar, keeps 2 weeks at room temp. Serve with yogurt, milk, or eat by the handful.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 压实不搅是形成大块cluster的秘诀 | Press flat and don't stir — that's how you get big clusters |
| 五香粉从少加起，太多会有药味 | Start with less five-spice — too much tastes medicinal |
| 枸杞必须冷却后再加，否则会变软 | Add goji after cooling or they'll go mushy |
| 纯枫糖浆口感远好于枫糖味糖浆 | Pure maple syrup is far superior to maple-flavored syrup |
