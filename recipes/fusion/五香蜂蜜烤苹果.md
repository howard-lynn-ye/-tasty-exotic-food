> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 五香粉中的八角含茴香脑（anethole），肉桂含肉桂醛（cinnamaldehyde），丁香含丁香酚（eugenol）——这三种芳香化合物都是脂溶性的，烤苹果释放的果胶和糖分恰好做它们的载体。相比西方肉桂烤苹果，五香粉增加了花椒的麻和八角的甜茴香，层次感翻倍。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Five-spice's star anise contains anethole, cinnamon has cinnamaldehyde, and cloves offer eugenol — all fat-soluble aromatic compounds carried perfectly by the apple's released pectin and sugars. Compared to Western cinnamon-baked apples, five-spice adds Sichuan peppercorn's tingle and star anise's sweet anise, doubling the complexity.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大苹果 | Large apples (Honeycrisp/Fuji) | 2个 / 2 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 核桃碎 | Chopped walnuts | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 香草冰淇淋 (可选) | Vanilla ice cream (optional) | 1勺 / 1 scoop |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备苹果 | Prep Apples
苹果切半去核（不要削皮），在果肉上划几刀帮助入味。

Halve apples and core them (keep the skin). Score flesh lightly to help flavors absorb.

### 2. 调味烤制 | Season & Bake
苹果切面朝上放烤盘。混合融化黄油、蜂蜜和五香粉，刷在苹果上，填入核桃碎。375°F (190°C) 烤30分钟。

Place apple halves cut-side up. Mix melted butter, honey, and five-spice; brush on apples, fill with walnuts. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30 min.

### 3. 上桌 | Serve
趁热配一勺香草冰淇淋，冷热交融最完美。

Serve hot with a scoop of vanilla ice cream — the hot-cold contrast is perfection.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 选硬实的苹果品种（Honeycrisp/Fuji），烤后不会烂 | Use firm apple varieties — they hold shape when baked |
| 五香粉从少量开始，太多会发苦 | Start with less five-spice — too much gets bitter |
| 可以撒几粒海盐提升甜味层次 | A few flakes of sea salt elevate the sweetness |
