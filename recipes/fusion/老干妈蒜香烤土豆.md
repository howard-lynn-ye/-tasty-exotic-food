> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 西式蒜香烤土豆的外酥内粉，加上老干妈辣椒油的"辣鲜油香"——这就是中西合璧最朴实最好吃的样子。老干妈的辣椒油代替了传统的橄榄油，在高温烤制中辣椒碎和豆豉碎直接焦化在土豆表面，形成一层"辣酥壳"。蒜泥在烤箱里变成金色蒜酥，和老干妈的蒜辣形成双重蒜味暴击。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Western garlic roasted potatoes' crispy-outside-fluffy-inside, plus LGM chili oil's "spicy-savory-oily fragrance" — this is East-meets-West at its most humble and delicious. LGM's chili oil replaces olive oil; during high-heat roasting, chili bits and fermented black bean bits char directly onto potato surfaces, forming a "spicy crisp shell." Garlic turns golden and crispy in the oven, creating a double-garlic assault with LGM's own garlic.*

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌料 | Toss
土豆切半，和整蒜瓣、老干妈、橄榄油、盐和黑胡椒一起放大碗里翻拌均匀。

Halve potatoes. Toss with whole garlic cloves, LGM, olive oil, salt, and black pepper in a large bowl.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱预热 220°C (425°F)。土豆切面朝下铺在烤盘上。烤 30-35分钟至切面金黄酥脆。

Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Place potatoes cut-side down on a baking sheet. Roast 30–35 minutes until cut sides are golden and crispy.

### 3. 出炉 | Remove
用锅铲铲起时如果听到"咔嗒"声，说明酥壳成功了。

When you hear a "click" as you lift them with a spatula, the crispy shell is a success.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒葱花和香菜，趁热吃。挤出烤软的蒜瓣涂在土豆上。

Sprinkle scallions and cilantro. Eat hot. Squeeze roasted garlic cloves onto the potatoes.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 切面朝下是酥脆的关键 | Cut-side down is the key to crispiness |
| 不要翻面！让底部一直接触热烤盘 | Don't flip — let the bottom stay on the hot pan |
| 整蒜瓣不用切，烤完变成蒜泥涂着吃 | Whole garlic cloves — they become spreadable paste after roasting |
| 用 Yukon Gold 或红皮小土豆最好 | Yukon Gold or red baby potatoes work best |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **老干妈为什么能让一切变好吃 / Why LGM makes everything better:**
> - 豆豉（发酵黑豆）= 浓缩的游离谷氨酸（鲜味炸弹）
> - 辣椒油 = 脂溶性辣椒素的载体（持久的温热感）
> - 炸蒜碎 = 蒜氨酸裂解产物（烤蒜香气的源头）
> - 三者混合在高温烤制中进一步美拉德反应 = 复杂到无法复制的风味
>
> *Fermented black beans = concentrated free glutamate (umami bomb). Chili oil = carrier for fat-soluble capsaicin (lingering warmth). Fried garlic bits = alliin breakdown products (source of roasted garlic aroma). All three undergo further Maillard reactions during high-heat roasting = irreplicably complex flavor.*
