# 老干妈鸡蛋三明治 | LGM Egg Sandwich

> ⏱ 15分钟 | 💰~$3/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、早餐、快手

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 老干妈辣椒酱是中国留学生的乡愁，把它抹进美式鸡蛋三明治，思乡的胃就有了着陆点。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Lao Gan Ma is homesickness in a jar for Chinese students abroad — spread it in an American egg sandwich, and your nostalgic stomach finally has a landing pad.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 吐司或贝果 | Toast or bagel | 2片 / 2 slices or 1 |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 老干妈风味豆豉 | Lao Gan Ma chili crisp | 20g / 1 heaping tbsp |
| 美式芝士片 | American cheese slice | 1片 / 1 |
| 生菜 | Lettuce leaf | 1片 / 1 |
| 蛋黄酱 | Mayonnaise | 15g / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煎蛋 | Fry Eggs
平底锅少油，打入鸡蛋煎至边缘焦脆、蛋黄半熟（或全熟随意）。
Fry eggs in a lightly oiled pan until edges are crispy, yolk still runny (or cook through to preference).

### 2. 涂酱组装 | Spread & Stack
吐司烤至金黄，一面涂蛋黄酱，另一面涂老干妈；依次叠放生菜、芝士片、煎蛋。
Toast bread golden, spread mayo on one side and Lao Gan Ma on the other. Stack lettuce, cheese, fried eggs.

### 3. 合拢切开 | Close & Cut
合上三明治，轻压后对角切开，豆豉的香辣与流心蛋黄交织。
Close sandwich, press gently, cut diagonally. Chili crisp's crunch mingles with flowing egg yolk.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 老干妈中的发酵豆豉富含生物胺和游离氨基酸，与蛋黄卵磷脂乳化后产生浓郁的鲜辣复合风味，蛋黄酱的醋酸提亮整体。 *LGM's fermented black beans are rich in biogenic amines and free amino acids; emulsified with egg yolk lecithin, they create a rich umami-spicy complex, brightened by mayo's acetic acid.*
