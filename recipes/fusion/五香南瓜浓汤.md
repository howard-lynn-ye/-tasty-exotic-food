# 五香南瓜浓汤 | Five-Spice Pumpkin Soup
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/五香南瓜浓汤.svg" width="500" alt="五香南瓜浓汤"></p>

> 🤖 AI Original | ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$3/4份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、秋冬暖汤、一锅出

> 西式南瓜浓汤遇上中国五香粉——八角、肉桂、花椒、丁香、茴香的温暖香气让经典南瓜汤从"好喝"升级到"惊艳"。一锅煮好用搅拌机打成丝绒般顺滑，淋一圈椰奶就是高级餐厅水准。
>
> *Western pumpkin soup meets Chinese five-spice — star anise, cinnamon, Sichuan pepper, clove, and fennel transform a classic from "good" to "stunning." One-pot, blender-smooth, drizzled with coconut cream for restaurant-level elegance.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 南瓜（去皮切块） | Pumpkin or butternut squash (cubed) | 500g / ~4 cups |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium |
| 五香粉 | Chinese five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 椰奶 | Coconut milk | 200ml / ~1 cup |
| 鸡汤或蔬菜汤 | Chicken or vegetable broth | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 姜 | Fresh ginger | 1小段 / 1" piece |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒香底料 | Sauté Aromatics
锅中少油，炒洋葱丁和姜片至软（约3分钟）。加五香粉炒30秒至香气释出。

Sauté diced onion and ginger in a little oil until soft (~3 min). Add five-spice, toast 30 sec until fragrant.

### 2. 煮至软烂 | Simmer Until Tender
倒入南瓜块和高汤，大火煮沸后转中小火煮20分钟至南瓜用叉子轻松戳透。

Add pumpkin and broth. Bring to boil, reduce to medium-low, cook 20 min until fork-tender.

### 3. 打成浓汤 | Blend Smooth
加入一半椰奶，用手持搅拌机（或倒入搅拌机）打至丝滑。调盐。盛碗后淋剩余椰奶画圈装饰。

Add half the coconut milk, blend with immersion blender (or in a blender) until silky. Season with salt. Ladle into bowls, swirl remaining coconut milk on top.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

五香粉中的反式茴香脑（八角和茴香的核心香气分子）与南瓜中的β-胡萝卜素通过脂肪介质（椰奶）结合，创造出一种类似"烘烤甜面包"的温暖复合香气。中国五香粉中的花椒成分提供的微麻感打破了浓汤的单一口感，增加了触觉维度。

*Trans-anethole from star anise and fennel in five-spice combines with pumpkin's beta-carotene via coconut milk's fat medium, creating a warm compound aroma akin to "toasted sweet bread." The Sichuan pepper component in five-spice adds a micro-tingle that breaks the soup's monotexture with a tactile dimension.*

## 替代食材 | Substitutions

| 原料 / Original | 替代 / Substitute | 备注 / Notes |
|-----------------|-------------------|--------------|
| 南瓜 | 冷冻南瓜泥 canned pumpkin purée 425g | 跳过切块步骤，煮10分钟即可 / Skip cubing |
| 五香粉 | 自制：等量八角+肉桂+花椒+丁香+茴香磨粉 | DIY blend for fresher flavor |
| 椰奶 | 淡奶油 heavy cream | 更西式 / More Western flavor profile |
