## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 燕麦片 | Rolled oats | 150g / 1.5 cups |
| 白巧克力 | White chocolate | 100g / 3.5 oz |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 杏仁碎 | Chopped almonds | 50g / 1/3 cup |
| 蔓越莓干 | Dried cranberries | 30g / 1/4 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1小撮 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤燕麦 | Toast Oats
燕麦片铺在烤盘上，170°C (340°F) 烤8分钟至微微金黄有坚果香。

Spread oats on a sheet pan. Toast at 170°C (340°F) for 8 min until lightly golden and nutty.

### 2. 调基底 | Make Base
锅中融化黄油，加入蜂蜜搅匀。离火后拌入抹茶粉至颜色均匀翠绿。

Melt butter in a pan, stir in honey. Off heat, whisk in matcha until evenly vivid green.

### 3. 混合压模 | Mix & Press
大碗中混合烤燕麦、杏仁碎、蔓越莓干和盐。倒入抹茶蜂蜜黄油，拌匀。压入铺了烘焙纸的方形模具（20×20cm），用力压紧压实。

In a bowl, combine toasted oats, almonds, cranberries, and salt. Pour in matcha-honey-butter, mix. Press firmly into a parchment-lined 20×20cm pan.

### 4. 白巧克力顶 | White Choc Top
白巧克力隔水融化，淋在燕麦棒表面。冷藏1小时至凝固，切成8条。

Melt white chocolate, drizzle over the oat base. Chill 1 hour until set, cut into 8 bars.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **抹茶+白巧：苦味遮蔽与鲜味释放 / Matcha + white choc: bitterness masking & umami release:**
> - 白巧克力只含可可脂（无可可粉），纯脂肪体系能高效遮蔽抹茶的儿茶素苦味
> - 被遮蔽苦味后，抹茶的L-茶氨酸鲜甜味"浮出水面"——类似加奶让咖啡变柔和的原理
> - 燕麦的β-葡聚糖是可溶性纤维，减缓糖分吸收速度，避免"血糖过山车"
> - 抹茶的咖啡因（~30mg/g）+ L-茶氨酸（~30mg/g）组合产生"警觉但不焦躁"的认知状态
>
> *White chocolate contains only cocoa butter (no cocoa solids) — a pure fat system that efficiently masks matcha catechin bitterness. With bitterness masked, matcha's L-theanine umami-sweetness "surfaces" — similar to how milk softens coffee. Oat beta-glucan is soluble fiber that slows sugar absorption, preventing "blood sugar rollercoaster." Matcha's caffeine (~30mg/g) + L-theanine (~30mg/g) combo produces an "alert but not jittery" cognitive state.*
