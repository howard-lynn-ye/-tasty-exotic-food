# 酱油焦糖烤无花果 | Soy Caramel Roasted Fig

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、甜点、开胃菜

> 无花果切半烤到软糯流蜜，淋上酱油焦糖——这个组合听起来大胆，但酱油的鲜咸让无花果天然的甜更加突出，焦糖提供了粘稠的质感和苦甜底味。配上一块布拉塔芝士或一球冰淇淋，就是一道让客人全体沉默的甜点。
>
> *Figs halved and roasted until honeyed and soft, drizzled with soy caramel — sounds bold, but soy's savory salt amplifies figs' natural sweetness while caramel adds viscous bittersweet depth. Paired with burrata or ice cream, this is a dessert that silences the entire table.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 新鲜无花果 | Fresh figs | 8个 / 8 |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | 60g |
| 水 | Water | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 20g |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp，温热 / warmed |
| 海盐片 | Flaky sea salt | 少许 / a pinch |
| 布拉塔芝士或冰淇淋 | Burrata or ice cream | 配菜 / to serve |
| 百里香 (可选) | Fresh thyme (optional) | 几枝 / a few sprigs |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤无花果 | Roast Figs
无花果纵向切半，切面朝上放在烤盘中。190°C烤12-15分钟至软化、边缘微焦、汁液渗出。

Halve figs lengthwise, place cut-side up on a baking tray. Roast at 190°C/375°F for 12-15 min until softened, edges caramelized, juices weeping.

### 2. 煮酱油焦糖 | Make Soy Caramel
趁无花果烤的时候：糖和水入锅中火加热不搅拌，煮至深琥珀色。关火加入酱油（小心溅射）、温奶油和黄油，搅至顺滑。

While figs roast: heat sugar and water over medium without stirring until deep amber. Off heat, add soy sauce (careful — spatters!), warm cream, and butter. Whisk until smooth.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
烤好的无花果摆盘，中间放布拉塔芝士（或旁边放冰淇淋），淋上酱油焦糖酱，撒海盐片。

Arrange roasted figs on a plate, place burrata in the center (or ice cream alongside). Drizzle soy caramel generously, finish with flaky salt.

### 4. 装饰 | Garnish
可以放几枝百里香增加草本香气，趁热上桌。

Add thyme sprigs for herbal fragrance if desired. Serve warm.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 无花果要选熟透的，捏起来微软、香气浓 | Choose ripe figs — they should give slightly when pressed and smell fragrant |
| 焦糖不要过深，酱油本身就有颜色和苦味 | Don't over-darken the caramel — soy already adds color and bitterness |
| 布拉塔芝士的奶香和酱油焦糖是绝配 | Burrata's milky creaminess is a perfect match for soy caramel |
| 无花果季节外可以用烤梨或烤桃代替 | Out of fig season? Roasted pears or peaches work beautifully |
