# 酱油枫糖烤蔬菜串 | Soy Maple Veggie Skewers

> ⏱ 准备15分+烹饪18分 | 💰~$7/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、素食、烧烤

> **💡 灵感** — 酱油的亚洲鲜味遇上北美枫糖的琥珀甜香，为烤蔬菜带来意想不到的深度。这道菜证明了蔬菜烧烤不需要靠肉来撑场，酱汁就是灵魂。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Asian soy umami meets North American maple's amber sweetness, bringing unexpected depth to grilled vegetables. This dish proves veggie BBQ needs no meat to shine — the glaze is the star.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 西葫芦 | Zucchini | 1根 / 1 large |
| 彩椒 | Bell peppers | 2个 / 2 pcs (mixed colors) |
| 红洋葱 | Red onion | 1个 / 1 large |
| 樱桃番茄 | Cherry tomatoes | 12个 / 12 pcs |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves (minced) |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 / To taste |
| 竹签 | Bamboo skewers | 8根 / 8 pcs (soaked) |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调酱 | Make Glaze
酱油、枫糖浆、蒜末和橄榄油搅匀成釉汁。Whisk soy sauce, maple syrup, minced garlic, and olive oil into a glaze.

### 2. 穿串 | Skewer
蔬菜切大块（约3cm），交替穿串：西葫芦、彩椒、红洋葱、樱桃番茄。刷满酱汁，腌5分钟。Cut vegetables into large chunks (~1 inch), thread alternately: zucchini, bell pepper, red onion, cherry tomato. Brush generously with glaze, rest 5 minutes.

### 3. 烤制 | Grill
烤架中高火或烤箱220°C烤15-18分钟，每5分钟翻面并刷酱，至蔬菜边缘微焦。Grill on medium-high or bake at 425°F for 15-18 minutes, turn and re-glaze every 5 minutes, until edges are lightly charred.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
摆盘磨黑胡椒，淋剩余酱汁，可搭配藜麦或面包。Plate with freshly ground black pepper, drizzle remaining glaze, serve with quinoa or crusty bread.
