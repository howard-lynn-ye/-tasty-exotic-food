# 抹茶蜂蜜烤香蕉 | Matcha Honey Baked Banana

> ⏱ 准备5分+烹饪15分 | 💰~$5/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、甜品、日式

> **💡 灵感** — 抹茶的微苦清香、蜂蜜的甜润、烤香蕉的软糯焦香三者合一，是一道十分钟就能完成的高级甜品。灵感来自东南亚烤香蕉与日本茶道美学的碰撞。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Matcha's gentle bitterness, honey's sweetness, and baked banana's caramelized softness merge into an elevated dessert in under 15 minutes. Inspired by Southeast Asian grilled bananas meeting Japanese tea ceremony aesthetics.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 香蕉 | Banana | 4根 / 4 pcs (ripe) |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 40ml / 3 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 20g / 1½ tbsp |
| 椰丝 | Shredded coconut | 15g / 2 tbsp |
| 香草冰淇淋 | Vanilla ice cream | 4勺 / 4 scoops (optional) |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 少许 / A pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调抹茶蜜 | Make Matcha Honey
抹茶粉过筛，加入温蜂蜜搅至无颗粒。Sift matcha powder, stir into warm honey until lump-free.

### 2. 准备香蕉 | Prep Bananas
香蕉纵切两半（不去皮亦可），切面朝上放入烤盘，刷融化黄油。Halve bananas lengthwise (can leave skin on), place cut-side up on a baking sheet, brush with melted butter.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
淋上一半抹茶蜂蜜，烤箱200°C烤12-15分钟至香蕉边缘焦化变金。Drizzle half the matcha honey over bananas, bake at 400°F for 12-15 minutes until edges caramelize and turn golden.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
出炉再淋剩余抹茶蜜，撒烤椰丝和海盐。搭配一勺香草冰淇淋，冷热交融。Remove from oven, drizzle remaining matcha honey, sprinkle toasted coconut and sea salt. Top with a scoop of vanilla ice cream for hot-cold contrast.
