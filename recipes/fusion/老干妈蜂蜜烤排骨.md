## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱腌制 | Make Marinade
碗中混合老干妈、蜂蜜、酱油、蒜泥、五香粉和米醋。排骨两面涂抹均匀，覆保鲜膜冷藏腌制至少2小时（过夜最佳）。留¼的酱汁备用刷面。

Mix LGM, honey, soy sauce, garlic, five-spice, and rice vinegar. Coat ribs on both sides, cover with plastic wrap, and refrigerate at least 2 hours (overnight is best). Reserve ¼ of the sauce for glazing.

### 2. 低温慢烤 | Low-and-Slow Bake
排骨放在锡纸上，肉面朝上。用锡纸松松包住。烤箱 150°C (300°F) 烤 1.5小时。

Place ribs meat-side up on foil. Loosely wrap with foil. Bake at 150°C (300°F) for 1.5 hours.

### 3. 高温收酱 | High-Heat Glaze
打开锡纸，刷上预留酱汁。烤箱升至 220°C (425°F)，不盖锡纸再烤 15分钟至表面焦糖化、闪亮。

Open foil, brush with reserved sauce. Raise oven to 220°C (425°F), bake uncovered 15 more minutes until caramelized and glossy.

### 4. 切盘 | Slice & Serve
沿骨头之间切开，撒葱花和芝麻。

Cut between bones. Sprinkle scallions and sesame seeds.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 低温慢烤是关键——急不来，胶原蛋白需要时间 | Low-and-slow is key — can't rush collagen conversion |
| 排骨背面那层白膜要撕掉（或让肉店帮忙） | Remove the membrane on the bone side (or ask the butcher) |
| 刷酱用预留的新鲜酱，不要用接触过生肉的 | Glaze with reserved fresh sauce, not the marinade that touched raw meat |
| 肉温达到 90°C+ 就是"fall-off-the-bone"状态 | Internal temp 90°C+ = fall-off-the-bone tender |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **低温慢烤的胶原蛋白魔法 / Collagen magic of low-and-slow:**
> - 猪肋排含大量胶原蛋白（结缔组织）
> - 胶原蛋白在 70-80°C 开始转化为明胶（gelatin）
> - 明胶 = 肉汁浓稠 + 嘴唇粘粘的满足感
> - 老干妈中的豆豉和辣椒在长时间加热中释放更多鲜味
> - 最后的高温收酱让蜂蜜焦糖化 = 酥脆外壳
>
> *Pork ribs contain abundant collagen (connective tissue). Collagen begins converting to gelatin at 70–80°C. Gelatin = thick juices + lip-smacking satisfaction. LGM's black beans and chilies release more umami during prolonged heating. The final high-heat glaze caramelizes honey = crispy shell.*
