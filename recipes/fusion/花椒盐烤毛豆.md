# 花椒盐烤毛豆 | Sichuan Salt Edamame

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 日式居酒屋的毛豆撒上四川花椒盐，一颗小小的豆荚承载了成都到东京的麻辣快线。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Izakaya edamame dusted with Sichuan pepper salt — one tiny pod carries an express tingle line from Chengdu to Tokyo.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 冷冻毛豆（带壳）| Frozen edamame (in pods) | 300g / 10 oz |
| 花椒粒 | Whole Sichuan peppercorns | 5g / 1 tbsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 2g / ½ tsp |
| 干辣椒碎 | Red pepper flakes | 2g / ½ tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 做花椒盐 | Make Pepper Salt
干锅小火炒花椒粒至出香冒烟，与海盐一起研磨成粉。
Dry-toast Sichuan peppercorns over low heat until fragrant and smoking. Grind with sea salt into a fine powder.

### 2. 烤毛豆 | Roast Edamame
毛豆解冻沥水，拌入橄榄油、蒜粉，220°C/425°F烤12-15分钟至豆荚微焦起斑。
Thaw and drain edamame, toss with olive oil and garlic powder. Roast at 220°C/425°F for 12-15 min until pods are charred in spots.

### 3. 调味上桌 | Season & Serve
出炉趁热撒花椒盐和辣椒碎，翻拌均匀，装盘即食。
Toss hot edamame with pepper salt and chili flakes, serve immediately.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 烘烤使毛豆壳的木质素局部焦化产生烟熏味，花椒素激活触觉神经带来麻感，与盐的咸味形成麻-咸-鲜的三维刺激。 *Roasting partially caramelizes pod lignin for smokiness; hydroxy-alpha-sanshool activates tactile nerves for tingle, creating a 3D numbing-salty-umami sensation with salt.*
