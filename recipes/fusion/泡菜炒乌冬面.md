# 泡菜炒乌冬面 | Kimchi Udon Stir-Fry

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、快手菜、宿舍可做

> 韩国泡菜的酸辣与日本乌冬面的弹牙Q弹在铁锅中激情相遇。发酵泡菜自带的乳酸鲜味让酱汁不需要复杂调味就层次丰富，加上肥美的五花肉片和溏心蛋，这碗面是深夜食堂的最佳选择。
>
> *Korean kimchi's tangy heat meets Japanese udon's bouncy chew in a sizzling wok. The lactic acid funk of fermented kimchi builds complex flavor without complex seasoning. Add fatty pork belly slices and a runny egg — this is the ultimate late-night noodle bowl.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 冷冻乌冬面 | Frozen udon noodles | 2包 / 2 packs |
| 韩国泡菜 (熟成) | Korean kimchi (aged) | 1杯，切段 / 1 cup, chopped |
| 五花肉片 | Pork belly slices | 100g |
| 泡菜汁 | Kimchi juice | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 韩式辣酱 (可选) | Gochujang (optional) | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 2个 / 2 |
| 葱花、芝麻 | Scallion, sesame seeds | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煎肉 | Sear Pork
中火热锅不放油，五花肉片平铺煎至两面焦脆出油，盛出备用。

Heat dry pan over medium heat. Lay pork belly slices flat, sear until crispy and rendered on both sides. Set aside.

### 2. 炒泡菜 | Stir-Fry Kimchi
用锅中猪油爆炒泡菜2分钟至微焦，加入酱油、泡菜汁、辣酱和糖翻炒均匀。

Stir-fry kimchi in the rendered pork fat for 2 min until slightly charred. Add soy, kimchi juice, gochujang, and sugar. Toss.

### 3. 加面翻炒 | Add Noodles
乌冬面微波1分钟解冻后下锅，大火翻炒2分钟让面条吸收酱汁，淋芝麻油，放回五花肉片。

Microwave udon 1 min to thaw, add to pan. Stir-fry on high 2 min to absorb sauce. Drizzle sesame oil, return pork slices.

### 4. 煎蛋上桌 | Top & Serve
另起锅煎两个太阳蛋，放在面上，撒葱花芝麻。

Fry two sunny-side-up eggs in a separate pan. Place on noodles, sprinkle scallions and sesame.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用发酵时间久的老泡菜，酸味更浓郁，炒出来更香 | Use well-aged kimchi — deeper funk, better stir-fry flavor |
| 泡菜汁是灵魂，别倒掉 | Kimchi juice is gold — never throw it away |
| 冷冻乌冬比干乌冬更Q弹，强烈推荐 | Frozen udon is chewier than dried — highly recommended |
| 素食版可以用豆腐代替五花肉 | Vegetarian? Swap pork for firm tofu |
