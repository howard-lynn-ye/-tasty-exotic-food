# 五香枫糖烤培根 Five-Spice Maple Bacon

> 中国 x 北美 | 早餐/佐酒
> China x North America | Breakfast / Bar Snack

---

## 简介 Introduction

中国五香粉（花椒、八角、肉桂、丁香、小茴香）遇上加拿大枫糖浆和美式培根，
产生了令人难以置信的化学反应。五香粉的温暖辛香让甜腻的枫糖培根多了一层
深度和复杂感，培根在烤箱中变得酥脆如饼干，每一条都是甜、咸、辣、香的
四重奏。这可能是你吃过的最上瘾的培根。

Chinese five-spice (peppercorn, star anise, cinnamon, clove, fennel) meets
Canadian maple syrup and American bacon — an incredible chemical reaction.
Five-spice adds warmth and depth to the sweet-salty maple bacon. Oven-crisped
to cookie-like crunch, each strip is a quartet of sweet, salty, spicy, and
aromatic. Possibly the most addictive bacon you will ever eat.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 厚切培根 Thick-cut bacon | 12条 / 12 strips |
| 枫糖浆 Maple syrup | 60ml |
| 五香粉 Five-spice powder | 5g |
| 黑胡椒 Black pepper | 3g |
| 辣椒粉（可选）Cayenne pepper | 1g |
| 酱油 Soy sauce | 5ml |
| 红糖 Brown sugar | 15g |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **预热 Preheat**: 烤箱预热200°C，烤盘铺锡纸再放烤架。
   Preheat oven to 200°C, line a baking sheet with foil and set a rack on top.

2. **调酱 Mix glaze**: 枫糖浆、五香粉、黑胡椒、辣椒粉、酱油和红糖搅匀。
   Whisk maple syrup, five-spice, pepper, cayenne, soy sauce, and brown sugar.

3. **刷酱 Coat bacon**: 培根条单面刷上一半的酱汁，酱面朝上放在烤架上。
   Brush one side of each strip with half the glaze, place glaze-side up on rack.

4. **第一次烤 First bake**: 200°C烤12分钟至培根开始收缩出油。
   Bake at 200°C for 12 min until bacon starts to shrink and render fat.

5. **翻面刷酱 Flip and glaze**: 取出翻面，刷上剩余酱汁，撒少量五香粉。
   Remove, flip, brush remaining glaze on the other side, dust with five-spice.

6. **二次烤 Second bake**: 继续烤8-10分钟至深褐色且酥脆，注意不要烤焦。
   Bake 8-10 more min until deep brown and crispy. Watch carefully to avoid burning.

7. **冷却 Cool**: 取出静置5分钟，酱汁会凝固变得更酥脆。趁温热食用最佳。
   Rest 5 min — glaze will set and become crispier. Best served warm.
