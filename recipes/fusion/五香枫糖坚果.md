> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 中式五香粉（八角、桂皮、丁香、花椒、小茴香）遇上北美的枫糖浆，是"东方暖香料"和"西方甜树液"的跨太平洋握手。枫糖的焦糖化温度比蜂蜜低，更容易在坚果表面形成酥脆糖壳；五香粉中五种香料的挥发性芳香化合物在烤制中逐层释放，让每一口都有不同的香气层次。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Chinese five-spice (star anise, cinnamon, clove, Sichuan pepper, fennel) meets North American maple syrup — a trans-Pacific handshake between "Eastern warm spices" and "Western sweet tree sap." Maple caramelizes at a lower temperature than honey, forming a crispier shell on nuts; five-spice's volatile aromatics release in layers during roasting, making each bite a different aromatic experience.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 混合坚果 | Mixed nuts (walnut, almond, pecan, cashew) | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 蛋白 | Egg white | 1个 / 1 |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌蛋白 | Coat with Egg White
蛋白打至起泡（不需要打发），加入枫糖浆、五香粉、盐和红糖搅匀，倒入坚果翻拌均匀。

Whisk egg white until frothy (no need to form peaks). Add maple syrup, five-spice, salt, and brown sugar. Stir, then toss with nuts until evenly coated.

### 2. 烤制 | Bake
烤箱预热 160°C (325°F)。坚果单层铺在铺了油纸的烤盘上。烤20分钟，每7分钟取出翻拌一次。

Preheat oven to 160°C (325°F). Spread nuts in a single layer on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake 20 minutes, stirring every 7 minutes.

### 3. 冷却 | Cool
出炉后不要动！让它在烤盘上完全冷却，糖壳会自然变硬变脆。

Don't touch them right out of the oven! Let them cool completely on the sheet — the sugar shell hardens and crisps as it cools.

### 4. 掰开装罐 | Break & Store
冷却后用手掰成小簇，装密封罐。常温可保存两周。

Break into small clusters, store in an airtight jar. Keeps 2 weeks at room temperature.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 蛋白是"胶水"——让糖壳粘在坚果上 | Egg white is the "glue" — makes the sugar shell stick |
| 低温慢烤，高温会让枫糖烧苦 | Low-and-slow baking — high heat burns maple bitter |
| 五香粉只要1茶匙，太多会药味重 | Keep five-spice to 1 tsp — too much tastes medicinal |
| 装玻璃罐配丝带就是完美的自制礼物 | In a glass jar with ribbon = perfect homemade gift |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **五香粉+枫糖的化学浪漫 / The chemistry romance of five-spice + maple:**
> - 枫糖含有300+种风味化合物，其中"枫呋喃酮"带来独特焦糖调
> - 八角中的茴香脑（anethole）与枫糖的焦糖味产生"温暖共鸣"
> - 桂皮醛（cinnamon）和枫糖的木质调同源，互相放大
> - 蛋白在烘烤中形成薄膜，锁住糖壳的酥脆质地
>
> *Maple syrup contains 300+ flavor compounds; "maple furanone" provides its signature caramel note. Star anise's anethole creates "warm resonance" with maple's caramel. Cinnamaldehyde and maple share woody origins, amplifying each other. Egg white forms a thin film during baking, locking in the shell's crispy texture.*
