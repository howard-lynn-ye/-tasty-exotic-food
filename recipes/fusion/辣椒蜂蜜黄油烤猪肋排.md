# 辣椒蜂蜜黄油烤猪肋排 | Chili Honey Butter Baby Ribs

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 2.5小时 | 💰 ~$10/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、主菜、聚餐

> 低温慢烤让猪肋排的骨肉分离，最后刷上辣椒蜂蜜黄油在高温下焦糖化——辣椒的热力、蜂蜜的甘甜和黄油的奶香形成一层令人无法抗拒的亮丽釉面。手撕一根，汁水横流，这就是烤肉的最高境界。
>
> *Low-and-slow roasting renders baby back ribs fall-off-the-bone tender, finished with a chili honey butter glaze that caramelizes under high heat — chili's fire, honey's sweetness, and butter's richness form an irresistible glossy shell. Pull one off, juices running, and you've reached BBQ nirvana.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **肋排** | **Ribs** | |
| 猪小排 (baby back ribs) | Baby back ribs | 1架，约800g / 1 rack, ~800g |
| 盐 | Salt | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

| 苹果醋 | Apple cider vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

| 盐 | Salt | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 干腌 | Dry Rub
撕去肋排背面的筋膜。盐、胡椒、蒜粉和烟熏辣椒粉混合，均匀涂满肋排两面。

Remove the membrane from the back of the ribs. Mix salt, pepper, garlic powder, and smoked paprika. Rub all over both sides.

### 2. 低温慢烤 | Slow Roast
铝箔紧紧包住肋排，140°C烤2小时至肉极其软嫩、骨头微松。

Wrap ribs tightly in foil. Roast at 140°C/275°F for 2 hours until extremely tender and bones loosen.

### 3. 做辣蜜黄油 | Make Glaze
黄油小火融化，加蜂蜜、辣椒片、苹果醋、蒜泥和盐，搅拌煮2分钟至冒泡融合。

Melt butter on low, add honey, pepper flakes, vinegar, garlic, and salt. Stir and simmer 2 min until combined and bubbly.

### 4. 上釉收尾 | Glaze & Finish
打开铝箔，肋排放在烤架上。刷上厚厚一层辣蜜黄油，220°C烤10-15分钟至表面焦化冒泡。出炉再刷一层。

Unwrap ribs, place on a rack. Brush generously with chili honey butter. Roast at 220°C/425°F for 10-15 min until caramelized and bubbly. Brush once more out of the oven.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定要撕掉背面筋膜，否则嚼不动 | Always remove the back membrane or ribs will be chewy |
| 铝箔包紧是关键，蒸汽让肉更嫩 | Tight foil seal is key — the steam tenderizes |
| 辣度可以用辣椒片多少控制，怕辣减半 | Adjust heat with pepper flake quantity — halve for mild |
| 剩余的辣蜜黄油可以刷面包、烤鸡翅、涂玉米 | Leftover glaze works on bread, wings, or corn on the cob |
