> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 郫县豆瓣酱被称为"川菜之魂"，其中蚕豆和辣椒经过数月发酵产生的游离氨基酸浓度极高。高温烤制时这些氨基酸与蔬菜表面的天然糖分发生剧烈的美拉德反应，在每块蔬菜上形成一层焦香辣味釉面。普通烤蔬菜只有甜，加了豆瓣酱后有甜+鲜+辣+焦香四重味觉。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Pixian doubanjiang, the "soul of Sichuan cooking," has extremely high free amino acid levels from months of fermenting broad beans and chilies. High-heat roasting triggers intense Maillard reactions between these amino acids and vegetables' natural sugars, forming a spicy caramelized glaze on every piece. Plain roasted veggies are just sweet — doubanjiang adds umami + heat + char for four-dimensional flavor.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 混合蔬菜 | Mixed vegetables (see below) | 4杯 / 4 cups total |
| 豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang (broad bean chili paste) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic (minced) | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

**推荐蔬菜 / Recommended vegetables:** 花椰菜 cauliflower、胡萝卜 carrots、西葫芦 zucchini、甜椒 bell peppers、红薯 sweet potato

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱拌菜 | Coat Vegetables
豆瓣酱、橄榄油、蒜末、蜂蜜混合。蔬菜切均匀大小的块，与酱汁拌匀。

Mix doubanjiang, olive oil, garlic, and honey. Cut vegetables into even-sized pieces, toss with the sauce.

### 2. 高温烤制 | Roast
铺在烤盘上（不要堆叠），425°F (220°C) 烤25分钟，中途翻一次。

Spread on baking sheet in a single layer (no overlapping). Roast at 425°F (220°C) for 25 min, flip halfway.

### 3. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻。配白饭或者当配菜都完美。

Sprinkle sesame seeds. Perfect over rice or as a side dish.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 蔬菜不要堆叠，否则是蒸不是烤 | Don't crowd the pan — steaming instead of roasting kills the char |
| 根茎类切小点、叶菜类切大点，确保同时熟 | Cut root veggies smaller, softer veggies larger for even cooking |
| 豆瓣酱本身很咸，不需要额外加盐 | Doubanjiang is already very salty — no extra salt needed |
