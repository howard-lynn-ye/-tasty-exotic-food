## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备蘑菇 | Prep Mushrooms
蘑菇撕成大块（不要切太小，烤后会缩水）。用厨房纸擦干表面水分——这是关键！

Tear mushrooms into large pieces (they shrink when roasting). Pat bone-dry with paper towels — this is critical!

### 2. 调味烤制 | Season & Roast
蘑菇与融化黄油、酱油、蒜片、黑胡椒拌匀。单层铺在烤盘上（不要挤！）。220°C (425°F) 烤15分钟不要翻动。

Toss mushrooms with melted butter, soy sauce, garlic slices, and pepper. Spread in a single layer (DON'T crowd!). Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 15 min without touching.

### 3. 翻面焦化 | Flip & Char
翻面一次，放上百里香枝，继续烤5分钟至边缘焦脆、蘑菇缩成深棕色"肉干"质感。

Flip once, add thyme sprigs, roast 5 more min until edges are crispy and mushrooms shrink to deep brown "jerky" texture.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
可选撒帕玛森芝士。作为牛排配菜、拌饭、夹三明治都绝了。

Optional Parmesan dusting. Perfect as steak side, over rice, or in sandwiches.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **鲜味协同效应的最佳演绎 / The ultimate umami synergy demonstration:**
> - 蘑菇含鸟苷酸（GMP ~100mg/100g），酱油含谷氨酸（~1000mg/100ml）
> - GMP + MSG 的协同比单独使用鲜味感知增强8-15倍（1+1=16的味觉数学）
> - 黄油的酪酸（butyric acid）提供奶香基底，同时乳脂包裹鲜味分子延长口腔停留时间
> - 烤蘑菇失水后细胞壁破裂，释放更多胞内鲜味物质——"浓缩即是正义"
> - 百里香的百里酚（thymol）提供清新草本对比，防止鲜味过重导致"味觉疲劳"
>
> *Mushrooms contain GMP (~100mg/100g), soy has glutamate (~1000mg/100ml). GMP + MSG synergy boosts umami perception 8–15x (the 1+1=16 of taste math). Butter's butyric acid provides a creamy base while milk fat coats umami molecules, extending mouth-linger time. Dehydrated mushrooms' burst cell walls release more intracellular umami — "concentration is justice." Thyme's thymol provides fresh herbal contrast, preventing umami overload fatigue.*
