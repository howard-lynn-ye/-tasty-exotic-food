> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Classic American applesauce with miso and caramel elements upgrades from "kids' snack" to "adult comfort food." Miso's fermented saltiness adds "depth" to sweet apples, while caramelized syrup coats each piece of soft, broken-down apple. This sauce pairs with ice cream, spreads on bread, mixes into yogurt, or gets eaten straight from the bowl at midnight.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 苹果 | Apples (Fuji or Gala) | 4个（去皮切块）/ 4 (peeled, diced) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Cinnamon | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 水 | Water | ¼杯 / ¼ cup |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 焦糖化 | Caramelize
锅中放黄油和红糖，中火加热至糖融化冒泡变深色（约3分钟）。

Melt butter and brown sugar in a pot over medium heat until sugar dissolves, bubbles, and darkens (~3 minutes).

### 2. 煮苹果 | Cook Apples
加入苹果块翻炒1分钟让焦糖裹住苹果。加水、肉桂和柠檬汁，盖盖小火煮20分钟至苹果完全软烂。

Add apple chunks, stir 1 minute to coat with caramel. Add water, cinnamon, and lemon juice. Cover and simmer 20 minutes until apples are completely soft.

### 3. 加味噌 | Add Miso
离火，加入味噌搅拌至完全融合。用叉子或土豆压泥器压成你喜欢的粗细度。

Remove from heat. Stir in miso until fully incorporated. Mash with a fork or potato masher to your preferred texture.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
温热搭配冰淇淋，或冷藏后涂面包、拌酸奶。装瓶冷藏可保存一周。

Serve warm over ice cream, or refrigerate and spread on bread or mix into yogurt. Keeps 1 week refrigerated.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 味噌不要加热太久，高温会破坏鲜味 | Don't cook miso too long — high heat destroys umami |
| 用甜一点的苹果（Fuji、Gala），不要用酸苹果 | Use sweet apples (Fuji, Gala), not tart ones |
| 焦糖步骤别跳过，这是灵魂 | Don't skip the caramel step — it's the soul |
| 想更丝滑可以用搅拌机打 | Use a blender for silkier texture |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **味噌为什么能升级苹果酱 / Why miso upgrades applesauce:**
> - 苹果酱的味道单一（甜+酸），味噌加入第三维度（鲜/咸）
> - 味噌的"鲜"让甜味感知深度增加（味觉交叉增强）
> - 焦糖化红糖产生的太妃糖风味和味噌的坚果调互补
> - 一小撮盐+味噌的咸鲜=对甜味感受的显著放大
>
> *Applesauce has a one-dimensional flavor (sweet + sour). Miso adds a third dimension (umami/salty). Miso's umami deepens sweetness perception (taste cross-enhancement). Caramelized brown sugar's toffee flavor complements miso's nutty notes. A pinch of salt + miso's saltiness = significant amplification of sweetness perception.*
