## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 隔夜米饭 | Day-old rice | 2碗 / 2 cups |
| 韩国泡菜 | Korean kimchi (aged) | 1杯 / 1 cup, chopped |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella (shredded) | 100g / 1 cup |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒肉和泡菜 | Fry Meat & Kimchi
中火热芝麻油，炒培根/午餐肉至微焦。加入泡菜翻炒2分钟至水分收干、边缘焦化。

Heat sesame oil over medium, fry bacon/Spam until slightly crispy. Add kimchi, stir-fry 2 min until dry and edges char.

### 2. 炒饭 | Fry the Rice
转大火，加入米饭摊平，压紧，不要翻动——让底部形成锅巴（2分钟）。然后翻炒打散，加韩式辣酱炒匀。

Turn up heat, add rice, press flat, DON'T touch it — let the bottom crisp into a crust (2 min). Then toss, break apart, add gochujang, stir evenly.

### 3. 铺芝士焗 | Melt Cheese
火调最小，芝士均匀铺在炒饭上，盖锅盖焖1-2分钟至芝士完全融化拉丝。

Turn heat to low, spread cheese over rice, cover and let melt 1–2 min until fully melted and stretchy.

### 4. 煎蛋上桌 | Egg & Serve
另起锅煎两个溏心蛋。炒饭盛碗，盖上煎蛋，撒葱花和海苔碎。

Fry 2 sunny-side-up eggs in another pan. Bowl the rice, top with eggs, scatter scallions and nori.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **双发酵鲜味叠加原理 / Double-fermentation umami stacking:**
> - 韩国泡菜经乳酸发酵产生大量谷氨酸（~240mg/100g）+ 乳酸（酸味）
> - 芝士经酪蛋白酶分解产生谷氨酸（帕玛森高达1680mg/100g）+ 酪氨酸晶体
> - 两种发酵产物叠加：鲜味协同效应让整体鲜度超过单独使用任一种的5倍
> - 锅巴（焦化米饭）在200°C以上产生2-乙酰基-1-吡咯啉（爆米花香气分子），增加层次感
>
> *Kimchi's lactic fermentation produces glutamate (~240mg/100g) + lactic acid. Cheese's casein breakdown yields glutamate (Parmesan hits 1680mg/100g) + tyrosine crystals. Combined: umami synergy makes total savoriness 5x greater than either alone. Rice crust (nurungji) at 200°C+ produces 2-acetyl-1-pyrroline (popcorn aroma molecule), adding another dimension.*
