# 花椒蜂蜜烤茄子 Sichuan Honey Roasted Eggplant

> 四川 x 地中海 | 素菜主菜
> Sichuan x Mediterranean | Vegetarian Main

---

## 简介 Introduction

花椒的麻与蜂蜜的甜看似矛盾，却能在烤茄子上达成惊人的和谐。茄子经高温烤制
后内部绵软如奶油，外皮微焦。花椒油带来标志性的酥麻感，蜂蜜在烤制过程中
焦糖化形成粘稠的光泽表面。这道菜灵感来自中东烤茄子泥（baba ganoush）与
四川凉拌茄子的交汇。

Sichuan peppercorn's numbing tingle and honey's sweetness seem contradictory
yet achieve stunning harmony on roasted eggplant. High-heat roasting makes
the inside creamy while the skin chars. Peppercorn oil delivers the signature
buzz, while honey caramelizes into a glossy, sticky surface. Inspired by the
intersection of Middle Eastern baba ganoush and Sichuan cold eggplant.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 长茄子 Chinese eggplant | 4根 / 4 |
| 花椒油 Sichuan peppercorn oil | 20ml |
| 蜂蜜 Honey | 30ml |
| 大蒜 Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 酱油 Soy sauce | 15ml |
| 米醋 Rice vinegar | 10ml |
| 芝麻 Toasted sesame seeds | 10g |
| 香菜 Cilantro | 一小把 / a small bunch |
| 干花椒粒 Dried peppercorns | 5g |
| 橄榄油 Olive oil | 15ml |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 辣椒片 Chili flakes | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **预热 Preheat**: 烤箱预热220°C。
   Preheat oven to 220°C.

2. **处理茄子 Prep eggplant**: 茄子纵切两半，在切面划菱形花刀，不切断皮。
   Halve eggplants lengthwise, score crosshatch on cut side without piercing skin.

3. **调蜜酱 Make glaze**: 蜂蜜、花椒油、酱油、米醋、蒜末混合成酱汁。
   Mix honey, peppercorn oil, soy sauce, vinegar, and minced garlic into a glaze.

4. **刷酱 Glaze**: 切面朝上放烤盘，刷橄榄油打底，再刷一半的蜜酱。
   Place cut-side up on baking sheet, brush olive oil first, then half the glaze.

5. **第一次烤 First bake**: 220°C烤20分钟至茄肉软塌。
   Roast at 220°C for 20 min until flesh collapses and softens.

6. **二次刷酱 Second glaze**: 取出刷剩余酱汁，撒干花椒粒，再烤10分钟至焦糖化。
   Remove, brush remaining glaze, sprinkle dried peppercorns, bake 10 more min.

7. **装盘 Serve**: 撒芝麻、香菜碎和辣椒片，趁热食用。
   Top with sesame, chopped cilantro, and chili flakes. Serve hot.
