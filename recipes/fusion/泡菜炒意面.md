## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 意面 | Spaghetti or linguine | 100g / 1 serving |
| 韩式泡菜（熟成） | Aged Korean kimchi | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup (~100g) |
| 培根或五花肉 | Bacon or pork belly | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣 / 2 cloves |
| 麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱花+芝麻+海苔 | Scallion + sesame + nori | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煎肉炒泡菜 | Fry Meat & Kimchi
培根切小块煎出油，加入切碎的泡菜和蒜末，大火翻炒2分钟至泡菜边缘微焦。

Dice bacon and render fat. Add chopped kimchi and minced garlic, stir-fry 2 min on high until kimchi edges char.

### 2. 煮面合一 | Cook & Combine
同时煮面至 al dente。面条直接用夹子夹入泡菜锅中，带入2-3汤匙面汤，大火翻炒收汁。

Meanwhile cook pasta al dente. Tongs-transfer noodles directly into kimchi pan with 2-3 tbsp pasta water. Toss over high heat.

### 3. 调味装盘 | Season & Plate
关火淋麻油，拌匀。盛盘后撒葱花、芝麻和手撕海苔碎。

Off heat, drizzle sesame oil, toss. Plate, top with scallion, sesame, and torn nori strips.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

泡菜发酵产生的乳酸菌代谢物提供与番茄相似的酸度曲线（pH 3.5-4.0），因此可以完美替代番茄酱作为意面的酸味基底。高温炒制让泡菜中的糖分焦化产生"镬气"，这种焦香是普通冷拌泡菜面无法获得的第五维风味。

*Kimchi's lactobacillus metabolites provide an acidity curve similar to tomato (pH 3.5-4.0), making it a perfect stand-in as pasta's acid base. High-heat charring caramelizes kimchi sugars for wok-hei — a fifth-dimension flavor unattainable in cold-tossed kimchi noodles.*
