# 五香肉桂苹果派 | Five-Spice Apple Pie

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 40分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份(6-8人) | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、烤箱、甜品、感恩节

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 在美国最经典的苹果派里加入中国五香粉，八角和丁香的温暖东方气息与肉桂苹果的西方经典完美交融。这是一道能让感恩节餐桌上所有人好奇追问配方的甜品。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Chinese five-spice powder infiltrates America's most iconic dessert. Star anise and clove bring warm Eastern mystery to the Western classic of cinnamon apple. This is the Thanksgiving pie that makes everyone at the table beg for the recipe.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 冷冻派皮 | Frozen pie crust | 2张 / 2 sheets |
| 苹果 | Apples (Granny Smith + Honeycrisp) | 4-5个 / 4-5 |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 80g / 1/3 cup |
| 中国五香粉 | Chinese five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 玉米淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg (for wash) | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌馅料 | Make Filling
苹果去皮切薄片，加红糖、五香粉、肉桂、玉米淀粉和柠檬汁拌匀，静置10分钟出汁。

Peel and thinly slice apples. Toss with brown sugar, five-spice, cinnamon, cornstarch, and lemon juice. Rest 10 min to release juices.

### 2. 组装 | Assemble
一张派皮铺入派盘，倒入馅料堆高。第二张派皮盖上，边缘捏紧，表面划几刀排气孔。刷蛋液。

Line pie dish with one crust, mound in the filling. Top with second crust, crimp edges, cut slits for steam. Brush with egg wash.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
烤箱预热190°C (375°F)，烤40-45分钟至表面金黄、汁液冒泡。最后10分钟边缘盖锡纸防焦。

Bake at 190°C (375°F) for 40–45 min until golden and bubbly. Tent edges with foil for the last 10 minutes to prevent burning.

### 4. 冷却 | Cool & Serve
出炉冷却至少30分钟再切（否则会散），配香草冰淇淋。

Cool at least 30 minutes before slicing (or it falls apart). Serve with vanilla ice cream.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么五香粉+苹果派是天作之合 / Why this works:**
> 中国五香粉（八角、丁香、花椒、肉桂、小茴香）和传统苹果派香料（肉桂、肉豆蔻、多香果）共享大量萜烯类芳香化合物。它们本就是"近亲"！五香粉中的八角带来茴香脑（anethole），这是一种能放大甜味感知的分子——让派吃起来更甜但实际用糖更少。苹果的苹果酸与五香粉的温暖风味形成酸-暖对比，味觉层次因此翻倍。
>
> *Chinese five-spice (star anise, clove, Sichuan pepper, cinnamon, fennel) and traditional pie spices (cinnamon, nutmeg, allspice) share extensive terpene aromatics — they're already "cousins"! Star anise contributes anethole, a molecule that amplifies sweetness perception, so the pie tastes sweeter with less sugar. Apple's malic acid creates a sour-warm contrast with five-spice, doubling the flavor dimensions.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 五香粉别加多，1茶匙是极限，多了八角味会抢戏 | 1 tsp five-spice MAX — more and the star anise overwhelms |
| 混用酸苹果+甜苹果，味觉层次更丰富 | Mix tart + sweet apple varieties for more complex flavor |
| 冷冻派皮 Pillsbury 即可，省时间 | Pillsbury frozen crusts work great and save tons of time |
| 配一球香草冰淇淋是标配 | A scoop of vanilla ice cream is non-negotiable |
