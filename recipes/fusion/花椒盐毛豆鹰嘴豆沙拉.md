## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 毛豆仁（熟） | Shelled edamame (cooked) | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 鹰嘴豆（罐头沥干） | Canned chickpeas (drained) | 1罐 / 1 can (15oz) |
| 小黄瓜 | Mini cucumber | 1根切丁 / 1, diced |
| 红甜椒 | Red bell pepper | ½个切丁 / ½, diced |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 香菜（可选） | Cilantro (optional) | 适量 / to taste |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做花椒盐 | Make Sichuan Salt
干锅小火炒花椒粉和海盐30秒至出香味，盛出备用。（也可以直接混合不炒。）

Dry-toast Sichuan pepper and sea salt in a small pan over low heat for 30 seconds until fragrant. Set aside. (Or simply mix without toasting.)

### 2. 调酱汁 | Make Dressing
碗中混合柠檬汁、芝麻油和橄榄油，搅匀。

Whisk lemon juice, sesame oil, and olive oil in a bowl.

### 3. 拌沙拉 | Toss Salad
大碗中放入毛豆、鹰嘴豆、黄瓜丁和甜椒丁。倒入酱汁翻拌，撒花椒盐调味。

Combine edamame, chickpeas, cucumber, and bell pepper in a large bowl. Pour dressing over, toss, and season with Sichuan salt.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻和香菜，立即吃或冷藏做便当。

Sprinkle sesame seeds and cilantro. Eat immediately or refrigerate for meal prep.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **花椒盐为什么是万能调料 / Why Sichuan salt is a universal seasoning:**
> - 花椒素激活触觉受体，让味蕾对所有其他味道更敏感
> - 盐本身就是味觉增强剂（提鲜、压苦、增甜）
> - 花椒+盐=双重味觉放大器，什么食物都能提升
> - 在冷沙拉中花椒的"麻"特别突出，因为没有热量竞争
>
> *Sanshool activates tactile receptors, making taste buds more sensitive to all other flavors. Salt itself is a taste enhancer (boosts umami, suppresses bitterness, amplifies sweetness). Sichuan pepper + salt = double taste amplifier for any food. In cold salads, the "numbing" stands out more because there's no heat competing.*
