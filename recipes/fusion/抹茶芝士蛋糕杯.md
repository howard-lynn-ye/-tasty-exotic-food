> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 抹茶中的L-茶氨酸（L-theanine）是一种天然的"提鲜"氨基酸，它和奶油芝士中的乳脂结合时产生类似"奶茶效应"：苦涩被脂肪包裹后变成柔和的回甘。免烤芝士蛋糕依靠吉利丁凝固，在4°C冰箱中形成入口即化的丝滑质地，比烤制版本更能保留抹茶的鲜绿色和清新香气。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Matcha's L-theanine is a natural flavor-enhancing amino acid. Combined with cream cheese's milk fat, it creates a "milk tea effect" — bitterness wrapped in fat transforms into gentle, lingering sweetness. No-bake cheesecake relies on gelatin to set, and at 4°C achieves a melt-in-mouth silkiness that preserves matcha's vivid green color and fresh aroma better than baked versions.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 奶油芝士 | Cream cheese (softened) | 8oz / 225g |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 消化饼碎 | Graham cracker crumbs | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 黄油 (融化) | Melted butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 吉利丁片 | Gelatin sheet (or 1 tsp powder) | 1片 / 1 sheet |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做饼底 | Make Crust
消化饼碎和融化黄油拌匀，分装到4个杯子底部压紧。冰箱冷藏备用。

Mix cracker crumbs with melted butter. Press into the bottom of 4 cups. Refrigerate.

### 2. 做芝士糊 | Make Filling
吉利丁冷水泡软后隔水融化。奶油芝士+糖+抹茶粉搅打至顺滑。淡奶油打至六分发后折叠入芝士糊，最后加入吉利丁液拌匀。

Bloom gelatin in cold water, then melt over warm water. Beat cream cheese + sugar + matcha until smooth. Whip cream to soft peaks, fold into cheese mixture, then stir in gelatin.

### 3. 冷藏定型 | Chill & Set
倒入杯中，冷藏至少2小时至凝固。上桌前撒少许抹茶粉装饰。

Pour into cups, refrigerate at least 2 hours until set. Dust with matcha powder before serving.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 抹茶先用少量热水化开再加入，防止结块 | Dissolve matcha in a little hot water first to prevent lumps |
| 奶油芝士必须室温软化，否则打不顺滑 | Cream cheese must be fully softened at room temperature |
| 可以做成渐变色：先倒白色层再倒抹茶层 | Make it layered: pour white layer first, then matcha layer |
