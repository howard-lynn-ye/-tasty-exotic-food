## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder (ceremonial) | 15g / 2 tbsp |
| 低筋面粉 | Cake flour | 120g / 1 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 4个 / 4 |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | 100g / 1/2 cup |
| 牛奶 | Whole milk | 60ml / 1/4 cup |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 40ml / 3 tbsp |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 300ml / 1.25 cups |
| 糖粉 | Powdered sugar | 30g / 1/4 cup |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 蛋糕糊 | Make Batter
蛋黄+30g糖搅匀，加入牛奶和油。筛入面粉和10g抹茶粉，拌至顺滑。蛋白+70g糖打发至硬性发泡，分三次翻入蛋黄糊。

Beat yolks + 30g sugar. Add milk and oil. Sift in flour + 10g matcha, mix smooth. Whip whites + 70g sugar to stiff peaks. Fold into yolk batter in 3 additions.

### 2. 烘烤 | Bake
倒入6寸圆模，轻震两下排气泡。160°C (325°F) 烤25分钟。倒扣冷却脱模。

Pour into 6-inch round pan, tap to remove bubbles. Bake at 160°C (325°F) for 25 min. Invert to cool, then unmold.

### 3. 抹茶奶油 | Matcha Cream
淡奶油+糖粉+5g抹茶粉+香草精，打发至七分发（能立起小弯钩）。

Whip cream + powdered sugar + 5g matcha + vanilla to medium-stiff peaks (soft hook stage).

### 4. 组装 | Assemble
蛋糕横切两层，每层抹厚厚的抹茶奶油，表面也抹平。冷藏2小时定型，上桌前筛一层抹茶粉。

Slice cake into 2 layers. Spread generous matcha cream between and on top. Chill 2 hours. Dust with matcha before serving.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **抹茶+奶油的分子级和谐 / Matcha + cream: molecular-level harmony:**
> - 抹茶含L-茶氨酸（~30mg/g），一种氨基酸，激活鲜味受体产生"鲜甜"感——这是抹茶不同于普通绿茶的关键
> - 奶油的乳脂球（milk fat globules）包裹抹茶的儿茶素（catechins），降低苦涩感达60%
> - 抹茶的叶绿素a和b在中性pH环境中呈鲜绿色，蛋糕的气泡隔绝空气防止氧化变褐
> - 打发奶油的气泡（平均直径20μm）在口中破裂时释放抹茶香气——"延迟芳香释放"技术
>
> *Matcha contains L-theanine (~30mg/g), an amino acid that activates umami receptors for "savory sweetness" — the key difference from regular green tea. Cream's fat globules coat matcha catechins, reducing bitterness by 60%. Chlorophyll a/b stays vivid green at neutral pH; cake bubbles shield against oxidative browning. Whipped cream bubbles (avg. 20μm diameter) burst in the mouth releasing matcha aroma — a "delayed fragrance release" technique.*
