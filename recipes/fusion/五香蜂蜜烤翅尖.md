No, they have the "highest skin-to-meat ratio," which means they roast into pure crispy skin and caramelized glaze. Five-spice's complex warmth (star anise, cinnamon, clove, Sichuan pepper, fennel) and honey's caramel sweetness form a "spice-sugar shell" on the thin wing tip skin. A masterclass in turning $1 ingredients into $10 flavor.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡翅尖 | Chicken wing tips | 500g (~20个 / ~20 pieces) |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine (or dry sherry) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | ¼茶匙 / ¼ tsp |
| 白胡椒 | White pepper | ¼茶匙 / ¼ tsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
翅尖洗净擦干。碗中混合蜂蜜、五香粉、酱油、料酒、蒜泥、盐和白胡椒。翅尖放入腌30分钟以上。

Wash and dry wing tips. Mix honey, five-spice, soy sauce, wine, garlic, salt, and white pepper. Marinate wing tips at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 铺盘 | Arrange
烤盘铺锡纸，放烤网。翅尖单层铺开，不要叠。

Line a baking sheet with foil, place a wire rack on top. Arrange wing tips in a single layer, no overlapping.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
烤箱预热 200°C (400°F)。烤15分钟，取出刷一层腌酱，再烤10分钟至深金色酥脆。

Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Bake 15 minutes, remove and brush with marinade, bake 10 more minutes until deep golden and crispy.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒葱花，趁热啃。配啤酒绝了。

Sprinkle scallions. Eat hot while gnawing. Perfect with beer.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 翅尖在亚洲超市很便宜（~$1/lb） | Wing tips are cheap at Asian markets (~$1/lb) |
| 烤网很重要，底部的热空气让翅尖全方位酥脆 | Wire rack is key — hot air crisps from all sides |
| 五香粉别超过1茶匙，多了会发苦 | Keep five-spice under 1 tsp — excess turns bitter |
| 可以用空气炸锅：180°C，12分钟，翻面一次 | Air fryer: 180°C, 12 min, flip once |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **翅尖为什么可以这么好吃 / Why wing tips can be this delicious:**
> - 高皮肉比 = 烤制时大量胶原蛋白转化为明胶 = 酥脆+黏嘴
> - 鸡皮脂肪在高温下渲染出去，留下酥脆的蛋白质骨架
> - 五香粉中的脂溶性芳香物质溶入鸡皮脂肪，增香效果倍增
> - 蜂蜜的果糖焦糖化温度低，快速形成琥珀色糖壳
>
> *High skin-to-meat ratio = abundant collagen converts to gelatin during roasting = crispy + sticky. Chicken skin fat renders out at high heat, leaving a crispy protein scaffold. Five-spice's fat-soluble aromatics dissolve into chicken skin fat, multiplying fragrance. Honey's fructose caramelizes at low temperatures, rapidly forming an amber sugar shell.*
