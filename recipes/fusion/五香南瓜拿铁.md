# 五香南瓜拿铁 | Five-Spice Pumpkin Latte

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 星巴克的Pumpkin Spice Latte用的是肉桂+肉豆蔻+姜+丁香，而中式五香粉恰好包含了其中大部分成分，还额外加了八角和花椒。八角中的茴香脑（anethole）为拿铁增添了一丝甜茴香味，花椒的微麻让咖啡的苦味层次更丰富。这是PSL的升级版——用东方香料重新定义秋天的味道。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Starbucks' PSL uses cinnamon + nutmeg + ginger + cloves, and Chinese five-spice already contains most of these plus star anise and Sichuan peppercorn. Star anise's anethole adds a sweet anise note, while peppercorn's subtle tingle adds depth to coffee's bitterness. This is PSL 2.0 — redefining autumn's flavor with Eastern spices.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 浓缩咖啡 | Espresso (or strong coffee) | 1杯 / 1 shot (or 1/2 cup strong coffee) |
| 牛奶 | Milk (any kind) | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 南瓜泥 | Pumpkin puree (canned) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1/8茶匙 / 1/8 tsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 1-2汤匙 / 1-2 tbsp |
| 淡奶油 (顶部) | Whipped cream (topping) | 适量 / optional |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮南瓜奶 | Heat Pumpkin Milk
小锅中牛奶+南瓜泥+五香粉+枫糖浆，小火加热搅拌至微沸且顺滑。

Heat milk + pumpkin puree + five-spice + maple syrup in a saucepan, stirring over low heat until smooth and barely simmering.

### 2. 打泡 | Froth
用奶泡器打出泡沫（或倒入密封罐使劲摇30秒）。

Froth with a milk frother (or pour into a sealed jar and shake vigorously 30 seconds).

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
杯中倒入浓缩咖啡，慢慢倒入南瓜牛奶，顶上奶油，撒一点五香粉装饰。

Pour espresso into mug, slowly add pumpkin milk, top with whipped cream, dust with a hint of five-spice.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 五香粉只要一丁点，1/8茶匙已经很浓 | A tiny amount of five-spice goes a long way — 1/8 tsp is plenty |
| 南瓜泥用 Libby's 罐头纯南瓜泥，不要买pie filling | Use pure pumpkin puree (Libby's), NOT pumpkin pie filling |
| 想要冰版的：所有东西冷藏后加冰块 | For iced version: chill everything and pour over ice |
