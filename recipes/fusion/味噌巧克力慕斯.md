# 味噌巧克力慕斯 | Miso Chocolate Mousse

> ⏱ 15分钟 + 冷藏 2小时 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、甜品、免烤箱、高级感

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 味噌中的谷氨酸和黑巧克力中的苦味生物碱形成"鲜+苦"的对比，这和高级巧克力中加海盐是同一原理，但味噌带来的是更深层的发酵鲜味。白味噌（甜味噌）的米曲发酵产生天然甜味，减少了需要添加的糖量，让慕斯的口感更纯净、巧克力味更突出。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Miso's glutamate and dark chocolate's bitter alkaloids create a "umami + bitter" contrast — same principle as salted chocolate, but miso delivers deeper fermented savoriness. White miso's rice koji fermentation produces natural sweetness, reducing added sugar and making the mousse taste cleaner with more pronounced chocolate flavor.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 黑巧克力 | Dark chocolate (60-70%) | 100g / 3.5 oz |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs (separated) | 2个 / 2 |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt flakes (topping) | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 融巧克力 | Melt Chocolate
巧克力隔水融化，离火后拌入味噌和蛋黄，搅至顺滑。

Melt chocolate over a double boiler. Off heat, whisk in miso and egg yolks until smooth.

### 2. 打发 | Whip
分别打发蛋白（加糖打至硬性发泡）和淡奶油（打至六分发）。先将1/3蛋白拌入巧克力中翻拌均匀，再轻柔折叠剩余蛋白和奶油。

Whip egg whites with sugar to stiff peaks; whip cream to soft peaks. Fold 1/3 whites into chocolate to lighten, then gently fold in remaining whites and cream.

### 3. 冷藏 | Chill
分装到杯子中，冷藏至少2小时。上桌前撒几粒海盐片。

Divide into cups, refrigerate at least 2 hours. Top with sea salt flakes before serving.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定要用白味噌（甜味噌），红味噌太咸 | Use white (shiro) miso — red miso is too salty for desserts |
| 翻拌手法要轻，保留空气感 | Fold gently to keep the airy texture |
| 冷藏过夜风味更佳 | Overnight chill gives the best flavor development |
