# 五香蜂蜜烤山药 | Five-Spice Honey Yam

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、素食、配菜

> 山药（紫薯/橙薯亦可）切厚块，裹上五香蜂蜜酱送入烤箱。高温让外皮焦脆，内部绵密如泥，五香粉的暖香与蜂蜜的甘甜把平凡的根茎蔬菜变成了让人吃到舔盘的配菜。
>
> *Yam (or sweet potato) chunks coated in five-spice honey glaze and roasted to perfection. High heat crisps the exterior while the interior turns pillowy soft. Five-spice warmth and honey sweetness transform a humble root vegetable into a side dish you'll lick the plate for.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 山药或红薯 | Yam or sweet potato | 500g |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 3/4茶匙 / 3/4 tsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 / to taste |
| 蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 白芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切块调味 | Cut & Season
山药去皮切3cm厚块。橄榄油、五香粉、盐、蒜粉和黑胡椒混合，倒在山药块上拌匀。

Peel yam and cut into 3cm chunks. Mix olive oil, five-spice, salt, garlic powder, and pepper. Toss with yam chunks until coated.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
预热烤箱200°C。山药块单层铺在烤盘上（不要堆叠），烤20分钟。

Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F. Spread yam in a single layer on baking sheet (no crowding). Roast 20 minutes.

### 3. 刷蜂蜜 | Glaze
取出翻面，刷上蜂蜜，再烤10分钟至焦糖化、外酥内软。

Flip, brush with honey, roast another 10 min until caramelized and crisp outside, fluffy inside.

### 4. 出盘 | Serve
撒葱花和芝麻，趁热上桌。可以配烤肉或作为独立小食。

Sprinkle scallions and sesame. Serve hot — great alongside grilled meats or as a standalone snack.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 单层铺开不堆叠，否则蒸不脆 | Single layer is crucial — crowding steams instead of roasting |
| 蜂蜜后半段再加，太早会焦黑 | Add honey in the second half — too early and it burns |
| 日本山药（长山药）口感更粘糯，美式红薯更甜 | Japanese yam (nagaimo) is stickier; American sweet potato is sweeter |
| 五香粉和肉桂都很搭甜味根茎蔬菜 | Five-spice and cinnamon both pair beautifully with sweet roots |
