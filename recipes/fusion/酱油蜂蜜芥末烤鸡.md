## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿 | Chicken leg quarters | 4个 / 4 pieces (~800g) |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 第戎芥末 | Dijon mustard | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱腌制 | Marinate
混合酱油、蜂蜜、芥末、蒜泥、橄榄油和黑胡椒。鸡腿划两刀便于入味，放入腌料中至少腌1小时。

Mix soy sauce, honey, mustard, garlic, olive oil, and pepper. Score chicken legs twice, marinate at least 1 hour.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱预热200°C (400°F)。鸡腿皮朝上放入烤盘，倒入一半腌料，放上迷迭香枝。烤35分钟。

Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Place legs skin-side up in a baking dish, pour half the marinade over, add rosemary. Roast 35 min.

### 3. 刷酱焦化 | Glaze
取出刷剩余腌料，Broil 5-8分钟至表面焦糖化、鸡皮酥脆金黄。

Brush remaining marinade. Broil 5–8 minutes until the glaze caramelizes and skin is golden-crispy.

### 4. 静置上桌 | Rest & Serve
出炉后静置5分钟，让肉汁回流。搭配烤蔬菜或米饭。

Rest 5 min to let juices redistribute. Serve with roasted veggies or rice.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **鲜+辣+甜的三角平衡 / The umami-spice-sweet triangle:**
> - 酱油中谷氨酸（鲜味）+ 鸡肉中肌苷酸（核苷酸）= 鲜味协同放大8倍
> - 第戎芥末的异硫氰酸烯丙酯（allyl isothiocyanate）刺激鼻腔TRPA1受体，打开嗅觉通道
> - 蜂蜜焦糖化产生的麦芽酚（maltol）是天然增甜剂，甜度感知翻倍
> - 迷迭香的桉叶素（1,8-cineole）提供清凉草本尾韵，防止味觉疲劳
>
> *Soy glutamate + chicken's inosinate = 8x umami synergy. Dijon's allyl isothiocyanate stimulates TRPA1 in the nasal cavity, opening aroma pathways. Honey caramelization produces maltol, a natural sweetness enhancer. Rosemary's 1,8-cineole provides a cool herbal finish, preventing palate fatigue.*
