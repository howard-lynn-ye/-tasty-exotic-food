> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 我把中国学生宿舍的"可乐鸡翅"和四川花椒做了一次"黑暗升级"。可乐含有磷酸和焦糖色素——磷酸软化鸡肉纤维（天然嫩肉剂），焦糖色素在烤箱中进一步深化为黑釉面。花椒素在可乐的碳酸环境中溶解后，麻感变得更加"尖锐精准"。这是可乐鸡翅的最终进化形态——从宿舍菜晋级烤箱大菜。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *I "dark-upgraded" Chinese dorm-room cola chicken wings with Sichuan pepper. Cola contains phosphoric acid and caramel color — phosphoric acid tenderizes chicken fibers (natural meat tenderizer), caramel color deepens into a dark glaze in the oven. Sanshool dissolved in cola's carbonated environment becomes a more "sharp and precise" tingle. This is cola chicken's final evolution — from dorm dish to oven showstopper.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿 | Chicken thighs (bone-in) | 6个 / 6 pieces |
| 可乐 | Coca-Cola (regular, not diet) | 1罐 / 1 can (355ml) |
| 花椒 | Whole Sichuan peppercorns | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 生姜 | Ginger | 3片 / 3 slices |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 干辣椒 | Dried chili peppers | 3个 / 3 |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
鸡腿用可乐、酱油、花椒、姜片和蒜拍碎混合腌制1小时。花椒在可乐中浸泡会释放更多麻味。

Marinate thighs in cola, soy, peppercorns, ginger, and smashed garlic for 1 hour. Peppercorns steep in cola to release more numbing compounds.

### 2. 收汁 | Reduce
锅中倒入鸡腿和全部腌料，加干辣椒，大火煮开后中火煮15分钟至汁收到一半。

Pour chicken and all marinade into a pot, add dried chilies. Boil, then simmer 15 min until sauce reduces by half.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
鸡腿转入烤盘皮朝上，淋上浓缩酱汁。220°C (425°F) 烤20分钟至鸡皮酥脆、酱汁成黑亮釉面。

Transfer thighs to a baking dish skin-up, spoon reduced sauce over. Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 20 min until skin crisps and sauce becomes a dark glossy glaze.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒葱花段，配白米饭。锅底的可乐浓缩酱是精华，一定要淋在饭上。

Garnish with scallion. Serve with white rice. The concentrated cola sauce at the bottom is gold — drizzle it over rice.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **可乐在烹饪中的三重化学身份 / Cola's triple chemical identity in cooking:**
> - 磷酸（pH ~2.5）：酸性环境溶解肉类胶原蛋白，使鸡肉变得格外柔嫩
> - 焦糖色素（E150d）：在高温烤制中进一步美拉德反应，形成深邃的"黑金"釉面
> - 高果糖玉米糖浆：提供约11g/100ml糖分，在200°C以上焦糖化形成酥脆糖壳
> - 花椒在碳酸（CO₂+H₂O）环境中，花椒素的水溶性提升——麻感释放更快更集中
>
> *Phosphoric acid (pH ~2.5): acidic environment dissolves collagen, making chicken extra tender. Caramel color (E150d): undergoes further Maillard reactions at high heat, forming a deep "black gold" glaze. High fructose corn syrup: ~11g/100ml sugar, caramelizing above 200°C into a crispy sugar shell. In carbonated (CO₂+H₂O) conditions, sanshool's water solubility increases — numbing release is faster and more concentrated.*
