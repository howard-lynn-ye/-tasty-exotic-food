# 五香蜂蜜烤橙子 | Five-Spice Honey Orange

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、甜点、素食

> 橙子切半送进烤箱，五香蜂蜜在高温中渗透进每瓣果肉——烤过的橙子甜度翻倍，五香粉的暖香让这个简单的水果变成一道优雅的暖甜点。配一球香草冰淇淋，就是米其林级别的收尾。
>
> *Halved oranges roasted in the oven as five-spice honey seeps into every segment — roasting doubles the sweetness while five-spice warmth transforms simple fruit into an elegant warm dessert. Add a scoop of vanilla ice cream and you have a Michelin-worthy finale.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 脐橙 (大) | Navel orange (large) | 3个 / 3 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 20g，切小块 / 20g, small pieces |
| 红糖 | Brown sugar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1小撮 / a tiny pinch |
| 香草冰淇淋 (配菜) | Vanilla ice cream (to serve) | 适量 / as desired |
| 薄荷叶 | Mint leaves | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备橙子 | Prep Oranges
橙子横向切半，切面朝上放入烤盘。底部切一小片让橙子能站稳。

Cut oranges in half crosswise. Place cut-side up in a baking dish. Slice a small bit off the bottom so they sit flat.

### 2. 调蜜 | Make Glaze
蜂蜜、五香粉和红糖搅匀。均匀淋在每半个橙子的切面上，顶部各放一小块黄油，撒一丁点盐。

Mix honey, five-spice, and brown sugar. Drizzle evenly over each orange half. Place a small piece of butter on top, add a tiny pinch of salt.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
预热190°C，烤18-20分钟至边缘微焦、蜂蜜冒泡、果汁流淌。

Preheat 190°C/375°F. Roast 18-20 min until edges caramelize, honey bubbles, and juices flow.

### 4. 出盘 | Serve
趁热上桌，每半个橙子旁放一球香草冰淇淋，用薄荷叶装饰。把烤盘中的汁淋上去。

Serve hot — place a scoop of vanilla ice cream beside each half, garnish with mint. Spoon pan juices over top.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 选甜橙（脐橙）而非酸橙，烤后酸度已经足够了 | Choose sweet navel oranges — roasting provides enough acidity |
| 那一小撮盐非常关键，它提升所有甜味 | That tiny pinch of salt is critical — it lifts all the sweetness |
| 烤盘中的焦糖汁别浪费，淋在冰淇淋上绝了 | Don't waste the pan juices — they're incredible over ice cream |
| 也可以用葡萄柚、血橙做变化 | Try grapefruit or blood orange for variation |
