> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 花椒中的山椒素（hydroxy-alpha-sanshool）激活触觉神经产生麻感，与可可中的可可碱（theobromine）和辣椒素形成"麻-苦-暖"三重感官体验。这其实是阿兹特克辣椒巧克力饮的现代四川版——古人早就发现辛辣和巧克力是天作之合。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Sichuan peppercorn's hydroxy-alpha-sanshool activates tactile nerves for a tingling sensation, pairing with cocoa's theobromine and chili's capsaicin for a "tingle-bitter-warm" triple sensory experience. This is essentially a modern Sichuan take on the Aztec spiced chocolate — ancients already knew spice and chocolate are a perfect match.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛奶 | Milk | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 黑巧克力 | Dark chocolate (70%+) | 30g / 1 oz |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorns | 6-8粒 / 6-8 whole |
| 可可粉 | Cocoa powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜或糖 | Honey or sugar | 1-2茶匙 / 1-2 tsp |
| 肉桂棒 (可选) | Cinnamon stick (optional) | 1根 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煸花椒 | Toast Peppercorns
小锅中放花椒干煸30秒至出香，加入牛奶，小火煮3分钟让花椒的麻味浸入牛奶。

Dry-toast peppercorns in a saucepan 30 seconds until fragrant. Add milk, simmer 3 min to infuse.

### 2. 融巧克力 | Melt Chocolate
捞出花椒（或用茶滤），加入掰碎的黑巧克力和可可粉，搅拌至完全融化顺滑。

Remove peppercorns (or use a strainer). Add broken dark chocolate and cocoa powder, stir until smooth.

### 3. 调味上桌 | Sweeten & Serve
加蜂蜜调甜度。倒入杯中，可放一根肉桂棒装饰。

Sweeten with honey. Pour into a mug, garnish with a cinnamon stick if desired.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花椒不要太多，6-8粒足够，否则舌头会太麻 | 6-8 peppercorns is enough — more will over-numb your tongue |
| 巧克力选70%以上的，太甜会掩盖花椒的层次 | Use 70%+ dark chocolate — too sweet masks the peppercorn complexity |
| 可以加一小撮辣椒粉增加暖感 | A tiny pinch of cayenne adds extra warmth |
