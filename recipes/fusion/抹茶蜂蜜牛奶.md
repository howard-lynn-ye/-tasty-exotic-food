# 抹茶蜂蜜牛奶 Matcha Honey Milk

> 日本 x 欧洲 | 饮品
> Japan x Europe | Beverage

---

## 简介 Introduction

抹茶源自日本茶道，讲究的是苦涩中的回甘与清幽禅意。将它与西方的牛奶和天然
蜂蜜融合，苦、甜、奶三味达成微妙平衡——抹茶的涩感被牛奶温柔包裹，蜂蜜提供
了花香甜味的收尾。无论是冬日的热饮还是夏天的冰版，这杯饮品都能让人瞬间安静
下来。简单到只需三种材料，却优雅到可以出现在任何场合。

Matcha from Japanese tea ceremony offers bitterness resolving into sweet
aftertaste and Zen calm. Blended with Western milk and natural honey, bitter,
sweet, and creamy reach a subtle equilibrium — milk gently wraps matcha's
astringency, honey finishes with floral sweetness. Hot in winter, iced in
summer, it calms instantly. Only three ingredients, yet elegant enough for
any occasion.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 抹茶粉（仪式级）Matcha powder (ceremonial grade) | 4g |
| 全脂牛奶 Whole milk | 250ml |
| 蜂蜜（洋槐蜜最佳）Honey (acacia preferred) | 15-20ml |
| 热水 Hot water (80°C) | 30ml |
| 冰块（冷饮版）Ice cubes (for iced version) | 适量 / as needed |

---

## 做法 Instructions

### 热饮版 Hot Version

1. **筛粉 Sift matcha**: 将抹茶粉过细筛入碗中，去除结块。
   Sift matcha through a fine sieve into a bowl to remove lumps.

2. **点茶 Whisk**: 加入80°C热水（非沸水），用茶筅以W形快速搅打至起泡。
   Add 80°C water (not boiling), whisk vigorously in W-motion until frothy.

3. **热奶 Heat milk**: 牛奶加热至65°C（可用奶泡机打出微泡更佳）。
   Heat milk to 65°C (a milk frother for microfoam is even better).

4. **加蜜 Add honey**: 蜂蜜加入热牛奶中搅匀。
   Stir honey into the warm milk until dissolved.

5. **合并 Combine**: 将抹茶液倒入杯底，缓缓注入蜂蜜牛奶，自然分层。
   Pour matcha into the cup base, slowly add honey milk for natural layering.

### 冷饮版 Iced Version

1. 步骤1-2相同，点好浓缩抹茶液。Follow steps 1-2 for concentrated matcha.
2. 杯中放满冰块，倒入蜂蜜牛奶至八分满。Fill glass with ice, pour honey milk to 80%.
3. 缓缓倒入抹茶液，会形成美丽的绿白渐变。Slowly pour matcha for a green-white gradient.
4. 搅拌或不搅拌均可，各有风味。Stir or leave layered — both are delightful.
