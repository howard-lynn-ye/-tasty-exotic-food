# 辣椒油煎蛋吐司 | Chili Crisp Egg Toast

> ⏱ 10分钟 | 💰~$3/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、早餐、快手

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 辣椒油在煎蛋边缘炸出蕾丝花边，铺上酥脆吐司就是中国厨房送给美式早午餐的一记辣吻。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *Chili oil fries lacy edges onto an egg, laid on crispy toast — a spicy kiss from a Chinese kitchen to an American brunch.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 吐司面包 | Thick-cut bread or sourdough | 1片 / 1 slice |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 / 1 |
| 辣椒油（带渣）| Chili crisp oil (with sediment) | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 5ml / 1 tsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion, sliced | 5g / 1 stalk |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds | 少许 / pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 辣油煎蛋 | Fry Egg in Chili Oil
小锅中倒入辣椒油（连渣），中火加热至冒泡，打入鸡蛋，用勺舀油泼淋蛋白边缘至焦脆透明。
Heat chili crisp oil (with bits) in a small pan over medium heat until bubbling. Crack in egg, spoon hot oil over whites until edges are lacy and crisp.

### 2. 烤吐司 | Toast Bread
吐司烤至金黄酥脆，可涂一层薄黄油。
Toast bread until golden and crispy, optionally spread with a thin layer of butter.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
吐司上放辣油煎蛋，淋酱油，撒葱花和白芝麻，立即享用。
Place chili oil egg on toast, drizzle soy sauce, scatter scallions and sesame seeds, eat immediately.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 辣椒油的高温使蛋白边缘发生剧烈美拉德反应，产生吡嗪类焦香化合物；辣椒素刺激TRPV1受体，与蛋黄卵磷脂的油润形成"辣-滑"的感官张力。 *High-temp chili oil triggers intense Maillard reactions at egg-white edges, producing pyrazine compounds; capsaicin activates TRPV1 receptors, creating a "hot-silky" tension with egg yolk's lecithin richness.*
