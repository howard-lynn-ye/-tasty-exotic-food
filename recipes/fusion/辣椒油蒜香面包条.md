## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 法棍或吐司 | Baguette or thick bread | 1根 / 1 loaf |
| 辣椒油 | Chili oil (with sediment) | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 黄油（融化） | Melted butter | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 适量 / for garnish |
| 帕玛森芝士（可选） | Parmesan (optional) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调辣蒜酱 | Mix Chili-Garlic Spread
混合辣椒油（连渣一起）、蒜泥、融化黄油和盐，搅匀。

Combine chili oil (including sediment), garlic, melted butter, and salt. Mix well.

### 2. 涂抹切条 | Spread & Cut
法棍纵向切半，切面朝上，均匀涂满辣蒜酱。再切成手指宽的长条。

Split baguette lengthwise, cut-side up. Spread chili-garlic mixture generously. Cut into finger-width sticks.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
200°C (400°F) 烤8-10分钟至表面金黄酥脆。最后1分钟撒芝麻和帕玛森。

Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 8–10 min until golden and crispy. Sprinkle sesame and Parmesan in the last minute.

### 4. 出炉 | Serve
出炉撒葱花，趁热蘸番茄酱或单吃都绝。

Top with scallions, serve hot. Great with ketchup or just on their own.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **辣椒油 vs 黄油：脂溶性风味载体的对决 / Chili oil vs butter — a fat-soluble flavor carrier showdown:**
> - 辣椒油是经过高温"炸"出来的调味油，辣椒素已预先溶解在油脂中，涂抹即入味
> - 大蒜素（allicin）在受热后转化为二烯丙基二硫（diallyl disulfide），产生烤蒜的甜香
> - 面包的气孔结构像"微型海绵"，在烤制时吸收油脂，然后在嘴里释放——产生"爆汁"口感
> - 芝麻加热释放2-乙酰基呋喃（2-acetylfuran），散发坚果-焦糖香气
>
> *Chili oil is pre-infused — capsaicin already dissolved in fat, so it flavors on contact. Allicin converts to diallyl disulfide when heated, producing roasted garlic's sweet aroma. Bread's pores act like "micro sponges," absorbing oil during baking and releasing it in your mouth — a "juice burst" effect. Toasted sesame releases 2-acetylfuran, giving nutty-caramel aromas.*
