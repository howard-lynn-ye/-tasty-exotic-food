## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 胡萝卜 | Carrots | 6根 / 6 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备料 | Prep
胡萝卜去皮，斜切成段或纵切成条。烤箱预热 220°C (425°F)。

Peel carrots, cut diagonally or into long wedges. Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F).

### 2. 拌料 | Toss
胡萝卜加橄榄油、蜂蜜、五香粉和盐，拌匀。

Toss carrots with olive oil, honey, five-spice, and salt.

### 3. 烤 | Roast
铺在烤盘上（不要重叠），烤20-25分钟至边缘焦糖化。

Spread on a baking sheet (don't overlap). Roast 20–25 min until edges caramelize.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻，趁热吃。

Sprinkle sesame seeds. Serve hot.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 胡萝卜不要切太细，太细容易烤焦 | Don't cut carrots too thin — they'll burn |
| 五香粉不要放太多，1茶匙足够 | 1 tsp five-spice is enough — it's potent |
| 感恩节、圣诞节的完美配菜 | Perfect Thanksgiving/Christmas side dish |
