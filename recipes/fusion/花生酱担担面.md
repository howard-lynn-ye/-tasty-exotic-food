# 花生酱担担面 | PB Dan Dan Noodles

> ⏱ 25分钟 | 💰~$6/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、面食、微辣

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 用美式花生酱替代传统芝麻酱，让四川担担面穿上纽约外衣，浓郁顺滑中藏着花椒的麻。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *American peanut butter stands in for sesame paste, dressing Sichuan dan dan noodles in a New York coat — creamy with a hidden tingle of Sichuan pepper.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 细碱面 | Thin alkaline noodles | 200g / 7 oz |
| 顺滑花生酱 | Creamy peanut butter | 45g / 3 tbsp |
| 猪肉末 | Ground pork | 150g / 5 oz |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan pepper | 2g / ½ tsp |
| 辣椒油 | Chili oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 生抽 | Light soy sauce | 30ml / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 炒臊子 | Cook Meat
猪肉末中火炒散至焦香，加少许酱油调色，盛出备用。
Brown ground pork over medium heat until crispy, season with a splash of soy sauce, set aside.

### 2. 调酱汁 | Mix Sauce
花生酱、生抽、辣椒油、花椒粉加3汤匙面汤搅匀成浓稠酱汁。
Whisk peanut butter, soy sauce, chili oil, Sichuan pepper, and 3 tbsp noodle water into a thick sauce.

### 3. 拌面装盘 | Toss & Serve
面条煮熟沥水，拌入酱汁，顶上肉臊子、碎花生和葱花。
Cook noodles, drain, toss with sauce, top with pork, crushed peanuts, and scallions.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 花生酱的油脂包裹花椒素（hydroxy-alpha-sanshool），延长麻感在口腔的停留时间，同时缓冲辣椒素的灼热。 *Peanut butter's fats encapsulate hydroxy-alpha-sanshool, prolonging the tingle while buffering capsaicin's heat.*
