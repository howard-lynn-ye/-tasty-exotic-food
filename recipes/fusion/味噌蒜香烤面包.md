# 味噌蒜香烤面包 | Miso Garlic Bread

> ⏱ 15分钟 | 💰~$4/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、小食、日×意

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 意式蒜香面包的黄油里藏了一勺味噌，鲜味深度瞬间从地中海跳跃到日本海。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *A spoonful of miso hides in Italian garlic bread's butter — umami depth leaps from the Mediterranean to the Sea of Japan in one bite.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 法棍面包 | Baguette or Italian bread | 1根 / 1 loaf |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter, softened | 60g / 4 tbsp |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 20g / 1 tbsp |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 15g / 3 cloves |
| 意大利欧芹 | Fresh parsley, chopped | 10g / 2 tbsp |
| 帕玛森碎 | Grated Parmesan | 20g / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调味噌蒜黄油 | Make Miso Garlic Butter
软化黄油与味噌、蒜末、欧芹拌匀至顺滑均匀。
Mix softened butter with miso, garlic, and parsley until smooth and evenly combined.

### 2. 涂抹切片 | Spread on Bread
法棍斜切但底部不切断（手风琴状），每片间涂满味噌蒜黄油，表面撒帕玛森碎。
Slice baguette diagonally without cutting through the bottom (accordion-style), spread miso garlic butter between each slice, sprinkle Parmesan on top.

### 3. 烘烤 | Bake
锡纸包裹，200°C/400°F烤10分钟，打开锡纸再烤3分钟至表面金黄酥脆。
Wrap in foil, bake at 200°C/400°F for 10 min, unwrap and bake 3 more min until golden and crispy.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 味噌的谷氨酸与帕玛森的谷氨酸叠加，形成"鲜味×鲜味"的指数级增强；黄油中的二乙酰（diacetyl）提供奶香基调，烤制后的美拉德反应再添一层坚果焦香。 *Miso glutamate stacks with Parmesan glutamate for exponential umami; butter's diacetyl provides a creamy baseline, and Maillard reactions during baking add a nutty, toasted layer.*
