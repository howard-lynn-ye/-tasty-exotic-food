## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 黑巧克力（70%+） | Dark chocolate (70%+) | 150g / 5.3 oz |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 80ml / 1/3 cup |
| 辣椒粉（卡宴椒） | Cayenne pepper | 1/4-1/2茶匙 / 1/4-1/2 tsp |
| 可可粉 | Cocoa powder (for coating) | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 1小撮 / a pinch |
| 黄油 | Butter | 10g / ~1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做甘纳许 | Make Ganache
黑巧切碎放碗中。淡奶油加热至冒小泡后倒在巧克力上，静置1分钟再搅拌至顺滑。加入辣椒粉、海盐和黄油拌匀。

Chop chocolate into a bowl. Heat cream until simmering, pour over chocolate, wait 1 min, stir until smooth. Mix in cayenne, salt, and butter.

### 2. 冷藏成型 | Chill & Shape
覆膜冷藏1小时至可塑。用茶匙挖出约15g一份，手掌搓成球（手沾少许可可粉防粘）。

Cover and chill 1 hour until firm. Scoop ~15g portions, roll into balls with cocoa-dusted palms.

### 3. 裹粉装盘 | Coat & Serve
松露球在可可粉中滚匀，放入纸托。室温回软5分钟后食用风味最佳。

Roll truffles in cocoa powder, place in paper cups. Let sit 5 min at room temp for best flavor.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

辣椒素与可可碱在口腔中作用于不同受体——辣椒素激活TRPV1痛觉/热觉通道，可可碱阻断腺苷受体产生愉悦感，两者同时触发内啡肽释放，制造"痛并快乐着"的上瘾体验。黑巧克力的脂肪基质减缓辣椒素释放，将灼烧感转化为温暖的余韵。

*Capsaicin and theobromine target different receptors — capsaicin fires TRPV1 pain/heat channels while theobromine blocks adenosine receptors for pleasure, both triggering endorphin release for an addictive "painful bliss." Dark chocolate's fat matrix slows capsaicin release, converting burn into a warm afterglow.*
