# 味噌花生酱拌饭 | Miso PB Rice Bowl

> 日本味噌的鲜味与花生酱的浓郁在温热米饭上融为一体——无需开火的终极懒人拌饭。两种发酵/烘烤的酱料混合后产生令人上瘾的umami炸弹，淋上一点麻油和米醋就是完美一餐。
>
> *Japanese miso meets peanut butter on warm rice — the ultimate no-cook comfort bowl. Two fermented/roasted pastes combine into an umami bomb; a drizzle of sesame oil and rice vinegar rounds it into a complete meal.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 热米饭 | Cooked warm rice | 1碗 / 1 bowl (~200g) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 花生酱（顺滑） | Smooth peanut butter | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱花+芝麻 | Scallion + sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Mix the Sauce
小碗中混合味噌、花生酱、米醋和1汤匙热水，搅拌至顺滑。

In a small bowl, combine miso, peanut butter, rice vinegar, and 1 tbsp hot water. Stir until smooth.

### 2. 拌饭 | Toss with Rice
将酱料倒在热米饭上，用筷子充分拌匀，让每粒米都裹上酱汁。

Pour the sauce over warm rice. Toss thoroughly with chopsticks until every grain is coated.

### 3. 点缀上桌 | Garnish & Serve
淋上麻油，撒葱花和芝麻。喜欢辣的可加一勺辣椒油。

Drizzle sesame oil, top with scallions and sesame seeds. Add chili oil if you like heat.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

味噌中的谷氨酸（天然味精）与花生酱的烘烤坚果香形成"鲜+香"的双重刺激，激活舌头上不同的味觉受体。米醋的酸度切断了花生酱的油腻感，让整体口感清爽而不厚重。

*Miso's glutamates and peanut butter's Maillard-derived nuttiness activate separate umami and aroma receptors for a layered savory hit. Rice vinegar's acidity cuts through the richness, keeping the bowl bright and balanced.*

## 替代食材 | Substitutions

| 原料 / Original | 替代 / Substitute | 备注 / Notes |
|-----------------|-------------------|--------------|
| 白味噌 | 黄味噌 or 韩式大酱 doenjang | 味道更浓，减量至 2/3 / Stronger, use 2/3 amount |
| 花生酱 | 芝麻酱 tahini 或杏仁酱 | 坚果过敏者用 sunflower seed butter |
| 米醋 | 苹果醋 apple cider vinegar | 酸度类似 / Similar acidity |
