## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 通心粉 | Elbow macaroni | 250g / 8 oz |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 切达芝士 | Sharp cheddar (shredded) | 200g / 2 cups |
| 牛奶 | Whole milk | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 黄油 | Butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 面包糠 | Panko breadcrumbs | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮面 | Cook Pasta
盐水煮通心粉至al dente（比包装少1分钟），沥干备用。

Boil macaroni in salted water to al dente (1 min less than package). Drain.

### 2. 芝士酱 | Cheese Sauce
同一锅融化黄油，撒入面粉炒1分钟（面糊roux）。缓缓倒入牛奶持续搅拌至浓稠。离火加切达芝士搅至完全融化顺滑。拌入2汤匙老干妈。

Melt butter in the same pot, sprinkle flour, stir 1 min (roux). Gradually add milk, whisk until thick. Off heat, stir in cheddar until smooth. Mix in 2 tbsp LGM.

### 3. 焗烤 | Bake
通心粉倒回芝士酱中拌匀，转入烤盘。面包糠+1汤匙老干妈拌匀撒在顶部。200°C (400°F) 烤10分钟至金黄冒泡。

Add pasta back to sauce, toss. Transfer to baking dish. Mix panko + 1 tbsp LGM, sprinkle on top. Bake 200°C (400°F) 10 min until golden and bubbly.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒葱花，趁热挖着吃。芝士拉丝+老干妈脆粒=终极碳水幸福感。

Top with scallions, eat hot. Stretchy cheese + LGM crunch = ultimate carb happiness.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **双发酵鲜味引擎 / Dual-fermentation umami engine:**
> - 切达芝士经数月熟成，酪蛋白分解产生谷氨酸（成熟切达含~300mg/100g）
> - 老干妈的豆豉经曲霉发酵，同样富含游离谷氨酸（~500mg/100g）
> - 两者叠加后总鲜味浓度接近帕玛森芝士水平，但多了辣椒的TRPV1激活
> - 面糊（roux）中淀粉糊化后形成稳定乳化体系，防止芝士出油分层
> - 面包糠在200°C烤制时美拉德反应产生吡嗪类物质——"烤面包香"的分子来源
>
> *Aged cheddar's months of maturation breaks casein into glutamate (~300mg/100g). LGM's Aspergillus-fermented black beans are also glutamate-rich (~500mg/100g). Combined, total umami approaches Parmesan levels — plus TRPV1 activation from chili. Roux's gelatinized starch creates a stable emulsion preventing cheese from splitting. Panko at 200°C undergoes Maillard reactions producing pyrazines — the molecular source of "toast aroma."*
