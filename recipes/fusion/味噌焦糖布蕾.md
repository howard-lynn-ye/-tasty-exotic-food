> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 传统法式布蕾的奶蛋底，加入一勺白味噌，就像给甜品装了一个"鲜味低音炮"。味噌的发酵咸鲜不会让你尝出"酱"味，而是让焦糖层的甜更有深度、更有"完成度"。敲碎焦糖壳的那一声脆响之后，是咸甜交织的丝滑蛋奶——这是能让约会对象刮目相看的甜品。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *A traditional French custard base with a spoonful of white miso — like installing an "umami subwoofer" in your dessert. Miso's fermented saltiness doesn't make it taste like "sauce" but gives the caramel layer more depth and "completeness." After cracking through the caramel shell, you meet a silky custard where salt and sweet intertwine — a dessert that impresses dates.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 重奶油 | Heavy cream | 1.5杯 / 1.5 cups |
| 蛋黄 | Egg yolks | 4个 / 4 |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | ¼杯 + 4茶匙（焦糖用）/ ¼ cup + 4 tsp (for torching) |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a tiny pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 热奶油 | Heat Cream
小锅中小火加热奶油至边缘冒小泡（不要煮沸）。离火，加入味噌搅至完全溶解。

Heat cream over low heat until small bubbles form around the edges (don't boil). Remove from heat, whisk in miso until fully dissolved.

### 2. 调蛋液 | Temper Yolks
蛋黄和糖打至淡黄色。将热奶油缓缓倒入蛋液中，边倒边搅（别倒太快，会煮成蛋花）。加入香草精和盐。过筛。

Whisk yolks and sugar until pale yellow. Slowly pour hot cream into yolks, whisking constantly (too fast = scrambled eggs). Add vanilla and salt. Strain through a sieve.

### 3. 水浴烤 | Bake in Water Bath
倒入4个布蕾碗。碗放烤盘里，烤盘注入热水至碗的一半高度。烤箱 150°C (300°F) 烤 40-45分钟至边缘凝固、中间微晃。

Pour into 4 ramekins. Place in a baking pan, fill pan with hot water halfway up the ramekins. Bake at 150°C (300°F) for 40–45 minutes until edges are set but center jiggles slightly.

### 4. 冷藏 + 焦糖 | Chill & Torch
冷藏至少4小时。吃前每个撒1茶匙糖，用喷枪烤至焦糖色。没有喷枪就用 Broil 档（盯紧！）。

Refrigerate at least 4 hours. Before serving, sprinkle 1 tsp sugar on each, torch until caramelized. No torch? Use Broil (watch closely!).

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用白味噌（sweet miso），红味噌太强会抢味 | Use white/sweet miso — red miso overpowers the custard |
| 过筛是关键步骤，去掉蛋黄结块 | Straining is essential — removes any yolk lumps |
| 水浴烤确保均匀受热，不要跳过 | Water bath ensures even cooking — don't skip it |
| 焦糖层吃前才做，提前做会融化 | Torch sugar right before serving — it dissolves if done early |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **味噌在甜品中的魔法 / Miso's magic in desserts:**
> - 白味噌发酵时间短，甜度高、咸度低，适合甜品
> - 味噌中的游离氨基酸增加味觉"复杂度"和"深度"
> - "咸+甜"是经典的味觉对比增强——盐让甜更甜
> - 味噌的发酵风味（类似焦糖/坚果）和布蕾天然互补
>
> *White miso is fermented shorter, making it sweeter and less salty — ideal for desserts. Free amino acids in miso add "complexity" and "depth" to taste. "Salty + sweet" is classic taste contrast enhancement — salt makes sweet taste sweeter. Miso's fermented flavor (caramel/nutty notes) naturally complements creme brulee.*
