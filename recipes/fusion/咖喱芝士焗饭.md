# 咖喱芝士焗饭 | Curry Cheese Baked Rice

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、烤箱、一锅搞定、治愈系

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 米饭 | Cooked rice | 2碗 / 2 cups |
| 日式咖喱块 | Japanese curry roux | 2块 / 2 cubes |
| 鸡腿肉 | Chicken thigh | 150g / 5 oz |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1/2个 / 1/2 |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot | 1/2根 / 1/2 |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella cheese | 1杯 / 1 cup shredded |
| 帕玛森芝士 | Parmesan cheese | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp grated |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做咖喱 | Make Curry
鸡腿肉切块，洋葱胡萝卜切丁。锅中少油炒鸡肉变色，加蔬菜炒2分钟。加水300ml煮开，放咖喱块搅匀至浓稠。

Dice chicken, onion, and carrot. Sear chicken in a little oil, add veggies and stir 2 min. Add 300ml water, bring to boil, stir in curry cubes until thick.

### 2. 组装 | Assemble
米饭铺在烤盘底部，浇上咖喱，铺厚厚一层马苏里拉和帕玛森芝士。

Spread rice in a baking dish, ladle curry over it, and pile on a thick layer of mozzarella and Parmesan.

### 3. 焗烤 | Broil
烤箱 Broil 高温档，烤5-8分钟至芝士起泡变金黄。

Broil on high for 5–8 minutes until cheese is bubbling and golden brown.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
出炉稍凉2分钟（小心烫嘴），直接端烤盘上桌，挖着吃。

Rest 2 minutes (it's lava-hot), bring the dish straight to the table, and dig in.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么咖喱+焗芝士能打 / Why this works:**
> 日式咖喱块含有大量苹果泥和蜂蜜，提供温和的甜味基底。芝士在焗烤时发生美拉德反应，产生数百种焦香化合物。当咖喱的姜黄素（脂溶性）遇上融化芝士的脂肪，风味释放效率提升3倍。帕玛森芝士本身就是谷氨酸（鲜味）的炸弹，叠加在咖喱的鲜味上产生"鲜味倍增"效应。
>
> *Japanese curry roux contains apple puree and honey, providing a mellow sweet base. Cheese undergoes Maillard reactions during broiling, producing hundreds of toasty compounds. When curry's turmeric (fat-soluble) meets melting cheese fat, flavor delivery triples in efficiency. Parmesan is a glutamate bomb — layered on curry's own umami, it triggers a "umami multiplication" effect.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 咖喱要做浓一点，太稀的话焗出来会水 | Make the curry THICK — too thin and it gets watery under the broiler |
| 芝士要铺厚才能拉丝 | Pile cheese on THICK for maximum stretch |
| 用隔夜饭更好，水分少，口感更Q | Day-old rice works better — less moisture, chewier texture |
| Broil 最后2分钟要盯着看，芝士容易焦 | Watch the last 2 min closely — cheese burns fast under Broil |
