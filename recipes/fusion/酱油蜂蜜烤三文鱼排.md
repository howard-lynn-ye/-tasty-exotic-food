# 酱油蜂蜜烤三文鱼排 | Soy Honey Salmon Fillet

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 腌制 15分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、快手菜、健康

> 酱油与蜂蜜是东亚料理中最经典的甜咸搭配，刷在三文鱼上送入烤箱，高温下蜂蜜焦糖化、酱油美拉德，鱼皮酥脆鱼肉嫩滑，五分钟准备就能端上一道看起来花了一小时的菜。
>
> *Soy and honey form East Asia's most iconic sweet-salty duo. Brushed on salmon and roasted, the honey caramelizes while soy triggers the Maillard reaction — crispy skin, silky flesh, and a dish that looks like an hour of effort from just five minutes of prep.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 三文鱼排 (带皮) | Salmon fillet (skin-on) | 2块，各约180g / 2 pieces, ~180g each |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣，切末 / 2 cloves, minced |
| 姜 | Ginger | 1小块，磨泥 / 1 small piece, grated |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱腌制 | Marinate
将酱油、蜂蜜、蒜末、姜泥、米醋和芝麻油混合。三文鱼放入密封袋，倒入一半酱汁腌15分钟。另一半留作刷面用。

Mix soy, honey, garlic, ginger, vinegar, and sesame oil. Place salmon in zip-lock bag with half the sauce, marinate 15 min. Reserve the other half for basting.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
预热烤箱200°C。三文鱼皮面朝下放在铺了锡纸的烤盘上，刷一层保留的酱汁。烤12-15分钟。

Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F. Place salmon skin-side down on foil-lined tray, brush with reserved sauce. Roast 12-15 minutes.

### 3. 炙烤收尾 | Broil to Finish
最后2分钟开上火broil模式，让表面焦糖化形成亮丽釉面，注意别烤焦。

Switch to broil for the last 2 minutes to caramelize the glaze. Watch closely to avoid burning.

### 4. 出盘 | Serve
撒白芝麻和葱花，搭配蒸饭和清炒蔬菜。

Sprinkle sesame seeds and scallions. Serve with steamed rice and sauteed greens.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 三文鱼提前30分钟从冰箱取出回温，烤出来更均匀 | Take salmon out of fridge 30 min early for even cooking |
| 腌制别超过30分钟，酱油会"腌熟"鱼肉 | Don't marinate over 30 min — soy will cure the fish |
| 用锡纸接住滴落的酱汁，方便清洁 | Foil catches dripping glaze for easy cleanup |
| 鱼肉中心微透明是最佳状态，别烤过头 | Slightly translucent center is perfect — don't overcook |
