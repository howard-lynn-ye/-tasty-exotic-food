> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 酱油的谷氨酸（鲜味）+蜂蜜的果糖（焦糖化温度低于蔗糖）+杏仁的油脂（脂溶性香味载体）=完美的甜咸坚果。高温烤制时蜂蜜和酱油同时发生美拉德反应，在杏仁表面形成一层酥脆的深琥珀色釉面，咬开后释放出复杂的咸甜鲜味。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Soy sauce's glutamate (umami) + honey's fructose (lower caramelization point than sucrose) + almond's fats (lipid-soluble flavor carrier) = the perfect sweet-salty nut. During roasting, honey and soy sauce undergo simultaneous Maillard reactions, forming a crispy amber glaze that releases complex salty-sweet-umami flavor when bitten.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 生杏仁 | Raw almonds | 2杯 / 2 cups |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 海盐 | Sea salt | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 黑胡椒 (可选) | Black pepper (optional) | 少许 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 拌匀 | Coat
将杏仁、酱油、蜂蜜拌匀，确保每颗杏仁都裹上酱汁。

Toss almonds with soy sauce and honey until every nut is coated.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
铺在烤盘上（铺油纸防粘），350°F (175°C) 烤15分钟，中途翻拌一次。

Spread on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Roast at 350°F (175°C) for 15 min, stirring halfway.

### 3. 调味冷却 | Season & Cool
出炉趁热撒海盐和黑胡椒。完全冷却后才会变脆，别心急。

Sprinkle salt and pepper while hot. They crisp up only after cooling completely — be patient.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定要铺油纸，否则粘到怀疑人生 | Always use parchment paper, or you'll never get them off the pan |
| 冷却后密封保存，可以放一周 | Store in airtight container — good for a week |
| 可以换成腰果、核桃，时间相同 | Works with cashews or walnuts too, same timing |
