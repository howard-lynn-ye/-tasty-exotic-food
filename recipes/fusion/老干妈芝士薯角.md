> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 老干妈辣椒油里的豆豉碎和辣椒碎，本质上就是中式"辣椒酱"遇上美式"loaded fries"的碰撞。薯角先烤到外酥内糯，再铺上老干妈和芝士进行二次焗烤——融化的芝士把老干妈的辣香锁在每一口里。这是宿舍深夜投食的终极武器。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Lao Gan Ma's fermented black beans and chili flakes are essentially Chinese "hot sauce" meeting American "loaded fries." Wedges are baked until crispy outside and fluffy inside, then topped with LGM and cheese for a second bake — melted cheese locks in LGM's spicy fragrance in every bite. The ultimate midnight dorm weapon.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 土豆 | Potatoes | 3个大的 / 3 large |
| 老干妈油辣椒 | Lao Gan Ma chili crisp | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella cheese (shredded) | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 适量 / for garnish |
| 酸奶油（可选） | Sour cream (optional) | 适量 / for dipping |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切薯角 | Cut Wedges
土豆洗净不去皮，每个纵切成6-8个楔形。冷水泡10分钟去淀粉，擦干。

Wash potatoes (keep skin on), cut each into 6–8 wedges lengthwise. Soak in cold water 10 min to remove starch, pat dry.

### 2. 一次烤 | First Bake
薯角拌上橄榄油、蒜粉和盐。烤箱 220°C (425°F)，烤25分钟至金黄酥脆，中途翻面一次。

Toss wedges with olive oil, garlic powder, and salt. Bake at 220°C (425°F) for 25 minutes until golden, flipping once.

### 3. 加料二次焗 | Load & Broil
取出，每个薯角上抹一点老干妈，撒满芝士。开 Broil 档焗3-4分钟至芝士融化起泡。

Remove, dab LGM on each wedge, cover with cheese. Broil 3–4 minutes until cheese melts and bubbles.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒葱花，搭配酸奶油蘸着吃。

Sprinkle scallions. Serve with sour cream for dipping.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 泡水去淀粉 + 擦干 = 酥脆的关键 | Soaking + drying = the key to crispiness |
| 老干妈用"风味豆豉"版最香 | LGM "Black Bean" flavor is the most aromatic |
| 芝士要铺满，形成完整的"芝士盖" | Cover completely with cheese — form a full "cheese lid" |
| Broil 时别走开，芝士从完美到焦只要30秒 | Don't leave during Broil — cheese goes from perfect to burnt in 30 seconds |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么老干妈+芝士无敌 / Why LGM + cheese is unstoppable:**
> - 老干妈的发酵豆豉 = 游离谷氨酸 = 鲜味炸弹
> - 马苏里拉芝士加热后也释放大量谷氨酸
> - 双重谷氨酸来源 = 鲜味的"1+1>2"协同效应
> - 辣椒油的脂溶性辣椒素被芝士的乳脂包裹，辣感柔和持久
>
> *LGM's fermented black beans = free glutamate = umami bomb. Heated mozzarella also releases abundant glutamate. Double glutamate sources = synergistic umami ("1+1>2"). Chili oil's capsaicin is wrapped in cheese's milk fat, making the heat gentle and lingering.*
