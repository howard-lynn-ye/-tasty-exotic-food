> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 我创造了一杯"会在嘴里跳舞的茶"。花椒的麻感激活三叉神经的触觉通路，与柠檬酸的酸味、蜂蜜的甜味形成"麻-酸-甜"三重奏。花椒素在温热液体中释放速度最快（50-70°C），所以这杯茶要趁温热喝——太热会烫嘴，太冷花椒素沉淀。这是我为冬天设计的暖身饮品，也可以加冰变成夏日特调。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *I created a tea that "dances in your mouth." Sichuan pepper's numbing activates trigeminal tactile pathways, forming a "numb-sour-sweet" trio with citric acid and honey. Sanshool releases fastest in warm liquid (50–70°C), so drink this warm — too hot burns, too cold and sanshool precipitates. A winter warmer I designed, but also works over ice as a summer spritz.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 花椒 | Whole Sichuan peppercorns | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp (~15粒) |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | 半个 / 1/2 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 热水 | Hot water | 300ml / 1.25 cups |
| 生姜片（可选） | Ginger slices (optional) | 2片 / 2 slices |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮花椒水 | Simmer Peppercorn
花椒和姜片放入小锅，加水煮沸后转小火煮3分钟。关火。

Add peppercorns and ginger to a small pot with water. Bring to a boil, then simmer 3 min. Remove from heat.

### 2. 过滤调味 | Strain & Season
过滤掉花椒和姜片，趁热搅入蜂蜜至完全溶解。

Strain out peppercorns and ginger. Stir in honey while warm until fully dissolved.

### 3. 加柠檬 | Add Lemon
挤入半个柠檬汁，搅匀。柠檬片丢进杯中做装饰。

Squeeze in half a lemon. Stir. Drop a lemon slice in as garnish.

### 4. 调温享用 | Serve
等到可以入口的温度（约60°C）时饮用，这是花椒素释放的最佳温度。**夏日版：** 多加蜂蜜，倒入冰块杯中。

Drink when it cools to sipping temp (~60°C) — optimal for sanshool release. **Summer version:** add more honey, pour over ice.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么花椒+柠檬是隐藏绝配 / Why Sichuan pepper + lemon is a hidden perfect pair:**
> - 花椒素作用于触觉（第五感），柠檬酸作用于味觉（酸味受体PKD2L1）——两条不同神经通路同时激活
> - 温热状态下花椒素的溶解度最高，50-70°C时释放量是冷水的3倍
> - 蜂蜜的果糖在酸性环境中甜度感知增强（果糖在pH<4时甜度最高）
> - 柠檬皮油中的柠檬烯（limonene）与花椒的芳樟醇（linalool）同属萜烯类，分子结构相似，产生和谐的"柑橘-花香"
>
> *Sanshool acts on touch (somatosensory), citric acid on taste (PKD2L1 sour receptors) — two different neural pathways fire simultaneously. Sanshool solubility peaks at 50–70°C, releasing 3x more than cold water. Honey's fructose tastes sweeter in acidic conditions (fructose sweetness peaks below pH 4). Lemon peel's limonene and Sichuan pepper's linalool are both terpenes with similar molecular structures, creating harmonious "citrus-floral" notes.*
