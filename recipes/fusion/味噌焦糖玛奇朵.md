# 味噌焦糖玛奇朵 | Miso Caramel Macchiato

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 8分钟 | 💰 ~$2/杯 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、饮品、咖啡

> 味噌焦糖是近年精品咖啡圈的新宠——白味噌的咸鲜让焦糖的甜度更立体、更有深度，淋在浓缩咖啡和蒸奶上，每一口都是咸甜交织的丝滑享受。一旦喝过就再也回不去普通焦糖了。
>
> *Miso caramel is the darling of specialty coffee — white miso's salinity gives caramel three-dimensional depth. Drizzled over espresso and steamed milk, every sip is a silky dance of salt and sweet. Once you try it, plain caramel will never be the same.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **味噌焦糖酱** | **Miso Caramel Sauce** | |
| 白砂糖 | Granulated sugar | 100g |
| 淡奶油 | Heavy cream | 60ml，温热 / 60ml, warmed |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 30g |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| **玛奇朵** | **Macchiato** | |
| 浓缩咖啡 | Espresso | 2份 / 2 shots |
| 牛奶 | Milk | 200ml |
| 味噌焦糖酱 | Miso caramel sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮焦糖 | Cook Caramel
中火将糖放入干净的小锅，不要搅拌，只轻轻晃动锅子。糖融化变成深琥珀色后立刻关火。

Heat sugar in a clean small saucepan over medium heat. Do not stir — just gently swirl the pan. When it turns deep amber, immediately remove from heat.

### 2. 加奶油味噌 | Add Cream & Miso
关火后小心加入温热的淡奶油（会剧烈冒泡），搅拌均匀。加入黄油和味噌搅至顺滑。放凉备用。

Off heat, carefully pour in warm cream (it will bubble violently). Stir smooth. Add butter and miso, whisk until silky. Let cool.

### 3. 打奶泡 | Steam Milk
牛奶用蒸汽棒或法压壶打出细腻泡沫，温度65°C左右。

Steam milk to ~65°C with fine microfoam, or pump vigorously in a French press.

### 4. 组装 | Assemble
杯底淋2汤匙味噌焦糖酱，倒入蒸奶，最后轻轻注入浓缩咖啡（形成分层）。顶部再淋少许焦糖酱画花纹。

Drizzle 2 tbsp miso caramel in cup bottom. Pour in steamed milk, then gently add espresso (to create layers). Drizzle more caramel on top in a pattern.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 焦糖一过色就关火，1秒之差就会焦苦 | Pull the caramel off heat the moment it turns amber — 1 second too long = bitter |
| 味噌焦糖酱可以冰箱保存2周，多做一些 | Miso caramel keeps 2 weeks in fridge — make a big batch |
| 冰版也很棒：杯中加冰块，不用打奶泡 | Iced version works great — skip the foam, just add ice |
| 没有咖啡机可以用摩卡壶或浓煮手冲代替 | No espresso machine? Moka pot or strong pour-over works |
