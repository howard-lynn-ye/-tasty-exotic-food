> 经典黑糖珍珠加入一味花椒，瞬间变得不平凡。嚼到Q弹珍珠时先是黑糖的焦甜，然后花椒的微麻在舌尖绽放，和冰牛奶混在一起简直是夏天的终极享受。这杯奶茶会让奶茶店老板想偷你的配方。

> *Classic brown sugar boba gains an extraordinary twist with Sichuan peppercorn. As you chew the bouncy pearls, first comes brown sugar's caramel sweetness, then peppercorn's gentle tingle blooms on your tongue — mixed with cold milk, it's summer's ultimate treat. This drink would make boba shop owners want to steal your recipe.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| **珍珠** | **Boba Pearls** | |
| 木薯淀粉 | Tapioca starch | 100g |
| 黑糖 | Dark brown sugar / muscovado | 40g |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 热水 | Boiling water | 50ml |
| **黑糖花椒糖浆** | **Brown Sugar Pepper Syrup** | |
| 黑糖 | Dark brown sugar | 80g |
| 水 | Water | 60ml |
| 花椒 (整粒) | Whole Sichuan peppercorn | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| **奶茶** | **Milk Tea** | |
| 牛奶或燕麦奶 | Milk or oat milk | 800ml (分4杯 / for 4 cups) |
| 冰块 | Ice | 适量 / as needed |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做珍珠 | Make Boba
黑糖和花椒粉溶于热水中。倒入木薯淀粉快速搅拌成团，揉至光滑不粘手。搓成小圆球（约1cm直径），撒薄粉防粘。

Dissolve brown sugar and peppercorn powder in boiling water. Pour into tapioca starch, stir quickly into a dough, knead until smooth. Roll into small balls (~1cm diameter), dust with starch to prevent sticking.

### 2. 煮珍珠 | Cook Boba
大锅水煮沸，下珍珠，中火煮15分钟至浮起且透明。关火盖盖焖10分钟。捞出过冷水沥干。

Boil a large pot of water, add boba, cook over medium heat 15 min until they float and turn translucent. Cover, let sit 10 min off heat. Drain and rinse briefly in cold water.

### 3. 煮糖浆 | Make Syrup
黑糖、水和整粒花椒小火煮5分钟至浓稠。滤出花椒，将糖浆倒在煮好的珍珠上拌匀。

Simmer brown sugar, water, and whole peppercorns on low heat for 5 min until syrupy. Strain out peppercorns, pour syrup over cooked boba and toss.

### 4. 组装 | Assemble
杯中先放珍珠和糖浆，加冰块，沿杯壁缓缓倒入冰牛奶形成分层。插粗吸管，搅拌后饮用。

Spoon boba and syrup into glasses, add ice, slowly pour cold milk along the glass wall for layers. Insert fat straw, stir and drink.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 揉面团时要趁热，凉了就揉不动了 | Knead the dough while hot — it becomes unworkable when cool |
| 花椒量从少开始调，太多会喧宾夺主 | Start with less peppercorn — too much overwhelms the drink |
| 珍珠做好当天吃完，隔夜会变硬 | Eat boba the same day — they harden overnight |
| 嫌麻烦可以用买的珍珠，只做花椒糖浆 | Shortcut: use store-bought boba, just make the pepper syrup |
