# 味噌枫糖烤红薯 | Miso Maple Roasted Sweet Potato

> 🤖 AI Original | ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 35分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、秋冬暖食、烤箱一盘

> 日本味噌的发酵咸鲜与加拿大枫糖的焦甜在烤红薯上合体——这个组合让普通红薯的甜度飙升，同时增加让人欲罢不能的umami深度。切面焦糖化的纹路美到可以当壁纸。
>
> *Japanese miso's fermented salt and Canadian maple's burnt sweetness unite on roasted sweet potato — this combo skyrockets a humble spud's sweetness while adding irresistible umami depth. The caramelized cross-hatch is wallpaper-worthy.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 红薯 | Sweet potatoes | 2个中等 / 2 medium |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1.5汤匙 / 1.5 tbsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 黄油 | Butter (melted) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切花备薯 | Score the Potatoes
红薯纵切对半，在切面划十字花刀（深约1cm）。烤箱预热200°C/400°F。

Halve sweet potatoes lengthwise. Score cut sides in a crosshatch (~1cm deep). Preheat oven to 200°C/400°F.

### 2. 刷酱烤制 | Glaze & Roast
混合味噌、枫糖浆和融化黄油，刷在红薯切面上（花刀缝隙也要刷到）。切面朝上烤30-35分钟至软糯焦糖化。

Mix miso, maple syrup, and melted butter. Brush on cut sides (get into the grooves). Roast cut-side up 30-35 min until tender and caramelized.

### 3. 撒料享用 | Top & Enjoy
出炉撒芝麻和葱花，趁热用勺子挖着吃。

Top with sesame seeds and scallions. Eat hot with a spoon.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

味噌中的谷氨酸钠和枫糖中的槭糖醇在美拉德反应中共同产生焦糖-鲜味复合物，比单独使用任一调料的风味复杂度高出数倍。红薯本身富含麦芽糖（约占干重的20%），三者叠加创造出"甜×鲜×焦"的三维味觉体验。

*Miso's sodium glutamate and maple's saccharol co-produce caramel-umami complexes during Maillard reactions, multiplying flavor complexity beyond either alone. Sweet potatoes already contain ~20% maltose by dry weight; the trio creates a three-dimensional sweet-savory-caramelized experience.*
