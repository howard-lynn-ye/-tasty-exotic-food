## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 意面 | Spaghetti or penne | 200g / 7 oz |
| 郫县豆瓣酱 | Pixian doubanjiang | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 番茄罐头 | Canned crushed tomatoes | 1罐 / 1 can (400g) |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 帕玛森芝士 | Parmesan cheese | 适量 / for topping |
| 新鲜罗勒 | Fresh basil | 几片 / a few leaves |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒酱底 | Build the Base
锅中橄榄油加热，小火炒豆瓣酱1分钟至出红油。加入蒜末炒香30秒。

Heat olive oil, fry doubanjiang on low for 1 min until red oil blooms. Add minced garlic, sizzle 30 sec.

### 2. 番茄酱汁 | Tomato Sauce
倒入番茄罐头和糖，中火煮10分钟至酱汁浓稠。加少量盐调味（注意豆瓣酱本身很咸）。

Add crushed tomatoes and sugar. Simmer 10 min until thickened. Season lightly (doubanjiang is already salty).

### 3. 煮面拌合 | Cook & Toss
同时煮意面至al dente。沥干面条直接倒入酱汁锅，加2汤匙煮面水，大火翻拌30秒。

Meanwhile, cook pasta to al dente. Drain and add directly to sauce. Splash in 2 tbsp pasta water, toss 30 sec over high heat.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
盛盘，撒帕玛森芝士和罗勒叶。

Plate, top with Parmesan and basil leaves.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **豆瓣酱 vs 意式辣酱的鲜味对决 / Doubanjiang vs Italian chili — an umami showdown:**
> - 郫县豆瓣酱经6-12个月日晒发酵，产生大量游离氨基酸（鲜味深度远超辣椒碎）
> - 番茄含有高浓度谷氨酸（260mg/100g），与豆瓣的氨基酸形成"鲜味和弦"
> - 橄榄油的油酸（oleic acid）作为脂溶性载体，让辣椒素和番茄红素均匀传递
> - 帕玛森芝士的酪氨酸晶体提供第三层鲜味——三重鲜味风暴
>
> *Pixian doubanjiang undergoes 6–12 months of sun-fermented aging, producing massive free amino acids (umami depth far beyond red pepper flakes). Tomatoes contain high glutamate (260mg/100g), forming an "umami chord" with doubanjiang. Olive oil's oleic acid carries capsaicin and lycopene evenly. Parmesan's tyrosine crystals add a third umami layer — a triple umami storm.*
