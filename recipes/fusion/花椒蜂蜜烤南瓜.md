Pumpkin's sugars caramelize at high heat, forming an amber "sweet barrier" on the surface; sanshool pierces through, injecting electric tingle into the sweetness. Honey adds caramel depth. A dish that makes you slightly sweat in the autumn breeze.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 南瓜/栗子南瓜 | Pumpkin or butternut squash | 500g, 切块 / cubed |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 香菜（可选） | Cilantro (optional) | 几枝 / a few sprigs |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调味 | Season
南瓜块与橄榄油、蒜粉、盐和一半花椒粉拌匀。

Toss pumpkin cubes with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and half the Sichuan pepper.

### 2. 初烤 | First Roast
铺在烤盘上，220°C (425°F) 烤20分钟至边缘开始焦化。

Spread on a baking sheet. Roast at 220°C (425°F) for 20 min until edges begin to char.

### 3. 蜂蜜花椒釉 | Honey-Pepper Glaze
蜂蜜和剩余花椒粉混合。取出南瓜淋上花椒蜂蜜，继续烤10分钟至釉面金黄冒泡。

Mix honey with remaining Sichuan pepper. Remove pumpkin, drizzle with the glaze, roast 10 more min until glaze is golden and bubbly.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
撒芝麻和香菜叶，趁热吃。配米饭或作为烤肉配菜都绝佳。

Top with sesame and cilantro. Eat hot. Perfect as a side with rice or grilled meats.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **南瓜+花椒的温度依赖性风味学 / Temperature-dependent flavor science of pumpkin + pepper:**
> - 南瓜含大量β-胡萝卜素和游离糖（蔗糖+葡萄糖），在200°C以上焦糖化产生焦甜香
> - 花椒素在高温油脂中溶解度极佳，橄榄油作为载体让麻感均匀分布
> - 蜂蜜的果糖在180°C以上生成5-羟甲基糠醛（HMF），赋予深色焦糖的复杂风味
> - 南瓜的柔软质地（烤后如丝绒）与花椒的触觉刺激形成"温柔/尖锐"的口感对比
>
> *Pumpkin's beta-carotene and free sugars (sucrose + glucose) caramelize above 200°C. Sanshool dissolves excellently in hot fats — olive oil carries the numbing sensation evenly. Honey's fructose produces HMF (5-hydroxymethylfurfural) above 180°C, giving deep caramel complexity. Pumpkin's velvety post-roast texture contrasts with pepper's tactile sting — a "gentle/sharp" mouthfeel dialogue.*
