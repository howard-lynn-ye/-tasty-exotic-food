# 辣椒油酸奶碗 | Chili Oil Yogurt Bowl

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 这个看似疯狂的搭配其实有坚实的科学基础：辣椒素（capsaicin）是脂溶性的，酸奶中的乳脂完美包裹辣椒素分子，让辣味变得柔和绵长而非尖锐刺激。中东和土耳其早就有"辣油+酸奶"的传统（如cilbir土耳其鸡蛋），而中式辣椒油加入了花椒、八角等复合香料，让这碗酸奶的风味维度远超想象。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *This seemingly wild combo has solid science: capsaicin is fat-soluble, and yogurt's milk fat perfectly encapsulates capsaicin molecules, making heat mellow and lingering instead of sharp. The Middle East and Turkey have long paired chili oil with yogurt (like cilbir), but Chinese chili oil's Sichuan peppercorn and star anise add flavor dimensions far beyond expectations.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 希腊酸奶 | Greek yogurt (full-fat) | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 中式辣椒油 | Chinese chili oil (crispy) | 1-2汤匙 / 1-2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 黄瓜丁 | Diced cucumber | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup |
| 花生碎 | Crushed peanuts | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香菜 (可选) | Cilantro (optional) | 少许 / a sprig |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 铺底 | Base
酸奶倒入碗中铺平。

Spoon yogurt into a bowl and spread flat.

### 2. 淋油加料 | Drizzle & Top
淋上辣椒油（带渣的那种更好），滴蜂蜜，放黄瓜丁和花生碎。

Drizzle chili oil (the kind with sediment is better), add honey, top with cucumber and crushed peanuts.

### 3. 拌着吃 | Mix & Eat
可以直接吃，也可以拌匀。配馕饼或吐司蘸着吃更好。

Eat as-is or mix together. Even better with naan or toast for dipping.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 酸奶必须用全脂的，脱脂的太稀且接不住辣油 | Full-fat yogurt only — nonfat is too thin and can't carry the oil |
| 辣椒油要选有颗粒感的（老干妈或自制） | Use chili crisp with crunchy bits, not just plain chili oil |
| 甜辣咸的比例自己调到喜欢的平衡 | Adjust the sweet-spicy-salty balance to your preference |
