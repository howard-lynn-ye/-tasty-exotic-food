## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 面条 | Noodles (any kind) | 200g / 7 oz |
| 芝麻酱 | Sesame paste or tahini | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 适量 / for garnish |
| 黄瓜丝 | Shredded cucumber | 适量 / for garnish |

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调酱 | Make Sauce
碗中放入芝麻酱，加2汤匙热水搅拌至顺滑（芝麻酱很稠，需要用水泄开）。加入老干妈、酱油、米醋、糖和蒜泥搅匀。

Put sesame paste in a bowl, add 2 tbsp hot water, stir until smooth (it's thick — water loosens it). Add LGM, soy, vinegar, sugar, and garlic. Mix well.

### 2. 煮面 | Cook Noodles
大锅水煮面条至刚好（按包装时间）。如果吃凉面，过冷水沥干。

Boil noodles according to package. For cold noodles, rinse under cold water and drain.

### 3. 拌面 | Toss
面条倒入酱汁中，用筷子大力翻拌至每根面条都裹满酱。如果太稠加一点煮面水调节。

Pour noodles into the sauce, toss vigorously with chopsticks until every strand is coated. Add a splash of noodle water if too thick.

### 4. 摆盘 | Plate
堆成小山状，顶上铺黄瓜丝和葱花。喜欢的话加个溏心蛋。

Mound into a hill, top with cucumber shreds and scallion. Add a soft-boiled egg if you like.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **芝麻酱+老干妈的乳化科学 / Sesame paste + LGM emulsion science:**
> - 芝麻酱含约50%脂肪和丰富的卵磷脂（lecithin），是天然乳化剂
> - 卵磷脂分子一端亲水一端亲油，将老干妈的辣椒油和酱油的水溶液"焊接"在一起
> - 形成的稳定乳液（emulsion）粘度高，能紧紧"抱住"面条——这就是为什么拌面比浇面入味
> - 芝麻中的芝麻素（sesamin）在口腔温度下释放坚果芳香，与老干妈的烟熏辣味形成"坚果-烟熏"味觉轴
>
> *Sesame paste is ~50% fat with rich lecithin — a natural emulsifier. Lecithin molecules have hydrophilic and hydrophobic ends, "welding" LGM's chili oil with soy's aqueous solution. The stable emulsion has high viscosity, "hugging" each noodle tightly — why tossed noodles taste better than sauced-on-top. Sesame's sesamin releases nutty aroma at mouth temperature, forming a "nutty-smoky" taste axis with LGM's smoky heat.*
