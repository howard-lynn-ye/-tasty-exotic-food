# 味噌红薯浓汤 | Miso Sweet Potato Soup

> 🤖 AI Original | ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$3/4份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、秋冬暖汤、日美混搭

> 美式红薯浓汤的甜暖底色加上日本味噌的发酵鲜味——这碗汤在口中展开像一幅秋日画卷：先是红薯的绵密甜，接着味噌的悠长鲜。一碗喝完，身心都被温暖。
>
> *American sweet potato soup's warm sweetness plus Japanese miso's fermented umami — this bowl unfolds like an autumn landscape: first potato's pillowy sweetness, then miso's lingering savory. One bowl warms body and soul.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 红薯 | Sweet potatoes (peeled, cubed) | 500g / ~3 medium |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium |
| 蔬菜汤 | Vegetable broth | 3杯 / 3 cups |
| 椰奶或淡奶油 | Coconut milk or cream | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 姜 | Fresh ginger | 1小段 / 1" piece |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒底料煮薯 | Sauté & Simmer
少油炒洋葱丁和姜片至透明。加入红薯块和蔬菜汤，大火煮沸后转中小火煮20分钟至红薯软烂。

Sauté diced onion and ginger in a little oil until translucent. Add sweet potato and broth. Boil, then simmer 20 min until potatoes are very tender.

### 2. 打成浓汤 | Blend Smooth
关火，加入椰奶。用手持搅拌机打至丝滑。取一小碗汤，将味噌在其中化开（避免味噌遇高温损失益生菌），再倒回锅中搅匀。

Off heat, add coconut milk. Blend until silky. Dissolve miso in a small bowl of the soup (avoids killing probiotics with high heat), pour back and stir.

### 3. 调味盛碗 | Season & Serve
调盐（味噌已有咸度，小心别过咸）。盛碗后可淋少许麻油、撒葱花或烤南瓜籽装饰。

Season with salt (miso is already salty — go easy). Ladle into bowls, finish with a drizzle of sesame oil, scallions, or toasted pumpkin seeds.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

红薯加热后淀粉酶将淀粉分解为麦芽糖，提供天然甜度；味噌的蛋白酶分解产物提供谷氨酸鲜味——两者在口腔中激活不同味觉受体，大脑将其整合为"丰富而完整"的综合味觉体验。味噌在非高温下加入保留了活性乳酸菌，为这碗汤增添了消化健康的额外益处。

*Heating activates sweet potato amylase to break starch into maltose for natural sweetness; miso's protease-derived glutamate provides umami — activating different taste receptors that the brain integrates as a "rich and complete" experience. Adding miso off-heat preserves live lactobacillus, giving this soup a bonus digestive-health benefit.*
