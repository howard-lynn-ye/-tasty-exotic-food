# 老干妈芝士烤玉米 | Lao Gan Ma Cheese Corn

> ⏱ 准备5分+烹饪20分 | 💰~$5/份 | 🏷️ 创意融合、小食、中西合璧

> **💡 灵感** — 老干妈辣椒酱的香辣与芝士的浓郁奶香在烤玉米上完美结合，灵感来自墨西哥烤玉米（elote）和中国街头小吃的混搭。一口咬下，油辣、奶香、甜玉米三重奏。
> **💡 Inspiration** — *Lao Gan Ma chili crisp's spicy crunch meets melted cheese on corn — a mashup of Mexican elote and Chinese street food. Each bite delivers a trio of oily heat, dairy richness, and sweet corn.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 甜玉米 | Sweet corn | 4根 / 4 ears |
| 老干妈油辣椒 | Lao Gan Ma chili crisp | 40g / 3 tbsp |
| 马苏里拉芝士 | Mozzarella (shredded) | 80g / ¾ cup |
| 帕尔马森芝士 | Parmesan (grated) | 20g / 2 tbsp |
| 蛋黄酱 | Mayonnaise | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 青柠 | Lime | 1个 / 1 pc |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮玉米 | Boil Corn
玉米去壳，沸水煮8分钟至刚熟，沥干。Shuck corn, boil 8 minutes until just cooked, drain.

### 2. 抹酱 | Coat
每根玉米均匀涂一层薄蛋黄酱，再抹上老干妈辣椒油，撒满马苏里拉芝士丝。Spread a thin layer of mayo on each ear, then smear Lao Gan Ma chili crisp, and cover with shredded mozzarella.

### 3. 烤制 | Broil
烤箱上火220°C烤8-10分钟，至芝士融化起泡微焦。Broil at 425°F for 8-10 minutes until cheese is melted, bubbly, and lightly charred.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
出炉撒帕尔马森芝士粉和香菜碎，挤青柠汁，趁热开吃。Remove from oven, dust with Parmesan and chopped cilantro, squeeze lime juice, eat while hot.
