> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 烤土豆的内部是完美的淀粉海绵体，能吸收任何酱汁。老干妈的辣椒油渗入松软的土豆泥中，豆豉颗粒提供咀嚼感对比。加上酸奶油的乳酸，形成"辣+鲜+酸+脂"四味平衡——这本质上是把西方loaded baked potato的黄油酸奶油体系，换成了中式辣酱鲜味体系。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *A baked potato's interior is a perfect starch sponge, absorbing any sauce. Lao Gan Ma's chili oil seeps into fluffy potato flesh while fermented black bean bits add textural contrast. Add sour cream's lactic acid for "spicy + umami + sour + fat" balance — essentially replacing the Western loaded baked potato's butter-sour cream system with a Chinese chili-umami system.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大号烤土豆 | Large baking potatoes (Russet) | 2个 / 2 |
| 老干妈辣椒酱 | Lao Gan Ma Chili Crisp | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 酸奶油 | Sour cream | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 芝士丝 | Shredded cheddar | 1/4杯 / 1/4 cup |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 橄榄油+盐 | Olive oil + salt (for skin) | 适量 / as needed |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 烤土豆 | Bake Potato
土豆洗净擦干，叉子戳几个洞。涂橄榄油撒盐。直接放烤架上，400°F (200°C) 烤45-50分钟至内部松软。

Wash, dry, and prick potatoes with a fork. Rub with oil and salt. Place directly on oven rack, bake at 400°F (200°C) for 45-50 min until fluffy inside.

### 2. 开花加料 | Split & Load
十字切开土豆（别切断），用叉子把内部稍微弄松。堆上老干妈、酸奶油、芝士丝。

Cut a cross on top (don't cut through). Fluff the inside with a fork. Load with Lao Gan Ma, sour cream, and cheese.

### 3. 回炉撒葱 | Broil & Garnish
回炉broil 2-3分钟至芝士融化。撒葱花上桌。

Broil 2-3 min until cheese melts. Sprinkle scallions and serve.

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 一定选 Russet 土豆，淀粉含量高才松软 | Always use Russet potatoes — high starch = fluffy interior |
| 微波炉快速版：戳洞后微波8分钟也行 | Microwave shortcut: prick and nuke 8 min for a quick version |
| 加培根碎和鸡肉丝变成完整一餐 | Add bacon bits and shredded chicken for a complete meal |
