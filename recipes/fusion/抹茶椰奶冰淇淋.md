> 不用冰淇淋机也能做出丝滑的抹茶冰淇淋——秘诀是椰奶的高脂肪含量。椰奶的热带奶香与抹茶的清苦完美中和，每一口都有抹茶的茶韵和椰子的丝滑。纯素友好，无乳糖，比大多数商业冰淇淋还好吃。

> *Silky matcha ice cream without an ice cream machine — the secret is coconut milk's high fat content. Coconut's tropical creaminess perfectly balances matcha's clean bitterness. Every spoonful carries tea fragrance and coconut silk. Vegan-friendly, lactose-free, and better than most store-bought ice cream.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 全脂椰奶 (罐装) | Full-fat coconut milk (canned) | 2罐 (800ml) / 2 cans (800ml) |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder (culinary grade) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp (12g) |
| 糖 | Sugar | 80g |
| 玉米淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1小撮 / a pinch |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 热水 | Hot water | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 化抹茶 | Bloom Matcha
抹茶粉过筛后加入热水，用茶筅或小打蛋器搅至完全无颗粒的浓抹茶糊。

Sift matcha, add hot water, whisk vigorously with a chasen or small whisk until a smooth, lump-free paste forms.

### 2. 煮基底 | Cook Base
椰奶倒入小锅，加糖和盐中火加热搅拌至糖溶。取2汤匙热椰奶和玉米淀粉调成浆，倒回锅中搅拌至微微浓稠。

Pour coconut milk into a saucepan, add sugar and salt, heat over medium while stirring until sugar dissolves. Mix 2 tbsp hot coconut milk with cornstarch, pour back in, stir until slightly thickened.

### 3. 混合 | Combine
离火，加入抹茶糊和香草精搅匀。倒入容器，贴面覆保鲜膜，冷却至室温后放入冰箱冷藏2小时。

Remove from heat, stir in matcha paste and vanilla. Pour into a container, press plastic wrap on the surface, cool to room temp, then refrigerate 2 hours.

### 4. 冷冻 | Freeze
倒入浅容器冷冻。每隔45分钟取出用叉子搅碎冰晶（共3-4次），最后一次后冻至完全凝固。或用冰淇淋机按说明搅拌。

Pour into a shallow container and freeze. Every 45 min, remove and break up ice crystals with a fork (repeat 3-4 times). After the last stir, freeze until solid. Or churn in an ice cream machine per its instructions.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 必须用全脂椰奶（罐装），低脂的做出来像冰沙 | Must use full-fat canned coconut milk — light versions make icy slush |
| 每45分钟搅拌是保证细腻的关键，别偷懒 | Stirring every 45 min prevents large ice crystals — don't skip this |
| 吃前放室温5分钟回软，口感更丝滑 | Let sit at room temp 5 min before serving for silkier texture |
| 可以加红豆、麻薯或椰果做toppings | Top with red bean, mochi, or nata de coco |
