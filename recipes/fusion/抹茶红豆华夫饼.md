# 抹茶红豆华夫饼 | Matcha Red Bean Waffle

> ⏱ 30分钟 | 💰~$6/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、早午餐、甜食

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 比利时华夫饼的酥脆格子里藏着京都抹茶的清苦，蜜红豆是来自东京的甜蜜问候。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *A Belgian waffle grid hides Kyoto matcha's gentle bitterness, topped with sweet red beans — a Tokyo greeting on a Brussels classic.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 200g / 1½ cups |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 10g / 2 tsp |
| 蜜红豆 | Sweetened red beans | 100g / ½ cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 牛奶 | Milk | 240ml / 1 cup |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter, melted | 45g / 3 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 调面糊 | Mix Batter
面粉与抹茶粉过筛混合，加入鸡蛋、牛奶、融化黄油搅拌至顺滑无颗粒。
Sift flour and matcha together, add eggs, milk, and melted butter, whisk until smooth and lump-free.

### 2. 烤华夫 | Cook Waffles
华夫饼机预热刷油，倒入面糊烤3-4分钟至金绿色酥脆。
Preheat and grease waffle iron, pour batter, cook 3-4 min until crisp and golden-green.

### 3. 叠层装盘 | Stack & Top
华夫饼叠放，浇上蜜红豆、淋少许炼乳和撒抹茶粉装饰。
Stack waffles, spoon sweetened red beans on top, drizzle condensed milk, dust with matcha.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 抹茶的儿茶素苦味被华夫面糊中的糖和油脂柔化，与红豆淀粉的绵密甜感形成三层口感梯度。 *Matcha catechin bitterness is softened by batter's sugar and fat, forming a three-tier texture gradient with red bean starch's pillowy sweetness.*
