# 抹茶白巧曲奇 | Matcha White Chocolate Cookie

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 冷藏 1小时 + 烹饪 11分钟 | 💰 ~$5/批(约18块) | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、烘焙

> 抹茶的草本苦韵与白巧克力的奶甜在一块曲奇中达到完美平衡。翠绿色的面团配上白色巧克力块，颜值与口感双重在线。外酥内软，茶香四溢——这是日式茶道与西式烘焙的甜蜜联姻。
>
> *Matcha's grassy bitterness and white chocolate's creamy sweetness find perfect balance in one cookie. Emerald dough studded with white chocolate chunks — stunning looks and incredible texture. Crisp edges, chewy center, fragrant with tea — a sweet marriage of Japanese ceremony and Western baking.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter (softened) | 115g |
| 糖粉 | Powdered sugar | 80g |
| 蛋黄 | Egg yolk | 1个 / 1 |
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 180g |
| 抹茶粉 (烘焙级) | Matcha powder (culinary grade) | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp (12g) |
| 白巧克力 | White chocolate | 100g，切粗块 / 100g, roughly chopped |
| 盐 | Salt | 1小撮 / a pinch |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 打黄油 | Cream Butter
软化黄油加糖粉打至蓬松发白（约2分钟），加入蛋黄和香草精拌匀。

Cream softened butter with powdered sugar until fluffy and pale (~2 min). Beat in egg yolk and vanilla.

### 2. 加干料 | Add Dry
面粉和抹茶粉一起过筛加入，用刮刀翻拌至刚好成团，拌入白巧克力块。不要过度搅拌。

Sift flour and matcha together, fold into butter mixture until just combined. Fold in white chocolate chunks. Do not overmix.

### 3. 冷藏成型 | Chill & Shape
面团整成直径5cm的圆柱，保鲜膜包紧，冷藏至少1小时至硬实。

Roll dough into a 5cm-diameter log, wrap tightly in plastic, refrigerate at least 1 hour until firm.

### 4. 切片烤制 | Slice & Bake
预热170°C。将面团切成8mm厚的圆片，间隔3cm放在烤盘上。烤10-11分钟至边缘微微变色。

Preheat to 170°C/340°F. Slice dough into 8mm rounds, place 3cm apart. Bake 10-11 min until edges barely turn golden.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用烘焙级抹茶而非仪式级，性价比高且风味足够 | Use culinary-grade matcha — ceremonial grade is too expensive for baking |
| 抹茶必须过筛，否则会有苦涩结块 | Always sift matcha — lumps are bitter |
| 白巧克力选可可脂含量高的，别用代可可脂 | Choose real white chocolate (cocoa butter), not confectionery coating |
| 冷藏越久切片越整齐，急的话可以冷冻20分钟 | Longer chill = cleaner slices; freezer 20 min works in a pinch |
