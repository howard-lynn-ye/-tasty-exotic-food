> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 泡菜的乳酸发酵产生的酸味与芝士的酪蛋白形成经典的"酸+脂"搭配（就像葡萄酒配芝士的原理）。泡菜加热后辣椒素释放加速，被融化的芝士包裹后辣度变得温和圆润。墨西哥饼皮煎至酥脆后提供质感对比——酥脆外壳+软糯芝士+爽脆泡菜。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Kimchi's lactic acid fermentation pairs with cheese's casein in a classic "acid + fat" combination (same principle as wine and cheese pairing). Heating releases capsaicin faster, but melted cheese wraps around it, making the heat mellow and round. The crispy tortilla provides textural contrast — crunchy shell + gooey cheese + tangy kimchi.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 大号面饼 | Large flour tortillas | 2张 / 2 |
| 韩式泡菜 | Kimchi (chopped) | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 芝士丝 | Shredded cheese (cheddar/mozzarella mix) | 1杯 / 1 cup |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 酸奶油 (蘸料) | Sour cream (for dipping) | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备馅料 | Prep Filling
泡菜沥干切碎（汁留着蘸），和芝士丝、葱花、芝麻油拌匀。

Drain and chop kimchi (save juice for dipping). Toss with cheese, scallions, and sesame oil.

### 2. 煎饼 | Cook
平底锅中火，放一张饼皮，铺上馅料，盖上另一张饼皮。煎3分钟至底部金黄，小心翻面再煎2-3分钟。

Medium heat skillet. Lay one tortilla, spread filling, top with second tortilla. Cook 3 min until golden, carefully flip, cook 2-3 min more.

### 3. 切块上桌 | Slice & Serve
切成6块三角形。配酸奶油或泡菜汁蘸着吃。

Cut into 6 triangles. Serve with sour cream or kimchi juice for dipping.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 泡菜一定要沥干，否则饼皮会软塌 | Drain kimchi well, or the tortilla goes soggy |
| 中小火慢煎，芝士才有时间完全融化 | Medium-low heat lets cheese fully melt before tortilla burns |
| 加一片午餐肉或培根碎，蛋白质翻倍 | Add Spam slices or bacon bits for extra protein |
