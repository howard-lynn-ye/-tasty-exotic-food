# 红油拌牛油果 | Chili Oil Avocado Bowl

> ⏱ 5分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、免烹饪、素食、健身

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 加州牛油果的奶油质地遇上四川红油的麻辣，一勺下去像是洛杉矶和成都在你嘴里举行了一场不可能的约会。不需要开火，五分钟搞定一碗让你说不出话的美味。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Creamy California avocado meets fiery Sichuan chili oil — one spoonful feels like LA and Chengdu having an impossible date on your tongue. No cooking needed, five minutes to a bowl that leaves you speechless.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛油果 | Ripe avocado | 1个 / 1 |
| 红油/辣椒油 | Chili oil (with sediment) | 1-2汤匙 / 1-2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 适量 / for garnish |
| 葱花 | Scallion | 1根 / 1 stalk |
| 米饭 (可选) | Steamed rice (optional) | 1碗 / 1 bowl |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切牛油果 | Prep Avocado
牛油果对半切开去核，切成厚片或大块摆入碗中。

Halve, pit, and slice avocado into thick pieces. Arrange in a bowl.

### 2. 调红油酱 | Mix Chili Oil Sauce
小碗中混合红油、酱油、米醋和蒜泥搅匀。

Combine chili oil, soy sauce, rice vinegar, and garlic in a small bowl. Stir well.

### 3. 浇酱上桌 | Drizzle & Serve
将红油酱浇在牛油果上，撒芝麻和葱花。配白米饭或直接用勺子挖着吃。

Pour the chili oil sauce over avocado, scatter sesame seeds and scallion. Eat over rice or straight from the bowl with a spoon.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么红油+牛油果是隐藏绝配 / Why this works:**
> 牛油果含有约15%的单不饱和脂肪酸，这些脂肪是辣椒素（capsaicin）的完美溶剂。辣椒素是脂溶性的——当它溶解在牛油果的脂肪中时，辣味被均匀分散并柔化，从"尖锐刺痛"变成"温暖绵长"。同时牛油果的奶油质感包裹住红油的香料颗粒，创造出一种"辣味奶油"的全新口感——这在自然界中不存在的质地，正是这道菜让人上瘾的原因。
>
> *Avocado's ~15% monounsaturated fat is the perfect solvent for capsaicin (which is fat-soluble). Dissolved in avocado fat, the heat transforms from "sharp sting" to "warm glow." The creamy texture also encapsulates chili oil's spice particles, creating a "spicy cream" mouthfeel that doesn't exist in nature — and that novel texture is exactly why this dish is addictive.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 牛油果一定要熟透，按压微软才行 | Avocado must be perfectly ripe — soft when gently squeezed |
| 红油要选带渣的（有辣椒碎和花生碎） | Use chili oil WITH sediment (chili flakes and peanut bits) |
| 加一个溏心蛋就是完美一餐 | Add a soft-boiled egg and it becomes a complete meal |
| 怕辣就少放红油，多加点醋 | If spice-shy, use less oil and add more vinegar |
