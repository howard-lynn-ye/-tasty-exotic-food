# 黑蒜蜂蜜烤排骨 | Black Garlic Honey Ribs

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 腌制 2小时 + 烹饪 90分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、烤箱、周末大菜、派对

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 黑蒜经过30天发酵，蒜辣素转化为S-烯丙基半胱氨酸，味道变得像蜜枣+巧克力+陈醋的复合体。把这种"发酵魔法"涂在排骨上再用蜂蜜焦糖化，出来的风味深邃到让人怀疑你暗中念了咒语。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Black garlic ferments for 30 days, transforming allicin into S-allyl cysteine — its flavor becomes a complex of dates + chocolate + aged vinegar. Smear this "fermentation magic" on ribs and caramelize with honey, and the resulting depth makes people suspect you used sorcery.*

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做黑蒜酱腌制 | Make Glaze & Marinate
黑蒜压成泥，混合蜂蜜、酱油、苹果醋、黑胡椒和五香粉搅匀。均匀涂抹在排骨上，冷藏腌制至少2小时（过夜最佳）。

Mash black garlic into a paste. Mix with honey, soy sauce, vinegar, pepper, and five-spice. Coat ribs evenly, refrigerate at least 2 hours (overnight is best).

### 2. 低温慢烤 | Slow Roast
排骨肉面朝上放在烤盘上，盖锡纸。150°C (300°F) 慢烤75分钟至肉质酥嫩。

Place ribs meat-side up on a baking sheet, cover tightly with foil. Slow-roast at 150°C (300°F) for 75 minutes until tender.

### 3. 刷酱高温烤 | Glaze & Broil
揭开锡纸，刷一层厚厚的腌制酱。Broil 高温烤5-8分钟至表面焦糖化起泡。

Remove foil, brush on a thick layer of marinade. Broil on high 5–8 minutes until glaze caramelizes and bubbles.

### 4. 切段上桌 | Slice & Serve
静置5分钟，切段摆盘，撒芝麻或葱花。

Rest 5 minutes, cut between bones, plate, and garnish with sesame seeds or scallion.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么黑蒜是风味炸弹 / Why this works:**
> 黑蒜在60-70°C下发酵30天，发生缓慢的美拉德反应。蒜氨酸分解产生S-烯丙基半胱氨酸（无辛辣），同时还原糖与氨基酸反应产生类黑精（melanoidins）——这些分子的风味谱跨越巧克力、黑醋、蜜枣和太妃糖。蜂蜜在Broil高温下再次焦糖化，叠加黑蒜已有的美拉德产物，形成"双重焦糖化"效应。排骨的胶原蛋白在慢烤中转化为明胶，创造出"肉化了但依然挂骨"的完美口感。
>
> *Black garlic ferments at 60-70°C for 30 days — a slow Maillard reaction. Alliin breaks into S-allyl cysteine (no pungency), while reducing sugars react with amino acids to form melanoidins — molecules spanning chocolate, black vinegar, date, and toffee flavors. Honey caramelizes again under the broiler, stacking on top of black garlic's existing Maillard products for a "double caramelization" effect. Collagen in the ribs converts to gelatin during slow roasting, creating a "fall-apart but still on the bone" texture.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 黑蒜是软的像果酱，直接用叉子压泥 | Black garlic is soft like jam — just mash with a fork |
| 低温慢烤+高温收尾是排骨的"双段式"秘诀 | Slow-then-broil is the "two-phase" rib secret |
| 锡纸要包紧，蒸汽让肉更嫩 | Seal foil tightly — steam tenderizes the meat |
| Broil 最后几分钟不要走开，蜂蜜烧焦很快 | Don't leave during the broil — honey burns in seconds |
