# 抹茶白味噌汤 | Matcha Miso Soup
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/fusion/抹茶白味噌汤.svg" width="500" alt="抹茶白味噌汤"></p>

> ⏱ 15分钟 | 💰~$5/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、融合菜、汤品、素食

> **🤖 AI 原创** — 日式味噌汤的温暖底色上，抹茶带来一抹翠绿的禅意清苦，宛如一碗可以喝的枯山水。
> **🤖 AI Original** — *A warm miso base crowned with a swirl of matcha — a drinkable zen garden in a bowl.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 白味噌 | White (shiro) miso paste | 45g / 3 tbsp |
| 抹茶粉 | Matcha powder | 3g / 1 tsp |
| 嫩豆腐 | Silken tofu | 150g / 5 oz |
| 干裙带菜 | Dried wakame seaweed | 5g / 2 tbsp |
| 昆布高汤 | Kombu dashi stock | 600ml / 2.5 cups |
| 葱花 | Scallion, sliced | 15g / 2 stalks |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮高汤 | Prepare Broth
昆布高汤加热至微沸，放入裙带菜和豆腐丁煮2分钟。
Heat kombu dashi to a gentle simmer, add wakame and diced tofu, cook 2 minutes.

### 2. 调味噌 | Dissolve Miso
取少量热汤将白味噌化开，离火后倒回锅中搅匀，避免沸腾以保留益生菌。
Dissolve miso in a small ladle of hot broth, remove pot from heat, stir back in — never boil to preserve probiotics.

### 3. 点抹茶 | Swirl Matcha
抹茶粉用少许温水打成糊，轻轻划入碗中形成翠绿旋涡，撒葱花即成。
Whisk matcha with a splash of warm water into a paste, swirl gently into each bowl, top with scallions.

---

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science
> 白味噌中的谷氨酸与抹茶的茶氨酸共同作用，产生温和的鲜味与回甘。 *Glutamates in white miso pair with matcha's L-theanine to create a gentle umami with a lingering sweet finish.*
