# 泡菜芝士烤饭团 Kimchi Cheese Grilled Onigiri

> 韩国 x 日本 x 西方 | 主食/小食
> Korea x Japan x Western | Main / Snack

---

## 简介 Introduction

日本烤饭团（焼きおにぎり）外壳焦脆、内部松软，是日本居酒屋的经典。韩国
泡菜带来爆炸性的酸辣发酵风味，而芝士则充当了两者之间的"调停者"——融化后
将泡菜的酸辣和米饭的清甜完美包裹。三国元素在炭火上相遇，每一口都是
文化碰撞的美味火花。

Japanese yaki onigiri — crispy shell, fluffy interior — is an izakaya classic.
Korean kimchi adds explosive fermented tang, while cheese acts as mediator,
wrapping kimchi's punch and rice's sweetness in melted richness. Three
cultures meet over charcoal; every bite sparks a delicious cultural collision.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 日本短粒米（寿司米）Japanese short-grain rice | 400g（熟饭）|
| 韩国泡菜（切碎）Chopped kimchi | 100g |
| 马苏里拉芝士 Mozzarella | 80g |
| 酱油 Soy sauce | 20ml |
| 味醂 Mirin | 10ml |
| 芝麻油 Sesame oil | 15ml |
| 白芝麻 White sesame | 10g |
| 海苔碎 Crushed nori | 适量 / to taste |
| 葱花 Chopped scallion | 15g |
| 韩式辣酱（可选）Gochujang (optional) | 10g |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **备饭 Prep rice**: 热米饭中拌入切碎的泡菜（挤去多余汁水）和芝麻。
   Mix chopped kimchi (squeezed dry) and sesame into hot rice.

2. **包芝士 Stuff cheese**: 手沾盐水，取一团饭在掌心压平，包入马苏里拉块，
   捏成三角形。Wet hands with salt water, flatten rice, stuff mozzarella cube
   inside, shape into a triangle.

3. **做酱汁 Make glaze**: 酱油、味醂和芝麻油混合成刷酱。
   Mix soy sauce, mirin, and sesame oil into a basting sauce.

4. **煎烤 Grill**: 平底锅（或烤网）中火预热，不放油，将饭团每面煎4-5分钟。
   Preheat a dry skillet or grill on medium, cook each side 4-5 min.

5. **刷酱 Baste**: 煎至一面微焦后刷酱汁，翻面继续煎，反复刷酱2-3次。
   When one side is lightly charred, brush glaze, flip, repeat 2-3 times.

6. **焦脆 Crisp up**: 最后大火每面再煎30秒，让酱油焦糖化形成脆壳。
   Finish on high heat 30 sec per side to caramelize soy into a crispy crust.

7. **装盘 Serve**: 撒海苔碎和葱花，可搭配韩式辣酱蘸食。
   Top with nori and scallion. Serve with gochujang dip on the side.
