White miso's koji amino acids and maple syrup's sucrose co-participate in Maillard reactions at 200°C, producing a "umami-sweet caramel" flavor that exists only in this dish. Bacon's smokiness + miso's fermentation depth + maple's woody sweetness — three dimensions of complexity meeting on one thin strip of meat.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 厚切培根 | Thick-cut bacon | 8片 / 8 slices |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1/4茶匙 / 1/4 tsp |
| 辣椒粉（可选） | Cayenne pepper (optional) | 1小撮 / a tiny pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调釉 | Make the Glaze
碗中混合味噌和枫糖浆，搅拌至完全融合均匀。

Whisk miso and maple syrup in a bowl until completely smooth.

### 2. 涂抹 | Coat the Bacon
培根铺在烤网上（下面放烤盘接油），每片正面刷一层味噌枫糖釉，撒黑胡椒。

Arrange bacon on a wire rack over a sheet pan. Brush the top of each slice with the miso-maple glaze. Sprinkle black pepper.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
200°C (400°F) 烤15分钟。取出翻面再刷一层釉，继续烤3-5分钟至深棕色酥脆。

Bake at 200°C (400°F) for 15 min. Flip, brush again, bake 3–5 more min until deep brown and crispy.

### 4. 冷却 | Cool
出炉后放2分钟让釉料凝固变脆。直接用手拿着吃。

Cool 2 min for the glaze to set and crisp up. Eat with your hands.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **味噌为什么能让培根升维 / Why miso elevates bacon to another dimension:**
> - 味噌由米曲霉（Aspergillus oryzae）发酵，产生蛋白酶分解大豆蛋白为游离氨基酸
> - 这些氨基酸在高温下与枫糖的还原糖发生美拉德反应，产生独特的焦糖-鲜味化合物
> - 培根的烟熏酚类化合物（愈创木酚、4-甲基愈创木酚）与味噌发酵产物相辅相成
> - 枫糖浆含有quebecol（魁北克醇），一种独特的多酚，带来木质-香草-焦糖的复合甜感
>
> *Miso is fermented by Aspergillus oryzae, producing proteases that break soy protein into free amino acids. These amino acids undergo Maillard reactions with maple's reducing sugars at high heat, creating unique caramel-umami compounds. Bacon's smoke phenols (guaiacol, 4-methylguaiacol) complement miso's fermentation products. Maple syrup contains quebecol, a unique polyphenol with woody-vanilla-caramel complexity.*
