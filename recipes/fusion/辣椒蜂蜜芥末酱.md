> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 美式蜂蜜芥末酱的甜酸基底，注入一剂中式辣椒油的火辣灵魂。芥末的辛辣是"鼻腔冲击"（异硫氰酸烯丙酯），辣椒的辣是"口腔灼烧"（辣椒素），蜂蜜的甜是"安慰剂"——三条完全不同的感官通道同时开火，大脑直接过载释放内啡肽。这瓶酱可以蘸一切：鸡块、薯条、沙拉、三明治。
>
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *American honey mustard's sweet-tangy base, injected with Chinese chili oil's fiery soul. Mustard's heat is "nasal impact" (allyl isothiocyanate), chili's heat is "oral burn" (capsaicin), honey's sweet is "the soother" — three completely different sensory channels firing simultaneously, causing the brain to release endorphins. This sauce dips everything: nuggets, fries, salads, sandwiches.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 第戎芥末酱 | Dijon mustard | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 辣椒油 | Chili oil (or LGM) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 辣椒粉 | Chili flakes | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜泥 | Minced garlic | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 / a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 混合 | Mix
所有材料放进碗里，搅拌均匀。就这么简单。

Put everything in a bowl. Stir until combined. That's it.

### 2. 试味调整 | Taste & Adjust
太辣加蜂蜜，太甜加芥末，太淡加盐。调到你喜欢的平衡点。

Too spicy? Add honey. Too sweet? Add mustard. Too bland? Add salt. Adjust to your preferred balance.

### 3. 装瓶冷藏 | Bottle & Refrigerate
装入密封瓶或罐中，冷藏可保存两周。

Transfer to a sealed bottle or jar. Refrigerates for up to 2 weeks.

### 4. 使用方式 | How to Use
蘸鸡块、淋沙拉、抹三明治、拌面条、刷烤肉——万能。

Dip nuggets, dress salads, spread on sandwiches, toss with noodles, glaze grilled meats — universal.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 用第戎芥末（Dijon），不是美式黄芥末，风味差很多 | Use Dijon mustard, NOT yellow mustard — huge flavor difference |
| 辣椒油用带渣的更香（如老干妈） | Chili oil with sediment (like LGM) is more flavorful |
| 放一天后味道更融合 | Tastes better after resting one day |
| 可以加一点芝麻油增加坚果香 | Add a drop of sesame oil for nuttiness |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **三种"辣"的不同机制 / Three types of "heat" and their mechanisms:**
> - 芥末辣：异硫氰酸烯丙酯刺激鼻腔三叉神经（往上冲）
> - 辣椒辣：辣椒素激活口腔TRPV1受体（在嘴里烧）
> - 花椒麻：花椒素激活触觉神经（嘴唇振动）
> - 蜂蜜甜：糖分激活甜味受体，大脑释放多巴胺来"安慰"
>
> *Mustard heat: allyl isothiocyanate stimulates nasal trigeminal nerve (goes up). Chili heat: capsaicin activates oral TRPV1 receptors (burns in mouth). Sichuan numbing: sanshool activates tactile nerves (lips vibrate). Honey sweet: sugars activate sweet receptors, brain releases dopamine to "soothe."*
