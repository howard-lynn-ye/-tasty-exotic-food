# 花椒巧克力熔岩蛋糕 | Sichuan Pepper Chocolate Lava Cake

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 12分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 🤖AI原创、烤箱、甜品、派对惊艳

> **🤖 AI 原创菜谱** — 花椒的"麻"不是味觉而是触觉振动，它让舌头对巧克力的苦甜变得格外敏感。熔岩蛋糕切开的瞬间，流心巧克力裹着花椒的酥麻涌出，这是一道让味蕾"过电"的甜品。
> **🤖 AI Original Recipe** — *Sichuan peppercorn's "numbing" is actually a tactile vibration that heightens sensitivity to chocolate's bittersweet notes. When you cut into this lava cake and the molten center surges out carrying that electric tingle, your taste buds experience a full sensory overload.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 黑巧克力 | Dark chocolate (70%) | 100g / 3.5 oz |
| 黄油 | Unsalted butter | 60g / 4 tbsp |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 糖 | Granulated sugar | 40g / 3 tbsp |
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 20g / 1.5 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Ground Sichuan peppercorn | 3/4茶匙 / 3/4 tsp |
| 盐 | Sea salt | 1小撮 / 1 pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 融化巧克力 | Melt Chocolate
黄油和巧克力隔水融化或微波30秒×3次搅拌至顺滑。加入花椒粉搅匀。

Melt butter and chocolate over a double boiler or microwave in 30-second bursts. Stir in Sichuan peppercorn powder.

### 2. 混合面糊 | Make Batter
另一碗打散鸡蛋和糖至颜色变浅。倒入巧克力混合物搅匀，筛入面粉和盐轻轻拌匀。

Whisk eggs and sugar until pale. Fold in the chocolate mixture, then sift in flour and salt. Gently combine.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
烤箱预热220°C (425°F)。面糊倒入抹油的烤杯（7分满），烤10-12分钟至边缘定型但中心微晃。

Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Pour batter into greased ramekins (70% full). Bake 10–12 min until edges set but center jiggles.

### 4. 脱模上桌 | Unmold & Serve
静置1分钟，倒扣在盘上。可撒少许花椒粉和海盐片，立即享用。

Rest 1 minute, invert onto plates. Dust with extra peppercorn and flaky salt. Serve immediately.

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **为什么花椒×巧克力有效 / Why this works:**
> 花椒素（sanshool）以50Hz频率刺激触觉神经，产生"电流感"。这种振动打开了味蕾对苦味和甜味的感知通道，让70%黑巧的可可脂风味层次被极度放大。同时花椒的柑橘类萜烯与可可的挥发性芳香化合物属于同一风味家族，在分子层面本就是天生一对。
>
> *Sanshool vibrates tactile nerves at ~50Hz, creating an "electric current" sensation that opens perception channels for both bitter and sweet — amplifying the layered flavors of 70% dark chocolate. Sichuan peppercorn's citrusy terpenes and cacao's volatile aromatics belong to the same flavor family, making them molecular soulmates.*

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 花椒粉要现磨，预磨粉风味只有1/3 | Freshly ground peppercorn has 3x more flavor than pre-ground |
| 宁可少烤1分钟也不要多烤——流心才是灵魂 | Better to underbake by 1 min — the molten center IS the dish |
| 提前把面糊装杯冷藏，客人来了直接烤 | Prep ramekins in advance, refrigerate, bake when guests arrive |
