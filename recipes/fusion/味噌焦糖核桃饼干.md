# 味噌焦糖核桃饼干 Miso Caramel Walnut Cookie

> 日本 x 美国 | 甜点
> Japan x USA | Dessert

---

## 简介 Introduction

美式巧克力曲奇的地位无可撼动，但加入日本白味噌后，一切都变了。味噌为黄油
饼干面团注入了一股深沉的咸鲜底味（umami），让焦糖的甜变得更加立体，核桃的
苦涩也被柔化。这款饼干外缘酥脆、中心半熟（chewy），是经典美式烘焙与日式
发酵智慧的完美联姻。

The American cookie is untouchable — until you add Japanese white miso.
Miso injects a deep umami undertone into the butter dough, making caramel
sweetness more dimensional and softening walnut bitterness. Crispy edges,
chewy center — a perfect marriage of American baking and Japanese fermentation.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 无盐黄油 Unsalted butter | 115g |
| 白味噌 White miso | 35g |
| 红糖 Brown sugar | 100g |
| 白砂糖 Granulated sugar | 50g |
| 鸡蛋 Egg | 1个 / 1 |
| 香草精 Vanilla extract | 5ml |
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 200g |
| 小苏打 Baking soda | 3g |
| 核桃碎 Chopped walnuts | 80g |
| 焦糖块（或太妃糖碎）Caramel chunks | 60g |
| 海盐片 Flaky sea salt | 适量 / for topping |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **化黄油 Brown butter**: 黄油中火加热至起泡变褐色出坚果香，离火冷却5分钟。
   Cook butter on medium until foamy, brown, and nutty. Cool 5 min off heat.

2. **混合湿料 Mix wet**: 褐化黄油中加入味噌、红糖、白糖搅打均匀，加蛋和香草精。
   Beat browned butter with miso, brown sugar, and white sugar. Add egg and vanilla.

3. **混合干料 Mix dry**: 面粉和小苏打过筛，倒入湿料中用刮刀翻拌至刚好成团。
   Sift flour and baking soda, fold into wet mixture until just combined.

4. **加料 Fold in**: 加入核桃碎和焦糖块，轻轻拌匀。
   Fold in walnut pieces and caramel chunks gently.

5. **冷藏 Chill**: 面团用保鲜膜包好，冷藏至少1小时（过夜更佳）。
   Wrap dough in plastic, chill at least 1 hour (overnight is better).

6. **成型 Shape**: 取出挖成约50g的球状，放在铺了烘焙纸的烤盘上，间距8cm。
   Scoop ~50g balls onto parchment-lined sheet, spaced 8cm apart.

7. **烘烤 Bake**: 180°C烤11-13分钟，边缘金黄但中心仍微软即可。
   Bake at 180°C for 11-13 min — edges golden but center still slightly soft.

8. **点缀 Finish**: 出炉立即撒海盐片，在烤盘上冷却10分钟后转移。
   Sprinkle flaky salt immediately. Cool on sheet 10 min before transferring.
