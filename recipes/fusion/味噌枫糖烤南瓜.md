# 味噌枫糖烤南瓜 | Miso Maple Pumpkin

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 融合创意、AI原创、素食、秋冬

> 白味噌的深邃鲜味与枫糖的温暖甜香在烤南瓜上合奏一曲秋日之歌。味噌在高温下进行美拉德反应，形成一层焦香釉面，让原本朴素的烤南瓜瞬间升华为餐厅级配菜。
>
> *White miso's deep umami and maple's warm sweetness play an autumn symphony on roasted pumpkin. Miso undergoes the Maillard reaction at high heat, forming a caramelized glaze that elevates humble roasted squash into a restaurant-quality side dish.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 南瓜 (贝贝南瓜或栗子南瓜) | Kabocha or butternut squash | 1个，约800g / 1, ~800g |
| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 2瓣，磨泥 / 2 cloves, grated |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 南瓜籽 (烤过) | Toasted pumpkin seeds | 装饰用 / for garnish |
| 香菜或薄荷 | Cilantro or mint | 装饰用 / for garnish |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切南瓜 | Prep Squash
南瓜去籽切成2cm厚的月牙形。如果用栗子南瓜皮可以吃，不用去皮。

Halve, seed, and cut pumpkin into 2cm-thick wedges. Kabocha skin is edible — no need to peel.

### 2. 调酱 | Make Glaze
味噌、枫糖、橄榄油、米醋和蒜泥搅拌至顺滑。

Whisk miso, maple syrup, olive oil, rice vinegar, and garlic until smooth.

### 3. 烤制 | Roast
预热200°C。南瓜块刷上一半酱料，切面朝下放在烤盘上，烤20分钟。翻面再刷剩余酱料，烤10分钟至焦黄软透。

Preheat 200°C/400°F. Brush pumpkin with half the glaze, place cut-side down on tray. Roast 20 min. Flip, brush with remaining glaze, roast 10 min more until caramelized and tender.

### 4. 装盘 | Plate
摆盘，撒烤南瓜籽和香菜叶，淋一点好的橄榄油。

Arrange on platter, scatter toasted pumpkin seeds and herb leaves, finish with a drizzle of good olive oil.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 贝贝南瓜皮可以吃而且好吃，别削 | Kabocha skin is delicious — don't peel it |
| 味噌容易焦，温度别超过210°C | Miso burns easily — keep oven below 210°C/410°F |
| 枫糖用纯的，不要用枫糖风味糖浆 | Use pure maple syrup, not maple-flavored syrup |
| 这道菜冷吃也很好，可以放在沙拉里 | Great cold too — toss into salads |
