Soy sauce's glutamate amplifies the egg's protein flavor, transforming French toast from simply "sweet" to "sweet-savory intertwined." When maple syrup hits, its caramel notes merge with soy's fermented depth, creating a "premium toffee" flavor. Once you tell your roommate this secret, your breakfast will never be solo again.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 厚切吐司 | Thick-cut bread | 4片 / 4 slices |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 / 2 |
| 牛奶 | Milk | ½杯 / ½ cup |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2茶匙 / 2 tsp |
| 枫糖浆 | Maple syrup | 适量 / for topping |
| 黄油 | Butter | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 肉桂粉（可选） | Cinnamon (optional) | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | ½茶匙 / ½ tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调蛋液 | Mix Batter
碗中打散鸡蛋，加入牛奶、酱油、香草精和肉桂粉搅匀。

Whisk eggs, milk, soy sauce, vanilla, and cinnamon in a shallow bowl.

### 2. 浸泡 | Soak
每片吐司在蛋液中浸泡15秒翻面再泡15秒。不要泡太久，否则太软。

Soak each bread slice in the batter for 15 seconds per side. Don't over-soak — it gets mushy.

### 3. 煎 | Pan-Fry
平底锅中火融化黄油，放入吐司煎2-3分钟至底部金黄，翻面再煎2-3分钟。

Melt butter in a skillet over medium heat. Cook toast 2–3 minutes until golden, flip, cook 2–3 more minutes.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
叠盘，淋枫糖浆，可搭配新鲜水果和一小块黄油。

Stack on plate, drizzle maple syrup. Serve with fresh fruit and a pat of butter.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 酱油只要2茶匙！多了吃起来像在吃寿司 | Only 2 tsp soy sauce! More and it tastes like sushi |
| 用厚切吐司或隔夜面包，更能吸收且不会烂 | Use thick-cut or day-old bread — absorbs better, holds shape |
| 中火煎，小火太慢大火太焦 | Medium heat — low is too slow, high burns |
| 先不告诉吃的人有酱油，让他们猜秘密调料 | Don't reveal the soy sauce — let them guess the secret |

## 风味科学 | Flavor Science

> **酱油在甜食中的"隐身术" / Soy sauce's "invisibility" in sweets:**
> - 少量酱油（<1%总量）不会产生"酱油味"
> - 但其中的谷氨酸钠会显著放大甜味和鸡蛋的浓郁感
> - 这和巧克力加盐、焦糖加海盐是同一个原理
> - 枫糖浆中的"枫呋喃酮"和酱油中的"酱油多肽"形成风味协同
>
> *A small amount of soy sauce (<1% of total) produces no "soy sauce flavor" but its sodium glutamate significantly amplifies sweetness and egg richness. Same principle as adding salt to chocolate or sea salt to caramel. Maple furanone and soy sauce peptides create flavor synergy.*
