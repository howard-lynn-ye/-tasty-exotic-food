# 干煸四季豆 | Dry-Fried Green Beans
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/vegetarian/干煸四季豆.svg" width="500" alt="干煸四季豆"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 素菜、下饭、川味、餐馆经典

> 干煸四季豆是中餐馆点击率最高的素菜之一。四季豆在热油中煸到表皮起皱微焦，加上碎肉末（或不加）、芽菜和干辣椒的爆香，咸鲜中带着微辣，下饭能力一流。
>
> *Dry-fried green beans are the single most popular vegetable dish at Chinese restaurants. Blistered and wrinkled in a hot wok, then tossed with savory bits and a touch of chili heat — they're impossibly addictive over rice. This version is fully vegetarian.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 四季豆 | Green beans | 400g / 14 oz |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 姜末 | Minced ginger | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 干辣椒 | Dried red chilies | 4-5个 / 4-5 |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1/2茶匙 / 1/2 tsp |
| 芝麻油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 3汤匙 / 3 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 准备四季豆 | Prep the Beans
四季豆摘去两头和老筋，掰成5厘米长段。洗净后彻底擦干——水分会影响煸炒效果。

Trim both ends and pull off any strings from the green beans. Snap into 2-inch pieces. Wash and dry thoroughly — moisture prevents proper blistering.

### 2. 煸炒四季豆 | Blister the Beans
锅中放较多油（3汤匙），中大火放入四季豆，不要频繁翻动，让四季豆表皮起皱变焦，约5-6分钟。煸好后盛出。

Add oil to a wok over medium-high heat. Add green beans and resist the urge to constantly stir — let them sit and blister, turning occasionally, for 5-6 minutes until wrinkled and charred in spots. Remove and set aside.

### 3. 爆香调味 | Aromatics & Finish
锅中留少许底油，小火爆香蒜末、姜末和干辣椒。倒回四季豆，加酱油和糖，大火翻炒均匀。关火淋芝麻油，拌匀出锅。

Leave a little oil in the wok. Over low heat, fry garlic, ginger, and dried chilies until fragrant. Return the beans, add soy sauce and sugar, and toss on high heat until well combined. Turn off heat, drizzle with sesame oil, toss, and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 四季豆一定要彻底擦干 | Beans must be completely dry for proper blistering |
| 煸炒时不要频繁翻动 | Don't stir too often — let them char |
| 四季豆必须煸熟，生的有毒素 | Beans MUST be fully cooked — raw beans contain toxins |
| 想要更香可以加猪肉末 | Add ground pork for a non-vegetarian version |
| 没有芽菜用榨菜碎代替也好吃 | Chopped pickled mustard (zha cai) adds great umami |
