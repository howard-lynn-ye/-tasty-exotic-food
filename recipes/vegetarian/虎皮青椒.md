# 虎皮青椒 | Tiger Skin Peppers
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/vegetarian/虎皮青椒.svg" width="500" alt="虎皮青椒"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 8分钟 | 💰 ~$1.50/份 | 🏷️ 素菜、下饭、微辣、超简单

> 虎皮青椒因青椒煎到表皮起皱像虎皮花纹而得名。这道菜极其简单：青椒煎到焦香，加酱油、醋、糖一炒，就是一道让人停不下来的下饭神菜。没有任何技术难度，新手必学。
>
> *Tiger Skin Peppers get their name from the wrinkled, blistered skin that resembles tiger stripes. Dead simple: blister peppers in a dry wok, hit them with soy sauce, vinegar, and sugar, and you have one of the most addictive rice-companion dishes in Chinese cooking. Zero skill required.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 青椒（薄皮尖椒） | Green peppers (thin-skinned) | 6-8个 / 6-8 |
| 蒜末 | Minced garlic | 3瓣 / 3 cloves |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 香醋 | Chinkiang vinegar (black vinegar) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 处理青椒 | Prep the Peppers
青椒洗净擦干，不要去蒂（方便翻面），用刀在每个青椒上划一刀让它更容易入味。

Wash and dry the peppers thoroughly. Keep the stems on (they make flipping easier). Score each pepper with one slit so the sauce can penetrate.

### 2. 煎出虎皮 | Blister the Peppers
锅烧热，先不放油（或极少油），放入青椒，用铲子按压使其贴紧锅面，中火煎至表皮起皱焦黄，翻面继续煎。两面都起虎皮纹后盛出。

Heat the wok with no oil (or just a thin film). Add peppers and press them flat against the surface with a spatula. Pan-fry over medium heat until the skin blisters and chars. Flip and repeat. Remove when both sides have tiger stripes.

### 3. 调味出锅 | Sauce & Serve
锅中加少许油，爆香蒜末，放回青椒，加入酱油、醋、糖和盐，大火翻炒30秒让酱汁裹匀。立即出锅。

Add a little oil to the wok, fry garlic until fragrant. Return peppers, add soy sauce, vinegar, sugar, and salt. Toss on high heat for 30 seconds until evenly coated. Serve immediately.

## 要点 | Tips

| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 煎的时候少油或不放油，靠干煎起皱 | Use minimal or no oil for blistering — dry heat is the key |
| 用铲子按压青椒，才能均匀起虎皮 | Press peppers flat for even charring |
| 选薄皮青椒，厚皮的不容易煎透 | Choose thin-skinned peppers — thick ones won't blister well |
| 怕辣可以去籽，不怕辣保留 | Remove seeds to reduce heat, keep them for more spice |
| 这道菜凉了也好吃 | Tastes great at room temperature too |
