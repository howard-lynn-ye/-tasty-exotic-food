# 犹太逾越节面包 | Jewish Passover Matzo (古以色列, ~1500 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 5分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、犹太、逾越节、无酵饼、圣经

> **📜 历史** — 逾越节无酵饼（Matzo）是人类历史上最古老的宗教食物之一。《出埃及记》记载以色列人逃离埃及时来不及等面团发酵，只能带着未发酵的扁饼上路。从此无酵饼成为逾越节（Passover）的核心食物，纪念从奴役到自由的出走。制作规定极为严格：从面粉接触水到烤制完成不得超过18分钟，以确保面团不会开始发酵。
> **📜 History** — *Passover Matzo is one of the oldest religious foods in human history. The Book of Exodus recounts that the Israelites, fleeing Egypt, had no time to let their dough rise and carried only unleavened flatbread. Since then, matzo has been the central food of Passover, commemorating the exodus from slavery to freedom. The rules of production are strict: from the moment flour touches water to the finished bake must not exceed 18 minutes, ensuring the dough never begins to ferment.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 冷水 | Cold water | 1/3杯 | 1/3 cup |
| 盐（可选） | Salt (optional) | 一小撮 | a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **快速和面** — 面粉倒在台面上堆成火山形，中间挖洞倒入冷水，快速揉成团。注意：传统上从加水开始计时，必须在18分钟内完成全部工序。
   *Mound flour on a work surface in a volcano shape. Pour cold water into the center and knead rapidly into a dough. Note: traditionally, timing starts when water touches flour — the entire process must be completed within 18 minutes.*

2. **擀制刺孔** — 面团分成4份，每份擀成极薄的饼（约2mm厚）。用叉子在表面密密刺孔，防止烤时鼓起。
   *Divide into 4 portions and roll each paper-thin (about 2mm). Prick the surface densely with a fork to prevent puffing during baking.*

3. **高温速烤** — 烤箱预热至最高温（500°F/260°C），将薄饼放在预热的烤盘上烤3-5分钟，至出现褐色斑点且变硬变脆。
   *Preheat oven to maximum (500°F/260°C). Place the thin rounds on a preheated baking sheet and bake 3-5 minutes until brown spots appear and the bread is hard and crisp.*
