# 东汉酱鸡 | Eastern Han Soy-Braised Chicken (东汉, ~100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 35分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、东汉、酱、鸡肉、炖煮

> **📜 历史** — 东汉时期（25-220）酱的制作工艺日趋成熟，豆酱（"酱"的前身，酱油尚未出现）成为最重要的调味品。《四民月令》记载了东汉士大夫家庭制酱的详细时令和工序。以酱汁炖煮鸡肉是当时中上层家庭的日常菜肴，鸡在汉代被视为"五畜"之一，饲养普遍。酱鸡的做法奠定了中国"红烧"烹饪体系的原型。
> **📜 History** — *During the Eastern Han (25-220 AD), fermented bean paste ("jiang") production matured into a refined craft, though soy sauce had not yet been invented. The "Monthly Ordinances for the Four Classes" documented detailed seasonal schedules for making jiang in gentry households. Braising chicken in bean paste was a common dish for middle- and upper-class families. Chickens were one of the "Five Domestic Animals" and widely raised. This soy-braised technique established the prototype for China's entire "red-braising" culinary tradition.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿（带骨） | Chicken legs (bone-in) | 4只 | 4 pcs |
| 豆瓣酱或黄豆酱 | Doubanjiang or soybean paste | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 生姜（切片） | Ginger (sliced) | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 大葱（切段） | Scallion (cut into sections) | 2根 | 2 stalks |
| 米酒 | Rice wine | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎鸡上色** — 鸡腿洗净沥干，锅中少许油中火煎至两面金黄约5分钟，取出备用。
   *Pat chicken legs dry. Pan-fry in a little oil over medium heat until golden on both sides, about 5 minutes. Remove and set aside.*

2. **酱汁炖煮** — 锅中留底油，爆香姜片和葱段。加入豆瓣酱炒出红油，倒入米酒和1杯水，放回鸡腿，加蜂蜜，大火煮开后转小火炖25分钟。
   *In the same pan, sauté ginger and scallions in the residual oil. Add bean paste and stir until red oil appears. Pour in rice wine and 1 cup water. Return chicken, add honey, bring to a boil, then simmer 25 minutes.*

3. **收汁装盘** — 转大火收汁至浓稠，让酱汁均匀裹在鸡腿上，盛出装盘，浇上锅中余汁。
   *Turn heat to high and reduce the sauce until thick and glossy, coating each chicken leg evenly. Plate and spoon remaining sauce over the top.*
