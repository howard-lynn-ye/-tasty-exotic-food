# 唐代羊肉泡馍 | Tang Dynasty Lamb Bread Soup (唐朝, ~700 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/唐代羊肉泡馍.svg" width="500" alt="唐代羊肉泡馍"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 烹饪 120分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、唐朝、汤品、肉类

> **📜 历史** — 羊肉泡馍是丝绸之路上中原与西域饮食文化交融的结晶。传说唐太宗李世民微服私访时在长安市集品尝了这道平民美食后赞不绝口。唐代长安作为国际大都会，汇聚了波斯、突厥、阿拉伯的饮食元素，羊肉泡馍正是将胡饼（馕）与羊汤结合的代表。宋代以后此菜在西安一带更加发扬光大，至今仍是陕西标志性美食。
> **📜 History** — *Lamb bread soup (yangrou paomo) is a culinary fusion born along the Silk Road, blending Central Plains and Western Regions food culture. Legend says Emperor Taizong of Tang tasted this commoner's dish at a Chang'an market during an incognito visit and praised it highly. As an international metropolis, Tang Dynasty Chang'an absorbed Persian, Turkic, and Arab culinary elements — this dish combines the flatbread (naan) with lamb broth. It flourished further in Xi'an after the Song Dynasty and remains Shaanxi's signature dish today.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 羊腿肉（带骨） | Lamb leg (bone-in) | 500g | 1 lb |
| 死面饼或馕 | Unleavened flatbread or naan | 2个 | 2 pieces |
| 姜 | Ginger | 1块 | 1 piece |
| 大葱 | Green onion | 2根 | 2 stalks |
| 八角 | Star anise | 2颗 | 2 pieces |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 粉丝 | Glass noodles | 1小把 | 1 small handful |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 香菜（切碎） | Cilantro (chopped) | 适量 | to taste |
| 辣椒酱 | Chili sauce | 适量 | to taste |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炖羊肉** — 羊腿冷水下锅焯水去血沫，另起锅加足量清水放入羊腿、姜块、葱段、八角和花椒，大火煮沸转小火慢炖2小时至肉烂。
   *Blanch lamb leg in cold water to remove scum. In a fresh pot, add plenty of water with lamb, ginger, scallion, star anise, and Sichuan peppercorns. Bring to a boil, then simmer on low 2 hours until meat is tender.*

2. **掰馍泡汤** — 将死面饼用手掰成黄豆大小的碎块（越小越好），羊肉捞出切薄片，粉丝泡软。碗中放馍块，浇上滚烫的羊汤，加入粉丝和羊肉片。
   *Break flatbread into pea-sized pieces by hand (the smaller the better). Remove lamb and slice thinly; soak glass noodles until soft. Place bread pieces in a bowl, ladle boiling lamb broth over them, and add noodles and lamb slices.*

3. **调味上桌** — 撒上香菜碎，配辣椒酱和糖蒜，趁热食用。
   *Garnish with chopped cilantro, serve with chili sauce and sweet pickled garlic on the side. Eat while piping hot.*
