# 丝绸之路果脯坚果饭 Silk Road Dried Fruit Nut Rice

> **时代 Era:** 南北朝至隋唐 Northern & Southern Dynasties to Sui-Tang · 约公元400年 (~400 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 丝绸之路沿线 Along the Silk Road (Central Asia to Chang'an)
> **类型 Type:** 主食 Staple / 抓饭 Pilaf
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

丝绸之路不仅是商贸通道，更是饮食文化的融合之路。这道果脯坚果饭融合了中亚抓饭（plov）的烹饪方式与东方的稻米文化。商队在绿洲补给时将当地的杏干、葡萄干和开心果与米饭同煮，既是高热量的行旅食粮，也是丝路驿站的待客之道。

The Silk Road was not just a trade route but a highway of culinary fusion. This dried fruit nut rice blends Central Asian pilaf (plov) technique with Eastern rice culture. Caravans at oasis stops cooked local dried apricots, raisins, and pistachios with rice — a high-calorie travel meal and a gesture of Silk Road hospitality.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 巴斯马蒂或长粒香米 Basmati or long-grain rice | 300g |
| 杏干 Dried apricots (切半 halved) | 60g |
| 葡萄干 Raisins | 40g |
| 开心果仁 Shelled pistachios | 40g |
| 黄油 Butter (或羊油 or lamb fat) | 40g |
| 肉桂棒 Cinnamon stick | 1 根 |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 泡米炒料 Soak and sauté
米洗净浸泡30分钟，沥干。厚底锅中融化黄油，放入肉桂棒、杏干和葡萄干，中火翻炒2分钟至果干微微膨胀出香。
Wash rice and soak 30 minutes; drain. Melt butter in a heavy pot. Add cinnamon stick, apricots, and raisins. Sauté over medium heat 2 minutes until fruit plumps and becomes fragrant.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 焖饭 Cook rice
倒入沥干的米翻炒1分钟使每粒米裹上油脂。加入450ml水和半茶匙盐，大火煮沸后转最小火，加盖严密焖15-18分钟至水完全吸收。
Add drained rice and stir 1 minute to coat each grain with fat. Add 450ml water and ½ tsp salt. Bring to a boil, reduce to lowest heat, cover tightly, and steam 15-18 minutes until water is fully absorbed.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 焖蒸上桌 Rest and serve
关火后不要开盖，继续焖10分钟。开盖取出肉桂棒，用叉子轻轻拨松米饭，撒上开心果仁，翻拌后装盘。
Turn off heat and keep covered for 10 more minutes. Remove lid and cinnamon stick. Fluff rice gently with a fork, scatter pistachios, fold through, and plate.

## 替代建议 Substitutions

| 原料 Original | 替代 Substitute | 说明 Note |
