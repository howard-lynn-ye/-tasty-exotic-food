# 古代高句丽冷面 | Goguryeo Cold Noodles

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 高句丽 Goguryeo Kingdom (~400AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 朝鲜半岛北部 Northern Korean Peninsula
> **类型 Type:** 面食 Noodle Dish

---

## 典故 Historical Background

冷面（냉면）的历史可追溯至高句丽时期，是朝鲜半岛北部山区居民在严冬中发明的独特面食。高句丽地处寒冷的山地，荞麦是主要农作物。农家在冬季将荞麦面条浸入冰凉的萝卜泡菜汤中食用，以寒制寒的饮食理念体现了古代朝鲜人对自然规律的深刻理解。这一传统在后来的高丽与朝鲜王朝中延续发展，最终成为半岛最具代表性的面食之一。

Cold noodles (naengmyeon) trace back to the Goguryeo era, a unique noodle dish invented by residents of the cold northern mountains. Goguryeo occupied frigid highland terrain where buckwheat was the primary crop. Farmers ate buckwheat noodles in icy radish kimchi broth during winter — the philosophy of "countering cold with cold" reflected ancient Koreans' deep understanding of nature. This tradition continued through Goryeo and Joseon, eventually becoming one of the peninsula's most iconic noodle dishes.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 荞麦粉 Buckwheat flour | 2杯 2 cups |
| 淀粉 Starch (potato or sweet potato) | 1/2杯 1/2 cup |
| 牛骨汤 Beef bone broth | 4杯 4 cups |
| 萝卜泡菜汁 Dongchimi (radish kimchi) brine | 1杯 1 cup |

| 朝鲜梨 Korean pear | 1/2个 1/2 |

| 米醋 Rice vinegar | 2大匙 2 tbsp |
| 芥末 Mustard | 适量 To taste |

## 做法 Preparation

1. **煮牛骨汤 Make broth:** 牛骨与牛腱肉冷水下锅，煮沸撇沫，转小火炖煮三至四个时辰至汤色清澈浓郁。Blanch beef bones and shank in cold water, bring to a boil and skim, simmer 3-4 hours until broth is clear and rich.
2. **冷却汤底 Chill broth:** 汤过滤去油，与萝卜泡菜汁混合，加米醋调味，放入地窖或冷处至冰凉，汤面结薄冰为最佳。Strain and defat the broth, mix with dongchimi brine, season with vinegar, chill in a cellar until ice-cold — a thin layer of ice on top is ideal.
3. **制面 Make noodles:** 荞麦粉与淀粉混合，加热水和成面团，放入木制压面器中，直接压入沸水锅中。Mix buckwheat flour and starch, knead with hot water into dough, press through a wooden noodle press directly into boiling water.
4. **煮面 Cook noodles:** 面条在沸水中煮至刚熟有弹性，立即捞入冰水中反复冲洗去黏液，使面条爽滑。Cook noodles in boiling water until just done and springy, immediately rinse in ice water repeatedly to remove starch and make them slippery-smooth.
5. **切配料 Prepare toppings:** 牛腱肉切薄片，鸡蛋对半切，朝鲜梨与黄瓜切细丝。Slice beef shank thin, halve boiled eggs, julienne pear and cucumber.
6. **装碗 Assemble:** 面条盘成整齐的鸟巢形放入铜碗，摆上牛肉片、蛋、梨丝、黄瓜丝，浇入冰冷的汤汁，配芥末与醋碟。Coil noodles into a neat nest in a brass bowl, arrange beef, egg, pear, cucumber on top, ladle in ice-cold broth, serve with mustard and vinegar on the side.

## 备注 Notes

- 古法中冷面在严冬食用，汤碗中浮冰是正宗的标志。In the old tradition, cold noodles were eaten in deep winter — floating ice in the broth is a mark of authenticity.
- 荞麦面条易断，制面需要技巧，压面时力度均匀才能保证面条连续不断。Buckwheat noodles break easily; pressing requires skill — even pressure ensures unbroken strands.
