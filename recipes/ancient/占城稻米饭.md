# 占城稻米饭 Champa Rice

> 约公元1000年 | 占城国（今越南中部）| 改变中国农业的稻米
> Circa 1000 AD | Champa (Central Vietnam) | The Rice That Changed Chinese Agriculture

---

## 简介 Introduction

占城稻原产于东南亚占城国（今越南中南部），北宋真宗年间引入中国，因其早熟、
耐旱的特性极大提高了粮食产量，被视为中国农业史上最重要的引种之一。占城稻
米粒细长，口感偏硬，适合做干饭。本食谱还原最朴素的占城稻米饭吃法。

Champa rice originated in the Champa Kingdom (central Vietnam) and was
introduced to China during the Northern Song dynasty. Its early-maturing,
drought-resistant traits revolutionized Chinese agriculture. The grains are
long, firm, and ideal for steamed dry rice. This recipe recreates the
simplest Champa-style preparation.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 长粒籼米（模拟占城稻）Long-grain indica rice | 400g |
| 清水 Water | 550ml |
| 粗盐 Coarse salt | 2g |
| 猪油（可选）Lard (optional) | 10g |
| 腌菜 Pickled vegetables | 适量 / as side |
| 咸鱼干 Dried salted fish | 一小块 / a small piece |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **淘米 Wash rice**: 将籼米用冷水反复淘洗三遍，去除多余淀粉。
   Rinse indica rice three times in cold water to remove excess starch.

2. **浸泡 Soak**: 加清水浸泡30分钟，让米粒充分吸水。
   Soak in water for 30 minutes to let grains fully absorb moisture.

3. **煮饭 Cook**: 按米水比1:1.4加水，大火煮沸后转最小火焖15分钟。
   Use 1:1.4 rice-to-water ratio. Bring to boil, then simmer 15 min on lowest heat.

4. **焖饭 Steam**: 关火后不开盖，继续焖10分钟让米饭松软。
   Turn off heat, keep lid on, steam for 10 more minutes.

5. **拌油 Mix fat**: 趁热拌入少许猪油和粗盐，增添香气。
   While hot, fold in a touch of lard and coarse salt for aroma.

6. **配菜 Serve**: 搭配腌菜和煎咸鱼干，简朴而满足。
   Serve alongside pickled vegetables and pan-fried salted fish.
