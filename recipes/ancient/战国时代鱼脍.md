# 战国时代鱼脍 Warring States Fish Sashimi

> **时代 Era:** 战国时期 Warring States Period · 约公元前400年 (~400 BC)
> **地区 Region:** 吴越之地 Wu-Yue Region (modern Jiangsu/Zhejiang)
> **类型 Type:** 生食 Raw Dish / 冷菜 Cold Dish
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

鱼脍（生鱼片）并非日本独创，中国食用鱼脍的历史远早于日本。《诗经》《楚辞》中均有"脍"的记载，孔子也说"食不厌精，脍不厌细"。战国时期吴越水乡盛产鲈鱼，当地人以利刃将鲜鱼切成薄如蝉翼的片，蘸芥酱食用，是上层社会的美食。

Fish sashimi (kuai) is not a Japanese invention — China's history of eating raw fish predates Japan's by centuries. The Book of Songs and Chu Ci both mention "kuai." Confucius said "fine food should be finely cut, and sashimi cannot be too thin." In the Warring States era, Wu-Yue was rich in perch; locals sliced fresh fish paper-thin with sharp blades, dipping in mustard sauce — a delicacy of the upper class.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 刺身级鲈鱼或鲷鱼 Sashimi-grade sea bass or sea bream | 300g |
| 芥末 Wasabi or Chinese mustard | 适量 to taste |
| 生姜 Ginger (切细丝 finely julienned) | 20g |
| 紫苏叶 Perilla (shiso) leaves | 6 片 leaves |
| 米醋 Rice vinegar | 1 汤匙 tbsp |
| 盐 Salt | 少许 a pinch |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 备鱼 Prepare fish
确保鱼肉为刺身级新鲜。去皮去骨，用厨房纸吸干表面水分。将鱼肉冷藏至半冻状态（约-2°C），更易切薄片。
Ensure fish is sashimi-grade fresh. Remove skin and bones. Pat dry with paper towels. Chill until semi-frozen (about -2°C) for easier slicing.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 切脍 Slice
用最锋利的刀，以拉切法将鱼肉切成2毫米厚的薄片。每片铺在紫苏叶上，摆盘成花形。
Using your sharpest knife, pull-slice the fish into 2mm-thin slices. Lay each slice on a perilla leaf and arrange on a plate in a floral pattern.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 蘸食 Serve with dip
小碟中放入芥末，另备一碟米醋加姜丝和少许盐作为蘸汁。食用时夹起鱼片蘸芥末或姜醋汁。
Place mustard in a small dish. Prepare another dish with rice vinegar, ginger julienne, and a pinch of salt as dipping sauce. Pick up fish slices and dip as desired.

## 替代建议 Substitutions

| 原料 Original | 替代 Substitute | 说明 Note |
