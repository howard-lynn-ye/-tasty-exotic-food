# 拜占庭皇宫鸡 | Byzantine Imperial Chicken (~600AD)

> ⏱ 准备25分+烹饪60分 | 💰~$14/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、拜占庭帝国

> **📜 历史** — 拜占庭宫廷融合希腊与东方烹饪，皇宫鸡以藏红花和香料彰显帝国的奢华与东西交融。
> **📜 History** — *Byzantine court cuisine blended Greek and Eastern traditions; imperial chicken with saffron and spices epitomized the empire's luxury and cultural fusion.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿 | Chicken thighs | 6个 / 6 pcs |
| 藏红花 | Saffron | 一小撮 / 1 pinch |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 45ml / 3 tbsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | 1个 / 1 |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 肉桂 | Cinnamon | 3g / 0.5 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调料 | Season

藏红花泡温水，与橄榄油、柠檬汁、蜂蜜和肉桂混合，涂抹鸡腿。
Steep saffron in warm water; mix with olive oil, lemon juice, honey, and cinnamon; coat chicken thighs.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast

鸡腿排入烤盘，190°C烤50分钟至表皮金黄。
Arrange thighs in a baking dish, roast at 375°F for 50 minutes until golden.

### 3. 装盘 | Plate

淋上盘底汤汁，配面包或米饭食用。
Drizzle with pan juices, serve with bread or rice.

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 藏红花至少泡10分钟才能充分释放颜色与香气 | Steep saffron at least 10 minutes to fully release its color and aroma |
| 2 | 烤前将鸡腿皮面朝上排列，中途不要翻面 | Arrange thighs skin-side up before roasting and do not flip during cooking |
| 3 | 蜂蜜容易焦糖化，最后15分钟可盖锡纸防止过度上色 | Honey caramelizes easily; tent with foil for the last 15 minutes to prevent over-browning |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

拜占庭帝国地跨欧亚，君士坦丁堡是东西方香料贸易的枢纽，宫廷料理因此得以大量使用藏红花、肉桂、丁香等昂贵香料。拜占庭宫廷宴会(deipnon)讲究以金银器皿盛装，菜肴色彩鲜艳，藏红花不仅提供风味更赋予食物帝王般的金黄色泽。这种甜咸交融、香料丰富的烹饪风格深刻影响了后来的奥斯曼帝国饮食文化。

The Byzantine Empire straddled Europe and Asia, with Constantinople serving as the hub of east-west spice trade, allowing the court kitchen liberal use of costly spices such as saffron, cinnamon, and cloves. Byzantine imperial banquets (deipnon) featured gold and silver tableware and vividly colored dishes; saffron provided not only flavor but also a regal golden hue. This sweet-savory, spice-rich cooking style profoundly influenced the later Ottoman culinary tradition.
