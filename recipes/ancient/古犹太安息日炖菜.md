# 古犹太安息日炖菜 | Jewish Sabbath Cholent (中欧, ~1000 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 12-18小时 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、犹太、炖菜、牛肉

> **📜 历史** — Cholent（读作"绍伦特"）是犹太安息日的标志性菜肴，其历史可追溯至中世纪的阿什肯纳兹犹太社区。因安息日（周五日落至周六日落）禁止生火做饭，犹太家庭在周五下午将所有食材放入锅中，送到面包房利用余温慢炖整夜。这种智慧的烹饪方式既遵守了宗教律法，又确保了安息日的热食。
> **📜 History** — *Cholent is the iconic dish of the Jewish Sabbath, tracing back to medieval Ashkenazi communities around 1000 AD. Since kindling fire on Shabbat (Friday sunset to Saturday sunset) is forbidden, families assembled all ingredients Friday afternoon and sent the pot to the bakery to slow-cook overnight in residual heat. This ingenious method honored religious law while ensuring a hot Sabbath meal.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **分层摆放** — 在大号厚底锅或慢炖锅底部铺洋葱，依次放入牛腩、白芸豆、大麦和土豆。
   *Layer onions at the bottom of a large heavy pot or slow cooker. Add brisket, then beans, barley, and potatoes.*

2. **调味** — 撒上甜椒粉、盐和胡椒，淋上蜂蜜，放入大蒜瓣，加水至没过所有食材约2厘米。
   *Sprinkle paprika, salt, and pepper over the layers. Drizzle honey, tuck in garlic cloves, and add water to cover ingredients by about 2cm.*

3. **慢炖** — 大火煮沸后转最小火，紧紧盖上锅盖，炖12-18小时（或慢炖锅Low档10-12小时）。期间不要打开锅盖。
   *Bring to a boil over high heat, then reduce to the lowest possible setting. Cover tightly and cook 12-18 hours (or slow cooker on Low for 10-12 hours). Do not open the lid.*

4. **装盘** — 食材将融为浓稠的棕色整体，牛肉入口即化。搭配犹太面包（Challah）食用。
   *The ingredients will meld into a thick brown stew with meltingly tender beef. Serve with challah bread.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- Cholent一词可能源自古法语"chaud lent"（慢热），也有学者认为来自希伯来语"she'lan"（已经煮过）。
  *The word "cholent" may derive from Old French "chaud lent" (slow heat), or from Hebrew "she'lan" (already cooked).*

- 中世纪欧洲的犹太社区每周五都会看到家庭主妇排队将Cholent锅送往面包房，这成为社区的标志性景象。
  *In medieval European Jewish communities, the sight of housewives queuing to deliver cholent pots to the bakery every Friday became an iconic community scene.*

- 塞法迪犹太人有类似的菜肴叫Hamin（哈敏），使用鹰嘴豆、米饭和鸡蛋代替大麦和芸豆。
  *Sephardic Jews have a similar dish called Hamin, using chickpeas, rice, and eggs instead of barley and beans.*
