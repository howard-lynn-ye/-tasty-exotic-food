# 中世纪修道院啤酒炖 | Medieval Monastery Ale Stew
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/中世纪修道院啤酒炖.svg" width="500" alt="中世纪修道院啤酒炖"></p>

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 中世纪盛期 High Middle Ages (~1100AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 佛兰德斯/莱茵兰修道院 Flanders / Rhineland Monasteries
> **类型 Type:** 炖菜 Stew

---

## 典故 Historical Background

中世纪欧洲修道院不仅是宗教中心，也是酿酒与烹饪的重镇。本笃会修士遵循"劳动即祈祷"的信条，在修道院中酿造啤酒并以之入菜。啤酒炖肉是修士们在大斋期以外的日常美食，以修道院自酿的黑啤搭配周边农庄的牛肉和根茎蔬菜，在壁炉旁的铁锅中慢炖数个时辰。

Medieval European monasteries were not just religious centers but hubs of brewing and cooking. Benedictine monks followed the creed "laborare est orare" (to work is to pray), brewing ale and cooking with it. Ale stew was the monks' everyday meal outside of Lent — monastery-brewed dark ale paired with beef and root vegetables from surrounding farms, slow-simmered for hours in an iron pot over the hearth.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 牛肩肉 Beef chuck | 500克 500g |
| 修道院黑啤 Dark abbey ale | 2杯 2 cups |
| 洋葱 Onion | 2个 2 whole |
| 胡萝卜 Carrots | 3根 3 whole |
| 欧防风 Parsnips | 2根 2 whole |
| 大蒜 Garlic | 4瓣 4 cloves |
| 百里香 Thyme | 数枝 Several sprigs |
| 面包 Dark bread (thickener) | 2片 2 slices |
| 蜂蜜 Honey | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 猪油 Lard | 2大匙 2 tbsp |

## 做法 Preparation

1. **切料 Cut ingredients:** 牛肉切大块，洋葱切厚环，胡萝卜与欧防风切滚刀块。Cut beef into large chunks, onions into thick rings, carrots and parsnips into rolling-cut pieces.
2. **煎肉 Sear meat:** 铁锅加猪油烧热，牛肉块分批煎至各面焦褐，取出备用。Heat lard in an iron pot, sear beef in batches until browned on all sides, set aside.
3. **炒蔬菜 Sautee vegetables:** 同锅炒洋葱至金黄出甜味，加大蒜翻炒片刻。In the same pot, cook onions until golden and sweet, add garlic and stir briefly.
4. **加啤酒 Add ale:** 倒入黑啤刮起锅底焦香，放回牛肉，加百里香、蜂蜜和撕碎的面包。Pour in dark ale, scrape up the fond, return beef, add thyme, honey, and torn bread as thickener.
5. **慢炖 Slow stew:** 加盖以文火慢炖三至四个时辰，中途加入胡萝卜与欧防风，直至牛肉酥烂。Cover and simmer on low heat for 3-4 hours, adding carrots and parsnips midway, until beef is fall-apart tender.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 盛入陶碗，配粗面包蘸汁食用，再来一杯同款修道院啤酒。Ladle into clay bowls, serve with coarse bread for dipping, and a mug of the same abbey ale.

---

## 备注 Notes

- 面包在中世纪炖菜中充当增稠剂的角色，面粉勾芡技法尚未普及。Bread served as a thickener in medieval stews; flour-based roux had not yet become common.
- 修道院啤酒至今仍由比利时特拉普派修士酿造，延续千年传统。Monastery ales are still brewed by Belgian Trappist monks today, continuing a thousand-year tradition.
