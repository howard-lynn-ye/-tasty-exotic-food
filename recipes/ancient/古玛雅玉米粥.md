# 古玛雅玉米粥 | Mayan Corn Porridge (~500AD)

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪30分 | 💰~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、玛雅文明

> **📜 历史** — 玛雅人视玉米为神赐之物，玉米粥"阿托尔"是日常主食，也用于宗教仪式和庆典。
> **📜 History** — *The Maya revered corn as a divine gift; corn porridge "atole" was their daily staple and featured in religious ceremonies and celebrations.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 玉米面 | Masa harina | 100g / 0.75 cup |
| 水 | Water | 500ml / 2 cups |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 肉桂 | Cinnamon | 1根 / 1 stick |
| 香草 | Vanilla | 3ml / 0.5 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 调糊 | Mix Batter
玉米面加少量冷水搅成无颗粒的糊状。

Whisk masa harina with a little cold water until lump-free.

### 2. 煮粥 | Cook Porridge
锅中烧开水，倒入玉米糊和肉桂棒，中火搅煮20分钟至浓稠。

Bring water to boil, stir in corn mixture and cinnamon stick, cook on medium heat 20 minutes until thick.

### 3. 调味 | Flavor
去掉肉桂棒，加蜂蜜和香草调味，趁热饮用。

Remove cinnamon stick, stir in honey and vanilla, serve warm.

---

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
玛雅创世神话《波波尔·乌》记载，神用玉米造人，玉米因此在中美洲文明中具有神圣地位。阿托尔玉米粥至今仍是墨西哥和中美洲的传统饮品，在节日和早餐中广泛饮用。

*The Mayan creation myth "Popol Vuh" tells that the gods made humans from corn, giving maize a sacred status in Mesoamerican civilization. Atole remains a traditional beverage across Mexico and Central America, widely enjoyed at festivals and for breakfast.*
