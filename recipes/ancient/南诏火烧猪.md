# 南诏火烧猪 | Nanzhao Fire-Roasted Pork (南诏, ~800 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/南诏火烧猪.svg" width="500" alt="南诏火烧猪"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 40分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、南诏、云南、烤猪、彝族

> **📜 历史** — 南诏国（738-902）是彝族和白族在今云南建立的强大政权。"火烧猪"是南诏国的标志性菜肴，整猪以松枝明火烤制，外皮焦脆、肉质鲜嫩。《蛮书》记载南诏人"以火烧猪为上食"，宴请宾客时必不可少。这种烤猪法至今仍保留在云南彝族的火把节庆典中，用松针烧猪皮，赋予独特的松脂香气。
> **📜 History** — *The Nanzhao Kingdom (738-902) was a powerful Yi and Bai realm in present-day Yunnan. "Fire-roasted pork" was its signature dish — whole pigs roasted over open pine-branch fires, yielding crispy skin and tender meat. The "Book of the Southern Barbarians" records that Nanzhao people regarded fire-roasted pig as their "finest food," essential for honoring guests. This roasting method survives in Yunnan's Yi Torch Festival, where pine needles are used to singe the skin, imparting a distinctive resinous aroma.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 五花肉（带皮） | Pork belly (skin-on) | 2磅 | 2 lb |
| 粗盐 | Coarse salt | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 花椒粉 | Sichuan peppercorn powder | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 干辣椒面 | Dried chili flakes | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 米酒 | Rice wine | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 香茅（可选） | Lemongrass (optional) | 1根 | 1 stalk |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **处理猪肉** — 五花肉皮面用刀尖密密刺孔，抹上粗盐、花椒粉和辣椒面，淋米酒，腌制15分钟。皮面额外多抹一层盐。
   *Score the pork belly skin densely with a sharp knife tip. Rub with coarse salt, Sichuan pepper, and chili flakes, drizzle with rice wine, and marinate 15 minutes. Apply an extra layer of salt on the skin side.*

2. **明火烤制** — 烤箱预热至425°F（220°C），五花肉皮面朝上放入烤盘架上，先烤30分钟。然后转broil模式，烤8-10分钟至猪皮起泡酥脆焦黄。
   *Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Place pork belly skin-side up on a roasting rack. Roast 30 minutes, then switch to broil for 8-10 minutes until the skin blisters and turns golden-crispy.*

3. **切块蘸食** — 取出静置5分钟，切成小块。配花椒盐（花椒粉+粗盐）和辣椒面蘸食，搭配米饭。
   *Rest 5 minutes, then cut into small pieces. Serve with pepper-salt dip (Sichuan pepper + coarse salt) and chili flakes alongside rice.*
