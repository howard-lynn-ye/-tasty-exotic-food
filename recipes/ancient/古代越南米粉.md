# 古代越南米粉 | Ancient Vietnamese Pho (河内, ~1900 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 3小时 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、越南、米粉、牛肉汤

> **📜 历史** — Pho（河粉）诞生于20世纪初法属印度支那时期的河内。它是中越法三种文化的融合结晶：中国的米粉制作工艺、法国殖民者引入的牛肉食用习惯（越南传统上较少吃牛）、以及越南本土的香草调味传统。最早的Pho摊出现在河内老城区的街头，挑担小贩沿街叫卖，成为越南最具标志性的国民美食。
> **📜 History** — *Pho was born in early 20th-century Hanoi during French Indochina. It is a fusion of three cultures: Chinese rice noodle craftsmanship, the French colonial introduction of beef consumption (Vietnam traditionally ate little beef), and Vietnam's native herb-seasoning traditions. The first pho stalls appeared on Hanoi Old Quarter streets, with hawkers carrying shoulder poles — birthing Vietnam's most iconic national dish.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 牛骨 | Beef bones | 1公斤 | 2.2 lbs |
| 牛腱肉 | Beef shank | 300克 | 10.5 oz |
| 河粉（干） | Rice noodles (dried, flat) | 400克 | 14 oz |
| 洋葱（烤焦） | Onion (charred) | 2个 | 2 |
| 生姜（烤焦） | Ginger (charred) | 1大块 | 1 large piece |
| 八角 | Star anise | 3颗 | 3 |
| 桂皮 | Cinnamon stick | 1根 | 1 |
| 丁香 | Cloves | 3颗 | 3 |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 冰糖 | Rock sugar | 1小块 | 1 small piece |
| 九层塔、豆芽、辣椒、青柠 | Thai basil, bean sprouts, chili, lime | 各适量 | assorted, to taste |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **焯水烤料** — 牛骨冷水入锅焯水5分钟，冲洗干净。同时将洋葱和生姜切半，用明火或烤箱烤至表面焦黑。
   *Blanch beef bones in cold water for 5 minutes, then rinse clean. Meanwhile, halve onion and ginger and char them over an open flame or under a broiler until blackened.*

2. **熬汤** — 大锅中放牛骨、牛腱肉、烤洋葱和生姜，加水没过食材。大火煮沸后撇去浮沫，转小火，加入八角、桂皮和丁香，慢炖2.5-3小时。
   *Place bones, beef shank, charred onion, and ginger in a large pot. Cover with water. Bring to a boil, skim foam, reduce heat. Add star anise, cinnamon, and cloves. Simmer 2.5-3 hours.*

3. **调味** — 取出牛腱切薄片备用。汤过滤，加鱼露和冰糖调味至鲜甜平衡。
   *Remove beef shank and slice thinly. Strain broth and season with fish sauce and rock sugar until the flavor is balanced between savory and sweet.*

4. **组装** — 河粉煮软沥干放入碗中，铺上牛腱片，浇滚烫的汤。配九层塔、豆芽、辣椒和青柠上桌。
   *Cook rice noodles, drain, and place in bowls. Top with sliced beef shank, ladle boiling-hot broth over. Serve with basil, sprouts, chili, and lime.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- "Pho"一词的来源有争议：可能源自法语"pot-au-feu"（火上锅），或广东话的"粉"。
  *The origin of "pho" is debated: it may derive from French "pot-au-feu" (pot on the fire) or Cantonese "fun" (noodles).*

- 1954年越南分裂后，大量北方人带着Pho南迁，衍生出风味不同的"南派Pho"（加更多香草和豆芽）。
  *After Vietnam's 1954 partition, northerners brought pho south, spawning the distinct "Southern pho" style (more herbs and sprouts).*

- 河内至今保留着百年老店"Pho Thin"，据称其配方自1979年开业以来从未改变。
  *Hanoi still has century-old shops like "Pho Thin," whose recipe reportedly hasn't changed since opening in 1979.*
