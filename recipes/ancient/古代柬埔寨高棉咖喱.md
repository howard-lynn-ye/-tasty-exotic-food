# 古代柬埔寨高棉咖喱 | Khmer Empire Curry (高棉帝国, ~1000 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 40分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、高棉帝国、咖喱、鸡肉

> **📜 历史** — 高棉帝国在苏利耶跋摩二世时期达到鼎盛，建造了举世闻名的吴哥窟。帝国控制了从今日泰国到越南南部的广大区域，丰富的物产催生了独特的宫廷美食。高棉咖喱（Samlor Korko）是柬埔寨的"国菜"，使用当地的香茅、高良姜、姜黄和鱼酱等制成咖喱膏，与印度和泰国咖喱截然不同。吴哥窟浮雕中描绘了宫廷宴会的壮观场景。
> **📜 History** — *The Khmer Empire reached its zenith under Suryavarman II, who built the legendary Angkor Wat. Controlling territory from modern Thailand to southern Vietnam, the empire's abundant resources fostered a unique court cuisine. Khmer curry (Samlor Korko) is Cambodia's "national dish," using local lemongrass, galangal, turmeric, and fermented fish paste — distinctly different from Indian or Thai curries. Angkor Wat reliefs depict spectacular court feast scenes.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿肉（切块） | Chicken thigh (cubed) | 400克 | 14 oz |
| 椰奶 | Coconut milk | 1罐（400ml） | 1 can (14 oz) |
| 香茅（拍碎） | Lemongrass (smashed) | 2根 | 2 stalks |
| 高良姜（切片） | Galangal (sliced) | 4片 | 4 slices |
| 姜黄粉 | Ground turmeric | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 棕榈糖（或红糖） | Palm sugar (or brown sugar) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 茄子（切块） | Eggplant (cubed) | 1个 | 1 |
| 青豆角（切段） | Green beans (cut) | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 新鲜罗勒叶 | Fresh basil leaves | 一把 | a handful |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮咖喱底** — 锅中倒入半罐椰奶，中火煮至微微分油，加入拍碎的香茅、高良姜和姜黄粉，炒香2分钟。
   *Pour half the coconut milk into a pot over medium heat, cook until it begins to split. Add smashed lemongrass, galangal, and turmeric; stir-fry for 2 minutes until fragrant.*

2. **煮鸡肉** — 加入鸡肉块翻炒至表面变白，倒入剩余椰奶和1杯水，加鱼露和棕榈糖，煮沸后转小火。
   *Add chicken and stir until surface turns white. Pour in remaining coconut milk and 1 cup water. Add fish sauce and palm sugar, bring to a boil, then reduce heat.*

3. **加蔬菜** — 加入茄子和青豆角，继续炖煮20分钟至鸡肉熟透、蔬菜软嫩。
   *Add eggplant and green beans, continue simmering 20 minutes until chicken is cooked through and vegetables are tender.*

4. **装盘** — 盛入碗中，撒新鲜罗勒叶，搭配米饭食用。
   *Ladle into bowls, scatter fresh basil leaves on top, and serve with steamed rice.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 吴哥窟浮雕中的"厨房场景"展示了大量使用石臼研磨香料的画面，与今日柬埔寨制作咖喱膏的方式一脉相承。
  *The "kitchen scenes" in Angkor Wat reliefs show extensive use of stone mortars for grinding spices — unchanged from how Cambodians make curry paste today.*

- 高棉帝国的洞里萨湖提供了丰富的淡水鱼资源，鱼酱（prahok）成为高棉料理的灵魂调味料。
  *The Khmer Empire's Tonle Sap Lake provided abundant freshwater fish; fermented fish paste (prahok) became the soul seasoning of Khmer cuisine.*
