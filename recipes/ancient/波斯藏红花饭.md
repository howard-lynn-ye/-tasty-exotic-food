# 波斯藏红花饭 | Persian Saffron Rice (波斯帝国, ~500 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 40分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、波斯帝国、米饭、素食

> **📜 历史** — 藏红花饭（Tahdig/Chelow）是波斯美食的灵魂，源头可追溯至阿契美尼德帝国时期。藏红花产自波斯高原，古波斯人最早将其用于烹饪和染色。居鲁士大帝的宫廷宴席上已有藏红花米饭的记载。波斯人发明了"tahdig"技法——在锅底形成金黄酥脆的米饭锅巴，至今仍是伊朗家庭最珍视的美食传统。
> **📜 History** — *Saffron rice (Tahdig/Chelow) is the soul of Persian cuisine, traceable to the Achaemenid Empire. Saffron, native to the Persian plateau, was first used by ancient Persians for cooking and dyeing. Saffron rice appeared at the court feasts of Cyrus the Great. Persians invented the "tahdig" technique — creating a golden, crispy rice crust at the bottom of the pot — a culinary tradition that remains the most treasured in Iranian households today.*

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |

| 酸奶（可选，用于锅巴） | Yogurt (optional, for tahdig) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **泡米和藏红花** — 米洗净浸泡30分钟，沥干。藏红花放入3汤匙热水中浸泡15分钟，使其释放颜色和香气。
   *Rinse rice and soak for 30 minutes, then drain. Bloom saffron in 3 tbsp hot water for 15 minutes to release color and aroma.*

2. **半煮米饭** — 大锅烧8杯水加盐至沸腾，倒入米煮6-7分钟至米粒外软内硬（约七分熟），沥干。
   *Bring 8 cups of salted water to a boil. Add rice and cook 6-7 minutes until grains are soft outside but firm inside (about 70% done). Drain.*

3. **制作锅巴底** — 锅中加黄油和植物油，中火加热。取2-3勺米饭与藏红花水和酸奶（如用）混合，铺在锅底形成薄薄一层。
   *Melt butter and oil in a non-stick pot over medium heat. Mix 2-3 scoops of rice with saffron water and yogurt (if using), spread evenly on the pot bottom as a thin layer.*

4. **蒸饭** — 将剩余米饭轻轻堆成山丘形放在锅巴层上，用筷子戳几个透气孔。盖紧锅盖（可用毛巾包裹锅盖吸收水汽），小火蒸30-35分钟。
   *Gently mound remaining rice on top in a pyramid shape. Poke a few holes with chopsticks for steam. Cover tightly (wrap lid with a towel to absorb moisture) and steam on low heat for 30-35 minutes.*

5. **翻扣上桌** — 将锅放在湿毛巾上30秒帮助脱模，然后倒扣在大盘中，金黄酥脆的tahdig锅巴在最上面。
   *Set the pot on a wet towel for 30 seconds to help release the crust. Invert onto a large plate — the golden, crispy tahdig crowns the top.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 藏红花是世界上最昂贵的香料，每磅需要约75,000朵番红花。波斯至今仍是全球最大的藏红花产地。
  *Saffron is the world's most expensive spice, requiring about 75,000 crocus flowers per pound. Iran remains the world's largest producer.*

- 阿契美尼德王朝的波斯波利斯浮雕上描绘了向国王进贡食物的场景，其中可能包含米饭。
  *Reliefs at Persepolis depict scenes of food being presented to the king, which may have included rice dishes.*

- "Tahdig"在波斯语中意为"锅底"，在伊朗文化中，客人争抢tahdig是对厨师最大的赞美。
  *"Tahdig" means "bottom of the pot" in Persian; in Iranian culture, guests fighting over tahdig is the highest compliment to the cook.*
