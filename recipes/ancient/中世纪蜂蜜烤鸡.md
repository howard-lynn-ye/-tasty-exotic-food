# 中世纪蜂蜜烤鸡 | Medieval Honey Roasted Chicken (中世纪欧洲, ~1200 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 60分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、中世纪欧洲、烤制、家禽

> **📜 历史** — 中世纪欧洲贵族宴席上，蜂蜜烤禽类是最受欢迎的主菜之一。由于当时欧洲尚未引进蔗糖，蜂蜜是唯一的甜味剂。此菜灵感源自约1390年的英国古食谱集《The Forme of Cury》，由理查二世的皇家厨师编写，是现存最早的英语烹饪手稿之一。
> **📜 History** — *Honey-roasted poultry was a prized main course at medieval European noble feasts. Since cane sugar had not yet been introduced to Europe, honey was the sole sweetener. This recipe is inspired by "The Forme of Cury" (~1390), compiled by the master cooks of King Richard II — one of the oldest surviving English-language culinary manuscripts.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 整鸡 | Whole chicken | 1只（约3磅） | 1 (about 3 lbs) |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 黄油（融化） | Butter (melted) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 大蒜（切碎） | Garlic (minced) | 3瓣 | 3 cloves |
| 新鲜迷迭香 | Fresh rosemary | 2枝 | 2 sprigs |
| 新鲜百里香 | Fresh thyme | 3枝 | 3 sprigs |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 生姜粉 | Ground ginger | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | 1个 | 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **预热烤箱** — 烤箱预热至375°F（190°C）。鸡洗净拍干，放入烤盘。
   *Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Rinse chicken, pat dry, and place in a roasting pan.*

2. **制作蜂蜜酱** — 将蜂蜜、融化黄油、蒜末、肉桂粉、姜粉、盐和黑胡椒混合均匀。
   *Mix honey, melted butter, minced garlic, cinnamon, ginger, salt, and black pepper in a bowl.*

3. **涂抹腌制** — 将蜂蜜酱均匀涂抹在鸡全身及鸡腔内。将柠檬切半和香草塞入鸡腔。
   *Rub the honey mixture evenly over the entire chicken and inside the cavity. Stuff the cavity with lemon halves and herb sprigs.*

4. **烤制** — 送入烤箱烤约55-65分钟，每20分钟用盘底汁液浇淋鸡身，直至鸡皮金黄、内部温度达165°F（74°C）。
   *Roast for 55-65 minutes, basting with pan juices every 20 minutes, until skin is golden and internal temperature reaches 165°F (74°C).*

5. **静置上桌** — 取出后静置10分钟再切块，淋上盘底汁液即可。
   *Rest for 10 minutes before carving. Drizzle with pan juices and serve.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 中世纪香料极为昂贵，肉桂和姜从东方经丝绸之路运来，只有贵族才能负担。
  *Medieval spices were extremely expensive — cinnamon and ginger traveled from the East via the Silk Road and were affordable only to nobility.*

- 中世纪厨师喜欢甜咸结合的口味，这种烹饪风格在文艺复兴后逐渐消失。
  *Medieval cooks favored sweet-savory combinations, a culinary style that gradually disappeared after the Renaissance.*

- 中世纪宴席上，整只烤鸡通常由侍者在宾客面前切分，是一种仪式性的展示。
  *At medieval feasts, whole roasted birds were carved before guests by a server — a ceremonial display of hospitality.*

## 替代食材 | American Substitutions

| 原始食材 | Original | 替代品 | Substitution |
|----------|----------|--------|-------------|
| 整鸡 | Whole chicken | 鸡腿或鸡胸（减少烤制时间至40分钟） | Chicken legs or breasts (reduce roasting to 40 min) |
| 新鲜迷迭香/百里香 | Fresh rosemary/thyme | 干迷迭香/百里香（用量减半） | Dried rosemary/thyme (use half the amount) |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 枫糖浆（味道略有不同） | Maple syrup (slightly different flavor) |
