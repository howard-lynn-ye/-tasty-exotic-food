# 古罗马角斗场香肠 | Roman Colosseum Sausage (古罗马, ~100 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古罗马角斗场香肠.svg" width="500" alt="古罗马角斗场香肠"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古罗马、烤肉、猪肉

> **📜 历史** — 在角斗士比赛日，罗马竞技场外聚集着大量小贩，烤香肠（lucanica）是最受欢迎的街头小吃。这种香肠源自南意大利的卢卡尼亚地区，用猪肉混合松子、胡椒和鱼露（garum）灌制。罗马美食家阿皮修斯在《论烹饪》中记录了详细配方。罗马皇帝甚至曾因节日期间香肠太受欢迎而试图禁止其销售。
> **📜 History** — *On gladiatorial fight days, vendors crowded outside the Colosseum selling roasted sausages (lucanica) — the most popular street food. These sausages originated from Lucania in southern Italy, stuffed with pork, pine nuts, pepper, and fish sauce (garum). Roman gourmand Apicius recorded the detailed recipe in "De Re Coquinaria." Emperors even tried to ban sausage sales during festivals because of their overwhelming popularity.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 猪肉糜 | Ground pork | 500克 | 1.1 lbs |
| 松子 | Pine nuts | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒（粗磨） | Black pepper (coarsely ground) | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 新鲜香菜（切碎） | Fresh savory/parsley (chopped) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 月桂叶（碾碎） | Bay leaf (crushed) | 1片 | 1 leaf |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 天然肠衣（可选） | Natural casings (optional) | 适量 | as needed |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **调味混合** — 将猪肉糜放入大碗，加入松子、黑胡椒、小茴香、鱼露、香菜和碎月桂叶，充分搅拌均匀。
   *Place ground pork in a large bowl. Add pine nuts, black pepper, cumin, fish sauce, parsley, and crushed bay leaf. Mix thoroughly.*

2. **成型** — 如用肠衣，将肉馅灌入肠衣制成香肠；若无肠衣，将肉馅搓成约10厘米长的条状。
   *If using casings, stuff the mixture into casings to form sausages. If not, shape the mixture into links about 10 cm (4 inches) long.*

3. **烤制** — 平底锅加橄榄油中火加热，放入香肠煎烤约12-15分钟，不断翻转至各面均匀焦黄熟透。
   *Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Pan-fry sausages for 12-15 minutes, turning frequently until evenly browned and cooked through.*

4. **食用** — 搭配粗面包和橄榄食用，蘸鱼露或蜂蜜芥末更佳。
   *Serve with rustic bread and olives. Dip in fish sauce or honey mustard for extra flavor.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 罗马鱼露（garum）由发酵鱼内脏制成，类似东南亚鱼露，是罗马烹饪的万能调味料。
  *Roman garum was made from fermented fish innards, similar to Southeast Asian fish sauce — a universal Roman seasoning.*

- 早期基督教会曾将香肠与异教节日联系，导致罗马皇帝康斯坦丁一世一度禁止香肠。
  *The early Christian Church linked sausages to pagan festivals, leading Emperor Constantine I to briefly ban them.*
