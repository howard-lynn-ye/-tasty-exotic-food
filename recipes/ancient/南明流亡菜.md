# 南明流亡菜 | Southern Ming Exile Cuisine (南明, ~1650 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/南明流亡菜.svg" width="500" alt="南明流亡菜"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、南明、流亡、野菜、简约

> **📜 历史** — 南明（1644-1662）是明朝覆亡后宗室在南方建立的流亡政权。永历帝朱由榔辗转于云贵缅甸之间，昔日锦衣玉食的宫廷变成了有什么吃什么的逃亡生涯。据笔记记载，流亡宫廷中的御厨用野菜、山芋和腌菜做出"三菜一汤"已属奢侈。这道菜以简陋食材做出最大滋味，体现了乱世中"化简为雅"的生存智慧。
> **📜 History** — *The Southern Ming (1644-1662) were exile courts established by Ming princes after the dynasty's fall. Emperor Yongli fled across Guizhou, Yunnan, and into Burma. Former palace kitchens accustomed to lavish feasts were reduced to cooking with whatever was at hand. Contemporary accounts note that imperial chefs who could produce "three dishes and a soup" from wild greens, yams, and pickles were considered luxurious. This dish maximizes flavor from humble ingredients — embodying the wartime wisdom of "turning simplicity into elegance."*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 红薯或山芋 | Sweet potato or yam | 2个 | 2 pcs |
| 野菜或菠菜 | Wild greens or spinach | 1大把 | 1 large bunch |
| 腌菜（榨菜或雪菜） | Pickled vegetables (zhacai or xuecai) | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 | 2 pcs |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮山芋汤底** — 红薯去皮切块，加4杯水煮至软烂约15分钟。用勺子将部分红薯压碎使汤变浓稠，保留一些块状。
   *Peel and chop sweet potatoes. Boil in 4 cups water until tender, about 15 minutes. Mash some pieces to thicken the broth while leaving others chunky.*

2. **炒野菜拌腌菜** — 另起锅加油，大火快炒野菜至断生，加入腌菜碎翻炒出咸鲜味，调少许盐。
   *In a separate pan, heat oil and stir-fry the greens over high heat until just wilted. Add chopped pickles and toss until the savory-salty flavor emerges. Season with a pinch of salt.*

3. **打蛋成席** — 鸡蛋打入红薯汤中搅成蛋花，关火。炒好的野菜腌菜铺在碗底，浇上红薯蛋花汤，即成一顿朴素而温暖的流亡餐。
   *Beat eggs into the sweet potato soup and stir to form ribbons, then turn off heat. Place the greens and pickles in the bottom of a bowl, ladle the sweet potato egg-drop soup over them — a humble yet warming exile meal.*
