# 古巴比伦羊肉饼 | Babylonian Lamb Pie (~1700BC)

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪45分 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、巴比伦

> **📜 历史** — 耶鲁大学收藏的巴比伦泥板记录了人类最古老的食谱，其中包括以羊肉和香料制成的肉饼。
> **📜 History** — *Yale's Babylonian clay tablets contain humanity's oldest known recipes, including lamb pies seasoned with herbs and spices.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 羊肉末 | Ground lamb | 400g / 14oz |
| 面粉 | Flour | 300g / 2.5 cups |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 |
| 大蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 孜然 | Cumin | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 一小把 / 1 bunch |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 做皮 | Make Crust
面粉加水和少许盐揉成面团，擀成薄饼做底和盖。Mix flour, water, and salt into dough; roll thin for bottom and top crusts.

### 2. 做馅 | Make Filling
羊肉末加洋葱碎、蒜末、孜然和香菜炒熟调味。Cook ground lamb with diced onion, garlic, cumin, and cilantro.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
馅料包入面皮中，烤箱180°C烤35分钟至金黄。Wrap filling in dough, bake at 350°F for 35 minutes until golden.
