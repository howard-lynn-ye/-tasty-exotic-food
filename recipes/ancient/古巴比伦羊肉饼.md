# 古巴比伦羊肉饼 | Babylonian Lamb Pie (~1700BC)

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪45分 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、巴比伦

> **📜 历史** — 耶鲁大学收藏的巴比伦泥板记录了人类最古老的食谱，其中包括以羊肉和香料制成的肉饼。
> **📜 History** — *Yale's Babylonian clay tablets contain humanity's oldest known recipes, including lamb pies seasoned with herbs and spices.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 羊肉末 | Ground lamb | 400g / 14oz |
| 面粉 | Flour | 300g / 2.5 cups |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 |
| 大蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 孜然 | Cumin | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 一小把 / 1 bunch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 做皮 | Make Crust
面粉加水和少许盐揉成面团，擀成薄饼做底和盖。

Mix flour, water, and salt into dough; roll thin for bottom and top crusts.

### 2. 做馅 | Make Filling
羊肉末加洋葱碎、蒜末、孜然和香菜炒熟调味。

Cook ground lamb with diced onion, garlic, cumin, and cilantro.

### 3. 烤制 | Bake
馅料包入面皮中，烤箱180°C烤35分钟至金黄。

Wrap filling in dough, bake at 350°F for 35 minutes until golden.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 面皮不要擀太薄，否则烤制时容易破裂漏馅 | Don't roll the crust too thin or it will crack and leak during baking |
| 羊肉馅要炒干水分再包，避免烤制时底部变湿 | Cook the filling until dry before wrapping to prevent a soggy bottom |
| 可在面皮表面刷蛋液，烤出来色泽更金黄诱人 | Brush the top with egg wash for a more golden, appealing finish |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
耶鲁大学保存的三块巴比伦泥板（约公元前1750年）是目前已知最古老的食谱记录，用楔形文字刻写了35道菜肴的做法。这些食谱展现了美索不达米亚高度发达的烹饪文明，其中羊肉饼使用了洋葱、大蒜、孜然等调味料，与今天中东料理一脉相承。

*Three Babylonian clay tablets preserved at Yale University (c. 1750 BC) contain the world's oldest known recipes, with 35 dishes inscribed in cuneiform. These recipes reveal a sophisticated culinary civilization in Mesopotamia; the lamb pie features onion, garlic, and cumin — seasonings that remain the backbone of Middle Eastern cooking to this day.*
