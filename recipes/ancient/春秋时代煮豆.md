# 春秋时代煮豆 | Spring & Autumn Period Boiled Beans (春秋, ~600 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/春秋时代煮豆.svg" width="500" alt="春秋时代煮豆"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 浸泡8小时 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、春秋、素食、豆类

> **📜 历史** — "菽"（大豆）是先秦时期的五谷之一，《管子》记载齐桓公时代大豆已广泛种植。春秋时期的平民以"藿羹"（豆叶汤）和煮豆为主要蛋白质来源。曹植名诗"煮豆燃豆萁"虽为三国时期所作，但煮豆的传统远溯至春秋。孔子在《论语》中提到的"饭疏食"中的"疏食"即包含豆类。这道简朴的煮豆代表了先秦平民饮食的核心——简单、实在、养身。
> **📜 History** — *Soybeans (shu) were one of the "Five Grains" in pre-Qin China. The Guanzi records that soybeans were widely cultivated during Duke Huan of Qi's era. Commoners in the Spring and Autumn period relied on bean-leaf soup (huo geng) and boiled beans as primary protein sources. While Cao Zhi's famous poem "cooking beans and burning bean stalks" dates to the Three Kingdoms, the boiled bean tradition stretches back to the Spring and Autumn period. This humble dish represents the core of pre-Qin commoner cuisine — simple, substantial, and nourishing.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 黄豆 | Dried soybeans | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 水 | Water | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 姜片 | Ginger slices | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 大葱 | Scallion | 1根 | 1 stalk |
| 粗盐 | Coarse salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 干辣椒（可选） | Dried chili (optional) | 2个 | 2 |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **浸泡** — 黄豆洗净后用清水浸泡8小时或隔夜至完全膨胀，沥干。
   *Rinse soybeans and soak in water 8 hours or overnight until fully plumped. Drain.*

2. **煮豆** — 锅中加水、黄豆、姜片、葱段、花椒和干辣椒，大火煮沸后转小火慢煮40分钟至豆子软烂但不破碎。
   *In a pot, combine water, soybeans, ginger, scallion, Sichuan peppercorn, and dried chili. Bring to a boil, then simmer on low 40 minutes until beans are tender but not mushy.*

3. **调味** — 加入粗盐和酱油拌匀，再煮5分钟入味，盛出即可作为主食或配菜食用。
   *Add coarse salt and soy sauce, stir well, and cook 5 more minutes to let flavors absorb. Serve as a main dish or side.*
