# 古代缅甸咖喱 | Ancient Burmese Curry (蒲甘王朝, ~800 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古代缅甸咖喱.svg" width="500" alt="古代缅甸咖喱"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、缅甸、咖喱、猪肉

> **📜 历史** — 缅甸蒲甘王朝时期，缅甸人发展出独特的"油咖喱"（Si Byan）烹饪法——将肉类在大量油脂和洋葱中慢炖至油脂分离浮面。这种烹饪方式受到印度和中国的双重影响，但又自成一派。蒲甘寺庙壁画中描绘了宫廷厨师烹饪咖喱的场景，证明了这道菜在缅甸的悠久历史。
> **📜 History** — *During the Pagan Kingdom era, the Burmese developed their unique "oil curry" (si byan) technique — slow-cooking meat in generous amounts of oil and onion until the oil separates and floats to the surface. This method shows dual Indian and Chinese influences yet stands entirely on its own. Pagan temple murals depict palace chefs cooking curry, attesting to this dish's long Burmese history.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炒洋葱底** — 锅中加花生油，中火炒洋葱15-20分钟至深棕色焦糖化（这是缅甸咖喱的关键步骤，不可省略）。
   *Heat peanut oil in a pot over medium heat. Cook onions 15-20 minutes until deep brown and caramelized — this is the crucial step of Burmese curry, do not skip.*

2. **炒香料** — 加入蒜泥、姜泥、姜黄和辣椒粉，翻炒2分钟至出香。
   *Add garlic paste, ginger paste, turmeric, and chili powder. Stir-fry 2 minutes until fragrant.*

3. **炖肉** — 加入猪肉块翻炒至变色，加番茄、鱼露和水，大火煮沸后转小火，炖30-35分钟至肉软烂，油脂浮面。
   *Add pork and stir until color changes. Add tomatoes, fish sauce, and water. Boil, then simmer 30-35 minutes until pork is tender and oil rises to the surface.*

4. **装盘** — 搭配白米饭和缅甸酸菜沙拉食用。
   *Serve with steamed white rice and Burmese pickled salad.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 缅甸咖喱的"油浮面"特征不是失误，而是一种古老的食物保存法——油层隔绝空气，延长保质期。
  *The "oil floating on top" in Burmese curry is not a mistake but an ancient preservation technique — the oil layer seals out air and extends shelf life.*

- 蒲甘时期的缅甸是东南亚最重要的贸易中心之一，印度香料通过海路大量进入缅甸。
  *Pagan-era Burma was one of Southeast Asia's key trade hubs; Indian spices arrived in large quantities via sea routes.*

- 缅甸咖喱与印度咖喱最大的区别是不使用椰奶，而是依靠大量洋葱和油脂创造浓郁口感。
  *The biggest difference between Burmese and Indian curry is the absence of coconut milk — Burmese curry relies on onions and oil for richness.*
