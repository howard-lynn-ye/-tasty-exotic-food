# 古代越南烤肉米纸卷 | Ancient Vietnamese Rice Paper Roll (~500AD)

> ⏱ 准备25分+烹饪15分 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、越南

> **📜 历史** — 越南米纸的历史可追溯至南朝时期的交趾郡。当地先民以米浆蒸成薄皮，包裹烤肉和香草，蘸鱼露食用，这一吃法历经1500年几乎未变。
> **📜 History** — *Vietnamese rice paper dates back to the Jiaozhi commandery era. Locals steamed rice batter into thin sheets, wrapping grilled meat and herbs, dipped in fish sauce — a method virtually unchanged for 1,500 years.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪颈肉 | Pork neck / shoulder | 300g / 10 oz |
| 米纸 | Rice paper | 10张 / 10 sheets |
| 米粉 | Rice vermicelli | 100g / 3.5 oz |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 柠檬草 | Lemongrass | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 薄荷 | Mint | 1把 / 1 handful |
| 生菜 | Lettuce | 6片 / 6 leaves |
| 青柠 | Lime | 1个 / 1 pc |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 15g / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 腌肉 | Marinate
猪肉切薄片，蒜末、柠檬草碎、鱼露15ml和糖拌匀，腌20分钟。Slice pork thin, mix with minced garlic, chopped lemongrass, 15ml fish sauce, and sugar, marinate 20 minutes.

### 2. 烤肉 | Grill
炭火或平底锅高温烤至两面焦香，约每面3分钟。Grill over charcoal or sear in a hot pan until charred on both sides, about 3 minutes per side.

### 3. 煮米粉 | Cook Noodles
米粉沸水煮2分钟，过冷水沥干。Boil vermicelli 2 minutes, rinse in cold water and drain.

### 4. 卷制上桌 | Roll & Serve
米纸蘸水软化，依次铺生菜、米粉、烤肉、薄荷，卷紧。蘸料：鱼露15ml + 青柠汁 + 水 + 糖调匀。Dip rice paper in water to soften, layer lettuce, noodles, grilled meat, and mint, roll tight. Dipping sauce: 15ml fish sauce + lime juice + water + sugar, mixed.
