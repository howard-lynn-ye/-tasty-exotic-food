# 清代宫廷酸梅汤 | Qing Dynasty Imperial Plum Drink (清代, ~1800 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/清代宫廷酸梅汤.svg" width="500" alt="清代宫廷酸梅汤"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 40分钟 + 冷藏 2小时 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、清代、饮品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 酸梅汤是清代宫廷御饮，在乾隆年间经御膳房改良定型。每到盛夏，紫禁城中从太后到宫女都饮用酸梅汤消暑。其核心原料乌梅（烟熏青梅）在中国有三千年的药食两用历史，《本草纲目》记载乌梅具有生津止渴、开胃消食的功效。清代北京街头也有小贩挑担叫卖酸梅汤，是老北京最经典的夏日记忆。
> **📜 History** — *Sour plum drink was an imperial beverage of the Qing court, refined and standardized by the Imperial Kitchen during the Qianlong reign. Every summer, everyone in the Forbidden City — from the Empress Dowager to the maids — drank it to beat the heat. Its core ingredient, smoked green plum (wumei), has 3,000 years of dual culinary-medicinal use in China. "Bencao Gangmu" records that wumei promotes saliva production and aids digestion. Qing-era Beijing street vendors also sold sour plum drink from shoulder poles — an iconic Old Beijing summer memory.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 乌梅 | Smoked plums (wumei) | 30克（约10颗） | 30g (about 10 pieces) |
| 山楂干 | Dried hawthorn slices | 20克 | 20g |
| 甘草 | Licorice root | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 桂花（干） | Dried osmanthus flowers | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 冰糖 | Rock sugar | 60-80克 | 60-80g |
| 水 | Water | 8杯 | 8 cups |
| 薄荷叶（装饰，可选） | Mint leaves (garnish, optional) | 几片 | a few leaves |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **浸泡材料** — 乌梅、山楂干和甘草用清水冲洗，放入锅中加8杯水浸泡20分钟。
   *Rinse smoked plums, hawthorn slices, and licorice root. Place in a pot with 8 cups of water and soak for 20 minutes.*

2. **熬煮** — 大火煮沸后转小火，盖上盖子熬煮30-40分钟，使药材充分释放风味，汤色变为深褐色。
   *Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat. Cover and simmer 30-40 minutes until ingredients have fully released their flavors and the liquid turns deep brown.*

3. **调味过滤** — 加入冰糖搅拌至融化，关火后加入桂花，盖盖焖5分钟。用细筛网过滤去渣。
   *Add rock sugar and stir until dissolved. Turn off heat, add osmanthus flowers, cover, and steep 5 minutes. Strain through a fine mesh sieve.*

4. **冷藏上桌** — 酸梅汤冷却后放入冰箱冷藏至少2小时。饮用时加冰块，可用薄荷叶装饰。口感应酸甜适中，回味悠长。
   *Cool to room temperature, then refrigerate at least 2 hours. Serve over ice, garnished with mint leaves if desired. The flavor should be balanced between sour and sweet with a lingering aftertaste.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 御膳房酸梅汤配方属于宫廷机密，清亡后才流入民间。老北京"信远斋"和"九龙斋"是最早商业化生产酸梅汤的店铺。
  *The Imperial Kitchen's plum drink recipe was a palace secret, only reaching the public after the Qing fell. "Xinyuanzhai" and "Jiulongzhai" were the first shops to commercialize it in old Beijing.*

- 乌梅的酸味来自柠檬酸和苹果酸，天然具有解暑功效，现代研究也证实其抗氧化活性。
  *The sourness of smoked plums comes from citric and malic acid, naturally cooling in summer. Modern research confirms their antioxidant activity.*

- 酸梅汤在清代宫廷中用冰窖存储的天然冰块降温——紫禁城的冰窖至今保存完好。
  *In the Qing court, plum drink was chilled with natural ice from imperial ice cellars — the Forbidden City's ice cellars are preserved to this day.*
