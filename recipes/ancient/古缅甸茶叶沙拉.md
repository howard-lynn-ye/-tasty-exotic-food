# 古缅甸茶叶沙拉 | Ancient Burmese Tea Leaf Salad (古缅甸, ~500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟（加腌制时间） + 拌制 5分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、缅甸、沙拉、素食

> **📜 历史** — 缅甸是世界上唯一将茶叶作为食物而非仅仅饮品的文化之一。拉佩豆（Lahpet Thoke）即发酵茶叶沙拉，其历史可追溯至骠国时期（约公元500年）。古代缅甸部落之间以交换发酵茶叶作为和平的象征，这一传统延续至今。发酵茶叶需要数月时间在竹筒中密封腌制，产生独特的酸涩鲜美滋味，配以炸豆、芝麻和蒜片，是缅甸人最引以为傲的国菜。
> **📜 History** — *Myanmar is one of the world's only cultures to eat tea leaves as food, not just brew them. Lahpet Thoke (fermented tea leaf salad) traces back to the Pyu city-states era (c. 500 AD). Ancient Burmese tribes exchanged fermented tea leaves as symbols of peace — a tradition still alive today. The leaves ferment for months sealed in bamboo tubes, developing a unique sour-bitter-umami flavor. Mixed with fried beans, sesame, and garlic chips, it is Myanmar's proudest national dish.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 发酵茶叶（拉佩） | Fermented tea leaves (lahpet) | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 炸黄豆 | Fried soybeans | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 炸花生 | Fried peanuts | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 炸蒜片 | Fried garlic chips | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 烤芝麻 | Toasted sesame seeds | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 干虾米 | Dried shrimp | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 青辣椒（切圈） | Green chili (sliced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 番茄（切丁） | Tomato (diced) | 1/2个 | 1/2 |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 花生油 | Peanut oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **准备配料** — 将炸黄豆、花生、蒜片和芝麻分别准备好（可购买现成品或自行油炸）。
   *Prepare fried soybeans, peanuts, garlic chips, and toasted sesame separately (buy ready-made or fry yourself).*

2. **调味茶叶** — 发酵茶叶加柠檬汁、鱼露和花生油拌匀，让茶叶充分吸收调味。
   *Mix fermented tea leaves with lemon juice, fish sauce, and peanut oil; let the leaves absorb the seasoning.*

3. **拌合** — 将所有配料加入茶叶中，加番茄丁和青辣椒圈，用手轻轻拌匀。
   *Add all toppings to the tea leaves along with diced tomato and sliced green chili. Gently toss by hand.*

4. **装盘** — 可以传统方式分格摆盘，也可拌匀装入一碗，即拌即食口感最佳。
   *Serve traditionally in separated compartments on a plate, or tossed together in a bowl. Best enjoyed immediately after mixing.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 缅甸谚语："茶叶是吃的，不是喝的"——反映了茶叶在缅甸饮食文化中的独特地位。
  *Burmese proverb: "Tea is for eating, not for drinking" — reflecting tea's unique role in Myanmar's food culture.*

- 缅甸传统婚礼上必须供应拉佩豆沙拉，象征新婚夫妇生活的丰富多彩。
  *Lahpet salad must be served at traditional Burmese weddings, symbolizing the richness and variety of married life.*
