## 历史背景 Historical Background

唐代是中国饮食国际化的巅峰时期。酪浆源自北方游牧民族的酸奶传统，经丝绸之路传入中原后成为长安贵族的时尚饮品。白居易诗中有"酪浆煮沸牛乳"的描写。唐人常加蜂蜜或蔗浆调味，冰镇后饮用，堪称古代的酸奶奶昔。

The Tang Dynasty was the peak of culinary internationalization in China. Yogurt drinks (laojiang) originated from northern nomadic fermented milk traditions and reached the Central Plains via the Silk Road, becoming a fashionable drink among Chang'an aristocrats. Bai Juyi's poetry describes "boiling cow's milk into yogurt." Tang people sweetened it with honey or sugarcane syrup and served it chilled — an ancient yogurt smoothie.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 全脂牛奶 Whole milk | 500ml |
| 原味酸奶（作引子）Plain yogurt (starter) | 2 汤匙 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 Honey | 2-3 汤匙 tbsp |
| 冰块 Ice cubes | 适量 as desired |
| 玫瑰花瓣 Dried rose petals (可选) | 少许 a pinch |
| 杏仁碎 Crushed almonds (可选) | 1 汤匙 tbsp |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 发酵 Ferment
牛奶加热至40°C（手感温热），拌入酸奶引子搅匀。倒入干净容器，密封后放在温暖处（30-40°C）发酵6-8小时至凝固酸香。
Heat milk to 40°C (warm to touch). Stir in yogurt starter. Pour into a clean container, seal, and keep in a warm place (30-40°C) for 6-8 hours until set and tangy.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 调味 Flavor
将发酵好的酸奶加入蜂蜜搅拌均匀。若喜欢更稀的口感，可再加少量冷牛奶稀释，搅打至顺滑。
Stir honey into the fermented yogurt. For a thinner consistency, add a little cold milk and whisk until smooth.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 冰镇上饮 Chill and serve
加入冰块搅拌或冷藏2小时。倒入杯中，撒上玫瑰花瓣和杏仁碎点缀，即可享用。
Add ice and stir, or refrigerate 2 hours. Pour into cups, garnish with rose petals and crushed almonds, and enjoy.
