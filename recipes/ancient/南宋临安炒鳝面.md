# 南宋临安炒鳝面 | Southern Song Eel Noodles (南宋, ~1200 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、宋朝、面食、海鲜

> **📜 历史** — 南宋定都临安（今杭州）后，大量北方移民带来了面食文化，与江南水乡的鳝鱼资源碰撞出独特的"鳝面"。《梦粱录》和《武林旧事》详细记录了临安城内"面食店"遍布大街小巷，其中鳝鱼面是最受欢迎的品种之一。黄鳝在南宋被视为滋补佳品，《本草纲目》后来称其"补虚损、益气力"。这道炒鳝面以浓油赤酱翻炒，面条吸饱了鳝鱼的鲜味，是杭帮面食的鼻祖。
> **📜 History** — *After the Southern Song established its capital in Lin'an (modern Hangzhou), northern migrants brought noodle culture that merged with the Jiangnan region's abundant eel resources, creating the distinctive "eel noodles." The Mengliang Lu and Wulin Jiushi record noodle shops lining every street in Lin'an, with eel noodles among the most popular. Yellow eel was prized as a nourishing delicacy — the Compendium of Materia Medica later noted it "replenishes deficiency and strengthens vitality." This stir-fried eel noodle dish, tossed in rich dark sauce, is the ancestor of Hangzhou-style noodles.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鲜面条（碱水面） | Fresh noodles (alkaline noodles) | 250g | 8 oz |
| 黄鳝（切丝） | Yellow eel (julienned) | 200g | 7 oz |
| 姜丝 | Ginger (julienned) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 蒜片 | Garlic (sliced) | 3瓣 | 3 cloves |
| 黄酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 生抽 | Light soy sauce | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | White sugar | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 | a pinch |
| 韭黄或蒜苗（切段） | Yellow chives or garlic sprouts | 1把 | 1 handful |
| 猪油或植物油 | Lard or vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮面与炒鳝** — 面条煮至八成熟捞出沥干拌少许油防粘。锅中大火烧热猪油，放入鳝丝快速翻炒至变色，加姜丝、蒜片和黄酒炒香，盛出备用。
   *Cook noodles until 80% done, drain, and toss with a little oil to prevent sticking. Heat lard in a wok on high, quickly stir-fry eel shreds until color changes. Add ginger, garlic, and Shaoxing wine; toss until fragrant, then set aside.*

2. **炒面** — 同锅加少许油，放入面条大火翻炒，加老抽和生抽炒至面条上色均匀，倒回鳝丝翻拌，加糖和白胡椒调味。
   *Add a bit more oil to the same wok. Stir-fry noodles on high heat, add dark and light soy sauce until evenly colored. Return eel, toss to combine, and season with sugar and white pepper.*

3. **出锅** — 加入韭黄段快速翻炒几下（保持脆嫩），立刻装盘趁热食用。
   *Add yellow chives and toss briefly (keep them crisp-tender). Plate immediately and serve hot.*
