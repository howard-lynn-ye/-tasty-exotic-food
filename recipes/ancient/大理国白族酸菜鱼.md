# 大理国白族酸菜鱼 | Dali Kingdom Bai Sour Fish (大理国, ~1200 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/大理国白族酸菜鱼.svg" width="500" alt="大理国白族酸菜鱼"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、大理国、白族、鱼、酸菜

> **📜 历史** — 大理国（937-1253）位于今云南，白族世代居于洱海之滨，以淡水鱼为食。白族人善于用自制酸菜与鲜鱼同煮，酸辣开胃。大理古城至今仍保留着这道传统菜，洱海弓鱼曾是最珍贵的食材。白族酸菜鱼体现了云南"酸辣为本"的饮食哲学。
> **📜 History** — *The Dali Kingdom (937-1253) occupied present-day Yunnan, where the Bai people lived along Erhai Lake and relied on freshwater fish. The Bai excelled at simmering homemade pickled greens with fresh fish, creating a sour-spicy dish that stimulates the appetite. This traditional recipe endures in Dali's ancient city today. Erhai bowfish was once the most prized ingredient. Bai sour fish embodies Yunnan's culinary philosophy of "sour and spicy as the foundation."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 白肉鱼片（鲈鱼或罗非鱼） | White fish fillets (bass or tilapia) | 1磅 | 1 lb |
| 酸菜（中式腌酸菜） | Pickled mustard greens (Chinese suan cai) | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 小米椒 | Thai chili peppers | 3根 | 3 pcs |
| 生姜（切丝） | Ginger (julienned) | 1块 | 1 piece |
| 大蒜（切片） | Garlic (sliced) | 4瓣 | 4 cloves |
| 香菜 | Cilantro | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煸炒酸菜** — 锅中少许油，小火炒香酸菜、姜丝、蒜片和小米椒，炒出酸香味约2分钟。加入4杯水或清汤，大火煮开。
   *Heat a little oil in a pot over low heat. Stir-fry the pickled greens, ginger, garlic, and chilies until fragrant, about 2 minutes. Add 4 cups water or broth and bring to a boil.*

2. **煮鱼片** — 鱼片切成薄片，轻拍少许盐和淀粉。汤底沸腾后转中火，将鱼片逐一滑入，煮2-3分钟至鱼肉变白熟透。
   *Slice fish into thin pieces and lightly pat with salt and cornstarch. Once the broth is boiling, reduce to medium heat and slide fish slices in one by one. Cook 2-3 minutes until opaque.*

3. **调味上桌** — 尝汤调盐，撒上香菜。酸菜的天然酸味即为主调味，无需加醋。趁热食用。
   *Taste the broth and adjust salt. Garnish with cilantro. The natural tang of the pickled greens provides the main seasoning — no vinegar needed. Serve hot.*
