# 拿破仑行军鸡 | Napoleon's Marengo Chicken (意大利马伦戈, ~1800 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 40分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、法国、鸡肉、战场料理

> **📜 历史** — 1800年6月14日，拿破仑在意大利马伦戈战役中击败奥地利军队。战后他饥肠辘辘，御厨杜南（Dunand）在战场附近搜罗到一只鸡、几个番茄、鸡蛋和小龙虾，即兴创作了这道菜。拿破仑大为赞赏，此后每逢战役纪念日都要求重现这道"胜利之鸡"。
> **📜 History** — *On June 14, 1800, Napoleon defeated Austrian forces at the Battle of Marengo in Italy. Famished after the victory, his chef Dunand scavenged a chicken, tomatoes, eggs, and crayfish near the battlefield and improvised this dish. Napoleon was so delighted that he demanded it be recreated on every anniversary of the battle — his "chicken of victory."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 整鸡（切块） | Whole chicken (cut up) | 1只（约1.5公斤） | 1 (~3.3 lbs) |

| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 | to taste |
| 面包片（炸至金黄） | Bread slices (fried golden) | 4片 | 4 slices |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎鸡肉** — 锅中加橄榄油，中大火将鸡块煎至两面金黄（约8分钟），取出备用。
   *Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken pieces until golden on both sides (about 8 minutes). Set aside.*

2. **炒配料** — 同锅炒蘑菇至出水收干（5分钟），加大蒜炒香，倒入番茄和白葡萄酒，大火煮沸。
   *In the same pan, sauté mushrooms until liquid evaporates (5 minutes). Add garlic, then tomatoes and white wine. Bring to a boil.*

3. **炖煮** — 放回鸡块，加百里香，盖锅小火炖25-30分钟至鸡肉熟透。加盐和胡椒调味。
   *Return chicken to the pan, add thyme, cover, and simmer 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through. Season with salt and pepper.*

4. **煎蛋装盘** — 另起锅煎4个荷包蛋。将鸡肉和酱汁盛入盘中，旁放炸面包片，顶上各放一个荷包蛋。
   *Fry 4 eggs sunny-side-up in a separate pan. Plate the chicken with sauce, place fried bread alongside, and top each piece with a fried egg.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 原版食谱中包含小龙虾，但拿破仑后来迷信地要求去掉小龙虾（一次去掉后他打了败仗，此后又加回）。
  *The original recipe included crayfish, but Napoleon superstitiously had them removed — then after losing a battle, insisted they be added back.*

- 这道菜标志着法国料理从贵族厨房走向战场，证明伟大的菜肴可以在最简陋的条件下诞生。
  *This dish marks French cuisine's journey from aristocratic kitchens to the battlefield, proving great dishes can be born under the humblest conditions.*

- 拿破仑的饮食习惯极不规律，经常15分钟内吃完一餐，但对食物品质要求极高。
  *Napoleon's eating habits were extremely irregular — he often finished a meal in 15 minutes — but his standards for food quality were exacting.*
