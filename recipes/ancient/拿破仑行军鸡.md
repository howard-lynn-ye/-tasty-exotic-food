# 拿破仑行军鸡 | Napoleon's Campaign Chicken (拿破仑时代, ~1800 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 35分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、拿破仑、法国、行军菜、鸡肉

> **📜 历史** — 传说拿破仑在马伦戈战役（1800年）大胜后饥肠辘辘，随军厨师Dunand只找到一只鸡、几个番茄、鸡蛋和小龙虾，用白兰地烹制了这道即兴菜肴。拿破仑大为满意，此后每次战役前都要求吃这道"马伦戈鸡"以求好运。这道菜成为法国军事烹饪史上最著名的传说之一，虽然历史学家对细节存疑，但它完美诠释了战场厨师的创造力。
> **📜 History** — *Legend has it that after Napoleon's victory at the Battle of Marengo (1800), his starving chef Dunand could only scrounge a chicken, a few tomatoes, eggs, and crayfish, cooking them in brandy as an improvised dish. Napoleon was so pleased that he demanded this "Chicken Marengo" before every battle for good luck. It became one of the most famous legends in French military culinary history. Historians debate the details, but the story perfectly illustrates battlefield culinary creativity.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿或鸡胸（切块） | Chicken thighs or breast (cut into pieces) | 1.5磅 | 1.5 lb |
| 番茄（切丁，或罐头番茄） | Tomatoes (diced, or canned) | 2个 | 2 pcs |
| 蘑菇（切片） | Mushrooms (sliced) | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 大蒜（切末） | Garlic (minced) | 3瓣 | 3 cloves |
| 白兰地或白葡萄酒 | Brandy or white wine | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎鸡块** — 鸡肉以盐和胡椒调味。锅中加橄榄油，中大火将鸡块煎至两面金黄约6分钟，取出备用。
   *Season chicken with salt and pepper. Heat olive oil and pan-fry chicken pieces over medium-high heat until golden on both sides, about 6 minutes. Set aside.*

2. **烹制酱汁** — 原锅加蒜末和蘑菇炒2分钟，倒入白兰地点火烧掉酒精（或大火收干），加入番茄丁翻炒。放回鸡块，加半杯水，盖锅中火炖25分钟。
   *In the same pan, sauté garlic and mushrooms 2 minutes. Pour in brandy and flambé (or reduce over high heat). Add diced tomatoes and stir. Return chicken, add 1/2 cup water, cover, and braise over medium heat 25 minutes.*

3. **装盘上桌** — 鸡肉盛出，汤汁大火收浓。将酱汁浇在鸡肉上，配法棍面包或米饭。历史上这道菜还会放一个煎蛋在顶部——可自由选择。
   *Plate the chicken. Reduce the sauce over high heat until thickened. Spoon over the chicken, serve with baguette or rice. Historically, a fried egg was placed on top — add one if you wish.*
