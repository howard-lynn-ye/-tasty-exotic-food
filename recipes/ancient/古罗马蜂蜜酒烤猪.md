# 古罗马蜂蜜酒烤猪 | Roman Honey Wine Roast Pork (~50AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪90分 | 💰~$15/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、罗马帝国

> **📜 历史** — 出自罗马美食家阿皮修斯的《论烹饪》，蜂蜜酒烤猪是罗马宴会的经典主菜。
> **📜 History** — *From Apicius' "De Re Coquinaria," honey wine roast pork was a classic centerpiece of Roman banquets.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪肩肉 | Pork shoulder | 1kg / 2.2lb |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 60ml / 4 tbsp |
| 白葡萄酒 | White wine | 200ml / 0.8 cup |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 月桂叶 | Bay leaves | 3片 / 3 leaves |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
猪肉抹上蜂蜜、鱼露和黑胡椒，腌1小时。

Rub pork with honey, fish sauce, and black pepper; marinate 1 hour.

### 2. 慢烤 | Roast
放入烤盘加白葡萄酒和月桂叶，160°C烤90分钟。

Place in roasting pan with wine and bay leaves, roast at 320°F for 90 minutes.

### 3. 收汁 | Glaze
取出烤猪，汤汁收浓浇在肉上，切片上桌。

Remove pork, reduce pan juices to a glaze, pour over sliced meat and serve.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 腌制时间越长越入味，可提前一晚放冰箱冷藏腌制 | Longer marinating means deeper flavor; marinate overnight in the fridge for best results |
| 烤制中途每30分钟浇一次锅底汤汁，保持肉质湿润 | Baste with pan juices every 30 minutes to keep the meat moist |
| 收汁时加少许醋可平衡蜂蜜的甜腻，更接近古罗马风味 | Add a splash of vinegar when reducing to balance the honey sweetness — closer to the Roman original |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
阿皮修斯的《论烹饪》（De Re Coquinaria）是西方现存最古老的食谱集，成书于公元1世纪的罗马帝国。书中大量使用蜂蜜、鱼露（garum）和胡椒，反映了罗马帝国贸易网络的广阔。蜂蜜酒烤猪是罗马宴会"cena"上的压轴大菜，象征主人的财富与品味。

*Apicius' "De Re Coquinaria" is the oldest surviving Western cookbook, compiled in the 1st century Roman Empire. Its heavy use of honey, fish sauce (garum), and pepper reflects the vast Roman trade network. Honey wine roast pork was the showstopper at Roman dinner parties ("cena"), symbolizing the host's wealth and refined taste.*
