# 古罗马蜂蜜酒烤猪 | Roman Honey Wine Roast Pork (~50AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪90分 | 💰~$15/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、罗马帝国

> **📜 历史** — 出自罗马美食家阿皮修斯的《论烹饪》，蜂蜜酒烤猪是罗马宴会的经典主菜。
> **📜 History** — *From Apicius' "De Re Coquinaria," honey wine roast pork was a classic centerpiece of Roman banquets.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 猪肩肉 | Pork shoulder | 1kg / 2.2lb |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 60ml / 4 tbsp |
| 白葡萄酒 | White wine | 200ml / 0.8 cup |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 月桂叶 | Bay leaves | 3片 / 3 leaves |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
猪肉抹上蜂蜜、鱼露和黑胡椒，腌1小时。Rub pork with honey, fish sauce, and black pepper; marinate 1 hour.

### 2. 慢烤 | Roast
放入烤盘加白葡萄酒和月桂叶，160°C烤90分钟。Place in roasting pan with wine and bay leaves, roast at 320°F for 90 minutes.

### 3. 收汁 | Glaze
取出烤猪，汤汁收浓浇在肉上，切片上桌。Remove pork, reduce pan juices to a glaze, pour over sliced meat and serve.
