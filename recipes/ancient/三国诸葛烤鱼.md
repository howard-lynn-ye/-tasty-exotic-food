# 三国诸葛烤鱼 | Three Kingdoms Grilled Fish (~230AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪30分 | 💰~$12/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、三国

> **📜 历史** — 传说诸葛亮南征时教当地百姓以香料烤鱼，改善军民饮食，后成为川渝烤鱼的雏形。
> **📜 History** — *Legend holds that Zhuge Liang taught locals to grill fish with spices during his southern campaign, later inspiring Sichuan-style grilled fish.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鲈鱼 | Sea bass | 1条 / 1 whole (~600g) |
| 豆瓣酱 | Doubanjiang | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 20g / 4 slices |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 6瓣 / 6 cloves |
| 干辣椒 | Dried chili | 5个 / 5 pcs |
| 葱 | Scallion | 3根 / 3 stalks |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 处理鱼 | Prep Fish
鱼身划刀，抹盐和姜片腌15分钟。

Score the fish, rub with salt and ginger, marinate 15 minutes.

### 2. 烤制 | Grill
炭火或烤箱200°C烤20分钟至表皮焦香。

Grill over charcoal or bake at 400°F for 20 minutes until skin is crispy.

### 3. 浇料 | Sauce
热油爆香蒜、辣椒和豆瓣酱，浇在烤鱼上，撒葱花。

Sizzle garlic, chili, and doubanjiang in hot oil, pour over fish, top with scallions.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 鱼身划刀要深至骨，方便入味且受热均匀 | Score the fish deep to the bone for better flavor penetration and even cooking |
| 烤前鱼身抹干水分，表皮才能烤得焦脆 | Pat the fish completely dry before grilling for crispy skin |
| 浇料时油温要够高，热油泼上才能激发香气 | Oil must be smoking hot when pouring over — the sizzle releases maximum aroma |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
诸葛亮七擒孟获的南征之路穿越云贵川地区，当地丰富的河鱼资源和香料为烤鱼提供了天然条件。传说诸葛亮以此法犒赏将士、安抚民心，后来演变为万州烤鱼、重庆烤鱼等川渝名菜。

*Zhuge Liang's southern campaign to capture Meng Huo seven times passed through Yunnan, Guizhou, and Sichuan, where abundant river fish and spices provided the perfect ingredients. The technique he popularized to reward troops and win local hearts eventually evolved into famous dishes like Wanzhou and Chongqing grilled fish.*
