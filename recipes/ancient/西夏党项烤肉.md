# 西夏党项烤肉 Western Xia Tangut BBQ

> 约公元1100年 | 西夏王朝 | 党项族传统烤肉
> Circa 1100 AD | Western Xia Dynasty | Tangut Nomadic BBQ

---

## 简介 Introduction

西夏（1038-1227）是党项族建立的王朝，地处今宁夏、甘肃一带。党项人以畜牧为主，
烤肉是其最核心的饮食方式。这道烤肉使用粗盐与野生香草腌制，在戈壁篝火上慢烤，
肉质外焦里嫩，带有大漠的粗犷风味。

The Western Xia (1038-1227) was a Tangut dynasty in modern Ningxia and Gansu.
As nomadic herders, roasted meat was central to Tangut cuisine. This BBQ uses
coarse salt and wild steppe herbs, slow-roasted over a Gobi campfire for a
charred exterior and juicy interior with rugged desert flavor.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 羊腿肉 Lamb leg | 800g |
| 粗海盐 Coarse sea salt | 15g |
| 孜然籽 Cumin seeds | 10g |
| 野葱 Wild chives | 一小把 / a handful |
| 干辣椒碎 Dried chili flakes | 5g |
| 羊尾油 Lamb tail fat | 50g |
| 清水 Water | 适量 / as needed |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **备肉 Prep meat**: 羊腿肉切成3cm厚块，用刀背轻拍松弛纤维。
   Cut lamb into 3cm-thick pieces, tenderize with the back of a knife.

2. **腌制 Marinate**: 将粗盐、孜然籽、辣椒碎混合搓揉羊肉，腌制2小时。
   Rub meat with salt, cumin, and chili. Marinate for 2 hours.

3. **串签 Skewer**: 将羊肉与羊尾油交替穿在铁签或木签上。
   Thread lamb and fat alternately onto metal or wooden skewers.

4. **生火 Build fire**: 以胡杨木或干牛粪为燃料，烧至明火熄灭剩余炭火。
   Burn poplar wood or dried dung until flames die down to embers.

5. **慢烤 Slow roast**: 将肉签架于炭火上方15cm处，每5分钟翻转一次，烤约25分钟。
   Place skewers 15cm above coals, turn every 5 minutes, roast ~25 min.

6. **撒料 Season**: 出炉前撒上野葱碎和额外孜然。
   Sprinkle wild chives and extra cumin before serving.
