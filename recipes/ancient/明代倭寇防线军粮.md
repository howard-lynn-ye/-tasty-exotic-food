# 明代倭寇防线军粮 | Ming Anti-Pirate Army Rations (~1550AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/明代倭寇防线军粮.svg" width="500" alt="明代倭寇防线军粮"></p>

> ⏱ 准备15分+烹饪30分 | 💰~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、明代、军旅

> **📜 历史** — 嘉靖年间倭寇侵扰东南沿海，戚继光率军抗倭。军粮讲究便携耐储：光饼（硬面饼）夹腌肉就是当时士兵的行军口粮，后来演变为福建光饼夹肉的地方小吃。
> **📜 History** — *During Jiajing-era Japanese pirate raids on the southeast coast, General Qi Jiguang led resistance forces. Army rations prioritized portability: hard flour biscuits (guang bing) stuffed with cured meat became soldiers' march food, later evolving into Fujian's local guang bing snack.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 300g / 2½ cups |
| 酵母 | Yeast | 3g / 1 tsp |
| 五花肉 | Pork belly | 200g / 7 oz |
| 盐 | Salt | 8g / 1½ tsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame | 15g / 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 3g / ½ tsp |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 和面 | Make Dough
面粉加酵母、盐和温水揉成光滑面团，醒发30分钟。Mix flour, yeast, salt, and warm water into a smooth dough, let rise 30 minutes.

### 2. 烤光饼 | Bake Guang Bing
面团分8份，擀成圆饼，表面刷水撒芝麻，烤箱220°C烤12-15分钟至金黄硬脆。Divide dough into 8 portions, flatten into rounds, brush with water and sprinkle sesame, bake at 425°F for 12-15 minutes until golden and crisp.

### 3. 炒腌肉 | Cook Cured Meat
五花肉切薄片，加酱油和五香粉翻炒至焦香，拌入葱花。Slice pork belly thin, stir-fry with soy sauce and five-spice until caramelized, toss with scallions.

### 4. 夹饼上桌 | Assemble & Serve
光饼横切开口，夹入炒肉，即为行军口粮。便携、耐饿、味浓。Split biscuits open, stuff with stir-fried meat — portable, sustaining, and flavorful, just as soldiers ate on the march.
