# 回鹘馕 | Uyghur Khaganate Naan Bread (回鹘, ~800 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/回鹘馕.svg" width="500" alt="回鹘馕"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、回鹘、面食、烘焙、中亚

> **📜 历史** — 回鹘汗国（744-840）是突厥语系的游牧帝国，控制着蒙古高原和丝绸之路的关键节点。馕是回鹘人从中亚粟特商人处学来的烤饼，经过改良后成为草原和城镇通用的主食。回鹘后来西迁至今新疆，馕的传统在当地延续千年。馕坑（烤炉）的制作技术随回鹘的迁徙传遍整个中亚。
> **📜 History** — *The Uyghur Khaganate (744-840) was a Turkic nomadic empire controlling the Mongolian Plateau and key Silk Road nodes. Naan was a flatbread the Uyghurs adopted from Sogdian merchants of Central Asia, refining it into a staple for steppe and town alike. When the Uyghurs later migrated to present-day Xinjiang, the naan tradition continued for a millennium. The tandoor oven technology spread across Central Asia with the Uyghur migrations.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2.5杯 | 2.5 cups |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 酵母 | Active dry yeast | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 黑芝麻和白芝麻 | Black and white sesame seeds | 各1汤匙 | 1 tbsp each |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面发酵** — 温水中溶入酵母，静置5分钟。加入面粉、盐和油揉成光滑面团，盖湿布发酵40分钟至体积翻倍。
   *Dissolve yeast in warm water and let stand 5 minutes. Add flour, salt, and oil; knead into a smooth dough. Cover with a damp cloth and let rise 40 minutes until doubled.*

2. **成型压花** — 面团擀成直径8寸的圆饼，中心用叉子密集扎孔（防止中心隆起），边缘保留1寸的厚圈。表面刷水，撒满芝麻。
   *Roll dough into an 8-inch round. Prick the center densely with a fork (to prevent puffing), leaving a 1-inch raised rim. Brush with water and cover with sesame seeds.*

3. **高温烤制** — 烤箱预热至450°F（230°C），将馕放在预热的烤盘或披萨石上，烤12-15分钟至表面金黄、边缘酥脆。
   *Preheat oven to 450°F (230°C). Place naan on a preheated baking sheet or pizza stone and bake 12-15 minutes until golden on top and crispy at the edges.*
