# 文艺复兴意面 | Renaissance Pasta with Butter & Cheese (文艺复兴, ~1500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、文艺复兴、面食、素食

> **📜 历史** — 虽然面条的起源众说纷纭，但文艺复兴时期的意大利将干制面条（pasta secca）发展成了一门艺术。巴托洛梅奥·斯卡皮（Bartolomeo Scappi, 1570年）在为教皇庇护五世编写的烹饪巨著中记录了数十种面食做法。当时最受贵族欢迎的做法是将煮熟的面条与大量黄油、帕尔马干酪和肉桂搭配——是的，文艺复兴时期的意面是甜咸混合的，反映了那个时代对香料的狂热追求。
> **📜 History** — *While the origin of noodles is debated, Renaissance Italy elevated dried pasta (pasta secca) into an art form. Bartolomeo Scappi (1570) recorded dozens of pasta preparations in his monumental cookbook for Pope Pius V. The most popular noble preparation was cooked pasta tossed with generous butter, Parmesan cheese, and cinnamon — yes, Renaissance pasta was a sweet-savory affair, reflecting the era's obsession with exotic spices.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 宽面条（tagliatelle） | Tagliatelle pasta | 250g | 8 oz |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 60g | 4 tbsp |
| 帕尔马干酪（磨碎） | Parmesan cheese (grated) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |
| 肉豆蔻（磨碎） | Nutmeg (grated) | 少许 | a pinch |
| 白糖 | White sugar | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮面** — 大锅水煮沸加盐，放入宽面条煮至弹牙（al dente），保留1/2杯面汤后沥干。
   *Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook tagliatelle until al dente, reserve 1/2 cup pasta water, then drain.*

2. **拌酱** — 面条回锅，小火加入黄油、肉桂粉和肉豆蔻，翻拌至黄油完全融化裹满面条，酌情加面汤调整浓稠度。
   *Return pasta to pot over low heat. Add butter, cinnamon, and nutmeg. Toss until butter is fully melted and coats the pasta, adding pasta water as needed for consistency.*

3. **撒干酪上桌** — 关火，拌入帕尔马干酪和少许糖，撒黑胡椒装盘即可。
   *Remove from heat, fold in Parmesan cheese and a touch of sugar, season with black pepper, and serve immediately.*
