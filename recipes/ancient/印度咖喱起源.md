# 印度咖喱起源 | Ancient Indian Curry (古印度, ~2500 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 35分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古印度、咖喱、素食可选

> **📜 历史** — 咖喱的历史可追溯至印度河文明（哈拉帕文明）。考古学家在4500年前的印度遗址中发现了姜、姜黄和大蒜的残留物，证实古印度人已开始使用复合香料烹饪。"咖喱"一词源自泰米尔语"kari"（酱汁）。古印度阿育吠陀医学体系认为香料具有药用价值——姜黄消炎、小茴香助消化、姜暖胃——因此咖喱既是美食也是药膳。
> **📜 History** — *Curry's history traces back to the Indus Valley (Harappan) civilization. Archaeologists found residues of ginger, turmeric, and garlic at Indian sites dating back 4,500 years, confirming that ancient Indians already used compound spice blends for cooking. "Curry" derives from the Tamil word "kari" (sauce). The Ayurvedic medical system attributed medicinal properties to spices — turmeric as anti-inflammatory, cumin for digestion, ginger for warming the stomach — making curry both cuisine and medicine.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炒香料底** — 锅中加油/酥油，中火炒洋葱至深金色（约8-10分钟，不要着急）。加入蒜和姜炒1分钟，再加入姜黄、小茴香、芫荽和辣椒粉，炒30秒至香气四溢。
   *Heat oil/ghee in a pot. Sauté onions over medium heat until deep golden (about 8-10 minutes, be patient). Add garlic and ginger, cook 1 minute. Add turmeric, cumin, coriander, and chili powder; stir 30 seconds until fragrant.*

2. **炖煮主料** — 加入鸡肉块翻炒3分钟使其裹上香料（素食版直接加鹰嘴豆），倒入椰奶，加盐调味。
   *Add chicken and stir 3 minutes to coat with spices (for vegan version, add chickpeas directly). Pour in coconut milk and season with salt.*

3. **慢炖** — 大火煮沸后转小火，盖上盖子炖25-30分钟至鸡肉熟透、酱汁浓稠。
   *Bring to a boil, reduce to low, cover, and simmer 25-30 minutes until chicken is cooked through and sauce has thickened.*

4. **调味上桌** — 撒入加拉姆马萨拉搅匀，装盘后撒新鲜香菜，配米饭或馕饼食用。
   *Stir in garam masala. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with rice or naan bread.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 黑胡椒在古印度被称为"黑金"，是全球贸易的驱动力，罗马帝国每年花费巨资从印度进口。
  *Black pepper was called "black gold" in ancient India and drove global trade — the Roman Empire spent vast sums importing it annually.*

- 椰奶在此配方中代表南印度风格；北印度古代咖喱更可能使用酸奶或水作为基底。
  *Coconut milk represents a South Indian style here; ancient North Indian curries more likely used yogurt or water as the base.*

- 加拉姆马萨拉（"温暖的混合香料"）的配方因地区和家庭而异，没有统一标准。
  *Garam masala ("warm spice blend") varies by region and family — there is no single standard recipe.*
