# 印加帝国烤豚鼠 | Inca Roasted Cuy

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 印加帝国 Inca Empire (~1400AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 安第斯高原（今秘鲁） Andean Highlands (modern Peru)
> **类型 Type:** 节庆烤肉 Festival Roast

---

## 典故 Historical Background

豚鼠（cuy）是印加文明最重要的蛋白质来源之一，在安第斯地区已有数千年的驯养历史。印加人将豚鼠视为神圣动物，在太阳神因蒂的祭典上献祭烤豚鼠。印加贵族的家中，豚鼠在厨房地面自由活动，靠食物残渣为生，既是宠物也是食物。烤豚鼠至今仍是秘鲁、厄瓜多尔和玻利维亚的传统美食。

Guinea pig (cuy) was one of the most important protein sources in Inca civilization, domesticated for thousands of years in the Andes. The Inca considered guinea pigs sacred, offering roasted cuy at festivals honoring the sun god Inti. In Inca noble homes, guinea pigs roamed kitchen floors freely, feeding on scraps — both pets and food. Roasted cuy remains a traditional delicacy in Peru, Ecuador, and Bolivia today.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **处理豚鼠 Prepare cuy:** 豚鼠去内脏洗净，以沸水烫后去毛，内外洗净晾干。Gut and clean the cuy, scald with boiling water to remove fur, wash inside and out, pat dry.
2. **制腌料 Make marinade:** 大蒜捣成泥，与黄辣椒酱、小茴香、盐和切碎的黑薄荷混合成浓稠腌料。Pound garlic into paste, mix with aji amarillo, cumin, salt, and chopped huacatay into a thick marinade.
3. **腌渍 Marinate:** 将腌料均匀涂抹豚鼠内外，静置至少两个时辰使之入味。Rub marinade evenly inside and out, rest for at least 2 hours to absorb flavor.
4. **架烤 Spit roast:** 以木棍从口至尾贯穿固定，架于炭火上方，缓慢转烤约一个时辰。Skewer from mouth to tail on a wooden spit, set over charcoal, slowly rotate and roast for about 1 hour.
5. **煮配菜 Cook sides:** 土豆连皮在余烬中焖烤至软糯，玉米棒直接在炭火上烤至焦香。Roast potatoes with skin in the embers until soft, grill corn directly over coals until charred.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 整只豚鼠置于陶盘中央，四周围以烤土豆与玉米棒，配黄辣椒蘸酱食用。Place the whole cuy on a clay platter, surround with roasted potatoes and corn, serve with aji dipping sauce.

## 备注 Notes

- 印加人通过观察豚鼠的内脏进行占卜，烤豚鼠同时具有宗教与饮食的双重意义。The Inca practiced divination by examining cuy entrails — roasted cuy held both religious and culinary significance.
- 烤好的豚鼠皮酥肉嫩，风味介于兔肉与鸡肉之间。Properly roasted cuy has crispy skin and tender meat, tasting somewhere between rabbit and chicken.
