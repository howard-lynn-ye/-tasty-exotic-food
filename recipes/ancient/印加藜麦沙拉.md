# 印加藜麦沙拉 | Inca Quinoa Salad (~1400AD)

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪20分 | 💰~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、印加帝国

> **📜 历史** — 印加人称藜麦为"粮食之母"，是安第斯山区数千年的主粮，印加士兵行军时随身携带。
> **📜 History** — *The Inca called quinoa "mother of all grains"; it sustained Andean civilizations for millennia and was carried by Inca soldiers on campaigns.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 藜麦 | Quinoa | 200g / 1 cup |
| 番茄 | Tomato | 2个 / 2 |
| 牛油果 | Avocado | 1个 / 1 |
| 玉米粒 | Corn kernels | 100g / 0.5 cup |
| 青柠 | Lime | 1个 / 1 |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮藜麦 | Cook Quinoa
藜麦洗净加两倍水煮15分钟，沥干放凉。Rinse quinoa, cook with double water for 15 minutes, drain and cool.

### 2. 备料 | Prep Vegetables
番茄和牛油果切丁，玉米粒煮熟。Dice tomato and avocado, cook corn kernels.

### 3. 拌匀 | Toss
所有食材混合，挤入青柠汁加盐拌匀即可。Combine all ingredients, squeeze in lime juice, season with salt, and toss.
