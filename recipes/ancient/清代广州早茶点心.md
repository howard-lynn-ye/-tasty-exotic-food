# 清代广州早茶点心 | Qing Guangzhou Dim Sum

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 清朝 Qing Dynasty (~1800AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 广州 Guangzhou (Canton)
> **类型 Type:** 点心/茶食 Dim Sum / Tea Snacks

---

## 典故 Historical Background

清代广州作为唯一对外通商口岸，茶楼文化空前繁荣。"一盅两件"（一壶茶配两笼点心）成为广州人的生活方式。早茶点心融合了岭南本地风味与南洋食材，虾饺、烧卖、叉烧包是最具代表性的"三大件"。茶楼也是商人洽谈生意、文人雅聚的重要场所。

As Qing-era Guangzhou was China's sole foreign trade port, teahouse culture flourished. "One pot, two baskets" (a pot of tea and two baskets of dim sum) became the Cantonese way of life. Dim sum blended Lingnan local flavors with Southeast Asian ingredients — shrimp dumplings, siu mai, and char siu bao were the iconic "big three." Teahouses also served as venues for business deals and literati gatherings.

## 食材 Ingredients（虾饺 Har Gow）

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 澄粉 Wheat starch | 1杯 1 cup |
| 生粉 Tapioca starch | 1/4杯 1/4 cup |
| 鲜虾仁 Fresh shrimp | 200克 200g |
| 猪肥膘 Pork back fat | 30克 30g |
| 冬笋丁 Diced bamboo shoot | 50克 50g |
| 麻油 Sesame oil | 1小匙 1 tsp |
| 白胡椒粉 White pepper | 少许 A pinch |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 To taste |

---

## 做法 Preparation

1. **制皮 Make wrapper:** 澄粉与生粉混合，冲入滚水迅速搅拌成团，趁热揉匀，加少许猪油增加延展性。Mix wheat starch and tapioca starch, pour in boiling water, stir quickly into a dough, knead while hot, add a little lard for pliability.
2. **制馅 Make filling:** 鲜虾去壳去肠线，部分切碎部分保留整只；猪肥膘切细丁；冬笋焯水切丁。Shell and devein shrimp — chop some, keep some whole; dice pork fat finely; blanch and dice bamboo shoots.
3. **调馅 Season filling:** 虾仁、肥膘、冬笋拌匀，加盐、白胡椒、麻油调味，搅拌至起胶。Mix shrimp, fat, bamboo shoots; season with salt, pepper, sesame oil, stir until sticky.
4. **包制 Wrap:** 面团搓条切小剂，用刀面压成薄圆皮，包入馅料，捏出十三褶。Roll dough into a rope, cut small pieces, press into thin rounds with a cleaver, fill and pleat into 13 folds.
5. **蒸制 Steam:** 竹蒸笼垫白菜叶，摆入虾饺，大火猛蒸五至六分钟至皮呈透明状。Line bamboo steamer with cabbage leaves, arrange dumplings, steam on high heat for 5-6 minutes until wrappers turn translucent.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 连笼上桌，趁热蘸少许生抽或辣椒酱食用。Serve in the steamer, eat hot with a dip of light soy sauce or chili sauce.

## 备注 Notes

- 正宗虾饺要求皮薄如纸能透见馅料，褶子整齐均匀。Authentic har gow demands paper-thin wrappers showing the filling, with neat and even pleats.
- 清代茶楼点心由推车女侍逐桌叫卖，即今日推车仔传统之来源。Qing teahouse dim sum was sold table-to-table from pushcarts — the origin of today's cart service tradition.
