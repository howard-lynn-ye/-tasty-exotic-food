# 古代秘鲁烤豚鼠 | Ancient Peruvian Cuy (~1200AD)

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪60分 | 💰~$20/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、秘鲁

> **📜 历史** — 安第斯高原的印加先民在五千年前即开始驯养豚鼠（cuy），用石板烤制是祭祀和节庆的核心美食，至今仍是秘鲁国菜。
> **📜 History** — *Inca ancestors domesticated guinea pigs (cuy) over 5,000 years ago on the Andean plateau; stone-roasted cuy was central to rituals and feasts, and remains a Peruvian national dish today.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 豚鼠 | Guinea pig (cuy) | 1只 / 1 whole (~800g) |
| 大蒜 | Garlic | 6瓣 / 6 cloves |
| 孜然粉 | Cumin | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 安第斯辣椒酱 | Aji panca paste | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 8g / 1½ tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 土豆 | Potato | 4个 / 4 small |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 腌制 | Marinate
蒜泥、孜然、辣椒酱和盐混合，涂满豚鼠内外，腌制至少30分钟。Mix garlic paste, cumin, aji panca, and salt; rub inside and out, marinate at least 30 minutes.

### 2. 烤制 | Roast
烤箱200°C，豚鼠放在架上，刷油，烤50-60分钟至皮酥脆金黄，中途翻面一次。Roast at 400°F on a rack, brush with oil, cook 50-60 minutes until skin is crispy and golden, flip once halfway.

### 3. 配菜 | Side Dish
土豆整个煮熟，或切块与豚鼠同烤最后20分钟。Boil potatoes whole, or add chunks to roasting pan for the last 20 minutes.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
整只上桌，配辣椒蘸酱和煮土豆，按传统撕食。Serve whole with chili dipping sauce and boiled potatoes; tear meat by hand, as is traditional.
