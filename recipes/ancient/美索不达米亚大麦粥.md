# 美索不达米亚大麦粥 Mesopotamian Barley Porridge

> **时代 Era:** 古苏美尔 Ancient Sumer · 约公元前2500年 (~2500 BC)
> **地区 Region:** 两河流域 Mesopotamia (modern Iraq)
> **类型 Type:** 主食 Staple / 粥品 Porridge
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

这是人类最早有文字记录的食谱之一，源自耶鲁大学收藏的苏美尔泥板。大麦是两河流域最重要的粮食作物，古人将其与洋葱、油脂一同熬煮，形成浓稠的咸粥，是劳动者的日常饮食。

This is one of the earliest known written recipes, originating from Sumerian clay tablets held at Yale University. Barley was the most important cereal crop of Mesopotamia. Ancient cooks simmered it with onions and fat into a thick savory porridge — daily sustenance for laborers.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 珍珠大麦 Pearl barley | 200g |
| 黄洋葱 Yellow onion (切丁 diced) | 1 个 |
| 羊尾油或黄油 Lamb tail fat or butter | 30g |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 to taste |
| 水 Water | 800ml |
| 新鲜香菜 Fresh cilantro (可选 optional) | 一小把 a handful |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 炒香 Sauté
中火融化羊尾油，下洋葱丁翻炒至金黄半透明，约5分钟。
Melt the lamb fat over medium heat. Add diced onion and sauté until golden and translucent, about 5 minutes.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 熬煮 Simmer
加入大麦和水，大火煮沸后转小火，加盖慢炖40-50分钟，期间偶尔搅拌，直到大麦完全软烂、粥体浓稠。
Add barley and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat. Cover and simmer 40-50 minutes, stirring occasionally, until barley is fully tender and porridge is thick.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 调味 Season
加盐调味，盛入碗中，撒上香菜碎即可食用。
Season with salt, ladle into bowls, and garnish with chopped cilantro.
