# 古代阿拉伯蜂蜜饼 | Arabian Honey Cake (阿拉伯半岛, ~700 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、阿拉伯、甜品、蜂蜜

> **📜 历史** — 伊斯兰黄金时代（约8-14世纪）的阿拉伯世界是全球美食最先进的文明之一。蜂蜜在《古兰经》中被描述为"治愈的饮品"，是阿拉伯甜品的灵魂。这道蜂蜜饼（Basbousa的前身）出现在10世纪的巴格达食谱集《Kitab al-Tabikh》中，用粗麦粉和蜂蜜制成，是哈里发宫廷和普通民众共同喜爱的甜食。
> **📜 History** — *The Arab world during the Islamic Golden Age (8th-14th centuries) was one of the most gastronomically advanced civilizations. Honey is described in the Quran as "a drink of healing" and is the soul of Arabic sweets. This honey cake (precursor to basbousa) appears in the 10th-century Baghdad cookbook "Kitab al-Tabikh," made with semolina and honey — a sweet loved by caliphs and commoners alike.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **制糖浆** — 蜂蜜和水小火煮5分钟，加柠檬汁和玫瑰水，冷却备用。
   *Simmer honey and water over low heat for 5 minutes. Add lemon juice and rose water. Cool completely.*

2. **混合面糊** — 粗麦粉、酸奶、1/2杯蜂蜜、融化黄油和泡打粉混合搅拌均匀，静置15分钟让粗麦粉吸收水分。
   *Mix semolina, yogurt, 1/2 cup honey, melted butter, and baking powder. Let rest 15 minutes so semolina absorbs moisture.*

3. **烤制** — 面糊倒入抹了油的方形烤盘，抹平表面，用刀切出菱形格子，每块中间按入一颗杏仁。175°C（350°F）烤25-30分钟至金黄。
   *Pour batter into a greased square pan, smooth the top, cut diamond shapes, and press an almond into each piece. Bake at 175°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes until golden.*

4. **浇糖浆** — 出炉后立刻将冷糖浆均匀浇在热蛋糕上，静置30分钟至吸收。
   *Immediately pour cold syrup evenly over hot cake. Let stand 30 minutes to absorb.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 《Kitab al-Tabikh》是世界上最早的系统性食谱集之一，记录了600多道菜的详细做法。
  *"Kitab al-Tabikh" is one of the world's earliest systematic cookbooks, recording detailed instructions for over 600 dishes.*

- 阿拉伯世界的制糖技术在十字军东征后传入欧洲，但在此之前蜂蜜是唯一的甜味来源。
  *Arab sugar-refining techniques reached Europe after the Crusades; before that, honey was the sole sweetener.*

- 阿拉伯人将蜂蜜饼切成菱形而非方形，菱形在伊斯兰几何美学中象征着无限与永恒。
  *Arabs cut honey cake in diamonds rather than squares — the diamond shape symbolizes infinity and eternity in Islamic geometric aesthetics.*
