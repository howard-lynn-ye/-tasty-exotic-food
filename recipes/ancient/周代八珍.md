# 周代八珍 | Zhou Eight Treasures Stew (~800BC)

> ⏱ 准备45分+烹饪120分 | 💰~$30/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、周朝

> **📜 历史** — 《周礼》记载"八珍"为天子御膳，包含淳熬、淳母等八道珍馐，是中国最早的宫廷菜系。
> **📜 History** — *The "Eight Treasures" recorded in the Rites of Zhou were imperial dishes for the Son of Heaven, representing China's earliest documented court cuisine.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛里脊 | Beef tenderloin | 500g / 1.1lb |
| 粳米 | Short-grain rice | 200g / 1 cup |
| 动物油脂 | Animal fat | 50g / 3 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 30g / 1oz |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |
| 黍米 | Millet | 100g / 0.5 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮饭 | Cook Rice
粳米和黍米混合蒸熟，浇上热油脂拌匀。Steam rice and millet together, drizzle with hot animal fat and mix well.

### 2. 炖肉 | Stew Meat
牛肉切块加姜片慢炖2小时至酥烂。Cut beef into chunks, add ginger slices, slow-stew for 2 hours until tender.

### 3. 合盛 | Assemble
将炖肉浇在饭上，撒少许盐调味即成。Ladle stewed meat over rice, season with salt, and serve.
