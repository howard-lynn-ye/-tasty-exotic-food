# 周代八珍 | Zhou Eight Treasures Stew (~800BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/周代八珍.svg" width="500" alt="周代八珍"></p>

> ⏱ 准备45分+烹饪120分 | 💰~$30/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、周朝

> **📜 历史** — 《周礼》记载"八珍"为天子御膳，包含淳熬、淳母等八道珍馐，是中国最早的宫廷菜系。
> **📜 History** — *The "Eight Treasures" recorded in the Rites of Zhou were imperial dishes for the Son of Heaven, representing China's earliest documented court cuisine.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛里脊 | Beef tenderloin | 500g / 1.1lb |
| 粳米 | Short-grain rice | 200g / 1 cup |
| 动物油脂 | Animal fat | 50g / 3 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 30g / 1oz |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |
| 黍米 | Millet | 100g / 0.5 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮饭 | Cook Rice

粳米和黍米混合蒸熟，浇上热油脂拌匀。
Steam rice and millet together, drizzle with hot animal fat and mix well.

### 2. 炖肉 | Stew Meat

牛肉切块加姜片慢炖2小时至酥烂。
Cut beef into chunks, add ginger slices, slow-stew for 2 hours until tender.

### 3. 合盛 | Assemble

将炖肉浇在饭上，撒少许盐调味即成。
Ladle stewed meat over rice, season with salt, and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 油脂要烧至冒烟后再浇饭，才能激发谷物香气 | Heat fat until smoking before drizzling over rice to unlock the grain's aroma |
| 2 | 炖肉时保持微沸而非大火滚煮，肉质更酥嫩 | Keep the stew at a gentle simmer rather than a rolling boil for more tender meat |
| 3 | 黍米需提前浸泡1小时，蒸出来口感更糯 | Soak millet for 1 hour beforehand so it steams up softer and stickier |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

周代八珍是中国最早有文字记载的宫廷菜谱体系，反映了西周礼乐制度下饮食与等级的紧密关联。"淳熬"是将肉酱浇于稻米饭之上，淋以油脂，代表了最原始的"浇头饭"形式，对后世盖浇饭产生了深远影响。

The Zhou Eight Treasures represent China's earliest documented court recipe system, reflecting the tight link between cuisine and social hierarchy under Western Zhou ritual culture. "Chun Ao" — meat sauce ladled over rice and drenched in fat — is the most primitive form of "topping rice," profoundly influencing later Chinese rice-bowl dishes.
