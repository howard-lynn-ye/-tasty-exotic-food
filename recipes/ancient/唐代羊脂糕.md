# 唐代羊脂糕 Tang Dynasty Lamb Fat Cake

> **时代 Era:** 唐朝 Tang Dynasty · 约公元750年 (~750 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 长安 Chang'an (modern Xi'an)
> **类型 Type:** 糕点 Pastry / 甜食 Dessert
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

唐代宫廷宴席极尽奢华，羊脂糕是贵族阶层喜爱的精致点心。以羊脂替代现代黄油制作糕饼，是西域胡风饮食文化深刻影响唐代饮食的缩影。《烧尾宴食单》中可窥见类似糕点的记载。

The Tang Dynasty court banquets were lavish affairs. Lamb fat cake was a refined pastry favored by the aristocracy. Using lamb fat in place of modern butter in pastry-making reflects the deep influence of Central Asian (Hu) food culture on Tang cuisine. Similar pastries appear in the famous "Shaowei Banquet" menu.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 250g |
| 羊脂（或黄油）Lamb fat (or butter) | 80g |
| 蜂蜜 Honey | 60ml |
| 红枣泥 Red date paste | 100g |
| 芝麻 Sesame seeds | 20g |
| 温水 Warm water | 60ml |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 和面 Make dough
将面粉堆成火山口形状，中间放入软化的羊脂和蜂蜜，慢慢加入温水，揉成光滑柔软的面团，盖湿布醒30分钟。
Mound the flour and make a well in the center. Add softened lamb fat and honey. Gradually add warm water and knead into a smooth, soft dough. Cover with a damp cloth and rest 30 minutes.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 包馅成型 Fill and shape
面团分成12等份，按扁后包入红枣泥馅，收口朝下按成圆饼状（厚约1.5厘米），表面刷蜂蜜水、撒芝麻。
Divide dough into 12 portions. Flatten each, place date paste in the center, seal seam-side down, and press into a round disc about 1.5 cm thick. Brush with honey-water and sprinkle sesame seeds.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 烤制 Bake
烤箱预热至180°C，将糕饼放在铺了烘焙纸的烤盘上，烤20-25分钟至表面金黄。也可用平底锅小火两面煎至金黄。
Preheat oven to 180°C. Place cakes on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Bake 20-25 minutes until golden. Alternatively, pan-fry over low heat until golden on both sides.
