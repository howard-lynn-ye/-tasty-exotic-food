# 安达卢西亚冷汤 Andalusian Gazpacho

> **时代 Era:** 摩尔人统治时期 Moorish Al-Andalus · 约公元800年 (~800 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 安达卢西亚 Andalusia (modern southern Spain)
> **类型 Type:** 冷汤 Cold Soup
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

现代西班牙冷汤以番茄为主角，但番茄要到16世纪才从美洲传入。摩尔人统治伊比利亚时期的原始冷汤（ajoblanco）是用陈面包、杏仁、大蒜和橄榄油捣碎混合而成的白色冷汤，是农民在炎热夏季的救命口粮。

Modern gazpacho stars the tomato, but tomatoes only arrived from the Americas in the 16th century. The original Moorish-era cold soup (ajoblanco) was a white blend of stale bread, almonds, garlic, and olive oil — a lifesaving meal for farmers in the scorching Andalusian summers.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 去皮杏仁 Blanched almonds | 150g |
| 陈面包（去皮）Stale white bread (crust removed) | 100g |
| 大蒜 Garlic | 2 瓣 cloves |
| 特级初榨橄榄油 Extra virgin olive oil | 60ml |
| 白葡萄酒醋 White wine vinegar | 2 汤匙 tbsp |
| 冰水 Ice water | 400ml |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 浸泡 Soak
面包撕碎放入碗中，加少许水浸泡10分钟至软。杏仁用开水浸泡5分钟，沥干。
Tear bread into pieces and soak in a little water for 10 minutes until soft. Blanch almonds in hot water for 5 minutes, then drain.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 搅打 Blend
将杏仁、泡软的面包、大蒜放入搅拌机，先打成细腻的糊状。然后缓缓倒入橄榄油和冰水，继续搅打至顺滑如奶。加入醋和盐调味。
Place almonds, soaked bread, and garlic in a blender. Process until smooth paste forms. Slowly pour in olive oil and ice water while blending until silky. Season with vinegar and salt.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 冷藏上桌 Chill and serve
倒入碗中，冷藏至少1小时。上桌时可搭配几粒葡萄或薄切黄瓜片作为装饰。
Pour into a bowl and refrigerate at least 1 hour. Serve topped with a few grapes or thin cucumber slices as garnish.
