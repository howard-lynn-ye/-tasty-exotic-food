# 古代匈牙利红椒鸡 | Ancient Hungarian Paprika Chicken (~1500AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古代匈牙利红椒鸡.svg" width="500" alt="古代匈牙利红椒鸡"></p>

> ⏱ 准备15分+烹饪45分 | 💰~$12/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、匈牙利

> **📜 历史** — 奥斯曼帝国将辣椒带入匈牙利后，马扎尔牧民开始用红椒粉炖鸡，这道菜逐渐成为匈牙利国菜"鸡肉paprikash"的原型。
> **📜 History** — *After the Ottomans introduced peppers to Hungary, Magyar herders began stewing chicken with paprika — the ancestor of the national dish Chicken Paprikash.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿 | Chicken thighs | 4个 / 4 pcs (~600g) |
| 匈牙利红椒粉 | Hungarian paprika | 20g / 3 tbsp |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 2个 / 2 medium |
| 酸奶油 | Sour cream | 100ml / ½ cup |
| 猪油/黄油 | Lard / Butter | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 大蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 炒洋葱 | Saut Onion
猪油融化，加洋葱丝炒至金黄透明，约10分钟。Melt lard, saut sliced onions until golden, about 10 minutes.

### 2. 加红椒粉 | Add Paprika
离火撒入红椒粉，快速搅拌以免烧焦，再放蒜末。Remove from heat, stir in paprika quickly to prevent burning, add minced garlic.

### 3. 炖鸡 | Braise Chicken
放入鸡腿，加少量水，盖盖小火炖35分钟至鸡肉熟透。Add chicken thighs, pour in a little water, cover and simmer 35 minutes until cooked through.

### 4. 收汁 | Finish
离火拌入酸奶油，调盐，搭配手擀面或面包。Off heat, stir in sour cream, season with salt, serve with egg noodles or bread.
