# 中世纪十字军炖肉 | Crusader Stew (中世纪, ~1100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 50分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、中世纪、十字军、炖肉、中东

> **📜 历史** — 十字军东征（1096-1291）期间，欧洲骑士在中东和圣地接触到了香料、杏干和鹰嘴豆等全新食材。十字军的行军炖肉融合了欧洲的炖煮技术与中东的香料调味，成为一道独特的"战争融合菜"。返回欧洲的十字军战士将中东烹饪习惯带回家乡，深刻改变了中世纪欧洲的饮食面貌，肉桂、孜然等香料从此进入欧洲厨房。
> **📜 History** — *During the Crusades (1096-1291), European knights in the Middle East and Holy Land encountered spices, dried apricots, and chickpeas for the first time. Crusader stew blended European braising techniques with Middle Eastern spice profiles, creating a unique "wartime fusion dish." Returning Crusaders brought these culinary habits home, profoundly reshaping medieval European food culture. Cinnamon, cumin, and other spices entered European kitchens permanently.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 牛肩肉（切块） | Beef chuck (cubed) | 1.5磅 | 1.5 lb |
| 鹰嘴豆（罐头，沥干） | Chickpeas (canned, drained) | 1罐 | 1 can |
| 杏干（切半） | Dried apricots (halved) | 1/3杯 | 1/3 cup |
| 洋葱（切丁） | Onion (diced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 孜然粉 | Ground cumin | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎肉炒香** — 牛肉块以盐和胡椒调味。锅中加油，大火将牛肉每面煎至焦褐后取出。原锅炒洋葱至透明，加孜然和肉桂炒出香味。
   *Season beef with salt and pepper. Heat oil in a pot and sear beef on all sides until browned. Remove. In the same pot, sauté onion until translucent, then add cumin and cinnamon and stir until fragrant.*

2. **慢炖** — 牛肉放回锅中，加入鹰嘴豆、杏干和2杯水或牛肉汤。大火煮开后转小火盖锅炖45-50分钟，至牛肉用叉子轻松插入。
   *Return beef to the pot. Add chickpeas, apricots, and 2 cups water or beef broth. Bring to a boil, reduce to low heat, cover and braise 45-50 minutes until beef is fork-tender.*

3. **上桌** — 调盐和胡椒，盛入深盘。配粗面包或米饭食用。杏干的甜和鹰嘴豆的粉糯与牛肉的浓郁形成丰富层次。
   *Adjust salt and pepper. Serve in a deep dish alongside crusty bread or rice. The sweetness of apricots, creaminess of chickpeas, and richness of beef create complex layers of flavor.*
