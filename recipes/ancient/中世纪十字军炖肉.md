# 中世纪十字军炖肉 | Crusader Lamb Stew (黎凡特, ~1100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 2小时 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、中世纪、羊肉、炖菜

> **📜 历史** — 11世纪的十字军东征是欧洲与中东文化碰撞的缩影，饮食也不例外。来自法兰西和英格兰的骑士在黎凡特发现了杏干、石榴和中东香料，将其融入传统的欧洲炖肉中。这道菜代表了十字军国家（如耶路撒冷王国）中形成的独特"混血"料理——既非纯粹的欧洲菜，也非纯粹的阿拉伯菜。
> **📜 History** — *The 11th-century Crusades were a microcosm of European-Middle Eastern cultural collision, and cuisine was no exception. Knights from France and England discovered dried apricots, pomegranates, and Middle Eastern spices in the Levant, blending them into traditional European stews. This dish represents the unique "hybrid" cuisine of the Crusader states like the Kingdom of Jerusalem — neither purely European nor purely Arab.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 羊腿肉（切大块） | Leg of lamb (large chunks) | 700克 | 1.5 lbs |
| 杏干（切半） | Dried apricots (halved) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 洋葱（切块） | Onion (chunked) | 2个 | 2 |
| 大蒜（切碎） | Garlic (minced) | 4瓣 | 4 cloves |
| 肉桂棒 | Cinnamon stick | 1根 | 1 |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 芫荽粉 | Ground coriander | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 红酒醋 | Red wine vinegar | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 羊肉高汤或水 | Lamb stock or water | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 石榴籽（装饰） | Pomegranate seeds (garnish) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎羊肉** — 锅中加橄榄油，大火将羊肉块煎至四面焦香（约6分钟），取出备用。
   *Heat olive oil in a Dutch oven over high heat. Sear lamb on all sides until deeply browned (about 6 minutes). Set aside.*

2. **炒底料** — 同锅中火炒洋葱至金黄（5分钟），加入大蒜、小茴香、芫荽和肉桂棒，炒香1分钟。
   *In the same pot, sauté onion over medium heat until golden (5 minutes). Add garlic, cumin, coriander, and cinnamon stick; stir 1 minute.*

3. **炖煮** — 放回羊肉，加高汤、杏干、蜂蜜和红酒醋，大火煮沸后转小火，盖锅炖1.5-2小时至羊肉软烂。
   *Return lamb, add stock, apricots, honey, and vinegar. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer 1.5-2 hours until lamb is tender.*

4. **装盘** — 取出肉桂棒，加盐调味。盛入深盘，撒上石榴籽，搭配扁面包或古斯古斯。
   *Remove cinnamon stick and season with salt. Ladle into bowls, scatter pomegranate seeds on top. Serve with flatbread or couscous.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 十字军骑士第一次品尝糖是在黎凡特，他们将制糖术和蔗糖带回欧洲，改变了西方饮食史。
  *Crusader knights first tasted sugar in the Levant; they brought sugar-making techniques and cane sugar back to Europe, changing Western culinary history.*

- 杏干在阿拉伯语中叫"mishmish"，十字军发现它可以长途保存，成为行军粮的重要组成部分。
  *Dried apricots, called "mishmish" in Arabic, were adopted by Crusaders as campaign rations because they stored well for long marches.*

- 十字军国家的混合菜肴深刻影响了后来的法国料理，普罗旺斯菜中大量使用的橄榄油和香草就是这一遗产。
  *The hybrid cuisine of Crusader states deeply influenced later French cooking — Provençal cuisine's heavy use of olive oil and herbs is part of this legacy.*
