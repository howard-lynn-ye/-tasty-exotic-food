# 古代墨西哥玉米饼 | Ancient Mexican Tortilla (中美洲, ~500 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古代墨西哥玉米饼.svg" width="500" alt="古代墨西哥玉米饼"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 静置30分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、墨西哥、面饼、素食

> **📜 历史** — 玉米饼（Tortilla）是美洲最古老的食物之一，中美洲原住民通过"碱化处理"（Nixtamalization）将玉米浸泡在石灰水中，使其更易研磨且营养更丰富。这一发明可追溯至公元前1500年，到公元500年左右，玉米饼已是玛雅和阿兹特克文明的绝对主食。阿兹特克语中"tlaxcalli"意为"煮熟的玉米面饼"，西班牙殖民者将其改称"tortilla"（小蛋糕）。
> **📜 History** — *Tortilla is one of the Americas' oldest foods. Mesoamerican natives invented nixtamalization — soaking corn in limewater — making it easier to grind and more nutritious. This technique dates to 1500 BC; by 500 AD, tortillas were the absolute staple of Maya and Aztec civilizations. The Aztec word "tlaxcalli" means "cooked corn flatbread"; Spanish colonizers renamed it "tortilla" (little cake).*

| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面** — 玉米粉和盐混合，逐渐加入温水，揉成柔软不粘手的面团（如耳垂般柔软）。盖湿布静置30分钟。
   *Mix masa harina and salt. Gradually add warm water, kneading into a soft, non-sticky dough (like an earlobe). Cover with a damp cloth and rest 30 minutes.*

2. **分团** — 取高尔夫球大小的面团搓圆，用玉米饼压制器（tortilla press）或两张保鲜膜之间用平底锅压成薄圆饼。
   *Pinch golf ball-sized portions and roll into balls. Flatten with a tortilla press or between two sheets of plastic wrap using a flat pan.*

3. **烙制** — 铸铁平底锅或comal大火预热（不加油），放上玉米饼，烙约45秒至底面出现棕色斑点，翻面再烙45秒。饼会略微鼓起。
   *Heat a cast iron skillet or comal over high heat (no oil). Cook tortilla about 45 seconds until brown spots appear, flip, and cook 45 seconds more. It should puff slightly.*

4. **保温** — 烙好的饼立即放入干净布巾中包裹保温，堆叠即可。趁热食用最佳。
   *Immediately wrap cooked tortillas in a clean towel to stay warm. Stack as you go. Best eaten hot.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 碱化处理（Nixtamalization）释放了玉米中被束缚的烟酸（维生素B3），没有这一步骤，以玉米为主食的人群会患上糙皮病。
  *Nixtamalization releases bound niacin (vitamin B3) in corn; without this step, populations relying on corn develop pellagra.*

- 阿兹特克妇女每天凌晨3-4点起床磨玉米、做饼，这是她们最重要的日常劳动。
  *Aztec women rose at 3-4 AM daily to grind corn and make tortillas — their most important daily labor.*

- 西班牙殖民者最初蔑视玉米饼，称其为"野蛮人的面包"，但最终也被征服——如今Tortilla是全球最畅销的面饼。
  *Spanish colonizers initially scorned tortillas as "savage bread" but were eventually conquered by them — today tortillas are the world's best-selling flatbread.*
