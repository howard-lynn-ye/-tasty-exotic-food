# 古代安纳托利亚炖肉 | Ancient Anatolian Stew (安纳托利亚, ~2000 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 1.5小时 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、安纳托利亚、炖煮、牛肉

> **📜 历史** — 安纳托利亚是人类最早从事农业的地区之一，哥贝克力石阵的发现证明了这里在一万年前已有复杂的社会结构。公元前2000年左右，安纳托利亚的哈提人和早期赫梯人利用当地丰富的谷物、豆类和畜牧资源制作炖菜。陶器的发明使长时间慢炖成为可能，这种烹饪方式使坚硬的肉类变得软烂可口。
> **📜 History** — *Anatolia was one of humanity's earliest agricultural regions; Göbekli Tepe proves complex social structures existed there 10,000 years ago. Around 2000 BC, the Hattians and early Hittites used local grains, legumes, and livestock to make stews. The invention of pottery made long slow-cooking possible, turning tough cuts of meat into tender, flavorful dishes.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 牛肩肉（切块） | Beef chuck (cubed) | 500克 | 1.1 lbs |
| 鹰嘴豆（浸泡过夜） | Chickpeas (soaked overnight) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 洋葱（切块） | Onion (chunked) | 1个 | 1 |
| 胡萝卜（切段） | Carrot (chunked) | 2根 | 2 |
| 大蒜 | Garlic | 4瓣 | 4 cloves |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 干牛至 | Dried oregano | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 盐和黑胡椒 | Salt and black pepper | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎肉** — 锅中加橄榄油，大火将牛肉块煎至各面焦黄（约5分钟），取出备用。
   *Heat olive oil in a pot over high heat. Sear beef cubes until browned on all sides (about 5 minutes). Remove and set aside.*

2. **炒蔬菜** — 同锅中火炒洋葱至半透明，加蒜末、小茴香和牛至炒香。
   *In the same pot over medium heat, sauté onion until translucent. Add garlic, cumin, and oregano; stir until fragrant.*

3. **炖煮** — 牛肉回锅，加入鹰嘴豆、胡萝卜和水，大火煮沸后转小火，加盖炖煮1.5小时至肉和豆都酥烂。
   *Return beef to pot. Add chickpeas, carrots, and water. Bring to a boil, reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer 1.5 hours until meat and beans are very tender.*

4. **调味装盘** — 加盐和黑胡椒调味，盛入深碗，可配面包食用。
   *Season with salt and pepper. Ladle into deep bowls and serve with bread.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 安纳托利亚的恰塔尔土丘遗址发现了公元前7000年的炊具残留，是最早的炖煮证据之一。
  *Cooking residue from 7000 BC found at Çatalhöyük in Anatolia provides some of the earliest evidence of stewing.*

- 鹰嘴豆是近东地区最古老的栽培作物之一，距今已有超过七千年历史。
  *Chickpeas are one of the oldest cultivated crops in the Near East, with a history spanning over 7,000 years.*
