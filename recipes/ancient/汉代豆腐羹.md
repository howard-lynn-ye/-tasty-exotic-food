# 汉代豆腐羹 | Han Dynasty Tofu Soup (西汉, ~100 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/汉代豆腐羹.svg" width="500" alt="汉代豆腐羹"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、汉朝、汤品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 豆腐的发明传说与西汉淮南王刘安（~164-122 BC）有关。《本草纲目》记载"豆腐之法，始于汉淮南王刘安"。虽然学界对确切年代存有争议，但考古证据表明汉代确已出现豆腐制品。汉代人将豆腐与山菜、菌类同煮成羹，既是宫廷美食，也是道家修行者的素斋，体现了汉代"药食同源"的饮食理念。
> **📜 History** — *The invention of tofu is traditionally attributed to Liu An, King of Huainan in the Western Han Dynasty (~164-122 BC). The Compendium of Materia Medica states that "the method of tofu began with Liu An." While scholars debate the exact date, archaeological evidence confirms tofu existed during the Han Dynasty. Han people simmered tofu with wild vegetables and mushrooms — served in both palace banquets and Daoist vegetarian meals, reflecting the Han philosophy of "food as medicine."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 嫩豆腐 | Soft tofu | 1块（约400g） | 1 block (~14 oz) |
| 干香菇（泡发） | Dried shiitake mushrooms (rehydrated) | 4朵 | 4 pieces |
| 山药（切片） | Chinese yam (sliced) | 100g | 3.5 oz |
| 枸杞 | Goji berries | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 姜片 | Ginger slices | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 鸡汤或素高汤 | Chicken or vegetable broth | 3杯 | 3 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 | a pinch |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **准备食材** — 香菇提前泡发30分钟切片（保留香菇水），豆腐切成2厘米方块，山药去皮切薄片。
   *Soak dried shiitake mushrooms 30 minutes and slice (reserve soaking liquid). Cut tofu into 2cm cubes and peel and thinly slice the yam.*

2. **煮羹** — 锅中倒入高汤和香菇水，加姜片和山药片煮沸，转中火煮10分钟至山药软糯，轻放入豆腐和香菇片，小火煮5分钟。
   *Pour broth and mushroom soaking liquid into a pot, add ginger and yam slices, bring to a boil, then simmer on medium heat 10 minutes until yam is tender. Gently add tofu and mushroom slices; cook on low heat 5 minutes.*

3. **调味出锅** — 加入枸杞、盐和白胡椒粉，关火后淋香油，轻轻搅匀后盛碗即可。
   *Add goji berries, salt, and white pepper. Turn off heat, drizzle with sesame oil, stir gently, and ladle into bowls.*
