# 吴国鲈鱼脍 | Wu Kingdom Perch Sashimi

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 三国·吴国 Three Kingdoms / Eastern Wu (~300AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 江东吴地（今苏州一带） Jiangdong Wu region (modern Suzhou area)
> **类型 Type:** 生鱼片 Raw Fish / Sashimi

---

## 典故 Historical Background

"莼鲈之思"是中国文学史上最著名的美食典故之一。西晋张翰在洛阳做官，秋风起时思念故乡吴中的莼菜羹和鲈鱼脍，遂辞官归乡。鲈鱼脍即生切鲈鱼薄片，是吴越地区自先秦以来的传统佳肴。三国吴地水网密布，松江鲈鱼肉质细嫩洁白，被视为淡水鱼中的极品。古人以锋利的刀具将鲈鱼片成薄如蝉翼的鱼脍，蘸芥酱食用。

"Longing for perch and water shield" is one of Chinese literary history's most famous food allusions. During the Western Jin dynasty, Zhang Han, serving as an official in Luoyang, felt autumn winds and yearned for his hometown's water shield soup and perch sashimi — so he resigned and went home. Perch sashimi — thinly sliced raw perch — was a traditional delicacy in the Wu-Yue region since pre-Qin times. The Wu region's dense waterways yielded Songjiang perch of exquisitely tender white flesh, considered the finest freshwater fish. Ancients sliced perch into cicada-wing-thin pieces with sharp blades and ate them dipped in mustard sauce.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **活杀鲈鱼 Kill fresh perch:** 鲈鱼必须极其新鲜，活杀后迅速放血，以冰冷井水洗净。Perch must be extremely fresh — kill live, bleed immediately, wash in ice-cold well water.
2. **去皮去骨 Skin and debone:** 以利刃去鱼鳞、剥皮、剔除所有骨刺，仅取洁白无瑕的鱼肉。With a sharp blade, remove scales, skin, and all bones, keeping only pristine white flesh.
3. **切脍 Slice sashimi:** 以极锋利的薄刃刀将鱼肉斜切为薄如蝉翼的片状——此为"脍"的核心技艺，刀工要求极高。With an extremely sharp thin blade, slice the fish diagonally into cicada-wing-thin pieces — this is the core skill of "kuai," demanding supreme knife technique.
4. **装盘 Arrange:** 鱼脍片片叠放于青瓷盘上如白牡丹绽放，垫以紫苏叶，旁置细葱丝与姜丝。Layer fish slices on a celadon plate like a blooming white peony, bedded on perilla leaves, with scallion and ginger shreds alongside.
5. **调蘸料 Prepare dipping sauce:** 黄芥末以少许米醋和盐调开成蘸酱——辛辣的芥末既提味又有杀菌之效。Mix yellow mustard with rice vinegar and salt into a dipping sauce — the pungent mustard both enhances flavor and has antibacterial properties.
6. **配莼菜羹 Serve with water shield soup:** 另煮莼菜清汤佐食——莼菜的滑嫩与鱼脍的鲜美相得益彰，还原"莼鲈之思"的完整意境。Prepare a clear water shield soup as accompaniment — the silky greens complement the fresh fish, recreating the complete "longing for perch and water shield" experience.

## 备注 Notes

- 中国古代食用生鱼的传统在唐宋时期达到鼎盛，此后逐渐式微，但日本保留了这一传统并发展为刺身文化。China's raw fish tradition peaked in the Tang-Song era, then gradually declined, while Japan preserved and developed it into sashimi culture.
- 松江鲈鱼（四鳃鲈）如今已极为稀有，被列为保护物种。Songjiang perch (four-gilled perch) is now extremely rare and listed as a protected species.
