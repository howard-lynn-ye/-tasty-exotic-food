# 蒙古奶茶 | Mongolian Milk Tea — Suutei Tsai (蒙古帝国, ~1200 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、蒙古、饮品

> **📜 历史** — 蒙古奶茶（сүүтэй цай）是蒙古游牧民族的灵魂饮品，自成吉思汗统一蒙古各部以来，便是蒙古包（ger）中待客的第一道礼仪。《马可·波罗游记》中描述了蒙古人饮用咸奶茶的习俗。这种以砖茶、鲜奶和盐为基底的饮品，是草原上牧民抵御风寒、补充营养的生命之水。蒙古人相信，奶茶的白色象征纯洁与吉祥。
> **📜 History** — *Mongolian milk tea (suutei tsai) is the soul drink of Mongolian nomads. Since Genghis Khan unified the Mongol tribes, it has been the first offering to guests in the ger (yurt). Marco Polo described the Mongolian custom of drinking salty milk tea. Made with brick tea, fresh milk, and salt, it is the lifeline of herders braving harsh steppe winds. Mongolians believe the white color of milk tea symbolizes purity and good fortune.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 砖茶或普洱茶 | Brick tea or pu-erh tea | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 全脂牛奶 | Whole milk | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 水 | Water | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 黄油（可选） | Butter (optional) | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 小米或炒米（可选） | Millet or toasted rice (optional) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮茶底** — 水煮沸后加入砖茶碎，小火煮5分钟至茶色深浓，捞出茶叶渣。
   *Bring water to a boil, add crushed brick tea, and simmer on low 5 minutes until deeply colored. Remove tea leaves.*

2. **加奶调味** — 倒入全脂牛奶，加盐和黄油，中火加热至即将沸腾，用勺子反复从高处倒回锅中（扬茶）数十次使奶茶融合。
   *Add whole milk, salt, and butter. Heat on medium until nearly boiling. Repeatedly ladle the tea from a height back into the pot (a traditional aeration technique) dozens of times to fully blend.*

3. **上桌** — 倒入碗中，可撒入少量炒米增加口感，趁热饮用。
   *Pour into bowls, optionally sprinkle with toasted rice for texture, and drink while hot.*
