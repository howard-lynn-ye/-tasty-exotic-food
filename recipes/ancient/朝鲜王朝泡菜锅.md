# 朝鲜王朝泡菜锅 | Joseon Kimchi Stew

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 朝鲜王朝 Joseon Dynasty (~1600AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 朝鲜半岛 Korean Peninsula
> **类型 Type:** 炖锅/家常菜 Stew / Home Cooking

---

## 典故 Historical Background

泡菜锅（김치찌개）是朝鲜王朝最具代表性的家常菜之一。辣椒在十六世纪末经日本传入朝鲜半岛后，泡菜由原来的白色咸菜演变为红色辣泡菜，泡菜锅也随之诞生。朝鲜王朝时期，每户人家在初冬举行"越冬泡菜"（김장）腌制仪式，发酵成熟的老泡菜最适合用来炖煮这道暖身大菜。

Kimchi stew (kimchi-jjigae) is one of the Joseon Dynasty's most iconic home dishes. After chili peppers arrived on the Korean Peninsula via Japan in the late 16th century, kimchi evolved from white pickled vegetables to the red spicy version, and kimchi stew was born. During the Joseon era, every household held the "kimjang" winter kimchi-making ceremony in early winter; well-fermented aged kimchi was ideal for this warming stew.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 老泡菜 Aged kimchi | 2杯 2 cups |
| 五花肉 Pork belly | 200克 200g |
| 嫩豆腐 Soft tofu | 1块 1 block |
| 泡菜汁 Kimchi juice | 1/2杯 1/2 cup |
| 辣椒粉 Gochugaru (chili flakes) | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 大蒜 Garlic | 4瓣 4 cloves |
| 葱 Scallion | 2根 2 stalks |
| 麻油 Sesame oil | 1小匙 1 tsp |
| 水或米淘水 Water or rice-washing water | 2杯 2 cups |

---

## 做法 Preparation

1. **切料 Cut ingredients:** 五花肉切薄片，老泡菜切段，豆腐切厚块，大蒜切末，葱切段。Slice pork belly thin, cut aged kimchi into pieces, cut tofu into thick blocks, mince garlic, cut scallion into sections.
2. **炒肉 Stir-fry pork:** 石锅或铁锅中加少许麻油，放入五花肉片煸炒至出油微焦。Add sesame oil to a stone or iron pot, stir-fry pork belly until fat renders and edges crisp.
3. **炒泡菜 Stir-fry kimchi:** 加入老泡菜与蒜末同炒，让泡菜吸收猪油香，炒至泡菜略微焦香。Add aged kimchi and garlic, stir-fry to let kimchi absorb the pork fat, cook until slightly caramelized.
4. **加液 Add liquid:** 倒入泡菜汁与米淘水（米淘水使汤底更浓郁），加辣椒粉，大火煮沸。Pour in kimchi juice and rice-washing water (which enriches the broth), add chili flakes, bring to a boil.
5. **炖煮 Simmer:** 转中小火炖煮约两刻钟至汤汁浓郁，放入豆腐块轻推入汤中，再煮一刻钟。Reduce to medium-low, simmer about 30 minutes until the broth is rich, gently slide in tofu blocks, simmer 15 more minutes.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 撒葱花，连锅端上桌，搭配白米饭趁热食用。Top with scallion, bring the pot directly to the table, eat piping hot with steamed white rice.

---

## 备注 Notes

- 泡菜发酵越久，炖出的锅越酸爽浓郁，新泡菜不宜用于此菜。The longer kimchi ferments, the more sour and rich the stew — fresh kimchi is not suitable for this dish.
- 米淘水是朝鲜王朝主妇的秘密武器，使汤底更加醇厚。Rice-washing water was the secret weapon of Joseon housewives, making the broth richer and more full-bodied.
