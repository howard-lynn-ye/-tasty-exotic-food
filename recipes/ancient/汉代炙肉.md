# 汉代炙肉 | Han Dynasty Roasted Meat (汉代, ~100 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/汉代炙肉.svg" width="500" alt="汉代炙肉"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、汉代、烤制、牛羊肉

> **📜 历史** — "炙"是汉代最重要的烹饪方法之一，即将肉串在签子上直接在炭火上烤制。《礼记》记载了"炙"的讲究做法，汉画像石中也大量出现烤肉场景。汉代贵族宴饮时，炙肉配以各种蘸酱（豆酱、醋、姜汁），是"钟鸣鼎食"生活的象征。西汉马王堆出土的遣策中列有多种炙肉，证实这是当时最受推崇的烹饪方式。
> **📜 History** — *"Zhi" (direct roasting) was one of the Han Dynasty's most important cooking methods — meat skewered on sticks and grilled directly over charcoal. The "Book of Rites" recorded elaborate roasting protocols, and Han tomb reliefs frequently depict roasting scenes. At noble banquets, roasted meat was served with various dipping sauces (bean paste, vinegar, ginger juice), symbolizing the lavish "bells and tripods" lifestyle. Bamboo slips from the Mawangdui tomb list multiple types of zhi meat, confirming it as the era's most esteemed cooking technique.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌肉** — 肉切成约3mm薄片或条状，与酱油、黄酒、蜂蜜、姜泥、花椒粉和葱白混合，腌制15-30分钟。
   *Slice meat into approximately 3mm thin pieces or strips. Mix with soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, honey, ginger, Sichuan pepper, and scallion whites. Marinate 15-30 minutes.*

2. **串签** — 将肉片穿在竹签上（提前浸泡竹签30分钟以防烧焦），尽量将肉展开铺平。
   *Thread meat onto bamboo skewers (soak skewers 30 minutes beforehand to prevent burning). Spread meat flat on skewers.*

3. **烤制** — 烤架或烤箱broil模式预热至高温。将肉串放上烤架，每面烤2-3分钟至表面微焦、边缘焦香。刷上少许蜂蜜增加光泽。
   *Preheat grill or oven broiler to high. Place skewers on grill and cook 2-3 minutes per side until lightly charred at the edges. Brush with a thin coat of honey for glaze.*

4. **配酱上桌** — 将豆瓣酱、米醋和香油混合成蘸酱。烤肉趁热上桌，蘸酱食用。
   *Mix doubanjiang, rice vinegar, and sesame oil into a dipping sauce. Serve the skewers hot with the sauce on the side.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 汉代没有辣椒（美洲作物）和酱油（酱油约在宋代发明），调味主要依靠盐、姜、花椒和豆酱。
  *The Han Dynasty had no chili peppers (New World crop) or soy sauce (invented around the Song Dynasty). Seasonings relied on salt, ginger, Sichuan pepper, and fermented bean paste.*

- 本食谱中的酱油是为现代口味而加的简化版，传统汉代蘸酱为发酵豆酱（"酱"）配醋和姜汁。
  *Soy sauce in this recipe is a modern simplification; traditional Han dipping sauce was fermented bean paste ("jiang") with vinegar and ginger juice.*

- 汉代炙肉用的签子多为竹签或金属签，贵族使用铜签或铁签。
  *Han Dynasty skewers were made of bamboo or metal; nobles used bronze or iron skewers.*
