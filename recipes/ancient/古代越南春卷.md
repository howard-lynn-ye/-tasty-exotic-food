# 古代越南春卷 Ancient Vietnamese Spring Roll

> **时代 Era:** 东山文化晚期 Late Dong Son Culture · 约公元前200年 (~200 BC)
> **地区 Region:** 红河三角洲 Red River Delta (modern northern Vietnam)
> **类型 Type:** 小食 Appetizer / 卷饼 Wrap
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

越南春卷的起源可追溯到百越族群以稻米制皮包裹食物的传统。在没有米纸的古代，人们用薄煎的米糊饼来包裹肉菜。这道食谱还原了早期春卷的质朴面貌——不炸，用鲜煎的米皮包裹简单食材，体现了东南亚水稻文明的饮食智慧。

Vietnamese spring rolls trace back to the Baiyue peoples' tradition of wrapping food in rice-based skins. Before modern rice paper, cooks used thin crepe-like rice batter pancakes. This recipe recreates the rustic original — not fried, but wrapped in fresh rice crepes with simple fillings, reflecting the culinary wisdom of Southeast Asian rice civilizations.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 粘米粉 Rice flour | 150g |
| 猪肉末 Minced pork | 200g |
| 木耳 Wood ear mushrooms (泡发切丝) | 30g (dry) |
| 豆芽 Bean sprouts | 100g |
| 鱼露 Fish sauce | 2 汤匙 tbsp |
| 水 Water | 300ml |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 摊米皮 Make rice crepes
粘米粉加水调成稀糊，不粘锅刷薄油中火加热，舀入一勺米糊转匀锅底，盖盖焖30秒揭起。重复至米糊用完，约可做8-10张。
Mix rice flour and water into a thin batter. Lightly oil a non-stick pan over medium heat, ladle in batter and swirl to coat. Cover 30 seconds, then peel off. Repeat for 8-10 crepes.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 炒馅 Cook filling
锅中加少油，炒熟猪肉末，加入木耳丝和豆芽翻炒2分钟，以鱼露调味，出锅晾至不烫手。
Heat a little oil, stir-fry pork until cooked. Add wood ear and bean sprouts, stir-fry 2 minutes. Season with fish sauce. Remove and cool until handleable.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 包卷 Wrap and serve
米皮铺平，放入适量馅料在下方三分之一处，左右两端折入，从下往上紧卷。可蘸鱼露蒜汁食用。
Lay a crepe flat, place filling on the lower third. Fold in left and right sides, then roll up tightly from the bottom. Serve with fish sauce-garlic dipping sauce.
