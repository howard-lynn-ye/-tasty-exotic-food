# 唐代回纥手抓饭 | Tang Dynasty Uyghur Pilaf (~700AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪50分 | 💰~$13/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、唐代、丝绸之路

> **📜 历史** — 唐代与回纥互市频繁，回纥商人将中亚抓饭带入长安。以羊肉、胡萝卜、洋葱和大米焖煮的手抓饭（polu），是丝绸之路文化交融的美味见证。
> **📜 History** — *Tang China traded extensively with the Uyghur Khaganate; merchants brought Central Asian pilaf (polu) to Chang'an. Lamb, carrots, onions, and rice steamed together — a delicious testament to Silk Road cultural exchange.*

| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 50ml / ¼ cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 8g / 1½ tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 炒肉 | Fry Meat
羊肉切大块，热油煎至四面焦黄。Cut lamb into large chunks, sear in hot oil until browned on all sides.

### 2. 炒蔬菜 | Cook Vegetables
加洋葱丝炒软，再放胡萝卜粗条和孜然翻炒，加水没过食材，煮沸后小火炖20分钟。Add sliced onion until soft, then thick carrot strips and cumin, stir-fry, add water to cover, boil then simmer 20 minutes.

### 3. 焖饭 | Steam Rice
米洗净铺在肉菜上（不搅拌），撒葡萄干，盖紧锅盖，小火焖25分钟至水干饭熟。Spread washed rice over meat and vegetables (do not stir), scatter raisins, cover tightly, steam on low 25 minutes until water is absorbed and rice is done.

### 4. 翻拌上桌 | Toss & Serve
从底部翻拌，使肉菜与饭混合，堆在大盘上，传统上以手抓食。Flip from the bottom to mix meat, vegetables, and rice, mound on a large platter — traditionally eaten by hand.
