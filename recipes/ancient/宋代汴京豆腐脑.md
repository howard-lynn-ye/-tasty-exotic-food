# 宋代汴京豆腐脑 | Song Kaifeng Tofu Pudding
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/宋代汴京豆腐脑.svg" width="500" alt="宋代汴京豆腐脑"></p>

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 北宋 Northern Song Dynasty (~1050AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 汴京（今开封） Bianjing (modern Kaifeng)
> **类型 Type:** 小吃/早点 Snack / Breakfast

---

## 典故 Historical Background

北宋汴京是当时世界上最繁华的城市，《东京梦华录》记载了街头巷尾售卖豆腐脑的盛况。宋代豆腐制作技艺已臻成熟，豆腐脑以其滑嫩细腻深受文人雅士与平民百姓喜爱。清晨的汴京街头，豆腐脑摊贩的叫卖声此起彼伏，成为北宋市井文化的一道风景。

Northern Song Bianjing was the world's most prosperous city, and *Dreams of Splendor of the Eastern Capital* recorded the bustling scene of tofu pudding vendors lining every street. Song-era tofu craftsmanship had reached maturity, and silken tofu pudding was beloved by scholars and commoners alike. At dawn, vendors' calls echoed through the streets — a hallmark of Song urban culture.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 黄豆 Dried soybeans | 2杯 2 cups |
| 石膏粉 Gypsum powder | 1小匙 1 tsp |
| 清水 Water | 8杯 8 cups |
| 酱油 Soy sauce | 2大匙 2 tbsp |
| 香醋 Aromatic vinegar | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 辣油 Chili oil | 适量 To taste |
| 榨菜碎 Minced pickled mustard | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 香菜 Cilantro | 适量 To taste |

---

## 做法 Preparation

1. **泡豆 Soak beans:** 黄豆清水浸泡一夜至充分膨胀。Soak soybeans overnight until fully swollen.
2. **磨浆 Grind:** 以石磨将黄豆加水磨成细浆（古法），过滤去渣得生豆浆。Grind soybeans with water using a stone mill (traditional method), strain to obtain raw soy milk.
3. **煮浆 Boil:** 生豆浆入锅大火煮沸，撇去浮沫，转小火煮一刻钟。Bring raw soy milk to a boil, skim foam, simmer for 15 minutes.
4. **点卤 Coagulate:** 石膏粉用少量温水化开，缓缓倒入热豆浆中，轻轻搅拌两三下即停。Dissolve gypsum in warm water, slowly pour into hot soy milk, stir gently two or three times then stop.
5. **静置 Rest:** 盖盖静置一刻钟，使豆浆凝结为嫩滑的豆腐脑。Cover and rest for 15 minutes, allowing soy milk to set into silken tofu pudding.
6. **调味 Season:** 舀入碗中，浇酱油、香醋、辣油，撒榨菜碎与香菜。Ladle into bowls, top with soy sauce, vinegar, chili oil, pickled mustard, and cilantro.

---

## 备注 Notes

- 宋代文人偏爱清淡口味，仅以少许盐与酱调味。Song literati preferred a mild flavor, seasoning with just a touch of salt and sauce.
- 石膏点卤是北方传统做法，南方则多用盐卤。Gypsum coagulation is the northern tradition; the south typically uses brine (nigari).
