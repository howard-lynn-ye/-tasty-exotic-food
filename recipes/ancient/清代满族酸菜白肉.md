# 清代满族酸菜白肉 | Qing Manchu Sauerkraut Pork (清朝东北/北京, ~1700 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 1.5小时 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、清朝、满族、猪肉、酸菜

> **📜 历史** — 酸菜白肉是满族人的灵魂菜肴，随清朝入关从东北白山黑水带入北京宫廷。满族先民在严寒的东北冬季大量腌制酸菜，搭配新年杀猪宴上的白煮五花肉，成为满族年节最重要的菜品。康熙、乾隆年间的宫廷御膳中都有这道菜的记录，它是满汉全席中不可或缺的满族代表菜。
> **📜 History** — *Sauerkraut with boiled pork is the soul food of the Manchu people, carried from the Northeast's white mountains and black waters into Beijing's imperial court when the Qing Dynasty took power. Manchu ancestors pickled vast quantities of sauerkraut during harsh northeastern winters, pairing it with boiled pork belly at New Year pig-slaughter feasts. This dish appears in court records from the Kangxi and Qianlong reigns and was an essential Manchu entry in the legendary Manchu-Han Imperial Feast.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 五花肉（整块） | Pork belly (whole slab) | 500克 | 1.1 lbs |
| 东北酸菜 | Chinese sauerkraut | 300克 | 10.5 oz |
| 粉丝 | Glass noodles | 1把 | 1 bundle |
| 葱段 | Scallion sections | 3根 | 3 stalks |
| 姜片 | Ginger slices | 5片 | 5 slices |
| 八角 | Star anise | 2颗 | 2 |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorns | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 水 | Water | 足量 | enough to cover |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮肉** — 五花肉整块冷水下锅，加葱段、姜片、八角和花椒，大火煮沸后撇浮沫，转中小火煮40-50分钟至筷子能插透。取出冷却后切薄片。
   *Place whole pork belly in cold water with scallion, ginger, star anise, and peppercorns. Boil, skim foam, then simmer 40-50 minutes until chopsticks pierce through easily. Remove, cool, and slice thin.*

2. **处理酸菜** — 酸菜挤干水分，切成细丝。如过酸可用清水冲洗一次。
   *Squeeze sauerkraut dry and shred finely. If too sour, rinse once with clean water.*

3. **炖煮** — 砂锅底部铺一层酸菜丝，上铺白肉片，再盖一层酸菜，倒入煮肉的原汤至没过食材，大火煮沸后转小火炖30分钟。最后10分钟加入泡软的粉丝。
   *Layer sauerkraut in a clay pot, arrange pork slices on top, cover with more sauerkraut. Add the pork-cooking broth to cover. Boil then simmer 30 minutes. Add soaked glass noodles for the last 10 minutes.*

4. **装盘** — 加盐调味，连锅上桌。配蒜泥酱油蘸碟和韭菜花酱食用。
   *Season with salt. Serve in the pot. Accompany with garlic-soy dipping sauce and chive flower paste.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 满族的"杀猪菜"传统延续至今，每年冬至或春节前杀年猪，全村共食酸菜白肉血肠。
  *The Manchu "pig-slaughter feast" tradition continues today — families butcher the annual pig before winter solstice or Spring Festival, sharing sauerkraut pork and blood sausage with the whole village.*

- 清宫御膳房每年冬季储备数万斤酸菜，由专门的"酸菜房"太监管理。
  *The Qing Imperial Kitchen stored tens of thousands of jin of sauerkraut each winter, managed by eunuchs in a dedicated "sauerkraut chamber."*

- 东北酸菜与德国酸菜（Sauerkraut）的发酵原理相同，但东北酸菜用整棵白菜腌制，风味截然不同。
  *Northeastern Chinese sauerkraut and German sauerkraut share the same fermentation principle, but the Chinese version pickles whole napa cabbages, yielding a distinctly different flavor.*
