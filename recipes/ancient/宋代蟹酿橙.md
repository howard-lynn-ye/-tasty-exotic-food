# 宋代蟹酿橙 Song Dynasty Crab-Stuffed Orange

> **时代 Era:** 南宋 Southern Song Dynasty · 约公元1150年 (~1150 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 临安 Lin'an (modern Hangzhou)
> **类型 Type:** 宴席菜 Banquet Dish
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★★★☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

蟹酿橙见载于南宋林洪《山家清供》，是将蟹肉拆入掏空的橙子内蒸制的雅致菜肴，体现了宋人追求"清雅"的饮食美学。橙皮的芳香与蟹肉的鲜甜相互映衬，堪称古代分子料理的灵感先驱。

Crab-Stuffed Orange appears in Lin Hong's "Mountain Home Provisions" from the Southern Song. Crab meat is placed inside a hollowed-out orange and steamed — embodying the Song literati pursuit of elegance in dining. The citrus aroma complements the sweet crab, making it a precursor to modern flavor-pairing gastronomy.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 大脐橙 Large navel oranges | 4 个 |
| 蟹肉（大闸蟹或梭子蟹）Crab meat | 200g |
| 黄酒 Shaoxing wine | 1 汤匙 tbsp |
| 米醋 Rice vinegar | 1 茶匙 tsp |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 to taste |
| 生姜 Ginger (切末 minced) | 10g |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 备橙 Prepare oranges
橙子顶部横切一刀当盖子，用小勺挖出果肉和汁水（果汁保留2汤匙备用），注意不要戳破橙皮底部。
Cut off the top of each orange as a lid. Scoop out pulp and juice with a small spoon (reserve 2 tbsp juice). Take care not to puncture the bottom of the peel.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 拌蟹馅 Mix crab filling
蟹肉拆碎，加入姜末、黄酒、米醋、盐和保留的橙汁拌匀。将馅料填入橙盅，盖上橙盖。
Shred the crab meat. Mix with ginger, wine, vinegar, salt, and reserved orange juice. Stuff the filling into each orange cup and replace the lid.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 蒸制 Steam
蒸锅水烧开后放入蟹酿橙，中火蒸15-18分钟。取出后趁热食用，可蘸姜醋汁。
Place stuffed oranges in a steamer over boiling water. Steam over medium heat 15-18 minutes. Serve hot; optionally with a ginger-vinegar dipping sauce.
