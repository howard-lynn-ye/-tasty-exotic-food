# 古代新疆大盘鸡 Ancient Xinjiang Big Plate Chicken

> 约公元700年 | 唐代西域 | 丝绸之路驿站菜
> Circa 700 AD | Tang Dynasty Western Regions | Silk Road Post-House Dish

---

## 简介 Introduction

唐代的西域是丝绸之路的核心地带，各族商旅在驿站歇脚时，将当地的鸡肉、土豆
（实际上土豆是后来引入的，此处以芋头替代）和各种香料一锅炖煮，形成了大盘鸡
的雏形。这道菜融合了中原的酱料、西域的孜然辣椒和中亚的烹饪手法，是丝路
文化交融的美味见证。

The Tang-era Western Regions were the heart of the Silk Road. Travelers at
post-houses stewed local chicken with taro (standing in for the later-arriving
potato) and spices in one big pot — the prototype of Big Plate Chicken. It
fuses Central Plains sauces, Western Region cumin and chili, and Central
Asian techniques — a delicious witness to Silk Road cultural exchange.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 整鸡 Whole chicken | 1只（约1.5kg）|
| 芋头（代替土豆）Taro (replacing potato) | 300g |
| 青椒 Green pepper | 2个 / 2 |
| 红椒 Red pepper | 2个 / 2 |
| 干辣椒 Dried chilies | 8个 / 8 |
| 花椒 Sichuan peppercorn | 5g |
| 孜然粉 Ground cumin | 8g |
| 酱（豆酱）Bean paste | 30g |
| 姜片 Ginger slices | 20g |
| 蒜 Garlic | 6瓣 / 6 cloves |
| 手擀面皮 Hand-pulled noodle dough | 300g |
| 植物油 Oil | 40ml |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 / to taste |

## 做法 Instructions

1. **斩鸡 Chop chicken**: 整鸡斩成核桃大小的块，冷水焯烫去血沫后沥干。
   Chop chicken into walnut-sized pieces, blanch in cold water, drain.

2. **炒鸡 Fry chicken**: 热油煸炒鸡块至表面金黄，加入姜片、蒜、干辣椒、花椒。
   Stir-fry chicken until golden, add ginger, garlic, chilies, peppercorns.

3. **加酱 Add paste**: 放入豆酱翻炒上色，使每块鸡肉均匀裹上酱色。
   Add bean paste, stir to coat every piece evenly.

4. **炖煮 Braise**: 加入没过鸡肉的热水，大火煮沸后转中火炖25分钟。
   Add hot water to cover, bring to boil, then braise on medium 25 min.

5. **加芋 Add taro**: 芋头去皮切块放入，继续炖15分钟至芋头酥软。
   Peel and cube taro, add to pot, braise 15 more minutes until soft.

6. **加椒 Add peppers**: 放入青红椒块，撒孜然粉，大火收汁3分钟。
   Add pepper chunks, sprinkle cumin, reduce sauce on high 3 min.

7. **配面 Serve with noodles**: 手擀面煮熟铺在盘底，将大盘鸡连汤浇上。
   Boil hand-pulled noodles, lay on plate, pour chicken and sauce over top.
