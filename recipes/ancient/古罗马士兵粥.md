# 古罗马士兵粥 | Roman Legionary Porridge (古罗马, ~100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古罗马、军粮、粥、谷物

> **📜 历史** — Puls（小麦粥）是罗马军团士兵的标准口粮，也是罗马共和国时期平民的主食。老加图在《论农业》中记载了多种puls的做法。罗马军团每个士兵每月配给约33磅小麦，自行研磨烹煮。军团行军时，士兵用便携石磨将小麦碾碎，加水在行军锅中煮成粥。这碗朴素的粥支撑了罗马帝国征服地中海的军事力量。
> **📜 History** — *Puls (wheat porridge) was the standard ration of Roman legionaries and the staple food of common citizens during the Republic. Cato the Elder recorded multiple puls recipes in "De Agri Cultura." Each legionary received roughly 33 pounds of wheat monthly, which he ground and cooked himself. On the march, soldiers crushed wheat with portable hand-mills and boiled it into porridge in campaign pots. This humble bowl of puls fueled the military machine that conquered the Mediterranean.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 碾碎小麦（bulgur wheat） | Bulgur wheat | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 水 | Water | 3杯 | 3 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 干奶酪（帕玛森或佩科里诺） | Hard cheese (Parmesan or Pecorino) | 2汤匙（磨碎） | 2 tbsp (grated) |
| 蜂蜜（可选） | Honey (optional) | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮粥** — 水加盐煮开，倒入碾碎小麦，搅拌均匀。转小火盖锅盖慢煮20-25分钟，期间偶尔搅拌防粘锅，直至小麦吸收大部分水分变软糯。
   *Bring salted water to a boil. Stir in bulgur wheat. Reduce to low heat, cover, and simmer 20-25 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking, until the wheat absorbs most of the water and turns soft and creamy.*

2. **调味** — 关火后拌入橄榄油，使粥更滑润。想吃咸味版就撒磨碎的干奶酪；想吃甜味版就淋蜂蜜——两种吃法在古罗马都有记载。
   *Remove from heat and stir in olive oil for smoothness. For the savory version, top with grated hard cheese. For the sweet version, drizzle with honey — both styles are documented in ancient Rome.*

3. **上桌** — 盛入碗中趁热食用。正宗吃法是用面包蘸食。可搭配烤蔬菜或腌橄榄还原罗马士兵的简单餐食。
   *Serve hot in bowls. The authentic way is to scoop it with bread. Pair with roasted vegetables or marinated olives to recreate a legionary's simple meal.*
