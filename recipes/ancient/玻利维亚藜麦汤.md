# 玻利维亚藜麦汤 | Bolivian Quinoa Soup (安第斯文明, ~1000 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/玻利维亚藜麦汤.svg" width="500" alt="玻利维亚藜麦汤"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、安第斯、汤品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 藜麦（quinoa）是安第斯高原原住民栽培了5000多年的"众谷之母"（chisaya mama），印加帝国将其视为神圣作物。在海拔3600米以上的的喀喀湖周围，艾玛拉族（Aymara）人用藜麦搭配土豆和辣椒煮成浓汤，是高寒地区最重要的热量来源。印加皇帝每年亲自用金铲播种第一粒藜麦种子。西班牙征服者一度试图禁止种植藜麦，但它在偏远山区顽强存续至今。
> **📜 History** — *Quinoa is the "Mother of All Grains" (chisaya mama), cultivated by Andean peoples for over 5,000 years and considered sacred by the Inca Empire. Around Lake Titicaca at 3,600+ meters altitude, the Aymara people cooked quinoa with potatoes and chili into hearty soups — the most vital calorie source in frigid highlands. The Inca emperor personally planted the first quinoa seed each year with a golden spade. Spanish conquistadors once tried to ban quinoa cultivation, but it stubbornly survived in remote mountains to this day.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 白藜麦 | White quinoa | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 土豆（切块） | Potato (cubed) | 2个中等 | 2 medium |
| 洋葱（切碎） | Onion (diced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 大蒜（切碎） | Garlic (minced) | 3瓣 | 3 cloves |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 蔬菜高汤 | Vegetable broth | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 新鲜香菜 | Fresh cilantro | 适量 | to taste |
| 酸橙（挤汁） | Lime (juiced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 盐和胡椒 | Salt and pepper | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炒底料** — 锅中加橄榄油，中火炒洋葱和蒜至透明，加入小茴香粉炒香30秒，放入土豆块翻炒2分钟。
   *Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté onion and garlic until translucent, add cumin and stir 30 seconds, then add potato cubes and toss for 2 minutes.*

2. **煮藜麦汤** — 倒入蔬菜高汤，加入洗净的藜麦，大火煮沸后转小火煮20-25分钟至藜麦开花、土豆绵软。加盐和胡椒调味。
   *Pour in vegetable broth, add rinsed quinoa. Bring to a boil, then simmer 20-25 minutes until quinoa blooms and potatoes are tender. Season with salt and pepper.*

3. **装盘** — 盛入碗中，挤入新鲜酸橙汁，撒上香菜碎即可食用。
   *Ladle into bowls, squeeze fresh lime juice over the soup, and garnish with chopped cilantro.*
