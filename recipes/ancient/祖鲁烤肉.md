# 祖鲁烤肉 | Zulu Braai Grilled Meat (祖鲁王国, ~1500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 腌制 1小时 + 烤制 20分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、祖鲁、烤肉、南非

> **📜 历史** — 祖鲁民族是南部非洲最强大的战士民族之一，在恰卡祖鲁王（约1816-1828年在位）时期建立了庞大的祖鲁王国。但烤肉（Braai）的传统远早于此，可追溯至公元1500年前后的恩古尼人迁徙时期。"Braai"一词源自南非荷兰语"braaivleis"（烤肉），但这种在明火上烤制肉类的方式是所有南部非洲部落共有的古老传统。祖鲁人以牛为财富的象征，重大庆典时宰牛烤肉是最高礼遇。
> **📜 History** — *The Zulu nation was one of southern Africa's most formidable warrior peoples, building a vast kingdom under King Shaka (r. c. 1816-1828). But the Braai tradition predates him by centuries, tracing to the Nguni migration era around 1500 AD. "Braai" comes from Afrikaans "braaivleis" (grilled meat), but cooking meat over open flame is an ancient tradition shared by all southern African tribes. The Zulu regarded cattle as a symbol of wealth; slaughtering and grilling beef at major celebrations was the highest honor.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **调料混合** — 将芫荽籽、黑胡椒、蒜粉、辣椒粉和粗盐混合。牛排两面涂油后均匀撒上混合调料，室温腌制1小时。
   *Combine crushed coriander, black pepper, garlic powder, paprika, and coarse salt. Oil both sides of the steak and season evenly. Marinate at room temperature for 1 hour.*

2. **起火** — 烤架（或烤箱烤盘）预热至高温。如使用炭火，等炭烧至白灰覆盖再开始烤制。
   *Preheat grill (or oven broiler) to high heat. If using charcoal, wait until coals are covered in white ash before grilling.*

3. **烤制** — 牛排每面烤4-5分钟（中等熟度），香肠翻转烤约12-15分钟至熟透。不要频繁翻动。
   *Grill steak 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness. Turn sausages and grill about 12-15 minutes until cooked through. Don't flip too frequently.*

4. **食用** — 牛排静置5分钟后切片，挤柠檬汁，与香肠一同上桌，传统配玉米糊（pap）。
   *Rest steak 5 minutes, slice, squeeze lemon juice over. Serve alongside sausage, traditionally with maize porridge (pap).*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 南非的Braai文化如此深入人心，9月24日被定为"国家Braai日"（与南非遗产日同天）。
  *Braai culture is so ingrained in South Africa that September 24 is celebrated as "National Braai Day" (coinciding with Heritage Day).*

- 祖鲁人的传统调味极简——仅用盐和火，让肉的天然风味主导，这一理念与现代烤肉哲学一致。
  *Traditional Zulu seasoning was minimalist — just salt and fire, letting the meat's natural flavor dominate — a philosophy that aligns with modern grilling ideals.*
