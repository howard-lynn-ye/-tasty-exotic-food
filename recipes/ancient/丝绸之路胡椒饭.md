# 丝绸之路胡椒饭 | Silk Road Pepper Rice (丝绸之路, ~500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、丝绸之路、米饭、胡椒

> **📜 历史** — 丝绸之路不仅是商贸通道，更是人类最伟大的美食交流网络。胡椒从印度出发，经波斯、中亚传入中国，在汉代已是珍贵调味品，价值一度与黄金等重。南北朝至隋唐时期，丝路沿线的粟特商人将胡椒与藏红花、肉桂等香料混合使用，创造出独特的"胡食"风味。这道胡椒饭融合了波斯抓饭的烹饪技法和中亚香料的浓郁，是丝路贸易站驿馆的经典菜品。
> **📜 History** — *The Silk Road was not just a trade route but humanity's greatest culinary exchange network. Pepper traveled from India through Persia and Central Asia to China, becoming a precious spice by the Han Dynasty — once valued equal to gold by weight. From the Northern-Southern Dynasties through the Sui-Tang era, Sogdian merchants along the Silk Road blended pepper with saffron and cinnamon, creating distinctive "Hu cuisine" flavors. This pepper rice combines Persian pilaf technique with rich Central Asian spices — a classic dish from Silk Road caravanserais.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 巴斯马蒂米 | Basmati rice | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 黑胡椒（粗磨） | Black pepper (coarsely ground) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 羊肉（切小块） | Lamb (diced small) | 200克 | 7 oz |
| 洋葱（切丝） | Onion (sliced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 胡萝卜（切丝） | Carrot (julienned) | 1根 | 1 |
| 藏红花丝 | Saffron threads | 一小撮 | a pinch |
| 肉桂棒 | Cinnamon stick | 1根 | 1 |
| 小茴香籽 | Cumin seeds | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 黄油 | Butter | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 水或高汤 | Water or broth | 3杯 | 3 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **泡米与藏红花** — 大米浸泡30分钟沥干。藏红花丝用2汤匙温水泡开。
   *Soak rice 30 minutes and drain. Steep saffron threads in 2 tbsp warm water.*

2. **炒肉和蔬菜** — 锅中融化黄油，加肉桂棒和小茴香籽炒香。加入羊肉煎至变色，加洋葱和胡萝卜炒软。
   *Melt butter in a pot, toast cinnamon stick and cumin seeds until fragrant. Add lamb and sear until browned. Add onion and carrot, stir until soft.*

3. **煮饭** — 加入沥干的大米翻炒1分钟让米粒裹油，加水/高汤、黑胡椒、藏红花水和盐，大火煮沸后转最小火加盖焖20分钟。
   *Add drained rice and stir 1 minute to coat grains in fat. Add water/broth, black pepper, saffron water, and salt. Bring to a boil, reduce to lowest heat, cover tightly and cook 20 minutes.*

4. **焖蒸装盘** — 关火后不开盖继续焖10分钟，用叉子轻轻拨松米饭，盛入盘中，金黄色泽中点缀着黑胡椒粒。
   *Turn off heat and let steam 10 minutes without lifting lid. Fluff rice gently with a fork, plate up — golden rice studded with black peppercorns.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 唐代长安的西市是丝路贸易终点站，胡商开设的餐馆供应"胡食"，胡椒饭是热门菜品。
  *Chang'an's West Market in the Tang Dynasty was the Silk Road's terminus; Sogdian merchants' restaurants served "Hu cuisine," with pepper rice a popular item.*

- 古罗马人消耗的胡椒量惊人，老普林尼抱怨罗马每年花五千万塞斯特斯从印度进口香料。
  *Ancient Rome consumed astonishing amounts of pepper; Pliny the Elder complained that Rome spent 50 million sesterces annually importing Indian spices.*
