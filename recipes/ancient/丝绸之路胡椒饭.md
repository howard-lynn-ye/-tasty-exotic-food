# 丝绸之路胡椒饭 | Silk Road Pepper Rice (中亚, ~500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 35分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、中亚、米饭、香料

> **📜 历史** — 公元5世纪的丝绸之路不仅运输丝绸和宝石，更是香料贸易的大动脉。胡椒从印度经波斯传入中亚，与中亚本地的大米结合，诞生了这道融汇东西方风味的胡椒饭。在撒马尔罕和布哈拉的商队驿站中，这道饭是疲惫旅人最温暖的慰藉。
> **📜 History** — *The 5th-century Silk Road transported not just silk and gems but was the main artery of the spice trade. Pepper traveled from India through Persia to Central Asia, where it merged with local rice to create this dish fusing Eastern and Western flavors. In the caravanserais of Samarkand and Bukhara, this rice was the warmest comfort for weary travelers.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 长粒米（巴斯马蒂） | Basmati rice | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 羊肉（切丁） | Lamb (diced) | 300克 | 10.5 oz |
| 洋葱（切丝） | Onion (sliced) | 1大个 | 1 large |
| 胡萝卜（切丝） | Carrots (julienned) | 2根 | 2 |
| 黑胡椒（现磨） | Black pepper (freshly ground) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 小茴香籽 | Cumin seeds | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 姜黄粉 | Turmeric | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 大蒜（整头） | Whole garlic head | 1头 | 1 head |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 3杯 | 3 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 1.5茶匙 | 1.5 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **泡米** — 巴斯马蒂米洗净后浸泡20分钟，沥干备用。
   *Rinse basmati rice and soak for 20 minutes. Drain and set aside.*

2. **炒肉底** — 厚底锅中加油，大火炒羊肉至焦香（5分钟），加入洋葱和胡萝卜丝，中火炒至洋葱金黄（约8分钟）。
   *Heat oil in a heavy-bottomed pot. Sear lamb over high heat until browned (5 minutes). Add onion and carrots, cook over medium heat until onion is golden (about 8 minutes).*

3. **调味** — 加入黑胡椒、小茴香和姜黄，翻炒1分钟至出香，加水和盐，大火煮沸。
   *Add black pepper, cumin, and turmeric. Stir for 1 minute until fragrant. Add water and salt, bring to a boil.*

4. **焖饭** — 将米均匀铺在肉上（不要搅拌），大蒜整头埋入米中，盖锅转最小火焖20分钟，关火再焖10分钟。
   *Spread rice evenly over the meat (do not stir). Bury the whole garlic head in the rice. Cover, reduce to lowest heat, and steam 20 minutes. Turn off heat and rest 10 minutes.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 黑胡椒在古代被称为"黑金"，价值等同于同等重量的白银，是丝路贸易最珍贵的商品之一。
  *Black pepper was called "black gold" in antiquity, valued equal to its weight in silver — one of the Silk Road's most precious commodities.*

- 这种肉饭的烹饪方法（Plov/Pilaf）从中亚传遍全世界，影响了印度Biryani、土耳其Pilav和西班牙Paella。
  *This rice-cooking method (Plov/Pilaf) spread from Central Asia worldwide, influencing Indian biryani, Turkish pilav, and Spanish paella.*

- 撒马尔罕自古是丝路枢纽，至今保留着千年前的Plov烹饪传统，每逢节日制作可供千人食用的巨型Plov。
  *Samarkand has been a Silk Road hub since antiquity and still preserves its thousand-year plov tradition, cooking giant batches for thousands on holidays.*
