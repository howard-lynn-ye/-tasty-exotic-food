# 秦代锅盔 | Qin Military Flatbread (~220BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/秦代锅盔.svg" width="500" alt="秦代锅盔"></p>

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪25分 | 💰~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、秦朝

> **📜 历史** — 相传秦军将士用头盔烙饼充饥，锅盔因此得名，是秦统一六国时的军粮。
> **📜 History** — *Legend says Qin soldiers baked flatbread on their helmets during campaigns, hence the name "guokui" (helmet bread), fueling the army that unified China.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 面粉 | All-purpose flour | 500g / 4 cups |
| 水 | Water | 250ml / 1 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 8g / 1.5 tsp |
| 酵母 | Yeast | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 和面 | Make Dough
面粉加酵母、盐和温水揉成光滑面团，醒发40分钟。

Mix flour with yeast, salt, and warm water into smooth dough; let rise 40 minutes.

### 2. 擀饼 | Shape
将面团分成大块，擀成2cm厚的圆饼，刷少许油。

Divide dough into large portions, roll into 2cm thick rounds, brush with oil.

### 3. 烙制 | Bake
平底锅中小火烙至两面金黄，约每面12分钟。

Cook in a dry skillet on medium-low heat until golden, about 12 minutes per side.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 面团要揉至表面光滑，才能烙出层次分明的口感 | Knead until smooth for the best layered texture |
| 烙制时用中小火，急火会外焦里生 | Use medium-low heat; high heat burns the outside while leaving the inside raw |
| 锅盔厚实耐存，常温可保存3-5天，适合出行携带 | Guokui keeps 3-5 days at room temperature, ideal for travel |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
锅盔是中国最古老的军粮之一，据传秦始皇统一六国时期士兵将面饼贴在头盔内侧，行军途中借体温和阳光慢慢烙熟。这种干硬耐储的面饼后来在关中地区流传至今，成为陕西八大怪之一——"烙馍像锅盖"。

*Guokui is one of China's oldest military rations. Soldiers during Qin Shi Huang's unification wars reportedly pressed dough inside their helmets, slowly baking it with body heat during marches. This dry, durable bread has survived in the Guanzhong region and remains one of the "Eight Curiosities of Shaanxi."*
