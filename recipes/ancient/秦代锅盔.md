# 秦代锅盔 | Qin Military Flatbread (~220BC)

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪25分 | 💰~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、秦朝

> **📜 历史** — 相传秦军将士用头盔烙饼充饥，锅盔因此得名，是秦统一六国时的军粮。
> **📜 History** — *Legend says Qin soldiers baked flatbread on their helmets during campaigns, hence the name "guokui" (helmet bread), fueling the army that unified China.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 面粉 | All-purpose flour | 500g / 4 cups |
| 水 | Water | 250ml / 1 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 8g / 1.5 tsp |
| 酵母 | Yeast | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 和面 | Make Dough
面粉加酵母、盐和温水揉成光滑面团，醒发40分钟。Mix flour with yeast, salt, and warm water into smooth dough; let rise 40 minutes.

### 2. 擀饼 | Shape
将面团分成大块，擀成2cm厚的圆饼，刷少许油。Divide dough into large portions, roll into 2cm thick rounds, brush with oil.

### 3. 烙制 | Bake
平底锅中小火烙至两面金黄，约每面12分钟。Cook in a dry skillet on medium-low heat until golden, about 12 minutes per side.

| 原料 | Ingredient | 替代 / Substitute | 备注 / Notes |
