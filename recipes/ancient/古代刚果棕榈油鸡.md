# 古代刚果棕榈油鸡 | Congo Palm Oil Chicken (刚果盆地, ~1000 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 50分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、非洲、鸡肉、炖菜

> **📜 历史** — 棕榈油是中西非最古老的食用油脂之一，刚果盆地的班图族人早在一千年前就用棕榈油烹饪家禽。棕榈果采自野生油棕榈树，经手工压榨取油，其浓郁的橙红色赋予菜肴独特的色泽。这道菜代表了非洲热带雨林地区最原始的烹饪智慧。
> **📜 History** — *Palm oil is one of the oldest cooking fats in Central and West Africa. Bantu peoples of the Congo Basin were cooking poultry in palm oil over a thousand years ago. The oil was hand-pressed from wild oil palm fruits, giving dishes a rich orange-red hue. This recipe represents the primordial culinary wisdom of Africa's tropical rainforest regions.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿（带骨） | Chicken thighs (bone-in) | 6只 | 6 pieces |
| 棕榈油（红棕榈油） | Red palm oil | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 洋葱（切丝） | Onion (sliced) | 1大个 | 1 large |
| 番茄（切块） | Tomatoes (chopped) | 3个 | 3 |
| 大蒜（捣碎） | Garlic (crushed) | 4瓣 | 4 cloves |
| 生姜（切片） | Ginger (sliced) | 1小块 | 1 small piece |
| 辣椒（整只） | Whole chili pepper | 2个 | 2 |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 水 | Water | 1杯 | 1 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎鸡肉** — 锅中加红棕榈油，中大火将鸡腿煎至两面金黄（约6分钟），取出备用。
   *Heat red palm oil in a heavy pot over medium-high heat. Sear chicken thighs until golden on both sides (about 6 minutes). Set aside.*

2. **炒底料** — 同锅中火炒洋葱至软（约5分钟），加入大蒜、生姜和辣椒，炒香1分钟。
   *In the same pot, sauté onion over medium heat until soft (about 5 minutes). Add garlic, ginger, and chili; stir for 1 minute.*

3. **炖煮** — 加入番茄翻炒3分钟至出汁，放回鸡腿，加水，大火煮沸后转小火，盖锅炖35-40分钟至鸡肉脱骨。
   *Add tomatoes and cook 3 minutes until juicy. Return chicken, add water, bring to a boil, then reduce heat. Cover and simmer 35-40 minutes until chicken is fall-off-the-bone tender.*

4. **调味装盘** — 加盐调味，搭配木薯泥或米饭食用。
   *Season with salt. Serve with fufu (cassava mash) or steamed rice.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 棕榈油在非洲的使用历史超过5000年，在加纳阿善提地区的考古遗址中发现了棕榈果残留。
  *Palm oil has been used in Africa for over 5,000 years; palm fruit residues were found at archaeological sites in the Ashanti region of Ghana.*

- 传统上刚果人用陶罐在明火上慢炖，棕榈油的高烟点使其非常适合这种烹饪方式。
  *Traditionally Congolese cooked in clay pots over open fire; palm oil's high smoke point made it ideal for this method.*

- 红棕榈油富含β-胡萝卜素和维生素E，是热带非洲最重要的营养来源之一。
  *Red palm oil is rich in beta-carotene and vitamin E, making it one of tropical Africa's most important nutritional sources.*
