# 南越国烤鱼 Nanyue Kingdom Grilled Fish
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/南越国烤鱼.svg" width="500" alt="南越国烤鱼"></p>

> 约公元前200年 | 南越国（今广东广西一带）| 岭南古味
> Circa 200 BC | Nanyue Kingdom (Modern Guangdong/Guangxi) | Ancient Lingnan Flavor

---

## 简介 Introduction

南越国（前203-前111）由秦将赵佗建立，定都番禺（今广州）。岭南水网密布，
鱼类资源丰富。南越人善用芭蕉叶包裹鲜鱼，以香茅和姜调味，在炭火上烤制。
考古出土的南越王墓中发现了大量鱼骨和烤具，证实了这种古老的烹鱼方式。

The Nanyue Kingdom (203-111 BC) was founded by Qin general Zhao Tuo, with
its capital at Panyu (modern Guangzhou). Rich in waterways, Lingnan abounded
in fish. Nanyue people wrapped fresh fish in banana leaves with lemongrass
and ginger, grilling over charcoal. Fish bones and grilling tools from the
Nanyue King's Tomb confirm this ancient cooking method.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鲈鱼（或鲫鱼）Sea bass or crucian carp | 1条（约600g）|
| 芭蕉叶 Banana leaves | 2大张 / 2 large sheets |
| 香茅 Lemongrass | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 老姜 Old ginger | 30g |
| 粗盐 Coarse salt | 10g |
| 野葱 Wild chives | 一小把 / a small bunch |
| 黄酒（或米酒）Rice wine | 20ml |
| 花椒叶（可选）Sichuan pepper leaves | 几片 / a few |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **处理鱼 Clean fish**: 鲈鱼去鳞去内脏，在鱼身两侧各划三刀。
   Scale and gut the fish, make three diagonal cuts on each side.

2. **调味 Season**: 内外抹上粗盐和米酒，塞入姜片和香茅段。
   Rub coarse salt and rice wine inside and out, stuff with ginger and lemongrass.

3. **包裹 Wrap**: 芭蕉叶过火软化，将鱼连同野葱、花椒叶一起包严实。
   Soften banana leaves over flame, wrap fish tightly with chives and pepper leaves.

4. **烤制 Grill**: 置于中等炭火上，每面烤约12分钟，共烤约25分钟。
   Place on medium charcoal, grill ~12 min per side, ~25 min total.

5. **检查 Check**: 打开叶包，鱼肉应呈白色且轻松脱骨即为熟透。
   Unwrap to check — flesh should be white and flake easily from bone.

6. **上桌 Serve**: 连同芭蕉叶一起上桌，保留原始的野趣与香气。
   Serve still in the banana leaf to preserve the rustic aroma.
