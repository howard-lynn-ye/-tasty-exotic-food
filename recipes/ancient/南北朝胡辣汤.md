# 南北朝胡辣汤 | N&S Dynasties Spicy Soup (~500AD)

> ⏱ 准备25分+烹饪40分 | 💰~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、南北朝

> **📜 历史** — 南北朝胡汉融合时期，胡椒经丝绸之路传入中原，与本地食材结合诞生了胡辣汤的原型。
> **📜 History** — *During the cultural fusion of the Northern and Southern Dynasties, pepper arrived via the Silk Road and merged with local ingredients to create the prototype of hot pepper soup.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛肉 | Beef | 200g / 7oz |
| 胡椒粉 | Black pepper | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 面粉 | Flour | 50g / 0.3 cup |
| 粉条 | Glass noodles | 50g / 2oz |
| 豆腐皮 | Tofu skin | 50g / 2oz |
| 木耳 | Wood ear mushroom | 20g / 0.7oz |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煮汤 | Make Broth
牛肉切丝煮成浓汤，加胡椒粉调味。

Shred beef and simmer into a rich broth, season with black pepper.

### 2. 勾芡 | Thicken
面粉加水调成糊，缓缓倒入汤中搅匀至浓稠。

Mix flour with water into a slurry, slowly stir into soup until thick.

### 3. 加料 | Add Toppings
下粉条、豆腐皮丝和木耳，煮5分钟即成。

Add glass noodles, tofu skin strips, and wood ear; cook 5 minutes and serve.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 胡椒粉要后放，煮太久会失去辛香，出锅前再撒一次最佳 | Add pepper late — prolonged cooking dulls its bite; sprinkle again just before serving |
| 勾芡时必须边倒边搅，否则容易结块 | Stir constantly while adding the flour slurry to prevent lumps |
| 搭配油条或烧饼食用是最传统的吃法 | Pair with fried dough sticks or sesame flatbread for the most traditional experience |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
南北朝是中国历史上民族大融合的时期，北方游牧民族带来了胡椒、孜然等西域香料，与中原的面食、豆制品结合，催生了胡辣汤这一独特的汤食。胡辣汤中的"胡"字即指胡椒，"辣"则是胡椒的辛辣，与后来的辣椒无关。

*The Northern and Southern Dynasties was an era of great ethnic fusion in China. Nomadic peoples from the north brought Silk Road spices like pepper and cumin, which merged with Central Plains staples — noodles and tofu — to create this distinctive soup. The "hu" in the name refers to pepper (a foreign spice), and the "la" (spicy) comes from pepper's heat, not from chili peppers, which arrived much later.*
