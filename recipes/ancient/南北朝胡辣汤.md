# 南北朝胡辣汤 | N&S Dynasties Spicy Soup (~500AD)

> ⏱ 准备25分+烹饪40分 | 💰~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、南北朝

> **📜 历史** — 南北朝胡汉融合时期，胡椒经丝绸之路传入中原，与本地食材结合诞生了胡辣汤的原型。
> **📜 History** — *During the cultural fusion of the Northern and Southern Dynasties, pepper arrived via the Silk Road and merged with local ingredients to create the prototype of hot pepper soup.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛肉 | Beef | 200g / 7oz |
| 胡椒粉 | Black pepper | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 面粉 | Flour | 50g / 0.3 cup |
| 粉条 | Glass noodles | 50g / 2oz |
| 豆腐皮 | Tofu skin | 50g / 2oz |
| 木耳 | Wood ear mushroom | 20g / 0.7oz |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煮汤 | Make Broth
牛肉切丝煮成浓汤，加胡椒粉调味。Shred beef and simmer into a rich broth, season with black pepper.

### 2. 勾芡 | Thicken
面粉加水调成糊，缓缓倒入汤中搅匀至浓稠。Mix flour with water into a slurry, slowly stir into soup until thick.

### 3. 加料 | Add Toppings
下粉条、豆腐皮丝和木耳，煮5分钟即成。Add glass noodles, tofu skin strips, and wood ear; cook 5 minutes and serve.
