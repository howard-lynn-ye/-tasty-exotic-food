# 西汉酱牛肉 Western Han Soy-Braised Beef

> **时代 Era:** 西汉 Western Han Dynasty · 约公元前100年 (~100 BC)
> **地区 Region:** 中原 Central Plains (modern Henan/Shaanxi)
> **类型 Type:** 卤味 Braised Meat / 冷菜 Cold Dish
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

西汉时期酱（豆酱）已是重要调味品，马王堆汉墓出土的遣册中记录了多种酱渍肉类。汉代牛虽主要作为耕畜受法律保护，但老病之牛仍可宰食。酱牛肉以豆酱长时间卤煮，使肉质酥烂入味，是汉代贵族宴饮中的佳肴。此版本以现代酱油代替古代豆酱，风味相近。

By the Western Han, fermented bean paste (jiang) was already a major seasoning. Bamboo inventories from the Mawangdui tomb list various paste-braised meats. Han-era cattle were legally protected as draft animals, but old or sick cattle could be slaughtered. Soy-braised beef was simmered long in bean paste until fall-apart tender — a banquet delicacy of the Han elite. This version uses modern soy sauce in place of ancient bean paste.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 牛腱子肉 Beef shank | 800g (整块 whole) |
| 生抽 Light soy sauce | 80ml |
| 老抽 Dark soy sauce | 30ml |
| 黄酒 Shaoxing wine | 50ml |
| 生姜 Ginger (拍碎 smashed) | 30g |
| 花椒 Sichuan peppercorns | 1 汤匙 tbsp |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 焯水 Blanch
牛腱整块冷水下锅，大火煮沸后撇去浮沫，继续煮5分钟。捞出用温水冲洗干净。
Place whole beef shank in cold water. Bring to a boil, skim off foam, and continue boiling 5 minutes. Remove and rinse with warm water.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 酱卤 Braise
锅中放入牛腱、生抽、老抽、黄酒、姜块、花椒和清水（刚好没过牛肉）。大火煮沸后转最小火，加盖慢炖2-2.5小时，每30分钟翻面一次。
Place shank in pot with both soy sauces, wine, ginger, peppercorns, and water (just covering the beef). Bring to a boil, reduce to lowest heat, cover, and braise 2-2.5 hours, flipping every 30 minutes.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 浸泡切片 Soak and slice
关火后不要取出牛肉，让其在卤汁中自然冷却浸泡至少4小时（过夜更佳）。取出后逆纹切薄片，摆盘即可。
Turn off heat but leave beef in the braising liquid to cool and soak at least 4 hours (overnight is better). Remove and slice thinly against the grain. Arrange on a plate to serve.
