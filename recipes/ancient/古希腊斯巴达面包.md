# 古希腊斯巴达面包 | Ancient Greek Spartan Bread (古希腊斯巴达, ~400 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古希腊、斯巴达、面包、军粮

> **📜 历史** — 斯巴达勇士以严苛的军事训练和简朴饮食闻名。他们的主食是一种粗糙的大麦面包（maza），配以臭名昭著的"黑汤"（melas zomos）食用。色诺芬记载斯巴达人在公共食堂（syssitia）集体进餐，面包是每餐必备。斯巴达面包不加蜂蜜或油脂，追求纯粹的饱腹功能，体现了斯巴达人"简朴即美德"的信条。
> **📜 History** — *Spartan warriors were famous for austere military training and plain food. Their staple was a coarse barley bread (maza), eaten alongside the infamous "black broth" (melas zomos). Xenophon recorded that Spartans ate communally in public mess halls (syssitia), where bread was served at every meal. Spartan bread used no honey or fat, pursuing pure sustenance — embodying the Spartan creed that "simplicity is virtue."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 大麦粉（或全麦面粉） | Barley flour (or whole wheat flour) | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 水 | Water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 橄榄油（可选，非正统） | Olive oil (optional, not traditional) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 干迷迭香（可选） | Dried rosemary (optional) | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **拌面团** — 大麦粉和盐混合，慢慢加水搅拌成粗糙的面团。斯巴达面包不需要光滑细腻——粗犷是它的本色。可加入橄榄油增加现代口感。
   *Mix barley flour and salt, gradually add water to form a rough dough. Spartan bread need not be smooth or refined — coarseness is its character. Add olive oil if desired for a modern touch.*

2. **手压成饼** — 面团分成4份，每份用手按压成约1cm厚的扁圆饼。表面可撒少许迷迭香。
   *Divide into 4 portions. Press each by hand into a flat round about 1cm thick. Sprinkle rosemary on the surface if desired.*

3. **烤制** — 烤箱预热至400°F（200°C），将面饼放在烤盘上烤20-25分钟至表面硬实、轻敲底部有空心声。放凉后外硬内韧，越嚼越香。
   *Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Place rounds on a baking sheet and bake 20-25 minutes until firm on the surface and the bottom sounds hollow when tapped. Once cooled, the bread is hard outside, chewy inside — more flavorful the longer you chew.*
