## 历史背景 Historical Background

满汉全席是清代宫廷最高规格的国宴，融合满族与汉族饮食精华。其中的"锅子"类菜肴是满族传统的铜锅涮煮方式，食材精选山珍海味。这里还原的是一个家庭可操作的简化版本，保留了清宫锅子多料并煮、汤底浓郁的核心特征。

The Manchu-Han Complete Banquet was the Qing court's highest-grade state dinner, fusing Manchu and Han culinary traditions. "Guozi" (hot pot) dishes used the Manchu tradition of copper-pot simmering with prized ingredients. This is a simplified home version preserving the hallmark rich broth and multi-ingredient approach.

|---|---|
| 鸡架或老母鸡 Chicken carcass or stewing hen | 1 副/半只 |
| 羊肉卷 Lamb rolls (薄切 thinly sliced) | 300g |
| 鲜虾 Fresh shrimp | 200g |
| 白萝卜 Daikon radish (切片 sliced) | 200g |
| 冻豆腐 Frozen tofu (切块 cubed) | 200g |
| 葱姜 Scallion & ginger | 各适量 to taste |

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 熬汤底 Prepare broth
鸡架冷水下锅焯水去血沫，捞出洗净。另起锅，加2升水、鸡架、葱段、姜片，大火煮沸后转小火熬1小时，撇去浮油，过滤得清汤。
Blanch chicken carcass in cold water to remove impurities; rinse. In a fresh pot, add 2L water, chicken, scallion, and ginger. Boil then simmer 1 hour, skimming fat. Strain for clear broth.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 涮煮配料 Cook ingredients
将鸡汤倒入铜锅或汤锅中烧沸，先下萝卜片和冻豆腐煮10分钟，再放入鲜虾煮至变色。
Pour broth into a copper pot or soup pot and bring to a boil. Add radish slices and frozen tofu first, cook 10 minutes, then add shrimp and cook until pink.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 涮肉 Swish meat
保持汤沸腾，将羊肉卷逐片涮入汤中，变色即熟。蘸芝麻酱、腐乳汁或韭菜花酱食用。
Keep broth at a rolling boil. Swish lamb slices one at a time until just cooked through. Dip in sesame paste, fermented tofu sauce, or chive flower paste.
