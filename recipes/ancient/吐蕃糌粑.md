# 吐蕃糌粑 | Tibetan Empire Tsampa (吐蕃, ~700 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 5分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、吐蕃、藏族、青稞、主食

> **📜 历史** — 糌粑是吐蕃帝国（618-842）的核心主食，由炒熟的青稞磨成粉制成。松赞干布统一青藏高原后，糌粑成为藏族军民的日常口粮。食用时将糌粑粉放入碗中，加入酥油茶用手捏成团。这种便携高热量的食物完美适应了高原寒冷稀薄的环境，至今仍是藏族饮食的灵魂。
> **📜 History** — *Tsampa was the staple food of the Tibetan Empire (618-842), made from roasted barley flour. After Songtsen Gampo unified the Tibetan Plateau, tsampa became the daily ration of both soldiers and civilians. It is eaten by placing the flour in a bowl, adding butter tea, and kneading it by hand into a dough ball. This portable, high-calorie food is perfectly adapted to the cold, thin air of the plateau and remains the soul of Tibetan cuisine to this day.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 青稞粉（或全麦面粉） | Roasted barley flour (or whole wheat flour) | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 酥油（或黄油） | Yak butter (or regular butter) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 浓红茶 | Strong black tea | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |
| 白糖或蜂蜜（可选） | Sugar or honey (optional) | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 制作酥油茶底 | Make Butter Tea Base

浓红茶中加入1汤匙酥油和盐，搅拌至酥油融化。传统做法是用茶筒反复抽打使茶与酥油充分融合。
Add 1 tbsp butter and salt to the strong black tea, stirring until the butter melts. Traditionally, a churn is used to vigorously blend the tea and butter together.

### 2. 拌糌粑 | Mix Tsampa

碗中放入青稞粉，倒入温热的酥油茶，用手指从碗边向中心搅拌、揉捏，直到粉与液体结合成不粘手的软团。
Place barley flour in a bowl, pour in the warm butter tea, and use your fingers to stir from the edges inward, kneading until the flour and liquid form a soft, non-sticky dough ball.

### 3. 食用 | Eat

捏成小团直接食用，可蘸蜂蜜或白糖增加甜味。剩余糌粑粉密封保存，随时可做。
Pinch off small pieces and eat directly. Dip in honey or sugar for sweetness if desired. Store remaining flour in an airtight container for anytime preparation.

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 酥油茶要趁热加入，冷茶无法与青稞粉充分融合 | Add butter tea while hot; cold tea cannot blend properly with the barley flour |
| 2 | 液体分次少量加入，边加边揉，避免一次加太多变成糊 | Add liquid gradually while kneading; too much at once turns it into paste |
| 3 | 用全麦面粉替代时需先干锅炒至微黄出香再使用 | When substituting whole wheat flour, dry-toast it in a pan until golden and fragrant first |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

吐蕃帝国在松赞干布(约604-650)和赤松德赞时期国力强盛，一度攻占唐朝都城长安。青稞是唯一能在海拔4000米以上高寒地区生长的主粮，糌粑因此成为青藏高原文明的基石。藏族谚语说"没有糌粑的日子不叫日子"，至今藏历新年(洛萨)的庆祝活动中，糌粑仍是必不可少的供品和食品。酥油茶与糌粑的搭配提供了高海拔环境下所需的高热量和抗寒能量。

The Tibetan Empire reached its peak under Songtsen Gampo (c. 604-650) and Trisong Detsen, at one point capturing the Tang capital of Chang'an. Highland barley is the only staple grain that grows above 4,000 meters, making tsampa the cornerstone of Tibetan Plateau civilization. A Tibetan proverb says "a day without tsampa is no day at all"; to this day, tsampa remains an essential offering and food during Losar (Tibetan New Year) celebrations. The combination of butter tea and tsampa provides the high calories and cold-resistance energy demanded by life at extreme altitude.
