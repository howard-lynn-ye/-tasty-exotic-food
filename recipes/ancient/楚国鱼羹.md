# 楚国鱼羹 | Chu State Fish Soup (楚国, ~400 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、楚国、战国、鱼羹、湖北

> **📜 历史** — 楚国是战国七雄中疆域最大的南方霸主，占据长江中下游的鱼米之乡。屈原《招魂》中写道"鲜蠵甘鸡，和楚酪些"，描绘了楚国的丰盛鱼宴。楚地水网密布，淡水鱼类丰富，鱼羹是楚国贵族和平民共享的日常美食。楚人以"酸甜"为主味，使用梅汁和蜂蜜调味，与北方的咸鲜口截然不同。
> **📜 History** — *The State of Chu was the largest southern power among the Warring States Seven, commanding the fertile fish-and-rice lands of the middle and lower Yangtze. Qu Yuan's poem "Summoning the Soul" describes lavish Chu fish banquets. Chu's dense waterways provided abundant freshwater fish, and fish soup was enjoyed by nobles and commoners alike. The Chu palate favored "sour-sweet" flavors using plum juice and honey — distinctly different from the salty-savory taste of the north.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 白肉鱼（鲫鱼或鲈鱼）| White fish (crucian carp or bass) | 1磅 | 1 lb |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 | 1 |
| 生姜（切丝） | Ginger (julienned) | 1块 | 1 piece |
| 梅子酱或白醋 | Plum sauce or white vinegar | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 淀粉（勾芡用） | Cornstarch (for thickening) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **熬鱼汤底** — 鱼洗净切块，锅中加少许油煎至两面微黄，加入姜丝和4杯水，大火煮开后转小火熬15分钟至汤色奶白。
   *Clean and cut fish into pieces. Pan-fry lightly in a little oil until both sides are golden. Add ginger and 4 cups water. Bring to a boil, then simmer 15 minutes until the broth turns milky white.*

2. **调酸甜味** — 捞出鱼骨，将鱼肉拆碎留在汤中。加入梅子酱和蜂蜜调出楚式酸甜味，淀粉加水调匀倒入汤中搅拌至浓稠。
   *Remove bones, leaving flaked fish in the broth. Stir in plum sauce and honey for the signature Chu sour-sweet flavor. Mix cornstarch with water and stir into the soup until thickened.*

3. **打蛋花上桌** — 鸡蛋打散，沿锅边缓缓倒入，轻轻搅动形成蛋花。调盐，盛入碗中趁热食用。
   *Beat the egg. Slowly drizzle along the edge of the pot, gently stir to form egg ribbons. Adjust salt, ladle into bowls, and serve hot.*
