# 古代葡萄牙蛋挞 Ancient Portuguese Egg Tart

> 约公元1500年 | 葡萄牙里斯本 | 修道院甜点
> Circa 1500 AD | Lisbon, Portugal | Monastery Dessert

---

## 简介 Introduction

葡萄牙蛋挞（Pastel de Nata）的历史可以追溯到中世纪的修道院。修女们用大量
蛋白来熨烫修道服，剩下的蛋黄便与糖、牛奶结合制成甜点。里斯本贝伦区的
热罗尼莫斯修道院被认为是蛋挞的发源地。焦糖斑点的酥皮、滑嫩的蛋奶馅，
这款甜点后来随葡萄牙航海传遍全世界。

The Portuguese egg tart (Pastel de Nata) traces back to medieval monasteries.
Nuns used egg whites to starch habits, then combined leftover yolks with
sugar and milk for desserts. The Jeronimos Monastery in Belem, Lisbon, is
considered the birthplace. Caramel-spotted crisp pastry with silky custard —
this dessert later sailed with Portuguese navigators across the globe.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 千层酥皮 Puff pastry | 1卷（约300g）|
| 蛋黄 Egg yolks | 6个 / 6 |
| 全蛋 Whole egg | 1个 / 1 |
| 牛奶 Whole milk | 250ml |
| 淡奶油 Heavy cream | 100ml |
| 白砂糖 Granulated sugar | 120g |
| 清水 Water | 60ml |
| 香草荚 Vanilla bean | 半根 / half |
| 柠檬皮 Lemon zest | 1个的量 / from 1 lemon |
| 肉桂棒 Cinnamon stick | 1根 / 1 |
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 20g |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **煮糖浆 Make syrup**: 糖和水加热至115°C，成浓糖浆。
   Heat sugar and water to 115°C to form a thick syrup.

2. **煮奶 Heat milk**: 牛奶与淡奶油加入柠檬皮、肉桂棒、香草籽，煮至微沸后过滤。
   Heat milk and cream with lemon zest, cinnamon, and vanilla seeds; strain.

3. **混合 Combine**: 将糖浆缓缓倒入热奶中搅匀，冷却至约60°C。
   Slowly pour syrup into hot milk, stir well, cool to ~60°C.

4. **加蛋 Add eggs**: 蛋黄和全蛋打散，筛入面粉搅匀，慢慢加入奶液拌匀过滤。
   Beat yolks and egg, sift in flour, slowly add milk mixture, strain smooth.

5. **做壳 Shape shells**: 酥皮紧密卷成长条，切成12段，每段按入挞模压出杯形。
   Roll pastry into a tight log, cut 12 pieces, press each into a tart mold.

6. **倒馅 Fill**: 蛋奶液倒入挞壳，约八分满。
   Pour custard into shells, filling to about 80%.

7. **烤制 Bake**: 烤箱预热250°C（或最高温度），烤12-15分钟至挞面出现焦斑。
   Preheat oven to 250°C (or max), bake 12-15 min until charred spots appear.
