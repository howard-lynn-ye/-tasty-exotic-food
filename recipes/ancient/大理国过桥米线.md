# 大理国过桥米线 Dali Kingdom Crossing Bridge Noodles

> 约公元1200年 | 大理国 | 云南经典
> Circa 1200 AD | Dali Kingdom | Yunnan Classic

---

## 简介 Introduction

大理国（937-1253）是白族建立的王朝，都城在今云南大理。过桥米线的传说始于
一位妻子每日过桥为丈夫送饭，以鸡油封面保温汤底。滚烫的高汤瞬间将生鲜食材
烫熟，这种"热汤涮食"的方式极具智慧，至今仍是云南最具代表性的美食。

The Dali Kingdom (937-1253) was a Bai dynasty in modern Yunnan. Legend says
a wife crossed a bridge daily to deliver meals to her studying husband,
using chicken oil to insulate the broth. The scalding soup flash-cooks
fresh ingredients — an ingenious technique still iconic in Yunnan cuisine.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 干米线 Dried rice noodles | 300g |
| 老母鸡 Whole old hen | 半只 / half |
| 猪骨 Pork bones | 500g |
| 鸡油 Chicken fat | 30ml |
| 鲜鸡胸薄片 Sliced chicken breast | 100g |
| 猪里脊薄片 Sliced pork loin | 100g |
| 鹌鹑蛋 Quail eggs | 4个 / 4 |
| 鲜豆皮 Fresh tofu skin | 50g |
| 韭菜 Chives | 30g |
| 豆芽 Bean sprouts | 80g |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 白胡椒 White pepper | 适量 / to taste |

## 做法 Instructions

1. **熬汤 Make broth**: 老母鸡与猪骨冷水下锅，大火煮沸后撇沫，转小火炖4小时。
   Simmer hen and pork bones from cold water, skim, then cook on low 4 hours.

2. **泡米线 Soak noodles**: 干米线提前用温水泡软约30分钟，沥干备用。
   Soak dry rice noodles in warm water 30 min, drain and set aside.

3. **备料 Prep toppings**: 鸡胸与猪里脊切纸片薄，豆皮切丝，韭菜切段。
   Slice chicken and pork paper-thin, shred tofu skin, cut chives.

4. **封油 Seal with oil**: 将鸡油浇在滚烫的浓汤表面，形成保温油膜。
   Pour chicken fat over the boiling broth to form an insulating layer.

5. **涮食 Flash-cook**: 依次放入生肉片、鹌鹑蛋、豆皮，利用高温汤底烫熟。
   Add raw meat, quail eggs, tofu skin — the hot broth cooks them instantly.

6. **下米线 Add noodles**: 最后放入米线和蔬菜，加盐和白胡椒调味。
   Finally add noodles and vegetables, season with salt and white pepper.
