# 乾隆下江南鱼头豆腐 | Qianlong's Fish Head Tofu

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 清朝 Qing Dynasty (~1770AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 杭州/江南 Hangzhou / Jiangnan
> **类型 Type:** 汤菜 Soup Dish

---

## 典故 Historical Background

相传乾隆皇帝六下江南时，有一次微服出游遇雨，躲入杭州西湖边一户渔家避雨。渔妇以刚捕获的鳙鱼头与自家磨的豆腐，加几味简单佐料炖了一锅汤招待不速之客。乾隆饥寒交迫之际喝下此汤，只觉鲜美无比，问其菜名，渔妇答曰无名。乾隆御笔题名"鱼头豆腐"，此后此菜名声大噪，成为杭州名菜。虽为传说，但鱼头豆腐汤确是江南最经典的家常美味。

Legend says that during one of Emperor Qianlong's six southern tours, he was traveling incognito when caught in rain, and took shelter in a fisherman's home by West Lake. The fisherwife cooked a soup with a freshly caught bighead carp and homemade tofu with simple seasonings. Cold and hungry, Qianlong found the soup incomparably delicious and asked its name. The woman said it had none. Qianlong inscribed "Fish Head Tofu" with his imperial brush, and the dish became famous overnight as a Hangzhou classic. Though possibly apocryphal, fish head tofu soup is genuinely one of Jiangnan's most classic home-cooked delicacies.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鳙鱼头（花鲢头） Bighead carp head | 1个 1 whole |
| 嫩豆腐 Soft tofu | 1大块 1 large block |
| 生姜 Ginger | 5片 5 slices |
| 葱 Scallion | 3根 3 stalks |
| 料酒 Shaoxing wine | 2大匙 2 tbsp |
| 白胡椒粉 White pepper | 少许 A pinch |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 To taste |
| 猪油 Lard | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 香菜 Cilantro | 适量 Garnish |

---

## 做法 Preparation

1. **处理鱼头 Prepare fish head:** 鳙鱼头从下颌劈开成两半，去鳃洗净，抹少许盐与料酒腌渍片刻。Split the carp head in two from the jaw, remove gills, wash clean, rub with a little salt and wine to marinate briefly.
2. **煎鱼头 Sear fish head:** 锅热猪油，放入姜片爆香，将鱼头两面煎至微黄——此步是汤色奶白的关键。Heat lard in the pot, sizzle ginger slices, sear fish head on both sides until lightly golden — this step is the key to achieving milky-white broth.
3. **加水炖煮 Add water and stew:** 冲入足量沸水（必须是沸水才能使汤呈奶白色），大火猛煮，加入葱段与料酒。Pour in plenty of boiling water (must be boiling to create the milky color), cook on high heat, add scallion sections and wine.
4. **加豆腐 Add tofu:** 嫩豆腐切成厚片，待汤色变白后轻轻滑入锅中，转中小火炖煮约二十分钟。Cut soft tofu into thick slices, gently slide into the pot after broth turns white, simmer on medium-low for about 20 minutes.
5. **调味 Season:** 以盐和白胡椒粉调味——此汤贵在清鲜，调味宜少不宜多。Season with salt and white pepper — the virtue of this soup is pure freshness, so season sparingly.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 连锅端上或盛入大汤碗，撒香菜叶，趁热饮用。汤色应洁白如乳，鲜美醇厚。Bring the pot to the table or ladle into a large soup bowl, garnish with cilantro, drink piping hot. The broth should be white as milk, fresh and mellow.

## 备注 Notes

- 汤色奶白的秘诀在于两点：一是鱼头必须先煎，二是必须加沸水而非冷水。The secret to milky-white broth lies in two points: the fish head must be seared first, and boiling water (not cold) must be added.
- 杭州本地人常说"千滚豆腐万滚鱼"——豆腐与鱼越炖越入味。Hangzhou locals say "a thousand boils for tofu, ten thousand for fish" — the longer they stew, the deeper the flavor.
