# 清代乾隆白菜 | Qing Emperor Qianlong's Cabbage (清代, ~1770 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、清代、素菜、白菜

> **📜 历史** — 乾隆皇帝六下江南，品遍天下美食，却对一道朴素的白菜念念不忘。传说乾隆微服私访时在民间小店尝到一道开水白菜，被其"至清至纯"的味道所折服，回宫后命御厨仿制。御厨用高汤反复吊制，以清澈如水的顶级鸡汤浇灌白菜心，将朴素食材演绎到极致。此菜后来成为国宴菜品，体现了中国烹饪"大道至简"的哲学。
> **📜 History** — *Emperor Qianlong toured south six times, tasting every delicacy, yet remained obsessed with a simple cabbage dish. Legend says he encountered "boiling water cabbage" at a humble roadside eatery during an incognito visit and was captivated by its "supremely clear and pure" flavor. He ordered imperial chefs to replicate it. They created a crystal-clear premium chicken broth, ladled over baby cabbage hearts — elevating humble ingredients to the sublime. The dish later became a state banquet item, embodying the Chinese culinary philosophy of "the greatest art lies in simplicity."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 娃娃菜（白菜心） | Baby napa cabbage | 2棵 | 2 heads |
| 鸡胸肉 | Chicken breast | 200克 | 7 oz |
| 火腿薄片 | Ham slices | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 鸡高汤 | Chicken broth | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 姜片 | Ginger slices | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 | a pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **吊高汤** — 鸡高汤加姜片和火腿片小火煮30分钟使其鲜味充分释放，过滤去渣得到清澈汤底。
   *Simmer chicken broth with ginger and ham slices over low heat for 30 minutes to extract full umami flavor. Strain to get a clear broth.*

2. **处理白菜** — 娃娃菜对半切开，取嫩芯部分，沸水中焯烫30秒至微软，捞出沥干摆入汤碗。
   *Halve the baby cabbages and take the tender inner hearts. Blanch in boiling water for 30 seconds until slightly soft, drain and arrange in a soup bowl.*

3. **浇汤** — 将清澈高汤再次煮沸，加盐和白胡椒调味，轻轻浇在白菜上。
   *Bring the clear broth back to a boil, season with salt and white pepper, and gently ladle over the cabbage.*

4. **上桌** — 此菜讲究汤清如水而味浓如金，上桌时汤面应清澈见底。
   *This dish demands broth as clear as water yet rich as gold. When served, the broth should be transparent to the bottom.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- "开水白菜"名为"开水"实则用顶级高汤，是川菜中"以简驭繁"的巅峰之作。
  *"Boiling water cabbage" is named for water but uses supreme broth — the pinnacle of Sichuan cuisine's "simplicity mastering complexity."*

- 乾隆时期的御膳房有数百名厨师，仅为皇帝一人的膳食就设有专门的汤品师。
  *Qianlong's imperial kitchen employed hundreds of chefs, with dedicated broth specialists solely for the emperor's meals.*
