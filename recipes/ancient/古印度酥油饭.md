# 古印度酥油饭 | Ancient Indian Ghee Rice (~1000BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古印度酥油饭.svg" width="500" alt="古印度酥油饭"></p>

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪25分 | 💰~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古印度

> **📜 历史** — 吠陀经典中记载酥油为"神之食物"，酥油饭是婆罗门祭祀后的圣餐，也是印度香饭的起源。
> **📜 History** — *Vedic texts call ghee "food of the gods"; ghee rice was served as sacred food after Brahmin rituals and is the origin of Indian biryani.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 巴斯马蒂米 | Basmati rice | 300g / 1.5 cups |
| 酥油 | Ghee | 45ml / 3 tbsp |
| 小豆蔻 | Cardamom | 4粒 / 4 pods |
| 丁香 | Cloves | 3粒 / 3 |
| 肉桂 | Cinnamon | 1根 / 1 stick |
| 盐 | Salt | 5g / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 炒香料 | Toast Spices
酥油加热，放入小豆蔻、丁香和肉桂炒出香味。

Heat ghee, add cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon; toast until fragrant.

### 2. 煮饭 | Cook Rice
洗净的大米下锅翻炒1分钟，加水和盐，大火煮开转小火焖15分钟。

Add rinsed rice, stir 1 minute, add water and salt, bring to boil then simmer 15 minutes.

### 3. 焖制 | Rest
关火焖5分钟，用叉子拨松米饭，去掉整粒香料后上桌。

Turn off heat, rest 5 minutes, fluff with fork, remove whole spices and serve.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 巴斯马蒂米煮前浸泡20分钟，米粒会更修长蓬松 | Soak basmati rice 20 minutes before cooking for longer, fluffier grains |
| 整粒香料要先炒出香味再下米，不要跳过这一步 | Toast whole spices before adding rice — never skip this step |
| 焖饭时不要揭盖，蒸汽循环是米饭松软的关键 | Do not lift the lid while resting; steam circulation is the key to fluffy rice |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
酥油在吠陀文明中地位神圣，《梨俱吠陀》将其比作"太阳的光芒"，是祭祀火供的必备之物。酥油饭作为祭祀后的"普拉萨德"（圣食）分发给信众，这一传统延续至今。后来莫卧儿帝国时期，酥油饭融合波斯香料技法，演变为举世闻名的印度香饭（Biryani）。

*Ghee held sacred status in Vedic civilization; the Rigveda compared it to "the radiance of the sun," and it was essential in fire rituals. Ghee rice was distributed as "prasad" (blessed food) after ceremonies — a tradition that continues today. During the Mughal Empire, ghee rice absorbed Persian spice techniques and evolved into the world-renowned Biryani.*
