# 古印度酥油饭 | Ancient Indian Ghee Rice (~1000BC)

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪25分 | 💰~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古印度

> **📜 历史** — 吠陀经典中记载酥油为"神之食物"，酥油饭是婆罗门祭祀后的圣餐，也是印度香饭的起源。
> **📜 History** — *Vedic texts call ghee "food of the gods"; ghee rice was served as sacred food after Brahmin rituals and is the origin of Indian biryani.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 巴斯马蒂米 | Basmati rice | 300g / 1.5 cups |
| 酥油 | Ghee | 45ml / 3 tbsp |
| 小豆蔻 | Cardamom | 4粒 / 4 pods |
| 丁香 | Cloves | 3粒 / 3 |
| 肉桂 | Cinnamon | 1根 / 1 stick |
| 盐 | Salt | 5g / 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 炒香料 | Toast Spices
酥油加热，放入小豆蔻、丁香和肉桂炒出香味。Heat ghee, add cardamom, cloves, and cinnamon; toast until fragrant.

### 2. 煮饭 | Cook Rice
洗净的大米下锅翻炒1分钟，加水和盐，大火煮开转小火焖15分钟。Add rinsed rice, stir 1 minute, add water and salt, bring to boil then simmer 15 minutes.

### 3. 焖制 | Rest
关火焖5分钟，用叉子拨松米饭，去掉整粒香料后上桌。Turn off heat, rest 5 minutes, fluff with fork, remove whole spices and serve.
