# 殷商甲骨烤肉 | Shang Dynasty Oracle Bone BBQ (殷商, ~1300 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/殷商甲骨烤肉.svg" width="500" alt="殷商甲骨烤肉"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、殷商、烤制、牛肉

> **📜 历史** — 殷商甲骨文中频繁出现"炙"字，证明烤肉是商代王室祭祀和宴饮的核心菜肴。考古发掘出土的青铜器上刻有宰牛烤肉的图案，商王武丁时期的占卜甲骨上也记载了以牛羊献祭后分食烤肉的仪式。商代贵族使用青铜鼎烹煮、石板烤制，调味以盐和花椒为主。
> **📜 History** — *The character for "roasting" (炙) appears frequently on Shang Dynasty oracle bones, confirming that grilled meat was central to royal sacrifices and banquets. Bronze vessels unearthed from Shang tombs bear engravings of cattle slaughter and roasting scenes. Oracle bones from King Wu Ding's reign describe rituals of offering cattle and sheep, then sharing the roasted meat. Shang nobles used bronze cauldrons for boiling and stone slabs for roasting, seasoned primarily with salt and Sichuan pepper.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 牛肩肉（切厚片） | Beef chuck (thick slices) | 1.5磅 | 1.5 lb |
| 粗盐 | Coarse salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 花椒粒 | Whole Sichuan peppercorns | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 生姜（切片） | Ginger (sliced) | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 葱白（切段） | Scallion whites (sections) | 3根 | 3 stalks |
| 黄酒 | Shaoxing wine | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌制** — 牛肉切成约1cm厚片，以粗盐、拍碎的花椒粒、姜片和葱白揉搓均匀，淋入黄酒，腌制20分钟。
   *Slice beef into 1cm-thick pieces. Rub with coarse salt, crushed peppercorns, ginger, and scallion whites. Drizzle with Shaoxing wine and marinate 20 minutes.*

2. **石板烤制** — 铸铁锅或平底烤盘大火预热至冒烟，不加油，将腌好的肉片平铺放入，每面烤3-4分钟至表面焦香。
   *Heat a cast-iron skillet or flat griddle over high heat until smoking. Without oil, lay the marinated slices flat and sear 3-4 minutes per side until charred and fragrant.*

3. **上桌** — 烤好的肉撒上现磨花椒粉，趁热食用。可蘸少许粗盐或豆酱。
   *Sprinkle freshly ground Sichuan pepper over the finished meat and serve hot. Dip in coarse salt or fermented bean paste if desired.*
