# 春秋鲁国孔府菜 | Spring & Autumn Confucius Family Dish (~500BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/春秋鲁国孔府菜.svg" width="500" alt="春秋鲁国孔府菜"></p>

> ⏱ 准备25分+烹饪40分 | 💰~$15/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、先秦、鲁菜

> **📜 历史** — 孔子曰"食不厌精，脍不厌细"，孔府菜是中国最古老的官府菜系之一。春秋时期鲁国宫廷以酱、醋、姜调味，烹制合乎礼制的宴饮佳肴。
> **📜 History** — *Confucius said "food cannot be too fine, mince cannot be too small." The Confucius Family cuisine is one of China's oldest aristocratic traditions. Spring & Autumn–era Lu state courts flavored dishes with soy paste, vinegar, and ginger per ritual protocol.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 五花肉 | Pork belly | 400g / 14 oz |
| 黄酱 | Yellow soybean paste | 40g / 3 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 20ml / 4 tsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 25g / 5 slices |
| 大葱 | Leek / scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 黄酒 | Shaoxing wine | 30ml / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 焯水 | Blanch
五花肉整块冷水下锅，加姜片，煮沸后撇沫，捞出切厚片。Place whole pork belly in cold water with ginger, bring to a boil, skim, remove and slice thick.

### 2. 酱烧 | Braise with Paste
锅底铺葱段，放肉片，抹上黄酱、蜂蜜和黄酒，加少量水，大火烧开后转小火焖30分钟。Line pot with scallion sections, layer pork, spread paste, honey, and wine, add a little water, bring to boil then simmer 30 minutes.

### 3. 收汁 | Reduce
开盖大火收汁，汁浓裹肉，淋米醋翻匀。Uncover, reduce on high until sauce coats meat, drizzle vinegar and toss.

### 4. 摆盘 | Plate
肉片叠放整齐，浇汁，以古法讲究"色正形端"。Arrange slices neatly, spoon sauce over — the ancient principle demands "proper color, upright form."
