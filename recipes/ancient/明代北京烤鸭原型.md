> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 明朝 Ming Dynasty (~1400AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 南京/北京 Nanjing / Beijing
> **类型 Type:** 宫廷菜 Imperial Court Dish

---

## 典故 Historical Background

北京烤鸭的前身可追溯至明太祖朱元璋定都南京时期的"金陵烤鸭"。永乐帝迁都北京后，御厨将此技法带入紫禁城。明代烤鸭采用焖炉烤制，与后世挂炉法不同。当时的烤鸭以枣木为燃料，鸭皮酥脆如纸，被列为皇家御膳的上品。

The predecessor of Peking Duck traces to "Jinling Roast Duck" from when Ming founder Zhu Yuanzhang established his capital in Nanjing. When the Yongle Emperor moved the capital to Beijing, imperial chefs brought the technique to the Forbidden City. Ming-era duck was roasted in a closed oven (menlu), different from the later open-oven (gualu) method. Date wood was the fuel, producing paper-crisp skin — ranked among the finest imperial dishes.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **处理鸭 Prepare duck:** 鸭去内脏不去毛皮，从腋下开小口吹气使皮肉分离。Gut the duck, make a small opening under the wing and blow air to separate skin from meat.
2. **烫皮 Scald skin:** 以沸水浇淋鸭身使皮收紧，然后挂于通风处晾干两个时辰。Pour boiling water over the duck to tighten the skin, then hang in a ventilated place to dry for 2 hours.
3. **上糖色 Glaze:** 将麦芽糖加料酒调匀，均匀涂抹鸭身表面，再次晾干。Mix maltose with rice wine, brush evenly over the duck surface, air-dry again.
4. **填料 Stuff:** 鸭腹内塞入葱段、姜片、花椒、八角，灌入少量开水封口。Stuff the cavity with scallion, ginger, peppercorn, star anise, pour in a small amount of boiling water and seal.
5. **焖烤 Roast:** 枣木烧旺后封闭炉门，利用余温焖烤约一个时辰，至鸭皮枣红油亮。Burn date wood until roaring, seal the oven door, roast with residual heat for about 1 hour until skin is date-red and glistening.
6. **片鸭 Carve:** 出炉后立即片皮片肉，皮肉分开装盘，配薄饼、葱丝、甜面酱食用。Carve immediately — skin and meat plated separately. Serve with thin pancakes, scallion shreds, and sweet bean paste.

## 备注 Notes

- 明代焖炉法烤出的鸭肉比清代挂炉法更为多汁。The Ming closed-oven method produced juicier meat than the Qing open-oven technique.
- 枣木燃烧时散发的甜香是明代烤鸭风味的关键。The sweet fragrance released by burning date wood is key to the Ming-era flavor.
