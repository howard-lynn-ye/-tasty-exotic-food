# 泰国素可泰青咖喱 | Sukhothai Green Curry (~1300AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪25分 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、素可泰王朝

> **📜 历史** — 素可泰王朝是泰国烹饪的黄金时期，青咖喱以新鲜青辣椒和椰奶为基底，展现了东南亚香料的精髓。
> **📜 History** — *The Sukhothai era was Thailand's culinary golden age; green curry with fresh green chilies and coconut milk showcases the essence of Southeast Asian spices.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡胸肉 | Chicken breast | 300g / 10oz |
| 青咖喱酱 | Green curry paste | 45g / 3 tbsp |
| 椰奶 | Coconut milk | 400ml / 1 can |
| 茄子 | Eggplant | 1个 / 1 small |
| 罗勒 | Thai basil | 一把 / 1 handful |
| 鱼露 | Fish sauce | 15ml / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 炒酱 | Fry Paste
锅中加少许椰奶炒香青咖喱酱至出油。Fry green curry paste in a splash of coconut milk until fragrant and oil separates.

### 2. 炖煮 | Simmer
加鸡肉块和茄子，倒入剩余椰奶，中火煮15分钟。Add chicken pieces and eggplant, pour in remaining coconut milk, simmer 15 minutes.

### 3. 调味 | Season
加鱼露调味，撒上罗勒叶，配米饭食用。Season with fish sauce, top with Thai basil, serve over steamed rice.
