# 古波斯石榴鸡 | Persian Pomegranate Chicken (波斯帝国, ~500 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古波斯石榴鸡.svg" width="500" alt="古波斯石榴鸡"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 50分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、波斯、鸡肉、石榴

> **📜 历史** — 波斯帝国阿契美尼德王朝时期，石榴被视为生命与丰饶的象征，广泛用于宫廷宴席。波斯人创造了将酸甜果汁与肉类结合的独特烹饪传统，"Fesenjan"（菲森江）的前身就诞生于这个时期。核桃和石榴的搭配在波斯新年（Nowruz）中象征着甜蜜的新开始，这道菜至今仍是伊朗料理的灵魂之作。
> **📜 History** — *During the Achaemenid Persian Empire, pomegranates symbolized life and fertility and featured prominently in court feasts. Persians created a unique tradition of pairing tangy fruit juices with meat — the ancestor of "Fesenjan" was born in this era. The walnut-pomegranate combination symbolizes sweet new beginnings during Nowruz (Persian New Year), and this dish remains the soul of Iranian cuisine today.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿（带骨） | Chicken thighs (bone-in) | 6只 | 6 pieces |
| 核桃（磨碎） | Walnuts (ground) | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 石榴糖浆 | Pomegranate molasses | 1/3杯 | 1/3 cup |
| 洋葱（切碎） | Onion (diced) | 2个 | 2 |
| 姜黄粉 | Turmeric | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 石榴籽（装饰） | Pomegranate seeds (garnish) | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炒核桃底酱** — 干锅中火翻炒磨碎的核桃3-4分钟至微微变色出香，取出备用。
   *Dry-toast ground walnuts in a skillet over medium heat for 3-4 minutes until lightly colored and fragrant. Set aside.*

2. **煎鸡炒葱** — 锅中加油，煎鸡腿至金黄取出。同锅炒洋葱至焦糖色（约10分钟），加姜黄和肉桂炒香。
   *Heat oil, sear chicken until golden and set aside. Sauté onion in the same pot until caramelized (about 10 minutes). Add turmeric and cinnamon, stir.*

3. **炖煮** — 加入烤核桃、石榴糖浆、糖和水，搅拌均匀，放回鸡腿，大火煮沸后转小火，盖锅炖40-45分钟，期间偶尔搅拌。
   *Add toasted walnuts, pomegranate molasses, sugar, and water. Stir well, return chicken, bring to a boil, then simmer covered 40-45 minutes, stirring occasionally.*

4. **装盘** — 酱汁应浓稠呈深棕色。调整酸甜度（加糖或石榴糖浆），撒石榴籽，配藏红花米饭。
   *Sauce should be thick and dark brown. Adjust sweet-sour balance. Garnish with pomegranate seeds and serve with saffron rice.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 波斯帝国的"万王之王"大流士的宫廷记录中，石榴被列为每日御膳的必备食材。
  *In the court records of Darius, the "King of Kings," pomegranates were listed as a mandatory ingredient in the daily royal menu.*

- 波斯人将石榴带到了地中海世界，西班牙城市格拉纳达（Granada）的名字就来源于石榴。
  *Persians introduced pomegranates to the Mediterranean world — the Spanish city of Granada takes its name from the fruit.*

- Fesenjan的酸甜平衡体现了波斯哲学中的二元论思想——善与恶、光与暗的共存。
  *Fesenjan's sweet-sour balance reflects Persian philosophical dualism — the coexistence of good and evil, light and dark.*
