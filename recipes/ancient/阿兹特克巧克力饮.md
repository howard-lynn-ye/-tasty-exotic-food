# 阿兹特克巧克力饮 | Aztec Chocolate Drink (阿兹特克, ~1400 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、阿兹特克、饮品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 巧克力起源于中美洲，阿兹特克人称之为"xocolātl"（苦水），是一种以可可豆、辣椒和香草制成的辛辣饮料，与现代甜巧克力截然不同。在阿兹特克帝国，可可豆珍贵到可用作货币——一只火鸡值100颗可可豆。皇帝蒙特祖马二世据说每天饮用50杯巧克力饮，盛在金杯中，饮后将杯子丢入湖中。
> **📜 History** — *Chocolate originated in Mesoamerica. The Aztecs called it "xocolātl" (bitter water) — a spicy drink made from cacao beans, chili peppers, and vanilla, utterly different from modern sweet chocolate. In the Aztec Empire, cacao beans were so precious they served as currency — one turkey cost 100 cacao beans. Emperor Montezuma II reportedly drank 50 cups of chocolate daily from golden goblets, which he then discarded into the lake.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 无糖可可粉 | Unsweetened cocoa powder | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 辣椒粉（cayenne） | Cayenne pepper | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 香草精 | Vanilla extract | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 蜂蜜或龙舌兰糖浆 | Honey or agave syrup | 1-2汤匙 | 1-2 tbsp |
| 玉米粉（可选，增稠） | Cornmeal (optional, for thickening) | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 一小撮 | a tiny pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **混合可可** — 将可可粉、辣椒粉、肉桂粉、玉米粉（如用）和盐在小碗中混合均匀。
   *Combine cocoa powder, cayenne, cinnamon, cornmeal (if using), and salt in a small bowl.*

2. **加热调制** — 水倒入小锅中加热至微沸，慢慢倒入可可粉混合物，不断搅拌以防结块。加入蜂蜜/龙舌兰糖浆和香草精。
   *Heat water in a small saucepan until barely simmering. Slowly whisk in the cocoa mixture, stirring constantly to prevent lumps. Add honey/agave and vanilla extract.*

3. **打泡** — 中火煮3-5分钟至略微浓稠。阿兹特克人通过在两个容器间反复倒来倒去制造泡沫——你可以用搅拌器快速搅打30秒制造丰富泡沫。
   *Cook over medium heat 3-5 minutes until slightly thickened. Aztecs created foam by pouring the drink between two vessels — you can whisk vigorously for 30 seconds or use a milk frother for a rich foam.*

4. **上桌** — 倒入杯中趁热饮用。泡沫层应浮在表面。口感应是苦、辣、微甜的复杂层次。
   *Pour into cups and serve hot. A foam layer should float on top. The flavor profile should be bitter, spicy, and subtly sweet — a complex layered experience.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 原版阿兹特克巧克力饮不含糖和牛奶——甜巧克力是欧洲人的发明，西班牙人16世纪将可可带回欧洲后加入蔗糖。
  *The original Aztec drink contained no sugar or milk — sweet chocolate was a European invention after the Spanish brought cacao to Europe in the 16th century and added cane sugar.*

- 阿兹特克人使用一种叫"molinillo"的木棒搅打泡沫，这个工具至今仍在墨西哥使用。
  *Aztecs used a wooden whisk called a "molinillo" to froth the drink — this tool is still used in Mexico today.*

- 可可豆在阿兹特克文化中与神灵Quetzalcoatl（羽蛇神）相关，具有神圣地位。
  *Cacao was associated with the deity Quetzalcoatl (Feathered Serpent) in Aztec culture and held sacred status.*
