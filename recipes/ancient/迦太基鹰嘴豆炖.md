## 历史背景 Historical Background

迦太基是腓尼基人在北非建立的强大城邦，其农学家马戈曾著有28卷农业百科全书。鹰嘴豆是地中海地区的古老作物，迦太基人将其与橄榄油、孜然和大蒜炖煮，既能喂饱远征的士兵，也是寻常百姓的家常菜。

Carthage was a powerful Phoenician city-state in North Africa. Its agronomist Mago wrote a 28-volume encyclopedia on farming. Chickpeas were an ancient Mediterranean staple; Carthaginians stewed them with olive oil, cumin, and garlic — feeding soldiers on campaign and families at home alike.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鹰嘴豆 Chickpeas (干 dried, 提前泡12h) | 250g |
| 大蒜 Garlic (切碎 minced) | 4 瓣 cloves |
| 孜然粉 Ground cumin | 1 茶匙 tsp |
| 特级初榨橄榄油 Extra virgin olive oil | 40ml |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 to taste |
| 柠檬 Lemon | 半个 half |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 煮豆 Cook beans
将泡好的鹰嘴豆放入锅中，加足量清水没过豆子约5厘米，大火煮沸后转中小火煮45-60分钟至软糯。保留200ml煮豆水，余水沥干。
Place soaked chickpeas in a pot, cover with water by 5 cm. Bring to a boil, then simmer 45-60 minutes until tender. Reserve 200ml cooking liquid and drain.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 调味炖煮 Season and stew
另起锅，加橄榄油中火加热，下蒜末和孜然粉炒香约1分钟。倒入鹰嘴豆和保留的煮豆水，小火炖15分钟使味道融合。
In a separate pot, heat olive oil over medium heat. Sauté garlic and cumin for 1 minute. Add chickpeas and reserved liquid. Simmer 15 minutes to meld flavors.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 出锅 Serve
用盐调味，挤入柠檬汁，淋上少许生橄榄油，趁热食用。
Season with salt, squeeze in lemon juice, drizzle with raw olive oil, and serve hot.
