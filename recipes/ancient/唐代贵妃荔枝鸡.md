# 唐代贵妃荔枝鸡 | Tang Concubine Yang's Lychee Chicken (唐代, ~750 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/唐代贵妃荔枝鸡.svg" width="500" alt="唐代贵妃荔枝鸡"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、唐代、鸡肉、荔枝

> **📜 历史** — "一骑红尘妃子笑，无人知是荔枝来。"唐代杨贵妃对荔枝的痴迷是中国历史上最著名的美食轶事之一。唐玄宗为博佳人一笑，命人从岭南快马加鞭运送新鲜荔枝至长安，设立了专门的"荔枝驿道"。宫廷御厨创制了多种荔枝菜肴，其中荔枝鸡以荔枝的甜蜜搭配嫩鸡肉，成为贵妃最爱的御膳之一。
> **📜 History** — *"A courier gallops — the imperial consort smiles; none know it is lychees that arrive." Yang Guifei's obsession with lychees is one of Chinese history's most famous culinary tales. Emperor Xuanzong established a "lychee relay road" to rush fresh lychees from Lingnan to Chang'an. Imperial chefs created many lychee dishes; lychee chicken — pairing the fruit's sweetness with tender chicken — became one of the consort's favorite imperial dishes.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿肉（切块） | Chicken thigh (cubed) | 400克 | 14 oz |
| 荔枝（去壳去核） | Lychees (peeled, pitted) | 12颗 | 12 |
| 生姜（切片） | Ginger (sliced) | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 淀粉（勾芡用） | Cornstarch (for thickening) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌鸡** — 鸡块加料酒和1汤匙酱油拌匀，腌制10分钟。
   *Toss chicken cubes with Shaoxing wine and 1 tbsp soy sauce; marinate 10 minutes.*

2. **炒鸡** — 锅中加油烧热，放入姜片爆香，加鸡块大火翻炒至变色（约5分钟）。
   *Heat oil in a wok, sauté ginger slices until fragrant, add chicken and stir-fry over high heat until color changes (about 5 minutes).*

3. **加荔枝调味** — 加入剩余酱油、蜂蜜和米醋，翻炒均匀。放入荔枝轻轻翻炒1分钟。淀粉加少许水调成芡汁倒入锅中，翻炒至酱汁浓稠裹住鸡块。
   *Add remaining soy sauce, honey, and rice vinegar; stir to combine. Add lychees and gently toss for 1 minute. Mix cornstarch with a splash of water, pour into wok, and stir until sauce thickens and coats the chicken.*

4. **装盘** — 盛入盘中，荔枝点缀其间，色泽红亮诱人。
   *Plate with lychees arranged among the chicken, showing an appetizing red-glazed sheen.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 从岭南到长安的荔枝驿道全程约两千公里，快马日夜兼程仅需五到七天。
  *The lychee relay road from Lingnan to Chang'an covered about 2,000 km; express riders could make the journey in five to seven days.*

- 杜牧的诗句使荔枝成为唐代奢靡生活的象征，后世多以此讽喻帝王的铺张浪费。
  *Du Mu's poem made lychees a symbol of Tang extravagance, often cited in later eras to critique imperial excess.*
