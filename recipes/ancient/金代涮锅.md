# 金代涮锅 | Jin Dynasty Hot Pot (金代, ~1150 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 边涮边吃 | 💰 ~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、金代、涮锅、火锅、女真

> **📜 历史** — 金朝（1115-1234）由女真族建立，统治中国北方。女真人的"涮锅"是将薄切的羊肉片在滚沸的锅中快速汆烫后蘸酱食用，这被认为是现代北京涮羊肉的直接前身。女真骑兵作战时用铜盔盛水煮肉，战场上的便捷吃法后来演变为宫廷和民间的火锅文化。金代涮锅用料简单粗犷，锅底只用清水或骨汤，蘸料以芝麻酱为主。
> **📜 History** — *The Jin Dynasty (1115-1234), founded by the Jurchen people, ruled northern China. The Jurchen "rinsing pot" — rapidly blanching thin-sliced lamb in boiling broth and dipping in sauce — is considered the direct ancestor of modern Beijing-style lamb hot pot. Jurchen cavalry reportedly boiled meat in their bronze helmets during campaigns, and this battlefield expedient evolved into court and folk hot pot culture. Jin hot pot was ruggedly simple: a clear water or bone broth base, with sesame paste as the primary dipping sauce.*

| 粉丝 | Glass noodles | 1把 | 1 bundle |

| 芝麻酱 | Sesame paste (tahini) | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **备汤底** — 锅中加8杯水，放入葱段、姜片和少许盐，大火煮开。汤底以清汤为正宗，追求原汁原味。
   *Add 8 cups water to the pot with scallion sections, ginger slices, and a pinch of salt. Bring to a boil. An authentic clear broth is the traditional base, prizing pure, unadulterated flavor.*

2. **调蘸料** — 芝麻酱加温水调稀，加入酱油1汤匙、醋1茶匙、蒜泥和香油各少许，拌匀成蘸碗。
   *Thin the sesame paste with warm water. Add 1 tbsp soy sauce, 1 tsp vinegar, a little minced garlic and sesame oil. Mix into individual dipping bowls.*

3. **涮食** — 汤底沸腾后，用筷子夹羊肉片在锅中涮8-10秒至变色，蘸酱食用。白菜、豆腐和粉丝放入锅中煮熟后捞食。
   *Once the broth boils, use chopsticks to swish lamb slices in the pot for 8-10 seconds until just pink. Dip in sauce and eat. Add cabbage, tofu, and glass noodles to the pot, cook until done, and fish out.*
