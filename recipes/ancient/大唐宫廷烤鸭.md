# 大唐宫廷烤鸭 | Tang Imperial Roast Duck (唐朝长安, ~700 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 风干12小时 + 烹饪 1.5小时 | 💰 ~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、唐朝、烤鸭、宫廷

> **📜 历史** — 烤鸭的历史远早于北京烤鸭。早在唐代（公元618-907年），长安宫廷中就有了精致的烤鸭料理。唐代食谱《烧尾宴食单》中记录了多种鸭肉做法。唐朝是中国烹饪艺术的黄金时代，丝绸之路带来的胡椒、孜然等香料丰富了宫廷菜的调味。这道烤鸭代表了大唐盛世的饮食巅峰。
> **📜 History** — *Roast duck's history far predates Peking duck. As early as the Tang Dynasty (618-907 AD), the imperial court in Chang'an featured refined duck dishes. The Tang-era menu "Shaowei Banquet" records numerous duck preparations. The Tang was China's golden age of culinary arts, with Silk Road spices like pepper and cumin enriching court cuisine. This roast duck represents the culinary peak of the prosperous Tang era.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 整鸭 | Whole duck | 1只（约2公斤） | 1 (~4.4 lbs) |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 花椒粉 | Sichuan pepper powder | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 生姜（切片） | Ginger (sliced) | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 葱段 | Scallion sections | 3根 | 3 stalks |
| 盐 | Salt | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 开水 | Boiling water | 适量 | as needed |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **处理鸭子** — 鸭子洗净，用开水浇淋鸭皮使其收紧。将盐、五香粉和花椒粉混合涂抹鸭腔内部，塞入姜片和葱段。
   *Clean the duck. Pour boiling water over the skin to tighten it. Mix salt, five-spice, and Sichuan pepper; rub inside the cavity. Stuff with ginger and scallion.*

2. **上皮水** — 将蜂蜜、米醋和酱油混合加热，均匀刷在鸭子表面。将鸭子挂在通风处风干12小时（或冰箱中敞开放置过夜）。
   *Mix honey, rice vinegar, and soy sauce; heat slightly. Brush evenly over the duck's surface. Hang the duck in a ventilated area to dry for 12 hours (or leave uncovered in the fridge overnight).*

3. **烤制** — 烤箱预热至220°C（425°F），鸭子胸部朝上放在烤架上（下接烤盘接油），先高温烤20分钟，再降至175°C（350°F）烤1小时，期间翻面一次。
   *Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Place duck breast-up on a rack over a drip pan. Roast at high heat 20 minutes, reduce to 175°C (350°F) and roast 1 hour more, flipping once.*

4. **装盘** — 烤至皮酥肉嫩、色泽红亮即可。切块后搭配葱丝、甜面酱和薄饼食用。
   *Roast until skin is crisp and color is a lustrous reddish-brown. Carve and serve with scallion slivers, sweet bean sauce, and thin pancakes.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 唐代的"烧尾宴"是官员升迁时举办的庆功宴，鸭肉是其中的高级菜品。
  *The Tang-era "Shaowei Banquet" was held to celebrate officials' promotions; duck dishes were among the premium courses.*

- 唐朝长安城人口超过百万，城中有专门的"鸭行"（鸭肉市场），供应来自渭河流域的肥鸭。
  *Tang-era Chang'an had over a million residents and dedicated "duck markets" supplying plump ducks from the Wei River valley.*

- 五香粉的使用始于唐代，最初是宫廷御用的秘制调料，后来才传入民间。
  *Five-spice powder originated in the Tang Dynasty as a secret imperial seasoning before eventually spreading to the common people.*
