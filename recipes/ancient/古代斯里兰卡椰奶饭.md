# 古代斯里兰卡椰奶饭 | Ancient Sri Lankan Coconut Rice (古锡兰, ~300 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、斯里兰卡、米饭、椰奶

> **📜 历史** — 斯里兰卡（古称锡兰）的稻作历史可追溯至公元前800年，而椰子树更被称为"生命之树"。古代僧伽罗王国时期，椰奶饭（Kiribath）已是最重要的仪式性食物，在新年、丰收节和婚礼上必不可少。公元前3世纪阿努拉德普勒王朝的铭文中记载了以椰奶煮饭供养佛寺的传统。这道看似简单的椰奶饭蕴含着斯里兰卡两千多年的稻米与椰子文化。
> **📜 History** — *Sri Lanka's (ancient Ceylon) rice cultivation dates to 800 BC, and the coconut palm is called the "tree of life." During the ancient Sinhalese kingdoms, coconut rice (Kiribath) was the most important ceremonial food — essential at New Year, harvest festivals, and weddings. Inscriptions from the 3rd century BC Anuradhapura dynasty record the tradition of cooking rice in coconut milk as temple offerings. This seemingly simple dish embodies over two millennia of Sri Lankan rice and coconut culture.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 长粒大米 | Long-grain rice | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 椰奶（浓） | Thick coconut milk | 1罐（400ml） | 1 can (14 oz) |
| 水 | Water | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 班兰叶（可选） | Pandan leaf (optional) | 1片 | 1 leaf |
| 肉桂棒 | Cinnamon stick | 1小段 | 1 small piece |
| 黑胡椒粒 | Black peppercorns | 5颗 | 5 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **洗米** — 大米洗净沥干，放入厚底锅中。
   *Rinse rice and drain, place in a heavy-bottomed pot.*

2. **煮饭** — 加入水、半量椰奶、盐、班兰叶、肉桂棒和黑胡椒粒，大火煮沸后转最小火，加盖煮15分钟至水分基本吸收。
   *Add water, half the coconut milk, salt, pandan leaf, cinnamon stick, and peppercorns. Bring to a boil, reduce to lowest heat, cover, and cook 15 minutes until liquid is mostly absorbed.*

3. **加浓椰奶** — 倒入剩余浓椰奶，轻轻搅拌，盖盖再焖5-10分钟至米饭完全熟透、椰奶被吸收。
   *Pour in remaining thick coconut milk, stir gently, cover and let steam 5-10 minutes until rice is fully cooked and coconut milk is absorbed.*

4. **装盘** — 取出肉桂棒和班兰叶，将饭压平后切成菱形块，搭配辣椒酱（lunu miris）食用。
   *Remove cinnamon stick and pandan leaf. Press rice flat, cut into diamond shapes, and serve with chili sambol (lunu miris).*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 斯里兰卡新年（4月14日）第一餐必须是椰奶饭，由家中长辈在吉时点火煮制。
  *The first meal of Sri Lankan New Year (April 14) must be Kiribath, cooked by the family elder at an auspicious time.*

- 古代斯里兰卡的灌溉工程（如帕拉克拉马水库）使稻米大规模种植成为可能。
  *Ancient Sri Lanka's irrigation works (such as the Parakrama Samudra reservoir) made large-scale rice cultivation possible.*
