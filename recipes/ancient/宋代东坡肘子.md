# 宋代东坡肘子 | Song Dynasty Dongpo Pork Knuckle (宋代, ~1080 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 3小时 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、宋代、猪肉、炖煮

> **📜 历史** — 苏东坡不仅是中国最伟大的文学家之一，更是一位传奇的美食家。东坡肘子据传源于苏轼被贬黄州期间，他将东坡肉的烹饪理念——"慢著火，少著水，火候足时它自美"——应用于整只猪肘，创造出皮糯肉烂、肥而不腻的经典。此菜在四川眉山（苏轼故乡）尤为盛行，与东坡肉并称为苏氏双璧。
> **📜 History** — *Su Dongpo was not only one of China's greatest literary figures but a legendary gourmand. Dongpo Pork Knuckle reportedly originated during Su Shi's exile to Huangzhou, where he applied his cooking philosophy — "slow fire, little water; when the heat is right, it becomes beautiful on its own" — to a whole pork knuckle. The dish is especially popular in Meishan, Sichuan (Su Shi's hometown), and stands alongside Dongpo Pork as a twin masterpiece.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **焯水** — 猪肘冷水下锅，煮沸后撇去浮沫，煮5分钟捞出洗净。
   *Place pork knuckle in cold water, bring to a boil, skim off scum, cook 5 minutes, then remove and rinse clean.*

2. **煎皮** — 锅中少许油，将猪肘皮朝下煎至金黄（约3分钟），翻面略煎。
   *Heat a little oil in a pot, place knuckle skin-side down and sear until golden (about 3 minutes), then briefly sear the other sides.*

3. **炖煮** — 加入姜、葱、八角、桂皮、干辣椒，倒入料酒、酱油和足量水（没过肘子），大火煮沸后加冰糖，转最小火加盖慢炖2.5-3小时至筷子能轻松插入。
   *Add ginger, green onion, star anise, cinnamon, and dried chili. Pour in Shaoxing wine, soy sauce, and enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, add rock sugar, reduce to the lowest heat, cover, and slow-braise 2.5-3 hours until chopsticks pierce easily.*

4. **收汁装盘** — 取出肘子装盘，大火将汤汁收浓，浇在肘子上，撒葱花即可。
   *Remove knuckle to a plate. Reduce sauce over high heat until thick, pour over the knuckle, and garnish with chopped green onion.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 苏轼在黄州写下了《猪肉颂》："黄州好猪肉，价贱如泥土。贵者不肯吃，贫者不解煮。"
  *Su Shi wrote "Ode to Pork" in Huangzhou: "Huangzhou has good pork, priced cheap as dirt. The rich won't eat it, the poor don't know how to cook it."*

- 东坡肘子与东坡肉的核心区别在于使用整只肘子，带皮带骨，口感更丰富。
  *The key difference between Dongpo Knuckle and Dongpo Pork is the use of a whole knuckle with skin and bone, yielding richer texture.*
