# 迈锡尼文明橄榄面包 | Mycenaean Olive Bread (迈锡尼文明, ~1200 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/迈锡尼文明橄榄面包.svg" width="500" alt="迈锡尼文明橄榄面包"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 发酵 1小时 + 烘烤 25分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、迈锡尼、面食、素食

> **📜 历史** — 迈锡尼文明是青铜时代晚期爱琴海地区的霸主，以狮子门和黄金面具闻名。线形文字B泥板记录了宫殿分配橄榄油和谷物的详细账目。橄榄树在迈锡尼经济中至关重要，橄榄油既用于烹饪也用于宗教仪式。将橄榄嵌入面包是当时常见的做法，这种传统延续至今日的地中海烘焙。
> **📜 History** — *The Mycenaean civilization dominated the Aegean in the Late Bronze Age, famous for the Lion Gate and golden death masks. Linear B tablets record detailed palace inventories of olive oil and grain distributions. Olive trees were vital to the Mycenaean economy; olive oil served both culinary and ritual purposes. Embedding olives in bread was common practice — a tradition that continues in Mediterranean baking today.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2.5杯 | 2.5 cups |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 干酵母 | Active dry yeast | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 卡拉马塔橄榄（去核切碎） | Kalamata olives (pitted, chopped) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 干迷迭香 | Dried rosemary | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **活化酵母** — 温水中加蜂蜜和酵母，静置10分钟至起泡。加入面粉、1汤匙橄榄油和盐，揉成面团。加入橄榄碎和迷迭香揉匀，盖布发酵1小时。
   *Dissolve honey and yeast in warm water; let sit 10 minutes until foamy. Add flour, 1 tbsp olive oil, and salt; knead into dough. Fold in chopped olives and rosemary, cover and rise for 1 hour.*

2. **整形** — 面团排气，整成圆形或椭圆形，放在铺烘焙纸的烤盘上，表面刷橄榄油，用手指戳几个小洞。
   *Punch down dough, shape into a round or oval loaf, place on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Brush top with olive oil and poke several dimples with your fingers.*

3. **烘烤** — 烤箱预热至190°C（375°F），烤25分钟至金黄，敲底部听到空洞声即熟透。
   *Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F). Bake 25 minutes until golden; the bread is done when it sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.*

4. **食用** — 冷却10分钟后切片，蘸橄榄油食用。
   *Cool 10 minutes, slice, and serve with olive oil for dipping.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 迈锡尼线形文字B记录了"e-ra-wo"（橄榄油），是欧洲最早的书面油脂记录之一。
  *Mycenaean Linear B records "e-ra-wo" (olive oil), one of Europe's earliest written references to cooking fat.*

- 迈锡尼人的面包烤炉遗迹在提林斯和皮洛斯宫殿都有发现。
  *Remains of Mycenaean bread ovens have been found at the palaces of Tiryns and Pylos.*
