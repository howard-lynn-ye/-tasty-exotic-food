# 祖鲁族烤肉 | Zulu Braai (南非祖鲁兰, ~1500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、非洲、牛肉、烧烤

> **📜 历史** — 祖鲁族是南非最大的民族之一，烤肉（Braai）是其文化中不可分割的社交仪式。15世纪的祖鲁兰地区，勇士们在围猎后围坐篝火旁烤制猎获的肉食。他们使用当地的硬木（如金合欢木）作为燃料，赋予肉类独特的烟熏风味。Braai至今仍是南非的"国民烹饪方式"。
> **📜 History** — *The Zulu are one of South Africa's largest ethnic groups, and braai (barbecue) is an inseparable social ritual in their culture. In 15th-century Zululand, warriors gathered around fires after communal hunts to roast their catch. They used local hardwoods like acacia for fuel, imparting a distinctive smoky flavor. Braai remains South Africa's "national cooking method" to this day.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 牛肋排 | Beef short ribs | 1公斤 | 2.2 lbs |
| 粗海盐 | Coarse sea salt | 2茶匙 | 2 tsp |
| 黑胡椒（现磨） | Black pepper (freshly ground) | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 芫荽籽（压碎） | Coriander seeds (crushed) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 大蒜粉 | Garlic powder | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 辣椒片 | Chili flakes | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 柠檬 | Lemon | 1个 | 1 |
| 玉米棒 | Corn on the cob | 4根 | 4 ears |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌制** — 将粗盐、黑胡椒、芫荽籽、蒜粉和辣椒片混合，均匀涂抹在牛肋排上，淋上植物油，室温腌制30分钟。
   *Mix coarse salt, black pepper, crushed coriander, garlic powder, and chili flakes. Rub evenly over ribs, drizzle with oil, and marinate at room temperature for 30 minutes.*

2. **烤制牛排** — 炭火烧至白灰覆盖（中高温），将牛肋排放上烤架，每面烤8-10分钟，期间翻面一次，烤至外焦内嫩。
   *When coals are covered in white ash (medium-high heat), place ribs on the grill. Cook 8-10 minutes per side, turning once, until charred outside and juicy inside.*

3. **烤玉米** — 同时将玉米棒刷油放上烤架，不断翻转烤约12分钟至表面微焦。
   *Meanwhile, brush corn with oil and grill alongside, turning frequently for about 12 minutes until lightly charred.*

4. **装盘** — 牛肋排静置5分钟后切块，挤上柠檬汁，搭配烤玉米上桌。
   *Rest ribs 5 minutes, then cut into portions. Squeeze lemon over meat and serve with grilled corn.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- "Braai"一词源自南非荷兰语（Afrikaans），但烤肉传统远早于欧洲殖民者到来，是班图族群的古老习俗。
  *The word "braai" comes from Afrikaans, but the grilling tradition predates European colonizers — it is an ancient Bantu custom.*

- 祖鲁勇士的饮食以肉类和发酵乳（amasi）为主，烤肉是获取蛋白质的主要方式。
  *Zulu warriors' diet centered on meat and fermented milk (amasi); grilling was the primary means of obtaining protein.*

- 传统祖鲁烤肉不用任何酱料，仅以盐和天然香料调味，强调肉本身的鲜味。
  *Traditional Zulu braai used no sauces — only salt and natural spices, emphasizing the meat's own flavor.*
