# 阿育王素食宴 Ashoka's Vegetarian Feast

> 约公元前250年 | 孔雀王朝 | 印度素食传统之源
> Circa 250 BC | Maurya Empire | Origin of Indian Vegetarian Tradition

---

## 简介 Introduction

阿育王（约前304-前232）是孔雀王朝的第三任君主，统一了几乎整个印度次大陆。
在经历了残酷的羯陵伽战争后，阿育王皈依佛教，颁布禁止杀生的法令，大力推广
素食。这道素食宴以扁豆、蔬菜和香料为主，再现了那个倡导"不害"（ahimsa）的
伟大时代。

Ashoka the Great (c. 304-232 BC), the third Maurya emperor, unified nearly
all of the Indian subcontinent. After the brutal Kalinga War, he embraced
Buddhism, issued edicts against killing, and promoted vegetarianism. This
feast of lentils, vegetables, and spices recreates the era of ahimsa
(non-violence).

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 红扁豆 Red lentils (masoor dal) | 200g |
| 鹰嘴豆 Chickpeas (soaked) | 150g |
| 菠菜 Spinach | 200g |
| 番茄 Tomato | 2个 / 2 |
| 洋葱 Onion | 1个 / 1 |
| 姜黄粉 Turmeric | 5g |
| 孜然粉 Ground cumin | 5g |
| 芫荽粉 Ground coriander | 5g |
| 酥油 Ghee | 30ml |
| 青辣椒 Green chili | 2个 / 2 |
| 芫荽叶 Fresh cilantro | 一把 / a handful |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 印度薄饼（全麦）Whole wheat chapati | 4张 / 4 |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **煮豆 Cook lentils**: 红扁豆和鹰嘴豆分别洗净，加水煮至软烂（扁豆约20分钟，
   鹰嘴豆约45分钟）。Cook lentils and chickpeas separately until very tender.

2. **炒料 Temper spices**: 酥油加热，放入孜然粉、姜黄粉、芫荽粉炒出香气。
   Heat ghee, fry cumin, turmeric, and coriander until fragrant.

3. **炒菜 Cook vegetables**: 加入切碎的洋葱炒至金黄，再加番茄煮至软烂。
   Add diced onion until golden, then tomatoes until broken down.

4. **合并 Combine**: 将煮好的扁豆和鹰嘴豆加入锅中，加菠菜搅拌均匀。
   Add cooked lentils and chickpeas, stir in spinach until wilted.

5. **调味 Season**: 加盐、青辣椒调味，小火焖煮10分钟让味道融合。
   Season with salt and green chili, simmer 10 min to meld flavors.

6. **上桌 Serve**: 撒上芫荽叶，搭配热乎的全麦薄饼食用。
   Garnish with cilantro, serve with warm whole wheat chapati.
