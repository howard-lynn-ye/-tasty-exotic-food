# 古罗马凯旋宴鹅肝 | Roman Triumph Foie Gras

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 罗马共和国晚期 Late Roman Republic (~50BC)
> **发源地 Origin:** 罗马 Rome
> **类型 Type:** 宴席珍馐 Banquet Delicacy

---

## 典故 Historical Background

古罗马人是最早系统性生产鹅肝的文明之一。罗马美食家阿皮修斯在《论烹饪》中详细记载了以无花果填饲鹅以获得肥美鹅肝的方法——"jecur ficatum"（无花果肝）即为今日法语"foie"的词源。凯旋宴上，将军凯旋归来，鹅肝被视为献给胜利者的最高规格美食。

Ancient Romans were among the first civilizations to systematically produce foie gras. The Roman gourmand Apicius detailed in *De Re Coquinaria* the method of force-feeding geese with figs to obtain rich livers — "jecur ficatum" (fig liver) is the etymological origin of the French word "foie." At triumph banquets celebrating a general's victorious return, foie gras was considered the supreme delicacy offered to the victor.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鹅肝 Goose liver (fattened) | 1副 1 whole |
| 蜂蜜 Honey | 2大匙 2 tbsp |
| 鱼露 Garum (fish sauce) | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 Black pepper | 1小匙 1 tsp |
| 白葡萄酒 White wine | 半杯 1/2 cup |
| 无花果干 Dried figs | 6颗 6 pieces |
| 月桂叶 Bay leaves | 2片 2 leaves |
| 松子 Pine nuts | 2大匙 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 Preparation

1. **处理鹅肝 Prepare liver:** 鹅肝去筋络血管，以冷盐水浸泡一个时辰去腥。Remove veins and sinew from the liver, soak in cold salted water for 1 hour to remove gaminess.
2. **制酱 Make sauce:** 白葡萄酒与蜂蜜、鱼露入陶罐小火煮沸，加入月桂叶与黑胡椒，收至浓稠。Simmer white wine with honey, garum, bay leaves, and black pepper in a clay pot until reduced and thick.
3. **煎制 Sear:** 陶锅烧热，将鹅肝切厚片，大火快煎两面至金黄焦香，内部保持粉嫩。Heat a clay pan, slice liver thick, sear on high heat until golden-brown on both sides, keeping the interior pink.
4. **焖煮 Braise:** 将煎好的鹅肝放入酱汁中，加无花果干，小火焖煮片刻使之入味。Place seared liver in the sauce, add dried figs, braise on low heat briefly to absorb flavors.
5. **烤松子 Toast pine nuts:** 松子在干锅中小火翻炒至微黄出香。Toast pine nuts in a dry pan on low heat until lightly golden and fragrant.
6. **装盘 Plate:** 鹅肝置于银盘或陶盘之上，浇蜂蜜酱汁，以无花果干与松子装饰，配面包食用。Place liver on a silver or clay platter, drizzle with honey sauce, garnish with figs and pine nuts, serve with bread.

## 备注 Notes

- Garum（鱼露）是古罗马最重要的调味料，由鱼内脏发酵而成，类似东南亚鱼露。Garum was Rome's most important condiment, fermented from fish entrails, similar to Southeast Asian fish sauce.
- 无花果是古罗马填饲鹅的主要饲料，赋予鹅肝独特的甜香。Figs were the primary feed for fattening geese, giving the liver its distinctive sweetness.
