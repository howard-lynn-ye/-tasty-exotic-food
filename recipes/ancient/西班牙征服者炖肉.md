# 西班牙征服者炖肉 Spanish Conquistador Stew
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/西班牙征服者炖肉.svg" width="500" alt="西班牙征服者炖肉"></p>

> 约公元1520年 | 新西班牙（墨西哥）| 旧世界与新世界的碰撞
> Circa 1520 AD | New Spain (Mexico) | Old World Meets New World

---

## 简介 Introduction

当西班牙征服者踏上美洲大陆时，他们带来了欧洲的猪肉、牛肉和大蒜，却遇到了
从未见过的食材——番茄、辣椒、玉米和豆类。在补给匮乏的征途中，士兵们将两个
世界的食材混在一口锅里炖煮，无意间创造出了早期的融合菜。这道炖肉记录的就是
那个充满冲突与融合的时代味道。

When Spanish conquistadors reached the Americas, they brought European pork,
beef, and garlic — but encountered unknown ingredients: tomatoes, chilies,
corn, and beans. On supply-scarce campaigns, soldiers threw both worlds into
one pot, accidentally creating early fusion cuisine. This stew captures the
taste of that era of conflict and blending.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 猪肩肉 Pork shoulder | 600g |
| 西班牙辣肠（chorizo）Chorizo | 150g |
| 番茄 Tomato | 3个 / 3 |
| 洋葱 Onion | 1个 / 1 |
| 大蒜 Garlic | 6瓣 / 6 cloves |
| 干辣椒（新世界）Dried chilies | 3个 / 3 |
| 红腰豆 Red kidney beans (soaked) | 150g |
| 玉米棒 Corn on the cob | 2根 / 2 |
| 红椒粉（pimenton）Smoked paprika | 8g |
| 孜然粉 Ground cumin | 5g |
| 橄榄油 Olive oil | 30ml |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 月桂叶 Bay leaf | 2片 / 2 |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **切肉 Cut meat**: 猪肩肉切3cm方块，chorizo切厚片。
   Cut pork shoulder into 3cm cubes, slice chorizo thick.

2. **煎肉 Sear meat**: 橄榄油热锅，分批将猪肉煎至四面焦褐，取出备用。
   Heat olive oil, sear pork in batches until browned on all sides, remove.

3. **炒底 Build base**: 同锅炒洋葱至透明，加蒜、红椒粉、孜然炒香。
   In the same pot, cook onion until translucent, add garlic, paprika, cumin.

4. **加番茄 Add tomatoes**: 番茄切块加入，煮5分钟至出汁。
   Add chopped tomatoes, cook 5 min until they release juices.

5. **炖煮 Stew**: 放回猪肉，加chorizo、红腰豆、月桂叶和干辣椒，加水没过食材。
   Return pork, add chorizo, beans, bay leaves, chilies, cover with water.

6. **慢炖 Slow cook**: 大火煮沸后转小火，加盖慢炖1.5小时至肉软烂。
   Bring to boil, reduce to low, cover and simmer 1.5 hours until fork-tender.

7. **加玉米 Add corn**: 最后30分钟加入切段的玉米棒，调盐出锅。
   Add corn segments in the last 30 min, season with salt and serve.
