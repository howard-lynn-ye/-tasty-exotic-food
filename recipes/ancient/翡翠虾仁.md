# 翡翠虾仁 | Empress Dowager's Jade Shrimp (清代 Qing Dynasty, ~1890)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 5分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、清代、宫廷菜、快炒

> **📜 历史** — 清代慈禧太后晚年极其讲究饮食，御膳房每餐备上百道菜。翡翠虾仁是其中最精致的一道：虾仁洁白如玉，配以碧绿的豌豆或黄瓜，色如翡翠，故名"翡翠虾仁"。慈禧说："色不正不食"——颜色不好看的菜她不吃。
>
> **📜 History** — *Empress Dowager Cixi was notoriously particular about her food — the imperial kitchen prepared over 100 dishes per meal. Jade Shrimp was among the most elegant: snow-white shrimp paired with emerald-green peas or cucumber, resembling jade — hence "Jade Shrimp." Cixi declared: "If the color is wrong, I won't eat it."*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 虾仁 | Shrimp (peeled, deveined) | 250g |
| 豌豆 | Green peas (frozen) | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 黄瓜丁 | Diced cucumber | 1/2杯 / 1/2 cup |
| 鸡蛋清 | Egg white | 1个 / 1 |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 适量 / as needed |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 上浆 | Velvet the Shrimp
虾仁加盐、蛋清、淀粉和料酒抓匀，腌15分钟。

Toss shrimp with salt, egg white, cornstarch, and wine. Marinate 15 minutes.

### 2. 滑油 | Oil-Blanch
锅中多放油，四成热（约120°C），放入虾仁滑至变色，迅速捞出。同时焯一下豌豆。

Heat oil to ~120°C. Slide in shrimp, cook until just opaque. Remove quickly. Blanch peas briefly.

### 3. 翻炒 | Stir-fry
锅留底油，放入虾仁、豌豆、黄瓜丁，加少许盐快速翻炒10秒即出锅。

Leave a film of oil. Add shrimp, peas, and cucumber. Season, toss 10 seconds. Plate immediately.

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

> 慈禧太后（1835-1908）是清朝晚期实际掌权者，也是中国历史上最讲究饮食的统治者之一。她的御膳房有128名厨师，每餐准备108道菜。翡翠虾仁的核心理念是"色香味形"四美——不仅要好吃，还要美如翡翠玉石。
>
> *Empress Dowager Cixi (1835-1908) was the de facto ruler of late Qing Dynasty China and one of history's most demanding diners. Her kitchen employed 128 chefs preparing 108 dishes per meal. Jade Shrimp embodies the Chinese culinary philosophy of "color, aroma, flavor, and form" — it must taste great AND look like actual jade.*
