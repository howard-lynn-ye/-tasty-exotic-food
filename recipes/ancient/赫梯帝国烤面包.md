# 赫梯帝国烤面包 | Hittite Empire Bread (赫梯帝国, ~1400 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/赫梯帝国烤面包.svg" width="500" alt="赫梯帝国烤面包"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 发酵 1小时 + 烘烤 30分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、赫梯帝国、面食、素食

> **📜 历史** — 赫梯帝国是安纳托利亚高原上的强大文明，以铁器和战车闻名。赫梯人种植大麦和小麦，面包是军队和祭祀的核心食物。楔形文字泥板中记载了超过180种面包配方，用于不同的宗教仪式和日常食用。赫梯国王在宴会上以面包搭配肉食和葡萄酒招待外国使节。
> **📜 History** — *The Hittite Empire was a powerful civilization on the Anatolian plateau, famous for iron-working and chariots. Hittites cultivated barley and wheat; bread was central to military rations and religious offerings. Cuneiform tablets record over 180 bread recipes for different rituals and daily consumption. Hittite kings served bread alongside meat and wine at banquets for foreign envoys.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 全麦面粉 | Whole wheat flour | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 大麦粉 | Barley flour | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 温水 | Warm water | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 干酵母 | Active dry yeast | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **发酵** — 温水中加入蜂蜜和酵母，静置10分钟至起泡。混合两种面粉和盐，倒入酵母液和橄榄油，揉成光滑面团，盖湿布发酵1小时。
   *Dissolve honey and yeast in warm water; let stand 10 minutes until foamy. Combine both flours and salt, add yeast mixture and olive oil, knead into a smooth dough, cover with a damp cloth and rise for 1 hour.*

2. **整形** — 将面团分成4份，每份擀成约1厘米厚的圆饼，表面刷水撒芝麻。
   *Divide dough into 4 pieces. Roll each into a round disc about 1 cm thick. Brush with water and sprinkle sesame seeds on top.*

3. **烘烤** — 烤箱预热至200°C（400°F），将饼放在烤盘上，烤25-30分钟至金黄酥脆。
   *Preheat oven to 200°C (400°F). Place discs on a baking sheet and bake 25-30 minutes until golden and crusty.*

4. **装盘** — 趁热食用，可搭配橄榄油和蜂蜜蘸食。
   *Serve warm, with olive oil and honey for dipping.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 赫梯人的面包炉是圆形泥制结构，与现代中东的坦都尔炉类似。
  *Hittite bread ovens were round clay structures, similar to modern Middle Eastern tandoor ovens.*

- 赫梯法典规定偷窃面包的惩罚，说明面包在社会中的重要地位。
  *Hittite law codes prescribed punishments for bread theft, highlighting its social importance.*
