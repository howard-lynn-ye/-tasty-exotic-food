# 北宋苏轼醉蟹 | Northern Song Su Shi Drunken Crab

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 北宋 Northern Song Dynasty (~1080AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 黄州/杭州 Huangzhou / Hangzhou
> **类型 Type:** 冷菜/腌制 Cold Dish / Cured

---

## 典故 Historical Background

苏轼（苏东坡）不仅是北宋最伟大的文学家，也是一位热忱的美食家。他在被贬黄州期间，创造了诸多名菜。苏轼对蟹情有独钟，曾写下"不到庐山辜负目，不食螃蟹辜负腹"的名句。醉蟹以黄酒浸渍活蟹，使蟹肉吸收酒香，是江南地区最风雅的佐酒冷菜。苏轼后移居杭州时，更是对阳澄湖大闸蟹赞不绝口，以诗酒蟹会款待文友。

Su Shi (Su Dongpo) was not only the Northern Song's greatest literary figure but also a passionate gourmand. During his exile in Huangzhou, he created many famous dishes. Su Shi adored crab, writing the celebrated line: "To not see Mount Lu wastes the eyes; to not eat crab wastes the belly." Drunken crab — live crabs steeped in Shaoxing wine — allows the meat to absorb the wine's bouquet, the most elegant cold dish for wine-pairing in the Jiangnan region. When Su Shi later moved to Hangzhou, he lavished praise on Yangcheng Lake hairy crabs, hosting poetry-wine-crab gatherings for literary friends.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **洗蟹 Clean crabs:** 活蟹以清水养半日吐净泥沙，用刷子仔细清洗蟹壳与腿缝。Keep live crabs in clean water for half a day to purge mud, scrub shells and leg joints with a brush.
2. **煮醉液 Prepare drunken brine:** 黄酒入砂锅，加盐、冰糖、花椒、姜片、陈皮，小火煮沸后冷却至室温。Pour wine into a clay pot, add salt, rock sugar, peppercorn, ginger, tangerine peel, bring to a boil on low heat, then cool to room temperature.
3. **醉蟹 Steep crabs:** 将洗净的活蟹放入醉液中，确保完全浸没，加盖密封。Place cleaned live crabs into the cooled brine, ensure fully submerged, seal with a lid.
4. **腌渍 Cure:** 置于阴凉处腌渍三至五日——时间越长蟹肉越入味，但不宜超过七日。Store in a cool place for 3-5 days — longer yields deeper flavor, but do not exceed 7 days.
5. **取蟹 Retrieve crabs:** 取出醉蟹，揭开蟹壳，蟹黄呈琥珀色、蟹肉呈半透明状即为上品。Remove drunken crabs; when the roe is amber-colored and the meat semi-translucent, the quality is superb.
6. **装盘 Plate:** 整蟹拆开摆盘，或保持完整置于紫苏叶上，配姜丝与香醋碟，以热黄酒佐食。Disassemble and plate, or present whole on perilla leaves, with ginger shreds and black vinegar on the side, accompanied by warm Shaoxing wine.

## 备注 Notes

- 苏轼认为食蟹最佳季节为农历九月至十月，"九月团脐十月尖"——九月吃母蟹，十月吃公蟹。Su Shi held that the best crab season was the 9th-10th lunar months: "round navels in the ninth, pointed in the tenth" — females in September, males in October.
- 醉蟹是生食，现代食用需注意食品安全，建议使用经检疫的养殖蟹。Drunken crab is eaten raw; for modern food safety, use inspected farmed crabs.
