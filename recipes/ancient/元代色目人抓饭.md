# 元代色目人抓饭 | Yuan Dynasty Semu People Pilaf (~1300AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪55分 | 💰~$14/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、元代、丝绸之路

> **📜 历史** — 元代色目人（中亚、波斯裔）在大都（北京）聚居，将波斯式抓饭带入中国宫廷。《饮膳正要》记载了以藏红花、羊肉、干果焖饭的做法，是中原与西域融合的珍贵食谱。
> **📜 History** — *Semu people (Central Asian and Persian descent) settled in Yuan-era Dadu (Beijing), introducing Persian-style pilaf to the Chinese court. The "Yinshan Zhengyao" records saffron-lamb-dried fruit rice — a precious recipe of East-West culinary fusion.*

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 泡藏红花 | Bloom Saffron
藏红花丝用50ml温水浸泡10分钟，备用。Steep saffron threads in 50ml warm water for 10 minutes, set aside.

### 2. 炖羊排 | Braise Lamb
酥油烧热，羊排煎至焦黄，加洋葱丝炒软，放肉桂棒，加水没过，小火炖25分钟。Heat ghee, sear lamb ribs until browned, add sliced onion until soft, add cinnamon, cover with water, simmer 25 minutes.

### 3. 焖饭 | Steam Rice
米洗净铺在羊排上，撒杏干和鹰嘴豆，淋藏红花水，盖紧焖25分钟至饭熟。Spread washed rice over lamb, scatter apricots and chickpeas, drizzle saffron water, cover tightly and steam 25 minutes until rice is done.

### 4. 翻拌上桌 | Toss & Serve
轻轻从底部翻拌，米饭染上金黄藏红花色，堆盘上桌。Gently flip from the bottom — rice takes on golden saffron hue — mound on a platter and serve.
