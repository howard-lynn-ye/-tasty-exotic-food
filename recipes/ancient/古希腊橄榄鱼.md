# 古希腊橄榄鱼 | Ancient Greek Olive Oil Baked Fish (古希腊, ~400 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古希腊、海鲜、烤制

> **📜 历史** — 古希腊三面环海，鱼类是餐桌上最重要的蛋白质来源。阿特纳奥斯（Athenaeus）在《智者之宴》中详细记录了数十种鱼的烹饪方法。古希腊人以橄榄油、香草和葡萄酒烹鱼，配方简洁而注重食材本味。橄榄油被希腊人视为"液体黄金"，不仅用于烹饪，还用于宗教仪式和运动员涂身。
> **📜 History** — *Surrounded by sea on three sides, ancient Greeks relied on fish as their primary protein. Athenaeus documented dozens of fish recipes in "Deipnosophistae" (The Dinner-Table Philosophers). Greeks cooked fish simply with olive oil, herbs, and wine, emphasizing the natural flavor of ingredients. Olive oil was considered "liquid gold" — used not only for cooking but also in religious rites and for anointing athletes.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **预热与准备** — 烤箱预热至400°F（200°C）。鱼排拍干，两面撒盐、胡椒和牛至。
   *Preheat oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat fish dry and season both sides with salt, pepper, and oregano.*

2. **摆盘** — 烤盘铺锡纸，放上鱼排，周围摆放蒜片、橄榄和小番茄。淋上橄榄油，挤上半个柠檬汁。
   *Line a baking sheet with foil. Place fish fillets in the center, scatter garlic slices, olives, and cherry tomatoes around them. Drizzle with olive oil and squeeze half a lemon over everything.*

3. **烤制** — 烤15-20分钟，至鱼肉用叉子轻轻一拨即可分开、呈不透明白色。
   *Bake 15-20 minutes until fish flakes easily with a fork and turns opaque white.*

4. **上桌** — 装盘后淋上烤盘中的汁液，配剩余柠檬角，趁热食用。
   *Plate the fish, spoon pan juices over it, serve with remaining lemon wedges, and enjoy hot.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 古希腊人认为海鱼比淡水鱼更高级，金枪鱼和鲈鱼是最受推崇的品种。
  *Ancient Greeks considered saltwater fish superior to freshwater; tuna and sea bass were the most esteemed varieties.*

- 小番茄在此配方中为现代添加——古希腊没有番茄（美洲作物），原版可能使用更多橄榄和香草。
  *Cherry tomatoes are a modern addition — ancient Greeks had no tomatoes (a New World crop). The original likely used more olives and herbs.*

- 古希腊的鱼市场（ichthyopolis）是城市生活的中心，柏拉图和亚里士多德都曾描述鱼贩的吆喝声。
  *Greek fish markets (ichthyopolis) were central to city life; both Plato and Aristotle described the cries of fish vendors.*
