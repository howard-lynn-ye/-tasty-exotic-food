# 元代蒙古烤全牛 | Yuan Mongol Roast Beef
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/元代蒙古烤全牛.svg" width="500" alt="元代蒙古烤全牛"></p>

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 元朝 Yuan Dynasty (~1300AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 蒙古草原 Mongolian Steppe
> **类型 Type:** 宴席大菜 Grand Banquet Dish

---

## 典故 Historical Background

元朝蒙古贵族继承了草原游牧民族的烹饪传统，烤全牛是最为隆重的宴席菜肴，仅在可汗登基、大军凯旋等重大庆典时才会制作。《饮膳正要》中记载了蒙元宫廷的肉食烹饪技法。整头牛在地坑中慢烤一整日，皮脆肉嫩，香飘数里，可供百人共享。

Yuan Mongol nobles inherited the culinary traditions of steppe nomads. Whole roast beef was the most prestigious banquet dish, prepared only for grand occasions such as a Khan's coronation or a victorious army's return. *Yinshan Zhengyao* documented Mongol court cooking techniques. An entire ox was slow-roasted in an earth pit for a full day — crispy skin, tender meat, aroma carrying for miles, feeding a hundred guests.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **备坑 Prepare pit:** 掘地坑深三尺、宽五尺，底部铺满干硬木与炭，燃烧至通红。Dig an earth pit 3 feet deep, 5 feet wide, fill the bottom with hardwood and charcoal, burn until glowing red.
2. **处理牛 Prepare ox:** 整牛去内脏后洗净，内外抹满粗盐与碾碎的香草，腹腔内塞入野葱与羊尾脂。Clean and gut the ox, rub inside and out with coarse salt and crushed herbs, stuff the cavity with wild onions and sheep tail fat.
3. **腌渍 Marinate:** 外皮涂抹马奶酒与黑胡椒，使之入味。Rub the skin with airag and black pepper to season.
4. **架烤 Roast:** 以粗铁架横跨地坑，将牛固定于上，上覆湿麻布与泥土封口，以余温与炭火慢烤。Set the ox on a heavy iron frame over the pit, cover with wet burlap and earth to seal, slow-roast using residual heat and coals.
5. **翻烤 Turn:** 每隔两个时辰开坑翻转一次，补充木炭，淋马奶酒保持湿润，全程需一日一夜。Open the pit every 2 hours to turn, replenish charcoal, and baste with airag. The entire process takes a full day and night.
6. **宴飨 Feast:** 烤成后抬至宴席中央，由主人持刀分割，蘸盐食用。When done, carry to the center of the banquet; the host carves with a knife, guests dip in salt to eat.

## 备注 Notes

- 此菜需数十人协作完成，是集体盛宴的象征。This dish requires dozens of people working together — a symbol of communal feasting.
- 现代可用小牛或大块牛腿肉在烤炉中模拟此法。Modern adaptation: use a calf or large leg roast in a covered oven to approximate.
