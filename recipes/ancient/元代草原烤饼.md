# 元代草原烤饼 | Yuan Grassland Flatbread (元代, ~1300 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/元代草原烤饼.svg" width="500" alt="元代草原烤饼"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、元代、蒙古、面食

> **📜 历史** — 元代蒙古骑兵征服了从太平洋到东欧的辽阔疆域，草原烤饼（蒙古语称"胡日赫"）是他们最重要的行军口粮之一。这种烤饼不需要酵母，用动物油脂和面粉制成，在铁板或石板上快速烙制。成吉思汗的军队将烤饼塞在马鞍下，利用骑行的体温和摩擦保持温热。元朝建立后，宫廷将这种粗犷的行军食品精致化，加入奶酪和香料。
> **📜 History** — *Yuan-era Mongol cavalry conquered territory from the Pacific to Eastern Europe; grassland flatbread (called "khuushuur" in Mongolian) was essential campaign rations. These unleavened breads were made with animal fat and flour, quickly grilled on iron or stone slabs. Genghis Khan's soldiers tucked flatbreads under their saddles, using body heat and friction to keep them warm. After the Yuan Dynasty was established, court cooks refined this rugged field food with cheese and spices.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 黄油（或羊油） | Butter (or mutton fat) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 奶酪碎（可选） | Shredded cheese (optional) | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面** — 面粉加盐混合，倒入温水和融化的黄油，揉成光滑面团，盖湿布醒面15分钟。
   *Mix flour and salt, pour in warm water and melted butter, knead into a smooth dough, cover with a damp cloth and rest 15 minutes.*

2. **擀饼** — 面团分成6份，每份擀成薄圆饼，撒葱花、小茴香和奶酪碎，对折后再擀平。
   *Divide dough into 6 pieces, roll each into a thin disc, sprinkle with scallion, cumin, and cheese, fold in half and roll flat again.*

3. **烙制** — 平底锅中火加热（不放油或少许油），放入饼烙制每面2-3分钟至出现金黄焦斑。
   *Heat a skillet over medium heat (no oil or just a touch). Cook each flatbread 2-3 minutes per side until golden spots appear.*

4. **食用** — 趁热食用，可搭配奶茶或烤肉。
   *Serve hot, paired with milk tea or grilled meat.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 马可波罗在游记中记述了蒙古人的干粮制作方法，包括将奶制品晒干随身携带。
  *Marco Polo described Mongol provisions in his travelogue, including drying dairy products for portable rations.*

- 蒙古军队的后勤补给极简高效：每名骑兵携带干肉、奶干和烤饼，可自给自足十天以上。
  *Mongol military logistics were minimalist and efficient: each rider carried dried meat, dried dairy, and flatbread — self-sufficient for over ten days.*
