# 大航海时代水手饼干 | Age of Exploration Hardtack (大航海时代, ~1500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 60分钟 | 💰 ~$1/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、大航海、面食、素食

> **📜 历史** — 水手饼干（hardtack/ship's biscuit）是大航海时代所有远洋船只的标配口粮。这种只用面粉、水和少许盐制成的超硬饼干可以保存数月甚至数年不变质，是哥伦布、麦哲伦和德雷克船队赖以生存的"铁粮"。水手们抱怨饼干硬得可以当砖头用，常常需要泡在汤或咖啡中才能咬动。更糟糕的是，长期航行后饼干中往往爬满了象鼻虫，水手们学会在黑暗中进食以避免看到虫子。
> **📜 History** — *Hardtack (ship's biscuit) was standard rations on every ocean-going vessel during the Age of Exploration. Made with only flour, water, and a pinch of salt, this rock-hard cracker could last months or even years without spoiling — the "iron bread" that sustained the fleets of Columbus, Magellan, and Drake. Sailors complained it was hard enough to use as a brick, often needing to soak it in soup or coffee to chew. Worse, after long voyages, hardtack was typically infested with weevils — sailors learned to eat in the dark to avoid seeing the bugs.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 水 | Water | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面** — 面粉加盐混合，慢慢加水揉成非常硬实的面团（比普通面团干硬得多），揉5分钟至表面光滑。
   *Mix flour and salt, gradually add water to form a very stiff dough (much drier than regular dough). Knead 5 minutes until surface is smooth.*

2. **擀制打孔** — 面团擀成约1厘米厚的大片，切成7×7厘米的方块，用叉子在每块上均匀戳满小孔（防止膨胀）。
   *Roll dough to about 1cm thickness. Cut into 7×7cm squares, then poke holes evenly all over each square with a fork (to prevent puffing).*

3. **低温烘烤** — 烤箱预热至120°C（250°F），将饼干放在烤盘上烘烤60分钟，中途翻面一次，烤至完全干燥变硬。冷却后可保存数月。
   *Preheat oven to 120°C (250°F). Place on a baking sheet and bake 60 minutes, flipping once halfway. Bake until completely dry and hard. Once cooled, stores for months.*
