# 毛利地窖烤肉 | Maori Hangi (新西兰, ~1300 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 烹饪 3-4小时 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、大洋洲、猪肉、地坑烤

> **📜 历史** — Hangi（杭伊）是新西兰毛利人最神圣的烹饪方式，约于13世纪随波利尼西亚航海者传入新西兰。食物被埋入地下，用火山岩加热的蒸汽慢慢烤熟，赋予食材独特的泥土芳香。Hangi不仅是烹饪，更是毛利社区凝聚力的象征，每逢重大庆典（hui）必不可少。
> **📜 History** — *Hangi is the most sacred cooking method of New Zealand's Maori people, brought to Aotearoa around the 13th century by Polynesian navigators. Food is buried underground and slowly steam-cooked by heat from volcanic rocks, imparting a unique earthy aroma. Hangi is not merely cooking — it symbolizes Maori community bonds and is essential at every major gathering (hui).*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 猪肩肉（带骨） | Pork shoulder (bone-in) | 1公斤 | 2.2 lbs |
| 鸡腿 | Chicken legs | 4只 | 4 pieces |
| 红薯（kuumara） | Sweet potato (kuumara) | 4个 | 4 |

| 卷心菜（切四瓣） | Cabbage (quartered) | 1个 | 1 |
| 洋葱（整颗） | Whole onions | 4个 | 4 |
| 盐 | Salt | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 水 | Water | 适量 | as needed |

## 做法 | Directions（现代烤箱改良版 | Modern Oven Adaptation）

1. **准备肉类** — 猪肩肉和鸡腿用盐和胡椒均匀涂抹，室温放置20分钟。
   *Rub pork shoulder and chicken legs evenly with salt and pepper. Let sit at room temperature for 20 minutes.*

2. **分层摆放** — 在大号深烤盘底部放一层洋葱，上铺猪肩肉和鸡腿，四周摆放红薯和南瓜块，最上层铺卷心菜。加入1杯水。
   *Layer onions at the bottom of a large deep roasting pan. Place pork and chicken on top. Arrange sweet potato and pumpkin around the sides. Top with cabbage. Add 1 cup water.*

3. **密封慢烤** — 用锡纸将烤盘密封两层，放入150°C（300°F）烤箱，烤3-4小时至肉质酥烂。
   *Double-wrap the pan tightly with foil. Place in a 150°C (300°F) oven and cook 3-4 hours until meat is fork-tender.*

4. **装盘** — 揭开锡纸（注意蒸汽），将肉类和蔬菜分别摆盘，淋上锅底汁液。
   *Carefully remove foil (watch for steam). Arrange meat and vegetables on a platter and drizzle with pan juices.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 传统Hangi需挖一个约1米深的地坑，用火将河卵石烧红，再将包好的食物埋入石头间，覆土焖烤。
  *Traditional hangi requires digging a pit about 1 meter deep, heating river stones with fire, burying wrapped food among the stones, and covering with earth.*

- 毛利人最初用蕨根和鸟肉制作Hangi，猪肉和红薯（kuumara）是后来引入的食材。
  *The earliest hangi used fern root and bird meat; pork and kuumara (sweet potato) were later introductions.*

- 在毛利文化中，Hangi的准备工作由男性完成，这是一种社会责任和荣誉的体现。
  *In Maori culture, hangi preparation is done by men — it is an expression of social responsibility and honor.*
