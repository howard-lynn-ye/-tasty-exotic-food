# 宋代梅花汤饼 | Song Dynasty Plum Blossom Soup Dumplings (宋代, ~1100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、宋代、面食、汤品

> **📜 历史** — 梅花汤饼是宋代文人雅士钟爱的精致面食，记载于林洪的《山家清供》。厨师将面片用梅花形铁模压出花形，放入清汤中煮熟，汤清面白如梅花飘落水面，极具诗意。宋代是中国饮食文化的巅峰时期，市井繁华，夜市美食遍布汴京（今开封），烹饪已从果腹上升为艺术。
> **📜 History** — *Plum Blossom Soup Cakes were an elegant noodle dish beloved by Song Dynasty literati, recorded in Lin Hong's "Mountain Home Provisions." Cooks used plum blossom-shaped iron molds to cut dough into flower shapes, then simmered them in clear broth — white blossoms floating on a limpid surface, exquisitely poetic. The Song era was the pinnacle of Chinese food culture, with night markets thriving in Bianjing (modern Kaifeng) and cooking elevated from sustenance to art.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 水 | Water (for dough) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 鸡高汤 | Chicken broth | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 姜片 | Ginger slices | 3片 | 3 slices |
| 枸杞 | Goji berries | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 小葱（切花） | Scallion (sliced) | 1根 | 1 stalk |
| 白胡椒粉 | White pepper | 少许 | a pinch |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面** — 面粉加水揉成光滑面团，盖上湿布醒面15分钟。
   *Mix flour and water, knead into a smooth dough, cover with a damp cloth, and rest for 15 minutes.*

2. **切梅花形** — 将面团擀至约2毫米薄，用小花形饼干模（约1.5寸）压出梅花形面片。无模具可用刀切成小菱形片。
   *Roll dough to about 2mm thin. Use a small flower-shaped cookie cutter (~1.5 inches) to stamp out plum blossom shapes. If no cutter is available, cut into small diamond shapes with a knife.*

3. **煮清汤** — 鸡高汤加姜片烧开，放入枸杞，调入盐和白胡椒粉。
   *Bring chicken broth to a boil with ginger slices. Add goji berries, salt, and white pepper.*

4. **煮面片** — 将梅花面片逐一放入沸腾的汤中，煮2-3分钟至面片浮起、熟透。
   *Drop the plum blossom dough pieces one by one into the boiling broth. Cook 2-3 minutes until they float and are cooked through.*

5. **装盘** — 盛入碗中，撒上葱花，淋少许香油。面片浮于清汤中如梅花落水。
   *Ladle into bowls, sprinkle with scallion, and drizzle with sesame oil. The floating dough pieces resemble plum blossoms on water.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 《山家清供》是南宋林洪所著的食谱集，记录了大量文人雅食，强调食物的审美意趣。
  *"Mountain Home Provisions" by Lin Hong is a Southern Song recipe collection emphasizing the aesthetic pleasures of food among the literati.*

- 宋代"汤饼"泛指在汤中煮的面食，并非现代意义上的饼。
  *In the Song Dynasty, "tang bing" (soup cakes) broadly referred to any noodle or dough cooked in broth, not cakes in the modern sense.*

- 梅花在中国文化中象征高洁品格，将食物做成梅花形体现了宋人对雅致生活的追求。
  *The plum blossom symbolizes noble character in Chinese culture; shaping food as blossoms reflects the Song pursuit of refined living.*
