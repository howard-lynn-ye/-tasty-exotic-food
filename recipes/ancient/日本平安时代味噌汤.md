# 日本平安时代味噌汤 | Heian Period Miso Soup (平安时代, ~900 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、日本、汤品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 味噌（みそ）在奈良时代（710-794年）从中国传入日本，到平安时代（794-1185年）已成为贵族饮食的重要组成。《延喜式》（927年）详细记录了味噌的制造与税收规定，当时味噌是珍贵的调味品，仅供贵族享用。平安贵族将味噌溶于出汁（dashi）制成汤品，搭配豆腐和海藻饮用。这种做法在�的仓时代传入武士阶层，最终在室町时代成为日本全民日常饮食的基石。
> **📜 History** — *Miso was transmitted from China to Japan during the Nara period (710-794), becoming an important part of aristocratic cuisine by the Heian period (794-1185). The Engishiki (927 AD) documented miso production regulations and taxes — at that time, miso was a precious condiment reserved for nobility. Heian aristocrats dissolved miso in dashi broth and served it with tofu and seaweed. This practice spread to the samurai class during the Kamakura period and eventually became a cornerstone of daily Japanese cuisine for all classes by the Muromachi period.*

| 白味噌 | White miso paste | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 嫩豆腐（切小块） | Soft tofu (cubed small) | 150g | 5 oz |

| 葱花 | Chopped scallion | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **制作出汁** — 锅中放3杯水和一片昆布，中火加热至即将沸腾时取出昆布，加入一把柴鱼片煮1分钟后过滤。（或直接用出汁粉加水。）
   *Place 3 cups water and a piece of kombu in a pot. Heat on medium until just before boiling, remove kombu. Add a handful of bonito flakes, cook 1 minute, then strain. (Or use instant dashi powder with water.)*

2. **加入豆腐和海藻** — 出汁中放入豆腐块和泡发的裙带菜，小火加热2-3分钟，不要煮沸。
   *Add tofu cubes and rehydrated wakame to the dashi. Heat gently on low 2-3 minutes — do not boil.*

3. **溶入味噌** — 关火，用筷子将味噌在漏勺中化入汤中（避免直接丢入产生结块），搅匀后盛碗撒葱花。
   *Turn off heat. Dissolve miso through a small strainer into the soup using chopsticks (to prevent lumps). Stir gently, ladle into bowls, and top with chopped scallion.*
