# 暹罗王国椰汁鸡 | Siamese Coconut Chicken

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 阿瑜陀耶王朝 Ayutthaya Kingdom (~1400AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 暹罗（今泰国） Siam (modern Thailand)
> **类型 Type:** 汤菜 Soup

---

## 典故 Historical Background

椰汁鸡汤（ต้มข่าไก่）源于暹罗阿瑜陀耶王朝时期，是宫廷御厨以热带食材创造的经典汤品。阿瑜陀耶作为东南亚最繁华的贸易中心，汇聚了来自中国、印度、波斯的香料，与本地的椰浆、南姜、柠檬草融合，形成了独特的暹罗风味。此汤以其温和的椰香与清新的草本气息，被视为暹罗宫廷待客的上品。

Coconut chicken soup (tom kha gai) originated during the Ayutthaya Kingdom, created by royal chefs using tropical ingredients. Ayutthaya, Southeast Asia's most prosperous trade hub, brought together spices from China, India, and Persia, merging them with local coconut cream, galangal, and lemongrass into a uniquely Siamese flavor. This soup, with its gentle coconut richness and fresh herbal notes, was considered a premium dish for receiving guests at the Siamese court.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鸡腿肉 Chicken thigh | 300克 300g |
| 椰浆 Coconut cream | 2杯 2 cups |
| 南姜 Galangal | 5片 5 slices |
| 柠檬草 Lemongrass | 2根 2 stalks |
| 青柠叶 Kaffir lime leaves | 5片 5 leaves |
| 草菇 Straw mushrooms | 100克 100g |
| 小米椒 Bird's eye chili | 3-5个 3-5 |
| 鱼露 Fish sauce | 2大匙 2 tbsp |
| 青柠汁 Lime juice | 3大匙 3 tbsp |
| 香菜 Cilantro | 适量 Garnish |

## 做法 Preparation

1. **备料 Prepare aromatics:** 南姜切厚片拍裂，柠檬草切段拍扁，青柠叶撕去中脉，小米椒拍裂。Slice galangal thick and smash, cut lemongrass into sections and bruise, tear the central vein from lime leaves, crush chilies.
2. **煮椰浆 Heat coconut cream:** 一半椰浆入锅中火煮至表面冒油花（破裂），加入南姜、柠檬草、青柠叶煮出香味。Heat half the coconut cream on medium until oil separates (cracks), add galangal, lemongrass, and lime leaves to release fragrance.
3. **煮鸡 Cook chicken:** 鸡腿肉切块放入，翻动使椰浆均匀包裹鸡肉，煮至变色。Add sliced chicken thigh, stir to coat in coconut cream, cook until color changes.
4. **加汤 Add remaining coconut:** 倒入剩余椰浆与草菇，加鱼露调味，小火煮至鸡肉熟透。Pour in remaining coconut cream and mushrooms, season with fish sauce, simmer until chicken is cooked through.
5. **调味 Final seasoning:** 关火后加入青柠汁与拍裂的小米椒——酸味与辣味不可加热过久以保持清新。After turning off heat, add lime juice and crushed chilies — acidity and heat should not be cooked too long to maintain freshness.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 盛入碗中，撒香菜叶，配茉莉香米饭。Ladle into bowls, garnish with cilantro leaves, serve alongside jasmine rice.

## 备注 Notes

- 此汤与冬阴功的区别在于基底——椰汁鸡以椰浆为主体，口感更温和柔滑。The difference from tom yum lies in the base — tom kha uses coconut cream, yielding a gentler, creamier texture.
- 南姜是此汤的灵魂，不可用普通生姜替代，二者风味截然不同。Galangal is the soul of this soup and cannot be replaced with common ginger — their flavors are entirely different.
