# 苏美尔啤酒面包 | Sumerian Beer Bread (苏美尔文明, ~3000 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 50分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、苏美尔、面食、素食

> **📜 历史** — 苏美尔人是已知最早酿造啤酒的文明，他们将面包发酵后制成啤酒，也用啤酒反过来制作面包。《尼卡西颂歌》（Hymn to Ninkasi, ~1800 BC）是现存最古老的啤酒配方，记录了用大麦面包"bappir"发酵酿酒的过程。这种面包质地密实、微带酸味，是美索不达米亚平原上工人和祭司的日常口粮。
> **📜 History** — *The Sumerians were among the earliest known civilizations to brew beer, using fermented bread to make beer and beer to make bread. The Hymn to Ninkasi (~1800 BC) is the oldest surviving beer recipe, describing how barley bread "bappir" was fermented into beer. This dense, slightly sour bread was daily sustenance for workers and priests across the Mesopotamian plains.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 全麦面粉 | Whole wheat flour | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 大麦粉 | Barley flour | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 啤酒（深色为佳） | Beer (dark preferred) | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 椰枣（切碎） | Dates (chopped) | 6颗 | 6 pieces |
| 芝麻 | Sesame seeds | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **混合面团** — 将全麦面粉、大麦粉和盐混合，加入啤酒和蜂蜜，搅拌成浓稠面糊，折入切碎的椰枣。
   *Mix whole wheat flour, barley flour, and salt. Add beer and honey, stir into a thick batter, and fold in chopped dates.*

2. **成型与发酵** — 面团放入抹油的烤盘，表面撒芝麻，盖上湿布室温静置30分钟。
   *Place dough in an oiled baking pan, sprinkle sesame seeds on top, cover with a damp cloth, and rest at room temperature for 30 minutes.*

3. **烘烤** — 烤箱预热至190°C（375°F），烘烤40-50分钟至表面金黄、敲击底部声音空洞。
   *Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F). Bake 40-50 minutes until golden brown and hollow-sounding when tapped on the bottom.*
