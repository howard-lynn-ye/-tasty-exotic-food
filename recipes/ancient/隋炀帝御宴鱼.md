# 隋炀帝御宴鱼 Emperor Yang of Sui Imperial Fish

> 约公元600年 | 隋朝 | 运河巡游御宴
> Circa 600 AD | Sui Dynasty | Grand Canal Imperial Banquet

---

## 简介 Introduction

隋炀帝杨广（569-618）虽以奢靡著称，却也是推动中国南北饮食文化交流的关键
人物。他三下江都（今扬州），乘龙舟沿大运河南巡，沿途御厨采集各地鲜鱼，
以北方的浓酱搭配南方的鲜甜技法烹制。这道御宴鱼以酱烧为主，鱼肉嫩滑、
酱香浓郁，再现了运河之上的奢华味道。

Emperor Yang of Sui (569-618), though famed for extravagance, was key to
north-south culinary exchange. He sailed dragon boats down the Grand Canal
to Jiangdu (Yangzhou) three times. Imperial chefs gathered fish along the
route, combining northern rich sauces with southern fresh-sweet techniques.
This braised fish recreates the lavish flavors of a canal-top banquet.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鲤鱼（或鳜鱼）Carp or mandarin fish | 1条（约700g）|
| 豆酱 Bean paste | 30g |
| 黄酒 Rice wine | 30ml |
| 老姜 Old ginger | 25g |
| 大葱 Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 蜂蜜（或麦芽糖）Honey or maltose | 15g |
| 米醋 Rice vinegar | 10ml |
| 花椒 Sichuan peppercorn | 3g |
| 陈皮 Dried tangerine peel | 一小片 / a small piece |
| 植物油 Oil | 40ml |
| 清水 Water | 300ml |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **处理鱼 Prep fish**: 鲤鱼去鳞去内脏，鱼身打花刀，抹盐和黄酒腌15分钟。
   Scale and gut fish, score the flesh, rub with salt and wine, rest 15 min.

2. **煎鱼 Pan-fry**: 热油将鱼两面煎至金黄定型，取出备用。
   Pan-fry fish on both sides until golden and firm, set aside.

3. **炒酱 Fry sauce**: 锅留底油，爆香姜片、葱段、花椒和陈皮，加入豆酱炒香。
   In remaining oil, fry ginger, scallion, peppercorn, and peel, add bean paste.

4. **烧鱼 Braise fish**: 放回鱼，加清水、蜂蜜和米醋，大火煮沸后转中小火。
   Return fish, add water, honey, and vinegar, bring to boil then reduce heat.

5. **收汁 Reduce**: 中小火烧15分钟，期间用勺将汁浇淋鱼身，至汤汁浓稠。
   Cook 15 min on medium-low, baste fish with sauce until thick and glossy.

6. **装盘 Plate**: 将鱼完整装盘，浇上浓酱，撒葱丝点缀。
   Place fish whole on plate, pour thick sauce over, garnish with scallion shreds.
