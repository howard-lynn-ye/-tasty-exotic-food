## 历史背景 Historical Background

中世纪的肉馅饼是英格兰最具代表性的日常食物之一。厚重的面壳（coffyn）最初只是容器而非食用部分，用以密封肉类长时间烘烤并保存。馅料常混合多种香料以掩盖不新鲜的味道。此版本改良为可食用的酥皮，保留了中世纪的香料组合。

The medieval meat pie was one of England's most iconic everyday foods. The thick pastry shell ("coffyn") was originally a container — not meant for eating — sealing meat for long baking and preservation. Fillings used abundant spices to mask less-than-fresh flavors. This version uses edible shortcrust while preserving the medieval spice blend.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 猪肩肉 Pork shoulder (切丁 diced) | 400g |
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 300g |
| 猪油或黄油 Lard or butter (冷) | 150g |
| 肉桂粉 Ground cinnamon | 半茶匙 ½ tsp |
| 肉豆蔻粉 Ground nutmeg | 四分之一茶匙 ¼ tsp |
| 盐与黑胡椒 Salt & black pepper | 适量 to taste |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 做饼皮 Make pastry
面粉加盐混合，切入冷猪油至粗砂状。加冷水3-4汤匙揉成面团，不要过度揉搓。分出三分之一做盖子，其余擀开铺入抹了油的烤盘中。
Mix flour and salt, cut in cold lard until sandy. Add 3-4 tbsp cold water and form dough — do not overwork. Set aside one-third for the lid. Roll the rest and line a greased pie tin.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 调馅 Prepare filling
猪肉丁加入肉桂、肉豆蔻、盐和胡椒拌匀，填入饼皮中，略微堆高。倒入2汤匙水保持湿润。
Toss pork with cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and pepper. Fill the pastry shell, mounding slightly. Pour in 2 tbsp water for moisture.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 封盖烘烤 Seal and bake
擀好上皮覆盖，捏紧边缘密封，中央戳一个小洞排气。刷蛋液，放入200°C烤箱烤40-45分钟至外皮金黄。
Roll out the lid, cover the pie, and crimp edges to seal. Poke a small steam hole in the center. Brush with egg wash. Bake at 200°C for 40-45 minutes until golden.

## 替代建议 Substitutions

| 原料 Original | 替代 Substitute | 说明 Note |
|---|---|---|
| 猪油 Lard | 黄油 Butter | 更易获取 More accessible |
