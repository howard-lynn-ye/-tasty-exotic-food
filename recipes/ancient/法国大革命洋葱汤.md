# 法国大革命洋葱汤 | French Revolution Onion Soup (法国大革命, ~1789 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 60分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、法国、汤品

> **📜 历史** — 法式洋葱汤的历史可追溯至古罗马，但它在法国大革命前后的巴黎达到了标志性地位。传说路易十五有一次深夜狩猎归来，厨房只剩洋葱、黄油和香槟，于是煮出了第一碗"现代"洋葱汤。大革命期间，巴黎中央市场Les Halles的工人和革命者们在深夜以洋葱汤暖身充饥，它因此成为"平民的汤"。安东尼·博维利尔（Beauvilliers）在1782年开设的巴黎第一家现代餐厅中将焗烤洋葱汤推上了精致料理的舞台。
> **📜 History** — *French onion soup dates back to ancient Rome but achieved iconic status in Paris around the French Revolution. Legend says Louis XV, returning from a late-night hunt to find only onions, butter, and champagne in the kitchen, created the first "modern" version. During the Revolution, workers and revolutionaries at Les Halles central market warmed themselves with onion soup in the small hours — earning it the title "the people's soup." Beauvilliers, who opened Paris's first modern restaurant in 1782, elevated gratinéed onion soup to fine dining.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 黄洋葱（切薄丝） | Yellow onions (thinly sliced) | 4个大 | 4 large |
| 无盐黄油 | Unsalted butter | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 牛肉高汤 | Beef broth | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 干白葡萄酒 | Dry white wine | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 法棍面包（切片烤脆） | Baguette (sliced and toasted) | 4片 | 4 slices |
| 格吕耶尔奶酪（磨碎） | Gruyère cheese (grated) | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 百里香 | Thyme | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 月桂叶 | Bay leaf | 1片 | 1 leaf |
| 盐和黑胡椒 | Salt and black pepper | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **焦糖化洋葱** — 大锅中融化黄油，加入所有洋葱丝，中小火慢炒35-40分钟，不时翻动，直至洋葱变成深琥珀色的焦糖色（这是关键步骤，不可急躁）。
   *Melt butter in a large pot. Add all sliced onions and cook on medium-low 35-40 minutes, stirring occasionally, until onions turn deep amber caramel color (this is the crucial step — do not rush).*

2. **煮汤** — 加入白葡萄酒烧至酒精蒸发，倒入牛肉高汤，加百里香和月桂叶，煮沸后小火炖15分钟。以盐和胡椒调味，去掉月桂叶。
   *Add white wine and cook until alcohol evaporates. Pour in beef broth, add thyme and bay leaf. Bring to a boil, then simmer 15 minutes. Season with salt and pepper, remove bay leaf.*

3. **焗烤上桌** — 汤盛入耐高温碗中，放上烤面包片，厚厚撒上格吕耶尔奶酪，放入烤箱上层用broil功能烤3-5分钟至奶酪金黄冒泡。
   *Ladle soup into oven-safe bowls, top with toasted baguette slices, and pile on Gruyère cheese. Broil on the top rack 3-5 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbling.*
