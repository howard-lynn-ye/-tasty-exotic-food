# 罗马角斗士粥 | Ancient Roman Gladiator Barley Porridge (罗马帝国, ~100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、罗马帝国、谷物、素食

> **📜 历史** — 古罗马角斗士被称为"大麦人"（hordearii），因为大麦粥是他们的主要饮食。考古学家在以弗所角斗士墓地的骨骼分析中证实，角斗士以大量大麦和豆类为食，辅以干果补充能量。这种高碳水化合物饮食帮助他们在皮下形成脂肪层，保护内脏免受刀伤。
> **📜 History** — *Roman gladiators were called "hordearii" (barley men) because barley porridge was their staple diet. Bone analysis from gladiator cemeteries at Ephesus confirmed they consumed large quantities of barley and beans, supplemented with dried fruit for energy. This high-carbohydrate diet helped them build a subcutaneous fat layer that protected organs from blade wounds.*

| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **浸泡豆类** — 将干豆提前浸泡8小时或隔夜，沥干备用。
   *Soak the dried beans for 8 hours or overnight, then drain.*

2. **煮大麦和豆** — 大麦和豆放入锅中加4杯水，大火煮沸后转小火，盖上盖子炖煮35-40分钟至大麦软烂、豆子熟透。
   *Combine barley and beans in a pot with 4 cups of water. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 35-40 minutes until barley is tender and beans are cooked through.*

3. **加入干果调味** — 加入切碎的无花果、椰枣、蜂蜜、小茴香粉和盐，搅拌均匀，再煮5分钟使味道融合。
   *Stir in chopped figs, dates, honey, cumin, and salt. Cook for 5 more minutes to let flavors meld.*

4. **装盘** — 盛入碗中，淋上橄榄油即可食用。
   *Ladle into bowls and drizzle with olive oil before serving.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 古罗马医学家盖伦（Galen, ~130-210 AD）在其著作中记录了角斗士的饮食配方。
  *The physician Galen (~130-210 AD) documented gladiator diet formulas in his medical writings.*

- 罗马人称大麦粥为"polenta"（与现代意大利玉米糊不同），是平民和士兵的日常主食。
  *Romans called barley porridge "polenta" (different from modern Italian cornmeal polenta) — it was everyday food for commoners and soldiers.*

- 干果在古罗马被视为重要的能量来源，尤其在长途行军中不可或缺。
  *Dried fruits were considered essential energy sources in ancient Rome, especially during long marches.*
