# 江户时代寿司原型 | Edo Period Proto-Sushi

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 江户时代 Edo Period (~1800AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 江户（今东京） Edo (modern Tokyo)
> **类型 Type:** 街头小吃 Street Food

---

## 典故 Historical Background

江户前寿司（握り寿司）诞生于江户时代末期，由华屋与兵卫发明。与此前需要发酵数月的熟鮨不同，这种新式寿司以醋饭搭配新鲜鱼生，现做现吃。江户湾（东京湾）渔获丰富，渔民清晨捕获的鱼虾在市场旁的寿司摊上即刻变成美味。当时的寿司比今天大三倍，是站着用手抓食的快餐。

Edomae sushi (nigiri-zushi) was born in the late Edo period, invented by Hanaya Yohei. Unlike earlier narezushi requiring months of fermentation, this new style paired vinegared rice with fresh raw fish, made and eaten on the spot. Edo Bay (Tokyo Bay) was rich with seafood; fish caught at dawn became sushi at stalls by the market within hours. Edo-era sushi was three times larger than today's — fast food eaten standing, by hand.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **煮饭 Cook rice:** 短粒米洗净浸泡半个时辰，以釜锅蒸熟。Wash and soak rice for 30 minutes, steam in a traditional pot (kama).
2. **制醋饭 Season rice:** 米醋、砂糖、盐调匀加热至溶解，趁热拌入饭中，以木扇扇凉使饭粒有光泽。Mix vinegar, sugar, salt and heat until dissolved; fold into hot rice while fanning with a wooden fan for glossy grains.
3. **处理鱼 Prepare fish:** 鲔鱼与鲷鱼切成比现代更大的厚片；虾以盐水煮至变色后剖开摊平。Cut tuna and sea bream into thick slices larger than modern style; boil shrimp in salted water until pink, butterfly and flatten.
4. **烤玉子 Make tamago:** 鸡蛋加出汁与砂糖打匀，在方形锅中层层卷制成厚实的甜蛋卷。Beat eggs with dashi and sugar, roll layer by layer in a rectangular pan into a thick sweet omelette.
5. **握寿司 Form nigiri:** 手蘸醋水，取一团醋饭约为今日两倍大小，抹少许山葵于鱼片上，将鱼覆于饭团上，以手指轻捏成形。Wet hands with vinegar water, take a rice ball twice today's size, dab wasabi on the fish slice, drape fish over rice, gently press into shape.
6. **上桌 Serve:** 寿司排列于木板上，佐酱油与腌姜片食用。不用筷子，以手直接取食。Arrange sushi on a wooden board, serve with soy sauce and pickled ginger. Eat by hand, no chopsticks.

## 备注 Notes

- 江户时代的寿司会用醋、盐或酱油腌渍鱼肉以延长保鲜，并非全部使用生鱼。Edo-era sushi often cured fish in vinegar, salt, or soy sauce for preservation — not all fish was served raw.
- 寿司摊是日本最早的"快餐"形式，食客站在摊前数分钟即可吃完。Sushi stalls were Japan's earliest "fast food" — customers stood and finished eating in minutes.
