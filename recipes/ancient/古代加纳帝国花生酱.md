# 古代加纳帝国花生酱 | Ghana Empire Peanut Sauce

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 加纳帝国 Ghana Empire (~1000AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 西非萨赫勒地区 West African Sahel
> **类型 Type:** 酱料/炖菜 Sauce / Stew

---

## 典故 Historical Background

加纳帝国（又称瓦加杜帝国）是西非最早的强大帝国之一，控制着撒哈拉黄金贸易路线。花生（落花生）在西非有悠久的种植历史，花生酱炖菜是撒赫勒地区最基本的料理之一。帝国的商人与工匠以花生酱搭配谷物和肉食，为跨撒哈拉贸易的长途旅行提供高热量的食物。花生酱至今仍是西非料理的灵魂调味品。

The Ghana Empire (also known as Wagadou) was one of West Africa's earliest powerful empires, controlling trans-Saharan gold trade routes. Groundnuts have a long cultivation history in West Africa, and peanut sauce stew is one of the Sahel's most fundamental dishes. The empire's merchants and artisans paired peanut sauce with grains and meat, providing high-calorie sustenance for long trans-Saharan journeys. Peanut sauce remains the soul condiment of West African cuisine today.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 秋葵 Okra | 6根 6 pods |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 To taste |

## 做法 Preparation

1. **制花生酱 Make peanut paste:** 烤花生去壳去皮，在石磨上反复研磨至出油成细腻的酱状。Shell and skin roasted peanuts, grind repeatedly on a stone mill until oil releases and a smooth paste forms.
2. **处理鸡肉 Prepare chicken:** 鸡肉斩块，以盐、姜、蒜腌渍。Cut chicken into pieces, marinate with salt, ginger, and garlic.
3. **炒底料 Cook base:** 棕榈油烧热，炒洋葱至金黄，加入切碎的番茄和整只辣椒，炒至番茄化成酱。Heat palm oil, fry onion until golden, add chopped tomatoes and whole scotch bonnet, cook until tomatoes break down into sauce.
4. **煮鸡 Cook chicken:** 放入鸡块翻炒至表面变色，加水没过食材，大火煮沸后转小火。Add chicken, stir-fry until surface changes color, add water to cover, bring to a boil then reduce to low.
5. **加花生酱 Add peanut paste:** 花生酱用少量热汤化开，缓缓倒入锅中搅匀，小火炖煮至酱汁浓稠包裹鸡肉，约半个时辰。Dissolve peanut paste in a little hot broth, slowly pour into the pot while stirring, simmer until sauce thickens and coats chicken, about 30 minutes.
6. **加秋葵上桌 Add okra and serve:** 最后加入切段的秋葵煮熟，盛入木碗中，搭配小米饭或捣碎的薯类（富富）食用。Finally add sliced okra and cook through, ladle into wooden bowls, serve with millet rice or pounded yam (fufu).

## 备注 Notes

- 花生酱炖菜在西非各国有不同名称：加纳称"groundnut soup"，塞内加尔称"mafé"。Peanut stew has different names across West Africa: "groundnut soup" in Ghana, "mafé" in Senegal.
- 辣椒整只放入汤中提香而不弄破，以免过辣。The scotch bonnet is added whole for fragrance without breaking it, to avoid excessive heat.
