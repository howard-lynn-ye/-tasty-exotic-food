# 埃塞俄比亚英吉拉 | Ethiopian Injera (~500AD)

> ⏱ 准备15分+发酵48小时+烹饪20分 | 💰~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、阿克苏姆王国

> **📜 历史** — 英吉拉是埃塞俄比亚千年主食，用苔麸发酵制成的海绵状薄饼，既当餐盘又当餐具。
> **📜 History** — *Injera has been Ethiopia's staple for millennia — a spongy fermented teff flatbread that serves as both plate and utensil.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 苔麸粉 | Teff flour | 250g / 2 cups |
| 水 | Water | 500ml / 2 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 3g / 0.5 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 少许 / A little |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 发酵 | Ferment
苔麸粉加水搅匀，室温发酵2-3天至起泡有酸味。

Mix teff flour with water, ferment at room temperature 2-3 days until bubbly and sour.

### 2. 烙饼 | Cook
加盐调味，不粘锅刷薄油，倒入面糊摊成薄饼，盖锅盖蒸2分钟至表面满布气孔。

Add salt, grease a non-stick pan, pour batter thin, cover and steam 2 minutes until surface is full of holes.

### 3. 食用 | Serve
英吉拉铺盘底，配炖肉或蔬菜，用手撕饼包着吃。

Lay injera on plate, top with stews or vegetables, tear and wrap to eat by hand.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 发酵时间越长酸味越浓，可根据口味调整 | Longer fermentation yields more sourness; adjust to taste |
| 烙饼时只煎一面，不要翻面，盖锅盖利用蒸汽熟透 | Cook only one side; cover the pan and let steam do the rest |
| 面糊稠度应如薄煎饼糊，太稠则加水稀释 | Batter should be thin like crepe batter; add water if too thick |

---

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
英吉拉的历史可追溯至阿克苏姆王国时期，苔麸是埃塞俄比亚高原特有的谷物，颗粒极小，营养丰富。发酵工艺使英吉拉具有独特的酸味和海绵质地，这种"可食用餐盘"的概念在世界饮食文化中独树一帜。

*Injera dates back to the Aksumite Kingdom; teff is a tiny, nutrient-dense grain native to the Ethiopian highlands. The fermentation process gives injera its signature sour tang and spongy texture, making this "edible plate" concept unique in world food culture.*
