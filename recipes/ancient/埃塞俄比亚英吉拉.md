# 埃塞俄比亚英吉拉 | Ethiopian Injera (~500AD)

> ⏱ 准备15分+发酵48小时+烹饪20分 | 💰~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、阿克苏姆王国

> **📜 历史** — 英吉拉是埃塞俄比亚千年主食，用苔麸发酵制成的海绵状薄饼，既当餐盘又当餐具。
> **📜 History** — *Injera has been Ethiopia's staple for millennia — a spongy fermented teff flatbread that serves as both plate and utensil.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 苔麸粉 | Teff flour | 250g / 2 cups |
| 水 | Water | 500ml / 2 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 3g / 0.5 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 少许 / A little |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 发酵 | Ferment
苔麸粉加水搅匀，室温发酵2-3天至起泡有酸味。Mix teff flour with water, ferment at room temperature 2-3 days until bubbly and sour.

### 2. 烙饼 | Cook
加盐调味，不粘锅刷薄油，倒入面糊摊成薄饼，盖锅盖蒸2分钟至表面满布气孔。Add salt, grease a non-stick pan, pour batter thin, cover and steam 2 minutes until surface is full of holes.

### 3. 食用 | Serve
英吉拉铺盘底，配炖肉或蔬菜，用手撕饼包着吃。Lay injera on plate, top with stews or vegetables, tear and wrap to eat by hand.
