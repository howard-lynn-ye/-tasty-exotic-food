# 奥匈帝国古拉什 Austro-Hungarian Goulash

> **时代 Era:** 奥匈帝国 Austro-Hungarian Empire · 约公元1800年 (~1800 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 匈牙利大平原 Hungarian Great Plain
> **类型 Type:** 炖肉 Stew / 主菜 Main Course
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

古拉什（Gulyás）最初是匈牙利牧民在草原上用大锅炖煮牛肉的粗犷菜肴。随着奥匈帝国的建立，这道菜被引入维也纳宫廷，成为帝国的代表性菜肴之一。大量使用甜红椒粉（paprika）是其灵魂调味，红椒粉虽在16世纪传入匈牙利，但到18-19世纪才成为国民调料。

Goulash (Gulyás) was originally a rustic dish cooked by Hungarian herdsmen in cauldrons on the plains. With the establishment of the Austro-Hungarian Empire, it entered Viennese court cuisine and became an iconic imperial dish. Sweet paprika is its soul seasoning — though peppers arrived in Hungary in the 16th century, paprika only became the national spice in the 18th-19th centuries.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 牛腩 Beef chuck (切3cm块) | 600g |
| 洋葱 Onions (切丁 diced) | 3 个 large |
| 甜红椒粉 Sweet paprika | 3 汤匙 tbsp |
| 番茄膏 Tomato paste | 2 汤匙 tbsp |
| 土豆 Potatoes (切块 cubed) | 2 个 medium |
| 葛缕子 Caraway seeds | 1 茶匙 tsp |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 炒洋葱 Sweat onions
厚底锅中加猪油或植物油，中火炒洋葱丁15-20分钟至深金黄色且完全软烂。离火加入红椒粉迅速拌匀（红椒粉遇高温易焦苦）。
Heat lard or oil in a heavy pot. Cook onions over medium heat 15-20 minutes until deep golden and completely soft. Remove from heat and quickly stir in paprika (paprika burns easily at high heat).

### 第二步 Step 2 — 炖牛肉 Braise beef
加入牛肉块、番茄膏、葛缕子和足量清水没过食材。大火煮沸后转小火，加盖炖1.5小时。加入土豆块，再炖30分钟至土豆软烂。
Add beef, tomato paste, caraway seeds, and enough water to cover. Bring to a boil, reduce to low, cover and braise 1.5 hours. Add potatoes and cook 30 more minutes until tender.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 调味上桌 Season and serve
加盐调味。古拉什应是浓稠的汤菜，介于汤和炖菜之间。搭配粗面包或蛋面条食用。
Season with salt. Goulash should be a thick soupy stew — between a soup and a braise. Serve with crusty bread or egg noodles.
