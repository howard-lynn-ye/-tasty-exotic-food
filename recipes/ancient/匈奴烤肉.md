# 匈奴烤肉 | Xiongnu Nomad Grilled Meat (匈奴, ~200 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/匈奴烤肉.svg" width="500" alt="匈奴烤肉"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$9/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、匈奴、游牧、烤制、羊肉

> **📜 历史** — 匈奴是活跃在北方草原的游牧民族，与秦汉帝国长期对峙。司马迁《史记》记载匈奴人"逐水草迁徙，以畜牧为业"，主食为马奶和烤肉。匈奴骑兵在战马鞍下夹生肉，以体温和摩擦使肉变软，这种"马背肉"后来影响了欧洲的鞑靼牛排。草原烤肉以粗犷豪放著称，仅用盐和野葱调味。
> **📜 History** — *The Xiongnu were nomads of the northern steppes who rivaled the Qin and Han empires for centuries. Sima Qian's "Records of the Grand Historian" describes how they "migrated following water and grass, living by herding," subsisting on mare's milk and roasted meat. Xiongnu cavalry placed raw meat under their saddles to tenderize it through body heat and friction — this "saddle meat" later influenced European steak tartare. Steppe-style grilled meat was famously rugged, seasoned with nothing but salt and wild onions.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 羊腿肉（切大块） | Leg of lamb (large chunks) | 1.5磅 | 1.5 lb |
| 粗海盐 | Coarse sea salt | 2茶匙 | 2 tsp |
| 野葱或大葱 | Wild onions or scallions | 4根 | 4 stalks |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备肉 | Prepare Meat

羊腿肉切成3cm见方大块，以粗盐和黑胡椒均匀揉搓，静置15分钟使其入味。葱切成5cm长段备用。
Cut lamb into 3cm cubes. Rub evenly with coarse salt and black pepper, then rest 15 minutes to absorb flavor. Cut scallions into 5cm lengths.

### 2. 炭火烤制 | Grill Over Fire

烤架预热至高温（或烤箱broil模式）。肉块和葱段交替串在签子上，刷薄油，烤制每面4-5分钟至外焦内嫩。
Preheat grill to high (or oven broiler). Thread lamb and scallion sections alternately onto skewers, brush with oil, and grill 4-5 minutes per side until charred outside and tender inside.

### 3. 草原式上桌 | Serve Nomad-Style

无需额外调料，直接用手撕食。可配以酸奶或粗盐蘸食，还原游牧民族的豪迈食风。
No additional seasoning needed — tear and eat with your hands. Serve with yogurt or coarse salt for dipping, echoing the bold eating style of the nomads.

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 肉块不要切太小，3cm以上才能保证外焦内嫩的口感 | Keep chunks at least 3cm so the outside chars while the inside stays juicy |
| 2 | 木签需提前浸水30分钟防止烤焦，金属签更佳 | Soak wooden skewers 30 minutes to prevent burning; metal skewers are even better |
| 3 | 烤制时只翻一次面，反复翻动会导致肉汁流失 | Flip only once during grilling; frequent turning causes juice loss |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

匈奴帝国在冒顿单于(约公元前209年)时期达到鼎盛，控制了从蒙古高原到中亚的广阔草原。汉武帝曾派张骞出使西域以联合对抗匈奴，意外开辟了丝绸之路。匈奴人的饮食完全依赖畜牧——羊肉、马奶酒(kumiss)和奶酪是三大主食。这种以盐和葱为唯一调味的烤肉方式体现了游牧民族"就地取材、极简烹饪"的生存哲学，与中原精细的烹饪传统形成鲜明对比。

The Xiongnu Empire reached its zenith under Modu Chanyu (c. 209 BC), controlling vast steppes from the Mongolian Plateau to Central Asia. Emperor Wu of Han sent Zhang Qian west to forge alliances against the Xiongnu, inadvertently opening the Silk Road. The Xiongnu diet depended entirely on herding -- lamb, fermented mare's milk (kumiss), and cheese were the three staples. This style of grilling with only salt and onion reflects the nomadic philosophy of "cook with what the land provides, keep it minimal," a stark contrast to the refined culinary traditions of the Chinese heartland.
