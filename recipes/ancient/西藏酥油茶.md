# 西藏酥油茶 | Tibetan Butter Tea — Po Cha (吐蕃/藏区, ~800 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 5分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、西藏、饮品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 酥油茶（བོད་ཇ，po cha）是藏族人民千年不变的日常饮品，起源于吐蕃王朝时期与唐朝的茶马贸易。藏族谚语说"宁可三日无粮，不可一日无茶"。在海拔4000米以上的高原，酥油茶提供了急需的热量和脂肪，帮助抵御严寒。传统做法使用特制的木质茶桶（董莫）反复搅打，使茶与酥油充分融合，口感浓滑如汤。
> **📜 History** — *Butter tea (po cha) has been the daily drink of Tibetan people for over a millennium, originating from the Tea-Horse trade between the Tubo Kingdom and Tang Dynasty. A Tibetan proverb says "Better to go three days without food than one day without tea." At altitudes above 4,000 meters, butter tea provides essential calories and fat to combat extreme cold. Traditionally made in a special wooden churn (dongmo), the tea and butter are vigorously churned until smooth and soup-like.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 普洱茶或砖茶 | Pu-erh tea or brick tea | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 牦牛酥油或无盐黄油 | Yak butter or unsalted butter | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 全脂牛奶 | Whole milk | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 水 | Water | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 小苏打（可选） | Baking soda (optional) | 一小撮 | a tiny pinch |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煮茶** — 水倒入锅中煮沸，加入普洱茶叶和小苏打，转小火慢煮10分钟至茶汤浓郁深红，过滤茶叶。
   *Bring water to a boil in a pot, add pu-erh tea leaves and baking soda. Simmer on low 10 minutes until tea is deep reddish-brown. Strain out leaves.*

2. **搅打融合** — 茶汤倒入搅拌机，加入黄油、牛奶和盐，高速搅打2分钟至完全乳化、表面起泡。
   *Pour tea into a blender, add butter, milk, and salt. Blend on high for 2 minutes until fully emulsified and frothy.*

3. **趁热饮用** — 倒入预热的茶碗中，趁热慢慢品饮。传统上一天可饮数十碗。
   *Pour into a preheated tea bowl and sip slowly while hot. Traditionally, dozens of bowls are enjoyed throughout the day.*

## 替代食材 | American Substitutions

| 原始食材 | Original | 替代品 | Substitution |
|----------|----------|--------|-------------|
| 牦牛酥油 | Yak butter | 优质无盐黄油（如Kerrygold） | Premium unsalted butter (e.g., Kerrygold) |
| 普洱茶 | Pu-erh tea | 普洱茶包（亚洲超市）或浓红茶 | Pu-erh tea bags (Asian grocery) or strong black tea |
| 砖茶 | Brick tea | 散装普洱茶叶 | Loose-leaf pu-erh tea |
| 全脂牛奶 | Whole milk | 半对半奶油（half-and-half）更浓郁 | Half-and-half for a richer result |
