# 古罗马香草沙拉 Roman Herb Salad

> **时代 Era:** 罗马帝国 Roman Empire · 约公元100年 (~100 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 罗马 Rome, Italy
> **类型 Type:** 沙拉 Salad / 前菜 Appetizer
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

古罗马人极重视蔬菜与香草的食用。阿皮修斯的《论烹饪》中记载了多种沙拉做法，将新鲜香草与鱼露（garum）和橄榄油混合食用。这道菜并非现代凯撒沙拉（那是1924年墨西哥发明的），而是还原了罗马帝国时期真正的香草拌菜。

Ancient Romans valued vegetables and herbs highly. Apicius' "De Re Coquinaria" records multiple salad preparations mixing fresh herbs with fish sauce (garum) and olive oil. This dish is not the modern Caesar salad (invented in Mexico in 1924) but an authentic recreation of a Roman Imperial-era herb salad.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 生菜心 Romaine lettuce hearts | 2 棵 heads |
| 新鲜薄荷 Fresh mint | 一把 a handful |
| 新鲜香菜 Fresh cilantro (芫荽 coriander) | 一把 a handful |
| 鱼露 Fish sauce (代替garum) | 1 汤匙 tbsp |
| 特级初榨橄榄油 Extra virgin olive oil | 3 汤匙 tbsp |
| 黑胡椒 Black pepper (现磨 freshly ground) | 适量 to taste |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 备菜 Prepare greens
生菜洗净沥干，撕成适口大小。薄荷和香菜取叶片，粗切几刀。
Wash and dry romaine, tear into bite-sized pieces. Pick mint and cilantro leaves, roughly chop.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 调酱汁 Make dressing
小碗中将鱼露和橄榄油搅拌乳化，加入现磨黑胡椒。罗马人不用醋拌沙拉，酸味全靠鱼露提供。
Whisk fish sauce and olive oil in a small bowl until emulsified. Add freshly ground pepper. Romans did not use vinegar in salads — acidity came from the fish sauce.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 拌匀上桌 Toss and serve
将酱汁淋在蔬菜上，轻轻翻拌均匀。立即食用，不可久放。可搭配粗面包。
Drizzle dressing over greens and toss gently. Serve immediately — do not let it sit. Pair with rustic bread.
