# 古代俄罗斯罗宋汤 | Ancient Russian Borscht (~1400AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古代俄罗斯罗宋汤.svg" width="500" alt="古代俄罗斯罗宋汤"></p>

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪70分 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、俄罗斯

> **📜 历史** — 甜菜根汤起源于东斯拉夫地区，中世纪时农民用地窖储存的甜菜和卷心菜熬煮过冬，后传遍整个俄罗斯帝国和东欧各国。
> **📜 History** — *Beetroot soup originated among East Slavic peoples; medieval peasants simmered cellar-stored beets and cabbage for winter sustenance, later spreading across the Russian Empire and Eastern Europe.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 甜菜根 | Beetroot | 3个 / 3 medium |
| 牛腩 | Beef brisket | 300g / 10 oz |
| 卷心菜 | Cabbage | ¼个 / ¼ head |
| 土豆 | Potato | 2个 / 2 medium |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot | 1根 / 1 pc |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 medium |
| 番茄酱 | Tomato paste | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 酸奶油 | Sour cream | 60ml / ¼ cup |
| 莳萝 | Dill | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 熬牛肉汤 | Make Beef Broth
牛腩冷水下锅，大火煮沸撇沫，转小火炖40分钟至软烂，取出切块。Place brisket in cold water, bring to boil, skim foam, simmer 40 minutes until tender, remove and dice.

### 2. 炒蔬菜 | Saut Vegetables
洋葱和胡萝卜切丝炒软，加番茄酱炒匀，再放入甜菜根丝，翻炒5分钟。Saut shredded onion and carrot, stir in tomato paste, add julienned beets, cook 5 minutes.

### 3. 合炖 | Combine & Simmer
蔬菜倒入牛肉汤中，加切块土豆和卷心菜丝，煮20分钟至所有蔬菜软透。Add vegetables to broth with diced potato and shredded cabbage, simmer 20 minutes until all vegetables are tender.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
盛入碗中，放一勺酸奶油，撒新鲜莳萝，配黑面包。Ladle into bowls, top with sour cream and fresh dill, serve with dark rye bread.
