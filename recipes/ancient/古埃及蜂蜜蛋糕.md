# 古埃及蜂蜜蛋糕 | Ancient Egyptian Honey Cake (古埃及, ~1500 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烘烤 35分钟 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古埃及、甜点、素食

> **📜 历史** — 蜂蜜在古埃及被视为神圣之物，法老的头衔之一便是"上下埃及的蜜蜂之王"。图坦卡蒙墓中出土了三千年前的蜂蜜罐，蜂蜜仍然可食用。古埃及人用蜂蜜、椰枣和二粒小麦制作甜饼，作为供奉给太阳神拉的祭品，也在节庆和婚礼上享用。这种蜂蜜蛋糕是古埃及最精致的甜点之一。
> **📜 History** — *Honey was sacred in ancient Egypt; one pharaonic title was "King of the Bee of Upper and Lower Egypt." Honey jars found in Tutankhamun's tomb — 3,000 years old — were still edible. Egyptians made sweet cakes from honey, dates, and emmer wheat as offerings to the sun god Ra, also enjoyed at festivals and weddings. This honey cake was one of ancient Egypt's most refined desserts.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 全麦面粉 | Whole wheat flour | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 椰枣（切碎） | Dates (chopped) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 2个 | 2 |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 肉桂粉 | Ground cinnamon | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 杏仁（切碎） | Almonds (chopped) | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **混合湿料** — 将蜂蜜、鸡蛋和橄榄油搅打均匀至顺滑。
   *Whisk together honey, eggs, and olive oil until smooth.*

2. **混合干料** — 另一碗中混合全麦面粉、肉桂和盐。将湿料倒入干料中搅拌至无干粉，折叠入椰枣碎和大部分杏仁碎。
   *In a separate bowl, mix whole wheat flour, cinnamon, and salt. Pour wet ingredients into dry, stir until no dry flour remains, then fold in chopped dates and most of the almonds.*

3. **烘烤** — 面糊倒入抹油的8英寸圆形烤盘，表面撒剩余杏仁碎。烤箱预热至170°C（340°F），烤30-35分钟至插入牙签取出干净。
   *Pour batter into a greased 8-inch round pan, sprinkle remaining almonds on top. Preheat oven to 170°C (340°F) and bake 30-35 minutes until a toothpick inserted comes out clean.*

4. **装饰** — 出炉冷却后，表面淋一层薄蜂蜜增加光泽，切块食用。
   *Cool after removing from oven, drizzle a thin layer of honey on top for glaze, then slice and serve.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 古埃及养蜂业高度发达，壁画中描绘了系统化的蜂箱管理和蜂蜜采集过程。
  *Ancient Egyptian beekeeping was highly developed; wall paintings depict systematic hive management and honey harvesting.*

- 二粒小麦（emmer wheat）是古埃及的主要谷物，比现代小麦口感更粗犷，全麦面粉是最佳替代。
  *Emmer wheat was ancient Egypt's primary grain, coarser than modern wheat — whole wheat flour is the best substitute.*
