# 古代菲律宾Adobo | Ancient Filipino Adobo (~1500AD)

> ⏱ 准备10分+烹饪45分 | 💰~$10/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、菲律宾

> **📜 历史** — 在西班牙殖民者到来之前，菲律宾原住民已用棕榈醋和盐腌煮肉类以防腐保鲜。西班牙人见此烹法类似其"adobar"（腌渍），遂命名为Adobo。此菜还原前殖民时代的椰醋炖法。
> **📜 History** — *Before Spanish colonizers arrived, Filipino natives already braised meat in palm vinegar and salt for preservation. Spaniards saw similarities to their "adobar" (marinating) and coined the name. This recipe recreates the pre-colonial coconut vinegar braise.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸡腿 | Chicken thighs | 4个 / 4 pcs (~500g) |
| 五花肉 | Pork belly | 200g / 7 oz |
| 椰醋/白醋 | Coconut vinegar | 80ml / ⅓ cup |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 60ml / ¼ cup |
| 蒜 | Garlic | 8瓣 / 8 cloves (crushed) |
| 黑胡椒粒 | Black peppercorns | 10粒 / 10 pcs |
| 香叶 | Bay leaves | 3片 / 3 pcs |
| 水 | Water | 120ml / ½ cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 合锅 | Combine
所有食材放入一个深锅：鸡腿、五花肉块、蒜、醋、酱油、胡椒粒、香叶和水，不需提前煎肉。Place everything in a deep pot: chicken, pork belly chunks, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, peppercorns, bay leaves, and water. No need to pre-sear.

### 2. 炖煮 | Braise
大火煮沸，转小火加盖炖35分钟，中途翻动一次，至肉软烂。Bring to a boil, reduce to low heat, cover and simmer 35 minutes, flip once halfway, until meat is very tender.

### 3. 收汁焦化 | Reduce & Crisp
取出肉，大火将酱汁收浓。肉放平底锅煎至表面焦香（可选），再回锅裹汁。Remove meat, reduce sauce on high. Optionally sear meat in a pan until crispy, then return to sauce and coat.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
盛在白米饭上，浇浓汁，配煎蒜片。简单、浓郁、永恒的菲律宾味道。Serve over steamed rice, spoon thick sauce over, garnish with fried garlic chips. Simple, bold, timeless Filipino flavor.
