# 隋代碎金饭 | Sui Golden Fried Rice (~600AD)

> ⏱ 准备15分+烹饪10分 | 💰~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、隋朝

> **📜 历史** — 隋炀帝巡幸扬州时，御厨以蛋炒饭色如碎金而名之，是扬州炒饭的前身。
> **📜 History** — *When Emperor Yang of Sui visited Yangzhou, his chef named this egg fried rice "broken gold" for its golden color — the ancestor of Yangzhou fried rice.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 隔夜米饭 | Day-old rice | 400g / 2 cups |
| 鸡蛋 | Eggs | 3个 / 3 |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |
| 猪油 | Lard | 30ml / 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 炒蛋 | Scramble Eggs
大火热猪油，打入鸡蛋快速翻炒至半凝固。Heat lard on high, crack in eggs and scramble until half-set.

### 2. 炒饭 | Fry Rice
倒入隔夜饭大火翻炒，让每粒米裹上蛋液，呈金黄色。Add day-old rice, stir-fry vigorously so each grain is coated in egg and turns golden.

### 3. 调味 | Season
加盐调味，撒葱花翻匀出锅。Season with salt, toss in scallions, stir evenly and serve.
