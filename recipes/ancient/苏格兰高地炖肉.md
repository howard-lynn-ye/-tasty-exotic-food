# 苏格兰高地炖肉 | Scottish Highland Stew (苏格兰高地, ~1300 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/苏格兰高地炖肉.svg" width="500" alt="苏格兰高地炖肉"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 2小时 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、苏格兰、炖菜、羊肉

> **📜 历史** — 苏格兰高地的凛冽气候塑造了一种质朴而强壮的饮食传统。13世纪的高地氏族（Clan）以放牧和狩猎为生，将羊肉与根茎蔬菜一锅炖煮是他们度过漫长冬天的主要方式。这道炖肉在盖尔语中叫"Stovies"的前身，是高地战士和牧羊人的日常食物，也是氏族聚会时铁锅上的共享佳肴。
> **📜 History** — *Scotland's fierce Highland climate forged a hearty, rugged culinary tradition. In the 13th century, Highland clans lived by herding and hunting, stewing lamb with root vegetables to endure long winters. This stew, an ancestor of "Stovies" in Gaelic tradition, was everyday fare for Highland warriors and shepherds, and the communal iron-pot dish at clan gatherings.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎羊肉** — 厚底锅中加黄油，大火将羊肉块煎至四面焦香（约5分钟），取出备用。
   *Melt butter in a heavy pot over high heat. Sear lamb on all sides until browned (about 5 minutes). Set aside.*

2. **炒蔬菜** — 同锅中火炒洋葱和大蒜3分钟，加入胡萝卜和芜菁翻炒2分钟。
   *In the same pot, sauté onion and garlic over medium heat for 3 minutes. Add carrots and turnip, cook 2 minutes.*

3. **炖煮** — 放回羊肉，加水/高汤、大麦、百里香和月桂叶，大火煮沸后转小火，盖锅炖1.5小时。加入土豆再炖30分钟至所有食材软烂。
   *Return lamb, add water/stock, barley, thyme, and bay leaves. Boil, then simmer covered 1.5 hours. Add potatoes and cook 30 more minutes until everything is tender.*

4. **调味装盘** — 取出月桂叶和百里香枝，加盐和胡椒调味。盛入深碗，搭配燕麦面包食用。
   *Remove bay leaves and thyme sprigs. Season with salt and pepper. Ladle into deep bowls and serve with oatcakes.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 苏格兰高地的芜菁（neeps）和土豆是炖菜的灵魂，尤其是芜菁，在土豆传入欧洲之前是最重要的根茎类蔬菜。
  *Turnips (neeps) and potatoes are the soul of Highland stew; turnips were the most important root vegetable before potatoes reached Europe.*

- 高地氏族的宴会传统上由族长（Chieftain）主持，炖肉锅放在长桌中央，象征氏族的团结。
  *Highland clan feasts were presided over by the chieftain, with the stew pot placed at the center of the long table symbolizing unity.*

- 苏格兰人在炖肉中加入大麦是独特的传统，大麦既增稠汤汁又提供碳水化合物。
  *Adding barley to stew is a distinctly Scottish tradition — barley both thickens the broth and provides carbohydrates.*
