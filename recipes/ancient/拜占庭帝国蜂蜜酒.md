# 拜占庭帝国蜂蜜酒 | Byzantine Mead

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 拜占庭帝国 Byzantine Empire (~800AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 君士坦丁堡 Constantinople
> **类型 Type:** 饮品/酒 Beverage / Alcoholic Drink

---

## 典故 Historical Background

蜂蜜酒（Mead）是人类最古老的酒精饮料之一，在拜占庭帝国时期达到了新的精致高度。君士坦丁堡的宫廷以多种香料和草药调制蜂蜜酒，将这种古老饮料提升为贵族的社交饮品。拜占庭皇帝在外交宴会上以蜂蜜酒款待外国使节，其配方被视为国家机密。修道院也是蜂蜜酒的重要酿造中心，修士们将此视为上帝的恩赐。

Mead is one of humanity's oldest alcoholic beverages, reaching new heights of refinement during the Byzantine Empire. Constantinople's court crafted mead with various spices and herbs, elevating this ancient drink into an aristocratic social beverage. Byzantine emperors served mead to foreign envoys at diplomatic banquets, with recipes guarded as state secrets. Monasteries were also major brewing centers, with monks viewing mead as God's gift.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |

| 泉水 Spring water | 4升 4 liters |

| 丁香 Cloves | 5颗 5 whole |
| 生姜 Fresh ginger | 3片 3 slices |
| 肉豆蔻 Nutmeg | 1/4颗 1/4 whole |
| 干玫瑰花瓣 Dried rose petals | 1大匙 1 tbsp |
| 酵母（野生或面包酵母） Yeast (wild or bread yeast) | 适量 As needed |

## 做法 Preparation

1. **煮蜜水 Boil honey water:** 泉水煮沸后加入蜂蜜，搅拌至完全溶解，撇去表面浮沫。Bring spring water to a boil, add honey, stir until dissolved, skim off surface foam.
2. **加香料 Add spices:** 放入肉桂、丁香、姜片、肉豆蔻碎，小火煮约一刻钟使香料出味。Add cinnamon, cloves, ginger slices, and grated nutmeg, simmer about 15 minutes to extract flavors.
3. **冷却 Cool:** 过滤去香料渣，将蜜水冷却至体温温度——过热会杀死酵母。Strain out spice residue, cool the honey water to body temperature — too hot will kill the yeast.
4. **加酵母 Add yeast:** 将酵母撒入蜜水中轻轻搅拌，倒入洗净的大陶罐中。Sprinkle yeast into the honey water, stir gently, pour into a clean large clay jar.
5. **发酵 Ferment:** 以布封口允许气体逸出但阻隔尘土，置于阴凉处发酵。最初数日会剧烈冒泡，约一个月后发酵趋缓。Seal the mouth with cloth to allow gas to escape but block dust, store in a cool place. Vigorous bubbling occurs the first few days, slowing after about a month.
6. **陈酿与饮用 Age and serve:** 发酵完成后加入干玫瑰花瓣，过滤后转入密封陶罐中陈酿至少三个月。饮用时倒入银杯或铜杯。After fermentation, add dried rose petals, filter and transfer to sealed jars, age at least 3 months. Serve in silver or copper cups.

## 备注 Notes

- 拜占庭蜂蜜酒的酒精度通常在8-14%之间，取决于蜂蜜与水的比例。Byzantine mead typically ranged from 8-14% alcohol, depending on the honey-to-water ratio.
- 玫瑰花瓣是拜占庭宫廷的标志性添加物，赋予蜂蜜酒优雅的花香和淡粉色。Rose petals were the signature Byzantine court addition, giving mead an elegant floral aroma and pale pink hue.
