# 西夏面食 | Western Xia Noodles (西夏, ~1100 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 25分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、西夏、面食、拉面

> **📜 历史** — 西夏王朝（1038-1227）由党项族建立，以畜牧和农耕并重。西夏人喜食面食和羊肉，其饮食文化融合了中原汉族与西北游牧民族的特色。出土的西夏文献中有关于制面和烹饪的记载，反映了当时面食在日常生活中的重要地位。西夏地处今宁夏、甘肃一带，干旱气候使小麦成为主粮。
> **📜 History** — *The Western Xia Dynasty (1038-1227) was founded by the Tangut people, who practiced both pastoralism and farming. The Tanguts favored noodles and mutton, their cuisine blending Han Chinese and northwestern nomadic traditions. Excavated Western Xia documents contain references to noodle-making and cooking, reflecting the importance of wheat-based foods in daily life. Located in present-day Ningxia and Gansu, the arid climate made wheat a staple grain.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 高筋面粉 | Bread flour | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 羊肉片 | Lamb slices | 半磅 | 1/2 lb |
| 大葱（切段） | Leek (cut into sections) | 1根 | 1 stalk |
| 枸杞 | Goji berries | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面拉制** — 面粉加盐，慢慢倒入温水揉成光滑面团，盖布醒面30分钟。将面团搓成长条，反复拉拽至成细面条。
   *Mix flour and salt, gradually add warm water and knead into a smooth dough. Cover and rest 30 minutes. Roll dough into a long rope and stretch repeatedly into thin noodles.*

2. **炒羊肉浇头** — 锅中加少许油，大火煸炒羊肉片至变色，加入大葱段翻炒出香，调入少许盐。
   *Heat a small amount of oil in a wok over high heat. Stir-fry lamb slices until browned, add leek sections and toss until fragrant. Season with salt.*

3. **煮面装碗** — 大锅水烧开，下手拉面条煮2-3分钟至浮起。捞入碗中，浇上羊肉浇头，撒枸杞点缀。
   *Boil a large pot of water, cook the hand-pulled noodles 2-3 minutes until they float. Transfer to a bowl, top with the lamb mixture, and garnish with goji berries.*
