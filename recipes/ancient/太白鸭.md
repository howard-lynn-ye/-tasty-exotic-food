# 太白鸭 | Li Bai's Duck (唐代 Tang Dynasty, ~740 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 2小时 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、唐代、李白、周末项目

> **📜 历史** — 相传唐代诗仙李白好酒好肉，在长安酒肆中常点此菜。太白鸭以黄酒慢炖整鸭，取"太白"之名既指李白字太白，也形容鸭肉炖至洁白如玉。这是一道属于唐代盛世的菜——丰盛、醇厚、不加修饰的美味。
>
> **📜 History** — *Legend has it that the great Tang Dynasty poet Li Bai — known by his courtesy name "Taibai" — was a lover of wine and fine food. He frequently ordered this dish at Chang'an's wine houses. The duck is slow-braised in Shaoxing wine until the meat turns jade-white. A dish from China's golden age — generous, rich, and unapologetically delicious.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鸭腿 | Duck legs | 4只 / 4 pieces |
| 绍兴黄酒 | Shaoxing yellow wine | 200ml |
| 姜 | Ginger | 5片 / 5 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 3根 / 3 stalks |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 冰糖 | Rock sugar | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 八角 | Star anise | 2颗 / 2 |
| 桂皮 | Cinnamon stick | 1小段 / 1 piece |
| 枸杞 (可选) | Goji berries (optional) | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 焯水 | Blanch
鸭腿冷水下锅，加姜片焯水5分钟去腥。捞出洗净。

Place duck legs in cold water with ginger. Blanch 5 minutes. Drain and rinse.

### 2. 炖煮 | Braise
砂锅/铸铁锅中铺葱段姜片，放入鸭腿。倒入黄酒和等量的水，加八角、桂皮、冰糖。大火烧开转最小火，加盖炖1.5-2小时。

Line a Dutch oven with scallion and ginger. Add duck legs. Pour in wine and equal water. Add star anise, cinnamon, rock sugar. Bring to a boil, reduce to lowest heat, cover, braise 1.5–2 hours.

### 3. 收汁 | Reduce
加盐调味，大火收汁至浓稠。最后撒枸杞装饰。

Season with salt. Raise heat to reduce sauce until thick. Garnish with goji berries.

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

> 李白（701-762），唐代最伟大的浪漫主义诗人，号称"诗仙"。他以饮酒闻名，据杜甫《饮中八仙歌》："李白斗酒诗百篇，长安市上酒家眠。" 太白鸭用大量黄酒炖煮，酒香入肉，正合李白的豪放性格。
>
> *Li Bai (701-762) was the Tang Dynasty's greatest romantic poet, known as the "Immortal of Poetry." Famous for his drinking, Du Fu wrote of him: "With a gallon of wine, Li Bai writes a hundred poems, sleeping in Chang'an's wine shops." This duck, braised in generous wine, embodies Li Bai's spirit.*
