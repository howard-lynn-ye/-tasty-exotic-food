# 魏晋五侯鲭 | Wei-Jin Five Marquis Fish (魏晋, ~300 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/魏晋五侯鲭.svg" width="500" alt="魏晋五侯鲭"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$12/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、魏晋、五侯鲭、名士、宴饮

> **📜 历史** — "五侯鲭"最早见于西汉，到魏晋时期成为名士宴饮的代表菜。据传此菜因五位同日封侯的外戚各自献出一味鱼鲜而得名，五种鱼鲜合烹一锅，滋味极为丰富。魏晋名士风流，崇尚清谈与美食并举，"五侯鲭"象征着当时贵族阶层的精致饮食和奢华排场。《世说新语》中多次提及魏晋宴饮的考究。
> **📜 History** — *"Five Marquis Fish" first appeared in the Western Han but became the defining banquet dish of the Wei-Jin era. Legend says it was named for five relatives of the emperor, each enfeoffed as a marquis on the same day, who contributed one type of seafood each to create a single extraordinary dish. Wei-Jin literati celebrated both witty conversation and fine food. The "Five Marquis Fish" symbolized the aristocratic class's refined dining and lavish entertaining. "A New Account of Tales of the World" frequently references the elaborate feasts of this period.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鲈鱼片 | Sea bass fillet | 半磅 | 1/2 lb |
| 虾仁 | Shrimp (peeled) | 半磅 | 1/2 lb |
| 蛤蜊 | Clams | 12个 | 12 pcs |
| 鱿鱼（切圈） | Squid (sliced into rings) | 半磅 | 1/2 lb |
| 干贝（或蟹肉） | Scallops (or crab meat) | 1/4磅 | 1/4 lb |
| 生姜（切丝） | Ginger (julienned) | 1块 | 1 piece |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **备五鲜** — 鲈鱼切片，虾仁去虾线，蛤蜊洗净泡水吐沙，鱿鱼切圈打花刀，干贝洗净。所有海鲜以少许盐和米酒拌匀。
   *Slice bass, devein shrimp, soak clams to purge sand, score and slice squid into rings, rinse scallops. Toss all seafood lightly with salt and rice wine.*

2. **合烹五味** — 锅中加清汤或水4杯煮开，下姜丝，先放蛤蜊和鱿鱼煮3分钟，再加鱼片、虾仁和干贝，中火煮5分钟至全部熟透。
   *Bring 4 cups broth or water to a boil with ginger. Add clams and squid first, cook 3 minutes. Then add fish, shrimp, and scallops. Simmer over medium heat 5 minutes until all cooked through.*

3. **清雅上桌** — 加盐和少许白胡椒调味，盛入大汤碗，五种海鲜错落摆放。撒葱花或香菜点缀，体现魏晋名士的雅致风范。
   *Season with salt and a touch of white pepper. Ladle into a large soup bowl, arranging the five types of seafood artfully. Garnish with scallion or cilantro, reflecting the Wei-Jin aesthetic of elegant refinement.*
