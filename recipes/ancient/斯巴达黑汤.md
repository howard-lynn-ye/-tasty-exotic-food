# 斯巴达黑汤 | Spartan Black Broth (古希腊斯巴达, ~500 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 60分钟 | 💰 ~$5/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古希腊、汤品、肉类

> **📜 历史** — 黑汤（melas zomos）是斯巴达战士最著名的食物，用猪肉、猪血、醋和盐煮成。普鲁塔克记载，一位意大利殖民地国王品尝黑汤后说："现在我明白为什么斯巴达人不怕死了。"这道汤以其令人望而却步的味道闻名，但它提供了战士们所需的高蛋白和铁质。斯巴达人在公共食堂（syssitia）中共同进餐，黑汤是每餐必备。
> **📜 History** — *Black broth (melas zomos) was the most famous food of Spartan warriors, made from pork, pig's blood, vinegar, and salt. Plutarch recorded that a king from an Italian colony, after tasting it, said: "Now I understand why Spartans do not fear death." Famous for its off-putting taste, it nevertheless provided the high protein and iron warriors needed. Spartans ate communally in public messes (syssitia), where black broth was served at every meal.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 猪肩肉（切块） | Pork shoulder (cubed) | 500g | 1 lb |
| 猪血或鸡血 | Pork or chicken blood | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 苹果醋 | Apple cider vinegar | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 水 | Water | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 洋葱（切碎） | Onion (diced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 黑胡椒 | Black pepper | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 月桂叶 | Bay leaf | 2片 | 2 leaves |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炖煮猪肉** — 猪肩肉放入锅中加水、洋葱和月桂叶，大火煮沸后撇去浮沫，转小火慢炖45分钟至肉烂。
   *Place pork shoulder in a pot with water, onion, and bay leaves. Bring to a boil, skim the foam, reduce heat, and simmer 45 minutes until meat is tender.*

2. **调入猪血和醋** — 小火状态下缓缓倒入猪血，不断搅拌防止结块，加入醋、盐和黑胡椒，继续煮10分钟至汤色深黑浓稠。
   *Over low heat, slowly pour in the blood while stirring constantly to prevent clumping. Add vinegar, salt, and black pepper; continue cooking 10 minutes until the broth turns dark and thick.*

3. **上桌** — 去掉月桂叶，盛入陶碗中趁热食用，配以粗粮面包。
   *Remove bay leaves, ladle into rustic bowls, and serve hot with coarse bread on the side.*
