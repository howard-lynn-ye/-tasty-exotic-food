# 五代十国羊汤 | Five Dynasties Lamb Soup (五代十国, ~900 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$8/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、五代十国、羊肉汤、炖煮

> **📜 历史** — 五代十国（907-960）是唐末藩镇割据演变而来的乱世，各地政权更迭频繁。在这个动荡年代，羊肉汤因做法简便、营养丰富而成为南北通行的暖身食物。后周世宗柴荣的军中以羊汤犒军闻名，士兵们在寒冬行军时依靠一碗热羊汤恢复体力。五代的羊汤做法直接影响了宋代开封的"羊肉汤"传统。
> **📜 History** — *The Five Dynasties and Ten Kingdoms (907-960) was a chaotic era of fragmented warlord states following the Tang collapse. In this turbulent age, lamb soup became a universal comfort food across north and south — simple to prepare and deeply nourishing. Emperor Shizong of Later Zhou was known for rewarding his troops with lamb soup. Soldiers marching through bitter winters relied on a bowl of hot broth to restore their strength. The lamb soup traditions of the Five Dynasties directly influenced the famous "mutton soup" culture of Song Dynasty Kaifeng.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 羊肩肉（带骨） | Lamb shoulder (bone-in) | 1.5磅 | 1.5 lb |
| 白萝卜（切块） | Daikon radish (chunked) | 1根 | 1 piece |
| 生姜（拍碎） | Ginger (smashed) | 3块 | 3 pieces |
| 大葱（切段） | Scallion (sections) | 2根 | 2 stalks |
| 花椒粒 | Whole Sichuan peppercorns | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **焯水去膻** — 羊肉冷水下锅，大火煮开后撇去浮沫，捞出羊肉冲洗干净。花椒粒装入茶包袋中备用。
   *Place lamb in cold water, bring to a boil and skim off the foam. Remove lamb and rinse clean. Place peppercorns in a tea bag for easy removal.*

2. **慢火炖煮** — 砂锅中重新加入6杯清水，放入羊肉、姜块、葱段和花椒包，大火煮开后转小火慢炖40分钟。最后15分钟加入萝卜块同炖。
   *In a clay pot, add 6 cups fresh water with the lamb, ginger, scallions, and peppercorn bag. Bring to a boil, reduce to low heat, and simmer 40 minutes. Add radish chunks for the last 15 minutes.*

3. **原味上桌** — 取出花椒包和葱姜，加盐调味。汤应呈乳白色。盛入大碗，撒香菜和葱花，配烧饼或馍食用。
   *Remove peppercorn bag, scallions, and ginger. Season with salt. The broth should be milky white. Ladle into large bowls, garnish with cilantro and scallion, and serve with flatbread.*
