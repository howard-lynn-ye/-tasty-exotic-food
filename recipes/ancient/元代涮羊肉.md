# 元代涮羊肉 | Yuan Mongolian Hot Pot (~1280AD)

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪即涮即食 | 💰~$20/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、元朝

> **📜 历史** — 相传忽必烈行军途中急于进食，厨师将羊肉薄切入沸水速涮，配简单调料，开创了涮羊肉的先河。
> **📜 History** — *Legend says Kublai Khan's cook sliced lamb paper-thin and flash-boiled it in a hurry before battle, inventing the Mongolian hot pot tradition.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 羊肉卷 | Sliced lamb | 400g / 14oz |
| 大葱 | Leek | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 姜 | Ginger | 30g / 1oz |
| 芝麻酱 | Sesame paste | 60g / 4 tbsp |
| 腐乳 | Fermented tofu | 1块 / 1 cube |
| 白菜 | Napa cabbage | 300g / 10oz |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 备锅 | Prepare Pot
清水加葱段和姜片煮沸作为锅底。

Bring water to boil with leek sections and ginger slices as the broth base.

### 2. 调酱 | Mix Sauce
芝麻酱加腐乳、少许汤水调匀成蘸料。

Blend sesame paste with fermented tofu and a splash of broth into a dipping sauce.

### 3. 涮食 | Hot Pot
羊肉卷和白菜分次下锅，涮熟蘸酱食用。

Swish lamb slices and cabbage in boiling broth briefly, dip in sauce and eat.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 羊肉切得越薄越好，涮几秒变色即可食用，过久则老 | Slice lamb as thin as possible; a few seconds until color changes is enough — overcooking toughens it |
| 芝麻酱蘸料可加韭菜花和辣椒油提味 | Enhance the sesame dip with chive flower paste and chili oil |
| 先涮肉后涮菜，最后喝汤，汤底会越煮越鲜 | Cook meat first, then vegetables, and drink the broth last — it gets richer as you go |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
涮羊肉的起源与蒙古铁骑的快速行军密切相关。忽必烈急于出征，厨师来不及炖煮，灵机一动将羊肉切薄片在沸水中一涮即熟，意外创造出这道经典。元大都（今北京）成为涮羊肉的发源地，至今北京铜锅涮肉仍是京城冬季美食的代表。

*The origin of instant-boiled lamb is closely tied to the Mongol cavalry's need for speed. When Kublai Khan demanded a quick meal before battle, his cook improvised by flash-boiling thin-sliced lamb — accidentally creating a classic. Yuan Dynasty capital Dadu (modern Beijing) became the birthplace of this tradition, and copper-pot lamb hot pot remains Beijing's quintessential winter dish.*
