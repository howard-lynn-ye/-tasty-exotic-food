# 元代涮羊肉 | Yuan Mongolian Hot Pot (~1280AD)

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪即涮即食 | 💰~$20/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、元朝

> **📜 历史** — 相传忽必烈行军途中急于进食，厨师将羊肉薄切入沸水速涮，配简单调料，开创了涮羊肉的先河。
> **📜 History** — *Legend says Kublai Khan's cook sliced lamb paper-thin and flash-boiled it in a hurry before battle, inventing the Mongolian hot pot tradition.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 羊肉卷 | Sliced lamb | 400g / 14oz |
| 大葱 | Leek | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 姜 | Ginger | 30g / 1oz |
| 芝麻酱 | Sesame paste | 60g / 4 tbsp |
| 腐乳 | Fermented tofu | 1块 / 1 cube |
| 白菜 | Napa cabbage | 300g / 10oz |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 备锅 | Prepare Pot
清水加葱段和姜片煮沸作为锅底。Bring water to boil with leek sections and ginger slices as the broth base.

### 2. 调酱 | Mix Sauce
芝麻酱加腐乳、少许汤水调匀成蘸料。Blend sesame paste with fermented tofu and a splash of broth into a dipping sauce.

### 3. 涮食 | Hot Pot
羊肉卷和白菜分次下锅，涮熟蘸酱食用。Swish lamb slices and cabbage in boiling broth briefly, dip in sauce and eat.
