# 古印尼沙爹 Ancient Indonesian Satay

> **时代 Era:** 爪哇岛中世纪 Medieval Java · 约公元1200年 (~1200 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 爪哇岛 Java, Indonesia
> **类型 Type:** 烤串 Grilled Skewer
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

沙爹的起源学界尚有争议，但普遍认为起源于爪哇岛的街头小贩文化，可能受到中东烤肉串（kebab）的影响——这与阿拉伯商人经海上丝绸之路来到东南亚的时间吻合。早期沙爹使用椰子壳炭火烤制，搭配以石臼研磨的花生酱，至今仍是印尼国民美食。

Satay's origin is debated, but it is widely believed to come from Javanese street vendor culture, possibly influenced by Middle Eastern kebabs — coinciding with Arab traders arriving via the maritime Silk Road. Early satay was grilled over coconut-shell charcoal and served with mortar-ground peanut sauce. It remains Indonesia's national dish today.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鸡腿肉 Chicken thigh (切2cm块) | 400g |
| 花生酱 Peanut butter (无糖) | 3 汤匙 tbsp |
| 酱油 Soy sauce (甜酱油 kecap manis更佳) | 2 汤匙 tbsp |
| 姜黄粉 Ground turmeric | 1 茶匙 tsp |
| 椰奶 Coconut milk | 60ml |
| 竹签 Bamboo skewers (泡水30分钟) | 12 根 |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 腌制 Marinate
鸡肉块加入姜黄粉和一半酱油拌匀，腌制至少30分钟（冷藏过夜更佳）。将鸡肉串上泡好水的竹签，每签4-5块。
Toss chicken with turmeric and half the soy sauce. Marinate at least 30 minutes (overnight in the fridge is better). Thread 4-5 pieces onto each soaked bamboo skewer.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 做花生酱 Make peanut sauce
小锅中将花生酱、剩余酱油和椰奶小火加热搅拌至顺滑。若太稠可加少量热水调节。可加一点辣椒粉增加辛辣度。
In a small pot, heat peanut butter, remaining soy sauce, and coconut milk over low heat, stirring until smooth. Add hot water if too thick. Optionally add chili powder for heat.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 烤制 Grill
炭火烤炉或烤箱设至最高温。将鸡肉串每面烤3-4分钟至表面焦香、内部熟透。烤制过程中刷一层薄花生酱。搭配剩余花生酱蘸食。
Heat charcoal grill or oven to highest setting. Grill skewers 3-4 minutes per side until charred and cooked through. Brush with a thin layer of peanut sauce while grilling. Serve with remaining sauce for dipping.
