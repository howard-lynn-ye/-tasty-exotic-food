# 古代朝鲜泡菜 Ancient Korean Kimchi

> **时代 Era:** 高句丽时期 Goguryeo Period · 约公元前37年 (~37 BC)
> **地区 Region:** 古朝鲜半岛 Ancient Korean Peninsula
> **类型 Type:** 腌菜 Fermented Vegetable
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

最早的泡菜并不含辣椒——辣椒直到16世纪才经葡萄牙人传入东亚。古代泡菜是用盐渍蔬菜加以发酵保存的技术，高句丽人在严寒的冬季依靠腌菜补充蔬菜营养。这个版本还原了无辣椒时代的原始泡菜风味。

The earliest kimchi contained no chili pepper — peppers only reached East Asia via the Portuguese in the 16th century. Ancient kimchi was a salt-fermented vegetable preservation technique. Goguryeo people relied on pickled vegetables for nutrition during harsh winters. This version recreates the original pre-chili kimchi.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 大白菜 Napa cabbage | 1 棵 (约800g) |
| 粗盐 Coarse salt | 60g |
| 生姜 Ginger (切丝 julienned) | 30g |
| 大蒜 Garlic (切碎 minced) | 4 瓣 cloves |
| 葱白 Scallion whites (切段 sliced) | 3 根 stalks |
| 水 Water | 适量 as needed |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 盐渍 Salt
白菜纵切四瓣，每层叶片间均匀抹上粗盐，放入大盆中静置4-6小时至白菜变软出水。用冷水冲洗两遍，挤干水分。
Quarter the cabbage lengthwise. Rub coarse salt between every layer of leaves. Rest in a large basin 4-6 hours until wilted. Rinse twice in cold water and squeeze dry.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 拌料 Mix seasoning
将姜丝、蒜碎、葱段混合均匀。把调料均匀塞入白菜每层叶片之间。
Mix ginger, garlic, and scallions together. Stuff the seasoning mixture evenly between every layer of cabbage leaves.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 发酵 Ferment
将白菜紧密装入干净的玻璃罐或陶缸中，压实使菜汁没过菜面。密封后室温放置2-3天，再移入冰箱冷藏发酵一周即可食用。
Pack cabbage tightly into a clean glass jar or crock, pressing down until brine covers the surface. Seal and leave at room temperature 2-3 days, then refrigerate for one week before eating.
