# 蒙古帝国马奶酒 | Mongol Empire Airag (蒙古草原, ~1200 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 发酵 2-3天 | 💰 ~$4/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、蒙古、饮品、发酵

> **📜 历史** — 马奶酒（Airag/Kumis）是蒙古帝国的"国饮"，成吉思汗的军队以此为行军粮液。13世纪的蒙古人将马奶装入皮囊中，利用草原上的自然温度发酵。欧洲旅行家威廉·鲁布鲁克在1253年的游记中详细记录了蒙古宫廷中饮用马奶酒的盛大场景。马奶酒不仅是饮品，更是蒙古人敬天祭地的圣物。
> **📜 History** — *Airag (kumis) was the "national drink" of the Mongol Empire — Genghis Khan's armies sustained themselves with it on campaigns. 13th-century Mongols fermented mare's milk in leather bags using the steppe's natural temperatures. European traveler William of Rubruck recorded elaborate airag-drinking ceremonies at the Mongol court in 1253. Airag was not just a beverage but a sacred offering to heaven and earth.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **温热牛奶** — 牛奶加热至约38°C（100°F，手感温热不烫），加入蜂蜜搅匀。
   *Heat milk to about 38°C (100°F, warm but not hot to the touch). Stir in honey until dissolved.*

2. **接种发酵** — 加入酸奶和酵母，充分搅拌均匀。倒入干净的玻璃罐中，松松盖上盖子（需要透气）。
   *Add yogurt and yeast, stir thoroughly. Pour into a clean glass jar and loosely cover (it needs to breathe).*

3. **发酵** — 置于室温（约20-25°C）发酵2-3天，每天搅拌2-3次。液体会变得微微起泡，味道酸爽。
   *Ferment at room temperature (20-25°C) for 2-3 days, stirring 2-3 times daily. The liquid will become slightly fizzy and pleasantly tart.*

4. **过滤饮用** — 用纱布过滤，冷藏后饮用。传统上用木碗盛饮，可配蒙古奶豆腐。
   *Strain through cheesecloth and refrigerate. Traditionally served in wooden bowls, paired with dried cheese curds.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 真正的马奶酒酒精含量仅1-3%，但蒙古人还会蒸馏制成烈酒"阿尔赫"（Arkhi），酒精度可达12%。
  *Authentic airag is only 1-3% alcohol, but Mongols also distilled it into "arkhi," a spirit reaching 12% ABV.*

- 成吉思汗规定醉酒每月不得超过三次，但在那达慕大会等节庆中，饮酒无限制。
  *Genghis Khan decreed that drunkenness should not exceed three times per month, but during festivals like Naadam, drinking was unrestricted.*

- 马奶酒富含维生素C，这在蔬菜稀少的草原饮食中至关重要，有效预防了坏血病。
  *Airag is rich in vitamin C, crucial in the vegetable-scarce steppe diet, effectively preventing scurvy.*
