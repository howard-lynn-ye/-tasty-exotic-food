# 东坡豆腐 | Su Dongpo's Tofu (宋代 Song Dynasty, ~1080 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、宋代、苏东坡、素食可

> **📜 历史** — 苏东坡不仅发明了东坡肉，还创造了东坡豆腐。被贬黄州时，他生活清苦，常以豆腐为主食，用酱料将廉价的豆腐变成美味，写下"煮豆作乳脂为酥"的诗句。这道菜证明了一个道理：伟大的食物不需要昂贵的食材。
>
> **📜 History** — *Su Dongpo didn't just invent Dongpo Pork — he also created Dongpo Tofu. Exiled to Huangzhou in poverty, he survived on tofu, transforming this humble ingredient into delicacy with clever seasoning. He wrote: "Boil beans to milk, press curds to butter." This dish proves: great food doesn't need expensive ingredients.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 老豆腐 | Firm tofu | 1块 / 1 block (~400g) |
| 猪肉馅 (可不放) | Ground pork (optional for vegetarian) | 50g |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 黄酒或料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 / 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 2片 / 2 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1茶匙 / 1 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 / 2 tbsp |
| 花椒 (可选) | Sichuan peppercorn (optional) | 少许 / a few |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煎豆腐 | Pan-fry Tofu
豆腐切厚片，两面煎至金黄。盛出。

Cut tofu into thick slices. Pan-fry until golden on both sides. Set aside.

### 2. 炒料 | Cook Aromatics
锅中热油，爆香姜末葱白。如果用肉馅，炒散至变色。

Heat oil, sizzle ginger and scallion whites. If using pork, stir-fry until browned.

### 3. 炖煮 | Braise
放回豆腐，加酱油、黄酒、糖和半杯水。小火炖10分钟让豆腐入味。

Return tofu. Add soy sauce, wine, sugar, and half cup water. Simmer 10 minutes to let tofu absorb flavors.

### 4. 收汁 | Thicken
淀粉加水勾芡，撒葱花和花椒。

Thicken with cornstarch slurry. Garnish with scallions and Sichuan pepper.

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

> 苏东坡（1037-1101），北宋文学家、美食家。被贬黄州（今湖北黄冈）期间创造了多道以廉价食材为主的菜肴。他的《老饕赋》是中国最早的美食散文之一。东坡豆腐体现了他"食不厌精"的理念——即使是最简单的食材，也值得认真对待。
>
> *Su Dongpo (1037-1101) was a Song Dynasty poet, politician, and gastronome. During his exile to Huangzhou, he invented several dishes using humble ingredients. His essay "Ode to a Gourmand" (老饕赋) is one of China's earliest food writings. Dongpo Tofu embodies his philosophy: even the simplest ingredient deserves your best effort.*
