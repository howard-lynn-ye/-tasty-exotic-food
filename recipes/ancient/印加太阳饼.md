# 印加太阳饼 | Inca Sun Bread (安第斯山脉, ~1400 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、南美洲、面饼、素食

> **📜 历史** — 印加帝国是美洲最大的前哥伦布文明，其太阳神因蒂（Inti）崇拜渗透到饮食文化中。在太阳祭（Inti Raymi）期间，祭司们用玉米粉制作圆形面饼象征太阳，供奉后分食。玉米是印加人的圣粮，被称为"Sara"，太阳饼则是神圣与世俗的完美结合。
> **📜 History** — *The Inca Empire was the largest pre-Columbian civilization in the Americas, and worship of the sun god Inti permeated their food culture. During the Inti Raymi (Festival of the Sun), priests made round corn bread to symbolize the sun, offering it before sharing. Corn, called "Sara," was the Incas' sacred grain, and sun bread was a perfect union of the divine and the mundane.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 玉米粉（细磨） | Fine cornmeal | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 南瓜泥 | Pumpkin puree | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 辣椒粉（aji） | Aji pepper powder | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 猪油或植物油 | Lard or vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面** — 将玉米粉、盐和辣椒粉混合，加入南瓜泥、蜂蜜和猪油，逐渐加入温水揉成柔软面团（不粘手即可）。
   *Mix cornmeal, salt, and aji powder. Add pumpkin puree, honey, and lard. Gradually add warm water and knead into a soft, non-sticky dough.*

2. **成型** — 将面团分成8等份，每份搓圆后压扁成直径约10厘米的圆饼，用刀背在表面刻出放射状太阳纹。
   *Divide dough into 8 equal portions. Roll each into a ball, flatten to about 10cm diameter discs, and score radiating sun-ray patterns on the surface with the back of a knife.*

3. **烤制** — 烤箱预热至190°C（375°F），将饼放在铺了烘焙纸的烤盘上，烤20-25分钟至金黄酥脆。
   *Preheat oven to 190°C (375°F). Place discs on a parchment-lined baking sheet and bake 20-25 minutes until golden and crisp.*

4. **装盘** — 趁热食用，可搭配蜂蜜或牛油果酱。
   *Serve warm, optionally with honey or guacamole on the side.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 印加帝国的道路系统（Qhapaq Ñan）上设有驿站，储备玉米饼供信使（Chasqui）食用。
  *The Inca road system (Qhapaq Ñan) had relay stations stocked with corn bread for messengers (Chasqui).*

- 印加人还用玉米酿造啤酒（Chicha），太阳祭上太阳饼配Chicha是标准组合。
  *The Incas also brewed beer from corn (Chicha); sun bread with chicha was the standard pairing at Inti Raymi.*

- 高海拔的安第斯山区玉米品种与平原玉米不同，颗粒更大、颜色更多样（紫色、红色、白色）。
  *High-altitude Andean corn varieties differ from lowland corn — the kernels are larger and come in purple, red, and white.*
