# 印加太阳饼 | Inca Sun Bread (印加帝国, ~1400 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烘烤 30分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、印加帝国、面食、玉米

> **📜 历史** — 印加帝国（塔万廷苏约）是美洲最大的前哥伦布文明，从哥伦比亚延伸至智利。太阳神因蒂是印加最高神祇，每年的太阳节（Inti Raymi）上，祭司用最优质的玉米制作圆形太阳饼献祭。这种玉米饼（Tanta Wawa的前身）象征太阳的光辉与力量。印加人没有小麦——玉米和藜麦是他们的主要谷物。库斯科的太阳神庙（Qorikancha）中发现了储存仪式用玉米的痕迹。
> **📜 History** — *The Inca Empire (Tawantinsuyu) was the Americas' largest pre-Columbian civilization, stretching from Colombia to Chile. The sun god Inti was the supreme deity; at the annual Sun Festival (Inti Raymi), priests offered round sun-shaped corn breads made from the finest maize. These corn cakes (precursors to Tanta Wawa) symbolized the sun's radiance and power. The Inca had no wheat — corn and quinoa were their primary grains. Traces of ritual corn storage were found at Cusco's Temple of the Sun (Qorikancha).*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 玉米粉 | Cornmeal (fine) | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 藜麦粉 | Quinoa flour | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 蜂蜜（或龙舌兰糖浆） | Honey (or agave syrup) | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 动物油脂或黄油 | Lard or butter | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 鸡蛋 | Egg | 1个 | 1 |
| 辣椒粉（aji） | Chili powder (aji) | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **混合** — 将玉米粉、藜麦粉和盐混合。加入融化的黄油、蜂蜜、鸡蛋和温水，搅拌成均匀的面糊（比面团略湿）。加入辣椒粉拌匀。
   *Mix cornmeal, quinoa flour, and salt. Add melted butter, honey, egg, and warm water. Stir into a uniform batter (slightly wetter than dough). Mix in chili powder.*

2. **整形** — 将面糊分成8份，用手捏成圆形饼状（象征太阳），约1.5厘米厚，可用叉子在表面刻出放射状太阳纹。
   *Divide batter into 8 portions. Shape each into a round disc (symbolizing the sun), about 1.5 cm thick. Use a fork to score radiating sun-ray patterns on the surface.*

3. **烘烤** — 烤箱预热至180°C（350°F），放在铺烘焙纸的烤盘上，烤25-30分钟至金黄定型。
   *Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F). Place on a parchment-lined baking sheet, bake 25-30 minutes until golden and set.*

4. **食用** — 趁温热食用，可搭配辣椒酱或牛油果。
   *Serve warm, paired with chili sauce or avocado.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 印加帝国通过"奇普"（结绳记事）记录玉米产量和分配，虽无文字但管理精密。
  *The Inca Empire recorded corn yields and distribution via "quipu" (knotted string) — no writing system, but highly precise administration.*

- 太阳节至今仍在库斯科每年6月24日庆祝，是秘鲁最重要的文化节日之一。
  *Inti Raymi is still celebrated in Cusco every June 24 and remains one of Peru's most important cultural festivals.*
