# 古代暹罗芒果饭 | Ancient Siamese Mango Rice

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 素可泰王朝 Sukhothai Kingdom (~1300AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 暹罗（今泰国） Siam (modern Thailand)
> **类型 Type:** 甜食/主食 Dessert / Staple

---

## 典故 Historical Background

芒果糯米饭是东南亚最古老的甜食之一，起源于素可泰王朝时期。暹罗稻米文化与热带水果的完美结合，被视为王室宴席上的珍贵甜品。古代暹罗人相信，芒果的金黄色象征繁荣，糯米则代表丰收与团聚。

Mango sticky rice is one of Southeast Asia's oldest desserts, originating during the Sukhothai Kingdom. It represents the perfect marriage of Siamese rice culture and tropical fruit, served as a prized dessert at royal banquets. Ancient Siamese believed the golden color of mango symbolized prosperity, while sticky rice represented harvest and togetherness.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 糯米 Glutinous rice | 2杯 2 cups |
| 成熟芒果 Ripe mango | 2个 2 whole |
| 椰浆 Coconut cream | 1杯 1 cup |
| 棕榈糖 Palm sugar | 3大匙 3 tbsp |
| 海盐 Sea salt | 少许 A pinch |
| 班兰叶 Pandan leaf | 2片 2 leaves |

---

## 做法 Preparation

1. **浸米 Soak rice:** 糯米清水浸泡四至六个时辰（一夜）。Soak glutinous rice in water for 4-6 hours (overnight).
2. **蒸米 Steam rice:** 将糯米铺于竹蒸笼中，以大火蒸至透明软糯，约半个时辰。Steam rice in a bamboo steamer over high heat until translucent and soft, about 30 minutes.
3. **煮椰酱 Make coconut sauce:** 椰浆加棕榈糖与盐，小火煮至糖化，放入班兰叶增香。Heat coconut cream with palm sugar and salt on low heat until dissolved; add pandan leaves for fragrance.
4. **拌饭 Mix:** 取出班兰叶，将椰酱浇于热糯米上，搅拌均匀，盖布静置使米饭吸收椰香。Remove pandan leaves, pour sauce over hot rice, mix well, cover and rest to absorb flavor.
5. **切芒果 Cut mango:** 芒果去皮切厚片，整齐排列于糯米旁。Peel mango and cut into thick slices, arrange neatly beside the rice.
6. **装盘 Plate:** 以芭蕉叶为盘，盛糯米与芒果，再淋少许椰浆，撒烤椰丝。Serve on banana leaf with rice and mango, drizzle with coconut cream and toasted coconut flakes.

## 备注 Notes

- 古代暹罗使用野生小芒果，味道更浓郁酸甜。Ancient Siam used wild small mangoes with a more intense sweet-sour flavor.
- 棕榈糖可以用蜂蜜替代，风味略有不同。Palm sugar can be substituted with honey for a slightly different taste.
