# 古代名菜全目录 | Ancient Recipes Catalog (217道)

> **跨越五千年，纵横五十余文明，一览人类餐桌上的史诗。**
>
> **Spanning 5,000 years and over 50 civilizations — an epic survey of humanity's table.**

本目录收录了从苏美尔文明到清末的 **217 道**古代名菜，涵盖中国各朝代、地中海世界、中亚丝路、东南亚、非洲、美洲及大洋洲的经典食谱。每道菜均附中英双语说明及详细做法链接。

This catalog contains **217** ancient recipes ranging from Sumerian civilization to the late Qing dynasty, covering Chinese dynasties, the Mediterranean world, the Central Asian Silk Road, Southeast Asia, Africa, the Americas, and Oceania. Each dish includes bilingual descriptions and a link to its full recipe.
