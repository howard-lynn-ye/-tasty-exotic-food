## 简介 Introduction

荷兰东印度公司（VOC，1602-1799）是历史上最强大的贸易公司，控制着东南亚的
香料贸易。荷兰殖民者在巴达维亚（今雅加达）定居后，将欧洲的炖煮技法与当地
丰富的香料相结合，创造出一种独特的"公司咖喱"——比印度咖喱温和，比荷兰炖菜
复杂，加入了肉豆蔻、丁香等当时价比黄金的香料。

The Dutch East India Company (VOC, 1602-1799) was history's most powerful
trading company, controlling Southeast Asian spice routes. Dutch settlers in
Batavia (modern Jakarta) merged European stewing with local spices, creating
a unique "Company Curry" — milder than Indian curry, more complex than Dutch
stew, enriched with nutmeg and cloves once worth their weight in gold.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 鸡腿肉 Chicken thigh | 600g |
| 椰浆 Coconut milk | 400ml |
| 洋葱 Onion | 2个 / 2 |
| 大蒜 Garlic | 5瓣 / 5 cloves |
| 姜 Ginger | 20g |
| 肉豆蔻粉 Ground nutmeg | 3g |
| 丁香 Whole cloves | 4粒 / 4 |
| 肉桂棒 Cinnamon stick | 1根 / 1 |
| 姜黄粉 Turmeric | 5g |
| 芫荽粉 Ground coriander | 5g |
| 柠檬草 Lemongrass | 1根 / 1 stalk |
| 黄油 Butter | 30g |
| 盐 Salt | 适量 / to taste |
| 白米饭 Steamed rice | 配菜 / as side |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **切肉 Prep chicken**: 鸡腿肉去骨切大块，用盐和姜黄粉抓匀腌15分钟。
   Debone thighs, cut into chunks, marinate with salt and turmeric 15 min.

2. **炒底 Cook base**: 黄油融化，炒洋葱至深金色约10分钟，加蒜末和姜末。
   Melt butter, cook onions to deep golden ~10 min, add minced garlic and ginger.

3. **加香料 Add spices**: 放入丁香、肉桂棒、芫荽粉、肉豆蔻粉和拍碎的柠檬草。
   Add cloves, cinnamon, coriander, nutmeg, and bruised lemongrass.

4. **煎鸡 Sear chicken**: 放入鸡肉翻炒至表面变白，约5分钟。
   Add chicken, stir-fry until surface turns opaque, about 5 min.

5. **加椰浆 Add coconut**: 倒入椰浆，搅匀后大火煮沸，转小火加盖炖30分钟。
   Pour in coconut milk, stir, bring to boil, cover and simmer on low 30 min.

6. **收汁 Reduce**: 开盖中火收汁10分钟至酱汁浓稠挂勺，取出肉桂棒和柠檬草。
   Uncover, reduce on medium 10 min until sauce coats a spoon. Remove aromatics.

7. **上桌 Serve**: 搭配白米饭食用，撒少许肉豆蔻粉点缀。
   Serve over steamed rice with a pinch of nutmeg to finish.
