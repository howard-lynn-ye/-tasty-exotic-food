# 古印度咖喱饭 | Ancient Indian Biryani (德里苏丹国, ~1400 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/古印度咖喱饭.svg" width="500" alt="古印度咖喱饭"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 腌制1小时 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、印度、米饭、羊肉

> **📜 历史** — Biryani（比尔亚尼）是印度次大陆最壮观的米饭料理，其起源众说纷纭。最主流的说法是它在14世纪德里苏丹国时期成型，融合了波斯Pilaf的烹饪技法和印度本土的浓烈香料。穆斯林统治者的宫廷厨师将藏红花米饭与香料腌制的羊肉层层叠放，密封慢焖，创造了这道"用锅封印的盛宴"。
> **📜 History** — *Biryani is the Indian subcontinent's most spectacular rice dish, with disputed origins. The prevailing theory is that it took shape during the 14th-century Delhi Sultanate, merging Persian pilaf techniques with India's intense local spices. Court chefs of Muslim rulers layered saffron rice with spice-marinated lamb, sealed the pot, and slow-cooked it — creating this "feast sealed in a pot."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 巴斯马蒂米 | Basmati rice | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 羊肉（带骨，切块） | Lamb (bone-in, cubed) | 500克 | 1.1 lbs |
| 酸奶 | Yogurt | 1杯 | 1 cup |
| 洋葱（切薄丝） | Onion (thinly sliced) | 3个 | 3 |
| 生姜蒜泥 | Ginger-garlic paste | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 藏红花 | Saffron threads | 一大撮 | a large pinch |
| 温牛奶 | Warm milk | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 全香料（桂皮、丁香、豆蔻、月桂叶） | Whole spices (cinnamon, cloves, cardamom, bay) | 各少许 | a few each |
| 辣椒粉 | Chili powder | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 植物油/酥油 | Oil or ghee | 4汤匙 | 4 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 新鲜薄荷和香菜 | Fresh mint and cilantro | 各1/4杯 | 1/4 cup each |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌制羊肉** — 羊肉加酸奶、姜蒜泥、辣椒粉和盐拌匀，腌制至少1小时。藏红花泡入温牛奶备用。
   *Marinate lamb with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, chili powder, and salt for at least 1 hour. Soak saffron in warm milk.*

2. **炸洋葱** — 油/酥油中火炸洋葱丝至深金棕色酥脆（约15分钟），取出一半备用作装饰，另一半留锅中。
   *Fry onion slices in oil/ghee over medium heat until deep golden and crisp (about 15 minutes). Remove half for garnish; leave the rest in the pot.*

3. **煮米炖肉** — 米洗净在加了全香料的盐水中煮至七分熟，沥干。腌好的羊肉放入炸洋葱锅中，铺上薄荷和香菜，上盖半熟米饭，淋藏红花牛奶。
   *Parboil rice in salted water with whole spices until 70% done; drain. Place marinated lamb in the onion pot, top with mint and cilantro, then the parboiled rice. Drizzle saffron milk over.*

4. **Dum焖制** — 锅盖用面团密封（或用锡纸+重锅盖），最小火焖30分钟。开盖后轻柔翻拌，撒炸洋葱。
   *Seal the lid with dough (or foil + heavy lid). Cook on lowest heat 30 minutes. Gently fold when opened and top with fried onions.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 莫卧儿皇帝沙贾汗（泰姬陵的建造者）的宫廷据说有50种不同配方的Biryani。
  *Shah Jahan (builder of the Taj Mahal) reportedly had 50 different biryani recipes in his Mughal court.*

- 海德拉巴Biryani和勒克瑙Biryani是两大流派：前者生肉和生米一起焖（Kacchi），后者分别预煮（Pakki）。
  *Hyderabadi and Lucknowi biryani are two major schools: the former cooks raw meat with raw rice (kacchi), the latter pre-cooks both (pakki).*

- "Dum"（密封焖制）技术是Biryani的灵魂，锅内蒸汽循环使米饭吸收肉汁精华。
  *"Dum" (sealed steaming) is biryani's soul — steam circulation inside the pot lets rice absorb the meat's essence.*
