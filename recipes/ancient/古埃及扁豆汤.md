# 古埃及扁豆汤 | Ancient Egyptian Lentil Soup (古埃及, ~2000 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 35分钟 | 💰 ~$3/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古埃及、汤品、素食

> **📜 历史** — 扁豆是古埃及人的主食之一，在金字塔建造者的口粮中占有重要地位。考古学家在法老墓葬中发现了扁豆遗存，《圣经》旧约中也记载了以扫用长子权换取的"红豆汤"很可能就是扁豆汤。古埃及人将扁豆视为平民和贵族共享的食物，是尼罗河文明最悠久的料理之一。
> **📜 History** — *Lentils were a staple food of ancient Egypt, forming a key part of the pyramid builders' rations. Archaeologists have found lentil remains in pharaonic tombs. The "red pottage" for which Esau traded his birthright in the Old Testament was likely a lentil soup. Egyptians considered lentils food for both commoners and nobles — one of the Nile civilization's most enduring dishes.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 红扁豆 | Red lentils | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 洋葱（切碎） | Onion (diced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 大蒜（切碎） | Garlic (minced) | 3瓣 | 3 cloves |
| 小茴香粉 | Ground cumin | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 芫荽粉 | Ground coriander | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 姜黄粉 | Ground turmeric | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 橄榄油 | Olive oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 水或蔬菜高汤 | Water or vegetable broth | 4杯 | 4 cups |
| 柠檬汁 | Lemon juice | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **炒香料底** — 锅中加橄榄油，中火炒洋葱至半透明（约4分钟），加入蒜末、小茴香、芫荽和姜黄，炒香30秒。
   *Heat olive oil in a pot over medium heat. Sauté onion until translucent (about 4 minutes). Add garlic, cumin, coriander, and turmeric; stir for 30 seconds until fragrant.*

2. **炖煮扁豆** — 加入洗净的红扁豆和水/高汤，大火煮沸后转小火，炖煮25-30分钟至扁豆完全软烂。
   *Add rinsed red lentils and water/broth. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer 25-30 minutes until lentils are completely soft.*

3. **搅打成泥** — 用浸入式搅拌器将汤打至细滑（或保留部分颗粒感），加盐和柠檬汁调味。
   *Blend with an immersion blender until smooth (or leave partially chunky). Season with salt and lemon juice.*

4. **装盘** — 盛入碗中，淋少许橄榄油，可撒小茴香粉和新鲜香菜装饰。
   *Ladle into bowls, drizzle with olive oil, and garnish with a pinch of cumin and fresh cilantro if desired.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 古埃及人没有番茄（美洲作物），所以传统版本不含番茄，颜色来自红扁豆和姜黄。
  *Ancient Egyptians had no tomatoes (a New World crop), so the traditional version gets its color from red lentils and turmeric.*

- 扁豆在古埃及语中称为"ꜥšrt"，在象形文字中有专门的符号。
  *Lentils were called "ꜥšrt" in ancient Egyptian and had their own hieroglyphic symbol.*

- 尼罗河泛滥后肥沃的土壤非常适合种植扁豆，使其成为最廉价的蛋白质来源。
  *The fertile soil left after Nile floods was ideal for growing lentils, making them the cheapest protein source available.*
