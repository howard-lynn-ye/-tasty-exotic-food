# 南宋西湖醋鱼原型 | Southern Song Proto-Vinegar Fish (~1200AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/南宋西湖醋鱼原型.svg" width="500" alt="南宋西湖醋鱼原型"></p>

> ⏱ 准备15分+烹饪20分 | 💰~$14/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、南宋、杭帮菜

> **📜 历史** — 南宋定都临安（今杭州），西湖醋鱼的前身据传源于宋嫂鱼羹的演变。渔民以西湖活草鱼现杀现烹，醋糖勾芡，是杭州菜最具代表性的古味之一。
> **📜 History** — *When the Southern Song moved its capital to Lin'an (modern Hangzhou), the precursor to West Lake Vinegar Fish evolved from "Sister Song's Fish Soup." Fishermen cooked live grass carp from the lake with vinegar-sugar sauce — an iconic ancient Hangzhou flavor.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 草鱼 | Grass carp | 1条 / 1 whole (~750g) |
| 镇江香醋 | Zhenjiang vinegar | 50ml / 3½ tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 25g / 5 slices |
| 黄酒 | Shaoxing wine | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 淀粉 | Starch | 10g / 2 tsp |
| 葱 | Scallion | 2根 / 2 stalks |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 处理鱼 | Prep Fish
草鱼去鳞去内脏，从背部剖开成两片，鱼身划花刀便于入味。Scale and gut the carp, butterfly from the back into two halves, score crosshatch cuts for flavor absorption.

### 2. 煮鱼 | Poach
锅中宽水加姜片、葱段、黄酒煮沸，放入鱼片，中火煮8-10分钟至刚熟，轻轻捞出装盘。Bring a wide pot of water to boil with ginger, scallion, and wine; poach fish 8-10 minutes until just cooked, gently transfer to plate.

### 3. 制醋汁 | Make Vinegar Sauce
取煮鱼汤200ml，加醋、糖、酱油煮沸，淀粉水勾薄芡，成琥珀色糖醋汁。Take 200ml poaching liquid, add vinegar, sugar, soy sauce, bring to a boil, thicken with starch slurry into an amber sweet-sour sauce.

### 4. 浇汁上桌 | Sauce & Serve
醋汁均匀浇在鱼身上，汁色晶亮，酸甜适口，配白米饭。Pour sauce evenly over fish — glossy, perfectly balanced sweet and sour — serve with steamed rice.
