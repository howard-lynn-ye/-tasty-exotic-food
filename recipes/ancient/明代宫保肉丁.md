# 明代宫保肉丁 | Ming Dynasty Gong Bao Pork (明代, ~1500 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/明代宫保肉丁.svg" width="500" alt="明代宫保肉丁"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 10分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、明代、猪肉、辣味

> **📜 历史** — 宫保菜式的历史可以追溯到明代宫廷，比清代丁宝桢创制的宫保鸡丁早了数百年。明代御厨已经掌握了辣椒传入前的"辛香炒制"技法，用花椒和姜制造麻辣口感。猪肉丁配以花生和酱料快速爆炒的方式在明代已有雏形。当时辣椒尚未从美洲传入，厨师依靠花椒、胡椒和茱萸来增添辛辣，味型独特，与后世版本大不相同。
> **📜 History** — *The Gong Bao style of cooking traces back to the Ming imperial court, predating the Qing-era Ding Baozhen version by centuries. Ming imperial chefs had mastered "aromatic stir-fry" techniques before chili peppers arrived, using Sichuan peppercorn and ginger for a numbing-spicy effect. The prototype of diced pork with peanuts in a quick-fried sauce existed in the Ming era. Without New World chilies, cooks relied on Sichuan peppercorn, black pepper, and dogwood berry for heat — a unique flavor profile quite different from later versions.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 猪里脊肉（切丁） | Pork tenderloin (diced) | 300克 | 10.5 oz |
| 花生米（炒熟） | Roasted peanuts | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 生姜（切末） | Ginger (minced) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

| 大葱（切段） | Green onion (cut in sections) | 2根 | 2 stalks |

| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 料酒 | Shaoxing wine | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌肉** — 猪肉丁加半量酱油、料酒和淀粉拌匀，腌制10分钟。
   *Toss diced pork with half the soy sauce, Shaoxing wine, and cornstarch; marinate 10 minutes.*

2. **调酱汁** — 碗中混合剩余酱油、米醋、白糖和少许水，搅匀备用。
   *In a small bowl, mix remaining soy sauce, rice vinegar, sugar, and a splash of water; stir and set aside.*

3. **爆炒** — 锅中大火加油，先放花椒炒至出香（约20秒），捞出花椒。放入肉丁快炒至变色，加姜蒜末炒香，倒入酱汁翻炒至浓稠。
   *Heat oil in a wok over high heat. Add Sichuan peppercorns and stir until fragrant (about 20 seconds); remove peppercorns. Add pork and stir-fry until color changes. Add ginger and garlic, stir-fry until fragrant, pour in sauce and toss until thickened.*

4. **装盘** — 加入花生米和葱段快速翻炒几下即出锅装盘。
   *Add roasted peanuts and green onion sections; toss a few times and immediately plate.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 辣椒在16世纪末才从美洲传入中国，明代中期及以前的"辣"均来自花椒、胡椒和茱萸。
  *Chili peppers didn't reach China until the late 16th century; in the mid-Ming and earlier, "spiciness" came from Sichuan peppercorn, black pepper, and dogwood berry.*

- "宫保"一词原指清代官职太子太保，但炒制肉丁配花生的烹饪风格在明代已有记载。
  *"Gong Bao" originally referred to the Qing-era court title "Grand Guardian," but the cooking style of stir-fried diced meat with peanuts was already documented in the Ming era.*
