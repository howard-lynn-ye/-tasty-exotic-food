# 高棉帝国椰咖喱 Khmer Empire Coconut Curry

> 约公元1100年 | 高棉帝国（吴哥时期）| 东南亚宫廷美食
> Circa 1100 AD | Khmer Empire (Angkor Period) | Southeast Asian Court Cuisine

---

## 简介 Introduction

高棉帝国（802-1431）鼎盛时期控制着大半个东南亚，吴哥窟的浮雕中可以看到
丰富的宴饮场景。高棉宫廷利用本地丰富的椰子、香料和淡水鱼，发展出独特的
咖喱体系。这道椰咖喱使用高良姜、柠檬草和鱼露，浓郁醇厚，是吴哥宴席的
灵魂之作。

At its peak, the Khmer Empire (802-1431) dominated Southeast Asia. Angkor Wat
reliefs depict elaborate feasts. The Khmer court developed a unique curry
tradition using local coconut, spices, and freshwater fish. This coconut curry
features galangal, lemongrass, and fish sauce — rich, fragrant, and the soul
of an Angkorian banquet.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 椰浆 Coconut cream | 400ml |
| 淡水鱼肉（如鲈鱼）Freshwater fish fillet | 400g |
| 高良姜 Galangal | 30g |
| 柠檬草 Lemongrass | 2根 / 2 stalks |
| 干红辣椒 Dried red chilies | 5个 / 5 |
| 蒜 Garlic | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 小红葱 Shallots | 3个 / 3 |
| 鱼露 Fish sauce | 20ml |
| 棕榈糖 Palm sugar | 15g |
| 青柠叶 Kaffir lime leaves | 4片 / 4 |
| 罗勒叶 Thai basil | 一把 / a handful |
| 竹笋片 Bamboo shoot slices | 100g |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **制咖喱酱 Make paste**: 将高良姜、柠檬草、干辣椒、蒜、小红葱捣成粗酱。
   Pound galangal, lemongrass, chilies, garlic, and shallots into a rough paste.

2. **炒酱 Fry paste**: 取100ml椰浆入锅加热至油脂分离，加入咖喱酱炒香约5分钟。
   Heat 100ml coconut cream until oil separates, fry paste 5 min until fragrant.

3. **加椰浆 Add coconut**: 倒入剩余椰浆，加入青柠叶，中火煮沸。
   Pour in remaining coconut cream, add lime leaves, bring to a simmer.

4. **炖煮 Simmer**: 放入竹笋片，煮10分钟至笋软。
   Add bamboo shoots, cook 10 minutes until tender.

5. **加鱼 Add fish**: 鱼肉切大块放入，轻柔翻动，小火煮8分钟至鱼肉熟透。
   Cut fish into chunks, add gently, cook on low 8 minutes until done.

6. **调味 Season**: 加鱼露和棕榈糖调味，出锅前撒罗勒叶。
   Season with fish sauce and palm sugar, top with Thai basil before serving.
