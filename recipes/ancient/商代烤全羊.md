# 商代烤全羊 | Shang Dynasty Roast Whole Lamb (~1200BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/商代烤全羊.svg" width="500" alt="商代烤全羊"></p>

> ⏱ 准备60分+烹饪180分 | 💰~$35/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、商朝

> **📜 历史** — 商代祭祀大典中，烤全羊是最隆重的献礼，甲骨文中多次记载以"牢"祭天。
> **📜 History** — *Roast whole lamb was the grandest sacrificial offering in Shang Dynasty rituals, frequently recorded on oracle bones as "lao" offerings to heaven.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 羊腿 | Lamb leg | 2kg / 4.4lb |
| 粗盐 | Coarse salt | 30g / 2 tbsp |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 10g / 1 tbsp |
| 姜 | Ginger | 50g / 2oz |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 葱 | Scallion | 4根 / 4 stalks |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 腌制 | Marinate

将盐、花椒、姜末涂抹羊腿，腌制2小时。
Rub lamb leg with salt, peppercorn, and minced ginger; marinate 2 hours.

### 2. 慢烤 | Slow Roast

放入烤箱160°C烤2.5小时，每40分钟刷一次蜂蜜。
Roast at 320°F for 2.5 hours, basting with honey every 40 minutes.

### 3. 上桌 | Serve

撒葱花，切大块装盘，配粗粮饼食用。
Garnish with scallions, carve into large pieces, serve with flatbread.

---

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 腌制时用手将调料按入划刀纹中，风味更深入 | Press the rub into score marks by hand so the flavor penetrates deeper |
| 2 | 烤箱底部放一盘水，保持湿度防止羊肉干柴 | Place a pan of water on the bottom rack to keep moisture and prevent dryness |
| 3 | 最后10分钟调高温至220°C使表皮焦脆 | Crank heat to 430°F for the last 10 minutes to crisp the skin |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

商代甲骨文中的"牢"字本义即为牛羊等祭牲，烤全羊在祭祀体系中地位仅次于牛牲。殷墟考古发掘出大量烧烤坑遗迹与动物骨骸，证实商人已掌握成熟的炭火烤制技术。蜂蜜涂抹烤制的工艺虽无法从甲骨文中直接印证，但商代贵族对甜味调料的使用已有文献佐证。

The oracle bone character "lao" originally referred to sacrificial livestock such as cattle and sheep; roast lamb ranked just below cattle in the ritual hierarchy. Excavations at Yinxu unearthed numerous roasting pits and animal remains, confirming the Shang people mastered charcoal-roasting techniques. While honey-basting cannot be directly verified from oracle inscriptions, the Shang elite's use of sweet seasonings is supported by historical records.
