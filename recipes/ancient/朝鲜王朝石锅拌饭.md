# 朝鲜王朝石锅拌饭 | Joseon Dynasty Dolsot Bibimbap (朝鲜王朝, ~1500 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 30分钟 + 烹饪 25分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、朝鲜、米饭、拌菜

> **📜 历史** — 拌饭（비빔밥）的文字记载最早见于朝鲜王朝中期的《是议全书》（约1800年），但其起源可追溯至更早。传说它源于祭祀后将祭品混合而食的"骨董饭"，也有说法认为是农忙时节将剩余食材拌入米饭的便捷做法。石锅（돌솥）版本使米饭底部形成香脆的锅巴，成为朝鲜宫廷和两班贵族喜爱的料理。五色蔬菜代表阴阳五行，是韩国饮食哲学的完美体现。
> **📜 History** — *The earliest written record of bibimbap appears in the Joseon-era cookbook "Siuijeonseo" (~1800), but its origins trace back further. Legend says it evolved from mixing sacrificial offerings after ancestral rites ("goldongban"), or as a practical way to combine leftovers with rice during busy farming seasons. The stone pot (dolsot) version creates a crispy rice crust at the bottom, beloved by both the royal court and yangban aristocracy. The five-colored vegetables represent yin-yang and the five elements — a perfect expression of Korean food philosophy.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **准备配菜** — 菠菜和豆芽分别焯水拌香油和盐；胡萝卜和西葫芦分别用少许油炒软；牛肉用酱油、蒜末和香油腌制后炒熟。
   *Blanch spinach and bean sprouts separately, toss with sesame oil and salt. Sauté carrot and zucchini separately with a little oil. Marinate beef with soy sauce, garlic, and sesame oil, then stir-fry until cooked.*

2. **组装石锅** — 石锅内壁刷香油，放入热米饭，将五色配菜分区摆在米饭上，中间打一个生鸡蛋，大火加热3-4分钟至底部米饭形成金色锅巴。
   *Brush inside of stone pot with sesame oil. Add hot rice, arrange the five-color toppings in sections over the rice, crack a raw egg in the center. Heat on high 3-4 minutes until the bottom rice forms a golden crust.*

3. **拌匀食用** — 上桌后加入韩式辣酱，用勺子从底部翻拌均匀，让锅巴碎混入拌饭中享用。
   *At the table, add gochujang and mix thoroughly from the bottom up with a spoon, incorporating the crispy rice crust throughout.*
