# 北宋蔡京宴客菜 | Song Dynasty Cai Jing Banquet (~1100AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/北宋蔡京宴客菜.svg" width="500" alt="北宋蔡京宴客菜"></p>

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪45分 | 💰~$18/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、北宋、官府菜

> **📜 历史** — 蔡京为北宋权臣，嗜好奢华饮食。据《东京梦华录》等文献记载，其府上一道蟹黄包子耗蟹数十只，仅取黄膏。此菜以蟹黄配鲜笋还原其宴席精神。
> **📜 History** — *Cai Jing, a powerful Song-dynasty minister, was infamous for lavish dining. Records describe crab buns requiring dozens of crabs for roe alone. This dish recreates the banquet spirit with crab roe and fresh bamboo shoots.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 蟹黄/蟹膏 | Crab roe / tomalley | 80g / 3 oz |
| 鲜笋 | Fresh bamboo shoot | 200g / 7 oz |
| 五花肉 | Pork belly | 200g / 7 oz |
| 姜 | Ginger | 15g / 3 slices |
| 黄酒 | Shaoxing wine | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 白糖 | Sugar | 10g / 2 tsp |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 20ml / 4 tsp |
| 猪油 | Lard | 20g / 1½ tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 处理笋 | Prep Bamboo Shoot
鲜笋剥壳切滚刀块，焯水去涩味，沥干备用。Peel bamboo shoots, cut into rolling-knife chunks, blanch to remove bitterness, drain.

### 2. 煎肉 | Sear Pork
五花肉切厚片，猪油煎至两面金黄，烹黄酒。Slice pork belly thick, pan-fry in lard until golden on both sides, splash with Shaoxing wine.

### 3. 烩蟹黄 | Braise with Crab Roe
加笋块、姜片、酱油、糖和少量水，小火焖30分钟。最后5分钟拌入蟹黄，翻炒至香气四溢。Add bamboo shoots, ginger, soy sauce, sugar, and a little water; braise 30 minutes on low. Fold in crab roe for the last 5 minutes, stir until fragrant.

### 4. 摆盘 | Plate
装入古朴深盘，蟹黄金灿覆于肉笋之上，再现北宋官府华筵。Serve in a rustic deep plate, golden crab roe draped over meat and shoots — a recreation of the Song-dynasty elite banquet.
