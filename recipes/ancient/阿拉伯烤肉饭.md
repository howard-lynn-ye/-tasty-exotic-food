# 阿拉伯烤肉饭 | Arabian Kabsa Rice (阿拉伯半岛, ~700 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 60分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、阿拉伯、米饭、肉类

> **📜 历史** — Kabsa（كبسة）是阿拉伯半岛最具代表性的米饭料理，其名源于阿拉伯语"kabas"（压），指将所有食材在一锅中压煮。这道菜的雏形可追溯至伊斯兰教兴起初期（7世纪），当时阿拉伯商人通过丝绸之路和海上贸易获得了印度的巴斯马蒂大米和东南亚的香料。沙特阿拉伯将Kabsa视为国菜，每个家庭都有自己的秘方，周五聚礼后的家庭午餐几乎必备此菜。
> **📜 History** — *Kabsa is the most iconic rice dish of the Arabian Peninsula, its name derived from Arabic "kabas" (to press), referring to cooking all ingredients pressed together in one pot. The dish traces back to early Islam (7th century), when Arab traders obtained Indian basmati rice and Southeast Asian spices via the Silk Road and maritime trade. Saudi Arabia considers kabsa the national dish — every family has its own secret recipe, and it is virtually mandatory at the Friday post-prayer family lunch.*

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎鸡和炒底料** — 鸡腿用盐和小茴香腌制，锅中加油大火煎至金黄捞出。同锅炒洋葱至焦糖色，加蒜、番茄、番茄酱和所有香料翻炒3分钟。
   *Season chicken with salt and cumin. Heat oil and sear chicken until golden, set aside. In the same pot, caramelize onion, add garlic, tomatoes, tomato paste, and all spices; cook 3 minutes.*

2. **焖饭** — 倒入鸡汤煮沸，加入洗净沥干的巴斯马蒂米和鸡腿，搅拌均匀，加盖转最小火焖煮25分钟至米饭蓬松、汤汁收干。
   *Pour in chicken broth and bring to a boil. Add rinsed and drained basmati rice and chicken. Stir, cover, and cook on lowest heat 25 minutes until rice is fluffy and liquid absorbed.*

3. **装盘** — 大盘中先铺米饭，鸡腿摆在上面，撒炸松子和葡萄干点缀即可。
   *Mound rice on a large platter, place chicken on top, and garnish with fried pine nuts and raisins.*
