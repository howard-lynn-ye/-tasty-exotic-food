# 北齐烤饼 Northern Qi Flatbread

> 约公元550年 | 北齐（今河北、山东一带）| 鲜卑族面食
> Circa 550 AD | Northern Qi (Modern Hebei/Shandong) | Xianbei Bread Culture

---

## 简介 Introduction

北齐（550-577）由鲜卑化汉人高氏建立，虽然国祚短暂，却留下了丰富的饮食
文化记录。《齐民要术》中记载了多种饼类做法。北方游牧与农耕交汇之地，面饼
是最重要的主食。这种烤饼外酥内软，撒上芝麻，可以搭配炖肉或蘸酱食用，
是南北朝时期北方军民的日常口粮。

The Northern Qi (550-577), founded by the sinicized Xianbei Gao clan, left
rich culinary records despite its short reign. "Qimin Yaoshu" documents many
bread recipes. At the junction of nomadic and farming cultures, flatbread was
the staple. Crisp outside, soft inside, sprinkled with sesame — eaten with
stew or dipping sauce, it was daily bread for Northern Dynasties soldiers.

---

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 500g |
| 温水 Warm water | 280ml |
| 酵母（或老面）Yeast or sourdough | 5g |
| 盐 Salt | 8g |
| 芝麻 Sesame seeds | 30g |
| 葱花 Chopped scallion | 40g |
| 植物油（或羊油）Oil or lamb fat | 30ml |
| 花椒盐 Sichuan pepper salt | 10g |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **和面 Make dough**: 面粉加温水和酵母揉成光滑面团，盖布醒发1小时。
   Knead flour, warm water, and yeast into smooth dough, rest covered 1 hour.

2. **擀制 Roll out**: 面团分成8份，每份擀成薄片，刷油撒花椒盐和葱花。
   Divide into 8 portions, roll each thin, brush with oil, add pepper salt and scallion.

3. **卷叠 Fold**: 将薄片卷成长条再盘成圆饼状，按扁擀成约1cm厚的圆饼。
   Roll into a log, coil into a round, press and roll to ~1cm thick disc.

4. **粘芝麻 Add sesame**: 饼面刷薄水，撒上白芝麻轻轻按实。
   Brush surface with water, sprinkle sesame seeds and press gently.

5. **烤制 Bake**: 平底锅或石板烤炉预热，中火烤每面约5分钟至金黄。
   Preheat flat pan or stone oven, cook each side ~5 min on medium until golden.

6. **焖熟 Final cook**: 盖盖小火焖3分钟，确保内部完全熟透。
   Cover and cook on low 3 min to ensure the center is fully cooked.
