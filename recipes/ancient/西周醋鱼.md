# 西周醋鱼 | Western Zhou Vinegar Fish (西周, ~900 BC)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 20分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、西周、鱼类

> **📜 历史** — 《诗经》和《周礼》中多次提及以醋（醯）调味鱼鲜的烹饪方式。西周时期，醋已是"调鼎"五味之一，周天子的宫廷设有专门掌管醯醢（醋和肉酱）的官职"醯人"。《礼记·内则》记载"鱼宜菹"，即鱼搭配醋酱食用。这道西周醋鱼以先秦文献为依据还原，体现了中国最早的"酸味调鲜"烹饪哲学——三千年后的杭州西湖醋鱼正是这一传统的继承。
> **📜 History** — *The Book of Songs and Rites of Zhou frequently mention using vinegar (xi) to season fish. During the Western Zhou, vinegar was already one of the "five flavors" for seasoning, and the royal court had a dedicated official "xi ren" in charge of vinegar and meat sauces. The Book of Rites records "fish goes well with zu (vinegar sauce)." This recipe is reconstructed from pre-Qin texts, embodying China's earliest philosophy of "using sourness to enhance freshness" — Hangzhou's West Lake Vinegar Fish, created 3,000 years later, is a direct descendant of this tradition.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鲤鱼或鲈鱼（整条） | Carp or bass (whole) | 1条（约500g） | 1 fish (~1 lb) |
| 米醋 | Rice vinegar | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 黄酒或米酒 | Shaoxing wine or rice wine | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 姜丝 | Ginger (julienned) | 1大块 | 1 large piece |
| 葱段 | Scallion sections | 2根 | 2 stalks |
| 酱油（古代用豆酱） | Soy sauce (ancients used bean paste) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 水 | Water | 1杯 | 1 cup |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **处理鱼** — 鱼去鳞去内脏洗净，鱼身两面各划3刀，用盐和黄酒抹匀腌制10分钟。
   *Scale and gut the fish, rinse clean. Score 3 cuts on each side, rub with salt and Shaoxing wine, and marinate 10 minutes.*

2. **煮醋汁鱼** — 锅中加水、姜丝和葱段煮沸，轻轻放入鱼，中火煮8-10分钟至鱼肉刚好熟透，小心捞出装盘。
   *Bring water, ginger, and scallion to a boil in a pot. Gently lower in the fish, cook on medium 8-10 minutes until just cooked through. Carefully remove to a platter.*

3. **浇醋汁** — 锅中留汤汁小火加热，加入米醋、酱油和蜂蜜搅匀煮2分钟成醋汁，浇在鱼身上即可上桌。
   *Keep the cooking liquid in the pot over low heat. Add rice vinegar, soy sauce, and honey; stir and cook 2 minutes to form the vinegar sauce. Pour over the fish and serve.*
