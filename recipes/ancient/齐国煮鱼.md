# 齐国煮鱼 | Qi State Boiled Fish (齐国, ~500 BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/齐国煮鱼.svg" width="500" alt="齐国煮鱼"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 10分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$7/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、齐国、春秋、煮鱼、山东

> **📜 历史** — 齐国地处今山东半岛，临海面河，渔业发达。管仲辅佐齐桓公称霸时，"鱼盐之利"是齐国富强的经济支柱。《齐民要术》虽成书于北魏，但记载了大量源自齐鲁地区的古老烹鱼法。齐人煮鱼讲究"清水本味"，以盐、姜、葱为主调，追求鱼肉本身的鲜甜，这种"以清为贵"的理念深刻影响了后世鲁菜。
> **📜 History** — *The State of Qi occupied today's Shandong Peninsula, facing the sea and bordered by rivers, with a thriving fishing industry. When Guan Zhong helped Duke Huan of Qi achieve hegemony, the "profits of fish and salt" formed the economic pillar of Qi's wealth. Though compiled in the Northern Wei era, the "Essential Arts for the People" records ancient Qi fish-cooking methods. Qi-style boiled fish prized "pure water, true flavor" — seasoned only with salt, ginger, and scallion to highlight the fish's natural sweetness. This philosophy of "clarity as elegance" profoundly influenced later Shandong (Lu) cuisine.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 整条鲈鱼或鲷鱼 | Whole sea bass or sea bream | 1条（约1.5磅） | 1 fish (~1.5 lb) |
| 生姜（切片） | Ginger (sliced) | 4片 | 4 slices |
| 大葱（切段） | Scallions (cut into sections) | 3根 | 3 stalks |
| 盐 | Salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 米酒 | Rice wine | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 整鱼处理 | Prepare Fish

鱼洗净，两面各划三刀以便入味。鱼身抹少许盐和米酒，塞入几片姜和葱段，腌5分钟。
Clean the fish and score three cuts on each side. Rub with a little salt and rice wine, stuff a few ginger slices and scallion sections inside, and rest 5 minutes.

### 2. 清水煮鱼 | Poach in Clear Water

锅中加水没过鱼身，放入剩余姜片和葱段，大火煮开后放入鱼，转中小火煮10-12分钟，保持微微冒泡而不翻滚。
Add enough water to a pot to cover the fish. Add remaining ginger and scallions. Bring to a boil, lower the fish in, reduce to medium-low, and simmer 10-12 minutes — the surface should barely bubble, never boil vigorously.

### 3. 原汤上桌 | Serve with Broth

轻轻将鱼盛出装盘，浇两勺煮鱼原汤，撒葱丝。汤可单独盛碗作为鲜汤饮用。
Gently transfer the fish to a serving plate. Ladle two spoonfuls of the cooking broth over it and top with scallion shreds. The broth can be served separately as a light soup.

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 煮鱼全程保持微沸，大火滚煮会使鱼肉散碎 | Maintain a gentle simmer throughout; a rolling boil will break the fish apart |
| 2 | 盛鱼时用宽铲托底，整条鱼滑入盘中保持完整 | Use a wide spatula to support the fish from below and slide it onto the plate intact |
| 3 | 煮鱼水量宁多勿少，水面需完全没过鱼身才能均匀受热 | Use enough water to fully submerge the fish for even cooking |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

齐国在春秋时期是最富庶的诸侯国之一，管仲的"官山海"政策将盐铁收归国有，开创了中国最早的国家专营制度。齐人善烹鱼的传统延续至今——山东鲁菜中的糖醋鲤鱼、清蒸鲈鱼等经典菜式都可追溯到齐国"清水本味"的烹饪哲学。孔子在齐国闻韶乐"三月不知肉味"，但齐国的鱼鲜美食想必也给圣人留下了深刻印象。

The State of Qi was one of the wealthiest feudal states during the Spring and Autumn period; Guan Zhong's policy of nationalizing salt and iron created China's earliest state monopoly system. The Qi tradition of expert fish cookery endures to this day -- classic Shandong dishes like sweet-and-sour carp and steamed sea bass trace their roots to Qi's "pure water, true flavor" philosophy. Confucius, while in Qi, heard the Shao music and "forgot the taste of meat for three months," but the superb seafood of Qi must also have left a lasting impression on the sage.
