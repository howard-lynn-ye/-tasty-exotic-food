# 北魏烤饼 Northern Wei Roasted Flatbread

> **时代 Era:** 北魏 Northern Wei Dynasty · 约公元450年 (~450 AD)
> **地区 Region:** 平城/洛阳 Pingcheng/Luoyang (modern Datong/Luoyang)
> **类型 Type:** 主食 Staple / 面饼 Flatbread
> **难度 Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

---

## 历史背景 Historical Background

北魏是鲜卑族建立的政权，贾思勰在《齐民要术》中详细记载了多种饼的做法。"胡饼"是经丝绸之路传入的西域烤饼，在北魏贵族和平民中都极为流行。饼面撒芝麻，用炉壁高温烤制，外脆内软，与今天的新疆馕和中亚烤饼一脉相承。

The Northern Wei was founded by the Xianbei people. Jia Sixie's "Essential Arts for the People" details many flatbread recipes. "Hu bing" (barbarian bread) arrived from Central Asia via the Silk Road and was hugely popular among both Wei aristocrats and commoners. Topped with sesame, baked against hot oven walls — crispy outside, soft inside — it is the ancestor of today's Xinjiang naan and Central Asian flatbreads.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 中筋面粉 All-purpose flour | 300g |
| 温水 Warm water | 180ml |
| 酵母 Yeast | 3g |
| 盐 Salt | 1 茶匙 tsp |
| 芝麻 Sesame seeds | 30g |
| 植物油 Vegetable oil | 1 汤匙 tbsp |

---

## 步骤 Steps

### 第一步 Step 1 — 和面发酵 Mix and proof
温水中溶解酵母，倒入面粉和盐中，揉成光滑面团。表面刷油，盖保鲜膜在温暖处发酵1小时至两倍大。
Dissolve yeast in warm water. Pour into flour and salt, knead into a smooth dough. Brush surface with oil, cover with plastic wrap, and proof in a warm place 1 hour until doubled.

### 第二步 Step 2 — 整形 Shape
面团排气后分成4份，每份擀成直径约15厘米、厚1厘米的圆饼。表面刷水，撒上芝麻并轻轻按压使其嵌入。
Punch down dough and divide into 4 pieces. Roll each into a round disc about 15 cm diameter and 1 cm thick. Brush surface with water, sprinkle sesame seeds, and press lightly to embed.

### 第三步 Step 3 — 烤制 Bake
烤箱预热至最高温（230-250°C）。将饼放在预热的烤盘或烤石上，烤10-12分钟至表面金黄鼓起。也可用铸铁锅两面烙后入烤箱烤5分钟。
Preheat oven to maximum (230-250°C). Place flatbreads on a preheated baking sheet or stone. Bake 10-12 minutes until golden and puffed. Alternatively, sear both sides in a cast-iron pan, then finish in the oven for 5 minutes.

## 替代建议 Substitutions

| 原料 Original | 替代 Substitute | 说明 Note |
|---|---|---|
| 酵母发面 Yeast dough | 无酵母死面 Unleavened dough | 更接近早期版本 Closer to earlier versions |
