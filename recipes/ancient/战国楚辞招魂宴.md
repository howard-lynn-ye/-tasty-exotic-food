# 战国楚辞招魂宴 | Warring States Chu Ci Feast (~300BC)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/战国楚辞招魂宴.svg" width="500" alt="战国楚辞招魂宴"></p>

> ⏱ 准备30分+烹饪50分 | 💰~$18/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、先秦、楚菜

> **📜 历史** — 屈原《招魂》中描述了一席惊人盛宴："稻粢穱麦，挐黄粱些"，列举了蜜饯、烤肉、醴酒等数十种美食，是中国最早的宴席菜单文献之一。
> **📜 History** — *Qu Yuan's "Summoning the Soul" describes a stunning feast — honeyed meats, millet wine, roasted game — one of China's earliest written banquet menus from the Chu kingdom.*

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 鹿肉/牛肉 | Venison / beef | 400g / 14 oz |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 40ml / 3 tbsp |
| 花椒 | Sichuan peppercorn | 5g / 1 tsp |
| 桂皮 | Cassia bark | 1小段 / 1 small stick |
| 黍米/小米 | Millet | 150g / ¾ cup |
| 姜 | Ginger | 20g / 4 slices |
| 葱 | Scallion | 3根 / 3 stalks |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 腌肉 | Marinate Meat
肉切大块，蜂蜜、花椒、姜片腌制20分钟。据《招魂》所述，楚人重蜜与椒的搭配。Cut meat into large chunks, marinate with honey, peppercorn, and ginger 20 minutes. The Chu people prized the pairing of honey and pepper.

### 2. 烤炙 | Roast
炭火或烤箱220°C烤25分钟，每面刷蜜汁，至外焦里嫩。Grill over charcoal or roast at 425°F for 25 minutes, basting each side with honey glaze until caramelized outside, tender within.

### 3. 煮粟饭 | Cook Millet
小米洗净，加1.5倍水，加桂皮煮15分钟至饭粒松散。Rinse millet, cook with 1.5x water and cassia bark for 15 minutes until fluffy.

### 4. 上桌 | Serve
烤肉切片铺于粟饭上，撒葱花，搭配蜜汁蘸碟，再现楚宫宴饮之盛景。Slice roasted meat over millet, top with scallions, serve with honey dipping sauce — a recreation of the grand Chu palace feast.
