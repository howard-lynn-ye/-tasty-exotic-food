# 古代巴西烤肉 Ancient Brazilian Churrasco

> 约公元1500年 | 巴西南部高原 | 高乔牧民传统
> Circa 1500 AD | Southern Brazilian Highlands | Gaucho Herder Tradition

---

## 简介 Introduction

巴西烤肉（Churrasco）起源于南美洲南部的高乔人（Gaucho）。这些游牧牧民在
潘帕斯草原上放牧，就地取材，用长铁签串起大块牛肉，插在篝火旁慢烤。仅用
粗盐调味，让牛肉本身的风味在炭火中绽放。这是最原始、最纯粹的烤肉方式，
至今仍是巴西国菜的灵魂。

Brazilian Churrasco originated with the Gauchos of southern South America.
These nomadic herders on the Pampas skewered massive cuts of beef on long
iron spits beside campfires. Seasoned with nothing but coarse salt, the
beef's natural flavor blooms over charcoal. This is the most primal, purest
form of barbecue — still the soul of Brazil's national cuisine.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 牛腰脊肉（Picanha）Top sirloin cap | 1kg |
| 牛肋排 Beef ribs | 800g |
| 粗海盐 Coarse sea salt | 40g |
| 大蒜（可选）Garlic (optional) | 4瓣 / 4 cloves |
| 柠檬汁 Lime juice | 适量 / to taste |
| 木炭 Charcoal | 足量 / sufficient |
| 法罗法粉（木薯粉）Farofa (cassava flour) | 配菜 / as side |
| 黑豆 Black beans | 配菜 / as side |

## 做法 Instructions

1. **选肉 Select meat**: Picanha保留脂肪盖，切成C形弯曲的厚块（约3cm）。
   Keep fat cap on Picanha, cut into thick C-curved pieces (~3cm).

2. **撒盐 Salt**: 烤前30分钟，在肉的所有表面均匀撒上粗海盐。
   30 minutes before grilling, coat all surfaces evenly with coarse salt.

3. **串签 Skewer**: 用长铁签将肉块穿成弯曲的C形，脂肪面朝外。
   Thread onto long iron skewers in a C-curve, fat side facing out.

4. **架火 Build fire**: 木炭烧至白灰覆盖，无明火，温度约200-230°C。
   Burn charcoal until covered in white ash, no flame, ~200-230°C.

5. **慢烤 Slow roast**: 将肉签斜插在火堆旁，距炭火约30cm，慢烤40-60分钟。
   Angle skewers beside the fire, ~30cm from coals, slow roast 40-60 min.

6. **片肉 Slice and serve**: 外层烤熟后用利刀片下外层，继续烤内层，反复片食。
   When outer layer is done, carve it off, continue roasting inside. Repeat.

7. **配菜 Sides**: 搭配法罗法粉和黑豆炖肉一同食用。
   Serve with farofa and black bean stew.
