# 都铎王朝肉馅饼 | Tudor Meat Pie

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 都铎王朝 Tudor Dynasty (~1550AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 英格兰 England
> **类型 Type:** 馅饼/主菜 Pie / Main Course

---

## 典故 Historical Background

都铎时期的英格兰，肉馅饼是从王室宴席到平民餐桌都不可或缺的食物。亨利八世的宫廷宴会上，巨大的肉馅饼被做成城堡或皇冠形状以彰显权势。当时的馅饼外壳（coffyn）极厚极硬，主要作为容器而非食用，内部可填充各种禽肉、野味与香料。

In Tudor England, meat pies were indispensable from royal feasts to common tables. At Henry VIII's court banquets, enormous pies were shaped like castles or crowns to display power. The pie crust ("coffyn") was extremely thick and hard, serving primarily as a vessel rather than food, filled with assorted poultry, game, and spices.

## 做法 Preparation

1. **制皮 Make crust:** 面粉加盐，猪油加水煮沸后倒入面粉中，揉成光滑热水面团。此为"coffyn"传统做法。Mix flour and salt; boil lard with water, pour into flour, knead into a smooth hot-water dough. This is the traditional "coffyn" method.
2. **切馅 Prepare filling:** 鹿肉与猪肉切小丁，洋葱切碎，拌入所有香料与盐，搅拌均匀。Dice venison and pork small, chop onion, mix with all spices and salt.
3. **塑形 Shape:** 取三分之二面团擀成圆片铺入深陶模，用手指沿模壁推起边缘，填入肉馅压实。Roll two-thirds of dough into a round, line a deep clay mold, push edges up along the walls, pack in the meat filling.
4. **封盖 Seal top:** 剩余面团擀成圆片盖在顶部，边缘捏紧密封，中间留蒸汽孔，刷蛋黄液。Roll remaining dough for the lid, seal edges tightly, cut a steam vent in the center, brush with egg yolk.
5. **烘烤 Bake:** 放入砖炉中以中火烤约两个时辰，至外壳坚硬金黄。Bake in a brick oven at moderate heat for about 2 hours until the crust is hard and golden.
6. **灌汁 Fill with jelly:** 烤好后从蒸汽孔灌入熬好的骨汤明胶，冷却后凝固，切开可见肉馅被琥珀色肉冻包裹。Pour bone broth gelatin through the steam vent after baking; once cooled and set, slicing reveals meat encased in amber aspic.

## 备注 Notes

- 都铎时代的馅饼可保存数周，是长途旅行和军队行军的重要干粮。Tudor pies could keep for weeks, serving as essential provisions for long journeys and military marches.
- 大型宫廷馅饼有时藏入活鸟，切开时飞出以娱宾客——即"四二十只黑鸟"童谣的来源。Large court pies sometimes concealed live birds that flew out when cut — the origin of the "four and twenty blackbirds" nursery rhyme.
