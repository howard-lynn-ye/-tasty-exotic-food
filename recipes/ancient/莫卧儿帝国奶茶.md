# 莫卧儿帝国奶茶 | Mughal Chai Tea

> **朝代/时期 Dynasty/Era:** 莫卧儿帝国 Mughal Empire (~1600AD)
> **发源地 Origin:** 印度次大陆 Indian Subcontinent
> **类型 Type:** 饮品 Beverage

---

## 典故 Historical Background

莫卧儿帝国时期，来自中亚与波斯的皇室将饮茶文化带入印度次大陆，并与当地丰富的香料传统结合，诞生了玛萨拉奶茶（Masala Chai）。莫卧儿宫廷中，奶茶被视为暖身养生的圣饮，帝王在冬日猎鹰之后以此暖腹。每位皇家厨师都有独门的香料配方，以肉桂、小豆蔻、丁香、黑胡椒与生姜的不同比例竞争最佳滋味。

During the Mughal Empire, royals of Central Asian and Persian descent brought tea-drinking culture to the Indian subcontinent, where it merged with the region's rich spice traditions to create Masala Chai. In the Mughal court, spiced milk tea was considered a sacred warming tonic; emperors drank it to warm up after winter falconry. Each royal chef guarded a secret spice formula, competing for the finest blend of cinnamon, cardamom, cloves, black pepper, and ginger.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 红茶叶（阿萨姆） Assam black tea | 2大匙 2 tbsp |
| 全脂牛奶 Whole milk | 2杯 2 cups |
| 水 Water | 1杯 1 cup |
| 生姜 Fresh ginger | 3片 3 slices |
| 绿豆蔻 Green cardamom | 4颗 4 pods |
| 肉桂棒 Cinnamon stick | 1根 1 stick |
| 丁香 Cloves | 3颗 3 whole |
| 黑胡椒粒 Black peppercorns | 4颗 4 whole |
| 粗糖或蜂蜜 Jaggery or honey | 适量 To taste |

## 做法 Preparation

1. **碾香料 Crush spices:** 小豆蔻拍开取籽，丁香与黑胡椒粒放入石臼中轻轻捣碎，肉桂掰成小段，生姜拍裂。Crack cardamom and extract seeds, lightly crush cloves and peppercorns in a mortar, break cinnamon into pieces, smash ginger.
2. **煮水 Boil water:** 水入铜壶或小锅，放入所有香料，大火煮沸后转小火煮约一刻钟，使香料精华充分释放。Bring water to a boil in a copper pot with all spices, reduce to low and simmer about 15 minutes to fully extract the essence.
3. **加茶 Add tea:** 放入红茶叶，煮三至五分钟至茶汤深红浓厚。Add black tea leaves, boil 3-5 minutes until the brew is deep red and strong.
4. **加奶 Add milk:** 倒入全脂牛奶，再次煮沸，转小火慢煮使茶与奶充分融合，颜色变为温暖的琥珀棕色。Pour in whole milk, bring to a boil again, then simmer on low to fully meld tea and milk into a warm amber-brown.
5. **调甜 Sweeten:** 加入粗糖或蜂蜜搅匀，甜度根据个人喜好调整。Stir in jaggery or honey, adjusting sweetness to personal taste.
6. **过滤上桌 Strain and serve:** 以细筛过滤去茶叶与香料渣，倒入陶杯中，趁热饮用。Strain through a fine sieve to remove leaves and spice residue, pour into clay cups, drink hot.

## 备注 Notes

- 莫卧儿宫廷有时会加入藏红花丝，使奶茶呈金色并增添高雅芬芳。The Mughal court sometimes added saffron threads, turning the chai golden with an elegant fragrance.
- 传统做法中茶与奶的反复煮沸是关键，现代速泡法无法还原其醇厚口感。The traditional repeated boiling of tea and milk is essential — modern quick-brew methods cannot replicate its full-bodied richness.
