# 非洲花生炖鸡 | West African Peanut Chicken Stew (西非, ~1200 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/非洲花生炖鸡.svg" width="500" alt="非洲花生炖鸡"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、西非、炖菜、肉类

> **📜 历史** — 花生（groundnut）原产南美洲，约在16世纪经葡萄牙人传入西非，但西非在此之前已有用本土坚果（如竹芋籽）制作类似炖菜的传统。花生迅速融入西非饮食，成为马里、塞内加尔和加纳最重要的食材之一。这道"maafe"（花生酱炖菜）在曼丁哥（Mandinka）文化中是节庆与待客的核心菜肴，体现了西非"一锅炖"的communal饮食传统。
> **📜 History** — *Peanuts (groundnuts) originated in South America and arrived in West Africa via Portuguese traders around the 16th century, but West Africans already had traditions of nut-based stews using indigenous seeds. Peanuts quickly became central to the cuisine of Mali, Senegal, and Ghana. This "maafe" (peanut butter stew) is a cornerstone dish in Mandinka culture for celebrations and hospitality, embodying West Africa's communal one-pot dining tradition.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿（带骨） | Chicken thighs (bone-in) | 4个 | 4 pieces |
| 天然花生酱（无糖） | Natural peanut butter (unsweetened) | 1/2杯 | 1/2 cup |
| 番茄（切碎）或罐头番茄 | Tomatoes (chopped) or canned tomatoes | 2个或1罐 | 2 or 1 can |
| 洋葱（切碎） | Onion (diced) | 1个大 | 1 large |
| 大蒜（切碎） | Garlic (minced) | 4瓣 | 4 cloves |
| 姜（磨碎） | Ginger (grated) | 1汤匙 | 1 tbsp |
| 红薯（切块） | Sweet potato (cubed) | 1个大 | 1 large |
| 鸡汤 | Chicken broth | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 辣椒粉 | Cayenne pepper | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **煎鸡腿** — 鸡腿用盐调味，锅中加油大火煎至两面金黄（每面约4分钟）捞出备用。同锅炒洋葱、蒜和姜至香软。
   *Season chicken with salt. Heat oil and sear chicken until golden on both sides (~4 min per side), set aside. In the same pot, sauté onion, garlic, and ginger until soft and fragrant.*

2. **炖煮** — 加入番茄炒软，倒入鸡汤，搅入花生酱至完全溶解。放回鸡腿和红薯块，加辣椒粉，煮沸后转小火盖盖炖30分钟至鸡肉脱骨、红薯软烂。
   *Add tomatoes and cook until soft. Pour in broth and stir in peanut butter until dissolved. Return chicken and add sweet potato cubes and cayenne. Bring to a boil, cover, and simmer 30 minutes until chicken falls off the bone and sweet potato is tender.*

3. **上桌** — 配白米饭或粗粮一起食用，撒碎花生和香菜点缀。
   *Serve over white rice or with coarse grains, garnished with crushed peanuts and cilantro.*
