# 凯尔特麦酒炖牛 | Celtic Ale Beef Stew (~300BC)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪120分 | 💰~$16/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、凯尔特

> **📜 历史** — 凯尔特人以铁锅炖煮牛肉，加入自酿麦酒和野菜，是不列颠群岛最古老的炖菜之一。
> **📜 History** — *Celts slow-cooked beef in iron cauldrons with homebrewed ale and wild herbs, one of the oldest stews of the British Isles.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛腩 | Beef chuck | 600g / 1.3lb |
| 麦酒/啤酒 | Ale / Beer | 350ml / 1.5 cups |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot | 2根 / 2 |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 |
| 百里香 | Thyme | 3枝 / 3 sprigs |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 煎肉 | Sear Beef
牛腩切块，大火煎至表面焦褐，取出备用。

Cut beef into chunks, sear on high heat until browned, set aside.

### 2. 炖煮 | Stew
加洋葱和胡萝卜炒软，倒入麦酒和牛肉，放百里香，小火炖2小时。

Saute onion and carrot, add ale, beef, and thyme, simmer on low for 2 hours.

### 3. 上桌 | Serve
炖至汤汁浓厚、肉质酥烂，配粗面包食用。

Cook until sauce thickens and meat is tender, serve with crusty bread.

---

## 要点 | Tips
| 要点 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 煎肉时不要翻动太勤，让每面充分焦化产生美拉德反应 | Don't flip meat too often — let each side develop a deep Maillard crust |
| 选用深色麦酒（如棕色艾尔或世涛），风味比淡啤更浓郁 | Use dark ales (brown ale or stout) for richer flavor than lager |
| 炖煮时间越长肉质越软，可用炖锅低温慢煮4-6小时效果更佳 | Longer cooking yields softer meat; a slow cooker on low for 4-6 hours works even better |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes
凯尔特人是欧洲最早掌握铁器和酿酒技术的民族之一，他们的大铁锅"cauldron"既是炊具也是祭祀器皿。麦酒炖牛体现了凯尔特人就地取材的烹饪智慧——用发酵麦酒嫩化坚韧的牛肉，同时赋予汤汁醇厚的风味。

*The Celts were among the first Europeans to master ironworking and brewing; their great iron cauldrons served both as cooking vessels and ritual objects. Ale-braised beef reflects Celtic resourcefulness — using fermented ale to tenderize tough cuts while imparting a rich, malty depth to the broth.*
