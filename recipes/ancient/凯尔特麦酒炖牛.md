# 凯尔特麦酒炖牛 | Celtic Ale Beef Stew (~300BC)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪120分 | 💰~$16/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、凯尔特

> **📜 历史** — 凯尔特人以铁锅炖煮牛肉，加入自酿麦酒和野菜，是不列颠群岛最古老的炖菜之一。
> **📜 History** — *Celts slow-cooked beef in iron cauldrons with homebrewed ale and wild herbs, one of the oldest stews of the British Isles.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 牛腩 | Beef chuck | 600g / 1.3lb |
| 麦酒/啤酒 | Ale / Beer | 350ml / 1.5 cups |
| 胡萝卜 | Carrot | 2根 / 2 |
| 洋葱 | Onion | 1个 / 1 |
| 百里香 | Thyme | 3枝 / 3 sprigs |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 / To taste |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 煎肉 | Sear Beef
牛腩切块，大火煎至表面焦褐，取出备用。Cut beef into chunks, sear on high heat until browned, set aside.

### 2. 炖煮 | Stew
加洋葱和胡萝卜炒软，倒入麦酒和牛肉，放百里香，小火炖2小时。Saute onion and carrot, add ale, beef, and thyme, simmer on low for 2 hours.

### 3. 上桌 | Serve
炖至汤汁浓厚、肉质酥烂，配粗面包食用。Cook until sauce thickens and meat is tender, serve with crusty bread.
