# 渤海国烤鹿肉 | Balhae Kingdom Roasted Venison (渤海国, ~700 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 30分钟 | 💰 ~$15/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、渤海国、鹿肉、烤制、东北

> **📜 历史** — 渤海国（698-926）由靺鞨族建立于今中国东北和朝鲜半岛北部，被誉为"海东盛国"。渤海国林深兽多，鹿是最重要的猎物之一。史料记载渤海国向唐朝进贡鹿肉和鹿茸，宫廷宴饮以烤鹿肉为上品。渤海人以松木炭火慢烤鹿肉，配以山野蘑菇，保留了东北森林民族的原始风味。
> **📜 History** — *The Balhae Kingdom (698-926), founded by the Mohe people in present-day Manchuria and northern Korea, was praised as the "Flourishing Land East of the Sea." Its deep forests teemed with game, deer being the most prized quarry. Records show Balhae sent venison and antler velvet as tribute to the Tang court, and roasted venison was the finest banquet dish. The Balhae people slow-roasted venison over pine charcoal, served with wild forest mushrooms — preserving the primal flavors of Manchuria's woodland peoples.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鹿肉排或鹿里脊 | Venison chops or loin | 1磅 | 1 lb |
| 松子 | Pine nuts | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 干蘑菇（香菇或松茸） | Dried mushrooms (shiitake or matsutake) | 6朵 | 6 pcs |
| 粗盐 | Coarse salt | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 生姜（拍碎） | Ginger (smashed) | 2块 | 2 pieces |
| 米酒 | Rice wine | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **腌制去腥** — 鹿肉以粗盐、拍碎的姜块和米酒揉搓均匀，腌制20分钟去除野味腥气。干蘑菇温水泡发后切片备用。
   *Rub venison with coarse salt, smashed ginger, and rice wine. Marinate 20 minutes to remove gaminess. Rehydrate dried mushrooms in warm water and slice.*

2. **炭火慢烤** — 烤箱预热至375°F（190°C）。鹿肉放入铸铁锅，四周围上蘑菇片，盖锡纸烤20分钟，揭开再烤10分钟至表面微焦。
   *Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C). Place venison in a cast-iron pan surrounded by mushroom slices. Cover with foil and roast 20 minutes, then uncover and roast 10 more minutes until lightly charred.*

3. **撒松子上桌** — 取出鹿肉切片，铺上烤蘑菇，撒烤香的松子。趁热食用，可蘸少许粗盐。
   *Remove venison and slice. Arrange roasted mushrooms alongside, sprinkle with toasted pine nuts. Serve hot with coarse salt for dipping.*
