> 约公元1400年 | 夏威夷群岛 | 波利尼西亚地炉烤猪
> Circa 1400 AD | Hawaiian Islands | Polynesian Imu Earth Oven Roast

---

## 简介 Introduction

夏威夷烤猪（Kalua Pig）是波利尼西亚文化中最重要的仪式性食物。"Kalua"意为
"在地下烤"——在地面挖一个大坑（imu），铺上火山石加热后，用芭蕉叶和蕉叶包裹
整只猪，埋入地炉中慢烤8-12小时。这种方式能让猪肉均匀受热，肉质极其松软，
带有淡淡的烟熏和植物清香，是Luau（夏威夷宴会）的核心菜肴。

Kalua Pig is the most important ceremonial food in Polynesian culture.
"Kalua" means "to cook underground" — a pit (imu) is dug, lined with heated
volcanic rocks, then a whole pig wrapped in banana and ti leaves is buried
and slow-roasted 8-12 hours. The result is incredibly tender, evenly cooked
meat with subtle smoke and herbal fragrance — the centerpiece of every Luau.

## 食材 Ingredients

| 食材 Ingredient | 用量 Amount |
|---|---|
| 猪肩肉（带皮）Pork shoulder (skin on) | 2kg |
| 夏威夷海盐（红盐）Hawaiian red salt | 30g |
| 芭蕉叶 Banana leaves | 4大张 / 4 large |
| 蕉叶（Ti leaves，可用铝箔替代）Ti leaves | 8片 / 8 |
| 液体烟熏调味（模拟imu风味）Liquid smoke | 15ml |
| 清水 Water | 100ml |

---

## 做法 Instructions

1. **处理猪肉 Prep pork**: 猪肩肉用叉子在表面密密戳孔，让调味渗透。
   Pierce pork shoulder all over with a fork for seasoning penetration.

2. **调味 Season**: 将红盐和液体烟熏均匀搓揉在猪肉表面和孔洞中。
   Rub red salt and liquid smoke evenly over the surface and into the holes.

3. **包裹 Wrap**: 先用蕉叶紧紧包裹猪肉，再用芭蕉叶包第二层。
   Wrap tightly first in ti leaves, then in a second layer of banana leaves.

4. **慢烤 Slow cook**: 放入预热150°C的烤箱（模拟地炉），加水在底部，烤6-8小时。
   Place in 150°C oven (simulating imu), add water to the bottom, roast 6-8 hours.

5. **检查 Check**: 肉应当能用叉子轻松撕开。如果还不够软，继续烤1-2小时。
   Meat should shred easily with a fork. If not, continue roasting 1-2 hours.

6. **撕肉 Shred**: 打开叶包，用两把叉子将猪肉撕成细丝，拌入烤盘中的汁液。
   Unwrap, shred meat with two forks, toss with the collected pan juices.

7. **上桌 Serve**: 传统上搭配椰子布丁（haupia）和芋泥（poi）食用。
   Traditionally served with haupia (coconut pudding) and poi (taro paste).
