# 唐代胡饼 | Tang Dynasty Sesame Flatbread (唐代, ~700 AD)
<p align="center">  <img src="../../images/ancient/唐代胡饼.svg" width="500" alt="唐代胡饼"></p>

> ⏱ 准备 20分钟 + 烹饪 15分钟 | 💰 ~$2/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、唐代、面食、烘焙

> **📜 历史** — 胡饼是经丝绸之路传入中国的西域面食，在唐代长安城风靡一时。白居易诗中写道"胡麻饼样学京都，面脆油香新出炉"，描述了刚出炉胡饼的诱人香气。唐代胡饼铺遍布长安街头，从皇帝到平民无不喜爱。这种撒满芝麻的烤饼是中国最早的"街头小吃"之一。
> **📜 History** — *Hu bing (barbarian flatbread) traveled to China via the Silk Road and became wildly popular in Tang Dynasty Chang'an. The poet Bai Juyi wrote of "sesame cakes modeled after the capital style, crispy-skinned and fragrant fresh from the oven." Hu bing shops lined the streets of Chang'an, loved by emperors and commoners alike. This sesame-covered baked bread was one of China's earliest "street foods."*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 中筋面粉 | All-purpose flour | 2杯 | 2 cups |
| 温水 | Warm water | 3/4杯 | 3/4 cup |
| 酵母 | Active dry yeast | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 糖 | Sugar | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 1/2茶匙 | 1/2 tsp |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 白芝麻 | White sesame seeds | 3汤匙 | 3 tbsp |
| 葱花 | Chopped scallions | 2根 | 2 stalks |
| 花椒粉（可选） | Sichuan peppercorn powder (optional) | 1/4茶匙 | 1/4 tsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **和面** — 温水中溶入酵母和糖，静置5分钟至起泡。加入面粉、盐和1汤匙油，揉成光滑面团，盖上湿布醒发30分钟。
   *Dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water; let stand 5 minutes until foamy. Add flour, salt, and 1 tbsp oil. Knead into a smooth dough, cover with a damp cloth, and let rise 30 minutes.*

2. **擀制成饼** — 面团分成4份，每份擀成约6寸圆饼。撒上葱花和花椒粉（可选），卷起再压扁擀开，使葱花均匀分布。
   *Divide dough into 4 pieces. Roll each into a 6-inch round. Sprinkle with scallions and Sichuan pepper (optional), roll up, then press flat and roll out again to distribute the filling.*

3. **沾芝麻** — 饼面刷薄薄一层水，将一面按入芝麻中，使芝麻紧密粘附。
   *Brush the surface lightly with water, then press one side into sesame seeds so they adhere firmly.*

4. **烤制** — 烤箱预热至425°F（220°C），烤盘刷油，将饼芝麻面朝上放入，烤12-15分钟至两面金黄酥脆。
   *Preheat oven to 425°F (220°C). Oil a baking sheet, place flatbreads sesame-side up, and bake 12-15 minutes until golden and crisp on both sides.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- "胡"字在唐代泛指西域各民族，胡饼因此得名，意为"外国饼"。
  *The character "胡" (hu) broadly referred to Central Asian peoples in the Tang era, so hu bing literally means "foreign flatbread."*

- 唐代长安是国际化大都市，胡饼的流行反映了丝绸之路上的文化美食交流。
  *Tang Chang'an was a cosmopolitan metropolis; the popularity of hu bing reflects the culinary exchange along the Silk Road.*

- 现代新疆的馕饼和西安的肉夹馍用的白吉馍都可追溯到唐代胡饼的传统。
  *Modern Xinjiang naan and the bun used in Xi'an roujiamo can both trace their lineage to Tang Dynasty hu bing.*
