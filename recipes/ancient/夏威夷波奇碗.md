# 夏威夷波奇碗 | Hawaiian Poke Bowl (~1400AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪0分 | 💰~$15/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古代夏威夷

> **📜 历史** — 波奇(Poke)在夏威夷语中意为"切块"，原住民渔民将新鲜捕获的鱼切块加海盐和海藻调味，是最古老的生鱼料理之一。
> **📜 History** — *"Poke" means "to cut" in Hawaiian; native fishermen seasoned freshly caught diced fish with sea salt and seaweed, one of the oldest raw fish preparations.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 金枪鱼(刺身级) | Sushi-grade tuna | 300g / 10oz |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 麻油 | Sesame oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 海藻沙拉 | Seaweed salad | 50g / 2oz |
| 米饭 | Steamed rice | 300g / 2 cups |
| 牛油果 | Avocado | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions

### 1. 切鱼 | Cut Fish

金枪鱼切成2cm方块，加酱油和麻油拌匀腌10分钟。
Cut tuna into 2cm cubes, toss with soy sauce and sesame oil, marinate 10 minutes.

### 2. 备碗 | Build Bowl

碗底铺米饭，一侧放海藻沙拉，另一侧放牛油果片。
Layer rice on the bottom, place seaweed salad on one side, sliced avocado on the other.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble

将腌好的金枪鱼摆在碗中央，撒芝麻点缀即可。
Place marinated tuna in the center, garnish with sesame seeds and serve.

## 要点 | Tips

| # | 要点 | Tip |
|---|------|-----|
| 1 | 鱼肉必须是刺身级新鲜度，购买时确认可生食 | Fish must be sushi/sashimi-grade; confirm it is safe to eat raw when purchasing |
| 2 | 腌制时间不超过15分钟，否则酱油会使鱼肉变咸变硬 | Do not marinate longer than 15 minutes or the soy sauce will over-salt and firm up the fish |
| 3 | 米饭放至微温再铺碗底，避免热饭将生鱼"烫熟" | Let rice cool to lukewarm before building the bowl so it does not cook the raw fish |

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

波奇碗的原始形态远比现代版本简朴——夏威夷原住民仅用海盐、海藻和碎烛果(kukui nut)调味切块生鱼，没有米饭和酱油。日本移民在19世纪末带来了酱油和芝麻油的调味方式，使波奇逐渐演变为今天的风格。现代波奇碗加入牛油果、毛豆等配料，已成为全球流行的健康快餐。

The original poke was far simpler than its modern form -- Hawaiian natives seasoned diced raw fish with only sea salt, seaweed, and crushed kukui nut, without rice or soy sauce. Japanese immigrants introduced soy sauce and sesame oil seasoning in the late 19th century, gradually transforming poke into today's style. Modern poke bowls, with additions like avocado and edamame, have become a globally popular healthy fast food.
