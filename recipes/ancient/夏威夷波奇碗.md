# 夏威夷波奇碗 | Hawaiian Poke Bowl (~1400AD)

> ⏱ 准备20分+烹饪0分 | 💰~$15/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、古代夏威夷

> **📜 历史** — 波奇(Poke)在夏威夷语中意为"切块"，原住民渔民将新鲜捕获的鱼切块加海盐和海藻调味，是最古老的生鱼料理之一。
> **📜 History** — *"Poke" means "to cut" in Hawaiian; native fishermen seasoned freshly caught diced fish with sea salt and seaweed, one of the oldest raw fish preparations.*

---

## 食材 | Ingredients
| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 / Amount |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 金枪鱼(刺身级) | Sushi-grade tuna | 300g / 10oz |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce | 30ml / 2 tbsp |
| 麻油 | Sesame oil | 15ml / 1 tbsp |
| 海藻沙拉 | Seaweed salad | 50g / 2oz |
| 米饭 | Steamed rice | 300g / 2 cups |
| 牛油果 | Avocado | 1个 / 1 |

---

## 做法 | Directions
### 1. 切鱼 | Cut Fish
金枪鱼切成2cm方块，加酱油和麻油拌匀腌10分钟。Cut tuna into 2cm cubes, toss with soy sauce and sesame oil, marinate 10 minutes.

### 2. 备碗 | Build Bowl
碗底铺米饭，一侧放海藻沙拉，另一侧放牛油果片。Layer rice on the bottom, place seaweed salad on one side, sliced avocado on the other.

### 3. 组装 | Assemble
将腌好的金枪鱼摆在碗中央，撒芝麻点缀即可。Place marinated tuna in the center, garnish with sesame seeds and serve.
