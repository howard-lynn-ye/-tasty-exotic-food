# 玛雅可可鸡 | Mayan Chocolate Chicken (尤卡坦半岛, ~600 AD)

> ⏱ 准备 15分钟 + 烹饪 45分钟 | 💰 ~$6/份 | 🏷️ 古代名菜、中美洲、鸡肉、可可

> **📜 历史** — 玛雅人是世界上最早驯化可可树的民族，可可在玛雅语中被称为"神之食物"（ka'kaw）。公元600年前后的玛雅古典期，贵族们不仅饮用可可饮品，还将可可磨成酱用于烹饪野禽。这道菜是后来墨西哥国菜Mole的远古原型，展现了可可从甜品到咸食的惊人跨越。
> **📜 History** — *The Maya were the first civilization to domesticate the cacao tree, calling it ka'kaw — "food of the gods." During the Classic Maya period around 600 AD, nobles not only drank cacao beverages but also ground cacao into paste for cooking wild fowl. This dish is the ancient prototype of Mexico's national dish Mole, showing cacao's remarkable leap from sweet drink to savory cuisine.*

## 食材 | Ingredients

| 食材 | Ingredient | 用量 | Amount |
|------|-----------|------|--------|
| 鸡腿（带骨） | Chicken thighs (bone-in) | 6只 | 6 pieces |
| 无糖可可粉 | Unsweetened cocoa powder | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |
| 干辣椒（去籽） | Dried chili (seeded) | 3个 | 3 |
| 番茄（切块） | Tomatoes (chopped) | 2个 | 2 |
| 南瓜籽 | Pumpkin seeds | 1/4杯 | 1/4 cup |
| 洋葱（切碎） | Onion (diced) | 1个 | 1 |
| 大蒜 | Garlic | 3瓣 | 3 cloves |
| 肉桂（一小段） | Cinnamon stick | 1段 | 1 piece |
| 鸡汤 | Chicken broth | 1.5杯 | 1.5 cups |
| 蜂蜜 | Honey | 1茶匙 | 1 tsp |
| 盐 | Salt | 适量 | to taste |
| 植物油 | Vegetable oil | 2汤匙 | 2 tbsp |

---

## 做法 | Directions

1. **烤辣椒和南瓜籽** — 干锅中火烘烤干辣椒和南瓜籽2-3分钟至微焦出香，取出放入搅拌机。
   *Dry-roast dried chili and pumpkin seeds in a skillet over medium heat for 2-3 minutes until lightly toasted and fragrant. Transfer to a blender.*

2. **制酱** — 搅拌机中加入番茄、洋葱、大蒜、可可粉、肉桂和半杯鸡汤，搅打成细腻酱汁。
   *Add tomatoes, onion, garlic, cocoa powder, cinnamon, and half the broth to the blender. Blend into a smooth sauce.*

3. **煎鸡肉** — 锅中加油，中大火将鸡腿煎至金黄（每面3-4分钟），取出备用。
   *Heat oil in a deep skillet over medium-high heat. Sear chicken until golden (3-4 minutes per side). Set aside.*

4. **炖煮** — 同锅倒入酱汁，中火煮沸后加入剩余鸡汤和蜂蜜，放回鸡腿，小火炖30分钟至鸡肉熟透，酱汁浓稠。加盐调味。
   *Pour sauce into the same skillet, bring to a boil, then add remaining broth and honey. Return chicken, reduce heat, and simmer 30 minutes until cooked through and sauce is thick. Season with salt.*

## 历史注解 | Historical Notes

- 玛雅人在可可饮料中加入辣椒、香草和蜂蜜，这种"辣巧克力"的传统延续至今。
  *The Maya added chili, vanilla, and honey to cacao drinks — the tradition of "spiced chocolate" continues today.*

- 可可豆在玛雅社会中兼作货币使用，一只火鸡大约值100颗可可豆。
  *Cacao beans doubled as currency in Maya society — a turkey was worth about 100 cacao beans.*

- 考古学家在危地马拉科尔哈遗址的陶罐中发现了可可与辣椒混合的残留物，证实了这种烹饪方式。
  *Archaeologists found cacao-and-chili residue in pottery at the Colha site in Guatemala, confirming this cooking method.*
