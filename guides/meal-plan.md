## 小贴士 | Tips

| 贴士 | Tip |
|------|-----|
| 米饭一次煮3-4天的量，冷藏保存 | Cook 3-4 days of rice at once, refrigerate |
| 隔夜饭做蛋炒饭，零浪费 | Leftover rice → fried rice, zero waste |
| Costco 买大包装肉类，分小包冷冻 | Buy bulk meat at Costco, portion and freeze |
| 冷冻蔬菜 (TJ's, Walmart) 比新鲜的便宜且不会坏 | Frozen veggies are cheaper and don't go bad |
| 调料一次买齐，能用好几个月 | Buy seasonings once — they last months |
