# 留学生厨房装备指南 | Student Kitchen Equipment Guide

> 刚到美国，不知道该买什么厨具？这份指南告诉你最值得投资的装备，以及如何用美国超市常见的厨具替代中式专业工具。
>
> *Just arrived in America and don't know what to buy for your kitchen? This guide covers the most worthwhile equipment investments and how to substitute Chinese cooking tools with what's available at American stores.*

## 必买清单 | Must-Have List

## 中式工具 vs 美式替代 | Chinese Tools vs. American Substitutes

| 中式工具 | Chinese Tool | 美式替代 | American Substitute | 效果 / How Well It Works |
|---------|-------------|---------|--------------------|-----------------------|
| 炒锅 (铁锅) | Chinese wok | Carbon steel wok (Amazon) 或大号不粘锅 | Wok from Amazon is ideal; non-stick skillet works for small batches | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 砂锅 | Clay pot | Dutch oven (Lodge/Le Creuset) | Cast iron retains heat similarly — very close results | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 蒸锅 | Multi-layer steamer | Steamer basket insert 或 Instant Pot | Metal steamer insert fits in any pot; Instant Pot has steam mode | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 中式菜刀 | Chinese cleaver | 8" chef's knife | Any good chef's knife does the job | ⭐⭐ |
| 笊篱 (漏勺) | Wire mesh skimmer | Slotted spoon 或 spider strainer | Spider strainer from Amazon (~$8) is identical | ⭐⭐⭐ |
| 擀面杖 | Chinese rolling pin | French rolling pin 或 wine bottle | A wine bottle works in a pinch! | ⭐⭐ |
| 石臼 (蒜臼) | Mortar & pestle | Garlic press 或用刀拍蒜 | A garlic press is faster; smashing with a knife is free | ⭐⭐ |
| 电磁炉 | Portable induction burner | 同 / Same — portable induction cooktop | Available at Amazon, Target ($30-40). Great for hot pot and dorm cooking | ⭐⭐⭐ |

## 宿舍党指南 | Dorm Room Cooking

如果你住宿舍，厨房空间有限或者只有微波炉，这些是你的救星：

*If you live in a dorm with limited kitchen access or only a microwave, these are your lifesavers:*

| 装备 / Equipment | 价格 / Price | 能做什么 / What You Can Make |
|-----------------|-------------|---------------------------|
| 电饭锅 (带蒸屉) / Rice cooker with steamer tray | $20-30 | 煮饭、蒸菜、煮粥、甚至煮面 / Rice, steamed veggies, congee, even noodles |
| 便携电磁炉 + 小锅 / Portable induction burner + small pot | $30-40 | 几乎所有菜都能做 / Almost everything becomes possible |
| 迷你电火锅 / Mini electric hot pot | $20-30 | 煮面、煮汤、一人食火锅 / Noodles, soup, solo hot pot |
| 微波炉专用蒸盒 / Microwave steamer | $10 | 蒸蔬菜、热饭菜 / Steam veggies, reheat meals |

> **提示 / Tip:** 很多宿舍不允许明火和大功率电器。购买前请确认你的宿舍规定！
>
> *Many dorms prohibit open flames and high-wattage appliances. Check your dorm rules before buying!*
