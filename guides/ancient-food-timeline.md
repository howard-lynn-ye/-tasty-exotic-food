## 有趣的食物历史冷知识 | Fun Food History Facts

| 冷知识 | Fun Fact |
|--------|---------|
| 最早的书面食谱来自巴比伦，刻在泥板上，距今3700年 | The oldest written recipes are Babylonian clay tablets from 1700 BC |
| 蛋炒饭的原型"碎金饭"出现在隋朝，距今1400年 | Egg fried rice's prototype appeared in the Sui Dynasty, 1400 years ago |
| 天妇罗不是日本原创——是16世纪葡萄牙传教士带来的 | Tempura isn't Japanese-original — Portuguese missionaries brought it in the 16th century |
| 巧克力最初是辣的苦饮，阿兹特克人加辣椒喝 | Chocolate was originally a bitter, spicy drink — Aztecs added chili |
| 火锅的原型可以追溯到元朝忽必烈 | Hot pot's prototype traces back to Kublai Khan in the Yuan Dynasty |
| 古罗马人用蜂蜜代替糖，因为糖还没传到欧洲 | Ancient Romans used honey instead of sugar — sugar hadn't reached Europe yet |
| 藏红花比黄金还贵，波斯人最早用它做饭 | Saffron was more expensive than gold — Persians first used it in cooking |
| 咖喱这个词来自泰米尔语"kari"，意思是酱汁 | The word "curry" comes from Tamil "kari," meaning sauce |
