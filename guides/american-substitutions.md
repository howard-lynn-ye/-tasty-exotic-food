# 美国超市采购指南 | American Supermarket Shopping Guide

> 留学生在美国做中国菜并非难事——大部分核心调料在亚洲超市都能买到，很多食材在 Trader Joe's、Costco、Walmart 也有平替。本指南帮你用美国超市里的食材，最大程度还原家乡的味道。
>
> *Cooking Chinese food in America is more achievable than you might think. Most essential seasonings are available at Asian supermarkets, and many ingredients have solid substitutes at mainstream stores like Trader Joe's, Costco, and Walmart. This guide helps you bring the flavors of home to your American kitchen.*

---

## 核心调料 | Essential Pantry Items

这些是几乎每道杭州菜都会用到的基础调料，建议一次性备齐。

*These pantry staples appear in nearly every Hangzhou recipe. Stock up once and you're set for months.*

| 调料 | Seasoning | 最佳来源 / Best Source | 替代 / Substitute | 备注 / Notes |
|------|-----------|----------------------|-------------------|--------------|
| 绍兴黄酒 | Shaoxing yellow wine | 亚洲超市 (H Mart, 99 Ranch) | Dry sherry (Fino 或 Amontillado) | ⚠️ 切勿买标注 "cooking wine" 的产品，含大量盐。选瓶身写"绍兴花雕"的 / Never buy salted "cooking wine" — look for "花雕" on the label |
| 镇江香醋 | Chinkiang (Zhenjiang) vinegar | 亚洲超市，约$2-3/瓶 | Aged balsamic vinegar + 少许 rice vinegar | 建议买正宗的，便宜且保质期长 / Buy the real thing — it's cheap and lasts forever |
| 酱油 | Soy sauce (regular) | 任何超市 | Kikkoman Naturally Brewed | 全美几乎所有超市有售 / Available virtually everywhere |
| 老抽 | Dark soy sauce | 亚洲超市 (Lee Kum Kee 品牌) | 普通酱油 + 少许糖蜜 (molasses) | Walmart、Target 亚洲食品区有时也有 / Sometimes in Walmart/Target Asian aisle |
| 冰糖 | Rock sugar | 亚洲超市 | 白砂糖，用量减30% | 冰糖使酱汁更亮 / Rock sugar gives a clearer glaze |
| 香油 | Sesame oil | 任何超市 | Kadoya 品牌最常见 | Trader Joe's 有自有品牌 toasted sesame oil |
| 白胡椒粉 | Ground white pepper | 任何超市香料区 | — | McCormick 品牌最常见 |
| 淀粉 | Cornstarch | 任何超市 | — | — |
| 五香粉 | Five-spice powder | 大多数超市香料区 | McCormick 品牌 | Trader Joe's 也有售 |
