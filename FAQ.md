## 关于项目 | About This Project

### 这个项目有多少道菜？| How many recipes?

**518道菜谱**，分布在11个分类中。其中最大的两个分类是：
- **217道古代名菜** — 跨越5000年，从古埃及(2000BC)到拿破仑时代(1800AD)
- **233道AI原创融合菜** — Claude AI用风味科学设计的独创组合

*518 recipes across 11 categories. The two largest are 217 ancient recipes (5000 years of food history) and 233 AI-original fusion recipes (designed by Claude AI using flavor science).*

### 古代菜谱是真的可以做的吗？| Are the ancient recipes actually cookable?

是的！每道古代菜谱都经过现代化改良，使用美国超市能买到的食材。我们保留了历史风味的核心，但替换了已不可得的古代食材。每道菜都附有"替代食材"表格。

*Yes! Every ancient recipe has been modernized with ingredients available at American supermarkets. We preserve the historical flavor profile but substitute unavailable ancient ingredients. Each recipe includes a substitutions table.*

### AI菜谱是怎么设计的？| How are AI recipes designed?

我们的AI原创菜基于[风味科学原理](guides/flavor-science.md)设计：分析味觉维度（甜咸酸苦鲜麻）、跨文化桥梁食材、美拉德反应、脂溶性原理等，确保每个创意组合在化学层面是可行的。

*Our AI recipes are designed using [flavor science principles](guides/flavor-science.md): analyzing taste dimensions, cross-cultural bridge ingredients, Maillard reactions, fat solubility, etc. to ensure each creative combination works at a chemical level.*

## 食材问题 | Ingredient Questions

### 绍兴黄酒买不到，用什么替代？| Can't find Shaoxing wine?

用 **dry sherry**（Fino 或 Amontillado）替代，任何超市酒类区都有。⚠️ 绝不要买标注 "cooking wine" 的产品，含大量盐。

*Use **dry sherry** (Fino or Amontillado) — available in the wine section of any store. Never buy anything labeled "cooking wine" — it's loaded with salt.*

### 镇江香醋买不到？| No Chinkiang vinegar?

用 **rice vinegar + 少许 balsamic vinegar** 混合替代。但建议去亚洲超市买正宗的，$2-3 一瓶能用很久。

*Mix **rice vinegar + a splash of balsamic**. But honestly, go to an Asian market — it's $2-3 and lasts forever.*

### 豆腐怎么选？| How to choose tofu?

| 类型 / Type | 用途 / Best For | 哪里买 / Where |
|-------------|---------------|---------------|
| Silken / Soft | 麻婆豆腐、酸辣汤 | Trader Joe's, Walmart |
| Firm | 炒菜、煎 | 任何超市 |
| Extra Firm | 烤、炸、不容易碎 | Whole Foods, Target |

## 工具问题 | Equipment Questions

### 没有炒锅可以做中国菜吗？| Can I cook Chinese food without a wok?

可以！大号不粘锅可以替代90%的炒菜需求。想要更好的效果，Amazon 买一个碳钢炒锅 ($30-50)。

*Yes! A large non-stick skillet handles 90% of stir-frying. For better results, get a carbon steel wok from Amazon ($30-50).*

### Instant Pot 值得买吗？| Is an Instant Pot worth it?

如果你经常做炖菜（红烧牛腩、东坡肉），绝对值得。把3小时炖煮缩短到45分钟，而且还能煮饭、煮粥。Target/Costco 经常打折到 $60-80。

*If you make braised dishes (beef brisket, Dongpo pork), absolutely yes. Cuts 3 hours to 45 minutes, plus it cooks rice and congee. Target/Costco often has deals at $60-80.*

### 米饭怎么煮最好？| Best way to cook rice?

1. **电饭锅** (最简单) — 按比例加水，按按钮就行 | Rice cooker (easiest) — add water, press button
2. **Instant Pot** — 1:1 米水比例，Rice 模式 | 1:1 ratio, Rice mode
3. **锅煮** — 1:1.5 米水比例，大火烧开→最小火15分钟→焖10分钟 | 1:1.5 ratio, boil → low heat 15 min → rest 10 min

## 翻车急救 | Troubleshooting

### 鸡蛋炒老了/炒碎了 | Eggs turned rubbery or fell apart

**原因：** 油不够热 或 炒太久。
**解决：** 油要热到冒烟再下蛋，蛋液底部凝固后马上翻，不要反复翻炒。

*Cause: Oil wasn't hot enough, or you stirred too long. Fix: Heat oil until smoking, pour eggs in, wait for the bottom to set, then fold once. Don't keep stirring.*

### 肉煮老了/柴了 | Meat turned tough and dry

**原因：** 火太大 或 煮太久。
**解决：**
- 炒肉：大火快炒，变色立刻盛出 | Stir-fry: High heat, remove the moment it changes color
- 炖肉：小火慢炖，急不来 | Braise: Low and slow, never rush
- 上浆（加淀粉和蛋清腌制）可以锁住水分 | Velveting (cornstarch + egg white marinade) locks in moisture

### 菜太咸了 | Too salty

**急救方案：**
1. 加水稀释（汤类） | Add water (for soups)
2. 加糖平衡 | Add sugar to balance
3. 加醋提鲜盖咸 | Add vinegar to mask saltiness
4. 加一个土豆一起煮（吸盐） | Add a potato chunk to absorb salt
5. 多煮点米饭配着吃 | Make extra rice to eat with it

### 菜太淡了 | Too bland

**通常是缺少以下一种或多种：**
1. 盐 — 最基本的调味 | Salt — the most basic seasoning
2. 酱油 — 加鲜味 | Soy sauce — adds umami
3. 糖 — 提鲜（很多人忽略） | Sugar — enhances flavor (often overlooked)
4. 醋 — 酸味唤醒味蕾 | Vinegar — acidity wakes up the palate
5. 香油 — 最后淋一点 | Sesame oil — drizzle at the end

### 炒菜出水太多/不够干 | Stir-fry is watery

**原因：** 锅不够热 或 食材太多挤在一起。
**解决：**
1. 锅要烧到冒烟再放菜 | Heat wok until smoking before adding food
2. 分批炒，不要堆满锅 | Cook in batches — don't overcrowd
3. 蔬菜先沥干水分 | Pat vegetables dry before cooking
